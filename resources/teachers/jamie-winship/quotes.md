## On identity vs. calling

> "I think of a calling as *doing* and identity as *being*."
— Jamie Winship, Jennie Allen interview ("Conflict Zones, the CIA, and Listening to God")

> "Linking identity to vocation is what destroys good people, especially in the police."
— Jamie Winship, Jennie Allen interview

## On the character of God

— Jamie Winship, Jennie Allen interview

## On hearing God

> "Hearing from God is fixing your eyes on Jesus, asking him a question, and waiting."
— Jamie Winship, Jennie Allen interview

> "What do you call me when you talk about me?"

— Jamie Winship, Christianity Today *Where Ya From?* ("Discovering Your Identity")
