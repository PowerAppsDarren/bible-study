# Jamie Winship — Resource Library

Curated reference material for Jamie Winship, modeled in this repo as the agent **`teacher-jamie-winship`** (`.claude/agents/teacher-jamie-winship.md`).

> **Copyright note:** This folder is shared-layer. It holds links, transformative study notes, and short attributed quotes only — no full transcripts, article copies, or downloaded copyrighted PDFs. (See `CONTRIBUTING.md` and `resources/teachers/README.md`.)

## Who he is

Jamie Winship is a former Metro Washington, D.C. police officer who spent nearly thirty years bringing peace and reconciliation work to some of the world's highest-conflict regions — Indonesia, Jordan, Iraq, Palestine, Israel — much of it across the Muslim world. After law enforcement he earned an M.A. in English and developed the **Identity Method**, now taught with his wife Donna through their ministry **Identity Exchange**. His central claim: *all human conflict originates from fear, and fear originates from a false view of God, of ourselves, and of others.*

- **Books:** *Living Fearless: Exchanging the Lies of the World for the Liberating Truth of God* (Bethany House, 2022 — 250,000+ copies); *War of Worldviews*; *Living Fearless Guided Journal*
- **Ministry:** Identity Exchange — courses, coaching, the Identity Method
- **Podcast:** *The Jamie & Donna Winship Podcast*
