How to abide — the four A's as a loop

flowchart TD
  A[Fix attention on Jesus] --> B[Ask Him a question]
  B --> C[Wait and listen]
  C --> D[Awareness: hear what is true]
  D --> E[Annunciation: declare it]
  E --> F[Action: obey it]
  F -. the commitment to stay with .-> A

What to notice: abiding is the loop you stay inside (dotted return), not a line you finish.

Why we need it — the fear → conflict chain, reversed

flowchart TD
  A[False view of God] --> B[Fear]
  B --> C[False identity]
  C --> D[Conflict]
  E[True identity in Christ] -->|dissolves| F[Fearless love and peace]
  D -. Jesus reverses .-> E

What to notice: every conflict traces back to fear, and fear to a false view of God — the exchange begins at the root.
