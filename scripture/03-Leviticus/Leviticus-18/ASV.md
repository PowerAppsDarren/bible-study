# Leviticus 18 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Jehovah spake unto Moses, saying,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Speak unto the children of Israel, and say unto them, I am Jehovah your God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** After the doings of the land of Egypt, wherein ye dwelt, shall ye not do: and after the doings of the land of Canaan, whither I bring you, shall ye not do; neither shall ye walk in their statutes.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Mine ordinances shall ye do, and my statutes shall ye keep, to walk therein: I am Jehovah your God.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Ye shall therefore keep my statutes, and mine ordinances; which if a man do, he shall live in them: I am Jehovah.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** None of you shall approach to any that are near of kin to him, to uncover their nakedness: I am Jehovah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The nakedness of thy father, even the nakedness of thy mother, shalt thou not uncover: she is thy mother; thou shalt not uncover her nakedness.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The nakedness of thy father’s wife shalt thou not uncover; it is thy father’s nakedness.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The nakedness of thy sister, the daughter of thy father, or the daughter of thy mother, whether born at home, or born abroad, even their nakedness thou shalt not uncover.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The nakedness of thy son’s daughter, or of thy daughter’s daughter, even their nakedness thou shalt not uncover: for theirs is thine own nakedness.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The nakedness of thy father’s wife’s daughter, begotten of thy father, she is thy sister, thou shalt not uncover her nakedness.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of thy father’s sister: she is thy father’s near kinswoman.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of thy mother’s sister: for she is thy mother’s near kinswoman.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of thy father’s brother, thou shalt not approach to his wife: she is thine aunt.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of thy daughter-in-law: she is thy son’s wife; thou shalt not uncover her nakedness.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of thy brother’s wife: it is thy brother’s nakedness.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of a woman and her daughter; thou shalt not take her son’s daughter, or her daughter’s daughter, to uncover her nakedness; they are near kinswomen: it is wickedness.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And thou shalt not take a wife to her sister, to be a rival to her, to uncover her nakedness, besides the other in her life-time.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And thou shalt not approach unto a woman to uncover her nakedness, as long as she is impure by her uncleanness.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And thou shalt not lie carnally with thy neighbor’s wife, to defile thyself with her.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And thou shalt not give any of thy seed to make them pass through the fire to Molech; neither shalt thou profane the name of thy God: I am Jehovah.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Thou shalt not lie with mankind, as with womankind: it is abomination.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And thou shalt not lie with any beast to defile thyself therewith; neither shall any woman stand before a beast, to lie down thereto: it is confusion.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Defile not ye yourselves in any of these things: for in all these the nations are defiled which I cast out from before you;

<a id="v25"></a>**25** and the land is defiled: therefore I do visit the iniquity thereof upon it, and the land vomiteth out her inhabitants.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Ye therefore shall keep my statutes and mine ordinances, and shall not do any of these abominations; neither the home-born, nor the stranger that sojourneth among you

<a id="v27"></a>**27** (for all these abominations have the men of the land done, that were before you, and the land is defiled);

<a id="v28"></a>**28** that the land vomit not you out also, when ye defile it, as it vomited out the nation that was before you.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** For whosoever shall do any of these abominations, even the souls that do them shall be cut off from among their people.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Therefore shall ye keep my charge, that ye practise not any of these abominable customs, which were practised before you, and that ye defile not yourselves therein: I am Jehovah your God.
