# Leviticus 18 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh said to Moses,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** “Speak to the children of Israel, and say to them, ‘I am Yahweh your God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You shall not do as they do in the land of Egypt, where you lived: and you shall not do as they do in the land of Canaan, where I am bringing you; neither shall you walk in their statutes.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You shall do my ordinances, and you shall keep my statutes, and walk in them: I am Yahweh your God.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You shall therefore keep my statutes and my ordinances; which if a man does, he shall live in them. I am Yahweh.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** “‘None of you shall approach anyone who are his close relatives, to uncover their nakedness: I am Yahweh.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** “‘You shall not uncover the nakedness of your father, nor the nakedness of your mother: she is your mother. You shall not uncover her nakedness.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** “‘You shall not uncover the nakedness of your father’s wife. It is your father’s nakedness.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** “‘You shall not uncover the nakedness of your sister, the daughter of your father, or the daughter of your mother, whether born at home, or born abroad.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** “‘You shall not uncover the nakedness of your son’s daughter, or of your daughter’s daughter, even their nakedness: for theirs is your own nakedness.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** “‘You shall not uncover the nakedness of your father’s wife’s daughter, conceived by your father, since she is your sister.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** “‘You shall not uncover the nakedness of your father’s sister: she is your father’s near kinswoman.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** “‘You shall not uncover the nakedness of your mother’s sister: for she is your mother’s near kinswoman.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** “‘You shall not uncover the nakedness of your father’s brother, you shall not approach his wife. She is your aunt.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** “‘You shall not uncover the nakedness of your daughter-in-law: she is your son’s wife. You shall not uncover her nakedness.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** “‘You shall not uncover the nakedness of your brother’s wife. It is your brother’s nakedness.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** “‘You shall not uncover the nakedness of a woman and her daughter. You shall not take her son’s daughter, or her daughter’s daughter, to uncover her nakedness; they are near kinswomen: it is wickedness.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** “‘You shall not take a wife to her sister, to be a rival, to uncover her nakedness, while her sister is yet alive.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** “‘You shall not approach a woman to uncover her nakedness, as long as she is impure by her uncleanness.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** “‘You shall not lie carnally with your neighbor’s wife, and defile yourself with her.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** “‘You shall not give any of your children to sacrifice to Molech; neither shall you profane the name of your God. I am Yahweh.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** “‘You shall not lie with a man, as with a woman. That is detestable.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** “‘You shall not lie with any animal to defile yourself with it; neither shall any woman give herself to an animal, to lie down with it: it is a perversion.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** “‘Don’t defile yourselves in any of these things: for in all these the nations which I am casting out before you were defiled.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** The land was defiled: therefore I punished its iniquity, and the land vomited out her inhabitants.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** You therefore shall keep my statutes and my ordinances, and shall not do any of these abominations; neither the native-born, nor the stranger who lives as a foreigner among you;

<a id="v27"></a>**27** (for all these abominations have the men of the land done, that were before you, and the land became defiled);

<a id="v28"></a>**28** that the land not vomit you out also, when you defile it, as it vomited out the nation that was before you.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** “‘For whoever shall do any of these abominations, even the souls that do them shall be cut off from among their people.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Therefore you shall keep my requirements, that you do not practice any of these abominable customs, which were practiced before you, and that you do not defile yourselves with them. I am Yahweh your God.’”
