# Leviticus 24 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And the Lord said to Moses,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Give orders to the children of Israel to give you clean olive oil for the light, so that a light may be burning at all times,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Outside the veil of the ark in the Tent of meeting; let Aaron see that it is burning from evening till morning at all times before the Lord: it is a rule for ever through all your generations.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Let Aaron put the lights in order on the support before the Lord at all times.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And take the best meal and make twelve cakes of it, a fifth part of an ephah in every cake.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And put them in two lines, six in a line, on the holy table before the Lord.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And on the lines of cakes put clean sweet-smelling spices, for a sign on the bread, an offering made by fire to the Lord.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Every Sabbath day regularly, the priest is to put it in order before the Lord: it is offered for the children of Israel, an agreement made for ever.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And it will be for Aaron and his sons; they are to take it for food in a holy place: it is the most holy of all the offerings made by fire to the Lord, a rule for ever.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And a son of an Israelite woman, whose father was an Egyptian, went out among the children of Israel and had a fight with a man of Israel by the tents;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And the son of the Israelite woman said evil against the holy Name, with curses; and they took him to Moses. His mother's name was Shelomith, the daughter of Dibri, of the tribe of Dan.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And they kept him shut up, till a decision might be given by the mouth of the Lord.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And the Lord said to Moses,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Take the curser outside the tent-circle; and let all in whose hearing the words were said put their hands on his head, and let him be stoned by all the people.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And say to the children of Israel, As for any man cursing God, his sin will be on his head.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And he who says evil against the name of the Lord will certainly be put to death; he will be stoned by all the people; the man who is not of your nation and one who is an Israelite by birth, whoever says evil against the holy Name is to be put to death.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And anyone who takes another's life is certainly to be put to death.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And anyone wounding a beast and causing its death, will have to make payment for it: a life for a life.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And if a man does damage to his neighbour, as he has done, so let it be done to him;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Wound for wound, eye for eye, tooth for tooth; whatever damage he has done, so let it be done to him.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He who puts a beast to death will have to make payment for it; he who puts a man to death will himself be put to death.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** You are to have the same law for a man of another nation living among you as for an Israelite; for I am the Lord your God.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And Moses said these words to the children of Israel, and they took the man who had been cursing outside the tent-circle and had him stoned. The children of Israel did as the Lord gave orders to Moses.
