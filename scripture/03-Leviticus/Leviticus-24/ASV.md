# Leviticus 24 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Jehovah spake unto Moses, saying,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Command the children of Israel, that they bring unto thee pure olive oil beaten for the light, to cause a lamp to burn continually.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Without the veil of the testimony, in the tent of meeting, shall Aaron keep it in order from evening to morning before Jehovah continually: it shall be a statute for ever throughout your generations.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He shall keep in order the lamps upon the pure candlestick before Jehovah continually.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And thou shalt take fine flour, and bake twelve cakes thereof: two tenth parts of an ephah shall be in one cake.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And thou shalt set them in two rows, six on a row, upon the pure table before Jehovah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And thou shalt put pure frankincense upon each row, that it may be to the bread for a memorial, even an offering made by fire unto Jehovah.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Every sabbath day he shall set it in order before Jehovah continually; it is on the behalf of the children of Israel, an everlasting covenant.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And it shall be for Aaron and his sons; and they shall eat it in a holy place: for it is most holy unto him of the offerings of Jehovah made by fire by a perpetual statute.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And the son of an Israelitish woman, whose father was an Egyptian, went out among the children of Israel; and the son of the Israelitish woman and a man of Israel strove together in the camp:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** and the son of the Israelitish woman blasphemed the Name, and cursed; and they brought him unto Moses. And his mother’s name was Shelomith, the daughter of Dibri, of the tribe of Dan.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And they put him in ward, that it might be declared unto them at the mouth of Jehovah.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And Jehovah spake unto Moses, saying,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Bring forth him that hath cursed without the camp; and let all that heard him lay their hands upon his head, and let all the congregation stone him.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And thou shalt speak unto the children of Israel, saying, Whosoever curseth his God shall bear his sin.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And he that blasphemeth the name of Jehovah, he shall surely be put to death; all the congregation shall certainly stone him: as well the sojourner, as the home-born, when he blasphemeth the name of Jehovah, shall be put to death.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And he that smiteth any man mortally shall surely be put to death.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And he that smiteth a beast mortally shall make it good, life for life.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And if a man cause a blemish in his neighbor; as he hath done, so shall it be done to him:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** breach for breach, eye for eye, tooth for tooth; as he hath caused a blemish in a man, so shall it be rendered unto him.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And he that killeth a beast shall make it good: and he that killeth a man shall be put to death.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Ye shall have one manner of law, as well for the sojourner, as for the home-born: for I am Jehovah your God.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And Moses spake to the children of Israel; and they brought forth him that had cursed out of the camp, and stoned him with stones. And the children of Israel did as Jehovah commanded Moses.
