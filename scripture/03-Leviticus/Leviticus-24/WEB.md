# Leviticus 24 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** “Command the children of Israel, that they bring to you pure olive oil beaten for the light, to cause a lamp to burn continually.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Outside of the veil of the Testimony, in the Tent of Meeting, shall Aaron keep it in order from evening to morning before Yahweh continually: it shall be a statute forever throughout your generations.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He shall keep in order the lamps on the pure gold lamp stand before Yahweh continually.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** “You shall take fine flour, and bake twelve cakes of it: two tenth parts of an ephah shall be in one cake.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You shall set them in two rows, six on a row, on the pure gold table before Yahweh.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You shall put pure frankincense on each row, that it may be to the bread for a memorial, even an offering made by fire to Yahweh.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Every Sabbath day he shall set it in order before Yahweh continually. It is on the behalf of the children of Israel an everlasting covenant.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** It shall be for Aaron and his sons; and they shall eat it in a holy place: for it is most holy to him of the offerings of Yahweh made by fire by a perpetual statute.”

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The son of an Israelite woman, whose father was an Egyptian, went out among the children of Israel; and the son of the Israelite woman and a man of Israel strove together in the camp.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The son of the Israelite woman blasphemed the Name, and cursed; and they brought him to Moses. His mother’s name was Shelomith, the daughter of Dibri, of the tribe of Dan.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They put him in custody, until Yahweh’s will should be declared to them.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** “Bring out of the camp him who cursed; and let all who heard him lay their hands on his head, and let all the congregation stone him.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** You shall speak to the children of Israel, saying, ‘Whoever curses his God shall bear his sin.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He who blasphemes Yahweh’s name, he shall surely be put to death. All the congregation shall certainly stone him. The foreigner as well as the native-born, when he blasphemes the Name, shall be put to death.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** “‘He who strikes any man mortally shall surely be put to death.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He who strikes an animal mortally shall make it good, life for life.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** If anyone injures his neighbor; as he has done, so shall it be done to him:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** fracture for fracture, eye for eye, tooth for tooth; as he has injured someone, so shall it be done to him.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He who kills an animal shall make it good; and he who kills a man shall be put to death.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** You shall have one kind of law for the foreigner as well as the native-born; for I am Yahweh your God.’”

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Moses spoke to the children of Israel; and they brought out him who had cursed out of the camp, and stoned him with stones. The children of Israel did as Yahweh commanded Moses.
