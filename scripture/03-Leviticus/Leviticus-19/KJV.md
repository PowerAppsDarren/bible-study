# Leviticus 19 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And the Lord spake unto Moses, saying,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Speak unto all the congregation of the children of Israel, and say unto them, Ye shall be holy: for I the Lord your God am holy.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Ye shall fear every man his mother, and his father, and keep my sabbaths: I am the Lord your God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Turn ye not unto idols, nor make to yourselves molten gods: I am the Lord your God.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And if ye offer a sacrifice of peace offerings unto the Lord, ye shall offer it at your own will.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** It shall be eaten the same day ye offer it, and on the morrow: and if ought remain until the third day, it shall be burnt in the fire.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And if it be eaten at all on the third day, it is abominable; it shall not be accepted.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Therefore every one that eateth it shall bear his iniquity, because he hath profaned the hallowed thing of the Lord: and that soul shall be cut off from among his people.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And when ye reap the harvest of your land, thou shalt not wholly reap the corners of thy field, neither shalt thou gather the gleanings of thy harvest.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And thou shalt not glean thy vineyard, neither shalt thou gather every grape of thy vineyard; thou shalt leave them for the poor and stranger: I am the Lord your God.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Ye shall not steal, neither deal falsely, neither lie one to another.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And ye shall not swear by my name falsely, neither shalt thou profane the name of thy God: I am the Lord.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thou shalt not defraud thy neighbour, neither rob him: the wages of him that is hired shall not abide with thee all night until the morning.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Thou shalt not curse the deaf, nor put a stumblingblock before the blind, but shalt fear thy God: I am the Lord.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Ye shall do no unrighteousness in judgment: thou shalt not respect the person of the poor, nor honour the person of the mighty: but in righteousness shalt thou judge thy neighbour.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Thou shalt not go up and down as a talebearer among thy people: neither shalt thou stand against the blood of thy neighbour: I am the Lord.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Thou shalt not hate thy brother in thine heart: thou shalt in any wise rebuke thy neighbour, and not suffer sin upon him.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Thou shalt not avenge, nor bear any grudge against the children of thy people, but thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself: I am the Lord.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Ye shall keep my statutes. Thou shalt not let thy cattle gender with a diverse kind: thou shalt not sow thy field with mingled seed: neither shall a garment mingled of linen and woollen come upon thee.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And whosoever lieth carnally with a woman, that is a bondmaid, betrothed to an husband, and not at all redeemed, nor freedom given her; she shall be scourged; they shall not be put to death, because she was not free.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And he shall bring his trespass offering unto the Lord, unto the door of the tabernacle of the congregation, even a ram for a trespass offering.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And the priest shall make an atonement for him with the ram of the trespass offering before the Lord for his sin which he hath done: and the sin which he hath done shall be forgiven him.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And when ye shall come into the land, and shall have planted all manner of trees for food, then ye shall count the fruit thereof as uncircumcised: three years shall it be as uncircumcised unto you: it shall not be eaten of.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** But in the fourth year all the fruit thereof shall be holy to praise the Lord withal.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And in the fifth year shall ye eat of the fruit thereof, that it may yield unto you the increase thereof: I am the Lord your God.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Ye shall not eat any thing with the blood: neither shall ye use enchantment, nor observe times.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Ye shall not round the corners of your heads, neither shalt thou mar the corners of thy beard.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Ye shall not make any cuttings in your flesh for the dead, nor print any marks upon you: I am the Lord.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Do not prostitute thy daughter, to cause her to be a whore; lest the land fall to whoredom, and the land become full of wickedness.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Ye shall keep my sabbaths, and reverence my sanctuary: I am the Lord.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Regard not them that have familiar spirits, neither seek after wizards, to be defiled by them: I am the Lord your God.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Thou shalt rise up before the hoary head, and honour the face of the old man, and fear thy God: I am the Lord.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** And if a stranger sojourn with thee in your land, ye shall not vex him.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** But the stranger that dwelleth with you shall be unto you as one born among you, and thou shalt love him as thyself; for ye were strangers in the land of Egypt: I am the Lord your God.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Ye shall do no unrighteousness in judgment, in meteyard, in weight, or in measure.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Just balances, just weights, a just ephah, and a just hin, shall ye have: I am the Lord your God, which brought you out of the land of Egypt.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Therefore shall ye observe all my statutes, and all my judgments, and do them: I am the Lord.
