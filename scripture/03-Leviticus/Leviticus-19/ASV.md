# Leviticus 19 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Jehovah spake unto Moses, saying,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Speak unto all the congregation of the children of Israel, and say unto them, Ye shall be holy; for I Jehovah your God am holy.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Ye shall fear every man his mother, and his father; and ye shall keep my sabbaths: I am Jehovah your God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Turn ye not unto idols, nor make to yourselves molten gods: I am Jehovah your God.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And when ye offer a sacrifice of peace-offerings unto Jehovah, ye shall offer it that ye may be accepted.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** It shall be eaten the same day ye offer it, and on the morrow: and if aught remain until the third day, it shall be burnt with fire.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And if it be eaten at all on the third day, it is an abomination; it shall not be accepted:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** but every one that eateth it shall bear his iniquity, because he hath profaned the holy thing of Jehovah: and that soul shall be cut off from his people.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And when ye reap the harvest of your land, thou shalt not wholly reap the corners of thy field, neither shalt thou gather the gleaning of thy harvest.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And thou shalt not glean thy vineyard, neither shalt thou gather the fallen fruit of thy vineyard; thou shalt leave them for the poor and for the sojourner: I am Jehovah your God.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Ye shall not steal; neither shall ye deal falsely, nor lie one to another.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And ye shall not swear by my name falsely, and profane the name of thy God: I am Jehovah.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thou shalt not oppress thy neighbor, nor rob him: the wages of a hired servant shall not abide with thee all night until the morning.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Thou shalt not curse the deaf, nor put a stumblingblock before the blind; but thou shalt fear thy God: I am Jehovah.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Ye shall do no unrighteousness in judgment: thou shalt not respect the person of the poor, nor honor the person of the mighty; but in righteousness shalt thou judge thy neighbor.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Thou shalt not go up and down as a talebearer among thy people: neither shalt thou stand against the blood of thy neighbor: I am Jehovah.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Thou shalt not hate thy brother in thy heart: thou shalt surely rebuke thy neighbor, and not bear sin because of him.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Thou shalt not take vengeance, nor bear any grudge against the children of thy people; but thou shalt love thy neighbor as thyself: I am Jehovah.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Ye shall keep my statutes. Thou shalt not let thy cattle gender with a diverse kind: thou shalt not sow thy field with two kinds of seed: neither shall there come upon thee a garment of two kinds of stuff mingled together.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And whosoever lieth carnally with a woman, that is a bondmaid, betrothed to a husband, and not at all redeemed, nor freedom given her; they shall be punished; they shall not be put to death, because she was not free.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And he shall bring his trespass-offering unto Jehovah, unto the door of the tent of meeting, even a ram for a trespass-offering.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And the priest shall make atonement for him with the ram of the trespass-offering before Jehovah for his sin which he hath sinned: and the sin which he hath sinned shall be forgiven him.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And when ye shall come into the land, and shall have planted all manner of trees for food, then ye shall count the fruit thereof as their uncircumcision: three years shall they be as uncircumcised unto you; it shall not be eaten.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** But in the fourth year all the fruit thereof shall be holy, for giving praise unto Jehovah.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And in the fifth year shall ye eat of the fruit thereof, that it may yield unto you the increase thereof: I am Jehovah your God.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Ye shall not eat anything with the blood: neither shall ye use enchantments, nor practise augury.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Ye shall not round the corners of your heads, neither shalt thou mar the corners of thy beard.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Ye shall not make any cuttings in your flesh for the dead, nor print any marks upon you: I am Jehovah.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Profane not thy daughter, to make her a harlot; lest the land fall to whoredom, and the land become full of wickedness.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Ye shall keep my sabbaths, and reverence my sanctuary: I am Jehovah.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Turn ye not unto them that have familiar spirits, nor unto the wizards; seek them not out, to be defiled by them: I am Jehovah your God.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Thou shalt rise up before the hoary head, and honor the face of the old man, and thou shalt fear thy God: I am Jehovah.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** And if a stranger sojourn with thee in your land, ye shall not do him wrong.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** The stranger that sojourneth with you shall be unto you as the home-born among you, and thou shalt love him as thyself; for ye were sojourners in the land of Egypt: I am Jehovah your God.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Ye shall do no unrighteousness in judgment, in measures of length, of weight, or of quantity.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Just balances, just weights, a just ephah, and a just hin, shall ye have: I am Jehovah your God, who brought you out of the land of Egypt.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** And ye shall observe all my statutes, and all mine ordinances, and do them: I am Jehovah.
