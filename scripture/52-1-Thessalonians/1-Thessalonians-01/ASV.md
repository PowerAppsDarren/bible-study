# 1 Thessalonians 1 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Paul, and Silvanus, and Timothy, unto the church of the Thessalonians in God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ: Grace to you and peace.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** We give thanks to God always for you all, making mentionof youin our prayers;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** remembering without ceasing your work of faith and labor of love and patience of hope in our Lord Jesus Christ, before our God and Father;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** knowing, brethren beloved of God, your election,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** how that our gospel came not unto you in word only, but also in power, and in the Holy Spirit, and in much assurance; even as ye know what manner of men we showed ourselves toward you for your sake.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And ye became imitators of us, and of the Lord, having received the word in much affliction, with joy of the Holy Spirit;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** so that ye became an ensample to all that believe in Macedonia and in Achaia.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For from you hath sounded forth the word of the Lord, not only in Macedonia and Achaia, but in every place your faith to God-ward is gone forth; so that we need not to speak anything.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For they themselves report concerning us what manner of entering in we had unto you; and how ye turned unto God from idols, to serve a living and true God,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** and to wait for his Son from heaven, whom he raised from the dead, even Jesus, who delivereth us from the wrath to come.
