# 1 Thessalonians 5 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** But concerning the times and the seasons, brethren, ye have no need that aught be written unto you.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For yourselves know perfectly that the day of the Lord so cometh as a thief in the night.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When they are saying, Peace and safety, then sudden destruction cometh upon them, as travail upon a woman with child; and they shall in no wise escape.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But ye, brethren, are not in darkness, that that day should overtake you as a thief:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** for ye are all sons of light, and sons of the day: we are not of the night, nor of darkness;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** so then let us not sleep, as do the rest, but let us watch and be sober.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For they that sleep sleep in the night; and they that are drunken are drunken in the night.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But let us, since we are of the day, be sober, putting on the breastplate of faith and love; and for a helmet, the hope of salvation.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For God appointed us not unto wrath, but unto the obtaining of salvation through our Lord Jesus Christ,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** who died for us, that, whether we wake or sleep, we should live together with him.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Wherefore exhort one another, and build each other up, even as also ye do.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But we beseech you, brethren, to know them that labor among you, and are over you in the Lord, and admonish you;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** and to esteem them exceeding highly in love for their work’s sake. Be at peace among yourselves.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And we exhort you, brethren, admonish the disorderly, encourage the fainthearted, support the weak, be longsuffering toward all.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** See that none render unto any one evil for evil; but always follow after that which is good, one toward another, and toward all.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Rejoice always;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** pray without ceasing;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** in everything give thanks: for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus to you-ward.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Quench not the Spirit;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** despise not prophesyings;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** prove all things; hold fast that which is good;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** abstain from every form of evil.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And the God of peace himself sanctify you wholly; and may your spirit and soul and body be preserved entire, without blame at the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Faithful is he that calleth you, who will also do it.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Brethren, pray for us.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Salute all the brethren with a holy kiss.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** I adjure you by the Lord that this epistle be read unto all the brethren.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you.
