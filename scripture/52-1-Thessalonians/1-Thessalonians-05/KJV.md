# 1 Thessalonians 5 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** But of the times and the seasons, brethren, ye have no need that I write unto you.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For yourselves know perfectly that the day of the Lord so cometh as a thief in the night.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For when they shall say, Peace and safety; then sudden destruction cometh upon them, as travail upon a woman with child; and they shall not escape.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But ye, brethren, are not in darkness, that that day should overtake you as a thief.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Ye are all the children of light, and the children of the day: we are not of the night, nor of darkness.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Therefore let us not sleep, as do others; but let us watch and be sober.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For they that sleep sleep in the night; and they that be drunken are drunken in the night.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But let us, who are of the day, be sober, putting on the breastplate of faith and love; and for an helmet, the hope of salvation.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For God hath not appointed us to wrath, but to obtain salvation by our Lord Jesus Christ,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Who died for us, that, whether we wake or sleep, we should live together with him.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Wherefore comfort yourselves together, and edify one another, even as also ye do.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And we beseech you, brethren, to know them which labour among you, and are over you in the Lord, and admonish you;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And to esteem them very highly in love for their work’s sake. And be at peace among yourselves.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Now we exhort you, brethren, warn them that are unruly, comfort the feebleminded, support the weak, be patient toward all men.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** See that none render evil for evil unto any man; but ever follow that which is good, both among yourselves, and to all men.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Rejoice evermore.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Pray without ceasing.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** In every thing give thanks: for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus concerning you.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Quench not the Spirit.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Despise not prophesyings.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Prove all things; hold fast that which is good.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Abstain from all appearance of evil.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And the very God of peace sanctify you wholly; and I pray God your whole spirit and soul and body be preserved blameless unto the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Faithful is he that calleth you, who also will do it.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Brethren, pray for us.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Greet all the brethren with an holy kiss.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** I charge you by the Lord that this epistle be read unto all the holy brethren.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you. Amen.  The first epistle unto the Thessalonians was written from Athens.
