# 1 Thessalonians 5 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** But concerning the times and the seasons, brothers, you have no need that anything be written to you.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For you yourselves know well that the day of the Lord comes like a thief in the night.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For when they are saying, “Peace and safety,” then sudden destruction will come on them, like birth pains on a pregnant woman; and they will in no way escape.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But you, brothers, aren’t in darkness, that the day should overtake you like a thief.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You are all children of light, and children of the day. We don’t belong to the night, nor to darkness,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** so then let’s not sleep, as the rest do, but let’s watch and be sober.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For those who sleep, sleep in the night, and those who are drunk are drunk in the night.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But let us, since we belong to the day, be sober, putting on the breastplate of faith and love, and, for a helmet, the hope of salvation.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For God didn’t appoint us to wrath, but to the obtaining of salvation through our Lord Jesus Christ,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** who died for us, that, whether we wake or sleep, we should live together with him.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Therefore exhort one another, and build each other up, even as you also do.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But we beg you, brothers, to know those who labor among you, and are over you in the Lord, and admonish you,

<a id="v13"></a>**13** and to respect and honor them in love for their work’s sake.   Be at peace among yourselves.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** We exhort you, brothers, admonish the disorderly, encourage the faint-hearted, support the weak, be patient toward all.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** See that no one returns evil for evil to anyone, but always follow after that which is good, for one another, and for all.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Rejoice always.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Pray without ceasing.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** In everything give thanks, for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus toward you.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Don’t quench the Spirit.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Don’t despise prophesies.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Test all things, and hold firmly that which is good.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Abstain from every form of evil.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** May the God of peace himself sanctify you completely. May your whole spirit, soul, and body be preserved blameless at the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** He who calls you is faithful, who will also do it.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Brothers, pray for us.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Greet all the brothers with a holy kiss.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** I solemnly command you by the Lord that this letter be read to all the holy brothers.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you. Amen.
