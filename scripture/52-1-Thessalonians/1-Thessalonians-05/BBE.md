# 1 Thessalonians 5 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** But about the times and their order, my brothers, there is no need for me to say anything to you.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For you yourselves have the knowledge that the day of the Lord will come like a thief in the night.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When they say, There is peace and no danger, then sudden destruction will come on them, as birth-pains on a woman with child; and they will not be able to get away from it.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But you, my brothers, are not in the dark, for that day to overtake you like a thief:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For you are all sons of light and of the day: we are not of the night or of the dark.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** So then, let us not take our rest as the others do, but let us be self-controlled and awake.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For those who are sleeping do so in the night; and those who are the worse for drink are so in the night;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But let us, who are of the day, be serious, putting on the breastplate of faith and love, and on our heads, the hope of salvation.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For God's purpose for us is not wrath, but salvation through our Lord Jesus Christ,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Who was put to death for us, so that, awake or sleeping, we may have a part in his life.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** So then, go on comforting and building up one another, as you have been doing.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But we make this request to you, my brothers: give attention to those who are working among you, who are over you in the Lord to keep order among you;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And have a high opinion of them in love because of their work. Be at peace among yourselves.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And our desire is that you will keep control over those whose lives are not well ordered, giving comfort to the feeble-hearted, supporting those with little strength, and putting up with much from all.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let no one give evil for evil; but ever go after what is good, for one another and for all.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Have joy at all times.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Keep on with your prayers.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** In everything give praise: for this is the purpose of God in Christ Jesus for you.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Do not put out the light of the Spirit;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Do not make little of the words of the prophets;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Let all things be tested; keep to what is good;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Keep from every form of evil.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And may the God of peace himself make you holy in every way; and may your spirit and soul and body be free from all sin at the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** God, by whom you have been marked out in his purpose, is unchanging and will make it complete.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Brothers, keep us in mind in your prayers.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Give all the brothers a holy kiss.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** I give orders in the name of the Lord that all the brothers are to be present at the reading of this letter.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you.
