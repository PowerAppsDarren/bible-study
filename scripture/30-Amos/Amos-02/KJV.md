# Amos 2 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Thus saith the Lord; For three transgressions of Moab, and for four, I will not turn away the punishment thereof; because he burned the bones of the king of Edom into lime:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** But I will send a fire upon Moab, and it shall devour the palaces of Kerioth: and Moab shall die with tumult, with shouting, and with the sound of the trumpet:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And I will cut off the judge from the midst thereof, and will slay all the princes thereof with him, saith the Lord.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thus saith the Lord; For three transgressions of Judah, and for four, I will not turn away the punishment thereof; because they have despised the law of the Lord, and have not kept his commandments, and their lies caused them to err, after the which their fathers have walked:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But I will send a fire upon Judah, and it shall devour the palaces of Jerusalem.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Thus saith the Lord; For three transgressions of Israel, and for four, I will not turn away the punishment thereof; because they sold the righteous for silver, and the poor for a pair of shoes;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** That pant after the dust of the earth on the head of the poor, and turn aside the way of the meek: and a man and his father will go in unto the same maid, to profane my holy name:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And they lay themselves down upon clothes laid to pledge by every altar, and they drink the wine of the condemned in the house of their god.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Yet destroyed I the Amorite before them, whose height was like the height of the cedars, and he was strong as the oaks; yet I destroyed his fruit from above, and his roots from beneath.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Also I brought you up from the land of Egypt, and led you forty years through the wilderness, to possess the land of the Amorite.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And I raised up of your sons for prophets, and of your young men for Nazarites. Is it not even thus, O ye children of Israel? saith the Lord.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But ye gave the Nazarites wine to drink; and commanded the prophets, saying, Prophesy not.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Behold, I am pressed under you, as a cart is pressed that is full of sheaves.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Therefore the flight shall perish from the swift, and the strong shall not strengthen his force, neither shall the mighty deliver himself:

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Neither shall he stand that handleth the bow; and he that is swift of foot shall not deliver himself: neither shall he that rideth the horse deliver himself.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And he that is courageous among the mighty shall flee away naked in that day, saith the Lord.
