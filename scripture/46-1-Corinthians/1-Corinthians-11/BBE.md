# 1 Corinthians 11 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** So take me for your example, even as I take Christ for mine.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Now I am pleased to see that you keep me in memory in all things, and that you give attention to the teaching which was handed down from me to you.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But it is important for you to keep this fact in mind, that the head of every man is Christ; and the head of the woman is the man, and the head of Christ is God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Every man who takes part in prayer, or gives teaching as a prophet, with his head covered, puts shame on his head.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But every woman who does so with her head unveiled, puts shame on her head: for it is the same as if her hair was cut off.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For if a woman is not veiled, let her hair be cut off; but if it is a shame to a woman to have her hair cut off, let her be veiled.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For it is not right for a man to have his head covered, because he is the image and glory of God: but the woman is the glory of the man.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For the man did not come from the woman, but the woman from the man.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And the man was not made for the woman, but the woman for the man.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For this reason it is right for the woman to have a sign of authority on her head, because of the angels.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But the woman is not separate from the man, and the man is not separate from the woman in the Lord.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For as the woman is from the man, so the man is through the woman; but all things are from God.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Be judges yourselves of the question: does it seem right for a woman to take part in prayer unveiled?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Does it not seem natural to you that if a man has long hair, it is a cause of shame to him?

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But if a woman has long hair, it is a glory to her: for her hair is given to her for a covering.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** But if any man will not be ruled in this question, this is not our way of doing things, and it is not done in the churches of God.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** But in giving you this order, there is one thing about which I am not pleased: it is that when you come together it is not for the better but for the worse.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For first of all, it has come to my ears that when you come together in the church, there are divisions among you, and I take the statement to be true in part.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** For divisions are necessary among you, in order that those who have God's approval may be clearly seen among you.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** But now, when you come together, it is not possible to take the holy meal of the Lord:

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For when you take your food, everyone takes his meal before the other; and one has not enough food, and another is the worse for drink.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** What? have you not houses to take your meals in? or have you no respect for the church of God, putting the poor to shame? What am I to say to you? am I to give you praise? certainly not.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** For it was handed down to me from the Lord, as I gave it to you, that the Lord Jesus, on the night when Judas was false to him, took bread,

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And when it had been broken with an act of praise, he said, This is my body which is for you: do this in memory of me.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** In the same way, with the cup, after the meal, he said, This cup is the new testament in my blood: do this, whenever you take it, in memory of me.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** For whenever you take the bread and the cup you give witness to the Lord's death till he comes.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** If, then, anyone takes the bread or the cup of the Lord in the wrong spirit, he will be responsible for the body and blood of the Lord.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** But let no man take of the bread and the cup without testing himself.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** For a man puts himself in danger, if he takes part in the holy meal without being conscious that it is the Lord's body.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** For this cause a number of you are feeble and ill, and a number are dead.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** But if we were true judges of ourselves, punishment would not come on us.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** But if punishment does come, it is sent by the Lord, so that we may be safe when the world is judged.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** So then, my brothers, when you come together to the holy meal of the Lord, let there be waiting for one another.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** If any man is in need of food, let him take his meal in his house; so that you may not come together to your damage. And the rest I will put in order when I come.
