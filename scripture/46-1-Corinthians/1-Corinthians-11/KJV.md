# 1 Corinthians 11 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Be ye followers of me, even as I also am of Christ.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Now I praise you, brethren, that ye remember me in all things, and keep the ordinances, as I delivered them to you.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But I would have you know, that the head of every man is Christ; and the head of the woman is the man; and the head of Christ is God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Every man praying or prophesying, having his head covered, dishonoureth his head.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But every woman that prayeth or prophesieth with her head uncovered dishonoureth her head: for that is even all one as if she were shaven.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For if the woman be not covered, let her also be shorn: but if it be a shame for a woman to be shorn or shaven, let her be covered.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For a man indeed ought not to cover his head, forasmuch as he is the image and glory of God: but the woman is the glory of the man.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For the man is not of the woman; but the woman of the man.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Neither was the man created for the woman; but the woman for the man.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For this cause ought the woman to have power on her head because of the angels.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Nevertheless neither is the man without the woman, neither the woman without the man, in the Lord.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For as the woman is of the man, even so is the man also by the woman; but all things of God.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Judge in yourselves: is it comely that a woman pray unto God uncovered?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Doth not even nature itself teach you, that, if a man have long hair, it is a shame unto him?

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But if a woman have long hair, it is a glory to her: for her hair is given her for a covering.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** But if any man seem to be contentious, we have no such custom, neither the churches of God.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Now in this that I declare unto you I praise you not, that ye come together not for the better, but for the worse.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For first of all, when ye come together in the church, I hear that there be divisions among you; and I partly believe it.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** For there must be also heresies among you, that they which are approved may be made manifest among you.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** When ye come together therefore into one place, this is not to eat the Lord’s supper.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For in eating every one taketh before other his own supper: and one is hungry, and another is drunken.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** What? have ye not houses to eat and to drink in? or despise ye the church of God, and shame them that have not? What shall I say to you? shall I praise you in this? I praise you not.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** For I have received of the Lord that which also I delivered unto you, That the Lord Jesus the same night in which he was betrayed took bread:

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And when he had given thanks, he brake it, and said, Take, eat: this is my body, which is broken for you: this do in remembrance of me.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** After the same manner also he took the cup, when he had supped, saying, This cup is the new testament in my blood: this do ye, as oft as ye drink it, in remembrance of me.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** For as often as ye eat this bread, and drink this cup, ye do shew the Lord’s death till he come.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Wherefore whosoever shall eat this bread, and drink this cup of the Lord, unworthily, shall be guilty of the body and blood of the Lord.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** But let a man examine himself, and so let him eat of that bread, and drink of that cup.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** For he that eateth and drinketh unworthily, eateth and drinketh damnation to himself, not discerning the Lord’s body.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** For this cause many are weak and sickly among you, and many sleep.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For if we would judge ourselves, we should not be judged.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** But when we are judged, we are chastened of the Lord, that we should not be condemned with the world.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Wherefore, my brethren, when ye come together to eat, tarry one for another.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** And if any man hunger, let him eat at home; that ye come not together unto condemnation. And the rest will I set in order when I come.
