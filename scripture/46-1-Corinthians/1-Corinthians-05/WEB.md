# 1 Corinthians 5 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** It is actually reported that there is sexual immorality among you, and such sexual immorality as is not even named among the Gentiles, that one has his father’s wife.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You are puffed up, and didn’t rather mourn, that he who had done this deed might be removed from among you.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For I most certainly, as being absent in body but present in spirit, have already, as though I were present, judged him who has done this thing.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** In the name of our Lord Jesus Christ, you being gathered together, and my spirit, with the power of our Lord Jesus Christ,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** are to deliver such a one to Satan for the destruction of the flesh, that the spirit may be saved in the day of the Lord Jesus.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Your boasting is not good. Don’t you know that a little yeast leavens the whole lump?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Purge out the old yeast, that you may be a new lump, even as you are unleavened. For indeed Christ, our Passover, has been sacrificed in our place.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Therefore let us keep the feast, not with old yeast, neither with the yeast of malice and wickedness, but with the unleavened bread of sincerity and truth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I wrote to you in my letter to have no company with sexual sinners;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** yet not at all meaning with the sexual sinners of this world, or with the covetous and extortionists, or with idolaters; for then you would have to leave the world.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But as it is, I wrote to you not to associate with anyone who is called a brother who is a sexual sinner, or covetous, or an idolater, or a slanderer, or a drunkard, or an extortionist. Don’t even eat with such a person.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For what have I to do with also judging those who are outside? Don’t you judge those who are within?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But those who are outside, God judges. “Put away the wicked man from among yourselves.”
