# 1 Corinthians 12 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Now concerning spiritualgifts, brethren, I would not have you ignorant.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Ye know that when ye were Gentiles ye were led away unto those dumb idols, howsoever ye might be led.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Wherefore I make known unto you, that no man speaking in the Spirit of God saith, Jesus is anathema; and no man can say, Jesus is Lord, but in the Holy Spirit.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Now there are diversities of gifts, but the same Spirit.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And there are diversities of ministrations, and the same Lord.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And there are diversities of workings, but the same God, who worketh all things in all.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But to each one is given the manifestation of the Spirit to profit withal.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For to one is given through the Spirit the word of wisdom; and to another the word of knowledge, according to the same Spirit:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** to another faith, in the same Spirit; and to another gifts of healings, in the one Spirit;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** and to another workings of miracles; and to another prophecy; and to another discernings of spirits: to another divers kinds of tongues; and to another the interpretation of tongues:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** but all these worketh the one and the same Spirit, dividing to each one severally even as he will.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For as the body is one, and hath many members, and all the members of the body, being many, are one body; so also is Christ.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For in one Spirit were we all baptized into one body, whether Jews or Greeks, whether bond or free; and were all made to drink of one Spirit.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For the body is not one member, but many.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** If the foot shall say, Because I am not the hand, I am not of the body; it is not therefore not of the body.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And if the ear shall say, Because I am not the eye, I am not of the body; it is not therefore not of the body.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** If the whole body were an eye, where were the hearing? If the whole were hearing, where were the smelling?

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But now hath God set the members each one of them in the body, even as it pleased him.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And if they were all one member, where were the body?

<a id="v20"></a>**20** But now they are many members, but one body.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And the eye cannot say to the hand, I have no need of thee: or again the head to the feet, I have no need of you.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Nay, much rather, those members of the body which seem to be more feeble are necessary:

<a id="v23"></a>**23** and those parts of the body, which we think to be less honorable, upon these we bestow more abundant honor; and our uncomely parts have more abundant comeliness;

<a id="v24"></a>**24** whereas our comely parts have no need: but God tempered the body together, giving more abundant honor to that part which lacked;

<a id="v25"></a>**25** that there should be no schism in the body; but that the members should have the same care one for another.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And whether one member suffereth, all the members suffer with it; or one member is honored, all the members rejoice with it.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Now ye are the body of Christ, and severally members thereof.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** And God hath set some in the church, first apostles, secondly prophets, thirdly teachers, then miracles, then gifts of healings, helps, governments, divers kinds of tongues.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Are all apostles? are all prophets? are all teachers? are all workers of miracles?

<a id="v30"></a>**30** have all gifts of healings? do all speak with tongues? do all interpret?

<a id="v31"></a>**31** But desire earnestly the greater gifts. And moreover a most excellent way show I unto you.
