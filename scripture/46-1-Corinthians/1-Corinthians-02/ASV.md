# 1 Corinthians 2 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And I, brethren, when I came unto you, came not with excellency of speech or of wisdom, proclaiming to you the testimony of God.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For I determined not to know anything among you, save Jesus Christ, and him crucified.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And I was with you in weakness, and in fear, and in much trembling.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And my speech and my preaching were not in persuasive words of wisdom, but in demonstration of the Spirit and of power:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** that your faith should not stand in the wisdom of men, but in the power of God.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** We speak wisdom, however, among them that are fullgrown: yet a wisdom not of this world, nor of the rulers of this world, who are coming to nought:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** but we speak God’s wisdom in a mystery, even the wisdom that hath been hidden, which God foreordained before the worlds unto our glory:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** which none of the rulers of this world hath known: for had they known it, they would not have crucified the Lord of glory:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** but as it is written,  Things which eye saw not, and ear heard not, Andwhichentered not into the heart of man, Whatsoever things God prepared for them that love him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But unto us God revealed them through the Spirit: for the Spirit searcheth all things, yea, the deep things of God.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For who among men knoweth the things of a man, save the spirit of the man, which is in him? even so the things of God none knoweth, save the Spirit of God.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But we received, not the spirit of the world, but the spirit which is from God; that we might know the things that were freely given to us of God.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Which things also we speak, not in words which man’s wisdom teacheth, but which the Spirit teacheth; combining spiritual things with spiritual words.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Now the natural man receiveth not the things of the Spirit of God: for they are foolishness unto him; and he cannot know them, because they are spiritually judged.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But he that is spiritual judgeth all things, and he himself is judged of no man.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For who hath known the mind of the Lord, that he should instruct him? But we have the mind of Christ.
