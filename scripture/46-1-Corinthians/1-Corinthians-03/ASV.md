# 1 Corinthians 3 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And I, brethren, could not speak unto you as unto spiritual, but as unto carnal, as unto babes in Christ.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I fed you with milk, not with meat; for ye were not yet able to bear it: nay, not even now are ye able;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** for ye are yet carnal: for whereas there is among you jealousy and strife, are ye not carnal, and do ye not walk after the manner of men?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For when one saith, I am of Paul; and another, I am of Apollos; are ye not men?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** What then is Apollos? and what is Paul? Ministers through whom ye believed; and each as the Lord gave to him.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I planted, Apollos watered; but God gave the increase.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** So then neither is he that planteth anything, neither he that watereth; but God that giveth the increase.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Now he that planteth and he that watereth are one: but each shall receive his own reward according to his own labor.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For we are God’s fellow-workers: ye are God’s husbandry, God’s building.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** According to the grace of God which was given unto me, as a wise masterbuilder I laid a foundation; and another buildeth thereon. But let each man take heed how he buildeth thereon.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For other foundation can no man lay than that which is laid, which is Jesus Christ.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But if any man buildeth on the foundation gold, silver, costly stones, wood, hay, stubble;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** each man’s work shall be made manifest: for the day shall declare it, because it is revealed in fire; and the fire itself shall prove each man’s work of what sort it is.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** If any man’s work shall abide which he built thereon, he shall receive a reward.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** If any man’s work shall be burned, he shall suffer loss: but he himself shall be saved; yet so as through fire.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Know ye not that ye are a temple of God, and that the Spirit of God dwelleth in you?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** If any man destroyeth the temple of God, him shall God destroy; for the temple of God is holy, and such are ye.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Let no man deceive himself. If any man thinketh that he is wise among you in this world, let him become a fool, that he may become wise.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** For the wisdom of this world is foolishness with God. For it is written, He that taketh the wise in their craftiness:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** and again, The Lord knoweth the reasonings of the wise, that they are vain.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Wherefore let no one glory in men. For all things are yours;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** whether Paul, or Apollos, or Cephas, or the world, or life, or death, or things present, or things to come; all are yours;

<a id="v23"></a>**23** and ye are Christ’s; and Christ is God’s.
