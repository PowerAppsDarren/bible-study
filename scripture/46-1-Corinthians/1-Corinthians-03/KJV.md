# 1 Corinthians 3 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And I, brethren, could not speak unto you as unto spiritual, but as unto carnal, even as unto babes in Christ.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I have fed you with milk, and not with meat: for hitherto ye were not able to bear it, neither yet now are ye able.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For ye are yet carnal: for whereas there is among you envying, and strife, and divisions, are ye not carnal, and walk as men?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For while one saith, I am of Paul; and another, I am of Apollos; are ye not carnal?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Who then is Paul, and who is Apollos, but ministers by whom ye believed, even as the Lord gave to every man?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I have planted, Apollos watered; but God gave the increase.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** So then neither is he that planteth any thing, neither he that watereth; but God that giveth the increase.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Now he that planteth and he that watereth are one: and every man shall receive his own reward according to his own labour.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For we are labourers together with God: ye are God’s husbandry, ye are God’s building.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** According to the grace of God which is given unto me, as a wise masterbuilder, I have laid the foundation, and another buildeth thereon. But let every man take heed how he buildeth thereupon.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For other foundation can no man lay than that is laid, which is Jesus Christ.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Now if any man build upon this foundation gold, silver, precious stones, wood, hay, stubble;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Every man’s work shall be made manifest: for the day shall declare it, because it shall be revealed by fire; and the fire shall try every man’s work of what sort it is.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** If any man’s work abide which he hath built thereupon, he shall receive a reward.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** If any man’s work shall be burned, he shall suffer loss: but he himself shall be saved; yet so as by fire.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Know ye not that ye are the temple of God, and that the Spirit of God dwelleth in you?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** If any man defile the temple of God, him shall God destroy; for the temple of God is holy, which temple ye are.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Let no man deceive himself. If any man among you seemeth to be wise in this world, let him become a fool, that he may be wise.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** For the wisdom of this world is foolishness with God. For it is written, He taketh the wise in their own craftiness.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And again, The Lord knoweth the thoughts of the wise, that they are vain.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Therefore let no man glory in men. For all things are yours;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Whether Paul, or Apollos, or Cephas, or the world, or life, or death, or things present, or things to come; all are yours;

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And ye are Christ’s; and Christ is God’s.
