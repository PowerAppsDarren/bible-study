# 1 Corinthians 16 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Now concerning the collection for the saints, as I gave order to the churches of Galatia, so also do ye.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Upon the first day of the week let each one of you lay by him in store, as he may prosper, that no collections be made when I come.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And when I arrive, whomsoever ye shall approve, them will I send with letters to carry your bounty unto Jerusalem:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** and if it be meet for me to go also, they shall go with me.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But I will come unto you, when I shall have passed through Macedonia; for I pass through Macedonia;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** but with you it may be that I shall abide, or even winter, that ye may set me forward on my journey whithersoever I go.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For I do not wish to see you now by the way; for I hope to tarry a while with you, if the Lord permit.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But I will tarry at Ephesus until Pentecost;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** for a great door and effectual is opened unto me, and there are many adversaries.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Now if Timothy come, see that he be with you without fear; for he worketh the work of the Lord, as I also do:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** let no man therefore despise him. But set him forward on his journey in peace, that he may come unto me: for I expect him with the brethren.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But as touching Apollos the brother, I besought him much to come unto you with the brethren: and it was not at all his will to come now; but he will come when he shall have opportunity.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Watch ye, stand fast in the faith, quit you like men, be strong.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Let all that ye do be done in love.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Now I beseech you, brethren (ye know the house of Stephanas, that it is the firstfruits of Achaia, and that they have set themselves to minister unto the saints),

<a id="v16"></a>**16** that ye also be in subjection unto such, and to every one that helpeth in the work and laboreth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And I rejoice at the coming of Stephanas and Fortunatus and Achaicus: for that which was lacking on your part they supplied.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For they refreshed my spirit and yours: acknowledge ye therefore them that are such.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The churches of Asia salute you. Aquila and Prisca salute you much in the Lord, with the church that is in their house.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** All the brethren salute you. Salute one another with a holy kiss.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The salutation of me Paul with mine own hand.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** If any man loveth not the Lord, let him be anathema. Maranatha.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** The grace of the Lord Jesus Christ be with you.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** My love be with you all in Christ Jesus. Amen.
