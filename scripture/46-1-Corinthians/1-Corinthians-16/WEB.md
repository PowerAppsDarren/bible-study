# 1 Corinthians 16 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Now concerning the collection for the saints, as I commanded the assemblies of Galatia, you do likewise.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** On the first day of the week, let each one of you save, as he may prosper, that no collections be made when I come.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When I arrive, I will send whoever you approve with letters to carry your gracious gift to Jerusalem.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** If it is appropriate for me to go also, they will go with me.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But I will come to you when I have passed through Macedonia, for I am passing through Macedonia.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** But with you it may be that I will stay, or even winter, that you may send me on my journey wherever I go.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For I do not wish to see you now in passing, but I hope to stay a while with you, if the Lord permits.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But I will stay at Ephesus until Pentecost,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** for a great and effective door has opened to me, and there are many adversaries.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Now if Timothy comes, see that he is with you without fear, for he does the work of the Lord, as I also do.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Therefore let no one despise him. But set him forward on his journey in peace, that he may come to me; for I expect him with the brothers.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Now concerning Apollos, the brother, I strongly urged him to come to you with the brothers; and it was not at all his desire to come now; but he will come when he has an opportunity.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Watch! Stand firm in the faith! Be courageous! Be strong!

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Let all that you do be done in love.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Now I beg you, brothers (you know the house of Stephanas, that it is the first fruits of Achaia, and that they have set themselves to serve the saints),

<a id="v16"></a>**16** that you also be in subjection to such, and to everyone who helps in the work and labors.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I rejoice at the coming of Stephanas, Fortunatus, and Achaicus; for that which was lacking on your part, they supplied.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For they refreshed my spirit and yours. Therefore acknowledge those who are like that.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The assemblies of Asia greet you. Aquila and Priscilla greet you much in the Lord, together with the assembly that is in their house.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** All the brothers greet you. Greet one another with a holy kiss.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** This greeting is by me, Paul, with my own hand.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** If any man doesn’t love the Lord Jesus Christ, let him be accursed.  Come, Lord!

<a id="v23"></a>**23** The grace of the Lord Jesus Christ be with you.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** My love to all of you in Christ Jesus. Amen.
