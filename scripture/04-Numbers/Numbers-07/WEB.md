<a id="v1"></a>**1** On the day that Moses had finished setting up the tabernacle, and had anointed it and sanctified it, with all its furniture, and the altar with all its vessels, and had anointed and sanctified them;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** the princes of Israel, the heads of their fathers’ houses, offered. These were the princes of the tribes. These are they who were over those who were numbered:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** and they brought their offering before Yahweh, six covered wagons, and twelve oxen; a wagon for every two of the princes, and for each one an ox: and they presented them before the tabernacle.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** “Accept these from them, that they may be used in doing the service of the Tent of Meeting; and you shall give them to the Levites, to every man according to his service.”

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Moses took the wagons and the oxen, and gave them to the Levites.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He gave two wagons and four oxen to the sons of Gershon, according to their service:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** and he gave four wagons and eight oxen to the sons of Merari, according to their service, under the direction of Ithamar the son of Aaron the priest.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But to the sons of Kohath he gave none, because the service of the sanctuary belonged to them; they carried it on their shoulders.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The princes gave offerings for the dedication of the altar in the day that it was anointed, even the princes gave their offerings before the altar.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Yahweh said to Moses, “They shall offer their offering, each prince on his day, for the dedication of the altar.”

<a id="v12"></a>**12** He who offered his offering the first day was Nahshon the son of Amminadab, of the tribe of Judah,

<a id="v13"></a>**13** and his offering was:   one silver platter, the weight of which was one hundred thirty shekels,   one silver bowl of seventy shekels, after the shekel of the sanctuary; both of them full of fine flour mixed with oil for a meal offering;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** one golden ladle of ten shekels, full of incense;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** one young bull,   one ram,   one male lamb a year old, for a burnt offering;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** one male goat for a sin offering;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** and for the sacrifice of peace offerings, two head of cattle, five rams, five male goats, and five male lambs a year old. This was the offering of Nahshon the son of Amminadab.

<a id="v84"></a>**84** This was the dedication of the altar, on the day when it was anointed, by the princes of Israel: twelve silver platters, twelve silver bowls, twelve golden ladles;

<a id="v85"></a>**85** each silver platter weighing one hundred thirty shekels, and each bowl seventy; all the silver of the vessels two thousand four hundred shekels, after the shekel of the sanctuary;
