# Numbers 29 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And in the seventh month, on the first day of the month, ye shall have a holy convocation; ye shall do no servile work: it is a day of blowing of trumpets unto you.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And ye shall offer a burnt-offering for a sweet savor unto Jehovah: one young bullock, one ram, seven he-lambs a year old without blemish;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** and their meal-offering, fine flour mingled with oil, three tenth parts for the bullock, two tenth parts for the ram,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** and one tenth part for every lamb of the seven lambs;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** and one he-goat for a sin-offering, to make atonement for you;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** besides the burnt-offering of the new moon, and the meal-offering thereof, and the continual burnt-offering and the meal-offering thereof, and their drink-offerings, according unto their ordinance, for a sweet savor, an offering made by fire unto Jehovah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And on the tenth day of this seventh month ye shall have a holy convocation; and ye shall afflict your souls: ye shall do no manner of work;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** but ye shall offer a burnt-offering unto Jehovah for a sweet savor: one young bullock, one ram, seven he-lambs a year old; they shall be unto you without blemish;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** and their meal-offering, fine flour mingled with oil, three tenth parts for the bullock, two tenth parts for the one ram,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** a tenth part for every lamb of the seven lambs:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** one he-goat for a sin-offering; besides the sin-offering of atonement, and the continual burnt-offering, and the meal-offering thereof, and their drink-offerings.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And on the fifteenth day of the seventh month ye shall have a holy convocation; ye shall do no servile work, and ye shall keep a feast unto Jehovah seven days:

<a id="v13"></a>**13** and ye shall offer a burnt-offering, an offering made by fire, of a sweet savor unto Jehovah; thirteen young bullocks, two rams, fourteen he-lambs a year old; they shall be without blemish;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** and their meal-offering, fine flour mingled with oil, three tenth parts for every bullock of the thirteen bullocks, two tenth parts for each ram of the two rams,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** and a tenth part for every lamb of the fourteen lambs;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** and one he-goat for a sin-offering; besides the continual burnt-offering, the meal-offering thereof, and the drink-offering thereof.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And on the second dayye shall offertwelve young bullocks, two rams, fourteen he-lambs a year old without blemish;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** and their meal-offering and their drink-offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, according to their number, after the ordinance;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** and one he-goat for a sin-offering; besides the continual burnt-offering, and the meal-offering thereof, and their drink-offerings.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And on the third day eleven bullocks, two rams, fourteen he-lambs a year old without blemish;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** and their meal-offering and their drink-offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, according to their number, after the ordinance;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** and one he-goat for a sin-offering; besides the continual burnt-offering, and the meal-offering thereof, and the drink-offering thereof.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And on the fourth day ten bullocks, two rams, fourteen he-lambs a year old without blemish;

<a id="v24"></a>**24** their meal-offering and their drink-offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, according to their number, after the ordinance;

<a id="v25"></a>**25** and one he-goat for a sin-offering; besides the continual burnt-offering, the meal-offering thereof, and the drink-offering thereof.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And on the fifth day nine bullocks, two rams, fourteen he-lambs a year old without blemish;

<a id="v27"></a>**27** and their meal-offering and their drink-offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, according to their number, after the ordinance;

<a id="v28"></a>**28** and one he-goat for a sin-offering; besides the continual burnt-offering, and the meal-offering thereof, and the drink-offering thereof.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And on the sixth day eight bullocks, two rams, fourteen he-lambs a year old without blemish;

<a id="v30"></a>**30** and their meal-offering and their drink-offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, according to their number, after the ordinance;

<a id="v31"></a>**31** and one he-goat for a sin-offering; besides the continual burnt-offering, the meal-offering thereof, and the drink-offerings thereof.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And on the seventh day seven bullocks, two rams, fourteen he-lambs a year old without blemish;

<a id="v33"></a>**33** and their meal-offering and their drink-offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, according to their number, after the ordinance;

<a id="v34"></a>**34** and one he-goat for a sin-offering; besides the continual burnt-offering, the meal-offering thereof, and the drink-offering thereof.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** On the eighth day ye shall have a solemn assembly: ye shall do no servile work;

<a id="v36"></a>**36** but ye shall offer a burnt-offering, an offering made by fire, of a sweet savor unto Jehovah: one bullock, one ram, seven he-lambs a year old without blemish;

<a id="v37"></a>**37** their meal-offering and their drink-offerings for the bullock, for the ram, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the ordinance:

<a id="v38"></a>**38** and one he-goat for a sin-offering; besides the continual burnt-offering, and the meal-offering thereof, and the drink-offering thereof.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** These ye shall offer unto Jehovah in your set feasts, besides your vows, and your freewill-offerings, for your burnt-offerings, and for your meal-offerings, and for your drink-offerings, and for your peace-offerings.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** And Moses told the children of Israel according to all that Jehovah commanded Moses.
