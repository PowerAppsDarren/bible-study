# Numbers 28 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** “Command the children of Israel, and tell them, ‘See that you present my offering, my food for my offerings made by fire, of a pleasant aroma to me, in their due season.’

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You shall tell them, ‘This is the offering made by fire which you shall offer to Yahweh: male lambs a year old without defect, two day by day, for a continual burnt offering.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You shall offer the one lamb in the morning, and you shall offer the other lamb at evening;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** with the tenth part of an ephah of fine flour for a meal offering, mixed with the fourth part of a hin of beaten oil.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** It is a continual burnt offering, which was ordained in Mount Sinai for a pleasant aroma, an offering made by fire to Yahweh.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Its drink offering shall be the fourth part of a hin for the one lamb. You shall pour out a drink offering of strong drink to Yahweh in the holy place.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The other lamb you shall offer at evening. As the meal offering of the morning, and as its drink offering, you shall offer it, an offering made by fire, of a pleasant aroma to Yahweh.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** “‘On the Sabbath day, two male lambs a year old without defect, and two tenth parts of an ephah of fine flour for a meal offering, mixed with oil, and its drink offering:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** this is the burnt offering of every Sabbath, besides the continual burnt offering, and its drink offering.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** “‘In the beginnings of your months, you shall offer a burnt offering to Yahweh: two young bulls, and one ram, seven male lambs a year old without defect;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** and three tenth parts of an ephah of fine flour for a meal offering, mixed with oil, for each bull; and two tenth parts of fine flour for a meal offering, mixed with oil, for the one ram;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** and a tenth part of fine flour mixed with oil for a meal offering to every lamb; for a burnt offering of a pleasant aroma, an offering made by fire to Yahweh.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Their drink offerings shall be half a hin of wine for a bull, and the third part of a hin for the ram, and the fourth part of a hin for a lamb. This is the burnt offering of every month throughout the months of the year.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** One male goat for a sin offering to Yahweh; it shall be offered besides the continual burnt offering, and its drink offering.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** “‘In the first month, on the fourteenth day of the month, is Yahweh’s Passover.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** On the fifteenth day of this month shall be a feast. Unleavened bread shall be eaten be eaten for seven days.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** In the first day shall be a holy convocation. You shall do no regular work;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** but you shall offer an offering made by fire, a burnt offering to Yahweh: two young bulls, and one ram, and seven male lambs a year old; they shall be to you without defect;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** and their meal offering, fine flour mixed with oil. You shall offer three tenth parts for a bull, and two tenth parts for the ram.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** You shall offer a tenth part for every lamb of the seven lambs;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** and one male goat for a sin offering, to make atonement for you.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** You shall offer these besides the burnt offering of the morning, which is for a continual burnt offering.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** In this way you shall offer daily, for seven days, the food of the offering made by fire, of a pleasant aroma to Yahweh. It shall be offered in addition to the continual burnt offering, and its drink offering.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** On the seventh day you shall have a holy convocation. You shall do no regular work.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** “‘Also in the day of the first fruits, when you offer a new meal offering to Yahweh in your feast of weeks, you shall have a holy convocation. You shall do no regular work;

<a id="v27"></a>**27** but you shall offer a burnt offering for a pleasant aroma to Yahweh: two young bulls, one ram, seven male lambs a year old;

<a id="v28"></a>**28** and their meal offering, fine flour mixed with oil, three tenth parts for each bull, two tenth parts for the one ram,

<a id="v29"></a>**29** a tenth part for every lamb of the seven lambs;

<a id="v30"></a>**30** one male goat, to make atonement for you.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Besides the continual burnt offering, and its meal offering, you shall offer them and their drink offerings. See that they are without defect.
