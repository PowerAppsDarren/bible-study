# Numbers 30 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Moses spake unto the heads of the tribes concerning the children of Israel, saying, This is the thing which the Lord hath commanded.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** If a man vow a vow unto the Lord, or swear an oath to bind his soul with a bond; he shall not break his word, he shall do according to all that proceedeth out of his mouth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** If a woman also vow a vow unto the Lord, and bind herself by a bond, being in her father’s house in her youth;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And her father hear her vow, and her bond wherewith she hath bound her soul, and her father shall hold his peace at her: then all her vows shall stand, and every bond wherewith she hath bound her soul shall stand.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But if her father disallow her in the day that he heareth; not any of her vows, or of her bonds wherewith she hath bound her soul, shall stand: and the Lord shall forgive her, because her father disallowed her.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And if she had at all an husband, when she vowed, or uttered ought out of her lips, wherewith she bound her soul;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And her husband heard it, and held his peace at her in the day that he heard it: then her vows shall stand, and her bonds wherewith she bound her soul shall stand.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But if her husband disallowed her on the day that he heard it; then he shall make her vow which she vowed, and that which she uttered with her lips, wherewith she bound her soul, of none effect: and the Lord shall forgive her.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But every vow of a widow, and of her that is divorced, wherewith they have bound their souls, shall stand against her.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And if she vowed in her husband’s house, or bound her soul by a bond with an oath;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And her husband heard it, and held his peace at her, and disallowed her not: then all her vows shall stand, and every bond wherewith she bound her soul shall stand.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But if her husband hath utterly made them void on the day he heard them; then whatsoever proceeded out of her lips concerning her vows, or concerning the bond of her soul, shall not stand: her husband hath made them void; and the Lord shall forgive her.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Every vow, and every binding oath to afflict the soul, her husband may establish it, or her husband may make it void.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But if her husband altogether hold his peace at her from day to day; then he establisheth all her vows, or all her bonds, which are upon her: he confirmeth them, because he held his peace at her in the day that he heard them.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But if he shall any ways make them void after that he hath heard them; then he shall bear her iniquity.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** These are the statutes, which the Lord commanded Moses, between a man and his wife, between the father and his daughter, being yet in her youth in her father’s house.
