# Psalms 112 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise Yah! Blessed is the man who fears Yahweh, who delights greatly in his commandments.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** His seed will be mighty in the land. The generation of the upright will be blessed.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Wealth and riches are in his house. His righteousness endures forever.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Light dawns in the darkness for the upright, gracious, merciful, and righteous.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** It is well with the man who deals graciously and lends. He will maintain his cause in judgment.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For he will never be shaken. The righteous will be remembered forever.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He will not be afraid of evil news. His heart is steadfast, trusting in Yahweh.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** His heart is established. He will not be afraid in the end when he sees his adversaries.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He has dispersed, he has given to the poor. His righteousness endures forever. His horn will be exalted with honor.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The wicked will see it, and be grieved. He shall gnash with his teeth, and melt away. The desire of the wicked will perish.
