# Psalms 112 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye Jehovah. Blessed is the man that feareth Jehovah, That delighteth greatly in his commandments.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** His seed shall be mighty upon earth: The generation of the upright shall be blessed.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Wealth and riches are in his house; And his righteousness endureth for ever.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Unto the upright there ariseth light in the darkness: He isgracious, and merciful, and righteous.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Well is it with the man that dealeth graciously and lendeth; He shall maintain his cause in judgment.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For he shall never be moved; The righteous shall be had in everlasting remembrance.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He shall not be afraid of evil tidings: His heart is fixed, trusting in Jehovah.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** His heart is established, he shall not be afraid, Until he seehis desireupon his adversaries.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He hath dispersed, he hath given to the needy; His righteousness endureth for ever: His horn shall be exalted with honor.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The wicked shall see it, and be grieved; He shall gnash with his teeth, and melt away: The desire of the wicked shall perish.
