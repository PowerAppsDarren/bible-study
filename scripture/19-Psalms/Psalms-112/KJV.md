# Psalms 112 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye the Lord. Blessed is the man that feareth the Lord, that delighteth greatly in his commandments.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** His seed shall be mighty upon earth: the generation of the upright shall be blessed.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Wealth and riches shall be in his house: and his righteousness endureth for ever.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Unto the upright there ariseth light in the darkness: he is gracious, and full of compassion, and righteous.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** A good man sheweth favour, and lendeth: he will guide his affairs with discretion.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Surely he shall not be moved for ever: the righteous shall be in everlasting remembrance.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He shall not be afraid of evil tidings: his heart is fixed, trusting in the Lord.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** His heart is established, he shall not be afraid, until he see his desire upon his enemies.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He hath dispersed, he hath given to the poor; his righteousness endureth for ever; his horn shall be exalted with honour.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The wicked shall see it, and be grieved; he shall gnash with his teeth, and melt away: the desire of the wicked shall perish.
