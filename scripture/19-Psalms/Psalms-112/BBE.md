# Psalms 112 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Let the Lord be praised. Happy is the man who gives honour to the Lord, and has great delight in his laws.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** His seed will be strong on the earth; blessings will be on the generation of the upright.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** A store of wealth will be in his house, and his righteousness will be for ever.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For the upright there is a light shining in the dark; he is full of grace and pity.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** All is well for the man who is kind and gives freely to others; he will make good his cause when he is judged.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He will not ever be moved; the memory of the upright will be living for ever.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He will have no fear of evil news; his heart is fixed, for his hope is in the Lord.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** His heart is resting safely, he will have no fear, till he sees trouble come on his haters.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He has given with open hands to the poor; his righteousness is for ever; his horn will be lifted up with honour.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The sinner will see it with grief; he will be wasted away with envy; the desire of the evil-doers will come to nothing.
