# Psalms 132 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Jehovah, remember for David All his affliction;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How he sware unto Jehovah, And vowed unto the Mighty One of Jacob:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Surely I will not come into the tabernacle of my house, Nor go up into my bed;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will not give sleep to mine eyes, Or slumber to mine eyelids;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Until I find out a place for Jehovah, A tabernacle for the Mighty One of Jacob.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Lo, we heard of it in Ephrathah: We found it in the field of the wood.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** We will go into his tabernacles; We will worship at his footstool.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Arise, O Jehovah, into thy resting-place; Thou, and the ark of thy strength.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let thy priests be clothed with righteousness; And let thy saints shout for joy.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For thy servant David’s sake Turn not away the face of thine anointed.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Jehovah hath sworn unto David in truth; He will not turn from it: Of the fruit of thy body will I set upon thy throne.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** If thy children will keep my covenant And my testimony that I shall teach them, Their children also shall sit upon thy throne for evermore.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For Jehovah hath chosen Zion; He hath desired it for his habitation.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** This is my resting-place for ever: Here will I dwell; for I have desired it.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I will abundantly bless her provision: I will satisfy her poor with bread.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Her priests also will I clothe with salvation; And her saints shall shout aloud for joy.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** There will I make the horn of David to bud: I have ordained a lamp for mine anointed.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** His enemies will I clothe with shame; But upon himself shall his crown flourish.
