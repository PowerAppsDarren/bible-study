# Psalms 132 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Lord, give thought to David, and to all his troubles;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How he made an oath to the Lord, and gave his word to the great God of Jacob, saying,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Truly, I will not come into my house, or go to my bed,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will not give sleep to my eyes, or rest to my eyeballs,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Till I have got a place for the Lord, a resting-place for the great God of Jacob.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** We had news of it at Ephrathah: we came to it in the fields of the wood.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Let us go into his tent; let us give worship at his feet.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Come back, O Lord, to your resting-place; you and the ark of your strength.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let your priests be clothed with righteousness; and let your saints give cries of joy.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Because of your servant David, do not give up your king.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The Lord gave a true oath to David, which he will not take back, saying, I will give your kingdom to the fruit of your body.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** If your children keep my word, and the teachings which I will give them, their children will be rulers of your kingdom for ever.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For the Lord's heart is on Zion, desiring it for his resting-place.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** This is my rest for ever: here will I ever be; for this is my desire.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** My blessing will be on her food; and her poor will be full of bread.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Her priests will be clothed with salvation; and her saints will give cries of joy.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** There I will make the horn of David fertile: I have made ready a light for my king.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** His haters will be clothed with shame; but I will make his crown shining.
