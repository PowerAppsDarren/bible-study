# Psalms 132 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Lord, remember David, and all his afflictions:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How he sware unto the Lord, and vowed unto the mighty God of Jacob;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Surely I will not come into the tabernacle of my house, nor go up into my bed;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will not give sleep to mine eyes, or slumber to mine eyelids,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Until I find out a place for the Lord, an habitation for the mighty God of Jacob.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Lo, we heard of it at Ephratah: we found it in the fields of the wood.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** We will go into his tabernacles: we will worship at his footstool.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Arise, O Lord, into thy rest; thou, and the ark of thy strength.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let thy priests be clothed with righteousness; and let thy saints shout for joy.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For thy servant David’s sake turn not away the face of thine anointed.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The Lord hath sworn in truth unto David; he will not turn from it; Of the fruit of thy body will I set upon thy throne.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** If thy children will keep my covenant and my testimony that I shall teach them, their children shall also sit upon thy throne for evermore.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For the Lord hath chosen Zion; he hath desired it for his habitation.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** This is my rest for ever: here will I dwell; for I have desired it.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I will abundantly bless her provision: I will satisfy her poor with bread.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** I will also clothe her priests with salvation: and her saints shall shout aloud for joy.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** There will I make the horn of David to bud: I have ordained a lamp for mine anointed.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** His enemies will I clothe with shame: but upon himself shall his crown flourish.
