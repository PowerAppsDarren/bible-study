# Psalms 132 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh, remember David and all his affliction,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** how he swore to Yahweh, and vowed to the Mighty One of Jacob:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** “Surely I will not come into the structure of my house, nor go up into my bed;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will not give sleep to my eyes, or slumber to my eyelids;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** until I find out a place for Yahweh, a dwelling for the Mighty One of Jacob.”

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Behold, we heard of it in Ephrathah. We found it in the field of Jaar:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** “We will go into his dwelling place. We will worship at his footstool.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Arise, Yahweh, into your resting place; you, and the ark of your strength.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let your priest be clothed with righteousness. Let your saints shout for joy!”

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For your servant David’s sake, don’t turn away the face of your anointed one.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Yahweh has sworn to David in truth. He will not turn from it: “I will set the fruit of your body on your throne.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** If your children will keep my covenant, my testimony that I will teach them, their children also will sit on your throne forever more.”

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For Yahweh has chosen Zion. He has desired it for his habitation.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** “This is my resting place forever. Here I will live, for I have desired it.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I will abundantly bless her provision. I will satisfy her poor with bread.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Her priests I will also clothe with salvation. Her saints will shout aloud for joy.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** There I will make the horn of David to bud. I have ordained a lamp for my anointed.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** I will clothe his enemies with shame, but on himself, his crown will be resplendent.”
