# Psalms 114 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** When Israel went out of Egypt, the house of Jacob from a people of strange language;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Judah was his sanctuary, and Israel his dominion.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The sea saw it, and fled: Jordan was driven back.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The mountains skipped like rams, and the little hills like lambs.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** What ailed thee, O thou sea, that thou fleddest? thou Jordan, that thou wast driven back?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Ye mountains, that ye skipped like rams; and ye little hills, like lambs?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Tremble, thou earth, at the presence of the Lord, at the presence of the God of Jacob;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Which turned the rock into a standing water, the flint into a fountain of waters.
