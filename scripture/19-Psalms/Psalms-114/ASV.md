# Psalms 114 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** When Israel went forth out of Egypt, The house of Jacob from a people of strange language;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Judah became his sanctuary, Israel his dominion.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The sea saw it, and fled; The Jordan was driven back.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The mountains skipped like rams, The little hills like lambs.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** What aileth thee, O thou sea, that thou fleest? Thou Jordan, that thou turnest back?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Ye mountains, that ye skip like rams; Ye little hills, like lambs?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Tremble, thou earth, at the presence of the Lord, At the presence of the God of Jacob,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Who turned the rock into a pool of water, The flint into a fountain of waters.
