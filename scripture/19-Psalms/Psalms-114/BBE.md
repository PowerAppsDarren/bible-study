# Psalms 114 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** When Israel came out of Egypt, the children of Jacob from a people whose language was strange to them;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Judah became his holy place, and Israel his kingdom.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The sea saw it, and went in flight; Jordan was turned back.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The mountains were jumping like goats, and the little hills like lambs.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** What was wrong with you, O sea, that you went in flight? O Jordan, that you were turned back?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You mountains, why were you jumping like goats, and you little hills like lambs?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Be troubled, O earth, before the Lord, before the God of Jacob;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Who made the rock into a water-spring, and the hard stone into a fountain.
