# Psalms 102 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear my prayer, O Jehovah, And let my cry come unto thee.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Hide not thy face from me in the day of my distress: Incline thine ear unto me; In the day when I call answer me speedily.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For my days consume away like smoke, And my bones are burned as a firebrand.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** My heart is smitten like grass, and withered; For I forget to eat my bread.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** By reason of the voice of my groaning My bones cleave to my flesh.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I am like a pelican of the wilderness; I am become as an owl of the waste places.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I watch, and am become like a sparrow That is alone upon the house-top.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Mine enemies reproach me all the day; They that are mad against me do curse by me.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For I have eaten ashes like bread, And mingled my drink with weeping,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Because of thine indignation and thy wrath: For thou hast taken me up, and cast me away.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My days are like a shadow that declineth; And I am withered like grass.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But thou, O Jehovah, wilt abide for ever; And thy memorial name unto all generations.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thou wilt arise, and have mercy upon Zion; For it is time to have pity upon her, Yea, the set time is come.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For thy servants take pleasure in her stones, And have pity upon her dust.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** So the nations shall fear the name of Jehovah, And all the kings of the earth thy glory.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For Jehovah hath built up Zion; He hath appeared in his glory.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He hath regarded the prayer of the destitute, And hath not despised their prayer.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** This shall be written for the generation to come; And a people which shall be created shall praise Jehovah.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** For he hath looked down from the height of his sanctuary; From heaven did Jehovah behold the earth;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** To hear the sighing of the prisoner; To loose those that are appointed to death;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** That men may declare the name of Jehovah in Zion, And his praise in Jerusalem;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** When the peoples are gathered together, And the kingdoms, to serve Jehovah.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** He weakened my strength in the way; He shortened my days.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** I said, O my God, take me not away in the midst of my days: Thy years are throughout all generations.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Of old didst thou lay the foundation of the earth; And the heavens are the work of thy hands.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** They shall perish, but thou shalt endure; Yea, all of them shall wax old like a garment; As a vesture shalt thou change them, and they shall be changed:

<a id="v27"></a>**27** But thou art the same, And thy years shall have no end.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The children of thy servants shall continue, And their seed shall be established before thee.
