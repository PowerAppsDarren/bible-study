# Psalms 102 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear my prayer, O Lord, and let my cry come unto thee.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Hide not thy face from me in the day when I am in trouble; incline thine ear unto me: in the day when I call answer me speedily.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For my days are consumed like smoke, and my bones are burned as an hearth.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** My heart is smitten, and withered like grass; so that I forget to eat my bread.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** By reason of the voice of my groaning my bones cleave to my skin.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I am like a pelican of the wilderness: I am like an owl of the desert.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I watch, and am as a sparrow alone upon the house top.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Mine enemies reproach me all the day; and they that are mad against me are sworn against me.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For I have eaten ashes like bread, and mingled my drink with weeping,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Because of thine indignation and thy wrath: for thou hast lifted me up, and cast me down.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My days are like a shadow that declineth; and I am withered like grass.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But thou, O Lord, shalt endure for ever; and thy remembrance unto all generations.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thou shalt arise, and have mercy upon Zion: for the time to favour her, yea, the set time, is come.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For thy servants take pleasure in her stones, and favour the dust thereof.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** So the heathen shall fear the name of the Lord, and all the kings of the earth thy glory.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** When the Lord shall build up Zion, he shall appear in his glory.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He will regard the prayer of the destitute, and not despise their prayer.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** This shall be written for the generation to come: and the people which shall be created shall praise the Lord.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** For he hath looked down from the height of his sanctuary; from heaven did the Lord behold the earth;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** To hear the groaning of the prisoner; to loose those that are appointed to death;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** To declare the name of the Lord in Zion, and his praise in Jerusalem;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** When the people are gathered together, and the kingdoms, to serve the Lord.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** He weakened my strength in the way; he shortened my days.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** I said, O my God, take me not away in the midst of my days: thy years are throughout all generations.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Of old hast thou laid the foundation of the earth: and the heavens are the work of thy hands.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** They shall perish, but thou shalt endure: yea, all of them shall wax old like a garment; as a vesture shalt thou change them, and they shall be changed:

<a id="v27"></a>**27** But thou art the same, and thy years shall have no end.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The children of thy servants shall continue, and their seed shall be established before thee.
