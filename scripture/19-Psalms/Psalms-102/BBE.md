# Psalms 102 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give ear to my prayer, O Lord, and let my cry come to you.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let not your face be veiled from me in the day of my trouble; give ear to me, and let my cry be answered quickly.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** My days are wasted like smoke, and my bones are burned up as in a fire.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** My heart is broken; it has become dry and dead like grass, so that I give no thought to food.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Because of the voice of my sorrow, my flesh is wasted to the bone.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I am like a bird living by itself in the waste places; like the night-bird in a waste of sand.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I keep watch like a bird by itself on the house-top.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** My haters say evil of me all day; those who are violent against me make use of my name as a curse.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I have had dust for bread and my drink has been mixed with weeping:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Because of your passion and your wrath, for I have been lifted up and then made low by you.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My days are like a shade which is stretched out; I am dry like the grass.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But you, O Lord, are eternal; and your name will never come to an end.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** You will again get up and have mercy on Zion: for the time has come for her to be comforted.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For your servants take pleasure in her stones, looking with love on her dust.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** So the nations will give honour to the name of the Lord, and all the kings of the earth will be in fear of his glory:

<a id="v16"></a>**16** When the Lord has put up the walls of Zion, and has been been in his glory;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** When he has given ear to the prayer of the poor, and has not put his request on one side.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** This will be put in writing for the coming generation, and the people of the future will give praise to the Lord.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** For from his holy place the Lord has seen, looking down on the earth from heaven;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Hearing the cry of the prisoner, making free those for whom death is ordered;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** So that they may give out the name of the Lord in Zion, and his praise in Jerusalem;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** When the peoples are come together, and the kingdoms, to give worship to the Lord.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** He has taken my strength from me in the way; he has made short my days.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** I will say, O my God, take me not away before my time; your years go on through all generations:

<a id="v25"></a>**25** In the past you put the earth on its base, and the heavens are the work of your hands.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** They will come to an end, but you will still go on; they all will become old like a coat, and like a robe they will be changed:

<a id="v27"></a>**27** But you are the unchanging One, and your years will have no end.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The children of your servants will have a safe resting-place, and their seed will be ever before you.
