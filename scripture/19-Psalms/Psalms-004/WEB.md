# Psalms 4 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Answer me when I call, God of my righteousness. Give me relief from my distress. Have mercy on me, and hear my prayer.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You sons of men, how long shall my glory be turned into dishonor? Will you love vanity, and seek after falsehood? Selah.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But know that Yahweh has set apart for himself him who is godly: Yahweh will hear when I call to him.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Stand in awe, and don’t sin. Search your own heart on your bed, and be still. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Offer the sacrifices of righteousness. Put your trust in Yahweh.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Many say, “Who will show us any good?” Yahweh, let the light of your face shine on us.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You have put gladness in my heart, more than when their grain and their new wine are increased.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** In peace I will both lay myself down and sleep, for you, Yahweh alone, make me live in safety.
