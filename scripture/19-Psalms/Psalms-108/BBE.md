# Psalms 108 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O God, my heart is fixed; I will make songs and melody, even with my glory.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Give out your sounds, O corded instruments: the dawn will be awaking with my song.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I will give you praise, O Lord, among the peoples; I will make melody to you among the nations.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For your mercy is higher than the heavens: and your unchanging faith than the clouds.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Be lifted up, O God, higher than the heavens; let your glory be over all the earth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let your right hand be stretched out for salvation, and give me an answer, so that your loved ones may be safe from danger.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** This is the word of the holy God: I will be glad; I will make Shechem a heritage, measuring out the valley of Succoth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Gilead is mine; Manasseh is mine; Ephraim is the strength of my head; Judah is my law-giver;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Moab is my washpot; on Edom is the resting-place of my shoe; over Philistia will I send out a glad cry.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Who will take me into the strong town? who will be my guide into Edom?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Have you not sent us away from you, O God? and you go not out with our armies.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Give us help in our trouble; for there is no help in man.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** With God we will do great things; for by him will our haters be crushed underfoot.
