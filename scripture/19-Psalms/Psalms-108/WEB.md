# Psalms 108 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My heart is steadfast, God. I will sing and I will make music with my soul.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Wake up, harp and lyre! I will wake up the dawn.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I will give thanks to you, Yahweh, among the nations. I will sing praises to you among the peoples.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For your loving kindness is great above the heavens. Your faithfulness reaches to the skies.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Be exalted, God, above the heavens! Let your glory be over all the earth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** That your beloved may be delivered, save with your right hand, and answer us.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** God has spoken from his sanctuary: “In triumph, I will divide Shechem, and measure out the valley of Succoth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Gilead is mine. Manasseh is mine. Ephraim also is my helmet. Judah is my scepter.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Moab is my wash pot. I will toss my sandal on Edom. I will shout over Philistia.”

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Who will bring me into the fortified city? Who has led me to Edom?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Haven’t you rejected us, God? You don’t go out, God, with our armies.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Give us help against the enemy, for the help of man is vain.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Through God, we will do valiantly. For it is he who will tread down our enemies.
