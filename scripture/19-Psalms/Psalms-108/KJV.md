# Psalms 108 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O God, my heart is fixed; I will sing and give praise, even with my glory.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Awake, psaltery and harp: I myself will awake early.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I will praise thee, O Lord, among the people: and I will sing praises unto thee among the nations.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For thy mercy is great above the heavens: and thy truth reacheth unto the clouds.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens: and thy glory above all the earth;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** That thy beloved may be delivered: save with thy right hand, and answer me.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** God hath spoken in his holiness; I will rejoice, I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Gilead is mine; Manasseh is mine; Ephraim also is the strength of mine head; Judah is my lawgiver;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Moab is my washpot; over Edom will I cast out my shoe; over Philistia will I triumph.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Who will bring me into the strong city? who will lead me into Edom?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Wilt not thou, O God, who hast cast us off? and wilt not thou, O God, go forth with our hosts?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Give us help from trouble: for vain is the help of man.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Through God we shall do valiantly: for he it is that shall tread down our enemies.
