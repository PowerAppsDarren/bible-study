# Psalms 108 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My heart is fixed, O God; I will sing, yea, I will sing praises, even with my glory.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Awake, psaltery and harp: I myself will awake right early.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I will give thanks unto thee, O Jehovah, among the peoples; And I will sing praises unto thee among the nations.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For thy lovingkindness is great above the heavens; And thy truth reacheth unto the skies.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens, And thy glory above all the earth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** That thy beloved may be delivered, Save with thy right hand, and answer us.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** God hath spoken in his holiness: I will exult; I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Gilead is mine; Manasseh is mine; Ephraim also is the defence of my head; Judah is my sceptre.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Moab is my washpot; Upon Edom will I cast my shoe; Over Philistia will I shout.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Who will bring me into the fortified city? Who hath led me unto Edom?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Hast not thou cast us off, O God? And thou goest not forth, O God, with our hosts.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Give us help against the adversary; For vain is the help of man.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Through God we shall do valiantly: For he it is that will tread down our adversaries.
