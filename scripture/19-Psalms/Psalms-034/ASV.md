# Psalms 34 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will bless Jehovah at all times: His praise shall continually be in my mouth.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My soul shall make her boast in Jehovah: The meek shall hear thereof, and be glad.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Oh magnify Jehovah with me, And let us exalt his name together.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I sought Jehovah, and he answered me, And delivered me from all my fears.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They looked unto him, and were radiant; And their faces shall never be confounded.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** This poor man cried, and Jehovah heard him, And saved him out of all his troubles.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The angel of Jehovah encampeth round about them that fear him, And delivereth them.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Oh taste and see that Jehovah is good: Blessed is the man that taketh refuge in him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Oh fear Jehovah, ye his saints; For there is no want to them that fear him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The young lions do lack, and suffer hunger; But they that seek Jehovah shall not want any good thing.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Come, ye children, hearken unto me: I will teach you the fear of Jehovah.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** What man is he that desireth life, And loveth many days, that he may see good?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Keep thy tongue from evil, And thy lips from speaking guile.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Depart from evil, and do good; Seek peace, and pursue it.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The eyes of Jehovah are toward the righteous, And his ears are open unto their cry.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The face of Jehovah is against them that do evil, To cut off the remembrance of them from the earth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The righteous cried, and Jehovah heard, And delivered them out of all their troubles.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Jehovah is nigh unto them that are of a broken heart, And saveth such as are of a contrite spirit.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Many are the afflictions of the righteous; But Jehovah delivereth him out of them all.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He keepeth all his bones: Not one of them is broken.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Evil shall slay the wicked; And they that hate the righteous shall be condemned.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Jehovah redeemeth the soul of his servants; And none of them that take refuge in him shall be condemned.
