# Psalms 34 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will be blessing the Lord at all times; his praise will be ever in my mouth.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My soul will say great things of the Lord: the poor in spirit will have knowledge of it and be glad.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** O give praise to the Lord with me; let us be witnesses together of his great name.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I was searching for the Lord, and he gave ear to my voice, and made me free from all my fears.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let your eyes be turned to him and you will have light, and your faces will not be shamed.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** This poor man's cry came before the Lord, and he gave him salvation from all his troubles.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The angel of the Lord is ever watching over those who have fear of him, to keep them safe.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** By experience you will see that the Lord is good; happy is the man who has faith in him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Keep yourselves in the fear of the Lord, all you his saints; for those who do so will have no need of anything.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The young lions are in need and have no food; but those who are looking to the Lord will have every good thing.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Come, children, give attention to me; I will be your teacher in the fear of the Lord.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** What man has a love of life, and a desire that his days may be increased so that he may see good?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Keep your tongue from evil, and your lips from words of deceit.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Be turned from evil, and do good; make a search for peace, desiring it with all your heart.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The eyes of the Lord are on the upright, and his ears are open to their cry.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The face of the Lord is against those who do evil, to take away the memory of them from the earth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The cry of the upright comes before the Lord, and he takes them out of all their troubles.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The Lord is near the broken-hearted; he is the saviour of those whose spirits are crushed down.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Great are the troubles of the upright: but the Lord takes him safely out of them all.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He keeps all his bones: not one of them is broken.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Evil will put an end to the sinner, and those who are haters of righteousness will come to destruction.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The Lord will be the saviour of the souls of his servants, and no one who has faith in him will be put to shame.
