# Psalms 34 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will bless Yahweh at all times. His praise will always be in my mouth.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My soul shall boast in Yahweh. The humble shall hear of it, and be glad.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Oh magnify Yahweh with me. Let us exalt his name together.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I sought Yahweh, and he answered me, and delivered me from all my fears.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They looked to him, and were radiant. Their faces shall never be covered with shame.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** This poor man cried, and Yahweh heard him, and saved him out of all his troubles.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Yahweh’s angel encamps around those who fear him, and delivers them.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Oh taste and see that Yahweh is good. Blessed is the man who takes refuge in him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Oh fear Yahweh, you his saints, for there is no lack with those who fear him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The young lions do lack, and suffer hunger, but those who seek Yahweh shall not lack any good thing.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Come, you children, listen to me. I will teach you the fear of Yahweh.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Who is someone who desires life, and loves many days, that he may see good?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Keep your tongue from evil, and your lips from speaking lies.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Depart from evil, and do good. seek peace, and pursue it.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Yahweh’s eyes are toward the righteous. His ears listen to their cry.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Yahweh’s face is against those who do evil, to cut off their memory from the earth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The righteous cry, and Yahweh hears, and delivers them out of all their troubles.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Yahweh is near to those who have a broken heart, and saves those who have a crushed spirit.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Many are the afflictions of the righteous, but Yahweh delivers him out of them all.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He protects all of his bones. Not one of them is broken.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Evil shall kill the wicked. Those who hate the righteous shall be condemned.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Yahweh redeems the soul of his servants. None of those who take refuge in him shall be condemned.
