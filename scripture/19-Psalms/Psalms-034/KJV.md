# Psalms 34 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will bless the Lord at all times: his praise shall continually be in my mouth.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My soul shall make her boast in the Lord: the humble shall hear thereof, and be glad.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** O magnify the Lord with me, and let us exalt his name together.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I sought the Lord, and he heard me, and delivered me from all my fears.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They looked unto him, and were lightened: and their faces were not ashamed.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** This poor man cried, and the Lord heard him, and saved him out of all his troubles.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The angel of the Lord encampeth round about them that fear him, and delivereth them.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** O taste and see that the Lord is good: blessed is the man that trusteth in him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** O fear the Lord, ye his saints: for there is no want to them that fear him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The young lions do lack, and suffer hunger: but they that seek the Lord shall not want any good thing.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Come, ye children, hearken unto me: I will teach you the fear of the Lord.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** What man is he that desireth life, and loveth many days, that he may see good?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Keep thy tongue from evil, and thy lips from speaking guile.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Depart from evil, and do good; seek peace, and pursue it.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The eyes of the Lord are upon the righteous, and his ears are open unto their cry.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The face of the Lord is against them that do evil, to cut off the remembrance of them from the earth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The righteous cry, and the Lord heareth, and delivereth them out of all their troubles.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The Lord is nigh unto them that are of a broken heart; and saveth such as be of a contrite spirit.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Many are the afflictions of the righteous: but the Lord delivereth him out of them all.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He keepeth all his bones: not one of them is broken.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Evil shall slay the wicked: and they that hate the righteous shall be desolate.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The Lord redeemeth the soul of his servants: and none of them that trust in him shall be desolate.
