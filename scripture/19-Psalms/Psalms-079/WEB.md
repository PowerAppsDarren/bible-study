# Psalms 79 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God, the nations have come into your inheritance. They have defiled your holy temple. They have laid Jerusalem in heaps.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** They have given the dead bodies of your servants to be food for the birds of the sky, the flesh of your saints to the animals of the earth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Their blood they have shed like water around Jerusalem. There was no one to bury them.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** We have become a reproach to our neighbors, a scoffing and derision to those who are around us.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** How long, Yahweh? Will you be angry forever? Will your jealousy burn like fire?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Pour out your wrath on the nations that don’t know you; on the kingdoms that don’t call on your name;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For they have devoured Jacob, and destroyed his homeland.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Don’t hold the iniquities of our forefathers against us. Let your tender mercies speedily meet us, for we are in desperate need.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Help us, God of our salvation, for the glory of your name. Deliver us, and forgive our sins, for your name’s sake.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Why should the nations say, “Where is their God?” Let it be known among the nations, before our eyes, that vengeance for your servants’ blood is being poured out.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let the sighing of the prisoner come before you. According to the greatness of your power, preserve those who are sentenced to death.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Pay back to our neighbors seven times into their bosom their reproach with which they have reproached you, Lord.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** So we, your people and sheep of your pasture, will give you thanks forever. We will praise you forever, to all generations.
