# Psalms 20 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The Lord hear thee in the day of trouble; the name of the God of Jacob defend thee;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Send thee help from the sanctuary, and strengthen thee out of Zion;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Remember all thy offerings, and accept thy burnt sacrifice; Selah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Grant thee according to thine own heart, and fulfil all thy counsel.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** We will rejoice in thy salvation, and in the name of our God we will set up our banners: the Lord fulfil all thy petitions.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Now know I that the Lord saveth his anointed; he will hear him from his holy heaven with the saving strength of his right hand.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Some trust in chariots, and some in horses: but we will remember the name of the Lord our God.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They are brought down and fallen: but we are risen, and stand upright.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Save, Lord: let the king hear us when we call.
