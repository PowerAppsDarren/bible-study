# Psalms 20 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** May Yahweh answer you in the day of trouble. May the name of the God of Jacob set you up on high,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** send you help from the sanctuary, grant you support from Zion,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** remember all your offerings, and accept your burnt sacrifice. Selah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** May He grant you your heart’s desire, and fulfill all your counsel.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** We will triumph in your salvation. In the name of our God, we will set up our banners. May Yahweh grant all your requests.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Now I know that Yahweh saves his anointed. He will answer him from his holy heaven, with the saving strength of his right hand.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Some trust in chariots, and some in horses, but we trust the name of Yahweh our God.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They are bowed down and fallen, but we rise up, and stand upright.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Save, Yahweh! Let the King answer us when we call!
