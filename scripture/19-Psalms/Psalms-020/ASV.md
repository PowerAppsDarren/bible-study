# Psalms 20 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Jehovah answer thee in the day of trouble; The name of the God of Jacob set thee up on high;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Send thee help from the sanctuary, And strengthen thee out of Zion;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Remember all thy offerings, And accept thy burnt-sacrifice; [Selah

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Grant thee thy heart’s desire, And fulfil all thy counsel.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** We will triumph in thy salvation, And in the name of our God we will set up our banners: Jehovah fulfil all thy petitions.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Now know I that Jehovah saveth his anointed; He will answer him from his holy heaven With the saving strength of his right hand.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Some trustin chariots, and some in horses; But we will make mention of the name of Jehovah our God.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They are bowed down and fallen; But we are risen, and stand upright.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Save, Jehovah: Let the King answer us when we call.
