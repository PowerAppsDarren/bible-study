# Psalms 100 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Make a joyful noise unto Jehovah, all ye lands.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Serve Jehovah with gladness: Come before his presence with singing.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Know ye that Jehovah, he is God: It is he that hath made us, and we are his; We are his people, and the sheep of his pasture.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Enter into his gates with thanksgiving, And into his courts with praise: Give thanks unto him, and bless his name.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For Jehovah is good; his lovingkindness endureth for ever, And his faithfulness unto all generations.
