# Psalms 100 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Shout for joy to Yahweh, all you lands!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Serve Yahweh with gladness. Come before his presence with singing.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Know that Yahweh, he is God. It is he who has made us, and we are his. We are his people, and the sheep of his pasture.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Enter into his gates with thanksgiving, into his courts with praise. Give thanks to him, and bless his name.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For Yahweh is good. His loving kindness endures forever, his faithfulness to all generations.
