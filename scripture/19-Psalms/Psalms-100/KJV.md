# Psalms 100 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Make a joyful noise unto the Lord, all ye lands.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Serve the Lord with gladness: come before his presence with singing.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Know ye that the Lord he is God: it is he that hath made us, and not we ourselves; we are his people, and the sheep of his pasture.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Enter into his gates with thanksgiving, and into his courts with praise: be thankful unto him, and bless his name.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For the Lord is good; his mercy is everlasting; and his truth endureth to all generations.
