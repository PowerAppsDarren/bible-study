# Psalms 100 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Make a glad sound to the Lord, all the earth.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Give worship to the Lord with joy; come before him with a song.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Be certain that the Lord is God; it is he who has made us, and we are his; we are his people, and the sheep to whom he gives food.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Come into his doors with joy, and into his house with praise; give him honour, blessing his name.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For the Lord is good, and his mercy is never-ending; his faith is unchanging through all generations.
