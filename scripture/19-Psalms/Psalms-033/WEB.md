# Psalms 33 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Rejoice in Yahweh, you righteous! Praise is fitting for the upright.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Give thanks to Yahweh with the lyre. Sing praises to him with the harp of ten strings.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Sing to him a new song. Play skillfully with a shout of joy!

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For Yahweh’s word is right. All his work is done in faithfulness.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He loves righteousness and justice. The earth is full of the loving kindness of Yahweh.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** By Yahweh’s word, the heavens were made; all their army by the breath of his mouth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He gathers the waters of the sea together as a heap. He lays up the deeps in storehouses.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let all the earth fear Yahweh. Let all the inhabitants of the world stand in awe of him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For he spoke, and it was done. He commanded, and it stood firm.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Yahweh brings the counsel of the nations to nothing. He makes the thoughts of the peoples to be of no effect.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The counsel of Yahweh stands fast forever, the thoughts of his heart to all generations.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Blessed is the nation whose God is Yahweh, the people whom he has chosen for his own inheritance.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Yahweh looks from heaven. He sees all the sons of men.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** From the place of his habitation he looks out on all the inhabitants of the earth,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** he who fashions all of their hearts; and he considers all of their works.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** There is no king saved by the multitude of an army. A mighty man is not delivered by great strength.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** A horse is a vain thing for safety, neither does he deliver any by his great power.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Behold, Yahweh’s eye is on those who fear him, on those who hope in his loving kindness;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** to deliver their soul from death, to keep them alive in famine.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Our soul has waited for Yahweh. He is our help and our shield.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For our heart rejoices in him, because we have trusted in his holy name.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Let your loving kindness be on us, Yahweh, since we have hoped in you.
