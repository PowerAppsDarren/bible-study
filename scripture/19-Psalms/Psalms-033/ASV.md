# Psalms 33 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Rejoice in Jehovah, O ye righteous: Praise is comely for the upright.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Give thanks unto Jehovah with the harp: Sing praises unto him with the psaltery of ten strings.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Sing unto him a new song; Play skilfully with a loud noise.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For the word of Jehovah is right; And all his work isdonein faithfulness.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He loveth righteousness and justice: The earth is full of the lovingkindness of Jehovah.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** By the word of Jehovah were the heavens made, And all the host of them by the breath of his mouth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He gathereth the waters of the sea together as a heap: He layeth up the deeps in store-houses.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let all the earth fear Jehovah: Let all the inhabitants of the world stand in awe of him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For he spake, and it was done; He commanded, and it stood fast.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Jehovah bringeth the counsel of the nations to nought; He maketh the thoughts of the peoples to be of no effect.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The counsel of Jehovah standeth fast for ever, The thoughts of his heart to all generations.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Blessed is the nation whose God is Jehovah, The people whom he hath chosen for his own inheritance.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Jehovah looketh from heaven; He beholdeth all the sons of men;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** From the place of his habitation he looketh forth Upon all the inhabitants of the earth,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He that fashioneth the hearts of them all, That considereth all their works.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** There is no king saved by the multitude of a host: A mighty man is not delivered by great strength.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** A horse is a vain thing for safety; Neither doth he deliver any by his great power.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Behold, the eye of Jehovah is upon them that fear him, Upon them that hope in his lovingkindness;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** To deliver their soul from death, And to keep them alive in famine.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Our soul hath waited for Jehovah: He is our help and our shield.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For our heart shall rejoice in him, Because we have trusted in his holy name.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Let thy lovingkindness, O Jehovah, be upon us, According as we have hoped in thee.
