# Psalms 33 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Rejoice in the Lord, O ye righteous: for praise is comely for the upright.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Praise the Lord with harp: sing unto him with the psaltery and an instrument of ten strings.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Sing unto him a new song; play skilfully with a loud noise.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For the word of the Lord is right; and all his works are done in truth.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He loveth righteousness and judgment: the earth is full of the goodness of the Lord.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** By the word of the Lord were the heavens made; and all the host of them by the breath of his mouth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He gathereth the waters of the sea together as an heap: he layeth up the depth in storehouses.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let all the earth fear the Lord: let all the inhabitants of the world stand in awe of him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For he spake, and it was done; he commanded, and it stood fast.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The Lord bringeth the counsel of the heathen to nought: he maketh the devices of the people of none effect.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The counsel of the Lord standeth for ever, the thoughts of his heart to all generations.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Blessed is the nation whose God is the Lord; and the people whom he hath chosen for his own inheritance.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The Lord looketh from heaven; he beholdeth all the sons of men.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** From the place of his habitation he looketh upon all the inhabitants of the earth.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He fashioneth their hearts alike; he considereth all their works.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** There is no king saved by the multitude of an host: a mighty man is not delivered by much strength.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** An horse is a vain thing for safety: neither shall he deliver any by his great strength.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Behold, the eye of the Lord is upon them that fear him, upon them that hope in his mercy;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** To deliver their soul from death, and to keep them alive in famine.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Our soul waiteth for the Lord: he is our help and our shield.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For our heart shall rejoice in him, because we have trusted in his holy name.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Let thy mercy, O Lord, be upon us, according as we hope in thee.
