# Psalms 33 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Be glad in the Lord, O doers of righteousness; for praise is beautiful for the upright.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Give praise to the Lord on the corded instrument; make melody to him with instruments of music.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Make a new song to him; playing expertly with a loud noise.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For the word of the Lord is upright, and all his works are certain.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** His delight is in righteousness and wisdom; the earth is full of the mercy of the Lord.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** By the word of the Lord were the heavens made; and all the army of heaven by the breath of his mouth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He makes the waters of the sea come together in a mass; he keeps the deep seas in store-houses.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let the earth be full of the fear of the Lord; let all the people of the world be in holy fear of him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For he gave the word, and it was done; by his order it was fixed for ever.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The Lord undoes the designs of the nations; he makes the thoughts of the peoples without effect.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The Lord's purpose is eternal, the designs of his heart go on through all the generations of man.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Happy is the nation whose God is the Lord; and the people whom he has taken for his heritage.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The Lord is looking down from heaven; he sees all the sons of men;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** From his house he keeps watch on all who are living on the earth;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He makes all their hearts; their works are clear to him.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** A king's salvation is not in the power of his army; a strong man does not get free by his great strength.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** A horse is a false hope; his great power will not make any man free from danger.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** See, the eye of the Lord is on those in whose hearts is the fear of him, on those whose hope is in his mercy;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** To keep their souls from death; and to keep them living in time of need.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Our souls are waiting for the Lord; he is our help and our salvation.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For in him our hearts have joy; in his holy name is our hope.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Let your mercy be on us, O Lord, as we are waiting for you.
