# Psalms 145 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will extol thee, my God, O King; And I will bless thy name for ever and ever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Every day will I bless thee; And I will praise thy name for ever and ever.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Great is Jehovah, and greatly to be praised; And his greatness is unsearchable.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** One generation shall laud thy works to another, And shall declare thy mighty acts.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Of the glorious majesty of thine honor, And of thy wondrous works, will I meditate.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And men shall speak of the might of thy terrible acts; And I will declare thy greatness.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They shall utter the memory of thy great goodness, And shall sing of thy righteousness.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Jehovah is gracious, and merciful; Slow to anger, and of great lovingkindness.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Jehovah is good to all; And his tender mercies are over all his works.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All thy works shall give thanks unto thee, O Jehovah; And thy saints shall bless thee.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They shall speak of the glory of thy kingdom, And talk of thy power;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** To make known to the sons of men his mighty acts, And the glory of the majesty of his kingdom.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thy kingdom is an everlasting kingdom, And thy dominion endureth throughout all generations.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Jehovah upholdeth all that fall, And raiseth up all those that are bowed down.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The eyes of all wait for thee; And thou givest them their food in due season.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Thou openest thy hand, And satisfiest the desire of every living thing.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Jehovah is righteous in all his ways, And gracious in all his works.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Jehovah is nigh unto all them that call upon him, To all that call upon him in truth.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He will fulfil the desire of them that fear him; He also will hear their cry and will save them.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Jehovah preserveth all them that love him; But all the wicked will he destroy.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** My mouth shall speak the praise of Jehovah; And let all flesh bless his holy name for ever and ever.
