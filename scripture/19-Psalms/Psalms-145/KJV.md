# Psalms 145 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will extol thee, my God, O king; and I will bless thy name for ever and ever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Every day will I bless thee; and I will praise thy name for ever and ever.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Great is the Lord, and greatly to be praised; and his greatness is unsearchable.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** One generation shall praise thy works to another, and shall declare thy mighty acts.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I will speak of the glorious honour of thy majesty, and of thy wondrous works.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And men shall speak of the might of thy terrible acts: and I will declare thy greatness.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They shall abundantly utter the memory of thy great goodness, and shall sing of thy righteousness.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The Lord is gracious, and full of compassion; slow to anger, and of great mercy.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The Lord is good to all: and his tender mercies are over all his works.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All thy works shall praise thee, O Lord; and thy saints shall bless thee.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They shall speak of the glory of thy kingdom, and talk of thy power;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** To make known to the sons of men his mighty acts, and the glorious majesty of his kingdom.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thy kingdom is an everlasting kingdom, and thy dominion endureth throughout all generations.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The Lord upholdeth all that fall, and raiseth up all those that be bowed down.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The eyes of all wait upon thee; and thou givest them their meat in due season.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Thou openest thine hand, and satisfiest the desire of every living thing.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The Lord is righteous in all his ways, and holy in all his works.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The Lord is nigh unto all them that call upon him, to all that call upon him in truth.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He will fulfil the desire of them that fear him: he also will hear their cry, and will save them.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The Lord preserveth all them that love him: but all the wicked will he destroy.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** My mouth shall speak the praise of the Lord: and let all flesh bless his holy name for ever and ever.
