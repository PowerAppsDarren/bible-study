# Psalms 145 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will exalt you, my God, the King. I will praise your name forever and ever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Every day I will praise you. I will extol your name forever and ever.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Great is Yahweh, and greatly to be praised! His greatness is unsearchable.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** One generation will commend your works to another, and will declare your mighty acts.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Of the glorious majesty of your honor, of your wondrous works, I will meditate.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Men will speak of the might of your awesome acts. I will declare your greatness.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They will utter the memory of your great goodness, and will sing of your righteousness.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yahweh is gracious, merciful, slow to anger, and of great loving kindness.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Yahweh is good to all. His tender mercies are over all his works.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All your works will give thanks to you, Yahweh. Your saints will extol you.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They will speak of the glory of your kingdom, and talk about your power;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** to make known to the sons of men his mighty acts, the glory of the majesty of his kingdom.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Your kingdom is an everlasting kingdom. Your dominion endures throughout all generations. Yahweh is faithful in all his words, and loving in all his deeds.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Yahweh upholds all who fall, and raises up all those who are bowed down.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The eyes of all wait for you. You give them their food in due season.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** You open your hand, and satisfy the desire of every living thing.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Yahweh is righteous in all his ways, and gracious in all his works.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Yahweh is near to all those who call on him, to all who call on him in truth.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He will fulfill the desire of those who fear him. He also will hear their cry, and will save them.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Yahweh preserves all those who love him, but all the wicked he will destroy.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** My mouth will speak the praise of Yahweh. Let all flesh bless his holy name forever and ever.
