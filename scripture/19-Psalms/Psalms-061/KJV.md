# Psalms 61 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear my cry, O God; attend unto my prayer.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** From the end of the earth will I cry unto thee, when my heart is overwhelmed: lead me to the rock that is higher than I.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For thou hast been a shelter for me, and a strong tower from the enemy.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will abide in thy tabernacle for ever: I will trust in the covert of thy wings. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For thou, O God, hast heard my vows: thou hast given me the heritage of those that fear thy name.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Thou wilt prolong the king’s life: and his years as many generations.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He shall abide before God for ever: O prepare mercy and truth, which may preserve him.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** So will I sing praise unto thy name for ever, that I may daily perform my vows.
