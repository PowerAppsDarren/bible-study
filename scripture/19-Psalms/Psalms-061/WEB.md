# Psalms 61 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear my cry, God. Listen to my prayer.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** From the end of the earth, I will call to you, when my heart is overwhelmed. Lead me to the rock that is higher than I.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For you have been a refuge for me, a strong tower from the enemy.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will dwell in your tent forever. I will take refuge in the shelter of your wings. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For you, God, have heard my vows. You have given me the heritage of those who fear your name.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You will prolong the king’s life; his years shall be for generations.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He shall be enthroned in God’s presence forever. Appoint your loving kindness and truth, that they may preserve him.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** So I will sing praise to your name forever, that I may fulfill my vows daily.
