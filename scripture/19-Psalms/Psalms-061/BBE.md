# Psalms 61 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Let my cry come to you, O God; let your ears be open to my prayer.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** From the end of the earth will I send up my cry to you, when my heart is overcome: take me to the rock which is over-high for me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For you have been my secret place, and my high tower from those who made war on me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will make your tent my resting-place for ever: I will keep myself under the cover of your wings. (Selah.)

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For you, O God, have made answer to my prayers; you have given me the heritage of those who give honour to your name.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You will give the king long life; and make his years go on through the generations.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** May the seat of his authority be before God for ever; may mercy and righteousness keep him safe.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** So will I make songs in praise of your name for ever, giving to God that which is right day by day.
