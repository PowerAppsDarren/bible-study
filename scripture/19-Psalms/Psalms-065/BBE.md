# Psalms 65 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** It is right for you, O God, to have praise in Zion: to you let the offering be made.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** To you, O hearer of prayer, let the words of all flesh come.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Evils have overcome us: but as for our sins, you will take them away.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Happy is the man of your selection, to whom you give a resting-place in your house; we will be full of the good things out of your holy place.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You will give us an answer in righteousness by great acts of power, O God of our salvation; you who are the hope of all the ends of the earth, and of the far-off lands of the sea;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The God by whose strength the mountains are fixed; who is robed with power:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Who makes the loud voice of the sea quiet, and puts an end to the sound of its waves.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Those in the farthest parts of the earth have fear when they see your signs: the outgoings of the morning and evening are glad because of you.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** You have given your blessing to the earth, watering it and making it fertile; the river of God is full of water: and having made it ready, you give men grain.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** You make the ploughed lands full of water; you make smooth the slopes: you make the earth soft with showers, sending your blessing on its growth.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The year is crowned with the good you give; life-giving rain is dropping from your footsteps,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Falling on the grass of the waste land: and the little hills are glad on every side.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The grass-land is thick with flocks; the valleys are full of grain; they give glad cries and songs of joy.
