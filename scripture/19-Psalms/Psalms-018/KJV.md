# Psalms 18 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will love thee, O Lord, my strength.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The Lord is my rock, and my fortress, and my deliverer; my God, my strength, in whom I will trust; my buckler, and the horn of my salvation, and my high tower.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I will call upon the Lord, who is worthy to be praised: so shall I be saved from mine enemies.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The sorrows of death compassed me, and the floods of ungodly men made me afraid.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The sorrows of hell compassed me about: the snares of death prevented me.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** In my distress I called upon the Lord, and cried unto my God: he heard my voice out of his temple, and my cry came before him, even into his ears.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Then the earth shook and trembled; the foundations also of the hills moved and were shaken, because he was wroth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** There went up a smoke out of his nostrils, and fire out of his mouth devoured: coals were kindled by it.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He bowed the heavens also, and came down: and darkness was under his feet.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And he rode upon a cherub, and did fly: yea, he did fly upon the wings of the wind.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He made darkness his secret place; his pavilion round about him were dark waters and thick clouds of the skies.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** At the brightness that was before him his thick clouds passed, hail stones and coals of fire.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The Lord also thundered in the heavens, and the Highest gave his voice; hail stones and coals of fire.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Yea, he sent out his arrows, and scattered them; and he shot out lightnings, and discomfited them.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Then the channels of waters were seen, and the foundations of the world were discovered at thy rebuke, O Lord, at the blast of the breath of thy nostrils.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He sent from above, he took me, he drew me out of many waters.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He delivered me from my strong enemy, and from them which hated me: for they were too strong for me.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** They prevented me in the day of my calamity: but the Lord was my stay.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He brought me forth also into a large place; he delivered me, because he delighted in me.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The Lord rewarded me according to my righteousness; according to the cleanness of my hands hath he recompensed me.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For I have kept the ways of the Lord, and have not wickedly departed from my God.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For all his judgments were before me, and I did not put away his statutes from me.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** I was also upright before him, and I kept myself from mine iniquity.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Therefore hath the Lord recompensed me according to my righteousness, according to the cleanness of my hands in his eyesight.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** With the merciful thou wilt shew thyself merciful; with an upright man thou wilt shew thyself upright;

<a id="v26"></a>**26** With the pure thou wilt shew thyself pure; and with the froward thou wilt shew thyself froward.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** For thou wilt save the afflicted people; but wilt bring down high looks.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** For thou wilt light my candle: the Lord my God will enlighten my darkness.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** For by thee I have run through a troop; and by my God have I leaped over a wall.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** As for God, his way is perfect: the word of the Lord is tried: he is a buckler to all those that trust in him.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For who is God save the Lord? or who is a rock save our God?

<a id="v32"></a>**32** It is God that girdeth me with strength, and maketh my way perfect.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** He maketh my feet like hinds’ feet, and setteth me upon my high places.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** He teacheth my hands to war, so that a bow of steel is broken by mine arms.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Thou hast also given me the shield of thy salvation: and thy right hand hath holden me up, and thy gentleness hath made me great.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Thou hast enlarged my steps under me, that my feet did not slip.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** I have pursued mine enemies, and overtaken them: neither did I turn again till they were consumed.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** I have wounded them that they were not able to rise: they are fallen under my feet.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** For thou hast girded me with strength unto the battle: thou hast subdued under me those that rose up against me.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** Thou hast also given me the necks of mine enemies; that I might destroy them that hate me.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** They cried, but there was none to save them: even unto the Lord, but he answered them not.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** Then did I beat them small as the dust before the wind: I did cast them out as the dirt in the streets.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Thou hast delivered me from the strivings of the people; and thou hast made me the head of the heathen: a people whom I have not known shall serve me.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** As soon as they hear of me, they shall obey me: the strangers shall submit themselves unto me.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** The strangers shall fade away, and be afraid out of their close places.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** The Lord liveth; and blessed be my rock; and let the God of my salvation be exalted.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** It is God that avengeth me, and subdueth the people under me.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** He delivereth me from mine enemies: yea, thou liftest me up above those that rise up against me: thou hast delivered me from the violent man.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** Therefore will I give thanks unto thee, O Lord, among the heathen, and sing praises unto thy name.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** Great deliverance giveth he to his king; and sheweth mercy to his anointed, to David, and to his seed for evermore.
