# Psalms 18 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I love you, Yahweh, my strength.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Yahweh is my rock, my fortress, and my deliverer; my God, my rock, in whom I take refuge; my shield, and the horn of my salvation, my high tower.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I call on Yahweh, who is worthy to be praised; and I am saved from my enemies.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The cords of death surrounded me. The floods of ungodliness made me afraid.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The cords of Sheol were around me. The snares of death came on me.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** In my distress I called on Yahweh, and cried to my God. He heard my voice out of his temple. My cry before him came into his ears.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Then the earth shook and trembled. The foundations also of the mountains quaked and were shaken, because he was angry.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Smoke went out of his nostrils. Consuming fire came out of his mouth. Coals were kindled by it.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He bowed the heavens also, and came down. Thick darkness was under his feet.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He rode on a cherub, and flew. Yes, he soared on the wings of the wind.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He made darkness his hiding place, his pavilion around him, darkness of waters, thick clouds of the skies.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** At the brightness before him his thick clouds passed, hailstones and coals of fire.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Yahweh also thundered in the sky. The Most High uttered his voice: hailstones and coals of fire.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He sent out his arrows, and scattered them; Yes, great lightning bolts, and routed them.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Then the channels of waters appeared. The foundations of the world were laid bare at your rebuke, Yahweh, at the blast of the breath of your nostrils.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He sent from on high. He took me. He drew me out of many waters.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He delivered me from my strong enemy, from those who hated me; for they were too mighty for me.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** They came on me in the day of my calamity, but Yahweh was my support.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He brought me out also into a large place. He delivered me, because he delighted in me.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Yahweh has rewarded me according to my righteousness. According to the cleanness of my hands has he recompensed me.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For I have kept the ways of Yahweh, and have not wickedly departed from my God.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For all his ordinances were before me. I didn’t put away his statutes from me.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** I was also blameless with him. I kept myself from my iniquity.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Therefore Yahweh has rewarded me according to my righteousness, according to the cleanness of my hands in his eyesight.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** With the merciful you will show yourself merciful. With the perfect man, you will show yourself perfect.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** With the pure, you will show yourself pure. With the crooked you will show yourself shrewd.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** For you will save the afflicted people, but the haughty eyes you will bring down.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** For you will light my lamp, Yahweh. My God will light up my darkness.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** For by you, I advance through a troop. By my God, I leap over a wall.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** As for God, his way is perfect. Yahweh’s word is tried. He is a shield to all those who take refuge in him.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For who is God, except Yahweh? Who is a rock, besides our God,

<a id="v32"></a>**32** the God who arms me with strength, and makes my way perfect?

<a id="v33"></a>**33** He makes my feet like deer’s feet, and sets me on my high places.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** He teaches my hands to war, so that my arms bend a bow of bronze.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** You have also given me the shield of your salvation. Your right hand sustains me. Your gentleness has made me great.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** You have enlarged my steps under me, My feet have not slipped.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** I will pursue my enemies, and overtake them. Neither will I turn again until they are consumed.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** I will strike them through, so that they will not be able to rise. They shall fall under my feet.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** For you have armed me with strength to the battle. You have subdued under me those who rose up against me.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** You have also made my enemies turn their backs to me, that I might cut off those who hate me.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** They cried, but there was no one to save; even to Yahweh, but he didn’t answer them.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** Then I beat them small as the dust before the wind. I cast them out as the mire of the streets.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** You have delivered me from the strivings of the people. You have made me the head of the nations. A people whom I have not known shall serve me.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** As soon as they hear of me they shall obey me. The foreigners shall submit themselves to me.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** The foreigners shall fade away, and shall come trembling out of their close places.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** Yahweh lives; and blessed be my rock. Exalted be the God of my salvation,

<a id="v47"></a>**47** even the God who executes vengeance for me, and subdues peoples under me.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** He rescues me from my enemies. Yes, you lift me up above those who rise up against me. You deliver me from the violent man.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** Therefore I will give thanks to you, Yahweh, among the nations, and will sing praises to your name.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** He gives great deliverance to his king, and shows loving kindness to his anointed, to David and to his seed, forever more.
