# Psalms 18 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I love thee, O Jehovah, my strength.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Jehovah is my rock, and my fortress, and my deliverer; My God, my rock, in whom I will take refuge; My shield, and the horn of my salvation, my high tower.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I will call upon Jehovah, who is worthy to be praised: So shall I be saved from mine enemies.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The cords of death compassed me, And the floods of ungodliness made me afraid.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The cords of Sheol were round about me; The snares of death came upon me.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** In my distress I called upon Jehovah, And cried unto my God: He heard my voice out of his temple, And my cry before him came into his ears.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Then the earth shook and trembled; The foundations also of the mountains quaked And were shaken, because he was wroth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** There went up a smoke out of his nostrils, And fire out of his mouth devoured: Coals were kindled by it.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He bowed the heavens also, and came down; And thick darkness was under his feet.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And he rode upon a cherub, and did fly; Yea, he soared upon the wings of the wind.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He made darkness his hiding-place, his pavilion round about him, Darkness of waters, thick clouds of the skies.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** At the brightness before him his thick clouds passed, Hailstones and coals of fire.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Jehovah also thundered in the heavens, And the Most High uttered his voice, Hailstones and coals of fire.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And he sent out his arrows, and scattered them; Yea, lightnings manifold, and discomfited them.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Then the channels of waters appeared, And the foundations of the world were laid bare, At thy rebuke, O Jehovah, At the blast of the breath of thy nostrils.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He sent from on high, he took me; He drew me out of many waters.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He delivered me from my strong enemy, And from them that hated me; for they were too mighty for me.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** They came upon me in the day of my calamity; But Jehovah was my stay.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He brought me forth also into a large place; He delivered me, because he delighted in me.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Jehovah hath rewarded me according to my righteousness; According to the cleanness of my hands hath he recompensed me.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For I have kept the ways of Jehovah, And have not wickedly departed from my God.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For all his ordinances were before me, And I put not away his statutes from me.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** I was also perfect with him, And I kept myself from mine iniquity.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Therefore hath Jehovah recompensed me according to my righteousness, According to the cleanness of my hands in his eyesight.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** With the merciful thou wilt show thyself merciful; With the perfect man thou wilt show thyself perfect;

<a id="v26"></a>**26** With the pure thou wilt show thyself pure; And with the perverse thou wilt show thyself froward.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** For thou wilt save the afflicted people; But the haughty eyes thou wilt bring down.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** For thou wilt light my lamp: Jehovah my God will lighten my darkness.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** For by thee I run upon a troop; And by my God do I leap over a wall.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** As for God, his way is perfect: The word of Jehovah is tried; He is a shield unto all them that take refuge in him.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For who is God, save Jehovah? And who is a rock, besides our God,

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The God that girdeth me with strength, And maketh my way perfect?

<a id="v33"></a>**33** He maketh my feet like hinds’ feet: And setteth me upon my high places.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** He teacheth my hands to war; So that mine arms do bend a bow of brass.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Thou hast also given me the shield of thy salvation; And thy right hand hath holden me up, And thy gentleness hath made me great.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Thou hast enlarged my steps under me, And my feet have not slipped.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** I will pursue mine enemies, and overtake them; Neither will I turn again till they are consumed.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** I will smite them through, so that they shall not be able to rise: They shall fall under my feet.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** For thou hast girded me with strength unto the battle: Thou hast subdued under me those that rose up against me.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** Thou hast also made mine enemies turn their backs unto me, That I might cut off them that hate me.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** They cried, but there was none to save; Even unto Jehovah, but he answered them not.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** Then did I beat them small as the dust before the wind; I did cast them out as the mire of the streets.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Thou hast delivered me from the strivings of the people; Thou hast made me the head of the nations: A people whom I have not known shall serve me.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** As soon as they hear of me they shall obey me; The foreigners shall submit themselves unto me.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** The foreigners shall fade away, And shall come trembling out of their close places.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** Jehovah liveth; and blessed be my rock; And exalted be the God of my salvation,

<a id="v47"></a>**47** Even the God that executeth vengeance for me, And subdueth peoples under me.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** He rescueth me from mine enemies; Yea, thou liftest me up above them that rise up against me; Thou deliverest me from the violent man.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** Therefore I will give thanks unto thee, O Jehovah, among the nations, And will sing praises unto thy name.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** Great deliverance giveth he to his king, And showeth lovingkindness to his anointed, To David and to his seed, for evermore.
