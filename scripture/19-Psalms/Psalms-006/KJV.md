# Psalms 6 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Lord, rebuke me not in thine anger, neither chasten me in thy hot displeasure.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Have mercy upon me, O Lord; for I am weak: O Lord, heal me; for my bones are vexed.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** My soul is also sore vexed: but thou, O Lord, how long?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Return, O Lord, deliver my soul: oh save me for thy mercies’ sake.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For in death there is no remembrance of thee: in the grave who shall give thee thanks?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I am weary with my groaning; all the night make I my bed to swim; I water my couch with my tears.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Mine eye is consumed because of grief; it waxeth old because of all mine enemies.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Depart from me, all ye workers of iniquity; for the Lord hath heard the voice of my weeping.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The Lord hath heard my supplication; the Lord will receive my prayer.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Let all mine enemies be ashamed and sore vexed: let them return and be ashamed suddenly.
