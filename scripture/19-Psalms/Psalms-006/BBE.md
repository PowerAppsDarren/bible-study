# Psalms 6 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Lord, do not be bitter with me in your wrath; do not send punishment on me in the heat of your passion.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Have mercy on me, O Lord, for I am wasted away: make me well, for even my bones are troubled.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** My soul is in bitter trouble; and you, O Lord, how long?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Come back, O Lord, make my soul free; O give me salvation because of your mercy.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For in death there is no memory of you; in the underworld who will give you praise?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The voice of my sorrow is a weariness to me; all the night I make my bed wet with weeping; it is watered by the drops flowing from my eyes.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** My eyes are wasting away with trouble; they are becoming old because of all those who are against me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Go from me, all you workers of evil; for the Lord has given ear to the voice of my weeping.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The Lord has given ear to my request; the Lord has let my prayer come before him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Let all those who are against me be shamed and deeply troubled; let them be turned back and suddenly put to shame.
