# Psalms 6 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Jehovah, rebuke me not in thine anger, Neither chasten me in thy hot displeasure.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Have mercy upon me, O Jehovah; for I am withered away: O Jehovah, heal me; for my bones are troubled.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** My soul also is sore troubled: And thou, O Jehovah, how long?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Return, O Jehovah, deliver my soul: Save me for thy lovingkindness’ sake.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For in death there is no remembrance of thee: In Sheol who shall give thee thanks?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I am weary with my groaning; Every night make I my bed to swim; I water my couch with my tears.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Mine eye wasteth away because of grief; It waxeth old because of all mine adversaries.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Depart from me, all ye workers of iniquity; For Jehovah hath heard the voice of my weeping.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Jehovah hath heard my supplication; Jehovah will receive my prayer.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All mine enemies shall be put to shame and sore troubled: They shall turn back, they shall be put to shame suddenly.
