# Psalms 96 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Sing to Yahweh a new song! Sing to Yahweh, all the earth.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Sing to Yahweh! Bless his name! Proclaim his salvation from day to day!

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Declare his glory among the nations, his marvelous works among all the peoples.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For great is Yahweh, and greatly to be praised! He is to be feared above all gods.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For all the gods of the peoples are idols, but Yahweh made the heavens.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Honor and majesty are before him. Strength and beauty are in his sanctuary.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Ascribe to Yahweh, you families of nations, ascribe to Yahweh glory and strength.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Ascribe to Yahweh the glory due to his name. Bring an offering, and come into his courts.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Worship Yahweh in holy array. Tremble before him, all the earth.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Say among the nations, “Yahweh reigns.” The world is also established. It can’t be moved. He will judge the peoples with equity.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice. Let the sea roar, and its fullness!

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Let the field and all that is in it exult! Then all the trees of the woods shall sing for joy

<a id="v13"></a>**13** before Yahweh; for he comes, for he comes to judge the earth. He will judge the world with righteousness, the peoples with his truth.
