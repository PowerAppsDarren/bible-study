# Psalms 96 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O sing unto the Lord a new song: sing unto the Lord, all the earth.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Sing unto the Lord, bless his name; shew forth his salvation from day to day.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Declare his glory among the heathen, his wonders among all people.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For the Lord is great, and greatly to be praised: he is to be feared above all gods.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For all the gods of the nations are idols: but the Lord made the heavens.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Honour and majesty are before him: strength and beauty are in his sanctuary.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Give unto the Lord, O ye kindreds of the people, give unto the Lord glory and strength.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Give unto the Lord the glory due unto his name: bring an offering, and come into his courts.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** O worship the Lord in the beauty of holiness: fear before him, all the earth.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Say among the heathen that the Lord reigneth: the world also shall be established that it shall not be moved: he shall judge the people righteously.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let the heavens rejoice, and let the earth be glad; let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Let the field be joyful, and all that is therein: then shall all the trees of the wood rejoice

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Before the Lord: for he cometh, for he cometh to judge the earth: he shall judge the world with righteousness, and the people with his truth.
