# Psalms 96 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Oh sing unto Jehovah a new song: Sing unto Jehovah, all the earth.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Sing unto Jehovah, bless his name; Show forth his salvation from day to day.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Declare his glory among the nations, His marvellous works among all the peoples.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For great is Jehovah, and greatly to be praised: He is to be feared above all gods.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For all the gods of the peoples are idols; But Jehovah made the heavens.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Honor and majesty are before him: Strength and beauty are in his sanctuary.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Ascribe unto Jehovah, ye kindreds of the peoples, Ascribe unto Jehovah glory and strength.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Ascribe unto Jehovah the glory due unto his name: Bring an offering, and come into his courts.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Oh worship Jehovah in holy array: Tremble before him, all the earth.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Say among the nations, Jehovah reigneth: The world also is established that it cannot be moved: He will judge the peoples with equity.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice; Let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Let the field exult, and all that is therein;  Then shall all the trees of the wood sing for joy

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Before Jehovah; for he cometh, For he cometh to judge the earth: He will judge the world with righteousness, And the peoples with his truth.
