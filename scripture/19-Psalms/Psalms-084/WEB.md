# Psalms 84 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** How lovely are your dwellings, Yahweh of Armies!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My soul longs, and even faints for the courts of Yahweh. My heart and my flesh cry out for the living God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yes, the sparrow has found a home, and the swallow a nest for herself, where she may have her young, near your altars, Yahweh of Armies, my King, and my God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Blessed are those who dwell in your house. They are always praising you. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Blessed are those whose strength is in you; who have set their hearts on a pilgrimage.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Passing through the valley of Weeping, they make it a place of springs. Yes, the autumn rain covers it with blessings.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They go from strength to strength. Everyone of them appears before God in Zion.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yahweh, God of Armies, hear my prayer. Listen, God of Jacob. Selah.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Behold, God our shield, look at the face of your anointed.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For a day in your courts is better than a thousand. I would rather be a doorkeeper in the house of my God, than to dwell in the tents of wickedness.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For Yahweh God is a sun and a shield. Yahweh will give grace and glory. He withholds no good thing from those who walk blamelessly.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yahweh of Armies, blessed is the man who trusts in you.
