# Psalms 84 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** How amiable are thy tabernacles, O Jehovah of hosts!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My soul longeth, yea, even fainteth for the courts of Jehovah; My heart and my flesh cry out unto the living God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yea, the sparrow hath found her a house, And the swallow a nest for herself, where she may lay her young, Even thine altars, O Jehovah of hosts, My King, and my God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Blessed are they that dwell in thy house: They will be still praising thee. [Selah

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Blessed is the man whose strength is in thee; In whose heart are the highways to Zion.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Passing through the valley of Weeping they make it a place of springs; Yea, the early rain covereth it with blessings.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They go from strength to strength; Every one of them appeareth before God in Zion.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** O Jehovah God of hosts, hear my prayer; Give ear, O God of Jacob. [Selah

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Behold, O God our shield, And look upon the face of thine anointed.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For a day in thy courts is better than a thousand. I had rather be a doorkeeper in the house of my God, Than to dwell in the tents of wickedness.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For Jehovah God is a sun and a shield: Jehovah will give grace and glory; No good thing will he withhold from them that walk uprightly.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** O Jehovah of hosts, Blessed is the man that trusteth in thee.
