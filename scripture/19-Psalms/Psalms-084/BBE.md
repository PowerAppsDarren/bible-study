# Psalms 84 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** How dear are your tents, O Lord of armies!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The passion of my soul's desire is for the house of the Lord; my heart and my flesh are crying out for the living God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The little birds have places for themselves, where they may put their young, even your altars, O Lord of armies, my King and my God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Happy are they whose resting-place is in your house: they will still be praising you. (Selah.)

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Happy is the man whose strength is in you; in whose heart are the highways to Zion.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Going through the valley of balsam-trees, they make it a place of springs; it is clothed with blessings by the early rain.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They go from strength to strength; every one of them comes before God in Zion.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** O Lord God of armies, let my prayer come to you: give ear, O God of Jacob. (Selah.)

<a id="v9"></a>**9** O God, let your eyes be on him who is our safe cover, and let your heart be turned to your king.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For a day in your house is better than a thousand. It is better to be a door-keeper in the house of my God, than to be living in the tents of sin.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The Lord God is our sun and our strength: the Lord will give grace and glory: he will not keep back any good thing from those whose ways are upright.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** O Lord of armies, happy is the man whose hope is in you.
