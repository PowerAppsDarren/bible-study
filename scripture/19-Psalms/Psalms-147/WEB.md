# Psalms 147 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise Yah, for it is good to sing praises to our God; for it is pleasant and fitting to praise him.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Yahweh builds up Jerusalem. He gathers together the outcasts of Israel.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He heals the broken in heart, and binds up their wounds.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He counts the number of the stars. He calls them all by their names.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Great is our Lord, and mighty in power. His understanding is infinite.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Yahweh upholds the humble. He brings the wicked down to the ground.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Sing to Yahweh with thanksgiving. Sing praises on the harp to our God,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** who covers the sky with clouds, who prepares rain for the earth, who makes grass grow on the mountains.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He provides food for the livestock, and for the young ravens when they call.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He doesn’t delight in the strength of the horse. He takes no pleasure in the legs of a man.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Yahweh takes pleasure in those who fear him, in those who hope in his loving kindness.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Praise Yahweh, Jerusalem! Praise your God, Zion!

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For he has strengthened the bars of your gates. He has blessed your children within you.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He makes peace in your borders. He fills you with the finest of the wheat.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He sends out his commandment to the earth. His word runs very swiftly.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He gives snow like wool, and scatters frost like ashes.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He hurls down his hail like pebbles. Who can stand before his cold?

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He sends out his word, and melts them. He causes his wind to blow, and the waters flow.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He shows his word to Jacob; his statutes and his ordinances to Israel.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He has not done this for just any nation. They don’t know his ordinances. Praise Yah!
