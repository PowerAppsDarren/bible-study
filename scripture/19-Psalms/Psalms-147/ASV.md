# Psalms 147 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye Jehovah; For it is good to sing praises unto our God; For it is pleasant, andpraise is comely.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Jehovah doth build up Jerusalem; He gathereth together the outcasts of Israel.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He healeth the broken in heart, And bindeth up their wounds.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He counteth the number of the stars; He calleth them all by their names.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Great is our Lord, and mighty in power; His understanding is infinite.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Jehovah upholdeth the meek: He bringeth the wicked down to the ground.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Sing unto Jehovah with thanksgiving; Sing praises upon the harp unto our God,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Who covereth the heavens with clouds, Who prepareth rain for the earth, Who maketh grass to grow upon the mountains.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He giveth to the beast his food, Andto the young ravens which cry.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He delighteth not in the strength of the horse: He taketh no pleasure in the legs of a man.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Jehovah taketh pleasure in them that fear him, In those that hope in his lovingkindness.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Praise Jehovah, O Jerusalem; Praise thy God, O Zion.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For he hath strengthened the bars of thy gates; He hath blessed thy children within thee.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He maketh peace in thy borders; He filleth thee with the finest of the wheat.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He sendeth out his commandment upon earth; His word runneth very swiftly.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He giveth snow like wool; He scattereth the hoar-frost like ashes.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He casteth forth his ice like morsels: Who can stand before his cold?

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He sendeth out his word, and melteth them: He causeth his wind to blow, and the waters flow.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He showeth his word unto Jacob, His statutes and his ordinances unto Israel.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He hath not dealt so with any nation; And as for his ordinances, they have not known them. Praise ye Jehovah.
