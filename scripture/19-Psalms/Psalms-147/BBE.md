# Psalms 147 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give praise to the Lord; for it is good to make melody to our God; praise is pleasing and beautiful.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The Lord is building up Jerusalem; he makes all the outlaws of Israel come together.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He makes the broken-hearted well, and puts oil on their wounds.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He sees the number of the stars; he gives them all their names.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Great is our Lord, and great his power; there is no limit to his wisdom.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The Lord gives help to the poor in spirit; but he sends sinners down in shame.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Make songs of praise to the Lord; make melody to our God with instruments of music.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** By his hand the heaven is covered with clouds and rain is stored up for the earth; he makes the grass tall on the mountains.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He gives food to every beast, and to the young ravens in answer to their cry.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He has no delight in the strength of a horse; he takes no pleasure in the legs of a man.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The Lord takes pleasure in his worshippers, and in those whose hope is in his mercy.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Give praise to the Lord, O Jerusalem; give praise to your God, O Zion.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He has made strong the iron bands of your doors; he has sent blessings on your children inside your walls.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He gives peace in all your land, making your stores full of fat grain.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He sends out his orders to the earth; his word goes out quickly.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He gives snow like wool; he sends out ice-drops like dust.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He sends down ice like raindrops: water is made hard by his cold.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** At the outgoing of his word, the ice is turned to water; when he sends out his wind, there is a flowing of waters.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He makes his word clear to Jacob, teaching Israel his laws and his decisions.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He has not done these things for any other nation: and as for his laws, they have no knowledge of them. Let the Lord be praised.
