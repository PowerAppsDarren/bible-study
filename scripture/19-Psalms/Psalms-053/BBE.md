# Psalms 53 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The foolish man has said in his heart, God will not do anything. They are unclean, they have done evil works; there is not one who does good.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** God was looking down from heaven on the children of men, to see if there were any who had wisdom, searching after God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Every one of them has gone back; they are unclean: there is not one who does good, no, not one.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Have the workers of evil no knowledge? they take my people for food, as they would take bread; they make no prayer to God.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They were in great fear, where there was no cause for fear: for the bones of those who make war on you have been broken by God; you have put them to shame, because God has no desire for them.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** May the salvation of Israel come out of Zion! When the fate of his people is changed by God, Jacob will have joy, and Israel will be glad.
