# Psalms 125 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Those whose hope is in the Lord are like the mountain of Zion, which may not be moved, but keeps its place for ever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** As the mountains are round about Jerusalem, so the Lord is round about his people, from this time and for ever.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For the rod of sinners will not be resting on the heritage of the upright; so that the upright may not put out their hands to evil.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Do good, O Lord, to those who are good, and to those who are upright in heart.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But as for such as are turned out of the straight way, the Lord will take them away with the workers of evil. Let peace be on Israel.
