# Psalms 125 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Those who trust in Yahweh are as Mount Zion, which can’t be moved, but remains forever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** As the mountains surround Jerusalem, so Yahweh surrounds his people from this time forward and forever more.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For the scepter of wickedness won’t remain over the allotment of the righteous; so that the righteous won’t use their hands to do evil.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Do good, Yahweh, to those who are good, to those who are upright in their hearts.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But as for those who turn aside to their crooked ways, Yahweh will lead them away with the workers of iniquity. Peace be on Israel.
