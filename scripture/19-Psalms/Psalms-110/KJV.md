# Psalms 110 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The Lord said unto my Lord, Sit thou at my right hand, until I make thine enemies thy footstool.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The Lord shall send the rod of thy strength out of Zion: rule thou in the midst of thine enemies.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thy people shall be willing in the day of thy power, in the beauties of holiness from the womb of the morning: thou hast the dew of thy youth.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The Lord hath sworn, and will not repent, Thou art a priest for ever after the order of Melchizedek.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The Lord at thy right hand shall strike through kings in the day of his wrath.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He shall judge among the heathen, he shall fill the places with the dead bodies; he shall wound the heads over many countries.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He shall drink of the brook in the way: therefore shall he lift up the head.
