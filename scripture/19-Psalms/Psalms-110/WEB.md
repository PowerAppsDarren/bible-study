# Psalms 110 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh says to my Lord, “Sit at my right hand, until I make your enemies your footstool for your feet.”

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Yahweh will send out the rod of your strength out of Zion. Rule in the midst of your enemies.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Your people offer themselves willingly in the day of your power, in holy array. Out of the womb of the morning, you have the dew of your youth.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Yahweh has sworn, and will not change his mind: “You are a priest forever in the order of Melchizedek.”

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The Lord is at your right hand. He will crush kings in the day of his wrath.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He will judge among the nations. He will heap up dead bodies. He will crush the ruler of the whole earth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He will drink of the brook on the way; therefore he will lift up his head.
