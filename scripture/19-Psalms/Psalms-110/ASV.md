# Psalms 110 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Jehovah saith unto my Lord, Sit thou at my right hand, Until I make thine enemies thy footstool.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Jehovah will send forth the rod of thy strength out of Zion: Rule thou in the midst of thine enemies.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thy people offer themselves willingly In the day of thy power, in holy array: Out of the womb of the morning Thou hast the dew of thy youth.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Jehovah hath sworn, and will not repent: Thou art a priest for ever After the order of Melchizedek.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The Lord at thy right hand Will strike through kings in the day of his wrath.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He will judge among the nations, He will fillthe placeswith dead bodies; He will strike through the head in many countries.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He will drink of the brook in the way: Therefore will he lift up the head.
