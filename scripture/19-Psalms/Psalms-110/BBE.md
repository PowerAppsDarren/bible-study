# Psalms 110 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The Lord said to my lord, Be seated at my right hand, till I put all those who are against you under your feet.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The Lord will send out the rod of your strength from Zion; be king over your haters.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Your people give themselves gladly in the day of your power; like the dew of the morning on the holy mountains is the army of your young men.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The Lord has made an oath, and will not take it back. You are a priest for ever, after the order of Melchizedek.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** In the day of his wrath kings will be wounded by the Lord at your right hand.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He will be judge among the nations, the valleys will be full of dead bodies; the head over a great country will be wounded by him.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He will take of the stream by the way; so his head will be lifted up.
