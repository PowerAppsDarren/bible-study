# Psalms 48 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Great is the Lord and greatly to be praised, in the town of our God, in his holy mountain.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Beautiful in its high position, the joy of all the earth, is the mountain of Zion, the mountain of God, the town of the great King.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** In its buildings God is seen to be a high tower.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For see! the kings came together by agreement, they were joined together.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They saw it, and so were full of wonder; they were troubled, and went quickly away in fear.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Shaking came on them and pain, as on a woman in childbirth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** By you the ships of Tarshish are broken as by an east wind.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** As it came to our ears so have we seen it, in the town of the Lord of armies, in the town of our God; God will keep it fixed for ever. (Selah.)

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Our thoughts were of your mercy, O God, while we were in your Temple.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** As your name is, O God, so is your praise to the ends of the earth; your right hand is full of righteousness.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let there be joy in the mountain of Zion, and let the daughters of Judah be glad, because of your wise decisions.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Make your way about Zion, and go round it, numbering its towers.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Take note of its strong walls, looking well at its fair buildings; so that you may give word of it to the generation which comes after.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Because this God is our God for ever and ever: he will be our guide.
