# Psalms 48 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Great is Jehovah, and greatly to be praised, In the city of our God, in his holy mountain.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Beautiful in elevation, the joy of the whole earth, Is mount Zion, on the sides of the north, The city of the great King.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** God hath made himself known in her palaces for a refuge.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For, lo, the kings assembled themselves, They passed by together.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They saw it, then were they amazed; They were dismayed, they hasted away.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Trembling took hold of them there, Pain, as of a woman in travail.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** With the east wind Thou breakest the ships of Tarshish.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** As we have heard, so have we seen In the city of Jehovah of hosts, in the city of our God: God will establish it for ever. [Selah

<a id="v9"></a>**9** We have thought on thy lovingkindness, O God, In the midst of thy temple.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** As is thy name, O God, So is thy praise unto the ends of the earth: Thy right hand is full of righteousness.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let mount Zion be glad, Let the daughters of Judah rejoice, Because of thy judgments.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Walk about Zion, and go round about her; Number the towers thereof;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Mark ye well her bulwarks; Consider her palaces: That ye may tell it to the generation following.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For this God is our God for ever and ever: He will be our guide even unto death.
