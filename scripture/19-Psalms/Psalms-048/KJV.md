# Psalms 48 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Great is the Lord, and greatly to be praised in the city of our God, in the mountain of his holiness.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Beautiful for situation, the joy of the whole earth, is mount Zion, on the sides of the north, the city of the great King.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** God is known in her palaces for a refuge.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For, lo, the kings were assembled, they passed by together.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They saw it, and so they marvelled; they were troubled, and hasted away.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Fear took hold upon them there, and pain, as of a woman in travail.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Thou breakest the ships of Tarshish with an east wind.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** As we have heard, so have we seen in the city of the Lord of hosts, in the city of our God: God will establish it for ever. Selah.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** We have thought of thy lovingkindness, O God, in the midst of thy temple.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** According to thy name, O God, so is thy praise unto the ends of the earth: thy right hand is full of righteousness.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let mount Zion rejoice, let the daughters of Judah be glad, because of thy judgments.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Walk about Zion, and go round about her: tell the towers thereof.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Mark ye well her bulwarks, consider her palaces; that ye may tell it to the generation following.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For this God is our God for ever and ever: he will be our guide even unto death.
