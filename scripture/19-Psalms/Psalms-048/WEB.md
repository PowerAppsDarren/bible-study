# Psalms 48 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Great is Yahweh, and greatly to be praised, in the city of our God, in his holy mountain.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Beautiful in elevation, the joy of the whole earth, is Mount Zion, on the north sides, the city of the great King.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** God has shown himself in her citadels as a refuge.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For, behold, the kings assembled themselves, they passed by together.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They saw it, then they were amazed. They were dismayed. They hurried away.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Trembling took hold of them there, pain, as of a woman in travail.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** With the east wind, you break the ships of Tarshish.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** As we have heard, so we have seen, in the city of Yahweh of Armies, in the city of our God. God will establish it forever. Selah.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** We have thought about your loving kindness, God, in the midst of your temple.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** As is your name, God, so is your praise to the ends of the earth. Your right hand is full of righteousness.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let Mount Zion be glad! Let the daughters of Judah rejoice, Because of your judgments.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Walk about Zion, and go around her. Number its towers.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Mark well her bulwarks. Consider her palaces, that you may tell it to the next generation.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For this God is our God forever and ever. He will be our guide even to death.
