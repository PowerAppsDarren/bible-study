# Psalms 29 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Ascribe unto Jehovah, O ye sons of the mighty, Ascribe unto Jehovah glory and strength.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Ascribe unto Jehovah the glory due unto his name; Worship Jehovah in holy array.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The voice of Jehovah is upon the waters: The God of glory thundereth, Even Jehovah upon many waters.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The voice of Jehovah is powerful; The voice of Jehovah is full of majesty.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The voice of Jehovah breaketh the cedars; Yea, Jehovah breaketh in pieces the cedars of Lebanon.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He maketh them also to skip like a calf; Lebanon and Sirion like a young wild-ox.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The voice of Jehovah cleaveth the flames of fire.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The voice of Jehovah shaketh the wilderness; Jehovah shaketh the wilderness of Kadesh.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The voice of Jehovah maketh the hinds to calve, And strippeth the forests bare: And in his temple everything saith, Glory.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Jehovah satas Kingat the Flood; Yea, Jehovah sitteth as King for ever.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Jehovah will give strength unto his people; Jehovah will bless his people with peace.
