# Psalms 29 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give unto the Lord, O ye mighty, give unto the Lord glory and strength.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Give unto the Lord the glory due unto his name; worship the Lord in the beauty of holiness.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The voice of the Lord is upon the waters: the God of glory thundereth: the Lord is upon many waters.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The voice of the Lord is powerful; the voice of the Lord is full of majesty.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The voice of the Lord breaketh the cedars; yea, the Lord breaketh the cedars of Lebanon.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He maketh them also to skip like a calf; Lebanon and Sirion like a young unicorn.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The voice of the Lord divideth the flames of fire.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The voice of the Lord shaketh the wilderness; the Lord shaketh the wilderness of Kadesh.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The voice of the Lord maketh the hinds to calve, and discovereth the forests: and in his temple doth every one speak of his glory.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The Lord sitteth upon the flood; yea, the Lord sitteth King for ever.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The Lord will give strength unto his people; the Lord will bless his people with peace.
