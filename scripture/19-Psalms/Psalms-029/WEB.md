# Psalms 29 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Ascribe to Yahweh, you sons of the mighty, ascribe to Yahweh glory and strength.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Ascribe to Yahweh the glory due to his name. Worship Yahweh in holy array.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yahweh’s voice is on the waters. The God of glory thunders, even Yahweh on many waters.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Yahweh’s voice is powerful. Yahweh’s voice is full of majesty.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The voice of Yahweh breaks the cedars. Yes, Yahweh breaks in pieces the cedars of Lebanon.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He makes them also to skip like a calf; Lebanon and Sirion like a young, wild ox.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Yahweh’s voice strikes with flashes of lightning.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yahweh’s voice shakes the wilderness. Yahweh shakes the wilderness of Kadesh.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Yahweh’s voice makes the deer calve, and strips the forests bare. In his temple everything says, “Glory!”

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Yahweh sat enthroned at the Flood. Yes, Yahweh sits as King forever.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Yahweh will give strength to his people. Yahweh will bless his people with peace.
