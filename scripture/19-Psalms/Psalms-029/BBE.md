# Psalms 29 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give to the Lord, you sons of the gods, give to the Lord glory and strength.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Give to the Lord the full glory of his name; give him worship in holy robes.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The voice of the Lord is on the waters: the God of glory is thundering, the Lord is on the great waters.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The voice of the Lord is full of power; the voice of the Lord has a noble sound.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** By the voice of the Lord are the cedar-trees broken, even the cedars of Lebanon are broken by the Lord.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He makes them go jumping about like a young ox; Lebanon and Sirion like a young mountain ox.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** At the voice of the Lord flames of fire are seen.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** At the voice of the Lord there is a shaking in the waste land, even a shaking in the waste land of Kadesh.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** At the voice of the Lord the roes give birth, the leaves are taken from the trees: in his Temple everything says, Glory.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The Lord had his seat as king when the waters came on the earth; the Lord is seated as king for ever.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The Lord will give strength to his people; the Lord will give his people the blessing of peace.
