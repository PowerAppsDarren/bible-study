# Psalms 47 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Oh clap your hands, all you nations. Shout to God with the voice of triumph!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For Yahweh Most High is awesome. He is a great King over all the earth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He subdues nations under us, and peoples under our feet.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He chooses our inheritance for us, the glory of Jacob whom he loved. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** God has gone up with a shout, Yahweh with the sound of a trumpet.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Sing praise to God, sing praises. Sing praises to our King, sing praises.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For God is the King of all the earth. Sing praises with understanding.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** God reigns over the nations. God sits on his holy throne.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The princes of the peoples are gathered together, the people of the God of Abraham. For the shields of the earth belong to God. He is greatly exalted!
