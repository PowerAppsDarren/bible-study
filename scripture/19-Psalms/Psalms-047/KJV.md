# Psalms 47 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O clap your hands, all ye people; shout unto God with the voice of triumph.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For the Lord most high is terrible; he is a great King over all the earth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He shall subdue the people under us, and the nations under our feet.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He shall choose our inheritance for us, the excellency of Jacob whom he loved. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** God is gone up with a shout, the Lord with the sound of a trumpet.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Sing praises to God, sing praises: sing praises unto our King, sing praises.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For God is the King of all the earth: sing ye praises with understanding.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** God reigneth over the heathen: God sitteth upon the throne of his holiness.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The princes of the people are gathered together, even the people of the God of Abraham: for the shields of the earth belong unto God: he is greatly exalted.
