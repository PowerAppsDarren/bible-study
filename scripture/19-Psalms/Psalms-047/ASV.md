# Psalms 47 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Oh clap your hands, all ye peoples; Shout unto God with the voice of triumph.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For Jehovah Most High is terrible; He is a great King over all the earth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He subdueth peoples under us, And nations under our feet.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He chooseth our inheritance for us, The glory of Jacob whom he loved. [Selah

<a id="v5"></a>**5** God is gone up with a shout, Jehovah with the sound of a trumpet.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Sing praises to God, sing praises: Sing praises unto our King, sing praises.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For God is the King of all the earth: Sing ye praises with understanding.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** God reigneth over the nations: God sitteth upon his holy throne.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The princes of the peoples are gathered together To bethe people of the God of Abraham; For the shields of the earth belong unto God: He is greatly exalted.
