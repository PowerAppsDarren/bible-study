# Psalms 58 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Do you indeed speak righteousness, silent ones? Do you judge blamelessly, you sons of men?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** No, in your heart you plot injustice. You measure out the violence of your hands in the earth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The wicked go astray from the womb. They are wayward as soon as they are born, speaking lies.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their poison is like the poison of a snake; like a deaf cobra that stops its ear,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** which doesn’t listen to the voice of charmers, no matter how skillful the charmer may be.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Break their teeth, God, in their mouth. Break out the great teeth of the young lions, Yahweh.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Let them vanish like water that flows away. When they draw the bow, let their arrows be made blunt.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let them be like a snail which melts and passes away, like the stillborn child, who has not seen the sun.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Before your pots can feel the heat of the thorns, he will sweep away the green and the burning alike.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The righteous shall rejoice when he sees the vengeance. He shall wash his feet in the blood of the wicked;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** so that men shall say, “Most certainly there is a reward for the righteous. Most certainly there is a God who judges the earth.”
