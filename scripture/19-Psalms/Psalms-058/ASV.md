# Psalms 58 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Do ye indeed in silence speak righteousness? Do ye judge uprightly, O ye sons of men?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Nay, in heart ye work wickedness; Ye weigh out the violence of your hands in the earth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The wicked are estranged from the womb: They go astray as soon as they are born, speaking lies.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their poison is like the poison of a serpent: They are like the deaf adder that stoppeth her ear,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Which hearkeneth not to the voice of charmers, Charming never so wisely.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Break their teeth, O God, in their mouth: Break out the great teeth of the young lions, O Jehovah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Let them melt away as water that runneth apace: When he aimeth his arrows, let them be as though they were cut off.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let them beas a snail which melteth and passeth away, Likethe untimely birth of a woman, that hath not seen the sun.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Before your pots can feel the thorns, He will take them away with a whirlwind, the green and the burning alike.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The righteous shall rejoice when he seeth the vengeance: He shall wash his feet in the blood of the wicked;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** So that men shall say, Verily there is a reward for the righteous: Verily there is a God that judgeth in the earth.
