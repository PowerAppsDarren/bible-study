# Psalms 58 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Is there righteousness in your mouths, O you gods? are you upright judges, O you sons of men?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The purposes of your hearts are evil; your hands are full of cruel doings on the earth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The evil-doers are strange from the first; from the hour of their birth they go out of the true way, saying false words.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their poison is like the poison of a snake; they are like the adder, whose ears are shut;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Who will not be moved by the voice of the wonder-worker, however great are his powers.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** O God, let their teeth be broken in their mouths; let the great teeth of the young lions be pulled out, O Lord.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Let them be turned to liquid like the ever-flowing waters; let them be cut off like the grass by the way.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let them be like an after-birth which is turned to water and comes to an end; like the fruit of a woman who gives birth before her time, let them not see the sun.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Before they are conscious of it, let them be cut down like thorns; let a strong wind take them away like waste growth.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The upright man will be glad when he sees their punishment; his feet will be washed in the blood of the evil-doer.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** So that men will say, Truly there is a reward for righteousness; truly there is a God who is judge on the earth.
