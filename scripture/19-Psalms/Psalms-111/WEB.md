# Psalms 111 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise Yah! I will give thanks to Yahweh with my whole heart, in the council of the upright, and in the congregation.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Yahweh’s works are great, pondered by all those who delight in them.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** His work is honor and majesty. His righteousness endures forever.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He has caused his wonderful works to be remembered. Yahweh is gracious and merciful.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He has given food to those who fear him. He always remembers his covenant.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He has shown his people the power of his works, in giving them the heritage of the nations.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The works of his hands are truth and justice. All his precepts are sure.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They are established forever and ever. They are done in truth and uprightness.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He has sent redemption to his people. He has ordained his covenant forever. His name is holy and awesome!

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The fear of Yahweh is the beginning of wisdom. All those who do his work have a good understanding. His praise endures forever!
