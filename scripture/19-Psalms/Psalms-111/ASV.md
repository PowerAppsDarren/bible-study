# Psalms 111 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye Jehovah. I will give thanks unto Jehovah with my whole heart, In the council of the upright, and in the congregation.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The works of Jehovah are great, Sought out of all them that have pleasure therein.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** His work is honor and majesty; And his righteousness endureth for ever.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He hath made his wonderful works to be remembered: Jehovah is gracious and merciful.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He hath given food unto them that fear him: He will ever be mindful of his covenant.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He hath showed his people the power of his works, In giving them the heritage of the nations.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The works of his hands are truth and justice; All his precepts are sure.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They are established for ever and ever; They are done in truth and uprightness.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He hath sent redemption unto his people; He hath commanded his covenant for ever: Holy and reverend is his name.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The fear of Jehovah is the beginning of wisdom; A good understanding have all they that dohis commandments: His praise endureth for ever.
