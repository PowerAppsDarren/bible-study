# Psalms 111 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Let the Lord be praised. I will give praise to the Lord with all my heart, among the upright, and in the meeting of the people.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The works of the Lord are great, searched out by all those who have delight in them.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** His work is full of honour and glory; and his righteousness is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Certain for ever is the memory of his wonders: the Lord is full of pity and mercy.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He has given food to his worshippers; he will keep his agreement in mind for ever.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He has made clear to his people the power of his works, giving them the heritage of the nations.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The works of his hands are faith and righteousness; all his laws are unchanging.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They are fixed for ever and ever, they are done in faith and righteousness.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He has sent salvation to his people; he has given his word for ever: holy is his name and greatly to be feared.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The fear of the Lord is the best part of wisdom: all those who keep his laws are wise: his praise is eternal.
