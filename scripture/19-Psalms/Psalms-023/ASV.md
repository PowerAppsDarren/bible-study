# Psalms 23 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Jehovah is my shepherd; I shall not want.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He maketh me to lie down in green pastures; He leadeth me beside still waters.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He restoreth my soul: He guideth me in the paths of righteousness for his name’s sake.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Yea, though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death, I will fear no evil; for thou art with me; Thy rod and thy staff, they comfort me.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thou preparest a table before me in the presence of mine enemies: Thou hast anointed my head with oil; My cup runneth over.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Surely goodness and lovingkindness shall follow me all the days of my life; And I shall dwell in the house of Jehovah for ever.
