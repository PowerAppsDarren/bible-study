# Psalms 106 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye Jehovah. Oh give thanks unto Jehovah; for he is good; For his lovingkindness endureth for ever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Who can utter the mighty acts of Jehovah, Or show forth all his praise?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Blessed are they that keep justice, And he that doeth righteousness at all times.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Remember me, O Jehovah, with the favor that thou bearest unto thy people; Oh visit me with thy salvation,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** That I may see the prosperity of thy chosen, That I may rejoice in the gladness of thy nation, That I may glory with thine inheritance.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** We have sinned with our fathers, We have committed iniquity, we have done wickedly.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Our fathers understood not thy wonders in Egypt; They remembered not the multitude of thy lovingkindnesses, But were rebellious at the sea, even at the Red Sea.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Nevertheless he saved them for his name’s sake, That he might make his mighty power to be known.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He rebuked the Red Sea also, and it was dried up: So he led them through the depths, as through a wilderness.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And he saved them from the hand of him that hated them, And redeemed them from the hand of the enemy.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And the waters covered their adversaries; There was not one of them left.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Then believed they his words; They sang his praise.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** They soon forgat his works; They waited not for his counsel,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But lusted exceedingly in the wilderness, And tempted God in the desert.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And he gave them their request, But sent leanness into their soul.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** They envied Moses also in the camp, AndAaron the saint of Jehovah.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The earth opened and swallowed up Dathan, And covered the company of Abiram.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And a fire was kindled in their company; The flame burned up the wicked.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** They made a calf in Horeb, And worshipped a molten image.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Thus they changed their glory For the likeness of an ox that eateth grass.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** They forgat God their Saviour, Who had done great things in Egypt,

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Wondrous works in the land of Ham, And terrible things by the Red Sea.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Therefore he said that he would destroy them, Had not Moses his chosen stood before him in the breach, To turn away his wrath, lest he should destroythem.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Yea, they despised the pleasant land, They believed not his word,

<a id="v25"></a>**25** But murmured in their tents, And hearkened not unto the voice of Jehovah.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Therefore he sware unto them, That he would overthrow them in the wilderness,

<a id="v27"></a>**27** And that he would overthrow their seed among the nations, And scatter them in the lands.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** They joined themselves also unto Baal-peor, And ate the sacrifices of the dead.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Thus they provoked him to anger with their doings; And the plague brake in upon them.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Then stood up Phinehas, and executed judgment; And so the plague was stayed.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** And that was reckoned unto him for righteousness, Unto all generations for evermore.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** They angered him also at the waters of Meribah, So that it went ill with Moses for their sakes;

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Because they were rebellious against his spirit, And he spake unadvisedly with his lips.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** They did not destroy the peoples, As Jehovah commanded them,

<a id="v35"></a>**35** But mingled themselves with the nations, And learned their works,

<a id="v36"></a>**36** And served their idols, Which became a snare unto them.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Yea, they sacrificed their sons and their daughters unto demons,

<a id="v38"></a>**38** And shed innocent blood, Even the blood of their sons and of their daughters, Whom they sacrificed unto the idols of Canaan; And the land was polluted with blood.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** Thus were they defiled with their works, And played the harlot in their doings.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** Therefore was the wrath of Jehovah kindled against his people, And he abhorred his inheritance.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** And he gave them into the hand of the nations; And they that hated them ruled over them.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** Their enemies also oppressed them, And they were brought into subjection under their hand.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Many times did he deliver them; But they were rebellious in their counsel, And were brought low in their iniquity.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** Nevertheless he regarded their distress, When he heard their cry:

<a id="v45"></a>**45** And he remembered for them his covenant, And repented according to the multitude of his lovingkindnesses.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** He made them also to be pitied Of all those that carried them captive.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** Save us, O Jehovah our God, And gather us from among the nations, To give thanks unto thy holy name, And to triumph in thy praise.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** Blessed be Jehovah, the God of Israel, From everlasting even to everlasting. And let all the people say, Amen. Praise ye Jehovah.
