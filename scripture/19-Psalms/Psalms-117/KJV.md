# Psalms 117 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O praise the Lord, all ye nations: praise him, all ye people.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For his merciful kindness is great toward us: and the truth of the Lord endureth for ever. Praise ye the Lord.
