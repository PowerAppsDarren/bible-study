# Psalms 135 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye the Lord. Praise ye the name of the Lord; praise him, O ye servants of the Lord.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Ye that stand in the house of the Lord, in the courts of the house of our God,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Praise the Lord; for the Lord is good: sing praises unto his name; for it is pleasant.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For the Lord hath chosen Jacob unto himself, and Israel for his peculiar treasure.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For I know that the Lord is great, and that our Lord is above all gods.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Whatsoever the Lord pleased, that did he in heaven, and in earth, in the seas, and all deep places.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He causeth the vapours to ascend from the ends of the earth; he maketh lightnings for the rain; he bringeth the wind out of his treasuries.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Who smote the firstborn of Egypt, both of man and beast.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Who sent tokens and wonders into the midst of thee, O Egypt, upon Pharaoh, and upon all his servants.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Who smote great nations, and slew mighty kings;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Sihon king of the Amorites, and Og king of Bashan, and all the kingdoms of Canaan:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And gave their land for an heritage, an heritage unto Israel his people.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thy name, O Lord, endureth for ever; and thy memorial, O Lord, throughout all generations.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For the Lord will judge his people, and he will repent himself concerning his servants.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The idols of the heathen are silver and gold, the work of men’s hands.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** They have mouths, but they speak not; eyes have they, but they see not;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** They have ears, but they hear not; neither is there any breath in their mouths.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** They that make them are like unto them: so is every one that trusteth in them.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Bless the Lord, O house of Israel: bless the Lord, O house of Aaron:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Bless the Lord, O house of Levi: ye that fear the Lord, bless the Lord.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Blessed be the Lord out of Zion, which dwelleth at Jerusalem. Praise ye the Lord.
