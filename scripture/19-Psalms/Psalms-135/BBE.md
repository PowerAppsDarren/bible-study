# Psalms 135 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Let the Lord be praised. O you servants of the Lord, give praise to the name of the Lord.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You who are in the house of the Lord, and in the open spaces of the house of our God,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Give praise to Jah, for he is good: make melody to his name, for it is pleasing.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For the Lord has taken Jacob for himself, and Israel for his property.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I know that the Lord is great, and that our Lord is greater than all other gods.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The Lord has done whatever was pleasing to him, in heaven, and on the earth, in the seas and in all the deep waters.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He makes the mists go up from the ends of the earth; he makes thunder-flames for the rain; he sends out the winds from his store-houses.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He put to death the first-fruits of Egypt, of man and of beast.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He sent signs and wonders among you, O Egypt, on Pharaoh, and on all his servants.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He overcame great nations, and put strong kings to death;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Sihon, king of the Amorites, and Og, king of Bashan, and all the kingdoms of Canaan;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And gave their land for a heritage, even for a heritage to Israel his people.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** O Lord, your name is eternal; and the memory of you will have no end.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For the Lord will be judge of his people's cause; his feelings will be changed to his servants.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The images of the nations are silver and gold, the work of men's hands.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** They have mouths, but no voice, they have eyes, but they do not see;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** They have ears, but no hearing; and there is no breath in their mouths.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Those who make them are like them; and so is everyone who puts his hope in them.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Give praise to the Lord, O children of Israel: give praise to the Lord, O sons of Aaron:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Give praise to the Lord, O sons of Levi: let all the worshippers of the Lord give him praise.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Praise be to the Lord out of Zion, even to the Lord whose house is in Jerusalem, Let the Lord be praised.
