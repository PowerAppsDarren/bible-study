# Psalms 135 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye Jehovah. Praise ye the name of Jehovah; Praisehim, O ye servants of Jehovah,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Ye that stand in the house of Jehovah, In the courts of the house of our God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Praise ye Jehovah; for Jehovah is good: Sing praises unto his name; for it is pleasant.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For Jehovah hath chosen Jacob unto himself, AndIsrael for his own possession.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For I know that Jehovah is great, And that our Lord is above all gods.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Whatsoever Jehovah pleased, that hath he done, In heaven and in earth, in the seas and in all deeps;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Who causeth the vapors to ascend from the ends of the earth; Who maketh lightnings for the rain; Who bringeth forth the wind out of his treasuries;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Who smote the first-born of Egypt, Both of man and beast;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Who sent signs and wonders into the midst of thee, O Egypt, Upon Pharaoh, and upon all his servants;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Who smote many nations, And slew mighty kings,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Sihon king of the Amorites, And Og king of Bashan, And all the kingdoms of Canaan,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And gave their land for a heritage, A heritage unto Israel his people.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thy name, O Jehovah, endurethfor ever; Thy memorial name, O Jehovah, throughout all generations.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For Jehovah will judge his people, And repent himself concerning his servants.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The idols of the nations are silver and gold, The work of men’s hands.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** They have mouths, but they speak not; Eyes have they, but they see not;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** They have ears, but they hear not; Neither is there any breath in their mouths.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** They that make them shall be like unto them; Yea, every one that trusteth in them.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** O house of Israel, bless ye Jehovah: O house of Aaron, bless ye Jehovah:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** O house of Levi, bless ye Jehovah: Ye that fear Jehovah, bless ye Jehovah.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Blessed be Jehovah out of Zion, Who dwelleth at Jerusalem. Praise ye Jehovah.
