# Psalms 135 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise Yah! Praise Yahweh’s name! Praise him, you servants of Yahweh,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** you who stand in Yahweh’s house, in the courts of our God’s house.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Praise Yah, for Yahweh is good. Sing praises to his name, for that is pleasant.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For Yah has chosen Jacob for himself; Israel for his own possession.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For I know that Yahweh is great, that our Lord is above all gods.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Whatever Yahweh pleased, that he has done, in heaven and in earth, in the seas and in all deeps;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** who causes the clouds to rise from the ends of the earth; who makes lightnings with the rain; who brings the wind out of his treasuries;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Who struck the firstborn of Egypt, both of man and animal;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Who sent signs and wonders into your midst, Egypt, on Pharaoh, and on all his servants;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** who struck many nations, and killed mighty kings,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Sihon king of the Amorites, Og king of Bashan, and all the kingdoms of Canaan,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** and gave their land for a heritage, a heritage to Israel, his people.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Your name, Yahweh, endures forever; your renown, Yahweh, throughout all generations.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For Yahweh will judge his people, and have compassion on his servants.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The idols of the nations are silver and gold, the work of men’s hands.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** They have mouths, but they can’t speak. They have eyes, but they can’t see.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** They have ears, but they can’t hear; neither is there any breath in their mouths.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Those who make them will be like them; yes, everyone who trusts in them.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** House of Israel, praise Yahweh! House of Aaron, praise Yahweh!

<a id="v20"></a>**20** House of Levi, praise Yahweh! You who fear Yahweh, praise Yahweh!

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Blessed be Yahweh from Zion, Who dwells at Jerusalem. Praise Yah!
