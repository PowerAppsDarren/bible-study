# Psalms 72 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give the king thy judgments, O God, and thy righteousness unto the king’s son.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He shall judge thy people with righteousness, and thy poor with judgment.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The mountains shall bring peace to the people, and the little hills, by righteousness.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He shall judge the poor of the people, he shall save the children of the needy, and shall break in pieces the oppressor.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They shall fear thee as long as the sun and moon endure, throughout all generations.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He shall come down like rain upon the mown grass: as showers that water the earth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** In his days shall the righteous flourish; and abundance of peace so long as the moon endureth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He shall have dominion also from sea to sea, and from the river unto the ends of the earth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** They that dwell in the wilderness shall bow before him; and his enemies shall lick the dust.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The kings of Tarshish and of the isles shall bring presents: the kings of Sheba and Seba shall offer gifts.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Yea, all kings shall fall down before him: all nations shall serve him.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For he shall deliver the needy when he crieth; the poor also, and him that hath no helper.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He shall spare the poor and needy, and shall save the souls of the needy.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He shall redeem their soul from deceit and violence: and precious shall their blood be in his sight.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And he shall live, and to him shall be given of the gold of Sheba: prayer also shall be made for him continually; and daily shall he be praised.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** There shall be an handful of corn in the earth upon the top of the mountains; the fruit thereof shall shake like Lebanon: and they of the city shall flourish like grass of the earth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** His name shall endure for ever: his name shall be continued as long as the sun: and men shall be blessed in him: all nations shall call him blessed.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Blessed be the Lord God, the God of Israel, who only doeth wondrous things.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And blessed be his glorious name for ever: and let the whole earth be filled with his glory; Amen, and Amen.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The prayers of David the son of Jesse are ended.
