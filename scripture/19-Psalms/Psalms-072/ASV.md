# Psalms 72 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give the king thy judgments, O God, And thy righteousness unto the king’s son.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He will judge thy people with righteousness, And thy poor with justice.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The mountains shall bring peace to the people, And the hills, in righteousness.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He will judge the poor of the people, He will save the children of the needy, And will break in pieces the oppressor.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They shall fear thee while the sun endureth, And so long as the moon, throughout all generations.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He will come down like rain upon the mown grass, As showers that water the earth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** In his days shall the righteous flourish, And abundance of peace, till the moon be no more.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He shall have dominion also from sea to sea, And from the River unto the ends of the earth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** They that dwell in the wilderness shall bow before him; And his enemies shall lick the dust.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The kings of Tarshish and of the isles shall render tribute: The kings of Sheba and Seba shall offer gifts.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Yea, all kings shall fall down before him; All nations shall serve him.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For he will deliver the needy when he crieth, And the poor, that hath no helper.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He will have pity on the poor and needy, And the souls of the needy he will save.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He will redeem their soul from oppression and violence; And precious will their blood be in his sight:

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And they shall live; and to him shall be given of the gold of Sheba: And men shall pray for him continually; They shall bless him all the day long.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** There shall be abundance of grain in the earth upon the top of the mountains; The fruit thereof shall shake like Lebanon: And they of the city shall flourish like grass of the earth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** His name shall endure for ever; His name shall be continued as long as the sun: And men shall be blessed in him; All nations shall call him happy.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Blessed be Jehovah God, the God of Israel, Who only doeth wondrous things:

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And blessed be his glorious name for ever; And let the whole earth be filled with his glory. Amen, and Amen.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The prayers of David the son of Jesse are ended.
