# Psalms 72 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give the king your authority, O God, and your righteousness to the king's son.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** May he be a judge of your people in righteousness, and make true decisions for the poor.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** May the mountains give peace to the people, and the hills righteousness.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** May he be a judge of the poor among the people, may he give salvation to the children of those who are in need; by him let the violent be crushed.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** May his life go on as long as the sun and moon, through all generations.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** May he come down like rain on the cut grass; like showers watering the earth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** In his days may the upright do well, living in peace as long as there is a moon in heaven.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let his kingdom be from sea to sea, from the River to the ends of the earth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let those who are against him go down before him; and let his haters be low in the dust.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Let the kings of Tarshish and of the islands come back with offerings; let the kings of Sheba and Seba give of their stores.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Yes, let all kings go down before him; let all nations be his servants.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For he will be a saviour to the poor in answer to his cry; and to him who is in need, without a helper.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He will have pity on the poor, and be the saviour of those who are in need.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He will keep their souls free from evil designs and violent attacks; and their blood will be of value in his eyes.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** May he have long life, and may gold from Sheba be given to him: may prayers be made for him at all times; may blessings be on him every day.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** May there be wide-stretching fields of grain in the land, shaking on the top of the mountains, full of fruit like Lebanon: may its stems be unnumbered like the grass of the earth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** May his name go on for ever, as long as the sun: may men be blessing themselves by him; may all nations be blessing his name.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Praise be to the Lord God, the God of Israel, the only doer of wonders.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Praise to the glory of his noble name for ever; let all the earth be full of his glory. So be it, So be it.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The prayers of David, the son of Jesse, are ended.
