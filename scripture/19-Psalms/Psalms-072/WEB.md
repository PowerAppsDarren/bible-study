# Psalms 72 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God, give the king your justice; your righteousness to the royal son.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He will judge your people with righteousness, and your poor with justice.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The mountains shall bring prosperity to the people. The hills bring the fruit of righteousness.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He will judge the poor of the people. He will save the children of the needy, and will break the oppressor in pieces.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They shall fear you while the sun endures; and as long as the moon, throughout all generations.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He will come down like rain on the mown grass, as showers that water the earth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** In his days, the righteous shall flourish, and abundance of peace, until the moon is no more.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He shall have dominion also from sea to sea, from the River to the ends of the earth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Those who dwell in the wilderness shall bow before him. His enemies shall lick the dust.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The kings of Tarshish and of the islands will bring tribute. The kings of Sheba and Seba shall offer gifts.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Yes, all kings shall fall down before him. All nations shall serve him.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For he will deliver the needy when he cries; the poor, who has no helper.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He will have pity on the poor and needy. He will save the souls of the needy.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He will redeem their soul from oppression and violence. Their blood will be precious in his sight.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** They shall live, and to him shall be given of the gold of Sheba. Men shall pray for him continually. They shall bless him all day long.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Abundance of grain shall be throughout the land. Its fruit sways like Lebanon. Let it flourish, thriving like the grass of the field.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** His name endures forever. His name continues as long as the sun. Men shall be blessed by him. All nations will call him blessed.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Praise be to Yahweh God, the God of Israel, who alone does marvelous deeds.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Blessed be his glorious name forever! Let the whole earth be filled with his glory! Amen and amen.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** This ends the prayers by David, the son of Jesse.
