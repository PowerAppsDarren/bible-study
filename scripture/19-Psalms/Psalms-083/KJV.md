# Psalms 83 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Keep not thou silence, O God: hold not thy peace, and be not still, O God.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For, lo, thine enemies make a tumult: and they that hate thee have lifted up the head.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They have taken crafty counsel against thy people, and consulted against thy hidden ones.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They have said, Come, and let us cut them off from being a nation; that the name of Israel may be no more in remembrance.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For they have consulted together with one consent: they are confederate against thee:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The tabernacles of Edom, and the Ishmaelites; of Moab, and the Hagarenes;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Gebal, and Ammon, and Amalek; the Philistines with the inhabitants of Tyre;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Assur also is joined with them: they have holpen the children of Lot. Selah.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Do unto them as unto the Midianites; as to Sisera, as to Jabin, at the brook of Kison:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Which perished at En–dor: they became as dung for the earth.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Make their nobles like Oreb, and like Zeeb: yea, all their princes as Zebah, and as Zalmunna:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Who said, Let us take to ourselves the houses of God in possession.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** O my God, make them like a wheel; as the stubble before the wind.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** As the fire burneth a wood, and as the flame setteth the mountains on fire;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** So persecute them with thy tempest, and make them afraid with thy storm.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Fill their faces with shame; that they may seek thy name, O Lord.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Let them be confounded and troubled for ever; yea, let them be put to shame, and perish:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** That men may know that thou, whose name alone is Jehovah, art the most high over all the earth.
