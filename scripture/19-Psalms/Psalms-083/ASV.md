# Psalms 83 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O God, keep not thou silence: Hold not thy peace, and be not still, O God.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For, lo, thine enemies make a tumult; And they that hate thee have lifted up the head.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They take crafty counsel against thy people, And consult together against thy hidden ones.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They have said, Come, and let us cut them off from being a nation; That the name of Israel may be no more in remembrance.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For they have consulted together with one consent; Against thee do they make a covenant:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The tents of Edom and the Ishmaelites; Moab, and the Hagarenes;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Gebal, and Ammon, and Amalek; Philistia with the inhabitants of Tyre:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Assyria also is joined with them; They have helped the children of Lot. [Selah

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Do thou unto them as unto Midian, As to Sisera, as to Jabin, at the river Kishon;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Who perished at Endor, Who became as dung for the earth.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Make their nobles like Oreb and Zeeb; Yea, all their princes like Zebah and Zalmunna;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Who said, Let us take to ourselves in possession The habitations of God.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** O my God, make them like the whirling dust; As stubble before the wind.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** As the fire that burneth the forest, And as the flame that setteth the mountains on fire,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** So pursue them with thy tempest, And terrify them with thy storm.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Fill their faces with confusion, That they may seek thy name, O Jehovah.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Let them be put to shame and dismayed for ever; Yea, let them be confounded and perish;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** That they may know that thou alone, whose name is Jehovah, Art the Most High over all the earth.
