# Psalms 83 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God, don’t keep silent. Don’t keep silent, and don’t be still, God.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For, behold, your enemies are stirred up. Those who hate you have lifted up their heads.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They conspire with cunning against your people. They plot against your cherished ones.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** “Come,” they say, “let’s destroy them as a nation, that the name of Israel may be remembered no more.”

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For they have conspired together with one mind. They form an alliance against you.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The tents of Edom and the Ishmaelites; Moab, and the Hagrites;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Gebal, Ammon, and Amalek; Philistia with the inhabitants of Tyre;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Assyria also is joined with them. They have helped the children of Lot. Selah.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Do to them as you did to Midian, as to Sisera, as to Jabin, at the river Kishon;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** who perished at Endor, who became as dung for the earth.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Make their nobles like Oreb and Zeeb; yes, all their princes like Zebah and Zalmunna;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** who said, “Let us take possession of God’s pasture lands.”

<a id="v13"></a>**13** My God, make them like tumbleweed; like chaff before the wind.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** As the fire that burns the forest, as the flame that sets the mountains on fire,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** so pursue them with your tempest, and terrify them with your storm.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Fill their faces with confusion, that they may seek your name, Yahweh.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Let them be disappointed and dismayed forever. Yes, let them be confounded and perish;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** that they may know that you alone, whose name is Yahweh, are the Most High over all the earth.
