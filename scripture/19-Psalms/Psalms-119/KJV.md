# Psalms 119 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Blessed are the undefiled in the way, who walk in the law of the Lord.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Blessed are they that keep his testimonies, and that seek him with the whole heart.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They also do no iniquity: they walk in his ways.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thou hast commanded us to keep thy precepts diligently.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** O that my ways were directed to keep thy statutes!

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Then shall I not be ashamed, when I have respect unto all thy commandments.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I will praise thee with uprightness of heart, when I shall have learned thy righteous judgments.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will keep thy statutes: O forsake me not utterly.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Wherewithal shall a young man cleanse his way? by taking heed thereto according to thy word.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** With my whole heart have I sought thee: O let me not wander from thy commandments.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Thy word have I hid in mine heart, that I might not sin against thee.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Blessed art thou, O Lord: teach me thy statutes.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** With my lips have I declared all the judgments of thy mouth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I have rejoiced in the way of thy testimonies, as much as in all riches.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I will meditate in thy precepts, and have respect unto thy ways.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** I will delight myself in thy statutes: I will not forget thy word.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Deal bountifully with thy servant, that I may live, and keep thy word.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Open thou mine eyes, that I may behold wondrous things out of thy law.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** I am a stranger in the earth: hide not thy commandments from me.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My soul breaketh for the longing that it hath unto thy judgments at all times.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Thou hast rebuked the proud that are cursed, which do err from thy commandments.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Remove from me reproach and contempt; for I have kept thy testimonies.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Princes also did sit and speak against me: but thy servant did meditate in thy statutes.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Thy testimonies also are my delight and my counsellors.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** My soul cleaveth unto the dust: quicken thou me according to thy word.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** I have declared my ways, and thou heardest me: teach me thy statutes.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Make me to understand the way of thy precepts: so shall I talk of thy wondrous works.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** My soul melteth for heaviness: strengthen thou me according unto thy word.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Remove from me the way of lying: and grant me thy law graciously.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** I have chosen the way of truth: thy judgments have I laid before me.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** I have stuck unto thy testimonies: O Lord, put me not to shame.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** I will run the way of thy commandments, when thou shalt enlarge my heart.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Teach me, O Lord, the way of thy statutes; and I shall keep it unto the end.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Give me understanding, and I shall keep thy law; yea, I shall observe it with my whole heart.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Make me to go in the path of thy commandments; for therein do I delight.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Incline my heart unto thy testimonies, and not to covetousness.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Turn away mine eyes from beholding vanity; and quicken thou me in thy way.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** Stablish thy word unto thy servant, who is devoted to thy fear.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** Turn away my reproach which I fear: for thy judgments are good.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** Behold, I have longed after thy precepts: quicken me in thy righteousness.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** Let thy mercies come also unto me, O Lord, even thy salvation, according to thy word.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** So shall I have wherewith to answer him that reproacheth me: for I trust in thy word.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** And take not the word of truth utterly out of my mouth; for I have hoped in thy judgments.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** So shall I keep thy law continually for ever and ever.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** And I will walk at liberty: for I seek thy precepts.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** I will speak of thy testimonies also before kings, and will not be ashamed.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** And I will delight myself in thy commandments, which I have loved.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** My hands also will I lift up unto thy commandments, which I have loved; and I will meditate in thy statutes.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** Remember the word unto thy servant, upon which thou hast caused me to hope.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** This is my comfort in my affliction: for thy word hath quickened me.

<a id="v51"></a>**51** The proud have had me greatly in derision: yet have I not declined from thy law.

<a id="v52"></a>**52** I remembered thy judgments of old, O Lord; and have comforted myself.

<a id="v53"></a>**53** Horror hath taken hold upon me because of the wicked that forsake thy law.

<a id="v54"></a>**54** Thy statutes have been my songs in the house of my pilgrimage.

<a id="v55"></a>**55** I have remembered thy name, O Lord, in the night, and have kept thy law.

<a id="v56"></a>**56** This I had, because I kept thy precepts.

<a id="v57"></a>**57** Thou art my portion, O Lord: I have said that I would keep thy words.

<a id="v58"></a>**58** I intreated thy favour with my whole heart: be merciful unto me according to thy word.

<a id="v59"></a>**59** I thought on my ways, and turned my feet unto thy testimonies.

<a id="v60"></a>**60** I made haste, and delayed not to keep thy commandments.

<a id="v61"></a>**61** The bands of the wicked have robbed me: but I have not forgotten thy law.

<a id="v62"></a>**62** At midnight I will rise to give thanks unto thee because of thy righteous judgments.

<a id="v63"></a>**63** I am a companion of all them that fear thee, and of them that keep thy precepts.

<a id="v64"></a>**64** The earth, O Lord, is full of thy mercy: teach me thy statutes.

<a id="v65"></a>**65** Thou hast dealt well with thy servant, O Lord, according unto thy word.

<a id="v66"></a>**66** Teach me good judgment and knowledge: for I have believed thy commandments.

<a id="v67"></a>**67** Before I was afflicted I went astray: but now have I kept thy word.

<a id="v68"></a>**68** Thou art good, and doest good; teach me thy statutes.

<a id="v69"></a>**69** The proud have forged a lie against me: but I will keep thy precepts with my whole heart.

<a id="v70"></a>**70** Their heart is as fat as grease; but I delight in thy law.

<a id="v71"></a>**71** It is good for me that I have been afflicted; that I might learn thy statutes.

<a id="v72"></a>**72** The law of thy mouth is better unto me than thousands of gold and silver.

<a id="v73"></a>**73** Thy hands have made me and fashioned me: give me understanding, that I may learn thy commandments.

<a id="v74"></a>**74** They that fear thee will be glad when they see me; because I have hoped in thy word.

<a id="v75"></a>**75** I know, O Lord, that thy judgments are right, and that thou in faithfulness hast afflicted me.

<a id="v76"></a>**76** Let, I pray thee, thy merciful kindness be for my comfort, according to thy word unto thy servant.

<a id="v77"></a>**77** Let thy tender mercies come unto me, that I may live: for thy law is my delight.

<a id="v78"></a>**78** Let the proud be ashamed; for they dealt perversely with me without a cause: but I will meditate in thy precepts.

<a id="v79"></a>**79** Let those that fear thee turn unto me, and those that have known thy testimonies.

<a id="v80"></a>**80** Let my heart be sound in thy statutes; that I be not ashamed.

<a id="v81"></a>**81** My soul fainteth for thy salvation: but I hope in thy word.

<a id="v82"></a>**82** Mine eyes fail for thy word, saying, When wilt thou comfort me?

<a id="v83"></a>**83** For I am become like a bottle in the smoke; yet do I not forget thy statutes.

<a id="v84"></a>**84** How many are the days of thy servant? when wilt thou execute judgment on them that persecute me?

<a id="v85"></a>**85** The proud have digged pits for me, which are not after thy law.

<a id="v86"></a>**86** All thy commandments are faithful: they persecute me wrongfully; help thou me.

<a id="v87"></a>**87** They had almost consumed me upon earth; but I forsook not thy precepts.

<a id="v88"></a>**88** Quicken me after thy lovingkindness; so shall I keep the testimony of thy mouth.

<a id="v89"></a>**89** For ever, O Lord, thy word is settled in heaven.

<a id="v90"></a>**90** Thy faithfulness is unto all generations: thou hast established the earth, and it abideth.

<a id="v91"></a>**91** They continue this day according to thine ordinances: for all are thy servants.

<a id="v92"></a>**92** Unless thy law had been my delights, I should then have perished in mine affliction.

<a id="v93"></a>**93** I will never forget thy precepts: for with them thou hast quickened me.

<a id="v94"></a>**94** I am thine, save me; for I have sought thy precepts.

<a id="v95"></a>**95** The wicked have waited for me to destroy me: but I will consider thy testimonies.

<a id="v96"></a>**96** I have seen an end of all perfection: but thy commandment is exceeding broad.

<a id="v97"></a>**97** O how love I thy law! it is my meditation all the day.

<a id="v98"></a>**98** Thou through thy commandments hast made me wiser than mine enemies: for they are ever with me.

<a id="v99"></a>**99** I have more understanding than all my teachers: for thy testimonies are my meditation.

<a id="v100"></a>**100** I understand more than the ancients, because I keep thy precepts.

<a id="v101"></a>**101** I have refrained my feet from every evil way, that I might keep thy word.

<a id="v102"></a>**102** I have not departed from thy judgments: for thou hast taught me.

<a id="v103"></a>**103** How sweet are thy words unto my taste! yea, sweeter than honey to my mouth!

<a id="v104"></a>**104** Through thy precepts I get understanding: therefore I hate every false way.

<a id="v105"></a>**105** Thy word is a lamp unto my feet, and a light unto my path.

<a id="v106"></a>**106** I have sworn, and I will perform it, that I will keep thy righteous judgments.

<a id="v107"></a>**107** I am afflicted very much: quicken me, O Lord, according unto thy word.

<a id="v108"></a>**108** Accept, I beseech thee, the freewill offerings of my mouth, O Lord, and teach me thy judgments.

<a id="v109"></a>**109** My soul is continually in my hand: yet do I not forget thy law.

<a id="v110"></a>**110** The wicked have laid a snare for me: yet I erred not from thy precepts.

<a id="v111"></a>**111** Thy testimonies have I taken as an heritage for ever: for they are the rejoicing of my heart.

<a id="v112"></a>**112** I have inclined mine heart to perform thy statutes alway, even unto the end.

<a id="v113"></a>**113** I hate vain thoughts: but thy law do I love.

<a id="v114"></a>**114** Thou art my hiding place and my shield: I hope in thy word.

<a id="v115"></a>**115** Depart from me, ye evildoers: for I will keep the commandments of my God.

<a id="v116"></a>**116** Uphold me according unto thy word, that I may live: and let me not be ashamed of my hope.

<a id="v117"></a>**117** Hold thou me up, and I shall be safe: and I will have respect unto thy statutes continually.

<a id="v118"></a>**118** Thou hast trodden down all them that err from thy statutes: for their deceit is falsehood.

<a id="v119"></a>**119** Thou puttest away all the wicked of the earth like dross: therefore I love thy testimonies.

<a id="v120"></a>**120** My flesh trembleth for fear of thee; and I am afraid of thy judgments.

<a id="v121"></a>**121** I have done judgment and justice: leave me not to mine oppressors.

<a id="v122"></a>**122** Be surety for thy servant for good: let not the proud oppress me.

<a id="v123"></a>**123** Mine eyes fail for thy salvation, and for the word of thy righteousness.

<a id="v124"></a>**124** Deal with thy servant according unto thy mercy, and teach me thy statutes.

<a id="v125"></a>**125** I am thy servant; give me understanding, that I may know thy testimonies.

<a id="v126"></a>**126** It is time for thee, Lord, to work: for they have made void thy law.

<a id="v127"></a>**127** Therefore I love thy commandments above gold; yea, above fine gold.

<a id="v128"></a>**128** Therefore I esteem all thy precepts concerning all things to be right; and I hate every false way.

<a id="v129"></a>**129** Thy testimonies are wonderful: therefore doth my soul keep them.

<a id="v130"></a>**130** The entrance of thy words giveth light; it giveth understanding unto the simple.

<a id="v131"></a>**131** I opened my mouth, and panted: for I longed for thy commandments.

<a id="v132"></a>**132** Look thou upon me, and be merciful unto me, as thou usest to do unto those that love thy name.

<a id="v133"></a>**133** Order my steps in thy word: and let not any iniquity have dominion over me.

<a id="v134"></a>**134** Deliver me from the oppression of man: so will I keep thy precepts.

<a id="v135"></a>**135** Make thy face to shine upon thy servant; and teach me thy statutes.

<a id="v136"></a>**136** Rivers of waters run down mine eyes, because they keep not thy law.

<a id="v137"></a>**137** Righteous art thou, O Lord, and upright are thy judgments.

<a id="v138"></a>**138** Thy testimonies that thou hast commanded are righteous and very faithful.

<a id="v139"></a>**139** My zeal hath consumed me, because mine enemies have forgotten thy words.

<a id="v140"></a>**140** Thy word is very pure: therefore thy servant loveth it.

<a id="v141"></a>**141** I am small and despised: yet do not I forget thy precepts.

<a id="v142"></a>**142** Thy righteousness is an everlasting righteousness, and thy law is the truth.

<a id="v143"></a>**143** Trouble and anguish have taken hold on me: yet thy commandments are my delights.

<a id="v144"></a>**144** The righteousness of thy testimonies is everlasting: give me understanding, and I shall live.

<a id="v145"></a>**145** I cried with my whole heart; hear me, O Lord: I will keep thy statutes.

<a id="v146"></a>**146** I cried unto thee; save me, and I shall keep thy testimonies.

<a id="v147"></a>**147** I prevented the dawning of the morning, and cried: I hoped in thy word.

<a id="v148"></a>**148** Mine eyes prevent the night watches, that I might meditate in thy word.

<a id="v149"></a>**149** Hear my voice according unto thy lovingkindness: O Lord, quicken me according to thy judgment.

<a id="v150"></a>**150** They draw nigh that follow after mischief: they are far from thy law.

<a id="v151"></a>**151** Thou art near, O Lord; and all thy commandments are truth.

<a id="v152"></a>**152** Concerning thy testimonies, I have known of old that thou hast founded them for ever.

<a id="v153"></a>**153** Consider mine affliction, and deliver me: for I do not forget thy law.

<a id="v154"></a>**154** Plead my cause, and deliver me: quicken me according to thy word.

<a id="v155"></a>**155** Salvation is far from the wicked: for they seek not thy statutes.

<a id="v156"></a>**156** Great are thy tender mercies, O Lord: quicken me according to thy judgments.

<a id="v157"></a>**157** Many are my persecutors and mine enemies; yet do I not decline from thy testimonies.

<a id="v158"></a>**158** I beheld the transgressors, and was grieved; because they kept not thy word.

<a id="v159"></a>**159** Consider how I love thy precepts: quicken me, O Lord, according to thy lovingkindness.

<a id="v160"></a>**160** Thy word is true from the beginning: and every one of thy righteous judgments endureth for ever.

<a id="v161"></a>**161** Princes have persecuted me without a cause: but my heart standeth in awe of thy word.

<a id="v162"></a>**162** I rejoice at thy word, as one that findeth great spoil.

<a id="v163"></a>**163** I hate and abhor lying: but thy law do I love.

<a id="v164"></a>**164** Seven times a day do I praise thee because of thy righteous judgments.

<a id="v165"></a>**165** Great peace have they which love thy law: and nothing shall offend them.

<a id="v166"></a>**166** Lord, I have hoped for thy salvation, and done thy commandments.

<a id="v167"></a>**167** My soul hath kept thy testimonies; and I love them exceedingly.

<a id="v168"></a>**168** I have kept thy precepts and thy testimonies: for all my ways are before thee.

<a id="v169"></a>**169** Let my cry come near before thee, O Lord: give me understanding according to thy word.

<a id="v170"></a>**170** Let my supplication come before thee: deliver me according to thy word.

<a id="v171"></a>**171** My lips shall utter praise, when thou hast taught me thy statutes.

<a id="v172"></a>**172** My tongue shall speak of thy word: for all thy commandments are righteousness.

<a id="v173"></a>**173** Let thine hand help me; for I have chosen thy precepts.

<a id="v174"></a>**174** I have longed for thy salvation, O Lord; and thy law is my delight.

<a id="v175"></a>**175** Let my soul live, and it shall praise thee; and let thy judgments help me.

<a id="v176"></a>**176** I have gone astray like a lost sheep; seek thy servant; for I do not forget thy commandments.
