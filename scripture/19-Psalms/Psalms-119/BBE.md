# Psalms 119 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Happy are they who are without sin in their ways, walking in the law of the Lord.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Happy are they who keep his unchanging word, searching after him with all their heart.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They do no evil; they go in his ways.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You have put your orders into our hearts, so that we might keep them with care.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** If only my ways were ordered so that I might keep your rules!

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Then I would not be put to shame, as long as I have respect for all your teaching.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I will give you praise with an upright heart in learning your right decisions.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will keep your rules: O give me not up completely.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** How may a young man make his way clean? by guiding it after your word.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I have made search for you with all my heart: O let me not go wandering far from your teaching.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I have kept your sayings secretly in my heart, so that I might do no sin against you.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Praise be to you, O Lord: give me knowledge of your rules.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** With my lips have I made clear all the decisions of your mouth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I have taken as much delight in the way of your unchanging word, as in all wealth.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I will give thought to your orders, and have respect for your ways.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** I will have delight in your rules; I will not let your word go out of my mind.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Give me, your servant, the reward of life, so that I may keep your word;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Let my eyes be open to see the wonders of your law.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** I am living in a strange land: do not let your teachings be kept secret from me.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My soul is broken with desire for your decisions at all times.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Your hand has been against the men of pride, a curse is on those who go wandering out of your way.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Take away from me shame and bitter words; for I have kept your unchanging word in my heart.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Rulers make evil designs against me; but your servant gives thought to your rules.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Your unchanging word is my delight, and the guide of my footsteps.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** My soul is joined to the dust: O give me life, in keeping with your word.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** I put the record of my ways before you, and you gave me an answer: O give me knowledge of your rules.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Make the way of your orders clear to me; then my thoughts will be ever on your wonders.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** My soul is wasted with sorrow; give me strength again in keeping with your word

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Take from me every false way; and in mercy give me your law.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** I have taken the way of faith: I have kept your decisions before me.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** I have been true to your unchanging word; O Lord, do not put me to shame.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** I will go quickly in the way of your teaching, because you have given me a free heart.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** O Lord, let me see the way of your rules, and I will keep it to the end.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Give me wisdom, so that I may keep your law; going after it with all my heart.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Make me go in the way of your teachings; for they are my delight.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Let my heart be turned to your unchanging word, and not to evil desire.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Let my eyes be turned away from what is false; give me life in your ways.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** Give effect to your word to your servant, in whose heart is the fear of you.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** Take away the shame which is my fear; for your decisions are good.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** See how great is my desire for your orders: give me life in your righteousness.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** Let your mercies come to me, O Lord, even your salvation, as you have said.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** So that I may have an answer for the man who would put me to shame; for I have faith in your word.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Take not your true word quite out of my mouth; for I have put my hope in your decisions.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** So that I may keep your law for ever and ever;

<a id="v45"></a>**45** So that my way may be in a wide place: because my search has been for your orders.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** So that I may give knowledge of your unchanging word before kings, and not be put to shame.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** And so that I may take delight in your teachings, to which I have given my love.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** And so that my hands may be stretched out to you; and I will give thought to your rules.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** Keep in mind your word to your servant, for on it has my hope been fixed.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** This is my comfort in my trouble; that your sayings have given me life.

<a id="v51"></a>**51** The men of pride have made great sport of me; but I have not been turned from your law.

<a id="v52"></a>**52** I have kept the memory of your decisions from times past, O Lord; and they have been my comfort.

<a id="v53"></a>**53** I am burning with wrath, because of the sinners who have given up your law.

<a id="v54"></a>**54** Your rules have been melodies to me, while I have been living in strange lands.

<a id="v55"></a>**55** I have given thought to your name in the night, O Lord, and have kept your law.

<a id="v56"></a>**56** This has been true of me, that I have kept your orders in my heart.

<a id="v57"></a>**57** The Lord is my heritage: I have said that I would be ruled by your words.

<a id="v58"></a>**58** I have given my mind to do your pleasure with all my heart; have mercy on me, as you have said.

<a id="v59"></a>**59** I gave thought to my steps, and my feet were turned into the way of your unchanging word.

<a id="v60"></a>**60** I was quick to do your orders, and let no time be wasted.

<a id="v61"></a>**61** The cords of evil-doers are round me; but I have kept in mind your law.

<a id="v62"></a>**62** In the middle of the night I will get up to give you praise, because of all your right decisions.

<a id="v63"></a>**63** I keep company with all your worshippers, and those who have your orders in their memory.

<a id="v64"></a>**64** The earth, O Lord, is full of your mercy: give me knowledge of your rules.

<a id="v65"></a>**65** You have done good to your servant, O Lord, in keeping with your word.

<a id="v66"></a>**66** Give me knowledge and good sense; for I have put my faith in your teachings.

<a id="v67"></a>**67** Before I was in trouble I went out of the way; but now I keep your word.

<a id="v68"></a>**68** You are good, and your works are good; give me knowledge of your rules.

<a id="v69"></a>**69** The men of pride have said false things about me; but I will keep your orders in my heart.

<a id="v70"></a>**70** Their hearts are shut up with fat; but my delight is in your law.

<a id="v71"></a>**71** It is good for me to have been through trouble; so that I might come to the knowledge of your rules.

<a id="v72"></a>**72** The law of your mouth is better to me than thousands of gold and silver.

<a id="v73"></a>**73** Your hands have made me, and given me form: give me wisdom, so that I may have knowledge of your teaching.

<a id="v74"></a>**74** Your worshippers will see me and be glad; because my hope has been in your word.

<a id="v75"></a>**75** I have seen, O Lord, that your decisions are right, and that in unchanging faith you have sent trouble on me.

<a id="v76"></a>**76** Let your mercy now be my comfort, as you have said to your servant.

<a id="v77"></a>**77** Let your gentle mercies come to me, so that I may have life; for your law is my delight.

<a id="v78"></a>**78** Let the men of pride be shamed; because they have falsely given decision against me; but I will give thought to your orders.

<a id="v79"></a>**79** Let your worshippers be turned to me, and those who have knowledge of your words.

<a id="v80"></a>**80** Let all my heart be given to your orders, so that I may not be put to shame.

<a id="v81"></a>**81** My soul is wasted with desire for your salvation: but I have hope in your word.

<a id="v82"></a>**82** My eyes are full of weariness with searching for your word, saying, When will you give me comfort?

<a id="v83"></a>**83** For I have become like a wine-skin black with smoke; but I still keep the memory of your rules.

<a id="v84"></a>**84** How short is the life of your servant! when will you give your decision against those who are attacking me?

<a id="v85"></a>**85** The men of pride, who are turned away from your law, have put nets for me.

<a id="v86"></a>**86** All your teachings are certain; they go after me with evil design; give me your help.

<a id="v87"></a>**87** They had almost put an end to me on earth; but I did not give up your orders.

<a id="v88"></a>**88** Give me life in your mercy; so that I may be ruled by the unchanging word of your mouth.

<a id="v89"></a>**89** For ever, O Lord, your word is fixed in heaven.

<a id="v90"></a>**90** Your faith is unchanging from generation to generation: you have put the earth in its place, and it is not moved.

<a id="v91"></a>**91** They are ruled this day by your decisions; for all things are your servants.

<a id="v92"></a>**92** If your law had not been my delight, my troubles would have put an end to me.

<a id="v93"></a>**93** I will ever keep your orders in mind; for in them I have life.

<a id="v94"></a>**94** I am yours, O be my saviour; for my desire has been for your rules.

<a id="v95"></a>**95** The sinners have been waiting for me to give me up to destruction; but I will give all my mind to your unchanging ward.

<a id="v96"></a>**96** I have seen that nothing on earth is complete; but your teaching is very wide.

<a id="v97"></a>**97** O what love I have for your law! I give thought to it all the day.

<a id="v98"></a>**98** Your teaching has made me wiser than my haters: for it is mine for ever.

<a id="v99"></a>**99** I have more knowledge than all my teachers, because I give thought to your unchanging word.

<a id="v100"></a>**100** I have more wisdom than the old, because I have kept your orders.

<a id="v101"></a>**101** I have kept back my feet from all evil ways, so that I might be true to your word.

<a id="v102"></a>**102** My heart has not been turned away from your decisions; for you have been my teacher.

<a id="v103"></a>**103** How sweet are your sayings to my taste! truly, they are sweeter than honey in my mouth!

<a id="v104"></a>**104** Through your orders I get wisdom; for this reason I am a hater of every false way.

<a id="v105"></a>**105** Your word is a light for my feet, ever shining on my way.

<a id="v106"></a>**106** I have made an oath and kept it, to be guided by your upright decisions.

<a id="v107"></a>**107** I am greatly troubled, O Lord, give me life in keeping with your word.

<a id="v108"></a>**108** Take, O Lord, the free offerings of my mouth, and give me knowledge of your decisions.

<a id="v109"></a>**109** My soul is ever in danger; but I still keep the memory of your law.

<a id="v110"></a>**110** Sinners have put a net to take me; but I was true to your orders.

<a id="v111"></a>**111** I have taken your unchanging word as an eternal heritage; for it is the joy of my heart.

<a id="v112"></a>**112** My heart is ever ready to keep your rules, even to the end.

<a id="v113"></a>**113** I am a hater of men of doubting mind; but I am a lover of your law.

<a id="v114"></a>**114** You are my secret place and my breastplate against danger; my hope is in your word.

<a id="v115"></a>**115** Go far from me, you evil-doers; so that I may keep the teachings of my God.

<a id="v116"></a>**116** Be my support as you have said, and give me life; let not my hope be turned to shame.

<a id="v117"></a>**117** Let me not be moved, and I will be safe, and ever take delight in your rules.

<a id="v118"></a>**118** You have overcome all those who are wandering from your rules; for all their thoughts are false.

<a id="v119"></a>**119** All the sinners of the earth are like waste metal in your eyes; and for this cause I give my love to your unchanging word.

<a id="v120"></a>**120** My flesh is moved for fear of you; I give honour to your decisions.

<a id="v121"></a>**121** I have done what is good and right: you will not give me into the hands of those who are working against me.

<a id="v122"></a>**122** Take your servant's interests into your keeping; let me not be crushed by the men of pride.

<a id="v123"></a>**123** My eyes are wasted with desire for your salvation, and for the word of your righteousness.

<a id="v124"></a>**124** Be good to your servant in your mercy, and give me teaching in your rules.

<a id="v125"></a>**125** I am your servant; give me wisdom, so that I may have knowledge of your unchanging word.

<a id="v126"></a>**126** It is time, O Lord, for you to let your work be seen; for they have made your law without effect.

<a id="v127"></a>**127** For this reason I have greater love far your teachings than for gold, even for shining gold.

<a id="v128"></a>**128** Because of it I keep straight in all things by your orders; and I am a hater of every false way.

<a id="v129"></a>**129** Your unchanging word is full of wonder; for this reason my soul keeps it.

<a id="v130"></a>**130** The opening of your words gives light; it gives good sense to the simple.

<a id="v131"></a>**131** My mouth was open wide, waiting with great desire for your teachings.

<a id="v132"></a>**132** Let your eyes be turned to me, and have mercy on me, as it is right for you to do to those who are lovers of your name.

<a id="v133"></a>**133** Let my steps be guided by your word; and let not sin have control over me.

<a id="v134"></a>**134** Make me free from the cruel rule of man; then I will keep your orders.

<a id="v135"></a>**135** Let your servant see the shining of your face; give me knowledge of your rules.

<a id="v136"></a>**136** Rivers of water are flowing from my eyes, because men do not keep your law.

<a id="v137"></a>**137** O Lord, great is your righteousness, and upright are your decisions.

<a id="v138"></a>**138** You have given your unchanging word in righteousness, and it is for ever.

<a id="v139"></a>**139** My passion has overcome me; because my haters are turned away from your words.

<a id="v140"></a>**140** Your word is of tested value; and it is dear to your servant.

<a id="v141"></a>**141** I am small and of no account; but I keep your orders in mind.

<a id="v142"></a>**142** Your righteousness is an unchanging righteousness, and your law is certain.

<a id="v143"></a>**143** Pain and trouble have overcome me: but your teachings are my delight.

<a id="v144"></a>**144** The righteousness of your unchanging word is eternal; give me wisdom so that I may have life.

<a id="v145"></a>**145** I have made my prayer with all my heart; give answer to me, O Lord: I will keep your rules.

<a id="v146"></a>**146** My cry has gone up to you; take me out of trouble, and I will be guided by your unchanging word.

<a id="v147"></a>**147** Before the sun is up, my cry for help comes to your ear; my hope is in your words.

<a id="v148"></a>**148** In the night watches I am awake, so that I may give thought to your saying.

<a id="v149"></a>**149** Let my voice come to you, in your mercy; O Lord, by your decisions give me life.

<a id="v150"></a>**150** Those who have evil designs against me come near; they are far from your law.

<a id="v151"></a>**151** You are near, O Lord; and all your teachings are true.

<a id="v152"></a>**152** I have long had knowledge that your unchanging word is for ever.

<a id="v153"></a>**153** O see my trouble, and be my saviour; for I keep your law in my mind,

<a id="v154"></a>**154** Undertake my cause, and come to my help, give me life, as you have said.

<a id="v155"></a>**155** Salvation is far from evil-doers; for they have made no search for your rules.

<a id="v156"></a>**156** Great is the number of your mercies, O Lord; give me life in keeping with your decisions.

<a id="v157"></a>**157** Great is the number of those who are against me; but I have not been turned away from your unchanging word.

<a id="v158"></a>**158** I saw with hate those who were untrue to you; for they did not keep your saying.

<a id="v159"></a>**159** See how great is my love for your orders: give me life, O Lord, in keeping with your mercy.

<a id="v160"></a>**160** Your word is true from the first; and your upright decision is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v161"></a>**161** Rulers have been cruel to me without cause; but I have the fear of your word in my heart.

<a id="v162"></a>**162** I am delighted by your saying, like a man who makes discovery of great wealth.

<a id="v163"></a>**163** I am full of hate and disgust for false words; but I am a lover of your law.

<a id="v164"></a>**164** Seven times a day do I give you praise, because of your upright decisions.

<a id="v165"></a>**165** Great peace have lovers of your law; they have no cause for falling.

<a id="v166"></a>**166** Lord, my hope has been in your salvation; and I have kept your teachings.

<a id="v167"></a>**167** My soul has kept your unchanging word; great is my love for it.

<a id="v168"></a>**168** I have been ruled by your orders; for all my ways are before you.

<a id="v169"></a>**169** Let my cry come before you, O Lord; give me wisdom in keeping with your word.

<a id="v170"></a>**170** Let my prayer come before you; take me out of trouble, as you have said.

<a id="v171"></a>**171** Let my lips be flowing with praise, because you have given me knowledge of your rules.

<a id="v172"></a>**172** Let my tongue make songs in praise of your word; for all your teachings are righteousness.

<a id="v173"></a>**173** Let your hand be near for my help; for I have given my heart to your orders.

<a id="v174"></a>**174** All my desire has been for your salvation, O Lord; and your law is my delight.

<a id="v175"></a>**175** Give life to my soul so that it may give you praise; and let your decisions be my support.

<a id="v176"></a>**176** I have gone out of the way like a wandering sheep; make search for your servant; for I keep your teachings ever in mind.
