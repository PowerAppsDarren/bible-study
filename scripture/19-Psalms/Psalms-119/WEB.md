# Psalms 119 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Blessed are those whose ways are blameless, who walk according to Yahweh’s law.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Blessed are those who keep his statutes, who seek him with their whole heart.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yes, they do nothing wrong. They walk in his ways.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You have commanded your precepts, that we should fully obey them.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Oh that my ways were steadfast to obey your statutes!

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Then I wouldn’t be disappointed, when I consider all of your commandments.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I will give thanks to you with uprightness of heart, when I learn your righteous judgments.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will observe your statutes. Don’t utterly forsake me.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** How can a young man keep his way pure? By living according to your word.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** With my whole heart, I have sought you. Don’t let me wander from your commandments.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I have hidden your word in my heart, that I might not sin against you.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Blessed are you, Yahweh. Teach me your statutes.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** With my lips, I have declared all the ordinances of your mouth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I have rejoiced in the way of your testimonies, as much as in all riches.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I will meditate on your precepts, and consider your ways.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** I will delight myself in your statutes. I will not forget your word.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Do good to your servant. I will live and I will obey your word.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Open my eyes, that I may see wondrous things out of your law.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** I am a stranger on the earth. Don’t hide your commandments from me.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My soul is consumed with longing for your ordinances at all times.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** You have rebuked the proud who are cursed, who wander from your commandments.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Take reproach and contempt away from me, for I have kept your statutes.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Though princes sit and slander me, your servant will meditate on your statutes.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Indeed your statutes are my delight, and my counselors.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** My soul is laid low in the dust. Revive me according to your word!

<a id="v26"></a>**26** I declared my ways, and you answered me. Teach me your statutes.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Let me understand the teaching of your precepts! Then I will meditate on your wondrous works.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** My soul is weary with sorrow: strengthen me according to your word.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Keep me from the way of deceit. Grant me your law graciously!

<a id="v30"></a>**30** I have chosen the way of truth. I have set your ordinances before me.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** I cling to your statutes, Yahweh. Don’t let me be disappointed.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** I run in the path of your commandments, for you have set my heart free.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Teach me, Yahweh, the way of your statutes. I will keep them to the end.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Give me understanding, and I will keep your law. Yes, I will obey it with my whole heart.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Direct me in the path of your commandments, for I delight in them.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Turn my heart toward your statutes, not toward selfish gain.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Turn my eyes away from looking at worthless things. Revive me in your ways.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** Fulfill your promise to your servant, that you may be feared.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** Take away my disgrace that I dread, for your ordinances are good.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** Behold, I long for your precepts! Revive me in your righteousness.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** Let your loving kindness also come to me, Yahweh, your salvation, according to your word.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** So I will have an answer for him who reproaches me, for I trust in your word.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Don’t snatch the word of truth out of my mouth, for I put my hope in your ordinances.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** So I will obey your law continually, forever and ever.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** I will walk in liberty, for I have sought your precepts.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** I will also speak of your statutes before kings, and will not be disappointed.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** I will delight myself in your commandments, because I love them.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** I reach out my hands for your commandments, which I love. I will meditate on your statutes.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** Remember your word to your servant, because you gave me hope.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** This is my comfort in my affliction, for your word has revived me.

<a id="v51"></a>**51** The arrogant mock me excessively, but I don’t swerve from your law.

<a id="v52"></a>**52** I remember your ordinances of old, Yahweh, and have comforted myself.

<a id="v53"></a>**53** Indignation has taken hold on me, because of the wicked who forsake your law.

<a id="v54"></a>**54** Your statutes have been my songs, in the house where I live.

<a id="v55"></a>**55** I have remembered your name, Yahweh, in the night, and I obey your law.

<a id="v56"></a>**56** This is my way, that I keep your precepts.

<a id="v57"></a>**57** Yahweh is my portion. I promised to obey your words.

<a id="v58"></a>**58** I sought your favor with my whole heart. Be merciful to me according to your word.

<a id="v59"></a>**59** I considered my ways, and turned my steps to your statutes.

<a id="v60"></a>**60** I will hurry, and not delay, to obey your commandments.

<a id="v61"></a>**61** The ropes of the wicked bind me, but I won’t forget your law.

<a id="v62"></a>**62** At midnight I will rise to give thanks to you, because of your righteous ordinances.

<a id="v63"></a>**63** I am a friend of all those who fear you, of those who observe your precepts.

<a id="v64"></a>**64** The earth is full of your loving kindness, Yahweh. Teach me your statutes.

<a id="v65"></a>**65** Do good to your servant, according to your word, Yahweh.

<a id="v66"></a>**66** Teach me good judgment and knowledge, for I believe in your commandments.

<a id="v67"></a>**67** Before I was afflicted, I went astray; but now I observe your word.

<a id="v68"></a>**68** You are good, and do good. Teach me your statutes.

<a id="v69"></a>**69** The proud have smeared a lie upon me. With my whole heart, I will keep your precepts.

<a id="v70"></a>**70** Their heart is as callous as the fat, but I delight in your law.

<a id="v71"></a>**71** It is good for me that I have been afflicted, that I may learn your statutes.

<a id="v72"></a>**72** The law of your mouth is better to me than thousands of pieces of gold and silver.

<a id="v73"></a>**73** Your hands have made me and formed me. Give me understanding, that I may learn your commandments.

<a id="v74"></a>**74** Those who fear you will see me and be glad, because I have put my hope in your word.

<a id="v75"></a>**75** Yahweh, I know that your judgments are righteous, that in faithfulness you have afflicted me.

<a id="v76"></a>**76** Please let your loving kindness be for my comfort, according to your word to your servant.

<a id="v77"></a>**77** Let your tender mercies come to me, that I may live; for your law is my delight.

<a id="v78"></a>**78** Let the proud be disappointed, for they have overthrown me wrongfully. I will meditate on your precepts.

<a id="v79"></a>**79** Let those who fear you turn to me. They will know your statutes.

<a id="v80"></a>**80** Let my heart be blameless toward your decrees, that I may not be disappointed.

<a id="v81"></a>**81** My soul faints for your salvation. I hope in your word.

<a id="v82"></a>**82** My eyes fail for your word. I say, “When will you comfort me?”

<a id="v83"></a>**83** For I have become like a wineskin in the smoke. I don’t forget your statutes.

<a id="v84"></a>**84** How many are the days of your servant? When will you execute judgment on those who persecute me?

<a id="v85"></a>**85** The proud have dug pits for me, contrary to your law.

<a id="v86"></a>**86** All of your commandments are faithful. They persecute me wrongfully. Help me!

<a id="v87"></a>**87** They had almost wiped me from the earth, but I didn’t forsake your precepts.

<a id="v88"></a>**88** Preserve my life according to your loving kindness, so I will obey the statutes of your mouth.

<a id="v89"></a>**89** Yahweh, your word is settled in heaven forever.

<a id="v90"></a>**90** Your faithfulness is to all generations. You have established the earth, and it remains.

<a id="v91"></a>**91** Your laws remain to this day, for all things serve you.

<a id="v92"></a>**92** Unless your law had been my delight, I would have perished in my affliction.

<a id="v93"></a>**93** I will never forget your precepts, for with them, you have revived me.

<a id="v94"></a>**94** I am yours. Save me, for I have sought your precepts.

<a id="v95"></a>**95** The wicked have waited for me, to destroy me. I will consider your statutes.

<a id="v96"></a>**96** I have seen a limit to all perfection, but your commands are boundless.

<a id="v97"></a>**97** How I love your law! It is my meditation all day.

<a id="v98"></a>**98** Your commandments make me wiser than my enemies, for your commandments are always with me.

<a id="v99"></a>**99** I have more understanding than all my teachers, for your testimonies are my meditation.

<a id="v100"></a>**100** I understand more than the aged, because I have kept your precepts.

<a id="v101"></a>**101** I have kept my feet from every evil way, that I might observe your word.

<a id="v102"></a>**102** I have not turned aside from your ordinances, for you have taught me.

<a id="v103"></a>**103** How sweet are your promises to my taste, more than honey to my mouth!

<a id="v104"></a>**104** Through your precepts, I get understanding; therefore I hate every false way.

<a id="v105"></a>**105** Your word is a lamp to my feet, and a light for my path.

<a id="v106"></a>**106** I have sworn, and have confirmed it, that I will obey your righteous ordinances.

<a id="v107"></a>**107** I am afflicted very much. Revive me, Yahweh, according to your word.

<a id="v108"></a>**108** Accept, I beg you, the willing offerings of my mouth. Yahweh, teach me your ordinances.

<a id="v109"></a>**109** My soul is continually in my hand, yet I won’t forget your law.

<a id="v110"></a>**110** The wicked have laid a snare for me, yet I haven’t gone astray from your precepts.

<a id="v111"></a>**111** I have taken your testimonies as a heritage forever, for they are the joy of my heart.

<a id="v112"></a>**112** I have set my heart to perform your statutes forever, even to the end.

<a id="v113"></a>**113** I hate double-minded men, but I love your law.

<a id="v114"></a>**114** You are my hiding place and my shield. I hope in your word.

<a id="v115"></a>**115** Depart from me, you evildoers, that I may keep the commandments of my God.

<a id="v116"></a>**116** Uphold me according to your word, that I may live. Let me not be ashamed of my hope.

<a id="v117"></a>**117** Hold me up, and I will be safe, and will have respect for your statutes continually.

<a id="v118"></a>**118** You reject all those who stray from your statutes, for their deceit is in vain.

<a id="v119"></a>**119** You put away all the wicked of the earth like dross. Therefore I love your testimonies.

<a id="v120"></a>**120** My flesh trembles for fear of you. I am afraid of your judgments.

<a id="v121"></a>**121** I have done what is just and righteous. Don’t leave me to my oppressors.

<a id="v122"></a>**122** Ensure your servant’s well-being. Don’t let the proud oppress me.

<a id="v123"></a>**123** My eyes fail looking for your salvation, for your righteous word.

<a id="v124"></a>**124** Deal with your servant according to your loving kindness. Teach me your statutes.

<a id="v125"></a>**125** I am your servant. Give me understanding, that I may know your testimonies.

<a id="v126"></a>**126** It is time to act, Yahweh, for they break your law.

<a id="v127"></a>**127** Therefore I love your commandments more than gold, yes, more than pure gold.

<a id="v128"></a>**128** Therefore I consider all of your precepts to be right. I hate every false way.

<a id="v129"></a>**129** Your testimonies are wonderful, therefore my soul keeps them.

<a id="v130"></a>**130** The entrance of your words gives light. It gives understanding to the simple.

<a id="v131"></a>**131** I opened my mouth wide and panted, for I longed for your commandments.

<a id="v132"></a>**132** Turn to me, and have mercy on me, as you always do to those who love your name.

<a id="v133"></a>**133** Establish my footsteps in your word. Don’t let any iniquity have dominion over me.

<a id="v134"></a>**134** Redeem me from the oppression of man, so I will observe your precepts.

<a id="v135"></a>**135** Make your face shine on your servant. Teach me your statutes.

<a id="v136"></a>**136** Streams of tears run down my eyes, because they don’t observe your law.

<a id="v137"></a>**137** You are righteous, Yahweh. Your judgments are upright.

<a id="v138"></a>**138** You have commanded your statutes in righteousness. They are fully trustworthy.

<a id="v139"></a>**139** My zeal wears me out, because my enemies ignore your words.

<a id="v140"></a>**140** Your promises have been thoroughly tested, and your servant loves them.

<a id="v141"></a>**141** I am small and despised. I don’t forget your precepts.

<a id="v142"></a>**142** Your righteousness is an everlasting righteousness. Your law is truth.

<a id="v143"></a>**143** Trouble and anguish have taken hold of me. Your commandments are my delight.

<a id="v144"></a>**144** Your testimonies are righteous forever. Give me understanding, that I may live.

<a id="v145"></a>**145** I have called with my whole heart. Answer me, Yahweh! I will keep your statutes.

<a id="v146"></a>**146** I have called to you. Save me! I will obey your statutes.

<a id="v147"></a>**147** I rise before dawn and cry for help. I put my hope in your words.

<a id="v148"></a>**148** My eyes stay open through the night watches, that I might meditate on your word.

<a id="v149"></a>**149** Hear my voice according to your loving kindness. Revive me, Yahweh, according to your ordinances.

<a id="v150"></a>**150** They draw near who follow after wickedness. They are far from your law.

<a id="v151"></a>**151** You are near, Yahweh. All your commandments are truth.

<a id="v152"></a>**152** Of old I have known from your testimonies, that you have founded them forever.

<a id="v153"></a>**153** Consider my affliction, and deliver me, for I don’t forget your law.

<a id="v154"></a>**154** Plead my cause, and redeem me! Revive me according to your promise.

<a id="v155"></a>**155** Salvation is far from the wicked, for they don’t seek your statutes.

<a id="v156"></a>**156** Great are your tender mercies, Yahweh. Revive me according to your ordinances.

<a id="v157"></a>**157** Many are my persecutors and my adversaries. I haven’t swerved from your testimonies.

<a id="v158"></a>**158** I look at the faithless with loathing, because they don’t observe your word.

<a id="v159"></a>**159** Consider how I love your precepts. Revive me, Yahweh, according to your loving kindness.

<a id="v160"></a>**160** All of your words are truth. Every one of your righteous ordinances endures forever.

<a id="v161"></a>**161** Princes have persecuted me without a cause, but my heart stands in awe of your words.

<a id="v162"></a>**162** I rejoice at your word, as one who finds great plunder.

<a id="v163"></a>**163** I hate and abhor falsehood. I love your law.

<a id="v164"></a>**164** Seven times a day, I praise you, because of your righteous ordinances.

<a id="v165"></a>**165** Those who love your law have great peace. Nothing causes them to stumble.

<a id="v166"></a>**166** I have hoped for your salvation, Yahweh. I have done your commandments.

<a id="v167"></a>**167** My soul has observed your testimonies. I love them exceedingly.

<a id="v168"></a>**168** I have obeyed your precepts and your testimonies, for all my ways are before you.

<a id="v169"></a>**169** Let my cry come before you, Yahweh. Give me understanding according to your word.

<a id="v170"></a>**170** Let my supplication come before you. Deliver me according to your word.

<a id="v171"></a>**171** Let my lips utter praise, for you teach me your statutes.

<a id="v172"></a>**172** Let my tongue sing of your word, for all your commandments are righteousness.

<a id="v173"></a>**173** Let your hand be ready to help me, for I have chosen your precepts.

<a id="v174"></a>**174** I have longed for your salvation, Yahweh. Your law is my delight.

<a id="v175"></a>**175** Let my soul live, that I may praise you. Let your ordinances help me.

<a id="v176"></a>**176** I have gone astray like a lost sheep. Seek your servant, for I don’t forget your commandments.
