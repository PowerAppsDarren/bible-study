# Psalms 119 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Blessed are they that are perfect in the way, Who walk in the law of Jehovah.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Blessed are they that keep his testimonies, That seek him with the whole heart.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yea, they do no unrighteousness; They walk in his ways.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thou hast commandedus thy precepts, That we should observe them diligently.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Oh that my ways were established To observe thy statutes!

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Then shall I not be put to shame, When I have respect unto all thy commandments.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I will give thanks unto thee with uprightness of heart, When I learn thy righteous judgments.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will observe thy statutes: Oh forsake me not utterly. ב BETH.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Wherewith shall a young man cleanse his way? By taking heed thereto according to thy word.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** With my whole heart have I sought thee: Oh let me not wander from thy commandments.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Thy word have I laid up in my heart, That I might not sin against thee.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Blessed art thou, O Jehovah: Teach me thy statutes.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** With my lips have I declared All the ordinances of thy mouth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I have rejoiced in the way of thy testimonies, As much as in all riches.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I will meditate on thy precepts, And have respect unto thy ways.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** I will delight myself in thy statutes: I will not forget thy word. ג GIMEL.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Deal bountifully with thy servant, that I may live; So will I observe thy word.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Open thou mine eyes, that I may behold Wondrous things out of thy law.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** I am a sojourner in the earth: Hide not thy commandments from me.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My soul breaketh for the longing That it hath unto thine ordinances at all times.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Thou hast rebuked the proud that are cursed, That do wander from thy commandments.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Take away from me reproach and contempt; For I have kept thy testimonies.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Princes also sat and talked against me; But thy servant did meditate on thy statutes.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Thy testimonies also are my delight Andmy counsellors. ד DALETH.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** My soul cleaveth unto the dust: Quicken thou me according to thy word.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** I declared my ways, and thou answeredst me: Teach me thy statutes.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Make me to understand the way of thy precepts: So shall I meditate on thy wondrous works.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** My soul melteth for heaviness: Strengthen thou me according unto thy word.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Remove from me the way of falsehood; And grant me thy law graciously.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** I have chosen the way of faithfulness: Thine ordinances have I set before me.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** I cleave unto thy testimonies: O Jehovah, put me not to shame.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** I will run the way of thy commandments, When thou shalt enlarge my heart. ה HE.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Teach me, O Jehovah, the way of thy statutes; And I shall keep it unto the end.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Give me understanding, and I shall keep thy law; Yea, I shall observe it with my whole heart.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Make me to go in the path of thy commandments; For therein do I delight.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Incline my heart unto thy testimonies, And not to covetousness.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Turn away mine eyes from beholding vanity, And quicken me in thy ways.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** Confirm unto thy servant thy word, Whichis in order unto the fear of thee.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** Turn away my reproach whereof I am afraid; For thine ordinances are good.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** Behold, I have longed after thy precepts: Quicken me in thy righteousness. ו VAV.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** Let thy lovingkindnesses also come unto me, O Jehovah, Even thy salvation, according to thy word.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** So shall I have an answer for him that reproacheth me; For I trust in thy word.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** And take not the word of truth utterly out of my mouth; For I have hoped in thine ordinances.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** So shall I observe thy law continually For ever and ever.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** And I shall walk at liberty; For I have sought thy precepts.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** I will also speak of thy testimonies before kings, And shall not be put to shame.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** And I will delight myself in thy commandments, Which I have loved.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** I will lift up my hands also unto thy commandments, which I have loved; And I will meditate on thy statutes. ז ZAYIN.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** Remember the word unto thy servant, Because thou hast made me to hope.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** This is my comfort in my affliction; For thy word hath quickened me.

<a id="v51"></a>**51** The proud have had me greatly in derision: Yethave I not swerved from thy law.

<a id="v52"></a>**52** I have remembered thine ordinances of old, O Jehovah, And have comforted myself.

<a id="v53"></a>**53** Hot indignation hath taken hold upon me, Because of the wicked that forsake thy law.

<a id="v54"></a>**54** Thy statutes have been my songs In the house of my pilgrimage.

<a id="v55"></a>**55** I have remembered thy name, O Jehovah, in the night, And have observed thy law.

<a id="v56"></a>**56** This I have had, Because I have kept thy precepts. ח HHETH.

<a id="v57"></a>**57** Jehovah is my portion: I have said that I would observe thy words.

<a id="v58"></a>**58** I entreated thy favor with my whole heart: Be merciful unto me according to thy word.

<a id="v59"></a>**59** I thought on my ways, And turned my feet unto thy testimonies.

<a id="v60"></a>**60** I made haste, and delayed not, To observe thy commandments.

<a id="v61"></a>**61** The cords of the wicked have wrapped me round; ButI have not forgotten thy law.

<a id="v62"></a>**62** At midnight I will rise to give thanks unto thee Because of thy righteous ordinances.

<a id="v63"></a>**63** I am a companion of all them that fear thee, And of them that observe thy precepts.

<a id="v64"></a>**64** The earth, O Jehovah, is full of thy lovingkindness: Teach me thy statutes. ט TETH.

<a id="v65"></a>**65** Thou hast dealt well with thy servant, O Jehovah, according unto thy word.

<a id="v66"></a>**66** Teach me good judgment and knowledge; For I have believed in thy commandments.

<a id="v67"></a>**67** Before I was afflicted I went astray; But now I observe thy word.

<a id="v68"></a>**68** Thou art good, and doest good; Teach me thy statutes.

<a id="v69"></a>**69** The proud have forged a lie against me: With my whole heart will I keep thy precepts.

<a id="v70"></a>**70** Their heart is as fat as grease; But I delight in thy law.

<a id="v71"></a>**71** It is good for me that I have been afflicted; That I may learn thy statutes.

<a id="v72"></a>**72** The law of thy mouth is better unto me Than thousands of gold and silver. י YODH.

<a id="v73"></a>**73** Thy hands have made me and fashioned me: Give me understanding, that I may learn thy commandments.

<a id="v74"></a>**74** They that fear thee shall see me and be glad, Because I have hoped in thy word.

<a id="v75"></a>**75** I know, O Jehovah, that thy judgments are righteous, And that in faithfulness thou hast afflicted me.

<a id="v76"></a>**76** Let, I pray thee, thy lovingkindness be for my comfort, According to thy word unto thy servant.

<a id="v77"></a>**77** Let thy tender mercies come unto me, that I may live; For thy law is my delight.

<a id="v78"></a>**78** Let the proud be put to shame; for they have overthrown me wrongfully: ButI will meditate on thy precepts.

<a id="v79"></a>**79** Let those that fear thee turn unto me; And they shall know thy testimonies.

<a id="v80"></a>**80** Let my heart be perfect in thy statutes, That I be not put to shame. כ KAPH.

<a id="v81"></a>**81** My soul fainteth for thy salvation; ButI hope in thy word.

<a id="v82"></a>**82** Mine eyes fail for thy word, While I say, When wilt thou comfort me?

<a id="v83"></a>**83** For I am become like a wine-skin in the smoke; Yet do I not forget thy statutes.

<a id="v84"></a>**84** How many are the days of thy servant? When wilt thou execute judgment on them that persecute me?

<a id="v85"></a>**85** The proud have digged pits for me, Who are not according to thy law.

<a id="v86"></a>**86** All thy commandments are faithful: They persecute me wrongfully; help thou me.

<a id="v87"></a>**87** They had almost consumed me upon earth; But I forsook not thy precepts.

<a id="v88"></a>**88** Quicken me after thy lovingkindness; So shall I observe the testimony of thy mouth. ל LAMEDH.

<a id="v89"></a>**89** For ever, O Jehovah, Thy word is settled in heaven.

<a id="v90"></a>**90** Thy faithfulness is unto all generations: Thou hast established the earth, and it abideth.

<a id="v91"></a>**91** They abide this day according to thine ordinances; For all things are thy servants.

<a id="v92"></a>**92** Unless thy law had been my delight, I should then have perished in mine affliction.

<a id="v93"></a>**93** I will never forget thy precepts; For with them thou hast quickened me.

<a id="v94"></a>**94** I am thine, save me; For I have sought thy precepts.

<a id="v95"></a>**95** The wicked have waited for me, to destroy me; But I will consider thy testimonies.

<a id="v96"></a>**96** I have seen an end of all perfection; But thy commandment is exceeding broad. מ MEM.

<a id="v97"></a>**97** Oh how love I thy law! It is my meditation all the day.

<a id="v98"></a>**98** Thy commandments make me wiser than mine enemies; For they are ever with me.

<a id="v99"></a>**99** I have more understanding than all my teachers; For thy testimonies are my meditation.

<a id="v100"></a>**100** I understand more than the aged, Because I have kept thy precepts.

<a id="v101"></a>**101** I have refrained my feet from every evil way, That I might observe thy word.

<a id="v102"></a>**102** I have not turned aside from thine ordinances; For thou hast taught me.

<a id="v103"></a>**103** How sweet are thy words unto my taste! Yea, sweeter than honey to my mouth!

<a id="v104"></a>**104** Through thy precepts I get understanding: Therefore I hate every false way. נ NUN.

<a id="v105"></a>**105** Thy word is a lamp unto my feet, And light unto my path.

<a id="v106"></a>**106** I have sworn, and have confirmed it, That I will observe thy righteous ordinances.

<a id="v107"></a>**107** I am afflicted very much: Quicken me, O Jehovah, according unto thy word.

<a id="v108"></a>**108** Accept, I beseech thee, the freewill-offerings of my mouth, O Jehovah, And teach me thine ordinances.

<a id="v109"></a>**109** My soul is continually in my hand; Yet do I not forget thy law.

<a id="v110"></a>**110** The wicked have laid a snare for me; Yet have I not gone astray from thy precepts.

<a id="v111"></a>**111** Thy testimonies have I taken as a heritage for ever; For they are the rejoicing of my heart.

<a id="v112"></a>**112** I have inclined my heart to perform thy statutes For ever, even unto the end. ס SAMEKH.

<a id="v113"></a>**113** I hate them that are of a double mind; But thy law do I love.

<a id="v114"></a>**114** Thou art my hiding-place and my shield: I hope in thy word.

<a id="v115"></a>**115** Depart from me, ye evil-doers, That I may keep the commandments of my God.

<a id="v116"></a>**116** Uphold me according unto thy word, that I may live; And let me not be ashamed of my hope.

<a id="v117"></a>**117** Hold thou me up, and I shall be safe, And shall have respect unto thy statutes continually.

<a id="v118"></a>**118** Thou hast set at nought all them that err from thy statutes; For their deceit is falsehood.

<a id="v119"></a>**119** Thou puttest away all the wicked of the earth like dross: Therefore I love thy testimonies.

<a id="v120"></a>**120** My flesh trembleth for fear of thee; And I am afraid of thy judgments. ע AYIN.

<a id="v121"></a>**121** I have done justice and righteousness: Leave me not to mine oppressors.

<a id="v122"></a>**122** Be surety for thy servant for good: Let not the proud oppress me.

<a id="v123"></a>**123** Mine eyes fail for thy salvation, And for thy righteous word.

<a id="v124"></a>**124** Deal with thy servant according unto thy lovingkindness, And teach me thy statutes.

<a id="v125"></a>**125** I am thy servant; give me understanding, That I may know thy testimonies.

<a id="v126"></a>**126** It is time for Jehovah to work; Forthey have made void thy law.

<a id="v127"></a>**127** Therefore I love thy commandments Above gold, yea, above fine gold.

<a id="v128"></a>**128** Therefore I esteem allthyprecepts concerning allthingsto be right; AndI hate every false way. פ PE.

<a id="v129"></a>**129** Thy testimonies are wonderful; Therefore doth my soul keep them.

<a id="v130"></a>**130** The opening of thy words giveth light; It giveth understanding unto the simple.

<a id="v131"></a>**131** I opened wide my mouth, and panted; For I longed for thy commandments.

<a id="v132"></a>**132** Turn thee unto me, and have mercy upon me, As thou usest to do unto those that love thy name.

<a id="v133"></a>**133** Establish my footsteps in thy word; And let not any iniquity have dominion over me.

<a id="v134"></a>**134** Redeem me from the oppression of man: So will I observe thy precepts.

<a id="v135"></a>**135** Make thy face to shine upon thy servant; And teach me thy statutes.

<a id="v136"></a>**136** Streams of water run down mine eyes, Because they observe not thy law. צ TSADHE.

<a id="v137"></a>**137** Righteous art thou, O Jehovah, And upright are thy judgments.

<a id="v138"></a>**138** Thou hast commanded thy testimonies in righteousness And very faithfulness.

<a id="v139"></a>**139** My zeal hath consumed me, Because mine adversaries have forgotten thy words.

<a id="v140"></a>**140** Thy word is very pure; Therefore thy servant loveth it.

<a id="v141"></a>**141** I am small and despised; Yet do I not forget thy precepts.

<a id="v142"></a>**142** Thy righteousness is an everlasting righteousness, And thy law is truth.

<a id="v143"></a>**143** Trouble and anguish have taken hold on me; Yet thy commandments are my delight.

<a id="v144"></a>**144** Thy testimonies are righteous for ever: Give me understanding, and I shall live. ק QOPH.

<a id="v145"></a>**145** I have called with my whole heart; answer me, O Jehovah: I will keep thy statutes.

<a id="v146"></a>**146** I have called unto thee; save me, And I shall observe thy testimonies.

<a id="v147"></a>**147** I anticipated the dawning of the morning, and cried: I hoped in thy words.

<a id="v148"></a>**148** Mine eyes anticipated the night-watches, That I might meditate on thy word.

<a id="v149"></a>**149** Hear my voice according unto thy lovingkindness: Quicken me, O Jehovah, according to thine ordinances.

<a id="v151"></a>**151** Thou art nigh, O Jehovah; And all thy commandments are truth.

<a id="v159"></a>**159** Consider how I love thy precepts: Quicken me, O Jehovah, according to thy lovingkindness.
