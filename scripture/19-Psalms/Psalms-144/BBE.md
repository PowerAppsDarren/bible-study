# Psalms 144 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise be to the God of my strength, teaching my hands the use of the sword, and my fingers the art of fighting:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He is my strength, and my Rock; my high tower, and my saviour; my keeper and my hope: he gives me authority over my people.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Lord, what is man, that you keep him in mind? or the son of man that you take him into account?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Man is like a breath: his life is like a shade which is quickly gone.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Come down, O Lord, from your heavens: at your touch let the mountains give out smoke.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** With your storm-flames send them in flight: send out your arrows for their destruction.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Put out your hand from on high; make me free, take me safely out of the great waters, and out of the hands of strange men;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** In whose mouths are false words, and whose right hand is a right hand of deceit.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will make a new song to you, O God; I will make melody to you on an instrument of ten cords.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** It is God who gives salvation to kings; and who kept his servant David from the wounding sword.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Make me free, and take me out of the hands of strange men, in whose mouths are false words, and whose right hand is a right hand of deceit.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Our sons are like tall young plants; and our daughters like the shining stones of a king's house;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Our store-houses are full of all good things; and our sheep give birth to thousands and ten thousands in our fields.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Our oxen are well weighted down; our cows give birth safely; there is no going out, and there is no cry of sorrow in our open places.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Happy is the nation whose ways are so ordered: yes, happy is the nation whose God is the Lord.
