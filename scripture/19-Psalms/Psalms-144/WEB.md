# Psalms 144 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Blessed be Yahweh, my rock, who teaches my hands to war, and my fingers to battle:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** my loving kindness, my fortress, my high tower, my deliverer, my shield, and he in whom I take refuge; who subdues my people under me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yahweh, what is man, that you care for him? Or the son of man, that you think of him?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Man is like a breath. His days are like a shadow that passes away.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Part your heavens, Yahweh, and come down. Touch the mountains, and they will smoke.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Throw out lightning, and scatter them. Send out your arrows, and rout them.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Stretch out your hand from above, rescue me, and deliver me out of great waters, out of the hands of foreigners;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** whose mouths speak deceit, Whose right hand is a right hand of falsehood.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will sing a new song to you, God. On a ten-stringed lyre, I will sing praises to you.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** You are he who gives salvation to kings, who rescues David, his servant, from the deadly sword.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Rescue me, and deliver me out of the hands of foreigners, whose mouths speak deceit, whose right hand is a right hand of falsehood.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Then our sons will be like well-nurtured plants, our daughters like pillars carved to adorn a palace.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Our barns are full, filled with all kinds of provision. Our sheep produce thousands and ten thousands in our fields.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Our oxen will pull heavy loads. There is no breaking in, and no going away, and no outcry in our streets.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Happy are the people who are in such a situation. Happy are the people whose God is Yahweh.
