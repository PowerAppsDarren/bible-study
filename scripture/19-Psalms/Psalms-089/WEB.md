# Psalms 89 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will sing of the loving kindness of Yahweh forever. With my mouth, I will make known your faithfulness to all generations.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I indeed declare, “Love stands firm forever. You established the heavens. Your faithfulness is in them.”

<a id="v3"></a>**3** “I have made a covenant with my chosen one, I have sworn to David, my servant,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** ‘I will establish your seed forever, and build up your throne to all generations.’” Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The heavens will praise your wonders, Yahweh; your faithfulness also in the assembly of the holy ones.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For who in the skies can be compared to Yahweh? Who among the sons of the heavenly beings is like Yahweh,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** a very awesome God in the council of the holy ones, to be feared above all those who are around him?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yahweh, God of Armies, who is a mighty one, like you? Yah, your faithfulness is around you.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** You rule the pride of the sea. When its waves rise up, you calm them.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** You have broken Rahab in pieces, like one of the slain. You have scattered your enemies with your mighty arm.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The heavens are yours. The earth also is yours; the world and its fullness. You have founded them.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The north and the south, you have created them. Tabor and Hermon rejoice in your name.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** You have a mighty arm. Your hand is strong, and your right hand is exalted.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Righteousness and justice are the foundation of your throne. Loving kindness and truth go before your face.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Blessed are the people who learn to acclaim you. They walk in the light of your presence, Yahweh.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** In your name they rejoice all day. In your righteousness, they are exalted.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For you are the glory of their strength. In your favor, our horn will be exalted.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For our shield belongs to Yahweh; our king to the Holy One of Israel.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Then you spoke in vision to your saints, and said, “I have bestowed strength on the warrior. I have exalted a young man from the people.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** I have found David, my servant. I have anointed him with my holy oil,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** with whom my hand shall be established. My arm will also strengthen him.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** No enemy will tax him. No wicked man will oppress him.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** I will beat down his adversaries before him, and strike those who hate him.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** But my faithfulness and my loving kindness will be with him. In my name, his horn will be exalted.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** I will set his hand also on the sea, and his right hand on the rivers.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** He will call to me, ‘You are my Father, my God, and the rock of my salvation!’

<a id="v27"></a>**27** I will also appoint him my firstborn, the highest of the kings of the earth.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** I will keep my loving kindness for him forever more. My covenant will stand firm with him.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** I will also make his seed endure forever, and his throne as the days of heaven.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** If his children forsake my law, and don’t walk in my ordinances;

<a id="v31"></a>**31** if they break my statutes, and don’t keep my commandments;

<a id="v32"></a>**32** then I will punish their sin with the rod, and their iniquity with stripes.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** But I will not completely take my loving kindness from him, nor allow my faithfulness to fail.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** I will not break my covenant, nor alter what my lips have uttered.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Once have I sworn by my holiness, I will not lie to David.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** His seed will endure forever, his throne like the sun before me.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** It will be established forever like the moon, the faithful witness in the sky.” Selah.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** But you have rejected and spurned. You have been angry with your anointed.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** You have renounced the covenant of your servant. You have defiled his crown in the dust.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** You have broken down all his hedges. You have brought his strongholds to ruin.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** All who pass by the way rob him. He has become a reproach to his neighbors.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** You have exalted the right hand of his adversaries. You have made all of his enemies rejoice.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Yes, you turn back the edge of his sword, and haven’t supported him in battle.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** You have ended his splendor, and thrown his throne down to the ground.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** You have shortened the days of his youth. You have covered him with shame. Selah.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** How long, Yahweh? Will you hide yourself forever? Will your wrath burn like fire?

<a id="v47"></a>**47** Remember how short my time is! For what vanity have you created all the children of men!

<a id="v48"></a>**48** What man is he who shall live and not see death, who shall deliver his soul from the power of Sheol? Selah.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** Lord, where are your former loving kindnesses, which you swore to David in your faithfulness?

<a id="v50"></a>**50** Remember, Lord, the reproach of your servants, how I bear in my heart the taunts of all the mighty peoples,

<a id="v51"></a>**51** With which your enemies have mocked, Yahweh, with which they have mocked the footsteps of your anointed one.

<a id="v52"></a>**52** Blessed be Yahweh forever more. Amen, and Amen.
