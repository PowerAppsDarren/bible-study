# Psalms 89 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My song will be of the mercies of the Lord for ever: with my mouth will I make his faith clear to all generations.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For you have said, Mercy will be made strong for ever; my faith will be unchanging in the heavens.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I have made an agreement with the man of my selection, I have made an oath to David my servant;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will make your seed go on for ever, your kingdom will be strong through all generations. (Selah.)

<a id="v5"></a>**5** In heaven let them give praise for your wonders, O Lord; and your unchanging faith among the saints.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For who is there in the heavens in comparison with the Lord? who is like the Lord among the sons of the gods?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** God is greatly to be feared among the saints, and to be honoured over all those who are about him.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** O Lord God of armies, who is strong like you, O Jah? and your unchanging faith is round about you.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** You have rule over the sea in storm; when its waves are troubled, you make them calm.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Rahab was crushed by you like one wounded to death; with your strong arm you put to flight all your haters.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Yours are the heavens, and the earth is yours; you have made the world, and everything which is in it.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** You have made the north and the south; Tabor and Hermon are sounding with joy at your name.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Yours is an arm of power; strong is your hand and high your right hand.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The seat of your kingdom is resting on righteousness and right judging: mercy and good faith come before your face.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Happy are the people who have knowledge of the holy cry: the light of your face, O Lord, will be shining on their way.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** In your name will they have joy all the day: in your righteousness will they be lifted up.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For you are the glory of their strength; in your pleasure will our horn be lifted up.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For our breastplate is the Lord; and our king is the Holy One of Israel's.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Then your voice came to your holy one in a vision, saying, I have put the crown on a strong one, lifting up one taken from among the people.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** I have made discovery of David my servant; I have put my holy oil on his head.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** My hand will be his support; my arm will give him strength.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The deceit of those who are against him will not overcome him; he will not be troubled by the sons of evil.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** I will have those who are against him broken before his face, and his haters will be crushed under my blows.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** But my faith and my mercy will be with him; and in my name will his horn be lifted up.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** I will put his hand in the sea, and his right hand in the rivers.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** He will say to me, You are my father, my God, and the Rock of my salvation.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** And I will make him the first of my sons, most high over the kings of the earth.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** I will keep my mercy for him for ever; my agreement with him will not be changed.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** His seed will keep their place for ever; his kingdom will be eternal, like the heavens.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** If his children give up my law, and are not ruled by my decisions;

<a id="v31"></a>**31** If my rules are broken, and my orders are not kept;

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Then I will send punishment on them for their sin; my rod will be the reward of their evil-doing.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** But I will not take away my mercy from him, and will not be false to my faith.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** I will be true to my agreement; the things which have gone out of my lips will not be changed.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** I have made an oath once by my holy name, that I will not be false to David.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** His seed will not come to an end for ever; the seat of his kingdom will be like the sun before me.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** It will be fixed for ever like the moon; and the witness in heaven is true. (Selah.)

<a id="v38"></a>**38** But you have put him away in disgust; you have been angry with the king of your selection.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** You have made your agreement with your servant of no effect: you have had no respect for his crown, it has come down even to the earth.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** All his walls are broken down; you have given his strong towers to destruction.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** All those who come by take away his goods; he is laughed at by his neighbours.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** You have given power to the right hand of his haters; you have made glad all those who are against him.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** His sword is turned back; you have not been his support in the fight.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** You have put an end to his glory: the seat of his kingdom has been levelled to the earth.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** You have made him old before his time; he is covered with shame. (Selah.)

<a id="v46"></a>**46** How long, O Lord, will you Keep yourself for ever from our eyes? how long will your wrath be burning like fire?

<a id="v47"></a>**47** See how short my time is; why have you made all men for no purpose?

<a id="v48"></a>**48** What man now living will not see death? will he be able to keep back his soul from the underworld? (Selah.)

<a id="v49"></a>**49** Lord, where are your earlier mercies? where is the oath which you made to David in unchanging faith?

<a id="v50"></a>**50** Keep in mind, O Lord, the shame of your servants, and how the bitter words of all the people have come into my heart;

<a id="v51"></a>**51** The bitter words of your haters, O Lord, shaming the footsteps of your king.

<a id="v52"></a>**52** Let the Lord be praised for ever. So be it, So be it.
