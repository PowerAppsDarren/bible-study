# Psalms 89 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will sing of the lovingkindness of Jehovah for ever: With my mouth will I make known thy faithfulness to all generations.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For I have said, Mercy shall be built up for ever; Thy faithfulness wilt thou establish in the very heavens.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I have made a covenant with my chosen, I have sworn unto David my servant:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thy seed will I establish for ever, And build up thy throne to all generations. [Selah

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And the heavens shall praise thy wonders, O Jehovah; Thy faithfulness also in the assembly of the holy ones.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For who in the skies can be compared unto Jehovah? Who among the sons of the mighty is like unto Jehovah,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** A God very terrible in the council of the holy ones, And to be feared above all them that are round about him?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** O Jehovah God of hosts, Who is a mighty one, like unto thee, O Jehovah? And thy faithfulness is round about thee.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Thou rulest the pride of the sea: When the waves thereof arise, thou stillest them.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Thou hast broken Rahab in pieces, as one that is slain; Thou hast scattered thine enemies with the arm of thy strength.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The heavens are thine, the earth also is thine: The world and the fulness thereof, thou hast founded them.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The north and the south, thou hast created them: Tabor and Hermon rejoice in thy name.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thou hast a mighty arm; Strong is thy hand, and high is thy right hand.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Righteousness and justice are the foundation of thy throne: Lovingkindness and truth go before thy face.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Blessed is the people that know the joyful sound: They walk, O Jehovah, in the light of thy countenance.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** In thy name do they rejoice all the day; And in thy righteousness are they exalted.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For thou art the glory of their strength; And in thy favor our horn shall be exalted.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For our shield belongeth unto Jehovah; And our king to the Holy One of Israel.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Then thou spakest in vision to thy saints, And saidst, I have laid help upon one that is mighty; I have exalted one chosen out of the people.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** I have found David my servant; With my holy oil have I anointed him:

<a id="v21"></a>**21** With whom my hand shall be established; Mine arm also shall strengthen him.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The enemy shall not exact from him, Nor the son of wickedness afflict him.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And I will beat down his adversaries before him, And smite them that hate him.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** But my faithfulness and my lovingkindness shall be with him; And in my name shall his horn be exalted.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** I will set his hand also on the sea, And his right hand on the rivers.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** He shall cry unto me, Thou art my Father, My God, and the rock of my salvation.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** I also will make him my first-born, The highest of the kings of the earth.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** My lovingkindness will I keep for him for evermore; And my covenant shall stand fast with him.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** His seed also will I make to endure for ever, And his throne as the days of heaven.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** If his children forsake my law, And walk not in mine ordinances;

<a id="v31"></a>**31** If they break my statutes, And keep not my commandments;

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Then will I visit their transgression with the rod, And their iniquity with stripes.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** But my lovingkindness will I not utterly take from him, Nor suffer my faithfulness to fail.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** My covenant will I not break, Nor alter the thing that is gone out of my lips.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Once have I sworn by my holiness: I will not lie unto David:

<a id="v36"></a>**36** His seed shall endure for ever, And his throne as the sun before me.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** It shall be established for ever as the moon, Andasthe faithful witness in the sky. [Selah

<a id="v38"></a>**38** But thou hast cast off and rejected, Thou hast been wroth with thine anointed.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** Thou hast abhorred the covenant of thy servant: Thou hast profaned his crown by casting it to the ground.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** Thou hast broken down all his hedges; Thou hast brought his strongholds to ruin.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** All that pass by the way rob him: He is become a reproach to his neighbors.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** Thou hast exalted the right hand of his adversaries; Thou hast made all his enemies to rejoice.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Yea, thou turnest back the edge of his sword, And hast not made him to stand in the battle.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** Thou hast made his brightness to cease, And cast his throne down to the ground.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** The days of his youth hast thou shortened: Thou hast covered him with shame. [Selah

<a id="v46"></a>**46** How long, O Jehovah? wilt thou hide thyself for ever? How longshall thy wrath burn like fire?

<a id="v47"></a>**47** Oh remember how short my time is: For what vanity hast thou created all the children of men!

<a id="v48"></a>**48** What man is he that shall live and not see death, That shall deliver his soul from the power of Sheol? [Selah

<a id="v49"></a>**49** Lord, where are thy former lovingkindnesses, Which thou swarest unto David in thy faithfulness?

<a id="v50"></a>**50** Remember, Lord, the reproach of thy servants; How I do bear in my bosom the reproach of all the mighty peoples,

<a id="v51"></a>**51** Wherewith thine enemies have reproached, O Jehovah, Wherewith they have reproached the footsteps of thine anointed.

<a id="v52"></a>**52** Blessed be Jehovah for evermore. Amen, and Amen.
