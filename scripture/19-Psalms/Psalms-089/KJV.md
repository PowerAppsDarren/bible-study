# Psalms 89 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will sing of the mercies of the Lord for ever: with my mouth will I make known thy faithfulness to all generations.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For I have said, Mercy shall be built up for ever: thy faithfulness shalt thou establish in the very heavens.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I have made a covenant with my chosen, I have sworn unto David my servant,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thy seed will I establish for ever, and build up thy throne to all generations. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And the heavens shall praise thy wonders, O Lord: thy faithfulness also in the congregation of the saints.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For who in the heaven can be compared unto the Lord? who among the sons of the mighty can be likened unto the Lord?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** God is greatly to be feared in the assembly of the saints, and to be had in reverence of all them that are about him.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** O Lord God of hosts, who is a strong Lord like unto thee? or to thy faithfulness round about thee?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Thou rulest the raging of the sea: when the waves thereof arise, thou stillest them.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Thou hast broken Rahab in pieces, as one that is slain; thou hast scattered thine enemies with thy strong arm.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The heavens are thine, the earth also is thine: as for the world and the fulness thereof, thou hast founded them.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The north and the south thou hast created them: Tabor and Hermon shall rejoice in thy name.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thou hast a mighty arm: strong is thy hand, and high is thy right hand.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Justice and judgment are the habitation of thy throne: mercy and truth shall go before thy face.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Blessed is the people that know the joyful sound: they shall walk, O Lord, in the light of thy countenance.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** In thy name shall they rejoice all the day: and in thy righteousness shall they be exalted.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For thou art the glory of their strength: and in thy favour our horn shall be exalted.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For the Lord is our defence; and the Holy One of Israel is our king.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Then thou spakest in vision to thy holy one, and saidst, I have laid help upon one that is mighty; I have exalted one chosen out of the people.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** I have found David my servant; with my holy oil have I anointed him:

<a id="v21"></a>**21** With whom my hand shall be established: mine arm also shall strengthen him.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The enemy shall not exact upon him; nor the son of wickedness afflict him.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And I will beat down his foes before his face, and plague them that hate him.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** But my faithfulness and my mercy shall be with him: and in my name shall his horn be exalted.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** I will set his hand also in the sea, and his right hand in the rivers.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** He shall cry unto me, Thou art my father, my God, and the rock of my salvation.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Also I will make him my firstborn, higher than the kings of the earth.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** My mercy will I keep for him for evermore, and my covenant shall stand fast with him.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** His seed also will I make to endure for ever, and his throne as the days of heaven.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** If his children forsake my law, and walk not in my judgments;

<a id="v31"></a>**31** If they break my statutes, and keep not my commandments;

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Then will I visit their transgression with the rod, and their iniquity with stripes.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Nevertheless my lovingkindness will I not utterly take from him, nor suffer my faithfulness to fail.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** My covenant will I not break, nor alter the thing that is gone out of my lips.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Once have I sworn by my holiness that I will not lie unto David.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** His seed shall endure for ever, and his throne as the sun before me.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** It shall be established for ever as the moon, and as a faithful witness in heaven. Selah.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** But thou hast cast off and abhorred, thou hast been wroth with thine anointed.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** Thou hast made void the covenant of thy servant: thou hast profaned his crown by casting it to the ground.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** Thou hast broken down all his hedges; thou hast brought his strong holds to ruin.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** All that pass by the way spoil him: he is a reproach to his neighbours.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** Thou hast set up the right hand of his adversaries; thou hast made all his enemies to rejoice.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Thou hast also turned the edge of his sword, and hast not made him to stand in the battle.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** Thou hast made his glory to cease, and cast his throne down to the ground.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** The days of his youth hast thou shortened: thou hast covered him with shame. Selah.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** How long, Lord? wilt thou hide thyself for ever? shall thy wrath burn like fire?

<a id="v47"></a>**47** Remember how short my time is: wherefore hast thou made all men in vain?

<a id="v48"></a>**48** What man is he that liveth, and shall not see death? shall he deliver his soul from the hand of the grave? Selah.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** Lord, where are thy former lovingkindnesses, which thou swarest unto David in thy truth?

<a id="v50"></a>**50** Remember, Lord, the reproach of thy servants; how I do bear in my bosom the reproach of all the mighty people;

<a id="v51"></a>**51** Wherewith thine enemies have reproached, O Lord; wherewith they have reproached the footsteps of thine anointed.

<a id="v52"></a>**52** Blessed be the Lord for evermore. Amen, and Amen.
