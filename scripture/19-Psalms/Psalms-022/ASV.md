# Psalms 22 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My God, my God, why hast thou forsaken me? Why art thou sofar from helping me, and from the words of my groaning?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** O my God, I cry in the daytime, but thou answerest not; And in the night season, and am not silent.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But thou art holy, O thou that inhabitest the praises of Israel.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Our fathers trusted in thee: They trusted, and thou didst deliver them.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They cried unto thee, and were delivered: They trusted in thee, and were not put to shame.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** But I am a worm, and no man; A reproach of men, and despised of the people.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** All they that see me laugh me to scorn: They shoot out the lip, they shake the head, saying,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Commit thyself unto Jehovah; let him deliver him: Let him rescue him, seeing he delighteth in him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But thou art he that took me out of the womb; Thou didst make me trust when I was upon my mother’s breasts.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I was cast upon thee from the womb; Thou art my God since my mother bare me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Be not far from me; for trouble is near; For there is none to help.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Many bulls have compassed me; Strong bulls of Bashan have beset me round.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** They gape upon me with their mouth, Asa ravening and a roaring lion.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I am poured out like water, And all my bones are out of joint: My heart is like wax; It is melted within me.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** My strength is dried up like a potsherd; And my tongue cleaveth to my jaws; And thou hast brought me into the dust of death.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For dogs have compassed me: A company of evil-doers have inclosed me; They pierced my hands and my feet.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I may count all my bones. They look and stare upon me;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** They part my garments among them, And upon my vesture do they cast lots.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** But be not thou far off, O Jehovah: O thou my succor, haste thee to help me.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Deliver my soul from the sword, My darling from the power of the dog.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Save me from the lion’s mouth; Yea, from the horns of the wild-oxen thou hast answered me.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** I will declare thy name unto my brethren: In the midst of the assembly will I praise thee.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Ye that fear Jehovah, praise him; All ye the seed of Jacob, glorify him; And stand in awe of him, all ye the seed of Israel.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** For he hath not despised nor abhorred the affliction of the afflicted; Neither hath he hid his face from him; But when he cried unto him, he heard.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Of thee cometh my praise in the great assembly: I will pay my vows before them that fear him.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** The meek shall eat and be satisfied; They shall praise Jehovah that seek after him: Let your heart live for ever.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** All the ends of the earth shall remember and turn unto Jehovah; And all the kindreds of the nations shall worship before thee.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** For the kingdom is Jehovah’s; And he is the ruler over the nations.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** All the fat ones of the earth shall eat and worship: All they that go down to the dust shall bow before him, Even he that cannot keep his soul alive.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** A seed shall serve him; It shall be told of the Lord unto the next generation.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** They shall come and shall declare his righteousness Unto a people that shall be born, that he hath done it.
