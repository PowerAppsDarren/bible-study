# Psalms 22 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My God, my God, why hast thou forsaken me? why art thou so far from helping me, and from the words of my roaring?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** O my God, I cry in the daytime, but thou hearest not; and in the night season, and am not silent.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But thou art holy, O thou that inhabitest the praises of Israel.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Our fathers trusted in thee: they trusted, and thou didst deliver them.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They cried unto thee, and were delivered: they trusted in thee, and were not confounded.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** But I am a worm, and no man; a reproach of men, and despised of the people.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** All they that see me laugh me to scorn: they shoot out the lip, they shake the head, saying,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He trusted on the Lord that he would deliver him: let him deliver him, seeing he delighted in him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But thou art he that took me out of the womb: thou didst make me hope when I was upon my mother’s breasts.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I was cast upon thee from the womb: thou art my God from my mother’s belly.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Be not far from me; for trouble is near; for there is none to help.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Many bulls have compassed me: strong bulls of Bashan have beset me round.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** They gaped upon me with their mouths, as a ravening and a roaring lion.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I am poured out like water, and all my bones are out of joint: my heart is like wax; it is melted in the midst of my bowels.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** My strength is dried up like a potsherd; and my tongue cleaveth to my jaws; and thou hast brought me into the dust of death.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For dogs have compassed me: the assembly of the wicked have inclosed me: they pierced my hands and my feet.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I may tell all my bones: they look and stare upon me.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** They part my garments among them, and cast lots upon my vesture.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** But be not thou far from me, O Lord: O my strength, haste thee to help me.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Deliver my soul from the sword; my darling from the power of the dog.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Save me from the lion’s mouth: for thou hast heard me from the horns of the unicorns.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** I will declare thy name unto my brethren: in the midst of the congregation will I praise thee.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Ye that fear the Lord, praise him; all ye the seed of Jacob, glorify him; and fear him, all ye the seed of Israel.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** For he hath not despised nor abhorred the affliction of the afflicted; neither hath he hid his face from him; but when he cried unto him, he heard.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** My praise shall be of thee in the great congregation: I will pay my vows before them that fear him.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** The meek shall eat and be satisfied: they shall praise the Lord that seek him: your heart shall live for ever.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** All the ends of the world shall remember and turn unto the Lord: and all the kindreds of the nations shall worship before thee.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** For the kingdom is the Lord’s: and he is the governor among the nations.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** All they that be fat upon earth shall eat and worship: all they that go down to the dust shall bow before him: and none can keep alive his own soul.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** A seed shall serve him; it shall be accounted to the Lord for a generation.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** They shall come, and shall declare his righteousness unto a people that shall be born, that he hath done this.
