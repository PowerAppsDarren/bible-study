# Psalms 22 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My God, my God, why are you turned away from me? why are you so far from helping me, and from the words of my crying?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** O my God, I make my cry in the day, and you give no answer; and in the night, and have no rest.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But you are holy, O you who are seated among the praises of Israel.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Our fathers had faith in you: they had faith and you were their saviour.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They sent up their cry to you and were made free: they put their faith in you and were not put to shame.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** But I am a worm and not a man; cursed by men, and looked down on by the people.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I am laughed at by all those who see me: pushing out their lips and shaking their heads they say,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He put his faith in the Lord; let the Lord be his saviour now: let the Lord be his saviour, because he had delight in him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But it was you who took care of me from the day of my birth: you gave me faith even from my mother's breasts.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I was in your hands even before my birth; you are my God from the time when I was in my mother's body.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Be not far from me, for trouble is near; there is no one to give help.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** A great herd of oxen is round me: I am shut in by the strong oxen of Bashan.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I saw their mouths wide open, like lions crying after food.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I am flowing away like water, and all my bones are out of place: my heart is like wax, it has become soft in my body.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** My throat is dry like a broken vessel; my tongue is fixed to the roof of my mouth, and the dust of death is on my lips.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Dogs have come round me: I am shut in by the band of evil-doers; they made wounds in my hands and feet.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I am able to see all my bones; their looks are fixed on me:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** They make a division of my robes among them, by the decision of chance they take my clothing.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Do not be far from me, O Lord: O my strength, come quickly to my help.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Make my soul safe from the sword, my life from the power of the dog.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Be my saviour from the lion's mouth; let me go free from the horns of the cruel oxen.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** I will give the knowledge of your name to my brothers: I will give you praise among the people.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** You who have fear of the Lord, give him praise; all you seed of Jacob, give him glory; go in fear of him, all you seed of Israel.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** For he has not been unmoved by the pain of him who is troubled; or kept his face covered from him; but he has given an answer to his cry.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** My praise will be of you in the great meeting: I will make my offerings before his worshippers.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** The poor will have a feast of good things: those who make search for the Lord will give him praise: your heart will have life for ever.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** All the ends of the earth will keep it in mind and be turned to the Lord: all the families of the nations will give him worship.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** For the kingdom is the Lord's; he is the ruler among the nations.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** All the fat ones of the earth will give him worship; all those who go down to the dust will make themselves low before him, even he who has not enough for the life of his soul.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** A seed will be his servant; the doings of the Lord will be made clear to the generation which comes after.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** They will come and make his righteousness clear to a people of the future because he has done this.
