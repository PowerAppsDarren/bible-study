# Psalms 7 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Lord my God, I put my faith in you; take me out of the hands of him who is cruel to me, and make me free;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** So that he may not come rushing on my soul like a lion, wounding it, while there is no one to be my saviour.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** O Lord my God, if I have done this; if my hands have done any wrong;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** If I have given back evil to him who did evil to me, or have taken anything from him who was against me without cause;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let my hater go after my soul and take it; let my life be crushed to the earth, and my honour into the dust. (Selah.)

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Come up, Lord, in your wrath; be lifted up against my haters; be awake, my God, give orders for the judging.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The meeting of the nations will be round you; take your seat, then, over them, on high.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The Lord will be judge of the peoples; give a decision for me, O Lord, because of my righteousness, and let my virtue have its reward.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** O let the evil of the evil-doer come to an end, but give strength to the upright: for men's minds and hearts are tested by the God of righteousness.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** God, who is the saviour of the upright in heart, is my breastplate.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** God is the judge of the upright, and is angry with the evil-doers every day.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** If a man is not turned from his evil, he will make his sword sharp; his bow is bent and ready.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He has made ready for him the instruments of death; he makes his arrows flames of fire.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** That man is a worker of evil; the seed of wrongdoing has given birth to deceit.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He has made a hole deep in the earth, and is falling into the hole which he has made

<a id="v16"></a>**16** His wrongdoing will come back to him, and his violent behaviour will come down on his head.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I will give praise to the Lord for his righteousness; I will make a song to the name of the Lord Most High.
