# Psalms 7 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Lord my God, in thee do I put my trust: save me from all them that persecute me, and deliver me:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Lest he tear my soul like a lion, rending it in pieces, while there is none to deliver.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** O Lord my God, if I have done this; if there be iniquity in my hands;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** If I have rewarded evil unto him that was at peace with me; (yea, I have delivered him that without cause is mine enemy:)

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let the enemy persecute my soul, and take it; yea, let him tread down my life upon the earth, and lay mine honour in the dust. Selah.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Arise, O Lord, in thine anger, lift up thyself because of the rage of mine enemies: and awake for me to the judgment that thou hast commanded.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** So shall the congregation of the people compass thee about: for their sakes therefore return thou on high.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The Lord shall judge the people: judge me, O Lord, according to my righteousness, and according to mine integrity that is in me.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Oh let the wickedness of the wicked come to an end; but establish the just: for the righteous God trieth the hearts and reins.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** My defence is of God, which saveth the upright in heart.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** God judgeth the righteous, and God is angry with the wicked every day.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** If he turn not, he will whet his sword; he hath bent his bow, and made it ready.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He hath also prepared for him the instruments of death; he ordaineth his arrows against the persecutors.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Behold, he travaileth with iniquity, and hath conceived mischief, and brought forth falsehood.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He made a pit, and digged it, and is fallen into the ditch which he made.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** His mischief shall return upon his own head, and his violent dealing shall come down upon his own pate.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I will praise the Lord according to his righteousness: and will sing praise to the name of the Lord most high.
