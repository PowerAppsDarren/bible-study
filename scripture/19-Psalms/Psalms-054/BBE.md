# Psalms 54 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Let your name be my salvation, O God; let my cause be judged by your strength.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let my prayer come before you, O God; give ear to the words of my mouth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For men who are going after me have come out against me, violent men are purposing to take my soul; they have not put God before their eyes. (Selah.)

<a id="v4"></a>**4** See, God is my helper: the Lord is the great supporter of my soul.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let the evil works of my haters come back on them again; let them be cut off by your good faith.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Freely will I make my offerings to you; I will give praise to your name, O Lord, for it is good.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Because it has been my saviour from all my trouble; and my eyes have seen the punishment of my haters.
