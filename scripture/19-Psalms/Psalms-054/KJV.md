# Psalms 54 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Save me, O God, by thy name, and judge me by thy strength.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Hear my prayer, O God; give ear to the words of my mouth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For strangers are risen up against me, and oppressors seek after my soul: they have not set God before them. Selah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Behold, God is mine helper: the Lord is with them that uphold my soul.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He shall reward evil unto mine enemies: cut them off in thy truth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I will freely sacrifice unto thee: I will praise thy name, O Lord; for it is good.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For he hath delivered me out of all trouble: and mine eye hath seen his desire upon mine enemies.
