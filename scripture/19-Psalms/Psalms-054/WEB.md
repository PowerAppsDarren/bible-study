# Psalms 54 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Save me, God, by your name. Vindicate me in your might.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Hear my prayer, God. Listen to the words of my mouth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For strangers have risen up against me. Violent men have sought after my soul. They haven’t set God before them. Selah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Behold, God is my helper. The Lord is the one who sustains my soul.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He will repay the evil to my enemies. Destroy them in your truth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** With a free will offering, I will sacrifice to you. I will give thanks to your name, Yahweh, for it is good.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For he has delivered me out of all trouble. My eye has seen triumph over my enemies.
