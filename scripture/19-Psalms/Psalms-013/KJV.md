# Psalms 13 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** How long wilt thou forget me, O Lord? for ever? how long wilt thou hide thy face from me?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How long shall I take counsel in my soul, having sorrow in my heart daily? how long shall mine enemy be exalted over me?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Consider and hear me, O Lord my God: lighten mine eyes, lest I sleep the sleep of death;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Lest mine enemy say, I have prevailed against him; and those that trouble me rejoice when I am moved.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But I have trusted in thy mercy; my heart shall rejoice in thy salvation.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I will sing unto the Lord, because he hath dealt bountifully with me.
