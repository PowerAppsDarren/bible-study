# Psalms 13 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** How long, O Jehovah? wilt thou forget me for ever? How long wilt thou hide thy face from me?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How long shall I take counsel in my soul, Having sorrow in my heart all the day? How long shall mine enemy be exalted over me?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Considerand answer me, O Jehovah my God: Lighten mine eyes, lest I sleep the sleep of death;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Lest mine enemy say, I have prevailed against him; Lest mine adversaries rejoice when I am moved.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But I have trusted in thy lovingkindness; My heart shall rejoice in thy salvation.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I will sing unto Jehovah, Because he hath dealt bountifully with me.
