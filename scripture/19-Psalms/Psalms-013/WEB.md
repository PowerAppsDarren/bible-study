# Psalms 13 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** How long, Yahweh? Will you forget me forever? How long will you hide your face from me?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How long shall I take counsel in my soul, having sorrow in my heart every day? How long shall my enemy triumph over me?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Behold, and answer me, Yahweh, my God. Give light to my eyes, lest I sleep in death;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Lest my enemy say, “I have prevailed against him”; Lest my adversaries rejoice when I fall.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But I trust in your loving kindness. My heart rejoices in your salvation.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I will sing to Yahweh, because he has been good to me.
