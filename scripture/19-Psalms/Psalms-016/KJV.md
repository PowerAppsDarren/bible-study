# Psalms 16 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Preserve me, O God: for in thee do I put my trust.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** O my soul, thou hast said unto the Lord, Thou art my Lord: my goodness extendeth not to thee;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But to the saints that are in the earth, and to the excellent, in whom is all my delight.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their sorrows shall be multiplied that hasten after another god: their drink offerings of blood will I not offer, nor take up their names into my lips.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The Lord is the portion of mine inheritance and of my cup: thou maintainest my lot.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The lines are fallen unto me in pleasant places; yea, I have a goodly heritage.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I will bless the Lord, who hath given me counsel: my reins also instruct me in the night seasons.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I have set the Lord always before me: because he is at my right hand, I shall not be moved.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Therefore my heart is glad, and my glory rejoiceth: my flesh also shall rest in hope.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For thou wilt not leave my soul in hell; neither wilt thou suffer thine Holy One to see corruption.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Thou wilt shew me the path of life: in thy presence is fulness of joy; at thy right hand there are pleasures for evermore.
