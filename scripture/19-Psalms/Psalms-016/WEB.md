# Psalms 16 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Preserve me, God, for in you do I take refuge.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My soul, you have said to Yahweh, “You are my Lord. Apart from you I have no good thing.”

<a id="v3"></a>**3** As for the saints who are in the earth, they are the excellent ones in whom is all my delight.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their sorrows shall be multiplied who give gifts to another god. Their drink offerings of blood I will not offer, nor take their names on my lips.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Yahweh assigned my portion and my cup. You made my lot secure.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The lines have fallen to me in pleasant places. Yes, I have a good inheritance.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I will bless Yahweh, who has given me counsel. Yes, my heart instructs me in the night seasons.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I have set Yahweh always before me. Because he is at my right hand, I shall not be moved.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Therefore my heart is glad, and my tongue rejoices. My body shall also dwell in safety.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For you will not leave my soul in Sheol, neither will you allow your holy one to see corruption.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** You will show me the path of life. In your presence is fullness of joy. In your right hand there are pleasures forever more.
