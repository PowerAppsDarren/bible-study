# Psalms 28 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** To you, Yahweh, I call. My rock, don’t be deaf to me; lest, if you are silent to me, I would become like those who go down into the pit.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Hear the voice of my petitions, when I cry to you, when I lift up my hands toward your Most Holy Place.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Don’t draw me away with the wicked, with the workers of iniquity who speak peace with their neighbors, but mischief is in their hearts.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Give them according to their work, and according to the wickedness of their doings. Give them according to the operation of their hands. Bring back on them what they deserve.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Because they don’t respect the works of Yahweh, nor the operation of his hands, he will break them down and not build them up.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Blessed be Yahweh, because he has heard the voice of my petitions.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Yahweh is my strength and my shield. My heart has trusted in him, and I am helped. Therefore my heart greatly rejoices. With my song I will thank him.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yahweh is their strength. He is a stronghold of salvation to his anointed.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Save your people, and bless your inheritance. Be their shepherd also, and bear them up forever.
