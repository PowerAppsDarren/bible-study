# Psalms 28 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Unto thee, O Jehovah, will I call: My rock, be not thou deaf unto me; Lest, if thou be silent unto me, I become like them that go down into the pit.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Hear the voice of my supplications, when I cry unto thee, When I lift up my hands toward thy holy oracle.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Draw me not away with the wicked, And with the workers of iniquity; That speak peace with their neighbors, But mischief is in their hearts.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Give them according to their work, and according to the wickedness of their doings: Give them after the operation of their hands; Render to them their desert.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Because they regard not the works of Jehovah, Nor the operation of his hands, He will break them down and not build them up.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Blessed be Jehovah, Because he hath heard the voice of my supplications.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Jehovah is my strength and my shield; My heart hath trusted in him, and I am helped: Therefore my heart greatly rejoiceth; And with my song will I praise him.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Jehovah is their strength, And he is a stronghold of salvation to his anointed.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Save thy people, and bless thine inheritance: Be their shepherd also, and bear them up for ever.
