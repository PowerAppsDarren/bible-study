# Psalms 118 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O give thanks unto the Lord; for he is good: because his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let Israel now say, that his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let the house of Aaron now say, that his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Let them now that fear the Lord say, that his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I called upon the Lord in distress: the Lord answered me, and set me in a large place.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The Lord is on my side; I will not fear: what can man do unto me?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The Lord taketh my part with them that help me: therefore shall I see my desire upon them that hate me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** It is better to trust in the Lord than to put confidence in man.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** It is better to trust in the Lord than to put confidence in princes.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All nations compassed me about: but in the name of the Lord will I destroy them.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They compassed me about; yea, they compassed me about: but in the name of the Lord I will destroy them.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They compassed me about like bees; they are quenched as the fire of thorns: for in the name of the Lord I will destroy them.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thou hast thrust sore at me that I might fall: but the Lord helped me.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The Lord is my strength and song, and is become my salvation.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The voice of rejoicing and salvation is in the tabernacles of the righteous: the right hand of the Lord doeth valiantly.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The right hand of the Lord is exalted: the right hand of the Lord doeth valiantly.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I shall not die, but live, and declare the works of the Lord.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The Lord hath chastened me sore: but he hath not given me over unto death.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Open to me the gates of righteousness: I will go into them, and I will praise the Lord:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** This gate of the Lord, into which the righteous shall enter.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** I will praise thee: for thou hast heard me, and art become my salvation.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The stone which the builders refused is become the head stone of the corner.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** This is the Lord’s doing; it is marvellous in our eyes.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** This is the day which the Lord hath made; we will rejoice and be glad in it.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Save now, I beseech thee, O Lord: O Lord, I beseech thee, send now prosperity.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Blessed be he that cometh in the name of the Lord: we have blessed you out of the house of the Lord.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** God is the Lord, which hath shewed us light: bind the sacrifice with cords, even unto the horns of the altar.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Thou art my God, and I will praise thee: thou art my God, I will exalt thee.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** O give thanks unto the Lord; for he is good: for his mercy endureth for ever.
