# Psalms 118 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Oh give thanks unto Jehovah; for he is good; For his lovingkindness endureth for ever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let Israel now say, That his lovingkindness endureth for ever.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let the house of Aaron now say, That his lovingkindness endureth for ever.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Let them now that fear Jehovah say, That his lovingkindness endureth for ever.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Out of my distress I called upon Jehovah: Jehovah answered me and set me in a large place.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Jehovah is on my side; I will not fear: What can man do unto me?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Jehovah is on my side among them that help me: Therefore shall I seemy desireupon them that hate me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** It is better to take refuge in Jehovah  Than to put confidence in man.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** It is better to take refuge in Jehovah  Than to put confidence in princes.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All nations compassed me about: In the name of Jehovah I will cut them off.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They compassed me about; yea, they compassed me about: In the name of Jehovah I will cut them off.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They compassed me about like bees; they are quenched as the fire of thorns: In the name of Jehovah I will cut them off.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thou didst thrust sore at me that I might fall; But Jehovah helped me.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Jehovah is my strength and song; And he is become my salvation.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The voice of rejoicing and salvation is in the tents of the righteous: The right hand of Jehovah doeth valiantly.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The right hand of Jehovah is exalted: The right hand of Jehovah doeth valiantly.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I shall not die, but live, And declare the works of Jehovah.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Jehovah hath chastened me sore; But he hath not given me over unto death.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Open to me the gates of righteousness: I will enter into them, I will give thanks unto Jehovah.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** This is the gate of Jehovah; The righteous shall enter into it.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** I will give thanks unto thee; for thou hast answered me, And art become my salvation.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The stone which the builders rejected  Is become the head of the corner.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** This is Jehovah’s doing; It is marvellous in our eyes.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** This is the day which Jehovah hath made; We will rejoice and be glad in it.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Save now, we beseech thee, O Jehovah: O Jehovah, we beseech thee, send now prosperity.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Blessed be he that cometh in the name of Jehovah: We have blessed you out of the house of Jehovah.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Jehovah is God, and he hath given us light: Bind the sacrifice with cords, even unto the horns of the altar.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Thou art my God, and I will give thanks unto thee: Thou art my God, I will exalt thee.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Oh give thanks unto Jehovah; for he is good; For his lovingkindness endureth for ever.
