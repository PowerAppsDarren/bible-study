# Psalms 118 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O give praise to the Lord, for he is good: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let Israel now say, that his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let the house of Aaron now say, that his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Let all worshippers of the Lord now say, that his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I made my prayer to the Lord in my trouble: and the Lord gave me an answer, and put me in a wide place.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The Lord is on my side; I will have no fear: what is man able to do to me?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The Lord is my great helper: I will see my desire against my haters.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** It is better to have faith in the Lord than to put one's hope in man.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** It is better to have faith in the Lord than to put one's hope in rulers.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All the nations have come round me; but in the name of the Lord I will have them cut down.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They are round me, yes, they are all about me; but in the name of the Lord I will have them cut down.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They are round me like bees; but they are put out like a fire among thorns; for in the name of the Lord I will have them cut down.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I have been hard pushed by you, so that I might have a fall: but the Lord was my helper.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The Lord is my strength and my song; he has become my salvation.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The sound of joy and salvation is in the tents of the upright; the right hand of the Lord does works of power.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The right hand of the Lord is lifted up; the right hand of the Lord does works of power.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Life and not death will be my part, and I will give out the story of the works of the Lord.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The hand of Jah has been hard on me; but he has not given me up to death.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Let the doors of righteousness be open to me; I will go in and give praise to the Lord.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** This is the door of the Lord's house; the workers of righteousness will go in through it.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** I will give you praise, for you have given me an answer, and have become my salvation.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The stone which the builders put on one side has become the chief stone of the building.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** This is the Lord's doing; it is a wonder in our eyes.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** This is the day which the Lord has made; we will be full of joy and delight in it.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Send salvation now, O Lord; Lord, send us your blessing.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** A blessing be on him who comes in the name of the Lord; we give you blessing from the house of the Lord.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** The Lord is God, and he has given us light; let the holy dance be ordered with branches, even up to the horns of the altar.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** You are my God, and I will give you praise; my God, and I will give honour to your name.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** O give praise to the Lord, for he is good: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.
