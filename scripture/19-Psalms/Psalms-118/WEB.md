# Psalms 118 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give thanks to Yahweh, for he is good, for his loving kindness endures forever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let Israel now say that his loving kindness endures forever.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let the house of Aaron now say that his loving kindness endures forever.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Now let those who fear Yahweh say that his loving kindness endures forever.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Out of my distress, I called on Yah. Yah answered me with freedom.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Yahweh is on my side. I will not be afraid. What can man do to me?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Yahweh is on my side among those who help me. Therefore I will look in triumph at those who hate me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** It is better to take refuge in Yahweh, than to put confidence in man.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** It is better to take refuge in Yahweh, than to put confidence in princes.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All the nations surrounded me, but in Yahweh’s name, I cut them off.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They surrounded me, yes, they surrounded me. In Yahweh’s name I indeed cut them off.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They surrounded me like bees. They are quenched like the burning thorns. In Yahweh’s name I cut them off.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** You pushed me back hard, to make me fall, but Yahweh helped me.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Yah is my strength and song. He has become my salvation.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The voice of rejoicing and salvation is in the tents of the righteous. “The right hand of Yahweh does valiantly.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The right hand of Yahweh is exalted! The right hand of Yahweh does valiantly!”

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I will not die, but live, and declare Yah’s works.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Yah has punished me severely, but he has not given me over to death.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Open to me the gates of righteousness. I will enter into them. I will give thanks to Yah.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** This is the gate of Yahweh; the righteous will enter into it.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** I will give thanks to you, for you have answered me, and have become my salvation.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The stone which the builders rejected has become the head of the corner.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** This is Yahweh’s doing. It is marvelous in our eyes.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** This is the day that Yahweh has made. We will rejoice and be glad in it!

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Save us now, we beg you, Yahweh! Yahweh, we beg you, send prosperity now.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Blessed is he who comes in Yahweh’s name! We have blessed you out of Yahweh’s house.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Yahweh is God, and he has given us light. Bind the sacrifice with cords, even to the horns of the altar.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** You are my God, and I will give thanks to you. You are my God, I will exalt you.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Oh give thanks to Yahweh, for he is good, for his loving kindness endures forever.
