# Psalms 36 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The transgression of the wicked saith within my heart, There is no fear of God before his eyes.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For he flattereth himself in his own eyes, That his iniquity will not be found out and be hated.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The words of his mouth are iniquity and deceit: He hath ceased to be wise and to do good.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He deviseth iniquity upon his bed; He setteth himself in a way that is not good; He abhorreth not evil.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thy lovingkindness, O Jehovah, is in the heavens; Thy faithfulness reacheth unto the skies.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Thy righteousness is like the mountains of God; Thy judgments are a great deep: O Jehovah, thou preservest man and beast.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** How precious is thy lovingkindness, O God! And the children of men take refuge under the shadow of thy wings.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They shall be abundantly satisfied with the fatness of thy house; And thou wilt make them drink of the river of thy pleasures.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For with thee is the fountain of life: In thy light shall we see light.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Oh continue thy lovingkindness unto them that know thee, And thy righteousness to the upright in heart.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let not the foot of pride come against me, And let not the hand of the wicked drive me away.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** There are the workers of iniquity fallen: They are thrust down, and shall not be able to rise.
