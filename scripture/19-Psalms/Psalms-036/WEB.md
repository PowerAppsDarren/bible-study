# Psalms 36 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** An oracle is within my heart about the disobedience of the wicked: “There is no fear of God before his eyes.”

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For he flatters himself in his own eyes, too much to detect and hate his sin.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The words of his mouth are iniquity and deceit. He has ceased to be wise and to do good.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He plots iniquity on his bed. He sets himself in a way that is not good. He doesn’t abhor evil.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Your loving kindness, Yahweh, is in the heavens. Your faithfulness reaches to the skies.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Your righteousness is like the mountains of God. Your judgments are like a great deep. Yahweh, you preserve man and animal.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** How precious is your loving kindness, God! The children of men take refuge under the shadow of your wings.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They shall be abundantly satisfied with the abundance of your house. You will make them drink of the river of your pleasures.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For with you is the spring of life. In your light shall we see light.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Oh continue your loving kindness to those who know you, your righteousness to the upright in heart.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Don’t let the foot of pride come against me. Don’t let the hand of the wicked drive me away.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** There the workers of iniquity are fallen. They are thrust down, and shall not be able to rise.
