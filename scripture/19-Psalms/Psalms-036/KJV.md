# Psalms 36 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The transgression of the wicked saith within my heart, that there is no fear of God before his eyes.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For he flattereth himself in his own eyes, until his iniquity be found to be hateful.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The words of his mouth are iniquity and deceit: he hath left off to be wise, and to do good.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He deviseth mischief upon his bed; he setteth himself in a way that is not good; he abhorreth not evil.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thy mercy, O Lord, is in the heavens; and thy faithfulness reacheth unto the clouds.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Thy righteousness is like the great mountains; thy judgments are a great deep: O Lord, thou preservest man and beast.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** How excellent is thy lovingkindness, O God! therefore the children of men put their trust under the shadow of thy wings.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They shall be abundantly satisfied with the fatness of thy house; and thou shalt make them drink of the river of thy pleasures.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For with thee is the fountain of life: in thy light shall we see light.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** O continue thy lovingkindness unto them that know thee; and thy righteousness to the upright in heart.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let not the foot of pride come against me, and let not the hand of the wicked remove me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** There are the workers of iniquity fallen: they are cast down, and shall not be able to rise.
