# Psalms 36 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The sin of the evil-doer says in his heart, There is no fear of the Lord before his eyes.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For he takes comfort in the thought that his sin will not be uncovered and hated.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** In the words of his mouth are evil and deceit; he has given up being wise and doing good.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He gives thought to evil on his bed; he takes a way which is not good; he is not a hater of evil.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Your mercy, O Lord, is in the heavens, and your strong purpose is as high as the clouds.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Your righteousness is like the mountains of God; your judging is like the great deep; O Lord, you give life to man and beast.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** How good is your loving mercy, O God! the children of men take cover under the shade of your wings.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The delights of your house will be showered on them; you will give them drink from the river of your pleasures.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For with you is the fountain of life: in your light we will see light.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** O let there be no end to your loving mercy to those who have knowledge of you, or of your righteousness to the upright in heart.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let not the foot of pride come against me, or the hand of the evil-doers put me out of my place.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** There the workers of evil have come down: they have been made low, and will not be lifted up.
