# Psalms 62 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Truly my soul waiteth upon God: from him cometh my salvation.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He only is my rock and my salvation; he is my defence; I shall not be greatly moved.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** How long will ye imagine mischief against a man? ye shall be slain all of you: as a bowing wall shall ye be, and as a tottering fence.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They only consult to cast him down from his excellency: they delight in lies: they bless with their mouth, but they curse inwardly. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My soul, wait thou only upon God; for my expectation is from him.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He only is my rock and my salvation: he is my defence; I shall not be moved.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** In God is my salvation and my glory: the rock of my strength, and my refuge, is in God.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Trust in him at all times; ye people, pour out your heart before him: God is a refuge for us. Selah.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Surely men of low degree are vanity, and men of high degree are a lie: to be laid in the balance, they are altogether lighter than vanity.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Trust not in oppression, and become not vain in robbery: if riches increase, set not your heart upon them.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** God hath spoken once; twice have I heard this; that power belongeth unto God.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Also unto thee, O Lord, belongeth mercy: for thou renderest to every man according to his work.
