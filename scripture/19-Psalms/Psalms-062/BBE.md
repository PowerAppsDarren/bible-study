# Psalms 62 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My soul, put all your faith in God; for from him comes my salvation.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He only is my Rock and my salvation; he is my high tower; I will not be greatly moved.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** How long will you go on designing evil against a man? running against him as against a broken wall, which is on the point of falling?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their only thought is to put him down from his place of honour; their delight is in deceit: blessing is in their mouths but cursing in their hearts. (Selah.)

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My soul, put all your faith in God; for from him comes my hope.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He only is my Rock and my salvation; he is my high tower; I will not be greatly moved.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** In God is my salvation, and my glory; the Rock of my strength, and my safe place.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Have faith in him at all times, you people; let your hearts go flowing out before him: God is our safe place. (Selah.)

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Truly men of low birth are nothing, and men of high position are not what they seem; if they are put in the scales together they are less than a breath.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Have no faith in the rewards of evil-doing, or in profits wrongly made: if your wealth is increased, do not put your hopes on it.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Once has God said, twice has it come to my ears, that power is God's:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And mercy, O Lord, is yours, for you give to every man the reward of his work.
