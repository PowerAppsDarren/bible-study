# Psalms 62 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My soul waiteth in silence for God only: From himcomethmy salvation.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He only is my rock and my salvation: He ismy high tower; I shall not be greatly moved.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** How long will ye set upon a man, That ye may slay him, all of you, Like a leaning wall, like a tottering fence?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They only consult to thrust him down from his dignity; They delight in lies; They bless with their mouth, but they curse inwardly. [Selah

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My soul, wait thou in silence for God only; For my expectation is from him.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He only is my rock and my salvation: He ismy high tower; I shall not be moved.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** With God is my salvation and my glory: The rock of my strength, and my refuge, is in God.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Trust in him at all times, ye people; Pour out your heart before him: God is a refuge for us. [Selah

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Surely men of low degree are vanity, and men of high degree are a lie: In the balances they will go up; They are together lighter than vanity.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Trust not in oppression, And become not vain in robbery: If riches increase, set not your heart thereon.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** God hath spoken once, Twice have I heard this, That power belongeth unto God.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Also unto thee, O Lord, belongeth lovingkindness; For thou renderest to every man according to his work.
