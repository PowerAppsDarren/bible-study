# Psalms 62 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My soul rests in God alone. My salvation is from him.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He alone is my rock and my salvation, my fortress— I will never be greatly shaken.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** How long will you assault a man, would all of you throw him down, Like a leaning wall, like a tottering fence?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They fully intend to throw him down from his lofty place. They delight in lies. They bless with their mouth, but they curse inwardly. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My soul, wait in silence for God alone, for my expectation is from him.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He alone is my rock and my salvation, my fortress. I will not be shaken.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** With God is my salvation and my honor. The rock of my strength, and my refuge, is in God.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Trust in him at all times, you people. Pour out your heart before him. God is a refuge for us. Selah.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Surely men of low degree are just a breath, and men of high degree are a lie. In the balances they will go up. They are together lighter than a breath.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Don’t trust in oppression. Don’t become vain in robbery. If riches increase, don’t set your heart on them.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** God has spoken once; twice I have heard this, that power belongs to God.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Also to you, Lord, belongs loving kindness, for you reward every man according to his work.
