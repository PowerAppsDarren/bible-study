# Psalms 8 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Lord, our Lord, whose glory is higher than the heavens, how noble is your name in all the earth!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You have made clear your strength even out of the mouths of babies at the breast, because of those who are against you; so that you may put to shame the cruel and violent man.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When I see your heavens, the work of your fingers, the moon and the stars, which you have put in their places;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** What is man, that you keep him in mind? the son of man, that you take him into account?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For you have made him only a little lower than the gods, crowning him with glory and honour.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You have made him ruler over the works of your hands; you have put all things under his feet;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** All sheep and oxen, and all the beasts of the field;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The birds of the air and the fish of the sea, and whatever goes through the deep waters of the seas.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** O Lord, our Lord, how noble is your name in all the earth!
