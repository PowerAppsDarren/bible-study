# Psalms 8 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Lord our Lord, how excellent is thy name in all the earth! who hast set thy glory above the heavens.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Out of the mouth of babes and sucklings hast thou ordained strength because of thine enemies, that thou mightest still the enemy and the avenger.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When I consider thy heavens, the work of thy fingers, the moon and the stars, which thou hast ordained;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** What is man, that thou art mindful of him? and the son of man, that thou visitest him?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For thou hast made him a little lower than the angels, and hast crowned him with glory and honour.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Thou madest him to have dominion over the works of thy hands; thou hast put all things under his feet:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** All sheep and oxen, yea, and the beasts of the field;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The fowl of the air, and the fish of the sea, and whatsoever passeth through the paths of the seas.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** O Lord our Lord, how excellent is thy name in all the earth!
