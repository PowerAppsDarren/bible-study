# Psalms 59 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Take me out of the hands of the cruel ones, O my God; keep me safe from those who come up against me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Take me out of the power of the workers of evil, and keep me safe from the men of blood.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For see, they are watching in secret for my soul; the strong have come together against me? but not because of my sin, or my evil-doing, O Lord.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For no sin of mine they go quickly and get themselves ready; be awake and come to my help, and see.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You, O Lord God of armies, are the God of Israel; come now and give punishment to the nations; have no mercy on any workers of deceit. (Selah.)

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They come back in the evening; they make a noise like a dog, and go round the town.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** See, hate is dropping from their lips; curses are on their tongues: they say, Who gives attention to it?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But you are laughing at them, O Lord; you will make sport of all the nations.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** O my strength, I will put my hope in you; because God is my strong tower.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The God of my mercy will go before me: God will let me see my desire effected on my haters.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Put them not to death, for so my people will keep the memory of them: let them be sent in all directions by your power; make them low, O Lord our saviour.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Because of the sin of their mouths and the word of their lips, let them even be taken in their pride; and for their curses and their deceit,

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Put an end to them in your wrath, put an end to them, so that they may not be seen again; let them see that God is ruling in Jacob and to the ends of the earth. (Selah.)

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And in the evening let them come back, and make a noise like a dog, and go round the town.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let them go wandering up and down in search of food, and be there all night if they have not enough.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** But I will make songs of your power; yes, I will give cries of joy for your mercy in the morning; because you have been my strength and my high tower in the day of my trouble.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** To you, O my strength, will I make my song: because God is my high tower, even the God of my mercy.
