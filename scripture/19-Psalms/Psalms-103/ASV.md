# Psalms 103 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Bless Jehovah, O my soul; And all that is within me, blesshis holy name.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Bless Jehovah, O my soul, And forget not all his benefits:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Who forgiveth all thine iniquities; Who healeth all thy diseases;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Who redeemeth thy life from destruction; Who crowneth thee with lovingkindness and tender mercies;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Who satisfieth thy desire with good things, So that thy youth is renewed like the eagle.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Jehovah executeth righteous acts, And judgments for all that are oppressed.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He made known his ways unto Moses, His doings unto the children of Israel.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Jehovah is merciful and gracious, Slow to anger, and abundant in lovingkindness.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He will not always chide; Neither will he keephis angerfor ever.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He hath not dealt with us after our sins, Nor rewarded us after our iniquities.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For as the heavens are high above the earth, So great is his lovingkindness toward them that fear him.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** As far as the east is from the west, So far hath he removed our transgressions from us.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Like as a father pitieth his children, So Jehovah pitieth them that fear him.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For he knoweth our frame; He remembereth that we are dust.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** As for man, his days are as grass; As a flower of the field, so he flourisheth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For the wind passeth over it, and it is gone; And the place thereof shall know it no more.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** But the lovingkindness of Jehovah is from everlasting to everlasting upon them that fear him, And his righteousness unto children’s children;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** To such as keep his covenant, And to those that remember his precepts to do them.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Jehovah hath established his throne in the heavens; And his kingdom ruleth over all.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Bless Jehovah, ye his angels, That are mighty in strength, that fulfil his word, Hearkening unto the voice of his word.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Bless Jehovah, all ye his hosts, Ye ministers of his, that do his pleasure.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Bless Jehovah, all ye his works, In all places of his dominion: Bless Jehovah, O my soul.
