# Psalms 103 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise Yahweh, my soul! All that is within me, praise his holy name!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Praise Yahweh, my soul, and don’t forget all his benefits;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** who forgives all your sins; who heals all your diseases;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** who redeems your life from destruction; who crowns you with loving kindness and tender mercies;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** who satisfies your desire with good things, so that your youth is renewed like the eagle’s.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Yahweh executes righteous acts, and justice for all who are oppressed.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He made known his ways to Moses, his deeds to the children of Israel.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yahweh is merciful and gracious, slow to anger, and abundant in loving kindness.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He will not always accuse; neither will he stay angry forever.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He has not dealt with us according to our sins, nor repaid us for our iniquities.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For as the heavens are high above the earth, so great is his loving kindness toward those who fear him.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** As far as the east is from the west, so far has he removed our transgressions from us.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Like a father has compassion on his children, so Yahweh has compassion on those who fear him.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For he knows how we are made. He remembers that we are dust.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** As for man, his days are like grass. As a flower of the field, so he flourishes.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For the wind passes over it, and it is gone. Its place remembers it no more.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** But Yahweh’s loving kindness is from everlasting to everlasting with those who fear him, his righteousness to children’s children;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** to those who keep his covenant, to those who remember to obey his precepts.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Yahweh has established his throne in the heavens. His kingdom rules over all.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Praise Yahweh, you angels of his, who are mighty in strength, who fulfill his word, obeying the voice of his word.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Praise Yahweh, all you armies of his, you servants of his, who do his pleasure.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Praise Yahweh, all you works of his, in all places of his dominion. Praise Yahweh, my soul!
