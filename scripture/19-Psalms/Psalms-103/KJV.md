# Psalms 103 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Bless the Lord, O my soul: and all that is within me, bless his holy name.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Bless the Lord, O my soul, and forget not all his benefits:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Who forgiveth all thine iniquities; who healeth all thy diseases;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Who redeemeth thy life from destruction; who crowneth thee with lovingkindness and tender mercies;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Who satisfieth thy mouth with good things; so that thy youth is renewed like the eagle’s.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The Lord executeth righteousness and judgment for all that are oppressed.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He made known his ways unto Moses, his acts unto the children of Israel.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The Lord is merciful and gracious, slow to anger, and plenteous in mercy.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He will not always chide: neither will he keep his anger for ever.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He hath not dealt with us after our sins; nor rewarded us according to our iniquities.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For as the heaven is high above the earth, so great is his mercy toward them that fear him.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** As far as the east is from the west, so far hath he removed our transgressions from us.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Like as a father pitieth his children, so the Lord pitieth them that fear him.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For he knoweth our frame; he remembereth that we are dust.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** As for man, his days are as grass: as a flower of the field, so he flourisheth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For the wind passeth over it, and it is gone; and the place thereof shall know it no more.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** But the mercy of the Lord is from everlasting to everlasting upon them that fear him, and his righteousness unto children’s children;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** To such as keep his covenant, and to those that remember his commandments to do them.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The Lord hath prepared his throne in the heavens; and his kingdom ruleth over all.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Bless the Lord, ye his angels, that excel in strength, that do his commandments, hearkening unto the voice of his word.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Bless ye the Lord, all ye his hosts; ye ministers of his, that do his pleasure.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Bless the Lord, all his works in all places of his dominion: bless the Lord, O my soul.
