# Psalms 103 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give praise to the Lord, O my soul; let everything in me give praise to his holy name.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Give praise to the Lord, O my soul; let not all his blessings go from your memory.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He has forgiveness for all your sins; he takes away all your diseases;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He keeps back your life from destruction, crowning you with mercy and grace.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He makes your mouth full of good things, so that your strength is made new again like the eagle's.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The Lord gives decisions in righteousness for all who are in trouble.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He gave knowledge of his way to Moses, and made his acts clear to the children of Israel.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The Lord is kind and full of pity, not quickly made angry, but ever ready to have mercy.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** His feeling will no longer be bitter; he will not keep his wrath for ever.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He has not given us the punishment for our sins, or the reward of our wrongdoing.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For as the heaven is high over the earth, so great is his mercy to his worshippers.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** As far as the east is from the west, so far has he put our sins from us.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** As a father has pity on his children, so the Lord has pity on his worshippers.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For he has knowledge of our feeble frame; he sees that we are only dust.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** As for man, his days are as grass: his beautiful growth is like the flower of the field.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The wind goes over it and it is gone; and its place sees it no longer.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** But the mercy of the Lord is eternal for his worshippers, and their children's children will see his righteousness;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** If they keep his agreement, and have his laws in mind to do them.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The Lord has made ready his high seat in the heavens; his kingdom is ruling over all.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Give praise to the Lord, you his angels, who are great in strength, doing his orders, and waiting for his voice.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Give praise to the Lord, all you his armies; and you his servants who do his pleasure.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Give praise to the Lord, all his works, in all places under his rule: give praise to the Lord, O my soul.
