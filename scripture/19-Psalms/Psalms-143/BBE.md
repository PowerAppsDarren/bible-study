# Psalms 143 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Let my prayer come to you, O Lord; give ear to my requests for your grace; keep faith with me, and give me an answer in your righteousness;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let not your servant come before you to be judged; for no man living is upright in your eyes.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The evil man has gone after my soul; my life is crushed down to the earth: he has put me in the dark, like those who have long been dead.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Because of this my spirit is overcome; and my heart is full of fear.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I keep in mind the early days of the past, giving thought to all your acts, even to the work of your hands.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** My hands are stretched out to you: my soul is turned to you, like a land in need of water. (Selah.)

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Be quick in answering me, O Lord, for the strength of my spirit is gone: let me see your face, so that I may not be like those who go down into the underworld.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let the story of your mercy come to me in the morning, for my hope is in you: give me knowledge of the way in which I am to go; for my soul is lifted up to you.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** O Lord, take me out of the hands of my haters; my soul is waiting for you.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Give me teaching so that I may do your pleasure; for you are my God: let your good Spirit be my guide into the land of righteousness.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Give me life, O Lord, because of your name; in your righteousness take my soul out of trouble.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And in your mercy put an end to my haters, and send destruction on all those who are against my soul; for I am your servant.
