# Psalms 143 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear my prayer, Yahweh. Listen to my petitions. In your faithfulness and righteousness, relieve me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Don’t enter into judgment with your servant, for in your sight no man living is righteous.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For the enemy pursues my soul. He has struck my life down to the ground. He has made me live in dark places, as those who have been long dead.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Therefore my spirit is overwhelmed within me. My heart within me is desolate.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I remember the days of old. I meditate on all your doings. I contemplate the work of your hands.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I spread out my hands to you. My soul thirsts for you, like a parched land. Selah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Hurry to answer me, Yahweh. My spirit fails. Don’t hide your face from me, so that I don’t become like those who go down into the pit.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Cause me to hear your loving kindness in the morning, for I trust in you. Cause me to know the way in which I should walk, for I lift up my soul to you.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Deliver me, Yahweh, from my enemies. I flee to you to hide me.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Teach me to do your will, for you are my God. Your Spirit is good. Lead me in the land of uprightness.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Revive me, Yahweh, for your name’s sake. In your righteousness, bring my soul out of trouble.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** In your loving kindness, cut off my enemies, and destroy all those who afflict my soul, For I am your servant.
