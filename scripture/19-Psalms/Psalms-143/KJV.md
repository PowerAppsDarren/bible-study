# Psalms 143 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear my prayer, O Lord, give ear to my supplications: in thy faithfulness answer me, and in thy righteousness.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And enter not into judgment with thy servant: for in thy sight shall no man living be justified.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For the enemy hath persecuted my soul; he hath smitten my life down to the ground; he hath made me to dwell in darkness, as those that have been long dead.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Therefore is my spirit overwhelmed within me; my heart within me is desolate.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I remember the days of old; I meditate on all thy works; I muse on the work of thy hands.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I stretch forth my hands unto thee: my soul thirsteth after thee, as a thirsty land. Selah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Hear me speedily, O Lord: my spirit faileth: hide not thy face from me, lest I be like unto them that go down into the pit.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Cause me to hear thy lovingkindness in the morning; for in thee do I trust: cause me to know the way wherein I should walk; for I lift up my soul unto thee.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Deliver me, O Lord, from mine enemies: I flee unto thee to hide me.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Teach me to do thy will; for thou art my God: thy spirit is good; lead me into the land of uprightness.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Quicken me, O Lord, for thy name’s sake: for thy righteousness’ sake bring my soul out of trouble.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And of thy mercy cut off mine enemies, and destroy all them that afflict my soul: for I am thy servant.
