# Psalms 44 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** We have heard with our ears, God; our fathers have told us, what work you did in their days, in the days of old.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You drove out the nations with your hand, but you planted them. You afflicted the peoples, but you spread them abroad.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For they didn’t get the land in possession by their own sword, neither did their own arm save them; but your right hand, and your arm, and the light of your face, because you were favorable to them.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You are my King, God. Command victories for Jacob!

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Through you, will we push down our adversaries. Through your name, will we tread them under who rise up against us.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For I will not trust in my bow, neither shall my sword save me.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But you have saved us from our adversaries, and have shamed those who hate us.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** In God we have made our boast all day long, we will give thanks to your name forever. Selah.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But now you rejected us, and brought us to dishonor, and don’t go out with our armies.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** You make us turn back from the adversary. Those who hate us take plunder for themselves.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** You have made us like sheep for food, and have scattered us among the nations.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** You sell your people for nothing, and have gained nothing from their sale.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** You make us a reproach to our neighbors, a scoffing and a derision to those who are around us.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** You make us a byword among the nations, a shaking of the head among the peoples.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** All day long my dishonor is before me, and shame covers my face,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** At the taunt of one who reproaches and verbally abuses, because of the enemy and the avenger.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** All this has come on us, yet have we not forgotten you, Neither have we been false to your covenant.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Our heart has not turned back, neither have our steps strayed from your path,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Though you have crushed us in the haunt of jackals, and covered us with the shadow of death.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** If we have forgotten the name of our God, or spread out our hands to a strange god;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** won’t God search this out? For he knows the secrets of the heart.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Yes, for your sake we are killed all day long. We are regarded as sheep for the slaughter.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Wake up! Why do you sleep, Lord? Arise! Don’t reject us forever.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Why do you hide your face, and forget our affliction and our oppression?

<a id="v25"></a>**25** For our soul is bowed down to the dust. Our body clings to the earth.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Rise up to help us. Redeem us for your loving kindness’ sake.
