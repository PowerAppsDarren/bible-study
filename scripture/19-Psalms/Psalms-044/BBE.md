# Psalms 44 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** It has come to our ears, O God, our fathers have given us the story, of the works which you did in their days, in the old times,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Uprooting the nations with your hand, and planting our fathers in their place; cutting down the nations, but increasing the growth of your people.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For they did not make the land theirs by their swords, and it was not their arms which kept them safe; but your right hand, and your arm, and the light of your face, because you had pleasure in them.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You are my King and my God; ordering salvation for Jacob.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Through you will we overcome our haters; by your name will they be crushed under our feet who are violent against us.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I will not put faith in my bow, my sword will not be my salvation.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But it is you who have been our saviour from those who were against us, and have put to shame those who had hate for us.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Our pride is in God at all times, to his name we give praise for ever. (Selah.)

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But now you have sent us away from you, and put us to shame; you do not go out with our armies.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Because of this we are turned back by the attacker: those who have hate for us take our goods for themselves.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** You have made us like sheep which are taken for meat; we are put to flight among the nations.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** You let your people go for nothing; your wealth is not increased by their price.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** You have made us to be looked down on by our neighbours, we are laughed at and shamed by those who are round about us.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Our name is a word of shame among the nations, a sign for the shaking of heads among the peoples.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** My downfall is ever before me, and I am covered with the shame of my face;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Because of the voice of him who says sharp and bitter words; because of the hater and him who is the instrument of punishment.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** All this has come on us, but still we have kept you in our memory; and we have not been false to your word.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Our hearts have not gone back, and our steps have not been turned out of your way;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Though you have let us be crushed in the place of jackals, though we are covered with darkest shade.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** If the name of our God has gone out of our minds, or if our hands have been stretched out to a strange god,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Will not God make search for it? for he sees the secrets of the heart.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Truly, because of you we are put to death every day; we are numbered like sheep for destruction.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Why are you sleeping, O Lord? awake! and come to our help, do not give us up for ever.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Why is your face covered, and why do you give no thought to our trouble and our cruel fate?

<a id="v25"></a>**25** For our souls are crushed down to the dust: our bodies are stretched out on the earth.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Up! and come to our help, and give us salvation because of your mercy.
