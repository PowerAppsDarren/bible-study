# Psalms 44 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** We have heard with our ears, O God, Our fathers have told us, What work thou didst in their days, In the days of old.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Thou didst drive out the nations with thy hand; But them thou didst plant: Thou didst afflict the peoples; But them thou didst spread abroad.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For they gat not the land in possession by their own sword, Neither did their own arm save them; But thy right hand, and thine arm, and the light of thy countenance, Because thou wast favorable unto them.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thou art my King, O God: Command deliverance for Jacob.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Through thee will we push down our adversaries: Through thy name will we tread them under that rise up against us.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For I will not trust in my bow, Neither shall my sword save me.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But thou hast saved us from our adversaries, And hast put them to shame that hate us.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** In God have we made our boast all the day long, And we will give thanks unto thy name for ever. [Selah

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But now thou hast cast us off, and brought us to dishonor, And goest not forth with our hosts.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Thou makest us to turn back from the adversary; And they that hate us take spoil for themselves.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Thou hast made us like sheep appointed for food, And hast scattered us among the nations.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Thou sellest thy people for nought, And hast not increased thy wealth by their price.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thou makest us a reproach to our neighbors, A scoffing and a derision to them that are round about us.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Thou makest us a byword among the nations, A shaking of the head among the peoples.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** All the day long is my dishonor before me, And the shame of my face hath covered me,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For the voice of him that reproacheth and blasphemeth, By reason of the enemy and the avenger.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** All this is come upon us; yet have we not forgotten thee, Neither have we dealt falsely in thy covenant.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Our heart is not turned back, Neither have our steps declined from thy way,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** That thou hast sore broken us in the place of jackals, And covered us with the shadow of death.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** If we have forgotten the name of our God, Or spread forth our hands to a strange god;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Will not God search this out? For he knoweth the secrets of the heart.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Yea, for thy sake are we killed all the day long; We are accounted as sheep for the slaughter.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Awake, why sleepest thou, O Lord? Arise, cast us not off for ever.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Wherefore hidest thou thy face, And forgettest our affliction and our oppression?

<a id="v25"></a>**25** For our soul is bowed down to the dust: Our body cleaveth unto the earth.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Rise up for our help, And redeem us for thy lovingkindness’ sake.
