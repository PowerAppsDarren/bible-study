# Psalms 98 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Sing to Yahweh a new song, for he has done marvelous things! His right hand, and his holy arm, have worked salvation for him.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Yahweh has made known his salvation. He has openly shown his righteousness in the sight of the nations.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He has remembered his loving kindness and his faithfulness toward the house of Israel. All the ends of the earth have seen the salvation of our God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Make a joyful noise to Yahweh, all the earth! Burst out and sing for joy, yes, sing praises!

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Sing praises to Yahweh with the harp, with the harp and the voice of melody.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** With trumpets and sound of the ram’s horn, make a joyful noise before the King, Yahweh.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Let the sea roar with its fullness; the world, and those who dwell therein.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let the rivers clap their hands. Let the mountains sing for joy together.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let them sing before Yahweh, for he comes to judge the earth. He will judge the world with righteousness, and the peoples with equity.
