# Psalms 98 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O make a new song to the Lord, because he has done works of wonder; with his right hand, and with his holy arm, he has overcome.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The Lord has given to all the knowledge of his salvation; he has made clear his righteousness in the eyes of the nations.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He has kept in mind his mercy and his unchanging faith to the house of Israel; all the ends of the earth have seen the salvation of our God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Let all the earth send out a glad cry to the Lord; sounding with a loud voice, and praising him with songs of joy.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Make melody to the Lord with instruments of music; with a corded instrument and the voice of song.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** With wind instruments and the sound of the horn, make a glad cry before the Lord, the King.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Let the sea be thundering, with all its waters; the world, and all who are living in it;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let the streams make sounds of joy with their hands; let the mountains be glad together,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Before the Lord, for he has come as judge of the earth; judging the world in righteousness, and giving true decisions for the peoples.
