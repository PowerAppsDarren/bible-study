# Psalms 98 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Oh sing unto Jehovah a new song; For he hath done marvellous things: His right hand, and his holy arm, hath wrought salvation for him.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Jehovah hath made known his salvation: His righteousness hath he openly showed in the sight of the nations.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He hath remembered his lovingkindness and his faithfulness toward the house of Israel: All the ends of the earth have seen the salvation of our God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Make a joyful noise unto Jehovah, all the earth: Break forth and sing for joy, yea, sing praises.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Sing praises unto Jehovah with the harp; With the harp and the voice of melody.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** With trumpets and sound of cornet Make a joyful noise before the King, Jehovah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof; The world, and they that dwell therein;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let the floods clap their hands; Let the hills sing for joy together

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Before Jehovah; for he cometh to judge the earth: He will judge the world with righteousness, And the peoples with equity.
