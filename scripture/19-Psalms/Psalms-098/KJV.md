# Psalms 98 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O sing unto the Lord a new song; for he hath done marvellous things: his right hand, and his holy arm, hath gotten him the victory.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The Lord hath made known his salvation: his righteousness hath he openly shewed in the sight of the heathen.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He hath remembered his mercy and his truth toward the house of Israel: all the ends of the earth have seen the salvation of our God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Make a joyful noise unto the Lord, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Sing unto the Lord with the harp; with the harp, and the voice of a psalm.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** With trumpets and sound of cornet make a joyful noise before the Lord, the King.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof; the world, and they that dwell therein.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let the floods clap their hands: let the hills be joyful together

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Before the Lord; for he cometh to judge the earth: with righteousness shall he judge the world, and the people with equity.
