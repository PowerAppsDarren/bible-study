# Psalms 91 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** He that dwelleth in the secret place of the Most High Shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I will say of Jehovah, He is my refuge and my fortress; My God, in whom I trust.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For he will deliver thee from the snare of the fowler, And from the deadly pestilence.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He will cover thee with his pinions, And under his wings shalt thou take refuge: His truth is a shield and a buckler.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thou shalt not be afraid for the terror by night, Nor for the arrow that flieth by day;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For the pestilence that walketh in darkness, Nor for the destruction that wasteth at noonday.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** A thousand shall fall at thy side, And ten thousand at thy right hand; Butit shall not come nigh thee.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Only with thine eyes shalt thou behold, And see the reward of the wicked.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For thou, O Jehovah, art my refuge! Thou hast made the Most High thy habitation;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** There shall no evil befall thee, Neither shall any plague come nigh thy tent.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For he will give his angels charge over thee, To keep thee in all thy ways.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They shall bear thee up in their hands, Lest thou dash thy foot against a stone.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thou shalt tread upon the lion and adder: The young lion and the serpent shalt thou trample under foot.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Because he hath set his love upon me, therefore will I deliver him: I will set him on high, because he hath known my name.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He shall call upon me, and I will answer him; I will be with him in trouble: I will deliver him, and honor him.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** With long life will I satisfy him, And show him my salvation.
