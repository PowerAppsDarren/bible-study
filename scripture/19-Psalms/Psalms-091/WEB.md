# Psalms 91 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** He who dwells in the secret place of the Most High will rest in the shadow of the Almighty.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I will say of Yahweh, “He is my refuge and my fortress; my God, in whom I trust.”

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For he will deliver you from the snare of the fowler, and from the deadly pestilence.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He will cover you with his feathers. Under his wings you will take refuge. His faithfulness is your shield and rampart.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You shall not be afraid of the terror by night, nor of the arrow that flies by day;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** nor of the pestilence that walks in darkness, nor of the destruction that wastes at noonday.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** A thousand may fall at your side, and ten thousand at your right hand; but it will not come near you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** You will only look with your eyes, and see the recompense of the wicked.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Because you have made Yahweh your refuge, and the Most High your dwelling place,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** no evil shall happen to you, neither shall any plague come near your dwelling.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For he will put his angels in charge of you, to guard you in all your ways.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They will bear you up in their hands, so that you won’t dash your foot against a stone.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** You will tread on the lion and cobra. You will trample the young lion and the serpent underfoot.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** “Because he has set his love on me, therefore I will deliver him. I will set him on high, because he has known my name.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He will call on me, and I will answer him. I will be with him in trouble. I will deliver him, and honor him.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** I will satisfy him with long life, and show him my salvation.”
