# Psalms 91 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Happy is he whose resting-place is in the secret of the Lord, and under the shade of the wings of the Most High;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Who says of the Lord, He is my safe place and my tower of strength: he is my God, in whom is my hope.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He will take you out of the bird-net, and keep you safe from wasting disease.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You will be covered by his feathers; under his wings you will be safe: his good faith will be your salvation.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You will have no fear of the evil things of the night, or of the arrow in flight by day,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Or of the disease which takes men in the dark, or of the destruction which makes waste when the sun is high.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You will see a thousand falling by your side, and ten thousand at your right hand; but it will not come near you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Only with your eyes will you see the reward of the evil-doers.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Because you have said, I am in the hands of the Lord, the Most High is my safe resting-place;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** No evil will come on you, and no disease will come near your tent.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For he will give you into the care of his angels to keep you wherever you go.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** In their hands they will keep you up, so that your foot may not be crushed against a stone.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** You will put your foot on the lion and the snake; the young lion and the great snake will be crushed under your feet.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Because he has given me his love, I will take him out of danger: I will put him in a place of honour, because he has kept my name in his heart.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** When his cry comes up to me, I will give him an answer: I will be with him in trouble; I will make him free from danger and give him honour.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** With long life will he be rewarded; and I will let him see my salvation.
