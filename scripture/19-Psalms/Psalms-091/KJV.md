# Psalms 91 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** He that dwelleth in the secret place of the most High shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I will say of the Lord, He is my refuge and my fortress: my God; in him will I trust.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Surely he shall deliver thee from the snare of the fowler, and from the noisome pestilence.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He shall cover thee with his feathers, and under his wings shalt thou trust: his truth shall be thy shield and buckler.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thou shalt not be afraid for the terror by night; nor for the arrow that flieth by day;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Nor for the pestilence that walketh in darkness; nor for the destruction that wasteth at noonday.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** A thousand shall fall at thy side, and ten thousand at thy right hand; but it shall not come nigh thee.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Only with thine eyes shalt thou behold and see the reward of the wicked.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Because thou hast made the Lord, which is my refuge, even the most High, thy habitation;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** There shall no evil befall thee, neither shall any plague come nigh thy dwelling.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For he shall give his angels charge over thee, to keep thee in all thy ways.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They shall bear thee up in their hands, lest thou dash thy foot against a stone.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thou shalt tread upon the lion and adder: the young lion and the dragon shalt thou trample under feet.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Because he hath set his love upon me, therefore will I deliver him: I will set him on high, because he hath known my name.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He shall call upon me, and I will answer him: I will be with him in trouble; I will deliver him, and honour him.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** With long life will I satisfy him, and shew him my salvation.
