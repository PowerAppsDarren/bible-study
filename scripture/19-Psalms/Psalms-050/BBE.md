# Psalms 50 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The God of gods, even the Lord, has sent out his voice, and the earth is full of fear; from the coming up of the sun to its going down.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** From Zion, most beautiful of places, God has sent out his light.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Our God will come, and will not keep quiet; with fire burning before him, and storm-winds round him.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** His voice will go out to the heavens and to the earth, for the judging of his people:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let my saints come together to me; those who have made an agreement with me by offerings.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And let the heavens make clear his righteousness; for God himself is the judge. (Selah.)

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Give ear, O my people, to my words; O Israel, I will be a witness against you; I am God, even your God.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will not take up a cause against you because of your offerings, or because of your burned offerings, which are ever before me.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will take no ox out of your house, or he-goats from your flocks;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For every beast of the woodland is mine, and the cattle on a thousand hills.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I see all the birds of the mountains, and the beasts of the field are mine.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** If I had need of food, I would not give you word of it; for the earth is mine and all its wealth.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Am I to take the flesh of the ox for my food, or the blood of goats for my drink?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Make an offering of praise to God; keep the agreements which you have made with the Most High;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let your voice come up to me in the day of trouble; I will be your saviour, so that you may give glory to me.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** But to the sinner, God says, What are you doing, talking of my laws, or taking the words of my agreement in your mouth?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Seeing that you have no desire for my teaching, turning your back on my words.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** When you saw a thief, you were in agreement with him, and you were joined with those who took other men's wives.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** You have given your mouth to evil, your tongue to words of deceit.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** You say evil of your brother; you make false statements against your mother's son.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** These things have you done, and I said nothing; it seemed to you that I was such a one as yourself; but I will make a protest against you, and put them in order before your eyes.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Now keep this in mind, you who have no memory of God, for fear that you may be crushed under my hand, with no one to give you help:

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Whoever makes an offering of praise gives glory to me; and to him who is upright in his ways I will make clear the salvation of God.
