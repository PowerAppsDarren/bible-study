# Psalms 50 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The Mighty One, God, Yahweh, speaks, and calls the earth from sunrise to sunset.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Out of Zion, the perfection of beauty, God shines out.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Our God comes, and does not keep silent. A fire devours before him. It is very stormy around him.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He calls to the heavens above, to the earth, that he may judge his people:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** “Gather my saints together to me, those who have made a covenant with me by sacrifice.”

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The heavens shall declare his righteousness, for God himself is judge. Selah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** “Hear, my people, and I will speak; Israel, and I will testify against you. I am God, your God.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I don’t rebuke you for your sacrifices. Your burnt offerings are continually before me.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I have no need for a bull from your stall, nor male goats from your pens.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For every animal of the forest is mine, and the livestock on a thousand hills.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I know all the birds of the mountains. The wild animals of the field are mine.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** If I were hungry, I would not tell you, for the world is mine, and all that is in it.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Will I eat the flesh of bulls, or drink the blood of goats?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Offer to God the sacrifice of thanksgiving. Pay your vows to the Most High.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Call on me in the day of trouble. I will deliver you, and you will honor me.”

<a id="v16"></a>**16** But to the wicked God says, “What right do you have to declare my statutes, that you have taken my covenant on your lips,

<a id="v17"></a>**17** since you hate instruction, and throw my words behind you?

<a id="v18"></a>**18** When you saw a thief, you consented with him, and have participated with adulterers.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** “You give your mouth to evil. Your tongue frames deceit.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** You sit and speak against your brother. You slander your own mother’s son.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** You have done these things, and I kept silent. You thought that I was just like you. I will rebuke you, and accuse you in front of your eyes.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** “Now consider this, you who forget God, lest I tear you into pieces, and there be no one to deliver.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Whoever offers the sacrifice of thanksgiving glorifies me, and prepares his way so that I will show God’s salvation to him.”
