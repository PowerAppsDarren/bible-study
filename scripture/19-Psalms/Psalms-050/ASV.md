# Psalms 50 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The Mighty One, God, Jehovah, hath spoken, And called the earth from the rising of the sun unto the going down thereof.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Out of Zion, the perfection of beauty, God hath shined forth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Our God cometh, and doth not keep silence: A fire devoureth before him, And it is very tempestuous round about him.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He calleth to the heavens above, And to the earth, that he may judge his people:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Gather my saints together unto me, Those that have made a covenant with me by sacrifice.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And the heavens shall declare his righteousness; For God is judge himself. [Selah

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Hear, O my people, and I will speak; O Israel, and I will testify unto thee: I am God, even thy God.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will not reprove thee for thy sacrifices; And thy burnt-offerings are continually before me.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will take no bullock out of thy house, Nor he-goats out of thy folds.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For every beast of the forest is mine, And the cattle upon a thousand hills.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I know all the birds of the mountains; And the wild beasts of the field are mine.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** If I were hungry, I would not tell thee; For the world is mine, and the fulness thereof.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Will I eat the flesh of bulls, Or drink the blood of goats?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Offer unto God the sacrifice of thanksgiving; And pay thy vows unto the Most High;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And call upon me in the day of trouble: I will deliver thee, and thou shalt glorify me.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** But unto the wicked God saith, What hast thou to do to declare my statutes, And that thou hast taken my covenant in thy mouth,

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Seeing that thou hatest instruction, And castest my words behind thee?

<a id="v18"></a>**18** When thou sawest a thief, thou consentedst with him, And hast been partaker with adulterers.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Thou givest thy mouth to evil, And thy tongue frameth deceit.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Thou sittest and speakest against thy brother; Thou slanderest thine own mother’s son.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** These things hast thou done, and I kept silence; Thou thoughtest that I was altogether such a one as thyself: ButI will reprove thee, and set them in order before thine eyes.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Now consider this, ye that forget God, Lest I tear you in pieces, and there be none to deliver:

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Whoso offereth the sacrifice of thanksgiving glorifieth me; And to him that ordereth his way aright Will I show the salvation of God.
