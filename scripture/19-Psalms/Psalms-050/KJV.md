# Psalms 50 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The mighty God, even the Lord, hath spoken, and called the earth from the rising of the sun unto the going down thereof.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Out of Zion, the perfection of beauty, God hath shined.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Our God shall come, and shall not keep silence: a fire shall devour before him, and it shall be very tempestuous round about him.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He shall call to the heavens from above, and to the earth, that he may judge his people.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Gather my saints together unto me; those that have made a covenant with me by sacrifice.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And the heavens shall declare his righteousness: for God is judge himself. Selah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Hear, O my people, and I will speak; O Israel, and I will testify against thee: I am God, even thy God.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will not reprove thee for thy sacrifices or thy burnt offerings, to have been continually before me.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will take no bullock out of thy house, nor he goats out of thy folds.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For every beast of the forest is mine, and the cattle upon a thousand hills.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I know all the fowls of the mountains: and the wild beasts of the field are mine.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** If I were hungry, I would not tell thee: for the world is mine, and the fulness thereof.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Will I eat the flesh of bulls, or drink the blood of goats?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Offer unto God thanksgiving; and pay thy vows unto the most High:

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And call upon me in the day of trouble: I will deliver thee, and thou shalt glorify me.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** But unto the wicked God saith, What hast thou to do to declare my statutes, or that thou shouldest take my covenant in thy mouth?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Seeing thou hatest instruction, and castest my words behind thee.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** When thou sawest a thief, then thou consentedst with him, and hast been partaker with adulterers.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Thou givest thy mouth to evil, and thy tongue frameth deceit.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Thou sittest and speakest against thy brother; thou slanderest thine own mother’s son.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** These things hast thou done, and I kept silence; thou thoughtest that I was altogether such an one as thyself: but I will reprove thee, and set them in order before thine eyes.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Now consider this, ye that forget God, lest I tear you in pieces, and there be none to deliver.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Whoso offereth praise glorifieth me: and to him that ordereth his conversation aright will I shew the salvation of God.
