# Psalms 122 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I was glad because they said to me, We will go into the house of the Lord.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** At last our feet were inside your doors, O Jerusalem.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** O Jerusalem, you are like a town which is well joined together;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** To which the tribes went up, even the tribes of the Lord, for a witness to Israel, to give praise to the name of the Lord.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For there seats for the judges were placed, even the rulers' seats of the line of David.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** O make prayers for the peace of Jerusalem; may they whose love is given to you do well.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** May peace be inside your walls, and wealth in your noble houses.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Because of my brothers and friends, I will now say, Let peace be with you.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Because of the house of the Lord our God, I will be working for your good.
