# Psalms 122 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I was glad when they said unto me, Let us go unto the house of Jehovah.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Our feet are standing  Within thy gates, O Jerusalem,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Jerusalem, that art builded As a city that is compact together;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Whither the tribes go up, even the tribes of Jehovah, Foran ordinance for Israel, To give thanks unto the name of Jehovah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For there are set thrones for judgment, The thrones of the house of David.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Pray for the peace of Jerusalem: They shall prosper that love thee.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Peace be within thy walls, And prosperity within thy palaces.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For my brethren and companions’ sakes, I will now say, Peace be within thee.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For the sake of the house of Jehovah our God  I will seek thy good.
