# Psalms 122 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I was glad when they said to me, “Let’s go to Yahweh’s house!”

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Our feet are standing within your gates, Jerusalem;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Jerusalem, that is built as a city that is compact together;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** where the tribes go up, even Yah’s tribes, according to an ordinance for Israel, to give thanks to Yahweh’s name.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For there are set thrones for judgment, the thrones of David’s house.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Pray for the peace of Jerusalem. Those who love you will prosper.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Peace be within your walls, and prosperity within your palaces.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For my brothers’ and companions’ sakes, I will now say, “Peace be within you.”

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For the sake of the house of Yahweh our God, I will seek your good.
