# Psalms 122 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I was glad when they said unto me, Let us go into the house of the Lord.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Our feet shall stand within thy gates, O Jerusalem.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Jerusalem is builded as a city that is compact together:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Whither the tribes go up, the tribes of the Lord, unto the testimony of Israel, to give thanks unto the name of the Lord.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For there are set thrones of judgment, the thrones of the house of David.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Pray for the peace of Jerusalem: they shall prosper that love thee.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Peace be within thy walls, and prosperity within thy palaces.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For my brethren and companions’ sakes, I will now say, Peace be within thee.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Because of the house of the Lord our God I will seek thy good.
