# Psalms 46 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God is our refuge and strength, A very present help in trouble.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Therefore will we not fear, though the earth do change, And though the mountains be shaken into the heart of the seas;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Though the waters thereof roar and be troubled, Though the mountains tremble with the swelling thereof. [Selah

<a id="v4"></a>**4** There is a river, the streams whereof make glad the city of God, The holy place of the tabernacles of the Most High.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** God is in the midst of her; she shall not be moved: God will help her, and that right early.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The nations raged, the kingdoms were moved: He uttered his voice, the earth melted.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Jehovah of hosts is with us; The God of Jacob is our refuge. [Selah

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Come, behold the works of Jehovah, What desolations he hath made in the earth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He maketh wars to cease unto the end of the earth; He breaketh the bow, and cutteth the spear in sunder; He burneth the chariots in the fire.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Be still, and know that I am God: I will be exalted among the nations, I will be exalted in the earth.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Jehovah of hosts is with us; The God of Jacob is our refuge. [Selah
