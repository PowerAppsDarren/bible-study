# Psalms 46 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Therefore we won’t be afraid, though the earth changes, though the mountains are shaken into the heart of the seas;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** though its waters roar and are troubled, though the mountains tremble with their swelling. Selah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** There is a river, the streams of which make the city of God glad, the holy place of the tents of the Most High.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** God is in her midst. She shall not be moved. God will help her at dawn.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The nations raged. The kingdoms were moved. He lifted his voice, and the earth melted.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Yahweh of Armies is with us. The God of Jacob is our refuge. Selah.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Come, see Yahweh’s works, what desolations he has made in the earth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He makes wars cease to the end of the earth. He breaks the bow, and shatters the spear. He burns the chariots in the fire.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** “Be still, and know that I am God. I will be exalted among the nations. I will be exalted in the earth.”

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Yahweh of Armies is with us. The God of Jacob is our refuge. Selah.
