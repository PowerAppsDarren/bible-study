# Psalms 46 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Therefore will not we fear, though the earth be removed, and though the mountains be carried into the midst of the sea;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Though the waters thereof roar and be troubled, though the mountains shake with the swelling thereof. Selah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** There is a river, the streams whereof shall make glad the city of God, the holy place of the tabernacles of the most High.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** God is in the midst of her; she shall not be moved: God shall help her, and that right early.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The heathen raged, the kingdoms were moved: he uttered his voice, the earth melted.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The Lord of hosts is with us; the God of Jacob is our refuge. Selah.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Come, behold the works of the Lord, what desolations he hath made in the earth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He maketh wars to cease unto the end of the earth; he breaketh the bow, and cutteth the spear in sunder; he burneth the chariot in the fire.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Be still, and know that I am God: I will be exalted among the heathen, I will be exalted in the earth.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The Lord of hosts is with us; the God of Jacob is our refuge. Selah.
