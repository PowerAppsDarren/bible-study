# Psalms 46 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God is our harbour and our strength, a very present help in trouble.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For this cause we will have no fear, even though the earth is changed, and though the mountains are moved in the heart of the sea;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Though its waters are sounding and troubled, and though the mountains are shaking with their violent motion. (Selah.)

<a id="v4"></a>**4** There is a river whose streams make glad the resting-place of God, the holy place of the tents of the Most High.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** God has taken his place in her; she will not be moved: he will come to her help at the dawn of morning.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The nations were angry, the kingdoms were moved; at the sound of his voice the earth became like wax.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The Lord of armies is with us; the God of Jacob is our high tower. (Selah.)

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Come, see the works of the Lord, the destruction which he has made in the earth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He puts an end to wars over all the earth; by him the bow is broken, and the spear cut in two, and the carriage burned in the fire.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Be at peace in the knowledge that I am God: I will be lifted up among the nations, I will be honoured through all the earth.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The Lord of armies is with us; the God of Jacob is our high tower. (Selah.)
