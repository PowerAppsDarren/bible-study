# Psalms 113 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye the Lord. Praise, O ye servants of the Lord, praise the name of the Lord.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Blessed be the name of the Lord from this time forth and for evermore.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** From the rising of the sun unto the going down of the same the Lord’s name is to be praised.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The Lord is high above all nations, and his glory above the heavens.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Who is like unto the Lord our God, who dwelleth on high,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Who humbleth himself to behold the things that are in heaven, and in the earth!

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He raiseth up the poor out of the dust, and lifteth the needy out of the dunghill;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** That he may set him with princes, even with the princes of his people.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He maketh the barren woman to keep house, and to be a joyful mother of children. Praise ye the Lord.
