# Psalms 113 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Let the Lord be praised. O you servants of the Lord, give praise to the name of the Lord.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let blessing be on the name of the Lord, from this time and for ever.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** From the coming up of the sun to its going down, the Lord's name is to be praised.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The Lord is high over all nations, and his glory is higher than the heavens.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Who is like the Lord our God, who is seated on high,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Looking down on the heavens, and on the earth?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He takes the poor man out of the dust, lifting him up from his low position;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** To give him a place among the rulers, even with the rulers of his people.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He gives the unfertile woman a family, making her a happy mother of children. Give praise to the Lord.
