# Psalms 113 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye Jehovah. Praise, O ye servants of Jehovah, Praise the name of Jehovah.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Blessed be the name of Jehovah  From this time forth and for evermore.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** From the rising of the sun unto the going down of the same Jehovah’s name is to be praised.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Jehovah is high above all nations, And his glory above the heavens.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Who is like unto Jehovah our God, That hath his seat on high,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** That humbleth himself to behold The things that arein heaven and in the earth?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He raiseth up the poor out of the dust, And lifteth up the needy from the dunghill;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** That he may set him with princes, Even with the princes of his people.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He maketh the barren woman to keep house, And to bea joyful mother of children. Praise ye Jehovah.
