# Psalms 27 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The Lord is my light and my salvation; whom shall I fear? the Lord is the strength of my life; of whom shall I be afraid?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** When the wicked, even mine enemies and my foes, came upon me to eat up my flesh, they stumbled and fell.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Though an host should encamp against me, my heart shall not fear: though war should rise against me, in this will I be confident.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** One thing have I desired of the Lord, that will I seek after; that I may dwell in the house of the Lord all the days of my life, to behold the beauty of the Lord, and to enquire in his temple.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For in the time of trouble he shall hide me in his pavilion: in the secret of his tabernacle shall he hide me; he shall set me up upon a rock.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And now shall mine head be lifted up above mine enemies round about me: therefore will I offer in his tabernacle sacrifices of joy; I will sing, yea, I will sing praises unto the Lord.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Hear, O Lord, when I cry with my voice: have mercy also upon me, and answer me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** When thou saidst, Seek ye my face; my heart said unto thee, Thy face, Lord, will I seek.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Hide not thy face far from me; put not thy servant away in anger: thou hast been my help; leave me not, neither forsake me, O God of my salvation.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** When my father and my mother forsake me, then the Lord will take me up.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Teach me thy way, O Lord, and lead me in a plain path, because of mine enemies.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Deliver me not over unto the will of mine enemies: for false witnesses are risen up against me, and such as breathe out cruelty.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I had fainted, unless I had believed to see the goodness of the Lord in the land of the living.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Wait on the Lord: be of good courage, and he shall strengthen thine heart: wait, I say, on the Lord.
