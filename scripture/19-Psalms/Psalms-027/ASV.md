# Psalms 27 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Jehovah is my light and my salvation; Whom shall I fear? Jehovah is the strength of my life; Of whom shall I be afraid?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** When evil-doers came upon me to eat up my flesh, Even mine adversaries and my foes, they stumbled and fell.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Though a host should encamp against me, My heart shall not fear: Though war should rise against me, Even then will I be confident.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** One thing have I asked of Jehovah, that will I seek after: That I may dwell in the house of Jehovah all the days of my life, To behold the beauty of Jehovah, And to inquire in his temple.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For in the day of trouble he will keep me secretly in his pavilion: In the covert of his tabernacle will he hide me; He will lift me up upon a rock.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And now shall my head be lifted up above mine enemies round about me; And I will offer in his tabernacle sacrifices of joy; I will sing, yea, I will sing praises unto Jehovah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Hear, O Jehovah, when I cry with my voice: Have mercy also upon me, and answer me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** When thou saidst, Seek ye my face; my heart said unto thee, Thy face, Jehovah, will I seek.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Hide not thy face from me; Put not thy servant away in anger: Thou hast been my help; Cast me not off, neither forsake me, O God of my salvation.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** When my father and my mother forsake me, Then Jehovah will take me up.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Teach me thy way, O Jehovah; And lead me in a plain path, Because of mine enemies.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Deliver me not over unto the will of mine adversaries: For false witnesses are risen up against me, And such as breathe out cruelty.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I had fainted, unless I had believed to see the goodness of Jehovah In the land of the living.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Wait for Jehovah: Be strong, and let thy heart take courage; Yea, wait thou for Jehovah.
