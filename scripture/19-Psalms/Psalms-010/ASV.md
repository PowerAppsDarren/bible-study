# Psalms 10 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Why standest thou afar off, O Jehovah? Why hidest thou thyself in times of trouble?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** In the pride of the wicked the poor is hotly pursued; Let them be taken in the devices that they have conceived.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For the wicked boasteth of his heart’s desire, And the covetous renounceth, yea, contemneth Jehovah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The wicked, in the pride of his countenance, saith, He will not requireit. All his thoughts are, There is no God.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** His ways are firm at all times; Thy judgments are far above out of his sight: As for all his adversaries, he puffeth at them.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He saith in his heart, I shall not be moved; To all generations I shall not be in adversity.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** His mouth is full of cursing and deceit and oppression: Under his tongue is mischief and iniquity.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He sitteth in the lurking-places of the villages; In the secret places doth he murder the innocent; His eyes are privily set against the helpless.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He lurketh in secret as a lion in his covert; He lieth in wait to catch the poor: He doth catch the poor, when he draweth him in his net.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He croucheth, he boweth down, And the helpless fall by his strong ones.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He saith in his heart: God hath forgotten; He hideth his face, he will never see it.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Arise, O Jehovah; O God, lift up thy hand: Forget not the poor.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Wherefore doth the wicked contemn God, And say in his heart, Thou wilt not requireit?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Thou hast seenit; for thou beholdest mischief and spite, to requite it with thy hand: The helpless committeth himself unto thee; Thou hast been the helper of the fatherless.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Break thou the arm of the wicked; And as for the evil man, seek out his wickedness till thou find none.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Jehovah is King for ever and ever: The nations are perished out of his land.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Jehovah, thou hast heard the desire of the meek: Thou wilt prepare their heart, thou wilt cause thine ear to hear;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** To judge the fatherless and the oppressed, That man who is of the earth may be terrible no more.
