# Psalms 32 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Happy is he who has forgiveness for his wrongdoing, and whose sin is covered.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Happy is the man in whom the Lord sees no evil, and in whose spirit there is no deceit.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When I kept my mouth shut, my bones were wasted, because of my crying all through the day.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For the weight of your hand was on me day and night; my body became dry like the earth in summer. (Selah.)

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I made my wrongdoing clear to you, and did not keep back my sin. I said, I will put it all before the Lord; and you took away my wrongdoing and my sin. (Selah.)

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For this cause let every saint make his prayer to you at a time when you are near: then the overflowing of the great waters will not overtake him.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You are my safe and secret place; you will keep me from trouble; you will put songs of salvation on the lips of those who are round me. (Selah.)

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will give you knowledge, teaching you the way to go; my eye will be your guide.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Do not be like the horse or the ass, without sense; ...

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The sinner will be full of trouble; but mercy will be round the man who has faith in the Lord.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Be glad in the Lord with joy, you upright men; give cries of joy, all you whose hearts are true.
