# Psalms 32 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Blessed is he whose disobedience is forgiven, whose sin is covered.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Blessed is the man to whom Yahweh doesn’t impute iniquity, in whose spirit there is no deceit.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When I kept silence, my bones wasted away through my groaning all day long.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For day and night your hand was heavy on me. My strength was sapped in the heat of summer. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I acknowledged my sin to you. I didn’t hide my iniquity. I said, I will confess my transgressions to Yahweh, and you forgave the iniquity of my sin. Selah.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For this, let everyone who is godly pray to you in a time when you may be found. Surely when the great waters overflow, they shall not reach to him.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You are my hiding place. You will preserve me from trouble. You will surround me with songs of deliverance. Selah.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will instruct you and teach you in the way which you shall go. I will counsel you with my eye on you.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Don’t be like the horse, or like the mule, which have no understanding, who are controlled by bit and bridle, or else they will not come near to you.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Many sorrows come to the wicked, but loving kindness shall surround him who trusts in Yahweh.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Be glad in Yahweh, and rejoice, you righteous! Shout for joy, all you who are upright in heart!
