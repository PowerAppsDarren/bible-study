# Psalms 30 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will extol thee, O Jehovah; for thou hast raised me up, And hast not made my foes to rejoice over me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** O Jehovah my God, I cried unto thee, and thou hast healed me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** O Jehovah, thou hast brought up my soul from Sheol; Thou hast kept me alive, that I should not go down to the pit.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Sing praise unto Jehovah, O ye saints of his, And give thanks to his holy memorial name.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For his anger is but for a moment; His favor is for a life-time: Weeping may tarry for the night, But joy cometh in the morning.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** As for me, I said in my prosperity, I shall never be moved.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Thou, Jehovah, of thy favor hadst made my mountain to stand strong: Thou didst hide thy face; I was troubled.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I cried to thee, O Jehovah; And unto Jehovah I made supplication:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** What profit is there in my blood, when I go down to the pit? Shall the dust praise thee? shall it declare thy truth?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Hear, O Jehovah, and have mercy upon me: Jehovah, be thou my helper.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Thou hast turned for me my mourning into dancing; Thou hast loosed my sackcloth, and girded me with gladness;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** To the end that my glory may sing praise to thee, and not be silent. O Jehovah my God, I will give thanks unto thee for ever.
