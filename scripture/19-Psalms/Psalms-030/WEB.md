# Psalms 30 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will extol you, Yahweh, for you have raised me up, and have not made my foes to rejoice over me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Yahweh my God, I cried to you, and you have healed me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yahweh, you have brought up my soul from Sheol. You have kept me alive, that I should not go down to the pit.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Sing praise to Yahweh, you saints of his. Give thanks to his holy name.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For his anger is but for a moment. His favor is for a lifetime. Weeping may stay for the night, but joy comes in the morning.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** As for me, I said in my prosperity, “I shall never be moved.”

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You, Yahweh, when you favored me, made my mountain stand strong; but when you hid your face, I was troubled.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I cried to you, Yahweh. To Yahweh I made supplication:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** “What profit is there in my destruction, if I go down to the pit? Shall the dust praise you? Shall it declare your truth?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Hear, Yahweh, and have mercy on me. Yahweh, be my helper.”

<a id="v11"></a>**11** You have turned my mourning into dancing for me. You have removed my sackcloth, and clothed me with gladness,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** To the end that my heart may sing praise to you, and not be silent. Yahweh my God, I will give thanks to you forever!
