# Psalms 85 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Lord, thou hast been favourable unto thy land: thou hast brought back the captivity of Jacob.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Thou hast forgiven the iniquity of thy people, thou hast covered all their sin. Selah.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thou hast taken away all thy wrath: thou hast turned thyself from the fierceness of thine anger.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Turn us, O God of our salvation, and cause thine anger toward us to cease.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Wilt thou be angry with us for ever? wilt thou draw out thine anger to all generations?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Wilt thou not revive us again: that thy people may rejoice in thee?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Shew us thy mercy, O Lord, and grant us thy salvation.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will hear what God the Lord will speak: for he will speak peace unto his people, and to his saints: but let them not turn again to folly.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Surely his salvation is nigh them that fear him; that glory may dwell in our land.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Mercy and truth are met together; righteousness and peace have kissed each other.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Truth shall spring out of the earth; and righteousness shall look down from heaven.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yea, the Lord shall give that which is good; and our land shall yield her increase.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Righteousness shall go before him; and shall set us in the way of his steps.
