# Psalms 85 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Lord, you were good to your land: changing the fate of Jacob.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The wrongdoing of your people had forgiveness; all their sin had been covered. (Selah.)

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You were no longer angry: you were turned from the heat of your wrath.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Come back to us, O God of our salvation, and be angry with us no longer.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Will you go on being angry with us for ever? will you keep your wrath against us through all the long generations?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Will you not give us life again, so that your people may be glad in you?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Let us see your mercy, O Lord, and give us your salvation.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will give ear to the voice of the Lord; for he will say words of peace to his people and to his saints; but let them not go back to their foolish ways.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Truly, his salvation is near to his worshippers; so that glory may be in our land.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Mercy and faith have come together; righteousness and peace have given one another a kiss.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Faith comes up from the earth like a plant; righteousness is looking down from heaven.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The Lord will give what is good; and our land will give its increase.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Righteousness will go before him, making a way for his footsteps.
