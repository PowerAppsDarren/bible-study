# Psalms 85 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh, you have been favorable to your land. You have restored the fortunes of Jacob.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You have forgiven the iniquity of your people. You have covered all their sin. Selah.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You have taken away all your wrath. You have turned from the fierceness of your anger.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Turn us, God of our salvation, and cause your indignation toward us to cease.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Will you be angry with us forever? Will you draw out your anger to all generations?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Won’t you revive us again, that your people may rejoice in you?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Show us your loving kindness, Yahweh. Grant us your salvation.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will hear what God, Yahweh, will speak, for he will speak peace to his people, his saints; but let them not turn again to folly.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Surely his salvation is near those who fear him, that glory may dwell in our land.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Mercy and truth meet together. Righteousness and peace have kissed each other.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Truth springs out of the earth. Righteousness has looked down from heaven.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yes, Yahweh will give that which is good. Our land will yield its increase.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Righteousness goes before him, And prepares the way for his steps.
