# Psalms 85 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Jehovah, thou hast been favorable unto thy land; Thou hast brought back the captivity of Jacob.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Thou hast forgiven the iniquity of thy people; Thou hast covered all their sin. [Selah

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thou hast taken away all thy wrath; Thou hast turned thyself from the fierceness of thine anger.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Turn us, O God of our salvation, And cause thine indignation toward us to cease.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Wilt thou be angry with us for ever? Wilt thou draw out thine anger to all generations?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Wilt thou not quicken us again, That thy people may rejoice in thee?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Show us thy lovingkindness, O Jehovah, And grant us thy salvation.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will hear what God Jehovah will speak; For he will speak peace unto his people, and to his saints: But let them not turn again to folly.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Surely his salvation is nigh them that fear him, That glory may dwell in our land.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Mercy and truth are met together; Righteousness and peace have kissed each other.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Truth springeth out of the earth; And righteousness hath looked down from heaven.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yea, Jehovah will give that which is good; And our land shall yield its increase.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Righteousness shall go before him, And shall make his footsteps a way to walk in.
