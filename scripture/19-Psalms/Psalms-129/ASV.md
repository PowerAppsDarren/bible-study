# Psalms 129 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Many a time have they afflicted me from my youth up, Let Israel now say,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Many a time have they afflicted me from my youth up: Yet they have not prevailed against me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The plowers plowed upon my back; They made long their furrows.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Jehovah is righteous: He hath cut asunder the cords of the wicked.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let them be put to shame and turned backward, All they that hate Zion.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let them be as the grass upon the housetops, Which withereth before it groweth up;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Wherewith the reaper filleth not his hand, Nor he that bindeth sheaves, his bosom:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Neither do they that go by say, The blessing of Jehovah be upon you; We bless you in the name of Jehovah.
