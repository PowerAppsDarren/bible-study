# Psalms 129 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Many a time have they afflicted me from my youth, may Israel now say:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Many a time have they afflicted me from my youth: yet they have not prevailed against me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The plowers plowed upon my back: they made long their furrows.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The Lord is righteous: he hath cut asunder the cords of the wicked.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let them all be confounded and turned back that hate Zion.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let them be as the grass upon the housetops, which withereth afore it groweth up:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Wherewith the mower filleth not his hand; nor he that bindeth sheaves his bosom.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Neither do they which go by say, The blessing of the Lord be upon you: we bless you in the name of the Lord.
