# Psalms 129 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Many times they have afflicted me from my youth up. Let Israel now say,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** many times they have afflicted me from my youth up, yet they have not prevailed against me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The plowers plowed on my back. They made their furrows long.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Yahweh is righteous. He has cut apart the cords of the wicked.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let them be disappointed and turned backward, all those who hate Zion.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let them be as the grass on the housetops, which withers before it grows up;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** with which the reaper doesn’t fill his hand, nor he who binds sheaves, his bosom.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Neither do those who go by say, “The blessing of Yahweh be on you. We bless you in Yahweh’s name.”
