# Psalms 129 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Great have been my troubles from the time when I was young (let Israel now say);

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Great have been my troubles from the time when I was young, but my troubles have not overcome me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The ploughmen were ploughing on my back; long were the wounds they made.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The Lord is true: the cords of the evil-doers are broken in two.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let all the haters of Zion be shamed and turned back.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let them be like the grass on the house-tops, which is dry before it comes to full growth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He who gets in the grain has no use for it; and they do not make bands of it for the grain-stems.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And those who go by do not say, The blessing of the Lord be on you; we give you blessing in the name of the Lord.
