# Psalms 139 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh, you have searched me, and you know me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You know my sitting down and my rising up. You perceive my thoughts from afar.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You search out my path and my lying down, and are acquainted with all my ways.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For there is not a word on my tongue, but, behold, Yahweh, you know it altogether.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You hem me in behind and before. You laid your hand on me.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** This knowledge is beyond me. It’s lofty. I can’t attain it.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Where could I go from your Spirit? Or where could I flee from your presence?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** If I ascend up into heaven, you are there. If I make my bed in Sheol, behold, you are there!

<a id="v9"></a>**9** If I take the wings of the dawn, and settle in the uttermost parts of the sea;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Even there your hand will lead me, and your right hand will hold me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** If I say, “Surely the darkness will overwhelm me; the light around me will be night”;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** even the darkness doesn’t hide from you, but the night shines as the day. The darkness is like light to you.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For you formed my inmost being. You knit me together in my mother’s womb.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I will give thanks to you, for I am fearfully and wonderfully made. Your works are wonderful. My soul knows that very well.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** My frame wasn’t hidden from you, when I was made in secret, woven together in the depths of the earth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Your eyes saw my body. In your book they were all written, the days that were ordained for me, when as yet there were none of them.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** How precious to me are your thoughts, God! How vast is their sum!

<a id="v18"></a>**18** If I would count them, they are more in number than the sand. When I wake up, I am still with you.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** If only you, God, would kill the wicked. Get away from me, you bloodthirsty men!

<a id="v20"></a>**20** For they speak against you wickedly. Your enemies take your name in vain.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Yahweh, don’t I hate those who hate you? Am I not grieved with those who rise up against you?

<a id="v22"></a>**22** I hate them with perfect hatred. They have become my enemies.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Search me, God, and know my heart. Try me, and know my thoughts.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** See if there is any wicked way in me, and lead me in the everlasting way.
