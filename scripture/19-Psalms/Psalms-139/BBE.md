# Psalms 139 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Lord, you have knowledge of me, searching out all my secrets.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You have knowledge when I am seated and when I get up, you see my thoughts from far away.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You keep watch over my steps and my sleep, and have knowledge of all my ways.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For there is not a word on my tongue which is not clear to you, O Lord.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I am shut in by you on every side, and you have put your hand on me.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Such knowledge is a wonder greater than my powers; it is so high that I may not come near it.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Where may I go from your spirit? how may I go in flight from you?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** If I go up to heaven, you are there: or if I make my bed in the underworld, you are there.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** If I take the wings of the morning, and go to the farthest parts of the sea;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Even there will I be guided by your hand, and your right hand will keep me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** If I say, Only let me be covered by the dark, and the light about me be night;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Even the dark is not dark to you; the night is as bright as the day: for dark and light are the same to you.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** My flesh was made by you, and my parts joined together in my mother's body.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I will give you praise, for I am strangely and delicately formed; your works are great wonders, and of this my soul is fully conscious.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** My frame was not unseen by you when I was made secretly, and strangely formed in the lowest parts of the earth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Your eyes saw my unformed substance; in your book all my days were recorded, even those which were purposed before they had come into being.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** How dear are your thoughts to me, O God! how great is the number of them!

<a id="v18"></a>**18** If I made up their number, it would be more than the grains of sand; when I am awake, I am still with you.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** If only you would put the sinners to death, O God; go far from me, you men of blood.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** For they go against you with evil designs, and your haters make sport of your name.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Are not your haters hated by me, O Lord? are not those who are lifted up against you a cause of grief to me?

<a id="v22"></a>**22** My hate for them is complete; my thoughts of them are as if they were making war on me.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** O God, let the secrets of my heart be uncovered, and let my wandering thoughts be tested:

<a id="v24"></a>**24** See if there is any way of sorrow in me, and be my guide in the eternal way.
