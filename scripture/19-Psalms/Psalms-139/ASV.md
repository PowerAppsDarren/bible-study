# Psalms 139 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Jehovah, thou hast searched me, and knownme.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Thou knowest my downsitting and mine uprising; Thou understandest my thought afar off.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thou searchest out my path and my lying down, And art acquainted with all my ways.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For there is not a word in my tongue, But, lo, O Jehovah, thou knowest it altogether.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thou hast beset me behind and before, And laid thy hand upon me.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Suchknowledge is too wonderful for me; It is high, I cannot attain unto it.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Whither shall I go from thy Spirit? Or whither shall I flee from thy presence?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** If I ascend up into heaven, thou art there: If I make my bed in Sheol, behold, thou art there.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** If I take the wings of the morning, And dwell in the uttermost parts of the sea;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Even there shall thy hand lead me, And thy right hand shall hold me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** If I say, Surely the darkness shall overwhelm me, And the light about me shall be night;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Even the darkness hideth not from thee, But the night shineth as the day: The darkness and the light are both aliketo thee.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For thou didst form my inward parts: Thou didst cover me in my mother’s womb.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I will give thanks unto thee; for I am fearfully and wonderfully made: Wonderful are thy works; And that my soul knoweth right well.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** My frame was not hidden from thee, When I was made in secret, And curiously wrought in the lowest parts of the earth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Thine eyes did see mine unformed substance; And in thy book they were all written, Eventhe days that were ordainedfor me, When as yet there was none of them.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** How precious also are thy thoughts unto me, O God! How great is the sum of them!

<a id="v18"></a>**18** If I should count them, they are more in number than the sand: When I awake, I am still with thee.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Surely thou wilt slay the wicked, O God: Depart from me therefore, ye bloodthirsty men.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** For they speak against thee wickedly, And thine enemies take thy name in vain.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Do not I hate them, O Jehovah, that hate thee? And am not I grieved with those that rise up against thee?

<a id="v22"></a>**22** I hate them with perfect hatred: They are become mine enemies.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Search me, O God, and know my heart: Try me, and know my thoughts;

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And see if there be any wicked way in me, And lead me in the way everlasting.
