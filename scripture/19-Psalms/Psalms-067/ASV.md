# Psalms 67 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God be merciful unto us, and bless us, And cause his face to shine upon us; [Selah

<a id="v2"></a>**2** That thy way may be known upon earth, Thy salvation among all nations.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let the peoples praise thee, O God; Let all the peoples praise thee.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Oh let the nations be glad and sing for joy; For thou wilt judge the peoples with equity, And govern the nations upon earth. [Selah

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let the peoples praise thee, O God; Let all the peoples praise thee.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The earth hath yielded its increase: God, even our own God, will bless us.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** God will bless us; And all the ends of the earth shall fear him.
