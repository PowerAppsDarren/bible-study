# Psalms 67 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** May God give us mercy and blessing, and let the light of his face be shining on us; (Selah.)

<a id="v2"></a>**2** So that men may see your way on the earth, and your salvation among all nations.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let the peoples give you praise, O God; let all the peoples give you praise.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** O let the nations be glad, and make song of joy; for you will be the judge of the peoples in righteousness, guiding the nations of the earth. (Selah.)

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let the peoples give you praise, O God; let all the peoples give you praise.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The earth has given her increase; and God, even our God, will give us his blessing.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** God will give us his blessing; so let all the ends of the earth be in fear of him.
