# Psalms 67 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God be merciful unto us, and bless us; and cause his face to shine upon us; Selah.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** That thy way may be known upon earth, thy saving health among all nations.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the people praise thee.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** O let the nations be glad and sing for joy: for thou shalt judge the people righteously, and govern the nations upon earth. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the people praise thee.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Then shall the earth yield her increase; and God, even our own God, shall bless us.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** God shall bless us; and all the ends of the earth shall fear him.
