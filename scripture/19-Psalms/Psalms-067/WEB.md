# Psalms 67 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** May God be merciful to us, bless us, and cause his face to shine on us. Selah.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** That your way may be known on earth, and your salvation among all nations,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** let the peoples praise you, God. Let all the peoples praise you.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Oh let the nations be glad and sing for joy, for you will judge the peoples with equity, and govern the nations on earth. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let the peoples praise you, God. Let all the peoples praise you.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The earth has yielded its increase. God, even our own God, will bless us.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** God will bless us. All the ends of the earth shall fear him.
