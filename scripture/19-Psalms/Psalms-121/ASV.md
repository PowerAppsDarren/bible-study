# Psalms 121 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will lift up mine eyes unto the mountains: From whence shall my help come?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My helpcomethfrom Jehovah, Who made heaven and earth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He will not suffer thy foot to be moved: He that keepeth thee will not slumber.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Behold, he that keepeth Israel Will neither slumber nor sleep.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Jehovah is thy keeper: Jehovah is thy shade upon thy right hand.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The sun shall not smite thee by day, Nor the moon by night.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Jehovah will keep thee from all evil; He will keep thy soul.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Jehovah will keep thy going out and thy coming in From this time forth and for evermore.
