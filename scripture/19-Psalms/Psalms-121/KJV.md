# Psalms 121 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will lift up mine eyes unto the hills, from whence cometh my help.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My help cometh from the Lord, which made heaven and earth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He will not suffer thy foot to be moved: he that keepeth thee will not slumber.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Behold, he that keepeth Israel shall neither slumber nor sleep.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The Lord is thy keeper: the Lord is thy shade upon thy right hand.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The sun shall not smite thee by day, nor the moon by night.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The Lord shall preserve thee from all evil: he shall preserve thy soul.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The Lord shall preserve thy going out and thy coming in from this time forth, and even for evermore.
