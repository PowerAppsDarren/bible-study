# Psalms 121 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My eyes are lifted up to the hills: O where will my help come from?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Your help comes from the Lord, who made heaven and earth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** May he not let your foot be moved: no need of sleep has he who keeps you.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** See, the eyes of Israel's keeper will not be shut in sleep.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The Lord is your keeper; the Lord is your shade on your right hand.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You will not be touched by the sun in the day, or by the moon at night.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The Lord will keep you safe from all evil; he will take care of your soul.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The Lord will keep watch over your going out and your coming in, from this time and for ever.
