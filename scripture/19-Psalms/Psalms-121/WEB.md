# Psalms 121 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will lift up my eyes to the hills. Where does my help come from?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My help comes from Yahweh, who made heaven and earth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He will not allow your foot to be moved. He who keeps you will not slumber.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Behold, he who keeps Israel will neither slumber nor sleep.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Yahweh is your keeper. Yahweh is your shade on your right hand.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The sun will not harm you by day, nor the moon by night.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Yahweh will keep you from all evil. He will keep your soul.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yahweh will keep your going out and your coming in, from this time forward, and forever more.
