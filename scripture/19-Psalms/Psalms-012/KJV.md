# Psalms 12 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Help, Lord; for the godly man ceaseth; for the faithful fail from among the children of men.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** They speak vanity every one with his neighbour: with flattering lips and with a double heart do they speak.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The Lord shall cut off all flattering lips, and the tongue that speaketh proud things:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Who have said, With our tongue will we prevail; our lips are our own: who is lord over us?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For the oppression of the poor, for the sighing of the needy, now will I arise, saith the Lord; I will set him in safety from him that puffeth at him.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The words of the Lord are pure words: as silver tried in a furnace of earth, purified seven times.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Thou shalt keep them, O Lord, thou shalt preserve them from this generation for ever.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The wicked walk on every side, when the vilest men are exalted.
