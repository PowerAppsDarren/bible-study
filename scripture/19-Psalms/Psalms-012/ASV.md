# Psalms 12 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Help, Jehovah; for the godly man ceaseth; For the faithful fail from among the children of men.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** They speak falsehood every one with his neighbor: With flattering lip, and with a double heart, do they speak.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Jehovah will cut off all flattering lips, The tongue that speaketh great things;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Who have said, With our tongue will we prevail; Our lips are our own: who is lord over us?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Because of the oppression of the poor, because of the sighing of the needy, Now will I arise, saith Jehovah; I will set him in the safety he panteth for.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The words of Jehovah are pure words; As silver tried in a furnace on the earth, Purified seven times.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Thou wilt keep them, O Jehovah, Thou wilt preserve them from this generation for ever.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The wicked walk on every side, When vileness is exalted among the sons of men.
