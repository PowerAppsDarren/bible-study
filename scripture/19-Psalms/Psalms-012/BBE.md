# Psalms 12 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Send help, Lord, for mercy has come to an end; there is no more faith among the children of men.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Everyone says false words to his neighbour: their tongues are smooth in their talk, and their hearts are full of deceit.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The smooth lips and the tongue of pride will be cut off by the Lord.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They have said, With our tongues will we overcome; our lips are ours: who is lord over us?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Because of the crushing of the poor and the weeping of those in need, now will I come to his help, says the Lord; I will give him the salvation which he is desiring.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The words of the Lord are true words: like silver tested by fire and burned clean seven times.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You will keep them, O Lord, you will keep them safe from this generation for ever.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The sinners are walking on every side, and evil is honoured among the children of men.
