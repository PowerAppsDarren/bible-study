# Psalms 136 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give thanks to Yahweh, for he is good; for his loving kindness endures forever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Give thanks to the God of gods; for his loving kindness endures forever.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Give thanks to the Lord of lords; for his loving kindness endures forever:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** To him who alone does great wonders; for his loving kindness endures forever:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** To him who by understanding made the heavens; for his loving kindness endures forever:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** To him who spread out the earth above the waters; for his loving kindness endures forever:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** To him who made the great lights; for his loving kindness endures forever:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The sun to rule by day; for his loving kindness endures forever;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The moon and stars to rule by night; for his loving kindness endures forever:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** To him who struck down the Egyptian firstborn; for his loving kindness endures forever;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And brought out Israel from among them; for his loving kindness endures forever;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** With a strong hand, and with an outstretched arm; for his loving kindness endures forever:

<a id="v13"></a>**13** To him who divided the Red Sea apart; for his loving kindness endures forever;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And made Israel to pass through its midst; for his loving kindness endures forever;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But overthrew Pharaoh and his army in the Red Sea; for his loving kindness endures forever:

<a id="v16"></a>**16** To him who led his people through the wilderness; for his loving kindness endures forever:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** To him who struck great kings; for his loving kindness endures forever;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And killed mighty kings; for his loving kindness endures forever:

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Sihon king of the Amorites; for his loving kindness endures forever;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Og king of Bashan; for his loving kindness endures forever;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And gave their land as an inheritance; for his loving kindness endures forever;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Even a heritage to Israel his servant; for his loving kindness endures forever:

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Who remembered us in our low estate; for his loving kindness endures forever;

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And has delivered us from our adversaries; for his loving kindness endures forever:

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Who gives food to every creature; for his loving kindness endures forever.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Oh give thanks to the God of heaven; for his loving kindness endures forever.
