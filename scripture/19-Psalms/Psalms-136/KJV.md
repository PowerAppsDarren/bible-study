# Psalms 136 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O give thanks unto the Lord; for he is good: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** O give thanks unto the God of gods: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** O give thanks to the Lord of lords: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** To him who alone doeth great wonders: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** To him that by wisdom made the heavens: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** To him that stretched out the earth above the waters: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** To him that made great lights: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The sun to rule by day: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The moon and stars to rule by night: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** To him that smote Egypt in their firstborn: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And brought out Israel from among them: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** With a strong hand, and with a stretched out arm: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** To him which divided the Red sea into parts: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And made Israel to pass through the midst of it: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But overthrew Pharaoh and his host in the Red sea: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** To him which led his people through the wilderness: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** To him which smote great kings: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And slew famous kings: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Sihon king of the Amorites: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And Og the king of Bashan: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And gave their land for an heritage: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Even an heritage unto Israel his servant: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Who remembered us in our low estate: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And hath redeemed us from our enemies: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Who giveth food to all flesh: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** O give thanks unto the God of heaven: for his mercy endureth for ever.
