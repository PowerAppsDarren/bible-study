# Psalms 136 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O give praise to the Lord, for he is good: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** O give praise to the God of gods: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** O give praise to the Lord of lords: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** To him who only does great wonders: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** To him who by wisdom made the heavens: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** To him by whom the earth was stretched out over the waters: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** To him who made great lights: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The sun to have rule by day: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The moon and the stars to have rule by night: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** To him who put to death the first-fruits of Egypt: for his mercy is unchanging for ever:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And took out Israel from among them: for his mercy is unchanging for ever:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** With a strong hand and an outstretched arm: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** To him who made a way through the Red Sea: for his mercy is unchanging for ever:

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And let Israel go through it: for his mercy is unchanging for ever:

<a id="v15"></a>**15** By him Pharaoh and his army were overturned in the Red Sea: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** To him who took his people through the waste land: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** To him who overcame great kings: for his mercy is unchanging for ever:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And put noble kings to death: for his mercy is unchanging for ever:

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Sihon, king of the Amorites: for his mercy is unchanging for ever:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And Og, king of Bashan: for his mercy is unchanging for ever:

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And gave their land to his people for a heritage: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Even a heritage for his servant Israel: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Who kept us in mind when we were in trouble: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And has taken us out of the hands of our haters: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Who gives food to all flesh: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** O give praise to the God of heaven: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.
