# Psalms 56 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Be merciful unto me, O God: for man would swallow me up; he fighting daily oppresseth me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Mine enemies would daily swallow me up: for they be many that fight against me, O thou most High.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** What time I am afraid, I will trust in thee.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** In God I will praise his word, in God I have put my trust; I will not fear what flesh can do unto me.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Every day they wrest my words: all their thoughts are against me for evil.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They gather themselves together, they hide themselves, they mark my steps, when they wait for my soul.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Shall they escape by iniquity? in thine anger cast down the people, O God.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Thou tellest my wanderings: put thou my tears into thy bottle: are they not in thy book?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** When I cry unto thee, then shall mine enemies turn back: this I know; for God is for me.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** In God will I praise his word: in the Lord will I praise his word.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** In God have I put my trust: I will not be afraid what man can do unto me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Thy vows are upon me, O God: I will render praises unto thee.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For thou hast delivered my soul from death: wilt not thou deliver my feet from falling, that I may walk before God in the light of the living?
