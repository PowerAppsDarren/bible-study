# Psalms 56 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Have mercy on me, O God, for man is attempting my destruction; every day he makes cruel attacks against me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My haters are ever ready to put an end to me; great numbers are lifting themselves up against me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** In the time of my fear, I will have faith in you.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** In God will I give praise to his word; in God have I put my hope; I will have no fear of what flesh may do to me.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Every day they make wrong use of my words; all their thoughts are against me for evil.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They come together, they are waiting in secret places, they take note of my steps, they are waiting for my soul.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** By evil-doing they will not get free from punishment. In wrath, O God, let the peoples be made low.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** You have seen my wanderings; put the drops from my eyes into your bottle; are they not in your record?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** When I send up my cry to you, my haters will be turned back; I am certain of this, for God is with me.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** In God will I give praise to his word; in the Lord will I give praise to his word.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** In God have I put my hope, I will have no fear of what man may do to me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I keep the memory of my debt to you, O God; I will give you the offerings of praise.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Because you have taken my soul from the power of death; and kept my feet from falling, so that I may be walking before God in the light of life.
