# Psalms 41 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Blessed is he that considereth the poor: Jehovah will deliver him in the day of evil.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Jehovah will preserve him, and keep him alive, And he shall be blessed upon the earth; And deliver not thou him unto the will of his enemies.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Jehovah will support him upon the couch of languishing: Thou makest all his bed in his sickness.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I said, O Jehovah, have mercy upon me: Heal my soul; for I have sinned against thee.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Mine enemies speak evil against me, saying, When will he die, and his name perish?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And if he come to see me, he speaketh falsehood; His heart gathereth iniquity to itself: When he goeth abroad, he telleth it.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** All that hate me whisper together against me; Against me do they devise my hurt.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** An evil disease, say they, cleaveth fast unto him; And now that he lieth he shall rise up no more.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Yea, mine own familiar friend, in whom I trusted, Who did eat of my bread, Hath lifted up his heel against me.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But thou, O Jehovah, have mercy upon me, and raise me up, That I may requite them.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** By this I know that thou delightest in me, Because mine enemy doth not triumph over me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And as for me, thou upholdest me in mine integrity, And settest me before thy face for ever.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Blessed be Jehovah, the God of Israel, From everlasting and to everlasting. Amen, and Amen.
