# Psalms 41 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Blessed is he that considereth the poor: the Lord will deliver him in time of trouble.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The Lord will preserve him, and keep him alive; and he shall be blessed upon the earth: and thou wilt not deliver him unto the will of his enemies.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The Lord will strengthen him upon the bed of languishing: thou wilt make all his bed in his sickness.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I said, Lord, be merciful unto me: heal my soul; for I have sinned against thee.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Mine enemies speak evil of me, When shall he die, and his name perish?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And if he come to see me, he speaketh vanity: his heart gathereth iniquity to itself; when he goeth abroad, he telleth it.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** All that hate me whisper together against me: against me do they devise my hurt.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** An evil disease, say they, cleaveth fast unto him: and now that he lieth he shall rise up no more.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Yea, mine own familiar friend, in whom I trusted, which did eat of my bread, hath lifted up his heel against me.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But thou, O Lord, be merciful unto me, and raise me up, that I may requite them.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** By this I know that thou favourest me, because mine enemy doth not triumph over me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And as for me, thou upholdest me in mine integrity, and settest me before thy face for ever.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Blessed be the Lord God of Israel from everlasting, and to everlasting. Amen, and Amen.
