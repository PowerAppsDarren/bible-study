# Psalms 42 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** As the hart panteth after the water brooks, So panteth my soul after thee, O God.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My soul thirsteth for God, for the living God: When shall I come and appear before God?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** My tears have been my food day and night, While they continually say unto me, Where is thy God?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** These things I remember, and pour out my soul within me, How I went with the throng, and led them to the house of God, With the voice of joy and praise, a multitude keeping holyday.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Why art thou cast down, O my soul? Andwhy art thou disquieted within me? Hope thou in God; for I shall yet praise him Forthe help of his countenance.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** O my God, my soul is cast down within me: Therefore do I remember thee from the land of the Jordan, And the Hermons, from the hill Mizar.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Deep calleth unto deep at the noise of thy waterfalls: All thy waves and thy billows are gone over me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yet Jehovah will command his lovingkindness in the day-time; And in the night his song shall be with me, Evena prayer unto the God of my life.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will say unto God my rock, Why hast thou forgotten me? Why go I mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** As with a sword in my bones, mine adversaries reproach me, While they continually say unto me, Where is thy God?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Why art thou cast down, O my soul? And why art thou disquieted within me? Hope thou in God; for I shall yet praise him, Who isthe help of my countenance, and my God.
