# Psalms 128 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Happy is the worshipper of the Lord, who is walking in his ways.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You will have the fruit of the work of your hands: happy will you be, and all will be well for you.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Your wife will be like a fertile vine in the inmost parts of your house: your children will be like olive plants round your table.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** See! this is the blessing of the worshipper of the Lord.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** May the Lord send you blessing out of Zion: may you see the good of Jerusalem all the days of your life.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** May you see your children's children. Peace be on Israel.
