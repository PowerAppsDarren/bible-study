# Psalms 128 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Blessed is everyone who fears Yahweh, who walks in his ways.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For you will eat the labor of your hands. You will be happy, and it will be well with you.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Your wife will be as a fruitful vine, in the innermost parts of your house; your children like olive plants, around your table.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Behold, thus is the man blessed who fears Yahweh.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** May Yahweh bless you out of Zion, and may you see the good of Jerusalem all the days of your life.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Yes, may you see your children’s children. Peace be upon Israel.
