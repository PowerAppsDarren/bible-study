# Psalms 128 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Blessed is every one that feareth the Lord; that walketh in his ways.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For thou shalt eat the labour of thine hands: happy shalt thou be, and it shall be well with thee.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thy wife shall be as a fruitful vine by the sides of thine house: thy children like olive plants round about thy table.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Behold, that thus shall the man be blessed that feareth the Lord.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The Lord shall bless thee out of Zion: and thou shalt see the good of Jerusalem all the days of thy life.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Yea, thou shalt see thy children’s children, and peace upon Israel.
