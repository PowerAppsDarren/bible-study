# Psalms 128 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Blessed is every one that feareth Jehovah, That walketh in his ways.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For thou shalt eat the labor of thy hands: Happy shalt thou be, and it shall be well with thee.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thy wife shall be as a fruitful vine, In the innermost parts of thy house; Thy children like olive plants, Round about thy table.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Behold, thus shall the man be blessed That feareth Jehovah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Jehovah bless thee out of Zion: And see thou the good of Jerusalem all the days of thy life.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Yea, see thou thy children’s children. Peace be upon Israel.
