# Psalms 69 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Be my saviour, O God; because the waters have come in, even to my neck.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My feet are deep in the soft earth, where there is no support; I have come into deep waters, the waves are flowing over me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I am tired with my crying; my throat is burning: my eyes are wasted with waiting for my God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Those who have hate for me without cause are greater in number than the hairs of my head; those who are against me, falsely desiring my destruction, are very strong; I gave back what I had not taken away.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** O God, you see how foolish I am; and my wrongdoing is clear to you.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let not those who have hope in you be put to shame because of me, O Lord God of armies: let not those who are waiting for you be made low because of me, O God of Israel.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I have been wounded with sharp words because of you; my face has been covered with shame.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I have become strange to my brothers, and like a man from a far country to my mother's children.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I am on fire with passion for your house; and the hard things which are said about you have come on me.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** My bitter weeping, and my going without food, were turned to my shame.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** When I put on the clothing of grief, they said evil of me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I am a cause of wonder to those in authority; a song to those who are given to strong drink.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But as for me, let my prayer be made to you, O Lord, at a time when you are pleased; O God, give me an answer in your great mercy, for your salvation is certain.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Take me from the grip of the sticky earth, so that I may not go down into it; let me be lifted up from the deep waters.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let me not be covered by the flowing waters; let not the deep waters go over my head, and let me not be shut up in the underworld.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Give an answer to my words, O Lord; for your mercy is good: be turned to me, because of your great pity.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Let not your face be covered from your servant, for I am in trouble; quickly give me an answer.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Come near to my soul, for its salvation: be my saviour, because of those who are against me.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** You have seen my shame, how I was laughed at and made low; my haters are all before you.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My heart is broken by bitter words, I am full of grief; I made a search for some to have pity on me, but there was no one; I had no comforter.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** They gave me poison for my food; and bitter wine for my drink.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Let their table before them be for their destruction; let their feasts become a net to take them.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Let their eyes be blind so that they may not see; let their bodies for ever be shaking.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Let your curse come on them; let the heat of your wrath overtake them.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Give their houses to destruction, and let there be no one in their tents.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Because they are cruel to him against whom your hand is turned; they make bitter the grief of him who is wounded by you.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Let their punishment be increased; let them not come into your righteousness.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Let their names be taken from the book of the living, let them not be numbered with the upright.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** But I am poor and full of sorrow; let me be lifted up by your salvation, O Lord.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** I will give praise to the name of God with a song; I will give glory to him for what he has done.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** This will be more pleasing to the Lord than an ox, or a young ox of full growth.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The poor will see it and be glad: you who are lovers of God, let your hearts have life.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** For the ears of the Lord are open to the poor, and he takes thought for his prisoners.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Let the heavens and the earth give praise to him, the seas, and everything moving in them.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** For God will be the saviour of Zion, and the builder of the towns of Judah; so that it may be their resting-place and heritage.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** The seed of his servants will have their part in it, and there the lovers of his name will have rest.
