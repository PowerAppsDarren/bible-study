# Psalms 69 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Save me, O God; for the waters are come in unto my soul.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I sink in deep mire, where there is no standing: I am come into deep waters, where the floods overflow me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I am weary of my crying: my throat is dried: mine eyes fail while I wait for my God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They that hate me without a cause are more than the hairs of mine head: they that would destroy me, being mine enemies wrongfully, are mighty: then I restored that which I took not away.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** O God, thou knowest my foolishness; and my sins are not hid from thee.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let not them that wait on thee, O Lord God of hosts, be ashamed for my sake: let not those that seek thee be confounded for my sake, O God of Israel.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Because for thy sake I have borne reproach; shame hath covered my face.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I am become a stranger unto my brethren, and an alien unto my mother’s children.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For the zeal of thine house hath eaten me up; and the reproaches of them that reproached thee are fallen upon me.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** When I wept, and chastened my soul with fasting, that was to my reproach.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I made sackcloth also my garment; and I became a proverb to them.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They that sit in the gate speak against me; and I was the song of the drunkards.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But as for me, my prayer is unto thee, O Lord, in an acceptable time: O God, in the multitude of thy mercy hear me, in the truth of thy salvation.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Deliver me out of the mire, and let me not sink: let me be delivered from them that hate me, and out of the deep waters.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let not the waterflood overflow me, neither let the deep swallow me up, and let not the pit shut her mouth upon me.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Hear me, O Lord; for thy lovingkindness is good: turn unto me according to the multitude of thy tender mercies.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And hide not thy face from thy servant; for I am in trouble: hear me speedily.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Draw nigh unto my soul, and redeem it: deliver me because of mine enemies.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Thou hast known my reproach, and my shame, and my dishonour: mine adversaries are all before thee.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Reproach hath broken my heart; and I am full of heaviness: and I looked for some to take pity, but there was none; and for comforters, but I found none.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** They gave me also gall for my meat; and in my thirst they gave me vinegar to drink.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Let their table become a snare before them: and that which should have been for their welfare, let it become a trap.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Let their eyes be darkened, that they see not; and make their loins continually to shake.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Pour out thine indignation upon them, and let thy wrathful anger take hold of them.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Let their habitation be desolate; and let none dwell in their tents.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** For they persecute him whom thou hast smitten; and they talk to the grief of those whom thou hast wounded.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Add iniquity unto their iniquity: and let them not come into thy righteousness.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Let them be blotted out of the book of the living, and not be written with the righteous.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** But I am poor and sorrowful: let thy salvation, O God, set me up on high.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** I will praise the name of God with a song, and will magnify him with thanksgiving.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** This also shall please the Lord better than an ox or bullock that hath horns and hoofs.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The humble shall see this, and be glad: and your heart shall live that seek God.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** For the Lord heareth the poor, and despiseth not his prisoners.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Let the heaven and earth praise him, the seas, and every thing that moveth therein.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** For God will save Zion, and will build the cities of Judah: that they may dwell there, and have it in possession.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** The seed also of his servants shall inherit it: and they that love his name shall dwell therein.
