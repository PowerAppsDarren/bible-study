# Psalms 69 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Save me, God, for the waters have come up to my neck!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I sink in deep mire, where there is no foothold. I have come into deep waters, where the floods overflow me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I am weary with my crying. My throat is dry. My eyes fail, looking for my God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Those who hate me without a cause are more than the hairs of my head. Those who want to cut me off, being my enemies wrongfully, are mighty. I have to restore what I didn’t take away.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** God, you know my foolishness. My sins aren’t hidden from you.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Don’t let those who wait for you be shamed through me, Lord Yahweh of Armies. Don’t let those who seek you be brought to dishonor through me, God of Israel.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Because for your sake, I have borne reproach. Shame has covered my face.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I have become a stranger to my brothers, an alien to my mother’s children.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For the zeal of your house consumes me. The reproaches of those who reproach you have fallen on me.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** When I wept and I fasted, that was to my reproach.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** When I made sackcloth my clothing, I became a byword to them.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Those who sit in the gate talk about me. I am the song of the drunkards.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But as for me, my prayer is to you, Yahweh, in an acceptable time. God, in the abundance of your loving kindness, answer me in the truth of your salvation.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Deliver me out of the mire, and don’t let me sink. Let me be delivered from those who hate me, and out of the deep waters.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Don’t let the flood waters overwhelm me, neither let the deep swallow me up. Don’t let the pit shut its mouth on me.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Answer me, Yahweh, for your loving kindness is good. According to the multitude of your tender mercies, turn to me.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Don’t hide your face from your servant, for I am in distress. Answer me speedily!

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Draw near to my soul, and redeem it. Ransom me because of my enemies.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** You know my reproach, my shame, and my dishonor. My adversaries are all before you.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Reproach has broken my heart, and I am full of heaviness. I looked for some to take pity, but there was none; for comforters, but I found none.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** They also gave me gall for my food. In my thirst, they gave me vinegar to drink.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Let their table before them become a snare. May it become a retribution and a trap.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Let their eyes be darkened, so that they can’t see. Let their backs be continually bent.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Pour out your indignation on them. Let the fierceness of your anger overtake them.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Let their habitation be desolate. Let no one dwell in their tents.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** For they persecute him whom you have wounded. They tell of the sorrow of those whom you have hurt.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Charge them with crime upon crime. Don’t let them come into your righteousness.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Let them be blotted out of the book of life, and not be written with the righteous.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** But I am in pain and distress. Let your salvation, God, protect me.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** I will praise the name of God with a song, and will magnify him with thanksgiving.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** It will please Yahweh better than an ox, or a bull that has horns and hoofs.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The humble have seen it, and are glad. You who seek after God, let your heart live.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** For Yahweh hears the needy, and doesn’t despise his captive people.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Let heaven and earth praise him; the seas, and everything that moves therein!

<a id="v35"></a>**35** For God will save Zion, and build the cities of Judah. They shall settle there, and own it.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** The children also of his servants shall inherit it. Those who love his name shall dwell therein.
