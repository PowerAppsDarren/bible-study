# Psalms 69 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Save me, O God; For the waters are come in unto my soul.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I sink in deep mire, where there is no standing: I am come into deep waters, where the floods overflow me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I am weary with my crying; my throat is dried: Mine eyes fail while I wait for my God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They that hate me without a cause are more than the hairs of my head: They that would cut me off, being mine enemies wrongfully, are mighty: That which I took not away I have to restore.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** O God, thou knowest my foolishness; And my sins are not hid from thee.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let not them that wait for thee be put to shame through me, O Lord Jehovah of hosts: Let not those that seek thee be brought to dishonor through me, O God of Israel.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Because for thy sake I have borne reproach; Shame hath covered my face.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I am become a stranger unto my brethren, And an alien unto my mother’s children.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For the zeal of thy house hath eaten me up; And the reproaches of them that reproach thee are fallen upon me.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** When I wept, and chastened my soul with fasting, That was to my reproach.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** When I made sackcloth my clothing, I became a byword unto them.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They that sit in the gate talk of me; AndI amthe song of the drunkards.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But as for me, my prayer is unto thee, O Jehovah, in an acceptable time: O God, in the abundance of thy lovingkindness, Answer me in the truth of thy salvation.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Deliver me out of the mire, and let me not sink: Let me be delivered from them that hate me, and out of the deep waters.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let not the waterflood overwhelm me, Neither let the deep swallow me up; And let not the pit shut its mouth upon me.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Answer me, O Jehovah; for thy lovingkindness is good: According to the multitude of thy tender mercies turn thou unto me.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And hide not thy face from thy servant; For I am in distress; answer me speedily.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Draw nigh unto my soul, and redeem it: Ransom me because of mine enemies.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Thou knowest my reproach, and my shame, and my dishonor: Mine adversaries are all before thee.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Reproach hath broken my heart; and I am full of heaviness: And I looked for some to take pity, but there was none; And for comforters, but I found none.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** They gave me also gall for my food; And in my thirst they gave me vinegar to drink.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Let their table before them become a snare; And when they are in peace, let it become a trap.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Let their eyes be darkened, so that they cannot see; And make their loins continually to shake.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Pour out thine indignation upon them, And let the fierceness of thine anger overtake them.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Let their habitation be desolate; Let none dwell in their tents.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** For they persecute him whom thou hast smitten; And they tell of the sorrow of those whom thou hast wounded.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Add iniquity unto their iniquity; And let them not come into thy righteousness.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Let them be blotted out of the book of life, And not be written with the righteous.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** But I am poor and sorrowful: Let thy salvation, O God, set me up on high.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** I will praise the name of God with a song, And will magnify him with thanksgiving.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** And it will please Jehovah better than an ox, Ora bullock that hath horns and hoofs.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The meek have seen it, and are glad: Ye that seek after God, let your heart live.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** For Jehovah heareth the needy, And despiseth not his prisoners.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Let heaven and earth praise him, The seas, and everything that moveth therein.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** For God will save Zion, and build the cities of Judah; And they shall abide there, and have it in possession.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** The seed also of his servants shall inherit it; And they that love his name shall dwell therein.
