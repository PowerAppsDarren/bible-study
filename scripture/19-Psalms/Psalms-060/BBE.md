# Psalms 60 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God, you have put us away from you, you have sent us in all directions, you have been angry; O be turned to us again.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** By the power of your hand the earth is shaking and broken; make it strong again, for it is moved.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You have made the people see hard times; you have given us the wine of shaking for our drink.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Give a safe place to those who have fear of you, where they may go in flight from before the bow. (Selah.)

<a id="v5"></a>**5** So that your loved ones may be made safe, let your right hand be my salvation, and give me an answer.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** God has said in his holy place, I will be glad: I will make a division of Shechem, and the valley of Succoth will be measured out.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Gilead is mine, and Manasseh is mine; and Ephraim is the strength of my head; Judah is my law-giver;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Moab is my washpot; over Edom will I put out my shoe; over Philistia will a glad cry be sounded.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Who will take me into the strong town? who will be my guide into Edom?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Have not you put us away, O God? and you have not gone out with our armies.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Give us help in our trouble; for there is no help in man.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Through God we will do great things, for through him our haters will be crushed under our feet.
