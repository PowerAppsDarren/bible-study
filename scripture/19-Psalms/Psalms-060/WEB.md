# Psalms 60 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God, you have rejected us. You have broken us down. You have been angry. Restore us, again.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You have made the land tremble. You have torn it. Mend its fractures, for it quakes.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You have shown your people hard things. You have made us drink the wine that makes us stagger.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You have given a banner to those who fear you, that it may be displayed because of the truth. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** So that your beloved may be delivered, save with your right hand, and answer us.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** God has spoken from his sanctuary: “I will triumph. I will divide Shechem, and measure out the valley of Succoth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Gilead is mine, and Manasseh is mine. Ephraim also is the defense of my head. Judah is my scepter.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Moab is my wash basin. I will throw my shoe on Edom. I shout in triumph over Philistia.”

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Who will bring me into the strong city? Who has led me to Edom?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Haven’t you, God, rejected us? You don’t go out with our armies, God.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Give us help against the adversary, for the help of man is vain.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Through God we shall do valiantly, for it is he who will tread down our adversaries.
