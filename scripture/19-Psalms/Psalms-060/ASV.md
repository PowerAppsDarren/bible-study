# Psalms 60 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O God thou hast cast us off, thou hast broken us down; Thou hast been angry; oh restore us again.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Thou hast made the land to tremble; thou hast rent it: Heal the breaches thereof; for it shaketh.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thou hast showed thy people hard things: Thou hast made us to drink the wine of staggering.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thou hast given a banner to them that fear thee, That it may be displayed because of the truth. [Selah

<a id="v5"></a>**5** That thy beloved may be delivered, Save with thy right hand, and answer us.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** God hath spoken in his holiness: I will exult; I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Gilead is mine, and Manasseh is mine; Ephraim also is the defence of my head; Judah is my sceptre.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Moab is my washpot; Upon Edom will I cast my shoe: Philistia, shout thou because of me.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Who will bring me into the strong city? Who hath led me unto Edom?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Hast not thou, O God, cast us off? And thou goest not forth, O God, with our hosts.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Give us help against the adversary; For vain is the help of man.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Through God we shall do valiantly; For he it is that will tread down our adversaries.
