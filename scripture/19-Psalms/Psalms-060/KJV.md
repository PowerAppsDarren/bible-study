# Psalms 60 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O God, thou hast cast us off, thou hast scattered us, thou hast been displeased; O turn thyself to us again.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Thou hast made the earth to tremble; thou hast broken it: heal the breaches thereof; for it shaketh.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thou hast shewed thy people hard things: thou hast made us to drink the wine of astonishment.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thou hast given a banner to them that fear thee, that it may be displayed because of the truth. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** That thy beloved may be delivered; save with thy right hand, and hear me.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** God hath spoken in his holiness; I will rejoice, I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Gilead is mine, and Manasseh is mine; Ephraim also is the strength of mine head; Judah is my lawgiver;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Moab is my washpot; over Edom will I cast out my shoe: Philistia, triumph thou because of me.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Who will bring me into the strong city? who will lead me into Edom?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Wilt not thou, O God, which hadst cast us off? and thou, O God, which didst not go out with our armies?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Give us help from trouble: for vain is the help of man.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Through God we shall do valiantly: for he it is that shall tread down our enemies.
