# Psalms 92 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** It is a good thing to give praise to the Lord, and to make melody to your name, O Most High;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** To make clear your mercy in the morning, and your unchanging faith every night;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** On a ten-corded instrument, and on an instrument of music with a quiet sound.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For you, O Lord, have made me glad through your work; I will have joy in the works of your hands.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** O Lord, how great are your works! and your thoughts are very deep.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** A man without sense has no knowledge of this; and a foolish man may not take it in.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** When the sinners come up like the grass, and all the workers of evil do well for themselves, it is so that their end may be eternal destruction.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But you, O Lord, are on high for ever.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For see! your haters, O Lord, will be put to death; all the workers of evil will be put to flight;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But my horn is lifted up like the horn of the ox: the best oil is flowing on my head.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My eyes have seen trouble come on my haters; my ears have news of the fate of the evil-doers who have come up against me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The good man will be like a tall tree in his strength; his growth will be as the wide-stretching trees of Lebanon.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Those who are planted in the house of the Lord will come up tall and strong in his gardens.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** They will give fruit even when they are old; they will be fertile and full of growth;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For a sign that the Lord is upright; he is my Rock, there is no deceit in him.
