# Psalms 92 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** It is a good thing to give thanks unto the Lord, and to sing praises unto thy name, O most High:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** To shew forth thy lovingkindness in the morning, and thy faithfulness every night,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Upon an instrument of ten strings, and upon the psaltery; upon the harp with a solemn sound.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For thou, Lord, hast made me glad through thy work: I will triumph in the works of thy hands.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** O Lord, how great are thy works! and thy thoughts are very deep.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** A brutish man knoweth not; neither doth a fool understand this.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** When the wicked spring as the grass, and when all the workers of iniquity do flourish; it is that they shall be destroyed for ever:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But thou, Lord, art most high for evermore.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For, lo, thine enemies, O Lord, for, lo, thine enemies shall perish; all the workers of iniquity shall be scattered.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But my horn shalt thou exalt like the horn of an unicorn: I shall be anointed with fresh oil.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Mine eye also shall see my desire on mine enemies, and mine ears shall hear my desire of the wicked that rise up against me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The righteous shall flourish like the palm tree: he shall grow like a cedar in Lebanon.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Those that be planted in the house of the Lord shall flourish in the courts of our God.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** They shall still bring forth fruit in old age; they shall be fat and flourishing;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** To shew that the Lord is upright: he is my rock, and there is no unrighteousness in him.
