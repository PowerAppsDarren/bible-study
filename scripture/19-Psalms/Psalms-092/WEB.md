# Psalms 92 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** It is a good thing to give thanks to Yahweh, to sing praises to your name, Most High;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** to proclaim your loving kindness in the morning, and your faithfulness every night,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** with the ten-stringed lute, with the harp, and with the melody of the lyre.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For you, Yahweh, have made me glad through your work. I will triumph in the works of your hands.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** How great are your works, Yahweh! Your thoughts are very deep.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** A senseless man doesn’t know, neither does a fool understand this:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** though the wicked spring up as the grass, and all the evildoers flourish, they will be destroyed forever.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But you, Yahweh, are on high forever more.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For, behold, your enemies, Yahweh, for, behold, your enemies shall perish. All the evildoers will be scattered.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But you have exalted my horn like that of the wild ox. I am anointed with fresh oil.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My eye has also seen my enemies. My ears have heard of the wicked enemies who rise up against me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The righteous shall flourish like the palm tree. He will grow like a cedar in Lebanon.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** They are planted in Yahweh’s house. They will flourish in our God’s courts.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** They will still produce fruit in old age. They will be full of sap and green,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** to show that Yahweh is upright. He is my rock, and there is no unrighteousness in him.
