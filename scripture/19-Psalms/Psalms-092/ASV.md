# Psalms 92 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** It is a good thing to give thanks unto Jehovah, And to sing praises unto thy name, O Most High;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** To show forth thy lovingkindness in the morning, And thy faithfulness every night,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** With an instrument of ten strings, and with the psaltery; With a solemn sound upon the harp.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For thou, Jehovah, hast made me glad through thy work: I will triumph in the works of thy hands.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** How great are thy works, O Jehovah! Thy thoughts are very deep.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** A brutish man knoweth not; Neither doth a fool understand this:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** When the wicked spring as the grass, And when all the workers of iniquity do flourish; It is that they shall be destroyed for ever.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But thou, O Jehovah, art on high for evermore.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For, lo, thine enemies, O Jehovah, For, lo, thine enemies shall perish; All the workers of iniquity shall be scattered.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But my horn hast thou exalted like the horn of the wild-ox: I am anointed with fresh oil.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Mine eye also hath seen my desireon mine enemies, Mine ears have heard my desire of the evil-doers that rise up against me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The righteous shall flourish like the palm-tree: He shall grow like a cedar in Lebanon.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** They are planted in the house of Jehovah; They shall flourish in the courts of our God.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** They shall still bring forth fruit in old age; They shall be full of sap and green:

<a id="v15"></a>**15** To show that Jehovah is upright; He is my rock, and there is no unrighteousness in him.
