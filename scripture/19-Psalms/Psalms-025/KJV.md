# Psalms 25 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Unto thee, O Lord, do I lift up my soul.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** O my God, I trust in thee: let me not be ashamed, let not mine enemies triumph over me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yea, let none that wait on thee be ashamed: let them be ashamed which transgress without cause.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Shew me thy ways, O Lord; teach me thy paths.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Lead me in thy truth, and teach me: for thou art the God of my salvation; on thee do I wait all the day.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Remember, O Lord, thy tender mercies and thy lovingkindnesses; for they have been ever of old.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Remember not the sins of my youth, nor my transgressions: according to thy mercy remember thou me for thy goodness’ sake, O Lord.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Good and upright is the Lord: therefore will he teach sinners in the way.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The meek will he guide in judgment: and the meek will he teach his way.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All the paths of the Lord are mercy and truth unto such as keep his covenant and his testimonies.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For thy name’s sake, O Lord, pardon mine iniquity; for it is great.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** What man is he that feareth the Lord? him shall he teach in the way that he shall choose.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** His soul shall dwell at ease; and his seed shall inherit the earth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The secret of the Lord is with them that fear him; and he will shew them his covenant.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Mine eyes are ever toward the Lord; for he shall pluck my feet out of the net.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Turn thee unto me, and have mercy upon me; for I am desolate and afflicted.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The troubles of my heart are enlarged: O bring thou me out of my distresses.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Look upon mine affliction and my pain; and forgive all my sins.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Consider mine enemies; for they are many; and they hate me with cruel hatred.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** O keep my soul, and deliver me: let me not be ashamed; for I put my trust in thee.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Let integrity and uprightness preserve me; for I wait on thee.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Redeem Israel, O God, out of all his troubles.
