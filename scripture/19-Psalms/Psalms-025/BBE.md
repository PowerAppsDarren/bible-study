# Psalms 25 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** To you, O Lord, my soul is lifted up.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** O my God, I have put my faith in you, let me not be shamed; let not my haters be glorying over me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let no servant of yours be put to shame; may those be shamed who are false without cause.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Make your steps clear to me, O Lord; give me knowledge of your ways.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Be my guide and teacher in the true way; for you are the God of my salvation; I am waiting for your word all the day.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** O Lord, keep in mind your pity and your mercies; for they have been from the earliest times.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Do not keep in mind my sins when I was young, or my wrongdoing: let your memory of me be full of mercy, O Lord, because of your righteousness.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Good and upright is the Lord: so he will be the teacher of sinners in the way.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He will be an upright guide to the poor in spirit: he will make his way clear to them.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All the ways of the Lord are mercy and good faith for those who keep his agreement and his witness.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Because of your name, O Lord, let me have forgiveness for my sin, which is very great.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** If a man has the fear of the Lord, the Lord will be his teacher in the way of his pleasure.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** His soul will be full of good things, and his seed will have the earth for its heritage.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The secret of the Lord is with those in whose hearts is the fear of him; he will make his agreement clear to them.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** My eyes are turned to the Lord at all times; for he will take my feet out of the net.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Be turned to me, and have mercy on me; for I am troubled and have no helper.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The troubles of my heart are increased: O take me out of my sorrows.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Give thought to my grief and my pain; and take away all my sins.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** See how those who are against me are increased, for bitter is their hate of me.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** O keep my soul, and take me out of danger: let me not be shamed, for I have put my faith in you.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For my clean and upright ways keep me safe, because my hope is in you.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Give Israel salvation, O God, out of all his troubles.
