# Psalms 25 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Unto thee, O Jehovah, do I lift up my soul.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** O my God, in thee have I trusted, Let me not be put to shame; Let not mine enemies triumph over me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yea, none that wait for thee shall be put to shame: They shall be put to shame that deal treacherously without cause.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Show me thy ways, O Jehovah; Teach me thy paths.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Guide me in thy truth, and teach me; For thou art the God of my salvation; For thee do I wait all the day.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Remember, O Jehovah, thy tender mercies and thy lovingkindness; For they have been ever of old.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Remember not the sins of my youth, nor my transgressions: According to thy lovingkindness remember thou me, For thy goodness’ sake, O Jehovah.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Good and upright is Jehovah: Therefore will he instruct sinners in the way.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The meek will he guide in justice; And the meek will he teach his way.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All the paths of Jehovah are lovingkindness and truth Unto such as keep his covenant and his testimonies.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For thy name’s sake, O Jehovah, Pardon mine iniquity, for it is great.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** What man is he that feareth Jehovah? Him shall he instruct in the way that he shall choose.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** His soul shall dwell at ease; And his seed shall inherit the land.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The friendship of Jehovah is with them that fear him; And he will show them his covenant.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Mine eyes are ever toward Jehovah; For he will pluck my feet out of the net.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Turn thee unto me, and have mercy upon me; For I am desolate and afflicted.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The troubles of my heart are enlarged: Oh bring thou me out of my distresses.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Consider mine affliction and my travail; And forgive all my sins.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Consider mine enemies, for they are many; And they hate me with cruel hatred.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Oh keep my soul, and deliver me: Let me not be put to shame, for I take refuge in thee.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Let integrity and uprightness preserve me, For I wait for thee.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Redeem Israel, O God, Out of all his troubles.
