# Psalms 25 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** To you, Yahweh, do I lift up my soul.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My God, I have trusted in you. Don’t let me be shamed. Don’t let my enemies triumph over me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yes, no one who waits for you shall be shamed. They shall be shamed who deal treacherously without cause.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Show me your ways, Yahweh. Teach me your paths.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Guide me in your truth, and teach me, For you are the God of my salvation, I wait for you all day long.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Yahweh, remember your tender mercies and your loving kindness, for they are from old times.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Don’t remember the sins of my youth, nor my transgressions. Remember me according to your loving kindness, for your goodness’ sake, Yahweh.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Good and upright is Yahweh, therefore he will instruct sinners in the way.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He will guide the humble in justice. He will teach the humble his way.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All the paths of Yahweh are loving kindness and truth to such as keep his covenant and his testimonies.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For your name’s sake, Yahweh, pardon my iniquity, for it is great.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** What man is he who fears Yahweh? He shall instruct him in the way that he shall choose.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** His soul shall dwell at ease. His seed shall inherit the land.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The friendship of Yahweh is with those who fear him. He will show them his covenant.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** My eyes are ever on Yahweh, for he will pluck my feet out of the net.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Turn to me, and have mercy on me, for I am desolate and afflicted.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The troubles of my heart are enlarged. Oh bring me out of my distresses.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Consider my affliction and my travail. Forgive all my sins.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Consider my enemies, for they are many. They hate me with cruel hatred.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Oh keep my soul, and deliver me. Let me not be disappointed, for I take refuge in you.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Let integrity and uprightness preserve me, for I wait for you.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Redeem Israel, God, out all of his troubles.
