# Psalms 21 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The king rejoices in your strength, Yahweh! How greatly he rejoices in your salvation!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You have given him his heart’s desire, and have not withheld the request of his lips. Selah.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For you meet him with the blessings of goodness. You set a crown of fine gold on his head.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He asked life of you, you gave it to him, even length of days forever and ever.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** His glory is great in your salvation. You lay honor and majesty on him.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For you make him most blessed forever. You make him glad with joy in your presence.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For the king trusts in Yahweh. Through the loving kindness of the Most High, he shall not be moved.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Your hand will find out all of your enemies. Your right hand will find out those who hate you.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** You will make them as a fiery furnace in the time of your anger. Yahweh will swallow them up in his wrath. The fire shall devour them.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** You will destroy their descendants from the earth, their posterity from among the children of men.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For they intended evil against you. They plotted evil against you which cannot succeed.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For you will make them turn their back, when you aim drawn bows at their face.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Be exalted, Yahweh, in your strength, so we will sing and praise your power.
