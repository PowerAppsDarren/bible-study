# Psalms 21 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The king shall joy in thy strength, O Lord; and in thy salvation how greatly shall he rejoice!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Thou hast given him his heart’s desire, and hast not withholden the request of his lips. Selah.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For thou preventest him with the blessings of goodness: thou settest a crown of pure gold on his head.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He asked life of thee, and thou gavest it him, even length of days for ever and ever.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** His glory is great in thy salvation: honour and majesty hast thou laid upon him.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For thou hast made him most blessed for ever: thou hast made him exceeding glad with thy countenance.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For the king trusteth in the Lord, and through the mercy of the most High he shall not be moved.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Thine hand shall find out all thine enemies: thy right hand shall find out those that hate thee.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Thou shalt make them as a fiery oven in the time of thine anger: the Lord shall swallow them up in his wrath, and the fire shall devour them.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Their fruit shalt thou destroy from the earth, and their seed from among the children of men.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For they intended evil against thee: they imagined a mischievous device, which they are not able to perform.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Therefore shalt thou make them turn their back, when thou shalt make ready thine arrows upon thy strings against the face of them.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Be thou exalted, Lord, in thine own strength: so will we sing and praise thy power.
