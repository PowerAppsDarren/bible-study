# Psalms 21 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The king shall joy in thy strength, O Jehovah; And in thy salvation how greatly shall he rejoice!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Thou hast given him his heart’s desire, And hast not withholden the request of his lips. [Selah

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For thou meetest him with the blessings of goodness: Thou settest a crown of fine gold on his head.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He asked life of thee, thou gavest it him, Even length of days for ever and ever.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** His glory is great in thy salvation: Honor and majesty dost thou lay upon him.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For thou makest him most blessed for ever: Thou makest him glad with joy in thy presence.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For the king trusteth in Jehovah; And through the lovingkindness of the Most High he shall not be moved.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Thy hand will find out all thine enemies; Thy right hand will find out those that hate thee.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Thou wilt make them as a fiery furnace in the time of thine anger: Jehovah will swallow them up in his wrath, And the fire shall devour them.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Their fruit wilt thou destroy from the earth, And their seed from among the children of men.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For they intended evil against thee; They conceived a device which they are not able to perform.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For thou wilt make them turn their back; Thou wilt make ready with thy bowstrings against their face.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Be thou exalted, O Jehovah, in thy strength: So will we sing and praise thy power.
