# Psalms 138 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will praise thee with my whole heart: before the gods will I sing praise unto thee.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I will worship toward thy holy temple, and praise thy name for thy lovingkindness and for thy truth: for thou hast magnified thy word above all thy name.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** In the day when I cried thou answeredst me, and strengthenedst me with strength in my soul.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** All the kings of the earth shall praise thee, O Lord, when they hear the words of thy mouth.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Yea, they shall sing in the ways of the Lord: for great is the glory of the Lord.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Though the Lord be high, yet hath he respect unto the lowly: but the proud he knoweth afar off.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Though I walk in the midst of trouble, thou wilt revive me: thou shalt stretch forth thine hand against the wrath of mine enemies, and thy right hand shall save me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The Lord will perfect that which concerneth me: thy mercy, O Lord, endureth for ever: forsake not the works of thine own hands.
