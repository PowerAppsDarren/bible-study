# Psalms 138 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will give thee thanks with my whole heart: Before the gods will I sing praises unto thee.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I will worship toward thy holy temple, And give thanks unto thy name for thy lovingkindness and for thy truth: For thou hast magnified thy word above all thy name.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** In the day that I called thou answeredst me, Thou didst encourage me with strength in my soul.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** All the kings of the earth shall give thee thanks, O Jehovah, For they have heard the words of thy mouth.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Yea, they shall sing of the ways of Jehovah; For great is the glory of Jehovah.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For though Jehovah is high, yet hath he respect unto the lowly; But the haughty he knoweth from afar.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Though I walk in the midst of trouble, thou wilt revive me; Thou wilt stretch forth thy hand against the wrath of mine enemies, And thy right hand will save me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Jehovah will perfect that which concerneth me: Thy lovingkindness, O Jehovah, endureth for ever; Forsake not the works of thine own hands.
