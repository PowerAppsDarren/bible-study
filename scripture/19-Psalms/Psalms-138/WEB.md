# Psalms 138 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will give you thanks with my whole heart. Before the gods, I will sing praises to you.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I will bow down toward your holy temple, and give thanks to your Name for your loving kindness and for your truth; for you have exalted your Name and your Word above all.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** In the day that I called, you answered me. You encouraged me with strength in my soul.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** All the kings of the earth will give you thanks, Yahweh, for they have heard the words of your mouth.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Yes, they will sing of the ways of Yahweh; for great is Yahweh’s glory.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For though Yahweh is high, yet he looks after the lowly; but the proud, he knows from afar.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Though I walk in the midst of trouble, you will revive me. You will stretch out your hand against the wrath of my enemies. Your right hand will save me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yahweh will fulfill that which concerns me; your loving kindness, Yahweh, endures forever. Don’t forsake the works of your own hands.
