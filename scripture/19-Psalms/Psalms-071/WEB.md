# Psalms 71 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In you, Yahweh, I take refuge. Never let me be disappointed.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Deliver me in your righteousness, and rescue me. Turn your ear to me, and save me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Be to me a rock of refuge to which I may always go. Give the command to save me, for you are my rock and my fortress.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Rescue me, my God, from the hand of the wicked, from the hand of the unrighteous and cruel man.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For you are my hope, Lord Yahweh; my confidence from my youth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I have relied on you from the womb. You are he who took me out of my mother’s womb. I will always praise you.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I am a marvel to many, but you are my strong refuge.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** My mouth shall be filled with your praise, with your honor all the day.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Don’t reject me in my old age. Don’t forsake me when my strength fails.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For my enemies talk about me. Those who watch for my soul conspire together,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** saying, “God has forsaken him. Pursue and take him, for no one will rescue him.”

<a id="v12"></a>**12** God, don’t be far from me. My God, hurry to help me.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Let my accusers be disappointed and consumed. Let them be covered with disgrace and scorn who want to harm me.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But I will always hope, and will add to all of your praise.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** My mouth will tell about your righteousness, and of your salvation all day, though I don’t know its full measure.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** I will come with the mighty acts of the Lord Yahweh. I will make mention of your righteousness, even of yours alone.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** God, you have taught me from my youth. Until now, I have declared your wondrous works.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Yes, even when I am old and gray-haired, God, don’t forsake me, until I have declared your strength to the next generation, your might to everyone who is to come.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Your righteousness also, God, reaches to the heavens; you have done great things. God, who is like you?

<a id="v20"></a>**20** You, who have shown us many and bitter troubles, you will let me live. You will bring us up again from the depths of the earth.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Increase my honor, and comfort me again.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** I will also praise you with the harp for your faithfulness, my God. I sing praises to you with the lyre, Holy One of Israel.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** My lips shall shout for joy! My soul, which you have redeemed, sings praises to you!

<a id="v24"></a>**24** My tongue will also talk about your righteousness all day long, for they are disappointed, and they are confounded, who want to harm me.
