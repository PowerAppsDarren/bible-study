# Psalms 71 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In thee, O Lord, do I put my trust: let me never be put to confusion.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Deliver me in thy righteousness, and cause me to escape: incline thine ear unto me, and save me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Be thou my strong habitation, whereunto I may continually resort: thou hast given commandment to save me; for thou art my rock and my fortress.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Deliver me, O my God, out of the hand of the wicked, out of the hand of the unrighteous and cruel man.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For thou art my hope, O Lord God: thou art my trust from my youth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** By thee have I been holden up from the womb: thou art he that took me out of my mother’s bowels: my praise shall be continually of thee.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I am as a wonder unto many; but thou art my strong refuge.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let my mouth be filled with thy praise and with thy honour all the day.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Cast me not off in the time of old age; forsake me not when my strength faileth.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For mine enemies speak against me; and they that lay wait for my soul take counsel together,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Saying, God hath forsaken him: persecute and take him; for there is none to deliver him.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** O God, be not far from me: O my God, make haste for my help.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Let them be confounded and consumed that are adversaries to my soul; let them be covered with reproach and dishonour that seek my hurt.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But I will hope continually, and will yet praise thee more and more.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** My mouth shall shew forth thy righteousness and thy salvation all the day; for I know not the numbers thereof.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** I will go in the strength of the Lord God: I will make mention of thy righteousness, even of thine only.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** O God, thou hast taught me from my youth: and hitherto have I declared thy wondrous works.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Now also when I am old and grayheaded, O God, forsake me not; until I have shewed thy strength unto this generation, and thy power to every one that is to come.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Thy righteousness also, O God, is very high, who hast done great things: O God, who is like unto thee!

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Thou, which hast shewed me great and sore troubles, shalt quicken me again, and shalt bring me up again from the depths of the earth.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Thou shalt increase my greatness, and comfort me on every side.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** I will also praise thee with the psaltery, even thy truth, O my God: unto thee will I sing with the harp, O thou Holy One of Israel.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** My lips shall greatly rejoice when I sing unto thee; and my soul, which thou hast redeemed.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** My tongue also shall talk of thy righteousness all the day long: for they are confounded, for they are brought unto shame, that seek my hurt.
