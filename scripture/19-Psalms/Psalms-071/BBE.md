# Psalms 71 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In you, O Lord, have I put my hope; let me never be shamed.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Keep me safe in your righteousness, and come to my help; give ear to my voice, and be my saviour.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Be my strong Rock, the strong place of my salvation; for you are my Rock, and my safe place.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** O my God, take me out of the hand of the sinner, out of the hand of the evil and cruel man.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For you are my hope, O Lord God; I have had faith in you from the time when I was young.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You have been my support from the day of my birth; you took me out of my mother's body; my praise will be ever of you.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I am a wonder to all; but you are my strong tower.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** My mouth will be full of your praise and glory all the day.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Do not give me up when I am old; be my help even when my strength is gone.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For my haters are waiting secretly for me; and those who are watching for my soul are banded together in their evil designs,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Saying, God has given him up; go after him and take him, for he has no helper.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** O God, be not far from me; O my God, come quickly to my help.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Let those who say evil against my soul be overcome and put to shame; let my haters be made low and have no honour.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But I will go on ever hoping, and increasing in all your praise.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** My mouth will make clear your righteousness and your salvation all the day; for they are more than may be measured.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** I will give news of the great acts of the Lord God; my words will be of your righteousness, and of yours only.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** O God, you have been my teacher from the time when I was young; and I have been talking of your works of wonder even till now.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Now when I am old and grey-headed, O God, give me not up; till I have made clear your strength to this generation, and your power to all those to come.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Your righteousness, O God, is very high; you have done great things; O God, who is like you?

<a id="v20"></a>**20** You, who have sent great and bitter troubles on me, will give me life again, lifting me up from the deep waters of the underworld.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** You will make me greater than before, and give me comfort on every side.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** I will give praise to you with instruments of music, O my God, for you are true; I will make songs to you with music, O Holy One of Israel.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Joy will be on my lips when I make melody to you; and in my soul, to which you have given salvation.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** My tongue will be talking of your righteousness all the day; for those whose purpose is to do me evil have been crushed and put to shame.
