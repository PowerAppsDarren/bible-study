# Psalms 51 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Have mercy upon me, O God, according to thy lovingkindness: according unto the multitude of thy tender mercies blot out my transgressions.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Wash me throughly from mine iniquity, and cleanse me from my sin.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For I acknowledge my transgressions: and my sin is ever before me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Against thee, thee only, have I sinned, and done this evil in thy sight: that thou mightest be justified when thou speakest, and be clear when thou judgest.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Behold, I was shapen in iniquity; and in sin did my mother conceive me.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Behold, thou desirest truth in the inward parts: and in the hidden part thou shalt make me to know wisdom.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Purge me with hyssop, and I shall be clean: wash me, and I shall be whiter than snow.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Make me to hear joy and gladness; that the bones which thou hast broken may rejoice.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Hide thy face from my sins, and blot out all mine iniquities.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Create in me a clean heart, O God; and renew a right spirit within me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Cast me not away from thy presence; and take not thy holy spirit from me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Restore unto me the joy of thy salvation; and uphold me with thy free spirit.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Then will I teach transgressors thy ways; and sinners shall be converted unto thee.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Deliver me from bloodguiltiness, O God, thou God of my salvation: and my tongue shall sing aloud of thy righteousness.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** O Lord, open thou my lips; and my mouth shall shew forth thy praise.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For thou desirest not sacrifice; else would I give it: thou delightest not in burnt offering.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit: a broken and a contrite heart, O God, thou wilt not despise.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Do good in thy good pleasure unto Zion: build thou the walls of Jerusalem.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Then shalt thou be pleased with the sacrifices of righteousness, with burnt offering and whole burnt offering: then shall they offer bullocks upon thine altar.
