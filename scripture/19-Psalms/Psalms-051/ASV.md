# Psalms 51 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Have mercy upon me, O God, according to thy lovingkindness: According to the multitude of thy tender mercies blot out my transgressions.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Wash me thoroughly from mine iniquity, And cleanse me from my sin.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For I know my transgressions; And my sin is ever before me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Against thee, thee only, have I sinned, And done that which is evil in thy sight; That thou mayest be justified when thou speakest, And be clear when thou judgest.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Behold, I was brought forth in iniquity; And in sin did my mother conceive me.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Behold, thou desirest truth in the inward parts; And in the hidden part thou wilt make me to know wisdom.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Purify me with hyssop, and I shall be clean: Wash me, and I shall be whiter than snow.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Make me to hear joy and gladness, That the bones which thou hast broken may rejoice.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Hide thy face from my sins, And blot out all mine iniquities.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Create in me a clean heart, O God; And renew a right spirit within me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Cast me not away from thy presence; And take not thy holy Spirit from me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Restore unto me the joy of thy salvation; And uphold me with a willing spirit.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Then will I teach transgressors thy ways; And sinners shall be converted unto thee.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Deliver me from bloodguiltiness, O God, thou God of my salvation; Andmy tongue shall sing aloud of thy righteousness.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** O Lord, open thou my lips; And my mouth shall show forth thy praise.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For thou delightest not in sacrifice; else would I give it: Thou hast no pleasure in burnt-offering.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit: A broken and a contrite heart, O God, thou wilt not despise.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Do good in thy good pleasure unto Zion: Build thou the walls of Jerusalem.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Then wilt thou delight in the sacrifices of righteousness, In burnt-offering and whole burnt-offering: Then will they offer bullocks upon thine altar.
