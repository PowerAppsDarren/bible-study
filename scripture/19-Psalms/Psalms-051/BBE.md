# Psalms 51 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Have pity on me, O God, in your mercy; out of a full heart, take away my sin.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let all my wrongdoing be washed away, and make me clean from evil.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For I am conscious of my error; my sin is ever before me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Against you, you only, have I done wrong, working that which is evil in your eyes; so that your words may be seen to be right, and you may be clear when you are judging.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Truly, I was formed in evil, and in sin did my mother give me birth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Your desire is for what is true in the inner parts: in the secrets of my soul you will give me knowledge of wisdom.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Make me free from sin with hyssop: let me be washed whiter than snow.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Make me full of joy and rapture; so that the bones which have been broken may be glad.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let your face be turned from my wrongdoing, and take away all my sins.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Make a clean heart in me, O God; give me a right spirit again.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Do not put me away from before you, or take your holy spirit from me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Give me back the joy of your salvation; let a free spirit be my support.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Then will I make your ways clear to wrongdoers; and sinners will be turned to you.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Be my saviour from violent death, O God, the God of my salvation; and my tongue will give praise to your righteousness.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** O Lord, let my lips be open, so that my mouth may make clear your praise.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** You have no desire for an offering or I would give it; you have no delight in burned offerings.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The offerings of God are a broken spirit; a broken and sorrowing heart, O God, you will not put from you.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Do good to Zion in your good pleasure, building up the walls of Jerusalem.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Then you will have delight in the offerings of righteousness, in burned offerings and offerings of beasts; then they will make offerings of oxen on your altar.
