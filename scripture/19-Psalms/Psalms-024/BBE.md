# Psalms 24 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The earth is the Lord's, with all its wealth; the world and all the people living in it.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For by him it was based on the seas, and made strong on the deep rivers.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Who may go up into the hill of the Lord? and who may come into his holy place?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He who has clean hands and a true heart; whose desire has not gone out to foolish things, who has not taken a false oath.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He will have blessing from the Lord, and righteousness from the God of his salvation.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** This is the generation of those whose hearts are turned to you, even to your face, O God of Jacob. (Selah.)

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Let your heads be lifted up, O doors; be lifted up, O you eternal doors: that the King of glory may come in.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Who is the King of glory? The Lord of strength and power, the Lord strong in war.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let your heads be lifted up, O doors; let them be lifted up, O you eternal doors: that the King of glory may come in.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Who is the King of glory? The Lord of armies, he is the King of glory. (Selah.)
