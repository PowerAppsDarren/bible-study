# Psalms 24 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The earth is Yahweh’s, with its fullness; the world, and those who dwell therein.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For he has founded it on the seas, and established it on the floods.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Who may ascend to Yahweh’s hill? Who may stand in his holy place?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He who has clean hands and a pure heart; who has not lifted up his soul to falsehood, and has not sworn deceitfully.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He shall receive a blessing from Yahweh, righteousness from the God of his salvation.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** This is the generation of those who seek Him, who seek your face—even Jacob. Selah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Lift up your heads, you gates! Be lifted up, you everlasting doors, and the King of glory will come in.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Who is the King of glory? Yahweh strong and mighty, Yahweh mighty in battle.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Lift up your heads, you gates; yes, lift them up, you everlasting doors, and the King of glory will come in.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Who is this King of glory? Yahweh of Armies is the King of glory! Selah.
