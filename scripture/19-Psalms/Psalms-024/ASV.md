# Psalms 24 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The earth is Jehovah’s, and the fulness thereof; The world, and they that dwell therein.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For he hath founded it upon the seas, And established it upon the floods.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Who shall ascend into the hill of Jehovah? And who shall stand in his holy place?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He that hath clean hands, and a pure heart; Who hath not lifted up his soul unto falsehood, And hath not sworn deceitfully.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He shall receive a blessing from Jehovah, And righteousness from the God of his salvation.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** This is the generation of them that seek after him, That seek thy face, even Jacob. [Selah

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Lift up your heads, O ye gates; And be ye lifted up, ye everlasting doors: And the King of glory will come in.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Who is the King of glory? Jehovah strong and mighty, Jehovah mighty in battle.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Lift up your heads, O ye gates; Yea, lift them up, ye everlasting doors: And the King of glory will come in.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Who is this King of glory? Jehovah of hosts, He is the King of glory. [Selah
