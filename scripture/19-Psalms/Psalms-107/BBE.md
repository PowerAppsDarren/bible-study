# Psalms 107 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O give praise to the Lord, for he is good: for his mercy is unchanging for ever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let those whose cause the Lord has taken up say so, his people whom he has taken out of the hands of their haters;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Making them come together out of all the lands, from the east and from the west, from the north and from the south.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They were wandering in the waste places; they saw no way to a resting-place.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Their souls became feeble for need of food and drink.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Then they sent up their cry to the Lord in their sorrow, and he gave them salvation out of all their troubles;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Guiding them in the right way, so that they might come into the town of their resting-place.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let men give praise to the Lord for his mercy, and for the wonders which he does for the children of men!

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He gives its desire to the unresting soul, so that it is full of good things.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Those who were in the dark, in the black night, in chains of sorrow and iron;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Because they went against the words of God, and gave no thought to the laws of the Most High:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** So that he made their hearts weighted down with grief; they were falling, and had no helper.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Then they sent up their cry to the Lord in their sorrow, and he gave them salvation out of all their troubles.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He took them out of the dark and the black night, and all their chains were broken.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let men give praise to the Lord for his mercy, and for the wonders which he does for the children of men!

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The doors of brass are broken by his arm, and the bands of iron are cut in two.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Foolish men, because of their sins, and because of their wrongdoing, are troubled;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** They are disgusted by all food, and they come near to the doors of death.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Then they send up their cry to the Lord in their sorrow, and he gives them salvation out of all their troubles.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He sent his word and made them well, and kept them safe from the underworld.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Let men give praise to the Lord for his mercy, and for the wonders which he does for the children of men!

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Let them make offerings of praise, giving news of his works with cries of joy.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Those who go down to the sea in ships, who do business in the great waters;

<a id="v24"></a>**24** They see the works of the Lord, and his wonders in the deep.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** For at his word comes up the storm-wind, lifting high the waves.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** The sailors go up to heaven, and down into the deep; their souls are wasted because of their trouble.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** They are turned here and there, rolling like a man who is full of wine; and all their wisdom comes to nothing.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Then they send up their cry to the Lord in their sorrow, and he gives them salvation out of all their troubles.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** He makes the storm into a calm, so that the waves are at peace.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Then they are glad, because the sea is quiet, and he takes them to the harbour of their desire.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Let men give praise to the Lord for his mercy, and for the wonders which he does for the children of men!

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Let them give glory to him in the meeting of the people, and praise among the chiefs.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** He makes rivers into waste places, and springs of water into a dry land;

<a id="v34"></a>**34** He makes a fertile country into a salt waste, because of the sins of those who are living there.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** He makes a waste land into a place of water, and a dry land into water-springs.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** And there he gives the poor a resting-place, so that they may make themselves a town;

<a id="v37"></a>**37** And put seed in the fields and make vine-gardens, to give them fruit.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** He gives them his blessing so that they are increased greatly, and their cattle do not become less.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** And when they are made low, and crushed by trouble and sorrow,

<a id="v40"></a>**40** He puts an end to the pride of kings, and sends them wandering in the waste lands where there is no way.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** But he puts the poor man on high from his troubles, and gives him families like a flock.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** The upright see it and are glad: the mouth of the sinner is stopped.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Let the wise give thought to these things, and see the mercies of the Lord.
