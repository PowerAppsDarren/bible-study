# Psalms 107 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give thanks to Yahweh, for he is good, for his loving kindness endures forever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let the redeemed by Yahweh say so, whom he has redeemed from the hand of the adversary,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And gathered out of the lands, from the east and from the west, from the north and from the south.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They wandered in the wilderness in a desert way. They found no city to live in.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Hungry and thirsty, their soul fainted in them.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Then they cried to Yahweh in their trouble, and he delivered them out of their distresses,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** he led them also by a straight way, that they might go to a city to live in.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let them praise Yahweh for his loving kindness, for his wonderful works to the children of men!

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For he satisfies the longing soul. He fills the hungry soul with good.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Some sat in darkness and in the shadow of death, being bound in affliction and iron,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** because they rebelled against the words of God, and condemned the counsel of the Most High.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Therefore he brought down their heart with labor. They fell down, and there was no one to help.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Then they cried to Yahweh in their trouble, and he saved them out of their distresses.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He brought them out of darkness and the shadow of death, and broke away their chains.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let them praise Yahweh for his loving kindness, for his wonderful works to the children of men!

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For he has broken the gates of brass, and cut through bars of iron.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Fools are afflicted because of their disobedience, and because of their iniquities.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Their soul abhors all kinds of food. They draw near to the gates of death.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Then they cry to Yahweh in their trouble, he saves them out of their distresses.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He sends his word, and heals them, and delivers them from their graves.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Let them praise Yahweh for his loving kindness, for his wonderful works to the children of men!

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Let them offer the sacrifices of thanksgiving, and declare his works with singing.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Those who go down to the sea in ships, who do business in great waters;

<a id="v24"></a>**24** These see Yahweh’s works, and his wonders in the deep.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** For he commands, and raises the stormy wind, which lifts up its waves.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** They mount up to the sky; they go down again to the depths. Their soul melts away because of trouble.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** They reel back and forth, and stagger like a drunken man, and are at their wits’ end.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Then they cry to Yahweh in their trouble, and he brings them out of their distress.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** He makes the storm a calm, so that its waves are still.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Then they are glad because it is calm, so he brings them to their desired haven.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Let them praise Yahweh for his loving kindness, for his wonderful works for the children of men!

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Let them exalt him also in the assembly of the people, and praise him in the seat of the elders.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** He turns rivers into a desert, water springs into a thirsty ground,

<a id="v34"></a>**34** and a fruitful land into a salt waste, for the wickedness of those who dwell in it.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** He turns a desert into a pool of water, and a dry land into water springs.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** There he makes the hungry live, that they may prepare a city to live in,

<a id="v37"></a>**37** sow fields, plant vineyards, and reap the fruits of increase.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** He blesses them also, so that they are multiplied greatly. He doesn’t allow their livestock to decrease.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** Again, they are diminished and bowed down through oppression, trouble, and sorrow.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** He pours contempt on princes, and causes them to wander in a trackless waste.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** Yet he lifts the needy out of their affliction, and increases their families like a flock.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** The upright will see it, and be glad. All the wicked will shut their mouths.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Whoever is wise will pay attention to these things. They will consider the loving kindnesses of Yahweh.
