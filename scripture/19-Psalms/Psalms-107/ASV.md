# Psalms 107 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O give thanks unto Jehovah; for he is good; For his lovingkindness endureth for ever.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let the redeemed of Jehovah sayso, Whom he hath redeemed from the hand of the adversary,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And gathered out of the lands, From the east and from the west, From the north and from the south.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They wandered in the wilderness in a desert way; They found no city of habitation.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Hungry and thirsty, Their soul fainted in them.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Then they cried unto Jehovah in their trouble, And he delivered them out of their distresses,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He led them also by a straight way, That they might go to a city of habitation.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Oh that men would praise Jehovah for his lovingkindness, And for his wonderful works to the children of men!

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For he satisfieth the longing soul, And the hungry soul he filleth with good.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Such as sat in darkness and in the shadow of death, Being bound in affliction and iron,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Because they rebelled against the words of God, And contemned the counsel of the Most High:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Therefore he brought down their heart with labor; They fell down, and there was none to help.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Then they cried unto Jehovah in their trouble, And he saved them out of their distresses.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He brought them out of darkness and the shadow of death, And brake their bonds in sunder.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Oh that men would praise Jehovah for his lovingkindness, And for his wonderful works to the children of men!

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For he hath broken the gates of brass, And cut the bars of iron in sunder.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Fools because of their transgression, And because of their iniquities, are afflicted.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Their soul abhorreth all manner of food; And they draw near unto the gates of death.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Then they cry unto Jehovah in their trouble, And he saveth them out of their distresses.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He sendeth his word, and healeth them, And delivereth them from their destructions.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Oh that men would praise Jehovah for his lovingkindness, And for his wonderful works to the children of men!

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And let them offer the sacrifices of thanksgiving, And declare his works with singing.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** They that go down to the sea in ships, That do business in great waters;

<a id="v24"></a>**24** These see the works of Jehovah, And his wonders in the deep.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** For he commandeth, and raiseth the stormy wind, Which lifteth up the waves thereof.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** They mount up to the heavens, they go down again to the depths: Their soul melteth away because of trouble.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** They reel to and fro, and stagger like a drunken man, And are at their wits’ end.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Then they cry unto Jehovah in their trouble, And he bringeth them out of their distresses.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** He maketh the storm a calm, So that the waves thereof are still.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Then are they glad because they are quiet; So he bringeth them unto their desired haven.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Oh that men would praise Jehovah for his lovingkindness, And for his wonderful works to the children of men!

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Let them exalt him also in the assembly of the people, And praise him in the seat of the elders.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** He turneth rivers into a wilderness, And watersprings into a thirsty ground;

<a id="v34"></a>**34** A fruitful land into a salt desert, For the wickedness of them that dwell therein.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** He turneth a wilderness into a pool of water, And a dry land into watersprings.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** And there he maketh the hungry to dwell, That they may prepare a city of habitation,

<a id="v37"></a>**37** And sow fields, and plant vineyards, And get them fruits of increase.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** He blesseth them also, so that they are multiplied greatly; And he suffereth not their cattle to decrease.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** Again, they are diminished and bowed down Through oppression, trouble, and sorrow.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** He poureth contempt upon princes, And causeth them to wander in the waste, where there is no way.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** Yet setteth he the needy on high from affliction, And maketh him families like a flock.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** The upright shall see it, and be glad; And all iniquity shall stop her mouth.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Whoso is wise will give heed to these things; And they will consider the lovingkindnesses of Jehovah.
