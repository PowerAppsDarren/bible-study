# Psalms 81 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Make a song to God our strength: make a glad cry to the God of Jacob.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Take up the melody, playing on an instrument of music, even on corded instruments.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let the horn be sounded in the time of the new moon, at the full moon, on our holy feast-day:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For this is a rule for Israel, and a law of the God of Jacob.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He gave it to Joseph as a witness, when he went out over the land of Egypt; then the words of a strange tongue were sounding in my ears.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I took the weight from his back; his hands were made free from the baskets.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You gave a cry in your trouble, and I made you free; I gave you an answer in the secret place of the thunder; I put you to the test at the waters of Meribah. (Selah.)

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Give ear, O my people, and I will give you my word, O Israel, if you will only do as I say!

<a id="v9"></a>**9** There is to be no strange god among you; you are not to give worship to any other god.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I am the Lord your God, who took you up from the land of Egypt: let your mouth be open wide, so that I may give you food.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But my people did not give ear to my voice; Israel would have nothing to do with me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** So I gave them up to the desires of their hearts; that they might go after their evil purposes.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** If only my people would give ear to me, walking in my ways!

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I would quickly overcome their haters: my hand would be turned against those who make war on them.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The haters of the Lord would be broken, and their destruction would be eternal.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** I would give them the best grain for food; you would be full of honey from the rock.
