# Psalms 81 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Sing aloud unto God our strength: Make a joyful noise unto the God of Jacob.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Raise a song, and bring hither the timbrel, The pleasant harp with the psaltery.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Blow the trumpet at the new moon, At the full moon, on our feast-day.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For it is a statute for Israel, An ordinance of the God of Jacob.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He appointed it in Joseph for a testimony, When he went out over the land of Egypt, WhereI heard a language that I knew not.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I removed his shoulder from the burden: His hands were freed from the basket.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Thou calledst in trouble, and I delivered thee; I answered thee in the secret place of thunder; I proved thee at the waters of Meribah. [Selah

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Hear, O my people, and I will testify unto thee: O Israel, if thou wouldest hearken unto me!

<a id="v9"></a>**9** There shall no strange god be in thee; Neither shalt thou worship any foreign god.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I am Jehovah thy God, Who brought thee up out of the land of Egypt: Open thy mouth wide, and I will fill it.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But my people hearkened not to my voice; And Israel would none of me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** So I let them go after the stubbornness of their heart, That they might walk in their own counsels.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Oh that my people would hearken unto me, That Israel would walk in my ways!

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I would soon subdue their enemies, And turn my hand against their adversaries.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The haters of Jehovah should submit themselves unto him: But their time should endure for ever.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He would feed them also with the finest of the wheat; And with honey out of the rock would I satisfy thee.
