# Psalms 64 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear my voice, God, in my complaint. Preserve my life from fear of the enemy.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Hide me from the conspiracy of the wicked, from the noisy crowd of the ones doing evil;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** who sharpen their tongue like a sword, and aim their arrows, deadly words,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** to shoot innocent men from ambushes. They shoot at him suddenly and fearlessly.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They encourage themselves in evil plans. They talk about laying snares secretly. They say, “Who will see them?”

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They plot injustice, saying, “We have made a perfect plan!” Surely man’s mind and heart are cunning.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But God will shoot at them. They will be suddenly struck down with an arrow.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Their own tongues shall ruin them. All who see them will shake their heads.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** All mankind shall be afraid. They shall declare the work of God, and shall wisely ponder what he has done.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The righteous shall be glad in Yahweh, and shall take refuge in him. All the upright in heart shall praise him!
