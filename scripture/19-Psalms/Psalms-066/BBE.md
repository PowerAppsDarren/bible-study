# Psalms 66 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Send up a glad cry to God, all the earth:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Make a song in honour of his name: give praise and glory to him.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Say to God, How greatly to be feared are your works! because of your great power your haters are forced to put themselves under your feet.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Let all the earth give you worship, and make songs to you; let them make songs to your name. (Selah.)

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Come and see the works of God: he is to be feared in all he does to the children of men.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The sea was turned into dry land: they went through the river on foot: there did we have joy in him.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He is ruling in power for ever; his eyes are watching the nations: may his haters have no strength against him. (Selah.)

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Give blessings to our God, O you peoples, let the voice of his praise be loud;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Because he gives us life, and has not let our feet be moved.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For you, O God, have put us to the test: testing us by fire like silver.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** You let us be put in prison; chains were put on our legs.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** You let men go driving over our heads; we went through fire and through water; but you took us out into a wide place.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I will come into your house with burned offerings, I will make payment of my debt to you,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Keeping the word which came from my lips, and which my mouth said, when I was in trouble.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I will give you burned offerings of fat beasts, and the smoke of sheep; I will make offerings of oxen and goats. (Selah.)

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Come, give ear to me, all you God-fearing men, so that I may make clear to you what he has done for my soul.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** My voice went up to him, and I was lifted up from the underworld.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** I said in my heart, The Lord will not give ear to me:

<a id="v19"></a>**19** But truly God's ear has been open; he has give attention to the voice of my prayer.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Praise be to God who has not taken away his good faith and his mercy from me.
