# Psalms 66 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Make a joyful noise unto God, all ye lands:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Sing forth the honour of his name: make his praise glorious.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Say unto God, How terrible art thou in thy works! through the greatness of thy power shall thine enemies submit themselves unto thee.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** All the earth shall worship thee, and shall sing unto thee; they shall sing to thy name. Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Come and see the works of God: he is terrible in his doing toward the children of men.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He turned the sea into dry land: they went through the flood on foot: there did we rejoice in him.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He ruleth by his power for ever; his eyes behold the nations: let not the rebellious exalt themselves. Selah.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** O bless our God, ye people, and make the voice of his praise to be heard:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Which holdeth our soul in life, and suffereth not our feet to be moved.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For thou, O God, hast proved us: thou hast tried us, as silver is tried.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Thou broughtest us into the net; thou laidst affliction upon our loins.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Thou hast caused men to ride over our heads; we went through fire and through water: but thou broughtest us out into a wealthy place.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I will go into thy house with burnt offerings: I will pay thee my vows,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Which my lips have uttered, and my mouth hath spoken, when I was in trouble.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I will offer unto thee burnt sacrifices of fatlings, with the incense of rams; I will offer bullocks with goats. Selah.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Come and hear, all ye that fear God, and I will declare what he hath done for my soul.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I cried unto him with my mouth, and he was extolled with my tongue.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** If I regard iniquity in my heart, the Lord will not hear me:

<a id="v19"></a>**19** But verily God hath heard me; he hath attended to the voice of my prayer.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Blessed be God, which hath not turned away my prayer, nor his mercy from me.
