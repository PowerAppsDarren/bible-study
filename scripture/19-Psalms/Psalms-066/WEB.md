# Psalms 66 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Make a joyful shout to God, all the earth!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Sing to the glory of his name! Offer glory and praise!

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Tell God, “How awesome are your deeds! Through the greatness of your power, your enemies submit themselves to you.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** All the earth will worship you, and will sing to you; they will sing to your name.” Selah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Come, and see God’s deeds— awesome work on behalf of the children of men.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He turned the sea into dry land. They went through the river on foot. There, we rejoiced in him.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He rules by his might forever. His eyes watch the nations. Don’t let the rebellious rise up against him. Selah.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Praise our God, you peoples! Make the sound of his praise heard,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** who preserves our life among the living, and doesn’t allow our feet to be moved.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For you, God, have tested us. You have refined us, as silver is refined.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** You brought us into prison. You laid a burden on our backs.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** You allowed men to ride over our heads. We went through fire and through water, but you brought us to the place of abundance.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I will come into your temple with burnt offerings. I will pay my vows to you,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** which my lips promised,  and my mouth spoke, when I was in distress.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I will offer to you burnt offerings of fat animals, with the offering of rams, I will offer bulls with goats. Selah.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Come, and hear, all you who fear God. I will declare what he has done for my soul.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I cried to him with my mouth. He was extolled with my tongue.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** If I cherished sin in my heart, the Lord wouldn’t have listened.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** But most certainly, God has listened. He has heard the voice of my prayer.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Blessed be God, who has not turned away my prayer, nor his loving kindness from me.
