# Psalms 82 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God is in the meeting-place of God; he is judging among the gods.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How long will you go on judging falsely, having respect for the persons of evil-doers? (Selah.)

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Give ear to the cause of the poor and the children without fathers; let those who are troubled and in need have their rights.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Be the saviour of the poor and those who have nothing: take them out of the hand of the evil-doers.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They have no knowledge or sense; they go about in the dark: all the bases of the earth are moved.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I said, You are gods; all of you are the sons of the Most High:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But you will come to death like men, falling like one of the rulers of the earth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Up! O God, come as judge of the earth; for all the nations are your heritage.
