# Psalms 82 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God presides in the great assembly. He judges among the gods.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** “How long will you judge unjustly, and show partiality to the wicked?” Selah.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** “Defend the weak, the poor, and the fatherless. Maintain the rights of the poor and oppressed.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Rescue the weak and needy. Deliver them out of the hand of the wicked.”

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They don’t know, neither do they understand. They walk back and forth in darkness. All the foundations of the earth are shaken.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I said, “You are gods, all of you are sons of the Most High.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Nevertheless you shall die like men, and fall like one of the rulers.”

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Arise, God, judge the earth, for you inherit all of the nations.
