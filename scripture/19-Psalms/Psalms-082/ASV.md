# Psalms 82 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God standeth in the congregation of God; He judgeth among the gods.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How long will ye judge unjustly, And respect the persons of the wicked? [Selah

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Judge the poor and fatherless: Do justice to the afflicted and destitute.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Rescue the poor and needy: Deliver them out of the hand of the wicked.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They know not, neither do they understand; They walk to and fro in darkness: All the foundations of the earth are shaken.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I said, Ye are gods, And all of you sons of the Most High.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Nevertheless ye shall die like men, And fall like one of the princes.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Arise, O God, judge the earth; For thou shalt inherit all the nations.
