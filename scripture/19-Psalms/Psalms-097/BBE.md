# Psalms 97 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The Lord is King, let the earth have joy; let all the sea-lands be glad.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Dark clouds are round him; his kingdom is based on righteousness and right judging.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Fire goes before him, burning up all those who are against him round about.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** His bright flames give light to the world; the earth saw it with fear.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The mountains became like wax at the coming of the Lord, at the coming of the Lord of all the earth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The heavens gave out the news of his righteousness, and all the people saw his glory.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Shamed be all those who give worship to images, and take pride in false gods; give him worship, all you gods.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Zion gave ear and was glad; and the daughters of Judah were full of joy, because of your decisions, O Lord.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For you, Lord, are most high over the earth; you are lifted up over all other gods.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** You who are lovers of the Lord, be haters of evil; he keeps the souls of his saints; he takes them out of the hand of sinners.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Light is shining on the lovers of righteousness, and for the upright in heart there is joy.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Be glad in the Lord, you upright men; praising the memory of his holy name.
