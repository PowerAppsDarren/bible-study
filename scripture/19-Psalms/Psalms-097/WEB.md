# Psalms 97 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh reigns! Let the earth rejoice! Let the multitude of islands be glad!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Clouds and darkness are around him. Righteousness and justice are the foundation of his throne.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** A fire goes before him, and burns up his adversaries on every side.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** His lightning lights up the world. The earth sees, and trembles.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The mountains melt like wax at the presence of Yahweh, at the presence of the Lord of the whole earth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The heavens declare his righteousness. All the peoples have seen his glory.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Let all them be shamed who serve engraved images, who boast in their idols. Worship him, all you gods!

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Zion heard and was glad. The daughters of Judah rejoiced, because of your judgments, Yahweh.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For you, Yahweh, are most high above all the earth. You are exalted far above all gods.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** You who love Yahweh, hate evil. He preserves the souls of his saints. He delivers them out of the hand of the wicked.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Light is sown for the righteous, and gladness for the upright in heart.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Be glad in Yahweh, you righteous people! Give thanks to his holy Name.
