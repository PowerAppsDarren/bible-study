# Psalms 99 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The Lord is King; let the peoples be in fear: his seat is on the winged ones; let the earth be moved.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The Lord is great in Zion; he is high over all the nations.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let them give praise to your name, for it is great and to be feared; holy is he.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The king's power is used for righteousness; you give true decisions, judging rightly in the land of Jacob.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Give high honour to the Lord our God, worshipping at his feet; holy is he.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Moses and Aaron among his priests, and Samuel among those who gave honour to his name; they made prayers to the Lord, and he gave answers to them.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** His voice came to them from the pillar of cloud; they kept his witness, and the law which he gave them.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** You gave them an answer, O Lord our God; you took away their sin, though you gave them punishment for their wrongdoing.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Give high honour to the Lord our God, worshipping with your faces turned to his holy hill; for the Lord our God is holy.
