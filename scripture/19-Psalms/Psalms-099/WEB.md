# Psalms 99 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh reigns! Let the peoples tremble. He sits enthroned among the cherubim. Let the earth be moved.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Yahweh is great in Zion. He is high above all the peoples.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let them praise your great and awesome name. He is Holy!

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The King’s strength also loves justice. You do establish equity. You execute justice and righteousness in Jacob.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Exalt Yahweh our God. Worship at his footstool. He is Holy!

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Moses and Aaron were among his priests, Samuel among those who call on his name; they called on Yahweh, and he answered them.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He spoke to them in the pillar of cloud. They kept his testimonies, the statute that he gave them.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** You answered them, Yahweh our God. You are a God who forgave them, although you took vengeance for their doings.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Exalt Yahweh, our God. Worship at his holy hill, for Yahweh, our God, is holy!
