# Psalms 99 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The Lord reigneth; let the people tremble: he sitteth between the cherubims; let the earth be moved.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The Lord is great in Zion; and he is high above all the people.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let them praise thy great and terrible name; for it is holy.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The king’s strength also loveth judgment; thou dost establish equity, thou executest judgment and righteousness in Jacob.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Exalt ye the Lord our God, and worship at his footstool; for he is holy.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Moses and Aaron among his priests, and Samuel among them that call upon his name; they called upon the Lord, and he answered them.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He spake unto them in the cloudy pillar: they kept his testimonies, and the ordinance that he gave them.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Thou answeredst them, O Lord our God: thou wast a God that forgavest them, though thou tookest vengeance of their inventions.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Exalt the Lord our God, and worship at his holy hill; for the Lord our God is holy.
