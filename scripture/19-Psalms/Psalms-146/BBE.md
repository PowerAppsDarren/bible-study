# Psalms 146 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Let the Lord be praised. Give praise to the Lord, O my soul.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** While I have breath I will give praise to the Lord: I will make melody to my God while I have my being.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Put not your faith in rulers, or in the son of man, in whom there is no salvation.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Man's breath goes out, he is turned back again to dust; in that day all his purposes come to an end.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Happy is the man who has the God of Jacob for his help, whose hope is in the Lord his God:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Who made heaven and earth, the sea, and all things in them; who keeps faith for ever:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Who gives their rights to those who are crushed down; and gives food to those who are in need of it: the Lord makes the prisoners free;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The Lord makes open the eyes of the blind; the Lord is the lifter up of those who are bent down; the Lord is a lover of the upright;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The Lord takes care of those who are in a strange land; he gives help to the widow and to the child who has no father; but he sends destruction on the way of sinners.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The Lord will be King for ever; your God, O Zion, will be King through all generations. Praise be to the Lord.
