# Psalms 146 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye Jehovah. Praise Jehovah, O my soul.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** While I live will I praise Jehovah: I will sing praises unto my God while I have any being.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Put not your trust in princes, Nor in the son of man, in whom there is no help.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** His breath goeth forth, he returneth to his earth; In that very day his thoughts perish.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Happy is he that hath the God of Jacob for his help, Whose hope is in Jehovah his God:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Who made heaven and earth, The sea, and all that in them is; Who keepeth truth for ever;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Who executeth justice for the oppressed; Who giveth food to the hungry. Jehovah looseth the prisoners;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Jehovah openeth the eyes of the blind; Jehovah raiseth up them that are bowed down; Jehovah loveth the righteous;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Jehovah preserveth the sojourners; He upholdeth the fatherless and widow; But the way of the wicked he turneth upside down.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Jehovah will reign for ever, Thy God, O Zion, unto all generations. Praise ye Jehovah.
