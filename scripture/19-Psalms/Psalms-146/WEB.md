# Psalms 146 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise Yah! Praise Yahweh, my soul.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** While I live, I will praise Yahweh. I will sing praises to my God as long as I exist.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Don’t put your trust in princes, each a son of man in whom there is no help.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** His spirit departs, and he returns to the earth. In that very day, his thoughts perish.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Happy is he who has the God of Jacob for his help, whose hope is in Yahweh, his God:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** who made heaven and earth, the sea, and all that is in them; who keeps truth forever;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** who executes justice for the oppressed; who gives food to the hungry. Yahweh frees the prisoners.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yahweh opens the eyes of the blind. Yahweh raises up those who are bowed down. Yahweh loves the righteous.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Yahweh preserves the foreigners. He upholds the fatherless and widow, but the way of the wicked he turns upside down.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Yahweh will reign forever; your God, O Zion, to all generations. Praise Yah!
