# Psalms 87 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** This house is resting on the holy mountain.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The Lord has more love for the doors of Zion than for all the tents of Jacob.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Noble things are said of you, O town of God. (Selah.)

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Rahab and Babylon will be named among those who have knowledge of me; see, Philistia and Tyre, with Ethiopia; this man had his birth there.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And of Zion it will be said, This or that man had his birth there; and the Most High will make her strong.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The Lord will keep in mind, when he is writing the records of the people, that this man had his birth there. (Selah.)

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The players on instruments will be there, and the dancers will say, All my springs are in you.
