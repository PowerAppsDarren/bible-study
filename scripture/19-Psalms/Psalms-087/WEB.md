# Psalms 87 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** His foundation is in the holy mountains.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Yahweh loves the gates of Zion more than all the dwellings of Jacob.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Glorious things are spoken about you, city of God. Selah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will record Rahab and Babylon among those who acknowledge me. Behold, Philistia, Tyre, and also Ethiopia: “This one was born there.”

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Yes, of Zion it will be said, “This one and that one was born in her”; the Most High himself will establish her.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Yahweh will count, when he writes up the peoples, “This one was born there.” Selah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Those who sing as well as those who dance say, “All my springs are in you.”
