# Psalms 87 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** His foundation is in the holy mountains.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Jehovah loveth the gates of Zion More than all the dwellings of Jacob.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Glorious things are spoken of thee, O city of God. [Selah

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will make mention of Rahab and Babylon as among them that know me: Behold, Philistia, and Tyre, with Ethiopia: This one was born there.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Yea, of Zion it shall be said, This one and that one was born in her; And the Most High himself will establish her.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Jehovah will count, when he writeth up the peoples, This one was born there. [Selah

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They that sing as well as they that dance shall say, All my fountains are in thee.
