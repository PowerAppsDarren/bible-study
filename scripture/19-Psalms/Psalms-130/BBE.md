# Psalms 130 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Out of the deep have I sent up my cry to you, O Lord.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Lord, let my voice come before you: let your ears be awake to the voice of my prayer.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** O Jah, if you took note of every sin, who would go free?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But there is forgiveness with you, so that you may be feared.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I am waiting for the Lord, my soul is waiting for him, and my hope is in his word.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** My soul is watching for the Lord more than those who are watching for the morning; yes, more than the watchers for the morning.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** O Israel, have hope in the Lord; for with the Lord is mercy and full salvation.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And he will make Israel free from all his sins.
