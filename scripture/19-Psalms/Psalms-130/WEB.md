# Psalms 130 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Out of the depths I have cried to you, Yahweh.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Lord, hear my voice. Let your ears be attentive to the voice of my petitions.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** If you, Yah, kept a record of sins, Lord, who could stand?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But there is forgiveness with you, therefore you are feared.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I wait for Yahweh. My soul waits. I hope in his word.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** My soul longs for the Lord more than watchmen long for the morning; more than watchmen for the morning.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Israel, hope in Yahweh, for with Yahweh there is loving kindness. With him is abundant redemption.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He will redeem Israel from all their sins.
