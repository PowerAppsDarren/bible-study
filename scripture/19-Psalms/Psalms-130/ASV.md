# Psalms 130 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Out of the depths have I cried unto thee, O Jehovah.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Lord, hear my voice: Let thine ears be attentive To the voice of my supplications.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** If thou, Jehovah, shouldest mark iniquities, O Lord, who could stand?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But there is forgiveness with thee, That thou mayest be feared.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I wait for Jehovah, my soul doth wait, And in his word do I hope.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** My soul waiteth for the Lord  More than watchmen wait for the morning; Yea, more thanwatchmen for the morning.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** O Israel, hope in Jehovah; For with Jehovah there is lovingkindness, And with him is plenteous redemption.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And he will redeem Israel  From all his iniquities.
