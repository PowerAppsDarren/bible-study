# Psalms 57 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Have mercy on me, O God, have mercy on me; for the hope of my soul is in you: I will keep myself safely under the shade of your wings, till these troubles are past.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I will send up my cry to the Most High God; to God who does all things for me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He will send from heaven, and take me from the power of him whose desire is for my destruction. God will send out his mercy and his good faith.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** My soul is among lions; I am stretched out among those who are on fire, even the sons of men, whose teeth are spears and arrows, and whose tongue is a sharp sword.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** O God, be lifted up higher than the heavens; let your glory be over all the earth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They have made ready a net for my steps; my soul is bent down; they have made a great hole before me, and have gone down into it themselves. (Selah.)

<a id="v7"></a>**7** My heart is fixed, O God, my heart is fixed; I will make songs, and give praise.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** You are my glory; let the instruments of music be awake; I myself will be awake with the dawn.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will give you praise, O Lord, among the peoples; I will make songs to you among the nations.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For your mercy is great, stretching up to the heavens, and your righteousness goes up to the clouds.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Be lifted up, O God, higher than the heavens, let your glory be over all the earth.
