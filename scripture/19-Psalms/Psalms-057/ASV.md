# Psalms 57 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Be merciful unto me, O God, be merciful unto me; For my soul taketh refuge in thee: Yea, in the shadow of thy wings will I take refuge, Until these calamities be overpast.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I will cry unto God Most High, Unto God that performeth all things for me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He will send from heaven, and save me, Whenhe that would swallow me up reproacheth; [Selah God will send forth his lovingkindness and his truth.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** My soul is among lions; I lie among them that are set on fire, Even the sons of men, whose teeth are spears and arrows, And their tongue a sharp sword.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens; Let thy glory be above all the earth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They have prepared a net for my steps; My soul is bowed down: They have digged a pit before me; They are fallen into the midst thereof themselves. [Selah

<a id="v7"></a>**7** My heart is fixed, O God, my heart is fixed: I will sing, yea, I will sing praises.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Awake up, my glory; awake, psaltery and harp: I myself will awake right early.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will give thanks unto thee, O Lord, among the peoples: I will sing praises unto thee among the nations.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For thy lovingkindness is great unto the heavens, And thy truth unto the skies.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens; Let thy glory be above all the earth.
