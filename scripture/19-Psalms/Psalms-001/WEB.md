# Psalms 1 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Blessed is the man who doesn’t walk in the counsel of the wicked, nor stand on the path of sinners, nor sit in the seat of scoffers;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** but his delight is in Yahweh’s law. On his law he meditates day and night.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He will be like a tree planted by the streams of water, that produces its fruit in its season, whose leaf also does not wither. Whatever he does shall prosper.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The wicked are not so, but are like the chaff which the wind drives away.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Therefore the wicked shall not stand in the judgment, nor sinners in the congregation of the righteous.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For Yahweh knows the way of the righteous, but the way of the wicked shall perish.
