# Psalms 1 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Happy is the man who does not go in the company of sinners, or take his place in the way of evil-doers, or in the seat of those who do not give honour to the Lord.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** But whose delight is in the law of the Lord, and whose mind is on his law day and night.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He will be like a tree planted by the rivers of water, which gives its fruit at the right time, whose leaves will ever be green; and he will do well in all his undertakings.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The evil-doers are not so; but are like the dust from the grain, which the wind takes away.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For this cause there will be no mercy for sinners when they are judged, and the evil-doers will have no place among the upright,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Because the Lord sees the way of the upright, but the end of the sinner is destruction.
