# Psalms 11 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In the Lord put I my faith; how will you say to my soul, Go in flight like a bird to the mountain?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** See, the bows of the evil-doers are bent, they make ready their arrows on the cord, so that they may send them secretly against the upright in heart.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** If the bases are broken down, what is the upright man to do?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The Lord is in his holy Temple, the Lord's seat is in heaven; his eyes are watching and testing the children of men.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The Lord puts the upright and the sinner to the test, but he has hate in his soul for the lover of violent acts.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** On the evil-doer he will send down fire and flames, and a burning wind; with these will their cup be full.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For the Lord is upright; he is a lover of righteousness: the upright will see his face.
