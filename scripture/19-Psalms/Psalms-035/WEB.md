# Psalms 35 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Contend, Yahweh, with those who contend with me. Fight against those who fight against me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Take hold of shield and buckler, and stand up for my help.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Brandish the spear and block those who pursue me. Tell my soul, “I am your salvation.”

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Let those who seek after my soul be disappointed and brought to dishonor. Let those who plot my ruin be turned back and confounded.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let them be as chaff before the wind, Yahweh’s angel driving them on.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let their way be dark and slippery, Yahweh’s angel pursuing them.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For without cause they have hidden their net in a pit for me. Without cause they have dug a pit for my soul.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let destruction come on him unawares. Let his net that he has hidden catch himself. Let him fall into that destruction.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** My soul shall be joyful in Yahweh. It shall rejoice in his salvation.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All my bones shall say, “Yahweh, who is like you, who delivers the poor from him who is too strong for him; yes, the poor and the needy from him who robs him?”

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Unrighteous witnesses rise up. They ask me about things that I don’t know about.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They reward me evil for good, to the bereaving of my soul.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But as for me, when they were sick, my clothing was sackcloth. I afflicted my soul with fasting. My prayer returned into my own bosom.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I behaved myself as though it had been my friend or my brother. I bowed down mourning, as one who mourns his mother.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But in my adversity, they rejoiced, and gathered themselves together. The attackers gathered themselves together against me, and I didn’t know it. They tore at me, and didn’t cease.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Like the profane mockers in feasts, they gnashed their teeth at me.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Lord, how long will you look on? Rescue my soul from their destruction, my precious life from the lions.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** I will give you thanks in the great assembly. I will praise you among many people.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Don’t let those who are my enemies wrongfully rejoice over me; neither let those who hate me without a cause wink their eyes.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** For they don’t speak peace, but they devise deceitful words against those who are quiet in the land.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Yes, they opened their mouth wide against me. They said, “Aha! Aha! Our eye has seen it!”

<a id="v22"></a>**22** You have seen it, Yahweh. Don’t keep silent. Lord, don’t be far from me.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Wake up! Rise up to defend me, my God! My Lord, contend for me!

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Vindicate me, Yahweh my God, according to your righteousness. Don’t let them gloat over me.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Don’t let them say in their heart, “Aha! That’s the way we want it!” Don’t let them say, “We have swallowed him up!”

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Let them be disappointed and confounded together who rejoice at my calamity. Let them be clothed with shame and dishonor who magnify themselves against me.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Let them shout for joy and be glad, who favor my righteous cause. Yes, let them say continually, “Yahweh be magnified, who has pleasure in the prosperity of his servant!”

<a id="v28"></a>**28** My tongue shall talk about your righteousness and about your praise all day long.
