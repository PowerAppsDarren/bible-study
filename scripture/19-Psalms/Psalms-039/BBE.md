# Psalms 39 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I said, I will give attention to my ways, so that my tongue may do no wrong; I will keep my mouth under control, while the sinner is before me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I made no sound, I said no word, even of good; and I was moved with sorrow.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** My heart was burning in my breast; while I was deep in thought the fire was lighted; then I said with my tongue,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Lord, give me knowledge of my end, and of the measure of my days, so that I may see how feeble I am.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You have made my days no longer than a hand's measure; and my years are nothing in your eyes; truly, every man is but a breath. (Selah.)

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Truly, every man goes on his way like an image; he is troubled for no purpose: he makes a great store of wealth, and has no knowledge of who will get it.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And now, Lord, what am I waiting for? my hope is in you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Make me free from all my sins; do not let me be shamed by the man of evil behaviour.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I was quiet, and kept my mouth shut; because you had done it.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** No longer let your hand be hard on me; I am wasted by the blows of your hand.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** By the weight of your wrath against man's sin, the glory of his form is wasted away; truly every man is but a breath. (Selah.)

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Let my prayer come to your ears, O Lord, and give attention to my cry, make an answer to my weeping: for my time here is short before you, and in a little time I will be gone, like all my fathers.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Let your wrath be turned away from me, so that I may be comforted, before I go away from here, and become nothing.
