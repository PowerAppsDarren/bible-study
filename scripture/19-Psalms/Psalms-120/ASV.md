# Psalms 120 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In my distress I cried unto Jehovah, And he answered me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Deliver my soul, O Jehovah, from lying lips, Andfrom a deceitful tongue.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** What shall be given unto thee, and what shall be done more unto thee, Thou deceitful tongue?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Sharp arrows of the mighty, With coals of juniper.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Woe is me, that I sojourn in Meshech, That I dwell among the tents of Kedar!

<a id="v6"></a>**6** My soul hath long had her dwelling With him that hateth peace.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I amforpeace: But when I speak, they are for war.
