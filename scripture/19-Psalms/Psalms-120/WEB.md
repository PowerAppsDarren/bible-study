# Psalms 120 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In my distress, I cried to Yahweh. He answered me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Deliver my soul, Yahweh, from lying lips, from a deceitful tongue.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** What will be given to you, and what will be done more to you, you deceitful tongue?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Sharp arrows of the mighty, with coals of juniper.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Woe is me, that I live in Meshech, that I dwell among the tents of Kedar!

<a id="v6"></a>**6** My soul has had her dwelling too long with him who hates peace.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I am for peace, but when I speak, they are for war.
