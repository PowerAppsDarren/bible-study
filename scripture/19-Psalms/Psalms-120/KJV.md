# Psalms 120 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In my distress I cried unto the Lord, and he heard me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Deliver my soul, O Lord, from lying lips, and from a deceitful tongue.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** What shall be given unto thee? or what shall be done unto thee, thou false tongue?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Sharp arrows of the mighty, with coals of juniper.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Woe is me, that I sojourn in Mesech, that I dwell in the tents of Kedar!

<a id="v6"></a>**6** My soul hath long dwelt with him that hateth peace.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I am for peace: but when I speak, they are for war.
