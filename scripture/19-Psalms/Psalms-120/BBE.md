# Psalms 120 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In my trouble my cry went up to the Lord, and he gave me an answer.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** O Lord, be the saviour of my soul from false lips, and from the tongue of deceit.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** What punishment will he give you? what more will he do to you, you false tongue?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Sharp arrows of the strong, and burning fire.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Sorrow is mine because I am strange in Meshech, and living in the tents of Kedar.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** My soul has long been living with the haters of peace.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I am for peace: but when I say so, they are for war.
