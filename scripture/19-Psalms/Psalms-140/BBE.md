# Psalms 140 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Lord, take me out of the power of the evil man; keep me safe from the violent man:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For their hearts are full of evil designs; and they are ever making ready causes of war.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Their tongues are sharp like the tongue of a snake; the poison of snakes is under their lips. (Selah.)

<a id="v4"></a>**4** O Lord, take me out of the hands of sinners; keep me safe from the violent man: for they are designing my downfall.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The men of pride have put secret cords for my feet; stretching nets in my way, so that they may take me with their tricks. (Selah.)

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I have said to the Lord, You are my God: give ear, O Lord, to the voice of my prayer.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** O Lord God, the strength of my salvation, you have been a cover over my head in the day of the fight.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** O Lord, give not the wrongdoer his desire; give him no help in his evil designs, or he may be uplifted in pride. (Selah.)

<a id="v9"></a>**9** As for those who come round me, let their heads be covered by the evil of their lips.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Let burning flames come down on them: let them be put into the fire, and into deep waters, so that they may not get up again.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let not a man of evil tongue be safe on earth: let destruction overtake the violent man with blow on blow.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I am certain that the Lord will take care of the cause of the poor, and of the rights of those who are troubled.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Truly, the upright will give praise to your name: the holy will have a place in your house.
