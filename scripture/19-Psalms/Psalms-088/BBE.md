# Psalms 88 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Lord, God of my salvation, I have been crying to you for help by day and by night:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let my prayer come before you; give ear to my cry:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For my soul is full of evils, and my life has come near to the underworld.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I am numbered among those who go down into the earth; I have become like a man for whom there is no help:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My soul is among the dead, like those in the underworld, to whom you give no more thought; for they are cut off from your care.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You have put me in the lowest deep, even in dark places.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The weight of your wrath is crushing me, all your waves have overcome me. (Selah.)

<a id="v8"></a>**8** You have sent my friends far away from me; you have made me a disgusting thing in their eyes: I am shut up, and not able to come out.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** My eyes are wasting away because of my trouble: Lord, my cry has gone up to you every day, my hands are stretched out to you.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Will you do works of wonder for the dead? will the shades come back to give you praise? (Selah.)

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Will the story of your mercy be given in the house of the dead? will news of your faith come to the place of destruction?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** May there be knowledge of your wonders in the dark? or of your righteousness where memory is dead?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But to you did I send up my cry, O Lord; in the morning my prayer came before you.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Lord, why have you sent away my soul? why is your face covered from me?

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I have been troubled and in fear of death from the time when I was young; your wrath is hard on me, and I have no strength.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The heat of your wrath has gone over me; I am broken by your cruel punishments.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** They are round me all the day like water; they have made a circle about me.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** You have sent my friends and lovers far from me; I am gone from the memory of those who are dear to me.
