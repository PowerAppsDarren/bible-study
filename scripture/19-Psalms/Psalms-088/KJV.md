# Psalms 88 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Lord God of my salvation, I have cried day and night before thee:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let my prayer come before thee: incline thine ear unto my cry;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For my soul is full of troubles: and my life draweth nigh unto the grave.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I am counted with them that go down into the pit: I am as a man that hath no strength:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Free among the dead, like the slain that lie in the grave, whom thou rememberest no more: and they are cut off from thy hand.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Thou hast laid me in the lowest pit, in darkness, in the deeps.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Thy wrath lieth hard upon me, and thou hast afflicted me with all thy waves. Selah.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Thou hast put away mine acquaintance far from me; thou hast made me an abomination unto them: I am shut up, and I cannot come forth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Mine eye mourneth by reason of affliction: Lord, I have called daily upon thee, I have stretched out my hands unto thee.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Wilt thou shew wonders to the dead? shall the dead arise and praise thee? Selah.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Shall thy lovingkindness be declared in the grave? or thy faithfulness in destruction?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Shall thy wonders be known in the dark? and thy righteousness in the land of forgetfulness?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But unto thee have I cried, O Lord; and in the morning shall my prayer prevent thee.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Lord, why castest thou off my soul? why hidest thou thy face from me?

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I am afflicted and ready to die from my youth up: while I suffer thy terrors I am distracted.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Thy fierce wrath goeth over me; thy terrors have cut me off.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** They came round about me daily like water; they compassed me about together.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Lover and friend hast thou put far from me, and mine acquaintance into darkness.
