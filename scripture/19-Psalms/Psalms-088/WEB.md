# Psalms 88 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh, the God of my salvation, I have cried day and night before you.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let my prayer enter into your presence. Turn your ear to my cry.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For my soul is full of troubles. My life draws near to Sheol.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I am counted among those who go down into the pit. I am like a man who has no help,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** set apart among the dead, like the slain who lie in the grave, whom you remember no more. They are cut off from your hand.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You have laid me in the lowest pit, in the darkest depths.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Your wrath lies heavily on me. You have afflicted me with all your waves. Selah.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** You have taken my friends from me. You have made me an abomination to them. I am confined, and I can’t escape.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** My eyes are dim from grief. I have called on you daily, Yahweh. I have spread out my hands to you.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Do you show wonders to the dead? Do the departed spirits rise up and praise you? Selah.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Is your loving kindness declared in the grave? Or your faithfulness in Destruction?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Are your wonders made known in the dark? Or your righteousness in the land of forgetfulness?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But to you, Yahweh, I have cried. In the morning, my prayer comes before you.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Yahweh, why do you reject my soul? Why do you hide your face from me?

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I am afflicted and ready to die from my youth up. While I suffer your terrors, I am distracted.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Your fierce wrath has gone over me. Your terrors have cut me off.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** They came around me like water all day long. They completely engulfed me.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** You have put lover and friend far from me, and my friends into darkness.
