# Psalms 88 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Jehovah, the God of my salvation, I have cried day and night before thee.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let my prayer enter into thy presence; Incline thine ear unto my cry.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For my soul is full of troubles, And my life draweth nigh unto Sheol.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I am reckoned with them that go down into the pit; I am as a man that hath no help,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Cast off among the dead, Like the slain that lie in the grave, Whom thou rememberest no more, And they are cut off from thy hand.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Thou hast laid me in the lowest pit, In dark places, in the deeps.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Thy wrath lieth hard upon me, And thou hast afflicted me with all thy waves. [Selah

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Thou hast put mine acquaintance far from me; Thou hast made me an abomination unto them: I am shut up, and I cannot come forth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Mine eye wasteth away by reason of affliction: I have called daily upon thee, O Jehovah; I have spread forth my hands unto thee.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Wilt thou show wonders to the dead? Shall they that are deceased arise and praise thee? [Selah

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Shall thy lovingkindness be declared in the grave? Or thy faithfulness in Destruction?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Shall thy wonders be known in the dark? And thy righteousness in the land of forgetfulness?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But unto thee, O Jehovah, have I cried; And in the morning shall my prayer come before thee.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Jehovah, why castest thou off my soul? Why hidest thou thy face from me?

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I am afflicted and ready to die from my youth up: While I suffer thy terrors I am distracted.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Thy fierce wrath is gone over me; Thy terrors have cut me off.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** They came round about me like water all the day long; They compassed me about together.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Lover and friend hast thou put far from me, And mine acquaintance into darkness.
