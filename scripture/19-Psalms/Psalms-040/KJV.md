# Psalms 40 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I waited patiently for the Lord; and he inclined unto me, and heard my cry.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He brought me up also out of an horrible pit, out of the miry clay, and set my feet upon a rock, and established my goings.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And he hath put a new song in my mouth, even praise unto our God: many shall see it, and fear, and shall trust in the Lord.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Blessed is that man that maketh the Lord his trust, and respecteth not the proud, nor such as turn aside to lies.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Many, O Lord my God, are thy wonderful works which thou hast done, and thy thoughts which are to us-ward: they cannot be reckoned up in order unto thee: if I would declare and speak of them, they are more than can be numbered.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Sacrifice and offering thou didst not desire; mine ears hast thou opened: burnt offering and sin offering hast thou not required.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Then said I, Lo, I come: in the volume of the book it is written of me,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I delight to do thy will, O my God: yea, thy law is within my heart.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I have preached righteousness in the great congregation: lo, I have not refrained my lips, O Lord, thou knowest.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I have not hid thy righteousness within my heart; I have declared thy faithfulness and thy salvation: I have not concealed thy lovingkindness and thy truth from the great congregation.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Withhold not thou thy tender mercies from me, O Lord: let thy lovingkindness and thy truth continually preserve me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For innumerable evils have compassed me about: mine iniquities have taken hold upon me, so that I am not able to look up; they are more than the hairs of mine head: therefore my heart faileth me.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Be pleased, O Lord, to deliver me: O Lord, make haste to help me.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Let them be ashamed and confounded together that seek after my soul to destroy it; let them be driven backward and put to shame that wish me evil.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let them be desolate for a reward of their shame that say unto me, Aha, aha.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Let all those that seek thee rejoice and be glad in thee: let such as love thy salvation say continually, The Lord be magnified.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** But I am poor and needy; yet the Lord thinketh upon me: thou art my help and my deliverer; make no tarrying, O my God.
