# Psalms 150 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise Yah! Praise God in his sanctuary! Praise him in his heavens for his acts of power!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Praise him for his mighty acts! Praise him according to his excellent greatness!

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Praise him with the sounding of the trumpet! Praise him with harp and lyre!

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Praise him with tambourine and dancing! Praise him with stringed instruments and flute!

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Praise him with loud cymbals! Praise him with resounding cymbals!

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let everything that has breath praise Yah! Praise Yah!
