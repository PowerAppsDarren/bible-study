# Psalms 150 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye Jehovah. Praise God in his sanctuary: Praise him in the firmament of his power.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Praise him for his mighty acts: Praise him according to his excellent greatness.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Praise him with trumpet sound: Praise him with psaltery and harp.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Praise him with timbrel and dance: Praise him with stringed instruments and pipe.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Praise him with loud cymbals: Praise him with high sounding cymbals.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let everything that hath breath praise Jehovah. Praise ye Jehovah.
