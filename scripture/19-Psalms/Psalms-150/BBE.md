# Psalms 150 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Let the Lord be praised. Give praise to God in his holy place: give him praise in the heaven of his power.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Give him praise for his acts of power: give him praise in the measure of his great strength.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Give him praise with the sound of the horn: give him praise with corded instruments of music.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Give him praise with instruments of brass and in the dance: give him praise with horns and corded instruments.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Give him praise with the loud brass: give him praise with the high-sounding brass.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let everything which has breath give praise to the Lord. Let the Lord be praised.
