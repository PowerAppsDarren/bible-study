# Psalms 150 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye the Lord. Praise God in his sanctuary: praise him in the firmament of his power.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Praise him for his mighty acts: praise him according to his excellent greatness.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Praise him with the sound of the trumpet: praise him with the psaltery and harp.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Praise him with the timbrel and dance: praise him with stringed instruments and organs.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Praise him upon the loud cymbals: praise him upon the high sounding cymbals.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let every thing that hath breath praise the Lord. Praise ye the Lord.
