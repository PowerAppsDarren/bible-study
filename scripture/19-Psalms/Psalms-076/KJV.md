# Psalms 76 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In Judah is God known: his name is great in Israel.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** In Salem also is his tabernacle, and his dwelling place in Zion.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** There brake he the arrows of the bow, the shield, and the sword, and the battle. Selah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thou art more glorious and excellent than the mountains of prey.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The stouthearted are spoiled, they have slept their sleep: and none of the men of might have found their hands.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** At thy rebuke, O God of Jacob, both the chariot and horse are cast into a dead sleep.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Thou, even thou, art to be feared: and who may stand in thy sight when once thou art angry?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Thou didst cause judgment to be heard from heaven; the earth feared, and was still,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** When God arose to judgment, to save all the meek of the earth. Selah.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Surely the wrath of man shall praise thee: the remainder of wrath shalt thou restrain.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Vow, and pay unto the Lord your God: let all that be round about him bring presents unto him that ought to be feared.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** He shall cut off the spirit of princes: he is terrible to the kings of the earth.
