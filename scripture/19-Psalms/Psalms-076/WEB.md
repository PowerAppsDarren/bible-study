# Psalms 76 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In Judah, God is known. His name is great in Israel.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** His tabernacle is also in Salem; His dwelling place in Zion.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** There he broke the flaming arrows of the bow, the shield, and the sword, and the weapons of war. Selah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Glorious are you, and excellent, more than mountains of game.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Valiant men lie plundered, they have slept their last sleep. None of the men of war can lift their hands.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** At your rebuke, God of Jacob, both chariot and horse are cast into a deep sleep.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You, even you, are to be feared. Who can stand in your sight when you are angry?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** You pronounced judgment from heaven. The earth feared, and was silent,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** when God arose to judgment, to save all the afflicted ones of the earth. Selah.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Surely the wrath of man praises you. The survivors of your wrath are restrained.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Make vows to Yahweh your God, and fulfill them! Let all of his neighbors bring presents to him who is to be feared.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** He will cut off the spirit of princes. He is feared by the kings of the earth.
