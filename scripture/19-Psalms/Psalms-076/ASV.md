# Psalms 76 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In Judah is God known: His name is great in Israel.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** In Salem also is his tabernacle, And his dwelling-place in Zion.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** There he brake the arrows of the bow; The shield, and the sword, and the battle. [Selah

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Glorious art thou and excellent, From the mountains of prey.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The stouthearted are made a spoil, They have slept their sleep; And none of the men of might have found their hands.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** At thy rebuke, O God of Jacob, Both chariot and horse are cast into a dead sleep.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Thou, even thou, art to be feared; And who may stand in thy sight when once thou art angry?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Thou didst cause sentence to be heard from heaven; The earth feared, and was still,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** When God arose to judgment, To save all the meek of the earth. [Selah

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Surely the wrath of man shall praise thee: The residue of wrath shalt thou gird upon thee.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Vow, and pay unto Jehovah your God: Let all that are round about him bring presents unto him that ought to be feared.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** He will cut off the spirit of princes: He is terrible to the kings of the earth.
