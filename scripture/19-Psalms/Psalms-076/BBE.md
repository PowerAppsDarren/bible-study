# Psalms 76 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In Judah is the knowledge of God; his name is great in Israel,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** In Salem is his tent, his resting-place in Zion.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** There were the arrows of the bow broken, there he put an end to body-cover, sword, and fight. (Selah.)

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You are shining and full of glory, more than the eternal mountains.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Gone is the wealth of the strong, their last sleep has overcome them; the men of war have become feeble.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** At the voice of your wrath, O God of Jacob, deep sleep has overcome carriage and horse.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You, you are to be feared; who may keep his place before you in the time of your wrath?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** From heaven you gave your decision; the earth, in its fear, gave no sound,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** When God took his place as judge, for the salvation of the poor on the earth. (Selah.)

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The ... will give you praise; the rest of ...

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Give to the Lord your God what is his by right; let all who are round him give offerings to him who is to be feared.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** He puts an end to the wrath of rulers; he is feared by the kings of the earth.
