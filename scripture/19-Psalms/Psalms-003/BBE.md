# Psalms 3 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Lord, how greatly are they increased who make attacks on me! in great numbers they come against me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Unnumbered are those who say of my soul, There is no help for him in God. (Selah.)

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But your strength, O Lord, is round me, you are my glory and the lifter up of my head.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I send up a cry to the Lord with my voice, and he gives me an answer from his holy hill. (Selah.)

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I took my rest in sleep, and then again I was awake; for the Lord was my support.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I will have no fear, though ten thousand have come round me, putting themselves against me.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Come to me, Lord; keep me safe, O my God; for you have given all my haters blows on their face-bones; the teeth of the evil-doers have been broken by you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Salvation comes from the Lord; your blessing is on your people. (Selah.)
