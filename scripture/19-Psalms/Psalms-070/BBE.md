# Psalms 70 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Let your salvation come quickly, O God; come quickly to my help, O Lord.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let those who go after my soul have shame and trouble; let those who have evil designs against me be turned back and made foolish.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let those who say Aha, aha! be turned back as a reward of their shame.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Let all those who are looking for you be glad and have joy in you; let the lovers of your salvation ever say, May God be great.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But I am poor and in need; come to me quickly, O God; you are my help and my saviour; let there be no waiting, O Lord.
