# Psalms 70 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Make haste, O God, to deliver me; Make haste to help me, O Jehovah.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let them be put to shame and confounded That seek after my soul: Let them be turned backward and brought to dishonor That delight in my hurt.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let them be turned back by reason of their shame That say, Aha, aha.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Let all those that seek thee rejoice and be glad in thee; And let such as love thy salvation say continually, Let God be magnified.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But I am poor and needy; Make haste unto me, O God: Thou art my help and my deliverer; O Jehovah, make no tarrying.
