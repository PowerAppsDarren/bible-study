# Psalms 70 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hurry, God, to deliver me. Come quickly to help me, Yahweh.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let them be disappointed and confounded who seek my soul. Let those who desire my ruin be turned back in disgrace.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let them be turned because of their shame Who say, “Aha! Aha!”

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Let all those who seek you rejoice and be glad in you. Let those who love your salvation continually say, “Let God be exalted!”

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But I am poor and needy. Come to me quickly, God. You are my help and my deliverer. Yahweh, don’t delay.
