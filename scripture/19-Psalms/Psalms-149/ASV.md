# Psalms 149 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye Jehovah. Sing unto Jehovah a new song, And his praise in the assembly of the saints.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let Israel rejoice in him that made him: Let the children of Zion be joyful in their King.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let them praise his name in the dance: Let them sing praises unto him with timbrel and harp.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For Jehovah taketh pleasure in his people: He will beautify the meek with salvation.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let the saints exult in glory: Let them sing for joy upon their beds.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Letthe high praises of God be in their mouth, And a two-edged sword in their hand;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** To execute vengeance upon the nations, And punishments upon the peoples;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** To bind their kings with chains, And their nobles with fetters of iron;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** To execute upon them the judgment written: This honor have all his saints. Praise ye Jehovah.
