# Psalms 149 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye the Lord. Sing unto the Lord a new song, and his praise in the congregation of saints.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let Israel rejoice in him that made him: let the children of Zion be joyful in their King.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let them praise his name in the dance: let them sing praises unto him with the timbrel and harp.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For the Lord taketh pleasure in his people: he will beautify the meek with salvation.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let the saints be joyful in glory: let them sing aloud upon their beds.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let the high praises of God be in their mouth, and a twoedged sword in their hand;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** To execute vengeance upon the heathen, and punishments upon the people;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** To bind their kings with chains, and their nobles with fetters of iron;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** To execute upon them the judgment written: this honour have all his saints. Praise ye the Lord.
