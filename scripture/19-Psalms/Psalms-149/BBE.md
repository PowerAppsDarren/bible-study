# Psalms 149 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Let the Lord be praised. Make a new song to the Lord, let his praise be in the meeting of his saints.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let Israel have joy in his maker; let the children of Zion be glad in their King.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let them give praise to his name in the dance: let them make melody to him with instruments of brass and corded instruments of music.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For the Lord has pleasure in his people: he gives the poor in spirit a crown of salvation.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let the saints have joy and glory: let them give cries of joy on their beds.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let the high praises of God be in their mouths, and a two-edged sword in their hands;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** To give the nations the reward of their sins, and the peoples their punishment;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** To put their kings in chains, and their rulers in bands of iron;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** To give them the punishment which is in the holy writings: this honour is given to all his saints. Praise be to the Lord.
