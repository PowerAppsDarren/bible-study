# Psalms 149 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise Yahweh! Sing to Yahweh a new song, his praise in the assembly of the saints.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let Israel rejoice in him who made them. Let the children of Zion be joyful in their King.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let them praise his name in the dance! Let them sing praises to him with tambourine and harp!

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For Yahweh takes pleasure in his people. He crowns the humble with salvation.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let the saints rejoice in honor. Let them sing for joy on their beds.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** May the high praises of God be in their mouths, and a two-edged sword in their hand;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** To execute vengeance on the nations, and punishments on the peoples;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** To bind their kings with chains, and their nobles with fetters of iron;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** to execute on them the written judgment. All his saints have this honor. Praise Yah!
