# Psalms 109 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God of my praise, don’t remain silent,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** for they have opened the mouth of the wicked and the mouth of deceit against me. They have spoken to me with a lying tongue.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They have also surrounded me with words of hatred, and fought against me without a cause.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** In return for my love, they are my adversaries; but I am in prayer.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They have rewarded me evil for good, and hatred for my love.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Set a wicked man over him. Let an adversary stand at his right hand.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** When he is judged, let him come out guilty. Let his prayer be turned into sin.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let his days be few. Let another take his office.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let his children be fatherless, and his wife a widow.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Let his children be wandering beggars. Let them be sought from their ruins.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let the creditor seize all that he has. Let strangers plunder the fruit of his labor.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Let there be no one to extend kindness to him, neither let there be anyone to have pity on his fatherless children.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Let his posterity be cut off. In the generation following let their name be blotted out.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Let the iniquity of his fathers be remembered by Yahweh. Don’t let the sin of his mother be blotted out.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let them be before Yahweh continually, that he may cut off their memory from the earth;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** because he didn’t remember to show kindness, but persecuted the poor and needy man, the broken in heart, to kill them.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Yes, he loved cursing, and it came to him. He didn’t delight in blessing, and it was far from him.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He clothed himself also with cursing as with his garment. It came into his inward parts like water, like oil into his bones.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Let it be to him as the clothing with which he covers himself, for the belt that is always around him.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** This is the reward of my adversaries from Yahweh, of those who speak evil against my soul.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** But deal with me, Yahweh the Lord, for your name’s sake, because your loving kindness is good, deliver me;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** for I am poor and needy. My heart is wounded within me.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** I fade away like an evening shadow. I am shaken off like a locust.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** My knees are weak through fasting. My body is thin and lacks fat.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** I have also become a reproach to them. When they see me, they shake their head.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Help me, Yahweh, my God. Save me according to your loving kindness;

<a id="v27"></a>**27** that they may know that this is your hand; that you, Yahweh, have done it.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** They may curse, but you bless. When they arise, they will be shamed, but your servant shall rejoice.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Let my adversaries be clothed with dishonor. Let them cover themselves with their own shame as with a robe.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** I will give great thanks to Yahweh with my mouth. Yes, I will praise him among the multitude.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For he will stand at the right hand of the needy, to save him from those who judge his soul.
