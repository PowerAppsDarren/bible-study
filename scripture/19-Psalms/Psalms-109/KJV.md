# Psalms 109 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hold not thy peace, O God of my praise;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For the mouth of the wicked and the mouth of the deceitful are opened against me: they have spoken against me with a lying tongue.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They compassed me about also with words of hatred; and fought against me without a cause.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For my love they are my adversaries: but I give myself unto prayer.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And they have rewarded me evil for good, and hatred for my love.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Set thou a wicked man over him: and let Satan stand at his right hand.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** When he shall be judged, let him be condemned: and let his prayer become sin.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let his days be few; and let another take his office.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let his children be fatherless, and his wife a widow.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Let his children be continually vagabonds, and beg: let them seek their bread also out of their desolate places.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let the extortioner catch all that he hath; and let the strangers spoil his labour.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Let there be none to extend mercy unto him: neither let there be any to favour his fatherless children.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Let his posterity be cut off; and in the generation following let their name be blotted out.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Let the iniquity of his fathers be remembered with the Lord; and let not the sin of his mother be blotted out.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let them be before the Lord continually, that he may cut off the memory of them from the earth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Because that he remembered not to shew mercy, but persecuted the poor and needy man, that he might even slay the broken in heart.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** As he loved cursing, so let it come unto him: as he delighted not in blessing, so let it be far from him.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** As he clothed himself with cursing like as with his garment, so let it come into his bowels like water, and like oil into his bones.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Let it be unto him as the garment which covereth him, and for a girdle wherewith he is girded continually.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Let this be the reward of mine adversaries from the Lord, and of them that speak evil against my soul.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** But do thou for me, O God the Lord, for thy name’s sake: because thy mercy is good, deliver thou me.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For I am poor and needy, and my heart is wounded within me.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** I am gone like the shadow when it declineth: I am tossed up and down as the locust.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** My knees are weak through fasting; and my flesh faileth of fatness.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** I became also a reproach unto them: when they looked upon me they shaked their heads.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Help me, O Lord my God: O save me according to thy mercy:

<a id="v27"></a>**27** That they may know that this is thy hand; that thou, Lord, hast done it.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Let them curse, but bless thou: when they arise, let them be ashamed; but let thy servant rejoice.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Let mine adversaries be clothed with shame, and let them cover themselves with their own confusion, as with a mantle.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** I will greatly praise the Lord with my mouth; yea, I will praise him among the multitude.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For he shall stand at the right hand of the poor, to save him from those that condemn his soul.
