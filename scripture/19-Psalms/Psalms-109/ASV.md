# Psalms 109 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hold not thy peace, O God of my praise;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For the mouth of the wicked and the mouth of deceit have they opened against me: They have spoken unto me with a lying tongue.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They have compassed me about also with words of hatred, And fought against me without a cause.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For my love they are my adversaries: But Igive myself untoprayer.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And they have rewarded me evil for good, And hatred for my love.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Set thou a wicked man over him; And let an adversary stand at his right hand.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** When he is judged, let him come forth guilty; And let his prayer be turned into sin.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let his days be few; Andlet another take his office.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let his children be fatherless, And his wife a widow.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Let his children be vagabonds, and beg; And let them seek their bread out of their desolate places.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let the extortioner catch all that he hath; And let strangers make spoil of his labor.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Let there be none to extend kindness unto him; Neither let there be any to have pity on his fatherless children.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Let his posterity be cut off; In the generation following let their name be blotted out.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Let the iniquity of his fathers be remembered with Jehovah; And let not the sin of his mother be blotted out.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let them be before Jehovah continually, That he may cut off the memory of them from the earth;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Because he remembered not to show kindness, But persecuted the poor and needy man, And the broken in heart, to slay them.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Yea, he loved cursing, and it came unto him; And he delighted not in blessing, and it was far from him.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He clothed himself also with cursing as with his garment, And it came into his inward parts like water, And like oil into his bones.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Let it be unto him as the raiment wherewith he covereth himself, And for the girdle wherewith he is girded continually.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** This is the reward of mine adversaries from Jehovah, And of them that speak evil against my soul.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** But deal thou with me, O Jehovah the Lord, for thy name’s sake: Because thy lovingkindness is good, deliver thou me;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For I am poor and needy, And my heart is wounded within me.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** I am gone like the shadow when it declineth: I am tossed up and down as the locust.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** My knees are weak through fasting; And my flesh faileth of fatness.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** I am become also a reproach unto them: When they see me, they shake their head.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Help me, O Jehovah my God; Oh save me according to thy lovingkindness:

<a id="v27"></a>**27** That they may know that this is thy hand; That thou, Jehovah, hast done it.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Let them curse, but bless thou: When they arise, they shall be put to shame, But thy servant shall rejoice.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Let mine adversaries be clothed with dishonor, And let them cover themselves with their own shame as with a robe.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** I will give great thanks unto Jehovah with my mouth; Yea, I will praise him among the multitude.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For he will stand at the right hand of the needy, To save him from them that judge his soul.
