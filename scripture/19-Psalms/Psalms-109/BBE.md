# Psalms 109 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God of my praise, let my prayer be answered;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For the mouth of the sinner is open against me in deceit: his tongue has said false things against me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Words of hate are round about me; they have made war against me without cause.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For my love they give me back hate; but I have given myself to prayer.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They have put on me evil for good; hate in exchange for my love.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Put an evil man over him; and let one be placed at his right hand to say evil of him.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** When he is judged, let the decision go against him; and may his prayer become sin.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let his life be short; let another take his position of authority.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let his children have no father, and his wife be made a widow.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Let his children be wanderers, looking to others for their food; let them be sent away from the company of their friends.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let his creditor take all his goods; and let others have the profit of his work.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Let no man have pity on him, or give help to his children when he is dead.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Let his seed be cut off; in the coming generation let their name go out of memory.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Let the Lord keep in mind the wrongdoing of his fathers; and may the sin of his mother have no forgiveness.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let them be ever before the eyes of the Lord, so that the memory of them may be cut off from the earth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Because he had no mercy, but was cruel to the low and the poor, designing the death of the broken-hearted.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** As he took pleasure in cursing, so let it come on him; and as he had no delight in blessing, let it be far from him.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He put on cursing like a robe, and it has come into his body like water, and into his bones like oil.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Let it be to him as a robe which he puts on, let it be like a band which is round him at all times.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Let this be the reward given to my haters from the Lord, and to those who say evil of my soul.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** But, O Lord God, give me your help, because of your name; take me out of danger, because your mercy is good.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For I am poor and in need, and my heart is wounded in me.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** I am gone like the shade when it is stretched out: I am forced out of my place like a locust.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** My knees are feeble for need of food; there is no fat on my bones.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** As for me, they make sport of me; shaking their heads when they see me.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Give me help, O Lord my God; in your mercy be my saviour;

<a id="v27"></a>**27** So that they may see that it is the work of your hand; that you, Lord, have done it.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** They may give curses but you give blessing; when they come up against me, put them to shame; but let your servant be glad.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Let my haters be clothed with shame, covering themselves with shame as with a robe.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** I will give the Lord great praise with my mouth; yes, I will give praise to him among all the people.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For he is ever at the right hand of the poor, to take him out of the hands of those who go after his soul.
