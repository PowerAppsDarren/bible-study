# Psalms 55 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Listen to my prayer, God. Don’t hide yourself from my supplication.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Attend to me, and answer me. I am restless in my complaint, and moan,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Because of the voice of the enemy, Because of the oppression of the wicked. For they bring suffering on me. In anger they hold a grudge against me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** My heart is severely pained within me. The terrors of death have fallen on me.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Fearfulness and trembling have come on me. Horror has overwhelmed me.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I said, “Oh that I had wings like a dove! Then I would fly away, and be at rest.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Behold, then I would wander far off. I would lodge in the wilderness.” Selah.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** “I would hurry to a shelter from the stormy wind and storm.”

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Confuse them, Lord, and confound their language, for I have seen violence and strife in the city.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Day and night they prowl around on its walls. Malice and abuse are also within her.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Destructive forces are within her. Threats and lies don’t depart from her streets.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For it was not an enemy who insulted me, then I could have endured it. Neither was it he who hated me who raised himself up against me, then I would have hidden myself from him.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But it was you, a man like me, my companion, and my familiar friend.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** We took sweet fellowship together. We walked in God’s house with company.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let death come suddenly on them. Let them go down alive into Sheol. For wickedness is in their dwelling, in their midst.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** As for me, I will call on God. Yahweh will save me.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Evening, morning, and at noon, I will cry out in distress. He will hear my voice.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He has redeemed my soul in peace from the battle that was against me, although there are many who oppose me.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** God, who is enthroned forever, will hear, and answer them. Selah.   They never change, who don’t fear God.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He raises his hands against his friends. He has violated his covenant.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** His mouth was smooth as butter, but his heart was war. His words were softer than oil, yet they were drawn swords.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Cast your burden on Yahweh, and he will sustain you. He will never allow the righteous to be moved.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** But you, God, will bring them down into the pit of destruction. Bloodthirsty and deceitful men shall not live out half their days, but I will trust in you.
