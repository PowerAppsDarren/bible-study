# Psalms 49 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear this, all you peoples. Listen, all you inhabitants of the world,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** both low and high, rich and poor together.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** My mouth will speak words of wisdom. My heart shall utter understanding.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will incline my ear to a proverb. I will open my riddle on the harp.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Why should I fear in the days of evil, when iniquity at my heels surrounds me?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Those who trust in their wealth, and boast in the multitude of their riches—

<a id="v7"></a>**7** none of them can by any means redeem his brother, nor give God a ransom for him.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For the redemption of their life is costly, no payment is ever enough,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** That he should live on forever, that he should not see corruption.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For he sees that wise men die; likewise the fool and the senseless perish, and leave their wealth to others.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Their inward thought is that their houses will endure forever, and their dwelling places to all generations. They name their lands after themselves.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But man, despite his riches, doesn’t endure. He is like the animals that perish.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** This is the destiny of those who are foolish, and of those who approve their sayings. Selah.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** They are appointed as a flock for Sheol. Death shall be their shepherd. The upright shall have dominion over them in the morning. Their beauty shall decay in Sheol, far from their mansion.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But God will redeem my soul from the power of Sheol, for he will receive me. Selah.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Don’t be afraid when a man is made rich, when the glory of his house is increased.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For when he dies he shall carry nothing away. His glory shall not descend after him.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Though while he lived he blessed his soul— and men praise you when you do well for yourself—

<a id="v19"></a>**19** he shall go to the generation of his fathers. They shall never see the light.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** A man who has riches without understanding, is like the animals that perish.
