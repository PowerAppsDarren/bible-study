# Psalms 49 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear this, all ye peoples; Give ear, all ye inhabitants of the world,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Both low and high, Rich and poor together.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** My mouth shall speak wisdom; And the meditation of my heart shall be of understanding.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will incline mine ear to a parable: I will open my dark saying upon the harp.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Wherefore should I fear in the days of evil, When iniquity at my heels compasseth me about?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They that trust in their wealth, And boast themselves in the multitude of their riches;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** None of them can by any means redeem his brother, Nor give to God a ransom for him

<a id="v8"></a>**8** (For the redemption of their life is costly, And it faileth for ever),

<a id="v9"></a>**9** That he should still live alway, That he should not see corruption.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For he shall see it. Wise men die; The fool and the brutish alike perish, And leave their wealth to others.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Their inward thought is, that their houses shall continue for ever, Andtheir dwelling-places to all generations; They call their lands after their own names.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But man being in honor abideth not: He is like the beasts that perish.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** This their way is their folly: Yet after them men approve their sayings. [Selah

<a id="v14"></a>**14** They are appointed as a flock for Sheol; Death shall be their shepherd: And the upright shall have dominion over them in the morning; And their beauty shall be for Sheol to consume, That there be no habitation for it.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But God will redeem my soul from the power of Sheol; For he will receive me. [Selah

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Be not thou afraid when one is made rich, When the glory of his house is increased:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For when he dieth he shall carry nothing away; His glory shall not descend after him.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Though while he lived he blessed his soul (And men praise thee, when thou doest well to thyself),

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He shall go to the generation of his fathers; They shall never see the light.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Man that is in honor, and understandeth not, Is like the beasts that perish.
