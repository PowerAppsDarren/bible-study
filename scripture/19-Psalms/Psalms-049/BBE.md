# Psalms 49 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give attention to this, all you peoples; let your ears be open, all you who are living in the world.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** High and low together, the poor, and those who have wealth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** From my mouth will come words of wisdom; and in the thoughts of my heart will be knowledge.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will put my teaching into a story; I will make my dark sayings clear with music.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** What cause have I for fear in the days of evil, when the evil-doing of those who are working for my downfall is round about me?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Even of those whose faith is in their wealth, and whose hearts are lifted up because of their stores.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Truly, no man may get back his soul for a price, or give to God the payment for himself;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** (Because it takes a great price to keep his soul from death, and man is not able to give it.)

<a id="v9"></a>**9** So that he might have eternal life, and never see the underworld.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For he sees that wise men come to their end, and foolish persons of low behaviour come to destruction together, letting their wealth go to others.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The place of the dead is their house for ever, and their resting-place through all generations; those who come after them give their names to their lands.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But man, like the animals, does not go on for ever; he comes to an end like the beasts.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** This is the way of the foolish; their silver is for those who come after them, and their children get the pleasure of their gold. (Selah.)

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Death will give them their food like sheep; the underworld is their fate and they will go down into it; their flesh is food for worms; their form is wasted away; the underworld is their resting-place for ever.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But God will get back my soul; for he will take me from the power of death. (Selah.)

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Have no fear when wealth comes to a man, and the glory of his house is increased;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For at his death, he will take nothing away; his glory will not go down after him.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Though he might have pride in his soul in his life-time, and men will give you praise if you do well for yourself,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He will go to the generation of his fathers; he will not see the light again.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Man, like the animals, does not go on for ever; he comes to an end like the beasts.
