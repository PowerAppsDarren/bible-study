# Psalms 49 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear this, all ye people; give ear, all ye inhabitants of the world:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Both low and high, rich and poor, together.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** My mouth shall speak of wisdom; and the meditation of my heart shall be of understanding.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will incline mine ear to a parable: I will open my dark saying upon the harp.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Wherefore should I fear in the days of evil, when the iniquity of my heels shall compass me about?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They that trust in their wealth, and boast themselves in the multitude of their riches;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** None of them can by any means redeem his brother, nor give to God a ransom for him:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** (For the redemption of their soul is precious, and it ceaseth for ever:)

<a id="v9"></a>**9** That he should still live for ever, and not see corruption.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For he seeth that wise men die, likewise the fool and the brutish person perish, and leave their wealth to others.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Their inward thought is, that their houses shall continue for ever, and their dwelling places to all generations; they call their lands after their own names.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Nevertheless man being in honour abideth not: he is like the beasts that perish.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** This their way is their folly: yet their posterity approve their sayings. Selah.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Like sheep they are laid in the grave; death shall feed on them; and the upright shall have dominion over them in the morning; and their beauty shall consume in the grave from their dwelling.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But God will redeem my soul from the power of the grave: for he shall receive me. Selah.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Be not thou afraid when one is made rich, when the glory of his house is increased;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For when he dieth he shall carry nothing away: his glory shall not descend after him.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Though while he lived he blessed his soul: and men will praise thee, when thou doest well to thyself.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He shall go to the generation of his fathers; they shall never see light.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Man that is in honour, and understandeth not, is like the beasts that perish.
