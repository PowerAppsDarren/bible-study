# Psalms 74 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God, why have you rejected us forever? Why does your anger smolder against the sheep of your pasture?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Remember your congregation, which you purchased of old, which you have redeemed to be the tribe of your inheritance; Mount Zion, in which you have lived.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Lift up your feet to the perpetual ruins, all the evil that the enemy has done in the sanctuary.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Your adversaries have roared in the midst of your assembly. They have set up their standards as signs.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They behaved like men wielding axes, cutting through a thicket of trees.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Now they break all its carved work down with hatchet and hammers.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They have burned your sanctuary to the ground. They have profaned the dwelling place of your Name.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They said in their heart, “We will crush them completely.” They have burned up all the places in the land where God was worshiped.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** We see no miraculous signs. There is no longer any prophet, neither is there among us anyone who knows how long.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** How long, God, shall the adversary reproach? Shall the enemy blaspheme your name forever?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Why do you draw back your hand, even your right hand? Take it out of your pocket and consume them!

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yet God is my King of old, working salvation in the midst of the earth.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** You divided the sea by your strength. You broke the heads of the sea monsters in the waters.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** You broke the heads of Leviathan in pieces. You gave him as food to people and desert creatures.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** You opened up spring and stream. You dried up mighty rivers.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The day is yours, the night is also yours. You have prepared the light and the sun.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** You have set all the boundaries of the earth. You have made summer and winter.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Remember this, that the enemy has mocked you, Yahweh. Foolish people have blasphemed your name.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Don’t deliver the soul of your dove to wild beasts. Don’t forget the life of your poor forever.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Honor your covenant, for haunts of violence fill the dark places of the earth.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Don’t let the oppressed return ashamed. Let the poor and needy praise your name.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Arise, God! Plead your own cause. Remember how the foolish man mocks you all day.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Don’t forget the voice of your adversaries. The tumult of those who rise up against you ascends continually.
