# Psalms 37 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Fret not thyself because of evildoers, neither be thou envious against the workers of iniquity.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For they shall soon be cut down like the grass, and wither as the green herb.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Trust in the Lord, and do good; so shalt thou dwell in the land, and verily thou shalt be fed.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Delight thyself also in the Lord; and he shall give thee the desires of thine heart.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Commit thy way unto the Lord; trust also in him; and he shall bring it to pass.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And he shall bring forth thy righteousness as the light, and thy judgment as the noonday.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Rest in the Lord, and wait patiently for him: fret not thyself because of him who prospereth in his way, because of the man who bringeth wicked devices to pass.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Cease from anger, and forsake wrath: fret not thyself in any wise to do evil.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For evildoers shall be cut off: but those that wait upon the Lord, they shall inherit the earth.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For yet a little while, and the wicked shall not be: yea, thou shalt diligently consider his place, and it shall not be.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But the meek shall inherit the earth; and shall delight themselves in the abundance of peace.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The wicked plotteth against the just, and gnasheth upon him with his teeth.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The Lord shall laugh at him: for he seeth that his day is coming.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The wicked have drawn out the sword, and have bent their bow, to cast down the poor and needy, and to slay such as be of upright conversation.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Their sword shall enter into their own heart, and their bows shall be broken.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** A little that a righteous man hath is better than the riches of many wicked.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For the arms of the wicked shall be broken: but the Lord upholdeth the righteous.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The Lord knoweth the days of the upright: and their inheritance shall be for ever.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** They shall not be ashamed in the evil time: and in the days of famine they shall be satisfied.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** But the wicked shall perish, and the enemies of the Lord shall be as the fat of lambs: they shall consume; into smoke shall they consume away.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The wicked borroweth, and payeth not again: but the righteous sheweth mercy, and giveth.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For such as be blessed of him shall inherit the earth; and they that be cursed of him shall be cut off.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** The steps of a good man are ordered by the Lord: and he delighteth in his way.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Though he fall, he shall not be utterly cast down: for the Lord upholdeth him with his hand.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** I have been young, and now am old; yet have I not seen the righteous forsaken, nor his seed begging bread.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** He is ever merciful, and lendeth; and his seed is blessed.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Depart from evil, and do good; and dwell for evermore.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** For the Lord loveth judgment, and forsaketh not his saints; they are preserved for ever: but the seed of the wicked shall be cut off.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** The righteous shall inherit the land, and dwell therein for ever.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** The mouth of the righteous speaketh wisdom, and his tongue talketh of judgment.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** The law of his God is in his heart; none of his steps shall slide.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The wicked watcheth the righteous, and seeketh to slay him.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** The Lord will not leave him in his hand, nor condemn him when he is judged.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Wait on the Lord, and keep his way, and he shall exalt thee to inherit the land: when the wicked are cut off, thou shalt see it.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** I have seen the wicked in great power, and spreading himself like a green bay tree.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Yet he passed away, and, lo, he was not: yea, I sought him, but he could not be found.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Mark the perfect man, and behold the upright: for the end of that man is peace.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** But the transgressors shall be destroyed together: the end of the wicked shall be cut off.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** But the salvation of the righteous is of the Lord: he is their strength in the time of trouble.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** And the Lord shall help them, and deliver them: he shall deliver them from the wicked, and save them, because they trust in him.
