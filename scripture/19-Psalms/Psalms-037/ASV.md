# Psalms 37 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Fret not thyself because of evil-doers, Neither be thou envious against them that work unrighteousness.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For they shall soon be cut down like the grass, And wither as the green herb.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Trust in Jehovah, and do good; Dwell in the land, and feed onhisfaithfulness.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Delight thyself also in Jehovah; And he will give thee the desires of thy heart.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Commit thy way unto Jehovah; Trust also in him, and he will bring it to pass.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And he will make thy righteousness to go forth as the light, And thy justice as the noonday.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Rest in Jehovah, and wait patiently for him: Fret not thyself because of him who prospereth in his way, Because of the man who bringeth wicked devices to pass.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Cease from anger, and forsake wrath: Fret not thyself, it tendeth only to evil-doing.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For evil-doers shall be cut off; But those that wait for Jehovah, they shall inherit the land.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For yet a little while, and the wicked shall not be: Yea, thou shalt diligently consider his place, and he shall not be.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But the meek shall inherit the land, And shall delight themselves in the abundance of peace.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The wicked plotteth against the just, And gnasheth upon him with his teeth.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The Lord will laugh at him; For he seeth that his day is coming.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The wicked have drawn out the sword, and have bent their bow, To cast down the poor and needy, To slay such as are upright in the way.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Their sword shall enter into their own heart, And their bows shall be broken.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Better is a little that the righteous hath Than the abundance of many wicked.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For the arms of the wicked shall be broken; But Jehovah upholdeth the righteous.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Jehovah knoweth the days of the perfect; And their inheritance shall be for ever.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** They shall not be put to shame in the time of evil; And in the days of famine they shall be satisfied.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** But the wicked shall perish, And the enemies of Jehovah shall be as the fat of lambs: They shall consume; in smoke shall they consume away.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The wicked borroweth, and payeth not again; But the righteous dealeth graciously, and giveth.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For such as are blessed of him shall inherit the land; And they that are cursed of him shall be cut off.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** A man’s goings are established of Jehovah; And he delighteth in his way.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Though he fall, he shall not be utterly cast down; For Jehovah upholdeth him with his hand.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** I have been young, and now am old; Yet have I not seen the righteous forsaken, Nor his seed begging bread.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** All the day long he dealeth graciously, and lendeth; And his seed is blessed.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Depart from evil, and do good; And dwell for evermore.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** For Jehovah loveth justice, And forsaketh not his saints; They are preserved for ever: But the seed of the wicked shall be cut off.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** The righteous shall inherit the land, And dwell therein for ever.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** The mouth of the righteous talketh of wisdom, And his tongue speaketh justice.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** The law of his God is in his heart; None of his steps shall slide.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The wicked watcheth the righteous, And seeketh to slay him.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Jehovah will not leave him in his hand, Nor condemn him when he is judged.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Wait for Jehovah, and keep his way, And he will exalt thee to inherit the land: When the wicked are cut off, thou shalt see it.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** I have seen the wicked in great power, And spreading himself like a green tree in its native soil.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** But one passed by, and, lo, he was not: Yea, I sought him, but he could not be found.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Mark the perfect man, and behold the upright; For there is ahappyend to the man of peace.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** As for transgressors, they shall be destroyed together: The end of the wicked shall be cut off.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** But the salvation of the righteous is of Jehovah: He is their stronghold in the time of trouble.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** And Jehovah helpeth them, and rescueth them: He rescueth them from the wicked, and saveth them, Because they have taken refuge in him.
