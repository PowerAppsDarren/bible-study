# Psalms 37 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Do not be angry because of the wrongdoers, or have envy of the workers of evil.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For they will quickly be cut down like grass, and become dry like the green plants.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Have faith in the Lord, and do good; be at rest in the land, and go after righteousness.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** So will your delight be in the Lord, and he will give you your heart's desires.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Put your life in the hands of the Lord; have faith in him and he will do it.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And he will make your righteousness be seen like the light, and your cause like the shining of the sun.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Take your rest in the Lord, waiting quietly for him; do not be angry because of the man who does well in his evil ways, and gives effect to his bad designs.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Put an end to your wrath and be no longer bitter; do not give way to angry feeling which is a cause of sin.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For the evil-doers will be cut off: but those who have faith in the Lord will have the earth for their heritage.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For in a short time the evil-doer will be gone: you will go searching for his place, and it will not be there.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But the gentle will have the earth for their heritage; they will take their delight in peace without measure.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The sinner has evil designs against the upright, lifting up the voice of wrath against him.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He will be laughed at by the Lord, who sees that his day is coming.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The evil-doers have taken out their swords, their bows are bent; for crushing the poor, and to put to death those who are upright in their ways.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But their swords will be turned into their hearts, and their bows will be broken.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The little which the good man has is better than the wealth of evil-doers.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For the arms of the evil-doers will be broken: but the Lord is the support of the good.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The days of the upright are numbered by the Lord, and their heritage will be for ever.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** They will not be shamed in the evil time, and in the days when all are in need of food they will have enough.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** But the wrongdoers will come to destruction, and the haters of the Lord will be like the fat of lambs, they will be burned up; they will go up in smoke, and never again be seen.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The sinner takes money and does not give it back; but the upright man has mercy, and gives to others.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Those who have his blessing will have the earth for their heritage; but those who are cursed by him will be cut off.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** The steps of a good man are ordered by the Lord, and he takes delight in his way.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Even if he has a fall he will not be without help: for the hand of the Lord is supporting him.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** I have been young, and now am old, but I have not seen the good man without help, or his children looking for bread.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** All the day he is ready to have mercy and to give; his children are a blessing.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Be turned from evil, and do good; and your place will be for ever.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** For the Lord is a lover of righteousness, and takes care of his saints; they will be kept safe for ever; but the seed of the evil-doers will be cut off.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** The upright will have the earth for their heritage, and will go on living there for ever.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** The mouth of the good man says words of wisdom; the talk of his tongue is of righteousness.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** The law of his God is in his heart; he will never make a false step.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The sinners are watching the upright man, desiring to put him to death.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** The Lord will not give him into their hands, or be against him when he is judged.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Be waiting for the Lord, and keep his way; and you will be lifted up, and have the land for your heritage: when the evil-doers are cut off, you will see it.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** I have seen the evil-doer in great power, covering the earth like a great tree.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** But he came to an end, and there was no sign of him; I made a search for him and he was not there.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Give attention to the good man, and take note of the upright; because the end of that man is peace.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** But as for the sinners, they will be cut off together; the end of the wrongdoers is destruction.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** But the Lord is the saviour of the upright: he is their strength in the time of trouble.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** And the Lord will be their help, and keep them safe: he will take them out of the hands of the evil-doers, and be their saviour, because they had faith in him.
