# Psalms 37 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Don’t fret because of evildoers, neither be envious against those who work unrighteousness.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For they shall soon be cut down like the grass, and wither like the green herb.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Trust in Yahweh, and do good. Dwell in the land, and enjoy safe pasture.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Also delight yourself in Yahweh, and he will give you the desires of your heart.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Commit your way to Yahweh. Trust also in him, and he will do this:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** he will make your righteousness go out as the light, and your justice as the noon day sun.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Rest in Yahweh, and wait patiently for him. Don’t fret because of him who prospers in his way, because of the man who makes wicked plots happen.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Cease from anger, and forsake wrath. Don’t fret, it leads only to evildoing.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For evildoers shall be cut off, but those who wait for Yahweh shall inherit the land.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For yet a little while, and the wicked will be no more. Yes, though you look for his place, he isn’t there.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But the humble shall inherit the land, and shall delight themselves in the abundance of peace.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The wicked plots against the just, and gnashes at him with his teeth.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The Lord will laugh at him, for he sees that his day is coming.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The wicked have drawn out the sword, and have bent their bow, to cast down the poor and needy, to kill those who are upright on the path.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Their sword shall enter into their own heart. Their bows shall be broken.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Better is a little that the righteous has, than the abundance of many wicked.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For the arms of the wicked shall be broken, but Yahweh upholds the righteous.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Yahweh knows the days of the perfect. Their inheritance shall be forever.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** They shall not be disappointed in the time of evil. In the days of famine they shall be satisfied.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** But the wicked shall perish. The enemies of Yahweh shall be like the beauty of the fields. They will vanish— vanish like smoke.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The wicked borrow, and don’t pay back, but the righteous give generously.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For such as are blessed by him shall inherit the land. Those who are cursed by him shall be cut off.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** A man’s goings are established by Yahweh. He delights in his way.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Though he stumble, he shall not fall, for Yahweh holds him up with his hand.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** I have been young, and now am old, yet I have not seen the righteous forsaken, nor his children begging for bread.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** All day long he deals graciously, and lends. His seed is blessed.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Depart from evil, and do good. Live securely forever.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** For Yahweh loves justice, and doesn’t forsake his saints. They are preserved forever, but the children of the wicked shall be cut off.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** The righteous shall inherit the land, and live in it forever.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** The mouth of the righteous talks of wisdom. His tongue speaks justice.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** The law of his God is in his heart. None of his steps shall slide.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The wicked watches the righteous, and seeks to kill him.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Yahweh will not leave him in his hand, nor condemn him when he is judged.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Wait for Yahweh, and keep his way, and he will exalt you to inherit the land. When the wicked are cut off, you shall see it.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** I have seen the wicked in great power, spreading himself like a green tree in its native soil.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** But he passed away, and behold, he was not. Yes, I sought him, but he could not be found.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Mark the perfect man, and see the upright, for there is a future for the man of peace.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** As for transgressors, they shall be destroyed together. The future of the wicked shall be cut off.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** But the salvation of the righteous is from Yahweh. He is their stronghold in the time of trouble.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** Yahweh helps them, and rescues them. He rescues them from the wicked, and saves them, Because they have taken refuge in him.
