# Psalms 26 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Judge me, O Lord; for I have walked in mine integrity: I have trusted also in the Lord; therefore I shall not slide.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Examine me, O Lord, and prove me; try my reins and my heart.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For thy lovingkindness is before mine eyes: and I have walked in thy truth.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I have not sat with vain persons, neither will I go in with dissemblers.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I have hated the congregation of evil doers; and will not sit with the wicked.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I will wash mine hands in innocency: so will I compass thine altar, O Lord:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** That I may publish with the voice of thanksgiving, and tell of all thy wondrous works.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Lord, I have loved the habitation of thy house, and the place where thine honour dwelleth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Gather not my soul with sinners, nor my life with bloody men:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** In whose hands is mischief, and their right hand is full of bribes.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But as for me, I will walk in mine integrity: redeem me, and be merciful unto me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** My foot standeth in an even place: in the congregations will I bless the Lord.
