# Psalms 26 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Judge me, O Jehovah, for I have walked in mine integrity: I have trusted also in Jehovah without wavering.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Examine me, O Jehovah, and prove me; Try my heart and my mind.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For thy lovingkindness is before mine eyes; And I have walked in thy truth.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I have not sat with men of falsehood; Neither will I go in with dissemblers.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I hate the assembly of evil-doers, And will not sit with the wicked.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I will wash my hands in innocency: So will I compass thine altar, O Jehovah;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** That I may make the voice of thanksgiving to be heard, And tell of all thy wondrous works.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Jehovah, I love the habitation of thy house, And the place where thy glory dwelleth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Gather not my soul with sinners, Nor my life with men of blood;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** In whose hands is wickedness, And their right hand is full of bribes.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But as for me, I will walk in mine integrity: Redeem me, and be merciful unto me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** My foot standeth in an even place: In the congregations will I bless Jehovah.
