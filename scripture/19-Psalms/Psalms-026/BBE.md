# Psalms 26 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Lord, be my judge, for my behaviour has been upright: I have put my faith in the Lord, I am not in danger of slipping.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Put me in the scales, O Lord, so that I may be tested; let the fire make clean my thoughts and my heart.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For your mercy is before my eyes; and I have gone in the way of your good faith.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I have not taken my seat with foolish persons, and I do not go with false men.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I have been a hater of the band of wrongdoers, and I will not be seated among sinners.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I will make my hands clean from sin; so will I go round your altar, O Lord;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** That I may give out the voice of praise, and make public all the wonders which you have done.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Lord, your house has been dear to me, and the resting-place of your glory.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let not my soul be numbered among sinners, or my life among men of blood;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** In whose hands are evil designs, and whose right hands take money for judging falsely.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But as for me, I will go on in my upright ways: be my saviour, and have mercy on me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I have a safe resting-place for my feet; I will give praise to the Lord in the meetings of the people.
