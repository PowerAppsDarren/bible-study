# Psalms 26 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Judge me, Yahweh, for I have walked in my integrity. I have trusted also in Yahweh without wavering.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Examine me, Yahweh, and prove me. Try my heart and my mind.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For your loving kindness is before my eyes. I have walked in your truth.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I have not sat with deceitful men, neither will I go in with hypocrites.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I hate the assembly of evildoers, and will not sit with the wicked.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I will wash my hands in innocence, so I will go about your altar, Yahweh;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** that I may make the voice of thanksgiving to be heard, and tell of all your wondrous works.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yahweh, I love the habitation of your house, the place where your glory dwells.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Don’t gather my soul with sinners, nor my life with bloodthirsty men;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** in whose hands is wickedness, their right hand is full of bribes.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But as for me, I will walk in my integrity. Redeem me, and be merciful to me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** My foot stands in an even place. In the congregations I will bless Yahweh.
