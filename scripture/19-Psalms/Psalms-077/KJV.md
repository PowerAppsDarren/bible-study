# Psalms 77 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I cried unto God with my voice, even unto God with my voice; and he gave ear unto me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** In the day of my trouble I sought the Lord: my sore ran in the night, and ceased not: my soul refused to be comforted.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I remembered God, and was troubled: I complained, and my spirit was overwhelmed. Selah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thou holdest mine eyes waking: I am so troubled that I cannot speak.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I have considered the days of old, the years of ancient times.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I call to remembrance my song in the night: I commune with mine own heart: and my spirit made diligent search.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Will the Lord cast off for ever? and will he be favourable no more?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Is his mercy clean gone for ever? doth his promise fail for evermore?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Hath God forgotten to be gracious? hath he in anger shut up his tender mercies? Selah.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And I said, This is my infirmity: but I will remember the years of the right hand of the most High.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I will remember the works of the Lord: surely I will remember thy wonders of old.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I will meditate also of all thy work, and talk of thy doings.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thy way, O God, is in the sanctuary: who is so great a God as our God?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Thou art the God that doest wonders: thou hast declared thy strength among the people.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Thou hast with thine arm redeemed thy people, the sons of Jacob and Joseph. Selah.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The waters saw thee, O God, the waters saw thee; they were afraid: the depths also were troubled.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The clouds poured out water: the skies sent out a sound: thine arrows also went abroad.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The voice of thy thunder was in the heaven: the lightnings lightened the world: the earth trembled and shook.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Thy way is in the sea, and thy path in the great waters, and thy footsteps are not known.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Thou leddest thy people like a flock by the hand of Moses and Aaron.
