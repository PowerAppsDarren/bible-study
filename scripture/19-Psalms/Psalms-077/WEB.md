# Psalms 77 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My cry goes to God! Indeed, I cry to God for help, and for him to listen to me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** In the day of my trouble I sought the Lord. My hand was stretched out in the night, and didn’t get tired. My soul refused to be comforted.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I remember God, and I groan. I complain, and my spirit is overwhelmed. Selah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You hold my eyelids open. I am so troubled that I can’t speak.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I have considered the days of old, the years of ancient times.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I remember my song in the night. I consider in my own heart; my spirit diligently inquires:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** “Will the Lord reject us forever? Will he be favorable no more?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Has his loving kindness vanished forever? Does his promise fail for generations?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Has God forgotten to be gracious? Has he, in anger, withheld his compassion?” Selah.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Then I thought, “I will appeal to this: the years of the right hand of the Most High.”

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I will remember Yah’s deeds; for I will remember your wonders of old.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I will also meditate on all your work, and consider your doings.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Your way, God, is in the sanctuary. What god is great like God?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** You are the God who does wonders. You have made your strength known among the peoples.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** You have redeemed your people with your arm, the sons of Jacob and Joseph. Selah.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The waters saw you, God. The waters saw you, and they writhed. The depths also convulsed.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The clouds poured out water. The skies resounded with thunder. Your arrows also flashed around.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The voice of your thunder was in the whirlwind. The lightnings lit up the world. The earth trembled and shook.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Your way was through the sea; your paths through the great waters. Your footsteps were not known.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** You led your people like a flock, by the hand of Moses and Aaron.
