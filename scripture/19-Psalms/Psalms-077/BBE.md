# Psalms 77 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I was crying to God with my voice; even to God with my voice, and he gave ear to me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** In the day of my trouble, my heart was turned to the Lord: my hand was stretched out in the night without resting; my soul would not be comforted.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I will keep God in memory, with sounds of grief; my thoughts are troubled, and my spirit is overcome. (Selah.)

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You keep my eyes from sleep; I am so troubled that no words come.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My thoughts go back to the days of the past, to the years which are gone.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The memory of my song comes back to me in the night; my thoughts are moving in my heart; my spirit is searching with care.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Will the Lord put me away for ever? will he be kind no longer?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Is his mercy quite gone for ever? has his word come to nothing?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Has God put away the memory of his pity? are his mercies shut up by his wrath? (Selah.)

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And I said, It is a weight on my spirit; but I will keep in mind the years of the right hand of the Most High.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I will keep in mind the works of Jah: I will keep the memory of your wonders in the past.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I will give thought to all your work, while my mind goes over your acts of power.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Your way, O God, is holy: what god is so great as our God?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** You are the God who does works of power: you have made your strength clear to the nations.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** With your arm you have made your people free, the sons of Jacob and Joseph. (Selah.)

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The waters saw you, O God; the waters saw you, they were in fear: even the deep was troubled.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The clouds sent out water; the skies gave out a sound; truly, your arrows went far and wide.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The voice of your thunder went rolling on; the world was flaming with the light of the storm; the earth was shaking.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Your way was in the sea, and your road in the great waters; there was no knowledge of your footsteps.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** You were guiding your people like a flock, by the hand of Moses and Aaron.
