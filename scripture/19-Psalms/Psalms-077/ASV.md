# Psalms 77 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will cry unto God with my voice, Even unto God with my voice; and he will give ear unto me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** In the day of my trouble I sought the Lord: My hand was stretched out in the night, and slacked not; My soul refused to be comforted.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I remember God, and am disquieted: I complain, and my spirit is overwhelmed. [Selah

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thou holdest mine eyes watching: I am so troubled that I cannot speak.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I have considered the days of old, The years of ancient times.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I call to remembrance my song in the night: I commune with mine own heart; And my spirit maketh diligent search.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Will the Lord cast off for ever? And will he be favorable no more?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Is his lovingkindness clean gone for ever? Doth his promise fail for evermore?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Hath God forgotten to be gracious? Hath he in anger shut up his tender mercies? [Selah

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And I said, This is my infirmity; But I will rememberthe years of the right hand of the Most High.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I will make mention of the deeds of Jehovah; For I will remember thy wonders of old.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I will meditate also upon all thy work, And muse on thy doings.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thy way, O God, is in the sanctuary: Who is a great god like unto God?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Thou art the God that doest wonders: Thou hast made known thy strength among the peoples.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Thou hast with thine arm redeemed thy people, The sons of Jacob and Joseph. [Selah

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The waters saw thee, O God; The waters saw thee, they were afraid: The depths also trembled.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The clouds poured out water; The skies sent out a sound: Thine arrows also went abroad.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The voice of thy thunder was in the whirlwind; The lightnings lightened the world: The earth trembled and shook.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Thy way was in the sea, And thy paths in the great waters, And thy footsteps were not known.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Thou leddest thy people like a flock, By the hand of Moses and Aaron.
