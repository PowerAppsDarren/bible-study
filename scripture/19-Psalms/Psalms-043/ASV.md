# Psalms 43 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Judge me, O God, and plead my cause against an ungodly nation: Oh deliver me from the deceitful and unjust man.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For thou art the God of my strength; why hast thou cast me off? Why go I mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Oh send out thy light and thy truth; let them lead me: Let them bring me unto thy holy hill, And to thy tabernacles.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Then will I go unto the altar of God, Unto God my exceeding joy; And upon the harp will I praise thee, O God, my God.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Why art thou cast down, O my soul? And why art thou disquieted within me? Hope thou in God; for I shall yet praise him, Who isthe help of my countenance, and my God.
