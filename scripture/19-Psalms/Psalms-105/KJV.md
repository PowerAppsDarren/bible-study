# Psalms 105 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O give thanks unto the Lord; call upon his name: make known his deeds among the people.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Sing unto him, sing psalms unto him: talk ye of all his wondrous works.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Glory ye in his holy name: let the heart of them rejoice that seek the Lord.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Seek the Lord, and his strength: seek his face evermore.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Remember his marvellous works that he hath done; his wonders, and the judgments of his mouth;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** O ye seed of Abraham his servant, ye children of Jacob his chosen.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He is the Lord our God: his judgments are in all the earth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He hath remembered his covenant for ever, the word which he commanded to a thousand generations.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Which covenant he made with Abraham, and his oath unto Isaac;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And confirmed the same unto Jacob for a law, and to Israel for an everlasting covenant:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Saying, Unto thee will I give the land of Canaan, the lot of your inheritance:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** When they were but a few men in number; yea, very few, and strangers in it.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** When they went from one nation to another, from one kingdom to another people;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He suffered no man to do them wrong: yea, he reproved kings for their sakes;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Saying, Touch not mine anointed, and do my prophets no harm.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Moreover he called for a famine upon the land: he brake the whole staff of bread.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He sent a man before them, even Joseph, who was sold for a servant:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Whose feet they hurt with fetters: he was laid in iron:

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Until the time that his word came: the word of the Lord tried him.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The king sent and loosed him; even the ruler of the people, and let him go free.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He made him lord of his house, and ruler of all his substance:

<a id="v22"></a>**22** To bind his princes at his pleasure; and teach his senators wisdom.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Israel also came into Egypt; and Jacob sojourned in the land of Ham.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And he increased his people greatly; and made them stronger than their enemies.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** He turned their heart to hate his people, to deal subtilly with his servants.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** He sent Moses his servant; and Aaron whom he had chosen.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** They shewed his signs among them, and wonders in the land of Ham.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** He sent darkness, and made it dark; and they rebelled not against his word.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** He turned their waters into blood, and slew their fish.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Their land brought forth frogs in abundance, in the chambers of their kings.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** He spake, and there came divers sorts of flies, and lice in all their coasts.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** He gave them hail for rain, and flaming fire in their land.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** He smote their vines also and their fig trees; and brake the trees of their coasts.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** He spake, and the locusts came, and caterpillers, and that without number,

<a id="v35"></a>**35** And did eat up all the herbs in their land, and devoured the fruit of their ground.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** He smote also all the firstborn in their land, the chief of all their strength.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** He brought them forth also with silver and gold: and there was not one feeble person among their tribes.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** Egypt was glad when they departed: for the fear of them fell upon them.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** He spread a cloud for a covering; and fire to give light in the night.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** The people asked, and he brought quails, and satisfied them with the bread of heaven.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** He opened the rock, and the waters gushed out; they ran in the dry places like a river.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** For he remembered his holy promise, and Abraham his servant.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** And he brought forth his people with joy, and his chosen with gladness:

<a id="v44"></a>**44** And gave them the lands of the heathen: and they inherited the labour of the people;

<a id="v45"></a>**45** That they might observe his statutes, and keep his laws. Praise ye the Lord.
