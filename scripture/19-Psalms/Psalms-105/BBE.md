# Psalms 105 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O give praise to the Lord; give honour to his name, talking of his doings among the peoples.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let your voice be sounding in songs and melody; let all your thoughts be of the wonder of his works.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Have glory in his holy name; let the hearts of those who are searching after the Lord be glad.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Let your search be for the Lord and for his strength; let your hearts ever be turned to him.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Keep in mind the great works which he has done; his wonders, and the decisions of his mouth;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** O you seed of Abraham, his servant, you children of Jacob, his loved ones.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He is the Lord our God: he is judge of all the earth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He has kept his agreement in mind for ever, the word which he gave for a thousand generations;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The agreement which he made with Abraham, and his oath to Isaac;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And he gave it to Jacob for a law, and to Israel for an eternal agreement;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Saying, To you will I give the land of Canaan, the measured line of your heritage:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** When they were still small in number, and strange in the land;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** When they went about from one nation to another, and from one kingdom to another people.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He would not let anyone do them wrong; he even kept back kings because of them,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Saying, Put not your hand on those who have been marked with my holy oil, and do my prophets no wrong.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And he took away all food from the land, so that the people were without bread.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He sent a man before them, even Joseph, who was given as a servant for a price:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** His feet were fixed in chains; his neck was put in iron bands;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Till the time when his word came true; he was tested by the word of the Lord.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The king sent men to take off his chains; even the ruler of the people, who let him go free.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He made him lord of his house, and ruler over everything he had;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** To give his chiefs teaching at his pleasure, and so that his law-givers might get wisdom from him.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Then Israel came into Egypt, and Jacob was living in the land of Ham.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And his people were greatly increased, and became stronger than those who were against them.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Their hearts were turned to hate against his people, so that they made secret designs against them.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** He sent Moses, his servant, and Aaron, the man of his selection.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** He let his signs be seen among the people, and his wonders in the land of Ham.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** He sent black night and made it dark; and they did not go against his word.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** At his word their waters were turned to blood, and he sent death on all their fish.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Their land was full of frogs, even in the rooms of the king.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** He gave the word, and there came the dog-fly, and insects over all the land.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** He gave them ice for rain, and flaming fire in their land.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** He gave their vines and their fig-trees to destruction, and the trees of their land were broken down.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** At his word the locusts came, and young locusts more than might be numbered,

<a id="v35"></a>**35** And put an end to all the plants of their land, taking all the fruit of the earth for food.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** He put to death the first child of every family in the land, the first-fruits of their strength.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** He took his people out with silver and gold: there was not one feeble person among them.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** Egypt was glad when they went; for the fear of them had come down on them.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** A cloud was stretched over them for a cover; and he sent fire to give light in the night.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** At the people's request he sent birds, and gave them the bread of heaven for food.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** His hand made the rock open, and the waters came streaming out; they went down through the dry places like a river.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** For he kept in mind his holy word, and Abraham, his servant.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** And he took his people out with joy, the men of his selection with glad cries:

<a id="v44"></a>**44** And gave them the lands of the nations; and they took the work of the peoples for a heritage;

<a id="v45"></a>**45** So that they might keep his orders, and be true to his laws. Give praise to the Lord.
