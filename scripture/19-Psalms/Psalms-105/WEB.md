# Psalms 105 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give thanks to Yahweh! Call on his name! Make his doings known among the peoples.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Sing to him, sing praises to him! Tell of all his marvelous works.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Glory in his holy name. Let the heart of those who seek Yahweh rejoice.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Seek Yahweh and his strength. Seek his face forever more.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Remember his marvelous works that he has done; his wonders, and the judgments of his mouth,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** you seed of Abraham, his servant, you children of Jacob, his chosen ones.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He is Yahweh, our God. His judgments are in all the earth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He has remembered his covenant forever, the word which he commanded to a thousand generations,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** the covenant which he made with Abraham, his oath to Isaac,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** and confirmed the same to Jacob for a statute; to Israel for an everlasting covenant,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** saying, “To you I will give the land of Canaan, the lot of your inheritance”;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** when they were but a few men in number, yes, very few, and foreigners in it.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** They went about from nation to nation, from one kingdom to another people.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He allowed no one to do them wrong. Yes, he reproved kings for their sakes,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** “Don’t touch my anointed ones! Do my prophets no harm!”

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He called for a famine on the land. He destroyed the food supplies.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He sent a man before them. Joseph was sold for a slave.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** They bruised his feet with shackles. His neck was locked in irons,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** until the time that his word happened, and Yahweh’s word proved him true.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The king sent and freed him; even the ruler of peoples, and let him go free.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He made him lord of his house, and ruler of all of his possessions;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** to discipline his princes at his pleasure, and to teach his elders wisdom.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Israel also came into Egypt. Jacob lived in the land of Ham.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** He increased his people greatly, and made them stronger than their adversaries.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** He turned their heart to hate his people, to conspire against his servants.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** He sent Moses, his servant, and Aaron, whom he had chosen.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** They performed miracles among them, and wonders in the land of Ham.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** He sent darkness, and made it dark. They didn’t rebel against his words.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** He turned their waters into blood, and killed their fish.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Their land swarmed with frogs, even in the rooms of their kings.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** He spoke, and swarms of flies came, and lice in all their borders.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** He gave them hail for rain, with lightning in their land.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** He struck their vines and also their fig trees, and shattered the trees of their country.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** He spoke, and the locusts came, and the grasshoppers, without number,

<a id="v35"></a>**35** ate up every plant in their land; and ate up the fruit of their ground.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** He struck also all the firstborn in their land, the first fruits of all their manhood.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** He brought them out with silver and gold. There was not one feeble person among his tribes.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** Egypt was glad when they departed, for the fear of them had fallen on them.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** He spread a cloud for a covering, fire to give light in the night.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** They asked, and he brought quails, and satisfied them with the bread of the sky.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** He opened the rock, and waters gushed out. They ran as a river in the dry places.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** For he remembered his holy word, and Abraham, his servant.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** He brought his people out with joy, his chosen with singing.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** He gave them the lands of the nations. They took the labor of the peoples in possession,

<a id="v45"></a>**45** that they might keep his statutes, and observe his laws. Praise Yah!
