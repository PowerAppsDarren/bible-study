# Psalms 105 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Oh give thanks unto Jehovah, call upon his name; Make known among the peoples his doings.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Sing unto him, sing praises unto him; Talk ye of all his marvellous works.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Glory ye in his holy name: Let the heart of them rejoice that seek Jehovah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Seek ye Jehovah and his strength; Seek his face evermore.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Remember his marvellous works that he hath done, His wonders, and the judgments of his mouth,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** O ye seed of Abraham his servant, Ye children of Jacob, his chosen ones.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He is Jehovah our God: His judgments are in all the earth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He hath remembered his covenant for ever, The word which he commanded to a thousand generations,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The covenantwhich he made with Abraham, And his oath unto Isaac,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And confirmed the same unto Jacob for a statute, To Israel for an everlasting covenant,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Saying, Unto thee will I give the land of Canaan, The lot of your inheritance;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** When they were but a few men in number, Yea, very few, and sojourners in it.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And they went about from nation to nation, From one kingdom to another people.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He suffered no man to do them wrong; Yea, he reproved kings for their sakes,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Saying, Touch not mine anointed ones, And do my prophets no harm.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And he called for a famine upon the land; He brake the whole staff of bread.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He sent a man before them; Joseph was sold for a servant:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** His feet they hurt with fetters: He was laid inchains ofiron,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Until the time that his word came to pass, The word of Jehovah tried him.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The king sent and loosed him; Even the ruler of peoples, and let him go free.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He made him lord of his house, And ruler of all his substance;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** To bind his princes at his pleasure, And teach his elders wisdom.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Israel also came into Egypt; And Jacob sojourned in the land of Ham.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And he increased his people greatly, And made them stronger than their adversaries.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** He turned their heart to hate his people, To deal subtly with his servants.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** He sent Moses his servant, AndAaron whom he had chosen.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** They set among them his signs, And wonders in the land of Ham.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** He sent darkness, and made it dark; And they rebelled not against his words.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** He turned their waters into blood, And slew their fish.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Their land swarmed with frogs In the chambers of their kings.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** He spake, and there came swarms of flies, And lice in all their borders.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** He gave them hail for rain, Andflaming fire in their land.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** He smote their vines also and their fig-trees, And brake the trees of their borders.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** He spake, and the locust came, And the grasshopper, and that without number,

<a id="v35"></a>**35** And did eat up every herb in their land, And did eat up the fruit of their ground.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** He smote also all the first-born in their land, The chief of all their strength.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** And he brought them forth with silver and gold; And there was not one feeble person among his tribes.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** Egypt was glad when they departed; For the fear of them had fallen upon them.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** He spread a cloud for a covering, And fire to give light in the night.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** They asked, and he brought quails, And satisfied them with the bread of heaven.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** He opened the rock, and waters gushed out; They ran in the dry places like a river.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** For he remembered his holy word, AndAbraham his servant.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** And he brought forth his people with joy, Andhis chosen with singing.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** And he gave them the lands of the nations; And they took the labor of the peoples in possession:

<a id="v45"></a>**45** That they might keep his statutes, And observe his laws. Praise ye Jehovah.
