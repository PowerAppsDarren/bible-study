# Psalms 124 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** If it had not been Jehovah who was on our side, Let Israel now say,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** If it had not been Jehovah who was on our side, When men rose up against us;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Then they had swallowed us up alive, When their wrath was kindled against us:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Then the waters had overwhelmed us, The stream had gone over our soul;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Then the proud waters had gone over our soul.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Blessed be Jehovah, Who hath not given us as a prey to their teeth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Our soul is escaped as a bird out of the snare of the fowlers: The snare is broken, and we are escaped.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Our help is in the name of Jehovah, Who made heaven and earth.
