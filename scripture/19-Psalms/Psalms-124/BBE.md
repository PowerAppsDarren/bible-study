# Psalms 124 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** If it had not been the Lord who was on our side (let Israel now say);

<a id="v2"></a>**2** If it had not been the Lord who was on our side, when men came up against us;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They would have made a meal of us while still living, in the heat of their wrath against us:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** We would have been covered by the waters; the streams would have gone over our soul;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Yes, the waters of pride would have gone over our soul.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Praise be to the Lord, who has not let us be wounded by their teeth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Our soul has gone free like a bird out of the net of those who would take her: the net is broken, and we are free.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Our help is in the name of the Lord, the maker of heaven and earth.
