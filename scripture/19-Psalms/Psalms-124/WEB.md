# Psalms 124 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** If it had not been Yahweh who was on our side, let Israel now say,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** if it had not been Yahweh who was on our side, when men rose up against us;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** then they would have swallowed us up alive, when their wrath was kindled against us;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** then the waters would have overwhelmed us, the stream would have gone over our soul;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** then the proud waters would have gone over our soul.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Blessed be Yahweh, who has not given us as a prey to their teeth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Our soul has escaped like a bird out of the fowler’s snare. The snare is broken, and we have escaped.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Our help is in Yahweh’s name, who made heaven and earth.
