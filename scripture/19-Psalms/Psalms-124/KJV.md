# Psalms 124 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** If it had not been the Lord who was on our side, now may Israel say;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** If it had not been the Lord who was on our side, when men rose up against us:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Then they had swallowed us up quick, when their wrath was kindled against us:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Then the waters had overwhelmed us, the stream had gone over our soul:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Then the proud waters had gone over our soul.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Blessed be the Lord, who hath not given us as a prey to their teeth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Our soul is escaped as a bird out of the snare of the fowlers: the snare is broken, and we are escaped.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Our help is in the name of the Lord, who made heaven and earth.
