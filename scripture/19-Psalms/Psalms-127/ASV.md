# Psalms 127 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Except Jehovah build the house, They labor in vain that build it: Except Jehovah keep the city, The watchman waketh but in vain.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** It is vain for you to rise up early, To take rest late, To eat the bread of toil; Forso he giveth unto his beloved sleep.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Lo, children are a heritage of Jehovah; Andthe fruit of the womb ishisreward.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** As arrows in the hand of a mighty man, So are the children of youth.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Happy is the man that hath his quiver full of them: They shall not be put to shame, When they speak with their enemies in the gate.
