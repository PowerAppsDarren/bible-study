# Psalms 127 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Unless Yahweh builds the house, they labor in vain who build it. Unless Yahweh watches over the city, the watchman guards it in vain.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** It is vain for you to rise up early, to stay up late, eating the bread of toil; for he gives sleep to his loved ones.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Behold, children are a heritage of Yahweh. The fruit of the womb is his reward.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** As arrows in the hand of a mighty man, so are the children of youth.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Happy is the man who has his quiver full of them. They won’t be disappointed when they speak with their enemies in the gate.
