# Psalms 126 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** When Jehovah brought back those that returned to Zion, We were like unto them that dream.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Then was our mouth filled with laughter, And our tongue with singing: Then said they among the nations, Jehovah hath done great things for them.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Jehovah hath done great things for us, Whereofwe are glad.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Turn again our captivity, O Jehovah, As the streams in the South.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They that sow in tears shall reap in joy.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He that goeth forth and weepeth, bearing seed for sowing, Shall doubtless come again with joy, bringing his sheaves with him.
