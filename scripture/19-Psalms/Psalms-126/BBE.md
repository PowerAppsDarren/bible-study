# Psalms 126 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** When the Lord made a change in Zion's fate, we were like men in a dream.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Then our mouths were full of laughing, and our tongues gave a glad cry; they said among the nations, The Lord has done great things for them.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The Lord has done great things for us; because of which we are glad.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Let our fate be changed, O Lord, like the streams in the South.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Those who put in seed with weeping will get in the grain with cries of joy.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Though a man may go out weeping, taking his vessel of seed with him; he will come again in joy, with the corded stems of grain in his arms.
