# Psalms 126 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** When the Lord turned again the captivity of Zion, we were like them that dream.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Then was our mouth filled with laughter, and our tongue with singing: then said they among the heathen, The Lord hath done great things for them.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The Lord hath done great things for us; whereof we are glad.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Turn again our captivity, O Lord, as the streams in the south.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They that sow in tears shall reap in joy.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He that goeth forth and weepeth, bearing precious seed, shall doubtless come again with rejoicing, bringing his sheaves with him.
