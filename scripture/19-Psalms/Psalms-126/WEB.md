# Psalms 126 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** When Yahweh brought back those who returned to Zion, we were like those who dream.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Then our mouth was filled with laughter, and our tongue with singing. Then they said among the nations, “Yahweh has done great things for them.”

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yahweh has done great things for us, and we are glad.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Restore our fortunes again, Yahweh, like the streams in the Negev.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Those who sow in tears will reap in joy.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He who goes out weeping, carrying seed for sowing, will certainly come again with joy, carrying his sheaves.
