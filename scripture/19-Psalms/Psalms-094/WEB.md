# Psalms 94 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh, you God to whom vengeance belongs, you God to whom vengeance belongs, shine out.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Rise up, you judge of the earth. Pay back the proud what they deserve.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yahweh, how long will the wicked, how long will the wicked triumph?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They pour out arrogant words. All the evildoers boast.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They break your people in pieces, Yahweh, and afflict your heritage.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They kill the widow and the alien, and murder the fatherless.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They say, “Yah will not see, neither will Jacob’s God consider.”

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Consider, you senseless among the people; you fools, when will you be wise?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He who implanted the ear, won’t he hear? He who formed the eye, won’t he see?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He who disciplines the nations, won’t he punish? He who teaches man knows.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Yahweh knows the thoughts of man, that they are futile.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Blessed is the man whom you discipline, Yah, and teach out of your law;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** that you may give him rest from the days of adversity, until the pit is dug for the wicked.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For Yahweh won’t reject his people, neither will he forsake his inheritance.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For judgment will return to righteousness. All the upright in heart shall follow it.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Who will rise up for me against the wicked? Who will stand up for me against the evildoers?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Unless Yahweh had been my help, my soul would have soon lived in silence.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** When I said, “My foot is slipping!” Your loving kindness, Yahweh, held me up.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** In the multitude of my thoughts within me, your comforts delight my soul.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Shall the throne of wickedness have fellowship with you, which brings about mischief by statute?

<a id="v21"></a>**21** They gather themselves together against the soul of the righteous, and condemn the innocent blood.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But Yahweh has been my high tower, my God, the rock of my refuge.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** He has brought on them their own iniquity, and will cut them off in their own wickedness. Yahweh, our God, will cut them off.
