# Psalms 94 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Jehovah, thou God to whom vengeance belongeth, Thou God to whom vengeance belongeth, shine forth.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Lift up thyself, thou judge of the earth: Render to the proudtheir desert.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Jehovah, how long shall the wicked, How long shall the wicked triumph?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They prate, they speak arrogantly: All the workers of iniquity boast themselves.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They break in pieces thy people, O Jehovah, And afflict thy heritage.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They slay the widow and the sojourner, And murder the fatherless.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And they say, Jehovah will not see, Neither will the God of Jacob consider.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Consider, ye brutish among the people; And ye fools, when will ye be wise?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He that planted the ear, shall he not hear? He that formed the eye, shall he not see?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He that chastiseth the nations, shall not he correct, Evenhe that teacheth man knowledge?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Jehovah knoweth the thoughts of man, That they are vanity.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Blessed is the man whom thou chastenest, O Jehovah, And teachest out of thy law;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** That thou mayest give him rest from the days of adversity, Until the pit be digged for the wicked.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For Jehovah will not cast off his people, Neither will he forsake his inheritance.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For judgment shall return unto righteousness; And all the upright in heart shall follow it.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Who will rise up for me against the evil-doers? Who will stand up for me against the workers of iniquity?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Unless Jehovah had been my help, My soul had soon dwelt in silence.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** When I said, My foot slippeth; Thy lovingkindness, O Jehovah, held me up.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** In the multitude of my thoughts within me  Thy comforts delight my soul.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Shall the throne of wickedness have fellowship with thee, Which frameth mischief by statute?

<a id="v21"></a>**21** They gather themselves together against the soul of the righteous, And condemn the innocent blood.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But Jehovah hath been my high tower, And my God the rock of my refuge.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And he hath brought upon them their own iniquity, And will cut them off in their own wickedness; Jehovah our God will cut them off.
