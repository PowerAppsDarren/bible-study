# Psalms 94 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O God, in whose hands is punishment, O God of punishment, let your shining face be seen.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Be lifted up, O judge of the earth; let their reward come to the men of pride.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** How long will sinners, O Lord, how long will sinners have joy over us?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Words of pride come from their lips; all the workers of evil say great things of themselves.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Your people are crushed by them, O Lord, your heritage is troubled,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They put to death the widow and the guest, they take the lives of children who have no father;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And they say, Jah will not see it, the God of Jacob will not give thought to it.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Give your mind to my words, you who are without wisdom among the people; you foolish men, when will you be wise?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Has he by whom your ears were planted no hearing? or is he blind by whom your eyes were formed?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He who is the judge of the nations, will he not give men the reward of their acts, even he who gives knowledge to man?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The Lord has knowledge of the thoughts of man, for they are only a breath.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Happy is the man who is guided by you, O Jah, and to whom you give teaching out of your law;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** So that you may give him rest from the days of evil, till a hole is made ready for the destruction of the sinners.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The Lord will not give up his people, or take away his support from his heritage;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But decisions will again be made in righteousness; and they will be kept by all whose hearts are true.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Who will give me help against the sinners? and who will be my support against the workers of evil?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** If the Lord had not been my helper, my soul would quickly have gone down into death.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** If I say, My foot is slipping; your mercy, O Lord, is my support.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Among all my troubled thoughts, your comforts are the delight of my soul.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** What part with you has the seat of sin, which makes evil into a law?

<a id="v21"></a>**21** They are banded together against the soul of the upright, to give decisions against those who have done no wrong.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But the Lord is my safe resting-place; my God is the Rock where I am safe.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And he has made their evil designs come back on themselves, cutting them off in their sin; the Lord our God will put an end to them.
