# Psalms 86 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Bow down thine ear, O Jehovah, and answer me; For I am poor and needy.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Preserve my soul; for I am godly: O thou my God, save thy servant that trusteth in thee.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Be merciful unto me, O Lord; For unto thee do I cry all the day long.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Rejoice the soul of thy servant; For unto thee, O Lord, do I lift up my soul.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For thou, Lord, art good, and ready to forgive, And abundant in lovingkindness unto all them that call upon thee.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Give ear, O Jehovah, unto my prayer; And hearken unto the voice of my supplications.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** In the day of my trouble I will call upon thee; For thou wilt answer me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** There is none like unto thee among the gods, O Lord; Neitherare there any workslike unto thy works.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** All nations whom thou hast made shall come and worship before thee, O Lord; And they shall glorify thy name.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For thou art great, and doest wondrous things: Thou art God alone.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Teach me thy way, O Jehovah; I will walk in thy truth: Unite my heart to fear thy name.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I will praise thee, O Lord my God, with my whole heart; And I will glorify thy name for evermore.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For great is thy lovingkindness toward me; And thou hast delivered my soul from the lowest Sheol.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** O God, the proud are risen up against me, And a company of violent men have sought after my soul, And have not set thee before them.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But thou, O Lord, art a God merciful and gracious, Slow to anger, and abundant in lovingkindness and truth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Oh turn unto me, and have mercy upon me; Give thy strength unto thy servant, And save the son of thy handmaid.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Show me a token for good, That they who hate me may see it, and be put to shame, Because thou, Jehovah, hast helped me, and comforted me.
