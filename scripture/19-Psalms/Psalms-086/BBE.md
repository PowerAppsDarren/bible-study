# Psalms 86 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Let your ears be open to my voice, O Lord, and give me an answer; for I am poor and in need.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Keep my soul, for I am true to you; O my God, give salvation to your servant, whose hope is in you.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Have mercy on me, O Lord; for my cry goes up to you all the day.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Make glad the soul of your servant; for it is lifted up to you, O Lord.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You are good, O Lord, and full of forgiveness; your mercy is great to all who make their cry to you.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** O Lord, give ear to my prayer; and take note of the sound of my requests.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** In the day of my trouble I send up my cry to you; for you will give me an answer.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** There is no god like you, O Lord; there are no works like your works.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let all the nations whom you have made come and give worship to you, O Lord, giving glory to your name.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For you are great, and do great works of wonder; you only are God.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Make your way clear to me, O Lord; I will go on my way in your faith: let my heart be glad in the fear of your name.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I will give you praise, O Lord my God, with all my heart; I will give glory to your name for ever.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For your mercy to me is great; you have taken my soul up from the deep places of the underworld.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** O God, men of pride have come up against me, and the army of violent men would take my life; they have not put you before them.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But you, O Lord, are a God full of pity and forgiveness, slow to get angry, great in mercy and wisdom.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** O be turned to me and have mercy on me: give your strength to your servant, and your salvation to the son of her who is your servant.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Give me a sign for good; so that my haters may see it and be shamed; because you, Lord, have been my help and comfort.
