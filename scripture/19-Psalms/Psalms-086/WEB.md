# Psalms 86 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear, Yahweh, and answer me, for I am poor and needy.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Preserve my soul, for I am godly. You, my God, save your servant who trusts in you.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Be merciful to me, Lord, for I call to you all day long.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Bring joy to the soul of your servant, for to you, Lord, do I lift up my soul.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For you, Lord, are good, and ready to forgive; abundant in loving kindness to all those who call on you.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Hear, Yahweh, my prayer. Listen to the voice of my petitions.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** In the day of my trouble I will call on you, for you will answer me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** There is no one like you among the gods, Lord, nor any deeds like your deeds.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** All nations you have made will come and worship before you, Lord. They shall glorify your name.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For you are great, and do wondrous things. You are God alone.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Teach me your way, Yahweh. I will walk in your truth. Make my heart undivided to fear your name.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I will praise you, Lord my God, with my whole heart. I will glorify your name forever more.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For your loving kindness is great toward me. You have delivered my soul from the lowest Sheol.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** God, the proud have risen up against me. A company of violent men have sought after my soul, and they don’t hold regard for you before them.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But you, Lord, are a merciful and gracious God, slow to anger, and abundant in loving kindness and truth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Turn to me, and have mercy on me! Give your strength to your servant. Save the son of your handmaid.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Show me a sign of your goodness, that those who hate me may see it, and be shamed, because you, Yahweh, have helped me, and comforted me.
