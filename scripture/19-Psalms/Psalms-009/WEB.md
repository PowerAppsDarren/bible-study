# Psalms 9 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will give thanks to Yahweh with my whole heart. I will tell of all your marvelous works.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I will be glad and rejoice in you. I will sing praise to your name, O Most High.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When my enemies turn back, they stumble and perish in your presence.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For you have maintained my just cause. You sit on the throne judging righteously.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You have rebuked the nations. You have destroyed the wicked. You have blotted out their name forever and ever.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The enemy is overtaken by endless ruin. The very memory of the cities which you have overthrown has perished.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But Yahweh reigns forever. He has prepared his throne for judgment.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He will judge the world in righteousness. He will administer judgment to the peoples in uprightness.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Yahweh will also be a high tower for the oppressed; a high tower in times of trouble.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Those who know your name will put their trust in you, for you, Yahweh, have not forsaken those who seek you.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Sing praises to Yahweh, who dwells in Zion, and declare among the people what he has done.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For he who avenges blood remembers them. He doesn’t forget the cry of the afflicted.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Have mercy on me, Yahweh. See my affliction by those who hate me, and lift me up from the gates of death;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** that I may show all of your praise. In the gates of the daughter of Zion, I will rejoice in your salvation.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The nations have sunk down in the pit that they made. In the net which they hid, their own foot is taken.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Yahweh has made himself known. He has executed judgment. The wicked is snared by the work of his own hands. Meditation. Selah.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The wicked shall be turned back to Sheol, even all the nations that forget God.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For the needy shall not always be forgotten, nor the hope of the poor perish forever.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Arise, Yahweh! Don’t let man prevail. Let the nations be judged in your sight.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Put them in fear, Yahweh. Let the nations know that they are only men. Selah.
