# Psalms 9 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will give you praise, O Lord, with all my heart; I will make clear all the wonder of your works.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I will be glad and have delight in you: I will make a song of praise to your name, O Most High.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When my haters are turned back, they will be broken and overcome before you.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For you gave approval to my right and my cause; you were seated in your high place judging in righteousness.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You have said sharp words to the nations, you have sent destruction on the sinners, you have put an end to their name for ever and ever.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You have given their towns to destruction; the memory of them has gone; they have become waste for ever.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But the Lord is King for ever: he has made ready his high seat for judging.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And he will be the judge of the world in righteousness, giving true decisions for the peoples.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The Lord will be a high tower for those who are crushed down, a high tower in times of trouble;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And those who have knowledge of your name will put their faith in you; because you, Lord, have ever given your help to those who were waiting for you.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Make songs of praise to the Lord, whose house is in Zion: make his doings clear to the people.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** When he makes search for blood, he has them in his memory: he is not without thought for the cry of the poor.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Have mercy on me, O Lord, and see how I am troubled by my haters; let me be lifted up from the doors of death;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** So that I may make clear all your praise in the house of the daughter of Zion: I will be glad because of your salvation.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The nations have gone down into the hole which they made: in their secret net is their foot taken.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The Lord has given knowledge of himself through his judging: the evil-doer is taken in the net which his hands had made. (Higgaion. Selah.)

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The sinners and all the nations who have no memory of God will be turned into the underworld.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For the poor will not be without help; the hopes of those in need will not be crushed for ever.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Up! O Lord; let not man overcome you: let the nations be judged before you.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Put them in fear, O Lord, so that the nations may see that they are only men. (Selah.)
