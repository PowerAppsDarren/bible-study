# Psalms 9 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I will praise thee, O Lord, with my whole heart; I will shew forth all thy marvellous works.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I will be glad and rejoice in thee: I will sing praise to thy name, O thou most High.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When mine enemies are turned back, they shall fall and perish at thy presence.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For thou hast maintained my right and my cause; thou satest in the throne judging right.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thou hast rebuked the heathen, thou hast destroyed the wicked, thou hast put out their name for ever and ever.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** O thou enemy, destructions are come to a perpetual end: and thou hast destroyed cities; their memorial is perished with them.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But the Lord shall endure for ever: he hath prepared his throne for judgment.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And he shall judge the world in righteousness, he shall minister judgment to the people in uprightness.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The Lord also will be a refuge for the oppressed, a refuge in times of trouble.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And they that know thy name will put their trust in thee: for thou, Lord, hast not forsaken them that seek thee.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Sing praises to the Lord, which dwelleth in Zion: declare among the people his doings.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** When he maketh inquisition for blood, he remembereth them: he forgetteth not the cry of the humble.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Have mercy upon me, O Lord; consider my trouble which I suffer of them that hate me, thou that liftest me up from the gates of death:

<a id="v14"></a>**14** That I may shew forth all thy praise in the gates of the daughter of Zion: I will rejoice in thy salvation.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The heathen are sunk down in the pit that they made: in the net which they hid is their own foot taken.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The Lord is known by the judgment which he executeth: the wicked is snared in the work of his own hands. Higgaion. Selah.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The wicked shall be turned into hell, and all the nations that forget God.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For the needy shall not alway be forgotten: the expectation of the poor shall not perish for ever.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Arise, O Lord; let not man prevail: let the heathen be judged in thy sight.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Put them in fear, O Lord: that the nations may know themselves to be but men. Selah.
