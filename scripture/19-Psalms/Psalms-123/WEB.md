# Psalms 123 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** To you I do lift up my eyes, you who sit in the heavens.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Behold, as the eyes of servants look to the hand of their master, as the eyes of a maid to the hand of her mistress; so our eyes look to Yahweh, our God, until he has mercy on us.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Have mercy on us, Yahweh, have mercy on us, for we have endured much contempt.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Our soul is exceedingly filled with the scoffing of those who are at ease, with the contempt of the proud.
