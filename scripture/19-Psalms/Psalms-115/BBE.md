# Psalms 115 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Not to us, O Lord, not to us, but to your name let glory be given, because of your mercy and your unchanging faith.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Why may the nations say, Where is now their God?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But our God is in heaven: he has done whatever was pleasing to him.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their images are silver and gold, the work of men's hands.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They have mouths, but no voice; they have eyes, but they see not;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They have ears, but no hearing; they have noses, but no sense of smell;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They have hands without feeling, and feet without power of walking; and no sound comes from their throat.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Those who make them are like them; and so is everyone who puts his faith in them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** O Israel, have faith in the Lord: he is their help and their breastplate.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** O house of Aaron, have faith in the Lord: he is their help and their breastplate.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** You worshippers of the Lord, have faith in the Lord: he is their help and their breastplate.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The Lord has kept us in mind and will give us his blessing; he will send blessings on the house of Israel and on the house of Aaron.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He will send blessings on the worshippers of the Lord, on the small and on the great.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** May the Lord give you and your children still greater increase.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** May you have the blessing of the Lord, who made heaven and earth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The heavens are the Lord's; but the earth he has given to the children of men.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The dead do not give praise to the Lord; or those who go down to the underworld.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But we will give praise to the Lord now and for ever. Praise be to the Lord.
