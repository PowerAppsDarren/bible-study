# Psalms 115 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Not unto us, O Jehovah, not unto us, But unto thy name give glory, For thy lovingkindness, and for thy truth’s sake.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Wherefore should the nations say, Where is now their God?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But our God is in the heavens: He hath done whatsoever he pleased.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their idols are silver and gold, The work of men’s hands.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They have mouths, but they speak not; Eyes have they, but they see not;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They have ears, but they hear not; Noses have they, but they smell not;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They have hands, but they handle not; Feet have they, but they walk not; Neither speak they through their throat.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They that make them shall be like unto them; Yea, every one that trusteth in them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** O Israel, trust thou in Jehovah: He is their help and their shield.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** O house of Aaron, trust ye in Jehovah: He is their help and their shield.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Ye that fear Jehovah, trust in Jehovah: He is their help and their shield.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Jehovah hath been mindful of us; he will bless us: He will bless the house of Israel; He will bless the house of Aaron.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He will bless them that fear Jehovah, Both small and great.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Jehovah increase you more and more, You and your children.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Blessed are ye of Jehovah, Who made heaven and earth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The heavens are the heavens of Jehovah; But the earth hath he given to the children of men.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The dead praise not Jehovah, Neither any that go down into silence;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But we will bless Jehovah From this time forth and for evermore. Praise ye Jehovah.
