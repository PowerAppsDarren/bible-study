# Psalms 115 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Not unto us, O Lord, not unto us, but unto thy name give glory, for thy mercy, and for thy truth’s sake.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Wherefore should the heathen say, Where is now their God?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But our God is in the heavens: he hath done whatsoever he hath pleased.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their idols are silver and gold, the work of men’s hands.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They have mouths, but they speak not: eyes have they, but they see not:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They have ears, but they hear not: noses have they, but they smell not:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They have hands, but they handle not: feet have they, but they walk not: neither speak they through their throat.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They that make them are like unto them; so is every one that trusteth in them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** O Israel, trust thou in the Lord: he is their help and their shield.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** O house of Aaron, trust in the Lord: he is their help and their shield.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Ye that fear the Lord, trust in the Lord: he is their help and their shield.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The Lord hath been mindful of us: he will bless us; he will bless the house of Israel; he will bless the house of Aaron.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He will bless them that fear the Lord, both small and great.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The Lord shall increase you more and more, you and your children.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Ye are blessed of the Lord which made heaven and earth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The heaven, even the heavens, are the Lord’s: but the earth hath he given to the children of men.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The dead praise not the Lord, neither any that go down into silence.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But we will bless the Lord from this time forth and for evermore. Praise the Lord.
