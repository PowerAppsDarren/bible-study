# Psalms 115 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Not to us, Yahweh, not to us, but to your name give glory, for your loving kindness, and for your truth’s sake.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Why should the nations say, “Where is their God, now?”

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But our God is in the heavens. He does whatever he pleases.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their idols are silver and gold, the work of men’s hands.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They have mouths, but they don’t speak. They have eyes, but they don’t see.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They have ears, but they don’t hear. They have noses, but they don’t smell.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They have hands, but they don’t feel. They have feet, but they don’t walk, neither do they speak through their throat.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Those who make them will be like them; yes, everyone who trusts in them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Israel, trust in Yahweh! He is their help and their shield.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** House of Aaron, trust in Yahweh! He is their help and their shield.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** You who fear Yahweh, trust in Yahweh! He is their help and their shield.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yahweh remembers us. He will bless us. He will bless the house of Israel. He will bless the house of Aaron.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He will bless those who fear Yahweh, both small and great.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** May Yahweh increase you more and more, you and your children.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Blessed are you by Yahweh, who made heaven and earth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The heavens are the heavens of Yahweh; but the earth has he given to the children of men.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The dead don’t praise Yah, neither any who go down into silence;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But we will bless Yah, from this time forward and forever more. Praise Yah!
