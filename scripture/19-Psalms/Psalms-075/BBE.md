# Psalms 75 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** To you, O God, we give praise, to you we give praise: and those who give honour to your name make clear your works of power.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** When the right time has come, I will be the judge in righteousness.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When the earth and all its people become feeble, I am the support of its pillars. (Selah.)

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I say to the men of pride, Let your pride be gone: and to the sinners, Let not your horn be lifted up.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let not your horn be lifted up: let no more words of pride come from your outstretched necks.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For honour does not come from the east, or from the west, or uplifting from the south;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But God is the judge, putting down one, and lifting up another.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For in the hand of the Lord is a cup, and the wine is red; it is well mixed, overflowing from his hand: he will make all the sinners of the earth take of it, even to the last drop.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But I will ever be full of joy, making songs of praise to the God of Jacob.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** By him will all the horns of the sinners be cut off; but the horns of the upright will be lifted up.
