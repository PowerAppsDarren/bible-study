# Psalms 75 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Unto thee, O God, do we give thanks, unto thee do we give thanks: for that thy name is near thy wondrous works declare.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** When I shall receive the congregation I will judge uprightly.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The earth and all the inhabitants thereof are dissolved: I bear up the pillars of it. Selah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I said unto the fools, Deal not foolishly: and to the wicked, Lift not up the horn:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Lift not up your horn on high: speak not with a stiff neck.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For promotion cometh neither from the east, nor from the west, nor from the south.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But God is the judge: he putteth down one, and setteth up another.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For in the hand of the Lord there is a cup, and the wine is red; it is full of mixture; and he poureth out of the same: but the dregs thereof, all the wicked of the earth shall wring them out, and drink them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But I will declare for ever; I will sing praises to the God of Jacob.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All the horns of the wicked also will I cut off; but the horns of the righteous shall be exalted.
