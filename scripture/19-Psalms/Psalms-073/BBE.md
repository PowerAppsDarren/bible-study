# Psalms 73 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Truly, God is good to Israel, even to such as are clean in heart.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** But as for me, my feet had almost gone from under me; I was near to slipping;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Because of my envy of the men of pride, when I saw the well-being of the wrongdoers.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For they have no pain; their bodies are fat and strong.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They are not in trouble as others are; they have no part in the unhappy fate of men.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For this reason pride is round them like a chain; they are clothed with violent behaviour as with a robe.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Their eyes are bursting with fat; they have more than their heart's desire.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Their thoughts are deep with evil designs; their talk from their seats of power is of cruel acts.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Their mouth goes up to heaven; their tongues go walking through the earth.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For this reason they are full of bread; and water is ever flowing for them.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And they say, How will the Lord see this? is there knowledge in the Most High?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Truly, such are the sinners; they do well at all times, and their wealth is increased.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** As for me, I have made my heart clean to no purpose, washing my hands in righteousness;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For I have been troubled all the day; every morning have I undergone punishment.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** If I would make clear what it is like, I would say, You are false to the generation of your children.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** When my thoughts were turned to see the reason of this, it was a weariness in my eyes;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Till I went into God's holy place, and saw the end of the evil-doers.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** You put their feet where there was danger of slipping, so that they go down into destruction.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** How suddenly are they wasted! fears are the cause of their destruction.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** As a dream when one is awake, they are ended; they are like an image gone out of mind when sleep is over.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** My heart was made bitter, and I was pained by the bite of grief:

<a id="v22"></a>**22** As for me, I was foolish, and without knowledge; I was like a beast before you.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** But still I am ever with you; you have taken me by my right hand.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Your wisdom will be my guide, and later you will put me in a place of honour.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Whom have I in heaven but you? and having you I have no desire for anything on earth.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** My flesh and my heart are wasting away: but God is the Rock of my heart and my eternal heritage.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** For those who are far away from you will come to destruction: you will put an end to all those who have not kept faith with you.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** But it is good for me to come near to God: I have put my faith in the Lord God, so that I may make clear all his works.
