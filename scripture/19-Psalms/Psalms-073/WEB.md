# Psalms 73 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Surely God is good to Israel, to those who are pure in heart.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** But as for me, my feet were almost gone. My steps had nearly slipped.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For I was envious of the arrogant, when I saw the prosperity of the wicked.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For there are no struggles in their death, but their strength is firm.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They are free from burdens of men, neither are they plagued like other men.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Therefore pride is like a chain around their neck. Violence covers them like a garment.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Their eyes bulge with fat. Their minds pass the limits of conceit.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They scoff and speak with malice. In arrogance, they threaten oppression.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** They have set their mouth in the heavens. Their tongue walks through the earth.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Therefore their people return to them, and they drink up waters of abundance.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They say, “How does God know? Is there knowledge in the Most High?”

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Behold, these are the wicked. Being always at ease, they increase in riches.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Surely in vain I have cleansed my heart, and washed my hands in innocence,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For all day long have I been plagued, and punished every morning.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** If I had said, “I will speak thus”; behold, I would have betrayed the generation of your children.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** When I tried to understand this, it was too painful for me;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Until I entered God’s sanctuary, and considered their latter end.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Surely you set them in slippery places. You throw them down to destruction.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** How they are suddenly destroyed! They are completely swept away with terrors.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** As a dream when one wakes up, so, Lord, when you awake, you will despise their fantasies.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For my soul was grieved. I was embittered in my heart.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** I was so senseless and ignorant. I was a brute beast before you.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Nevertheless, I am continually with you. You have held my right hand.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** You will guide me with your counsel, and afterward receive me to glory.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Whom do I have in heaven? There is no one on earth whom I desire besides you.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** My flesh and my heart fails, but God is the strength of my heart and my portion forever.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** For, behold, those who are far from you shall perish. You have destroyed all those who are unfaithful to you.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** But it is good for me to come close to God. I have made the Lord Yahweh my refuge, that I may tell of all your works.
