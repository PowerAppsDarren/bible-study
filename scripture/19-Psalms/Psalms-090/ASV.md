# Psalms 90 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Lord, thou hast been our dwelling-place In all generations.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Before the mountains were brought forth, Or ever thou hadst formed the earth and the world, Even from everlasting to everlasting, thou art God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thou turnest man to destruction, And sayest, Return, ye children of men.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For a thousand years in thy sight Are but as yesterday when it is past, And as a watch in the night.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thou carriest them away as with a flood; they are as a sleep: In the morning they are like grass which groweth up.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** In the morning it flourisheth, and groweth up; In the evening it is cut down, and withereth.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For we are consumed in thine anger, And in thy wrath are we troubled.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Thou hast set our iniquities before thee, Our secret sins in the light of thy countenance.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For all our days are passed away in thy wrath: We bring our years to an end as a sigh.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The days of our years are threescore years and ten, Or even by reason of strength fourscore years; Yet is their pride but labor and sorrow; For it is soon gone, and we fly away.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Who knoweth the power of thine anger, And thy wrath according to the fear that is due unto thee?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** So teach us to number our days, That we may get us a heart of wisdom.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Return, O Jehovah; how long? And let it repent thee concerning thy servants.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Oh satisfy us in the morning with thy lovingkindness, That we may rejoice and be glad all our days.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Make us glad according to the days wherein thou hast afflicted us, And the years wherein we have seen evil.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Let thy work appear unto thy servants, And thy glory upon their children.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And let the favor of the Lord our God be upon us; And establish thou the work of our hands upon us; Yea, the work of our hands establish thou it.
