# Psalms 90 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Lord, you have been our resting-place in all generations.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Before the mountains were made, before you had given birth to the earth and the world, before time was, and for ever, you are God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You send man back to his dust; and say, Go back, you children of men.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For to you a thousand years are no more than yesterday when it is past, and like a watch in the night.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** ...

<a id="v6"></a>**6** In the morning it is green; in the evening it is cut down, and becomes dry.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** We are burned up by the heat of your passion, and troubled by your wrath.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** You have put our evil doings before you, our secret sins in the light of your face.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For all our days have gone by in your wrath; our years come to an end like a breath.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The measure of our life is seventy years; and if through strength it may be eighty years, its pride is only trouble and sorrow, for it comes to an end and we are quickly gone.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Who has knowledge of the power of your wrath, or who takes note of the weight of your passion?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** So give us knowledge of the number of our days, that we may get a heart of wisdom.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Come back, O Lord; how long? let your purpose for your servants be changed.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** In the morning give us your mercy in full measure; so that we may have joy and delight all our days.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Make us glad in reward for the days of our sorrow, and for the years in which we have seen evil.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Make your work clear to your servants, and your glory to their children.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Let the pleasure of the Lord our God be on us: O Lord, give strength to the work of our hands.
