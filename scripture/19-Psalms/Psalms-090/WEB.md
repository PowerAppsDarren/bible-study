# Psalms 90 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Lord, you have been our dwelling place for all generations.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Before the mountains were born, before you had formed the earth and the world, even from everlasting to everlasting, you are God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You turn man to destruction, saying, “Return, you children of men.”

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For a thousand years in your sight are just like yesterday when it is past, like a watch in the night.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You sweep them away as they sleep. In the morning they sprout like new grass.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** In the morning it sprouts and springs up. By evening, it is withered and dry.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For we are consumed in your anger. We are troubled in your wrath.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** You have set our iniquities before you, our secret sins in the light of your presence.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For all our days have passed away in your wrath. We bring our years to an end as a sigh.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The days of our years are seventy, or even by reason of strength eighty years; yet their pride is but labor and sorrow, for it passes quickly, and we fly away.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Who knows the power of your anger, your wrath according to the fear that is due to you?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** So teach us to number our days, that we may gain a heart of wisdom.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Relent, Yahweh! How long? Have compassion on your servants!

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Satisfy us in the morning with your loving kindness, that we may rejoice and be glad all our days.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Make us glad for as many days as you have afflicted us, for as many years as we have seen evil.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Let your work appear to your servants; your glory to their children.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Let the favor of the Lord our God be on us; establish the work of our hands for us; yes, establish the work of our hands.
