# Psalms 14 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The fool hath said in his heart, There is no God. They are corrupt, they have done abominable works, there is none that doeth good.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The Lord looked down from heaven upon the children of men, to see if there were any that did understand, and seek God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They are all gone aside, they are all together become filthy: there is none that doeth good, no, not one.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Have all the workers of iniquity no knowledge? who eat up my people as they eat bread, and call not upon the Lord.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** There were they in great fear: for God is in the generation of the righteous.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Ye have shamed the counsel of the poor, because the Lord is his refuge.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Oh that the salvation of Israel were come out of Zion! when the Lord bringeth back the captivity of his people, Jacob shall rejoice, and Israel shall be glad.
