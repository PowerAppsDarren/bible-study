# Psalms 14 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The foolish man has said in his heart, God will not do anything. They are unclean, they have done evil works; there is not one who does good.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The Lord was looking down from heaven on the children of men, to see if there were any who had wisdom, searching after God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They have all gone out of the way together; they are unclean, there is not one who does good, no, not one.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Have all the workers of evil no knowledge? they take my people for food as they would take bread; they make no prayer to the Lord.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Then were they in great fear: for God is in the generation of the upright.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You have put to shame the thoughts of the poor, but the Lord is his support.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** May the salvation of Israel come out of Zion! when the fate of his people is changed by the Lord, Jacob will have joy and Israel will be glad.
