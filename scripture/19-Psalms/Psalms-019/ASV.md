# Psalms 19 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The heavens declare the glory of God; And the firmament showeth his handiwork.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Day unto day uttereth speech, And night unto night showeth knowledge.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** There is no speech nor language; Their voice is not heard.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their line is gone out through all the earth, And their words to the end of the world. In them hath he set a tabernacle for the sun,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Which is as a bridegroom coming out of his chamber, And rejoiceth as a strong man to run his course.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** His going forth is from the end of the heavens, And his circuit unto the ends of it; And there is nothing hid from the heat thereof.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The law of Jehovah is perfect, restoring the soul: The testimony of Jehovah is sure, making wise the simple.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The precepts of Jehovah are right, rejoicing the heart: The commandment of Jehovah is pure, enlightening the eyes.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The fear of Jehovah is clean, enduring for ever: The ordinances of Jehovah are true, and righteous altogether.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** More to be desired are they than gold, yea, than much fine gold; Sweeter also than honey and the droppings of the honeycomb.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Moreover by them is thy servant warned: In keeping them there is great reward.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Who can discern his errors? Clear thou me from hidden faults.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Keep back thy servant also from presumptuous sins; Let them not have dominion over me: Then shall I be upright, And I shall be clear from great transgression.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Let the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart Be acceptable in thy sight, O Jehovah, my rock, and my redeemer.
