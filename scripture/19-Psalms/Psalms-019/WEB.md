# Psalms 19 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The heavens declare the glory of God. The expanse shows his handiwork.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Day after day they pour out speech, and night after night they display knowledge.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** There is no speech nor language, where their voice is not heard.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their voice has gone out through all the earth, their words to the end of the world. In them he has set a tent for the sun,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** which is as a bridegroom coming out of his room, like a strong man rejoicing to run his course.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** His going out is from the end of the heavens, his circuit to its ends; There is nothing hidden from its heat.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Yahweh’s law is perfect, restoring the soul. Yahweh’s testimony is sure, making wise the simple.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yahweh’s precepts are right, rejoicing the heart. Yahweh’s commandment is pure, enlightening the eyes.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The fear of Yahweh is clean, enduring forever. Yahweh’s ordinances are true, and righteous altogether.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** More to be desired are they than gold, yes, than much fine gold; sweeter also than honey and the extract of the honeycomb.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Moreover by them is your servant warned. In keeping them there is great reward.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Who can discern his errors? Forgive me from hidden errors.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Keep back your servant also from presumptuous sins. Let them not have dominion over me. Then I will be upright. I will be blameless and innocent of great transgression.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Let the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart be acceptable in your sight, Yahweh, my rock, and my redeemer.
