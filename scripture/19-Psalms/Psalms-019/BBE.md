# Psalms 19 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The heavens are sounding the glory of God; the arch of the sky makes clear the work of his hands.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Day after day it sends out its word, and night after night it gives knowledge.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** There are no words or language; their voice makes no sound.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their line has gone out through all the earth, and their words to the end of the world. In them has he put a tent for the sun,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Who is like a newly married man coming from his bride-tent, and is glad like a strong runner starting on his way.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** His going out is from the end of the heaven, and his circle to the ends of it; there is nothing which is not open to his heat.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The law of the Lord is good, giving new life to the soul: the witness of the Lord is certain, giving wisdom to the foolish.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The orders of the Lord are right, making glad the heart: the rule of the Lord is holy, giving light to the eyes.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The fear of the Lord is clean, and has no end; the decisions of the Lord are true and full of righteousness.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** More to be desired are they than gold, even than much shining gold; sweeter than the dropping honey.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** By them is your servant made conscious of danger, and in keeping them there is great reward.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Who has full knowledge of his errors? make me clean from secret evil.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Keep your servant back from sins of pride; let them not have rule over me: then will I be upright and free from great sin.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Let the words of my mouth and the thoughts of my heart be pleasing in your eyes, O Lord, my strength and my salvation.
