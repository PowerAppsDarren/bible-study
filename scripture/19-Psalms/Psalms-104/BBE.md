# Psalms 104 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give praise to the Lord, O my soul. O Lord my God, you are very great; you are robed with honour and power.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You are clothed with light as with a robe; stretching out the heavens like a curtain:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The arch of your house is based on the waters; you make the clouds your carriage; you go on the wings of the wind:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He makes winds his angels, and flames of fire his servants.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He has made the earth strong on its bases, so that it may not be moved for ever and ever;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Covering it with the sea as with a robe: the waters were high over the mountains;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** At the voice of your word they went in flight; at the sound of your thunder they went away in fear;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The mountains came up and the valleys went down into the place which you had made ready for them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** You made a limit over which they might not go, so that the earth would never again be covered by them.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** You sent the springs into the valleys; they are flowing between the hills.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They give drink to every beast of the field; the mountain asses come to them for water.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The birds of the air have their resting-places by them, and make their song among the branches.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He sends down rain from his store-houses on the hills: the earth is full of the fruit of his works.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He makes the grass come up for the cattle, and plants for the use of man; so that bread may come out of the earth;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And wine to make glad the heart of man, and oil to make his face shining, and bread giving strength to his heart.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The trees of the Lord are full of growth, the cedars of Lebanon of his planting;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Where the birds have their resting-places; as for the stork, the tall trees are her house.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The high hills are a safe place for the mountain goats, and the rocks for the small beasts.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He made the moon for a sign of the divisions of the year; teaching the sun the time of its going down.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** When you make it dark, it is night, when all the beasts of the woods come quietly out of their secret places.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The young lions go thundering after their food; searching for their meat from God.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The sun comes up, and they come together, and go back to their secret places to take their rest.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Man goes out to his work, and to his business, till the evening.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** O Lord, how great is the number of your works! in wisdom you have made them all; the earth is full of the things you have made.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** There is the great, wide sea, where there are living things, great and small, more than may be numbered.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** There go the ships; there is that great beast, which you have made as a plaything.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** All of them are waiting for you, to give them their food in its time.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** They take what you give them; they are full of the good things which come from your open hand.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** If your face is veiled, they are troubled; when you take away their breath, they come to an end, and go back to the dust.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** If you send out your spirit, they are given life; you make new the face of the earth.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Let the glory of the Lord be for ever; let the Lord have joy in his works:

<a id="v32"></a>**32** At whose look the earth is shaking; at whose touch the mountains send out smoke.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** I will make songs to the Lord all my life; I will make melody to my God while I have my being.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Let my thoughts be sweet to him: I will be glad in the Lord.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Let sinners be cut off from the earth, and let all evil-doers come to an end. Give praise to the Lord, O my soul. Give praise to the Lord.
