# Psalms 104 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Bless Yahweh, my soul. Yahweh, my God, you are very great. You are clothed with honor and majesty.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He covers himself with light as with a garment. He stretches out the heavens like a curtain.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He lays the beams of his rooms in the waters. He makes the clouds his chariot. He walks on the wings of the wind.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He makes his messengers winds; his servants flames of fire.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He laid the foundations of the earth, that it should not be moved forever.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You covered it with the deep as with a cloak. The waters stood above the mountains.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** At your rebuke they fled. At the voice of your thunder they hurried away.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The mountains rose, the valleys sank down, to the place which you had assigned to them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** You have set a boundary that they may not pass over; that they don’t turn again to cover the earth.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He sends springs into the valleys. They run among the mountains.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They give drink to every animal of the field. The wild donkeys quench their thirst.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The birds of the sky nest by them. They sing among the branches.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He waters the mountains from his rooms. The earth is filled with the fruit of your works.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He causes the grass to grow for the livestock, and plants for man to cultivate, that he may produce food out of the earth:

<a id="v15"></a>**15** wine that makes glad the heart of man, oil to make his face to shine, and bread that strengthens man’s heart.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Yahweh’s trees are well watered, the cedars of Lebanon, which he has planted;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** where the birds make their nests. The stork makes its home in the fir trees.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The high mountains are for the wild goats. The rocks are a refuge for the rock badgers.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He appointed the moon for seasons. The sun knows when to set.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** You make darkness, and it is night, in which all the animals of the forest prowl.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The young lions roar after their prey, and seek their food from God.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The sun rises, and they steal away, and lay down in their dens.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Man goes out to his work, to his labor until the evening.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Yahweh, how many are your works! In wisdom have you made them all. The earth is full of your riches.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** There is the sea, great and wide, in which are innumerable living things, both small and large animals.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** There the ships go, and leviathan, whom you formed to play there.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** These all wait for you, that you may give them their food in due season.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** You give to them; they gather. You open your hand; they are satisfied with good.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** You hide your face: they are troubled; you take away their breath: they die, and return to the dust.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** You send out your Spirit and they are created. You renew the face of the ground.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Let Yahweh’s glory endure forever. Let Yahweh rejoice in his works.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** He looks at the earth, and it trembles. He touches the mountains, and they smoke.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** I will sing to Yahweh as long as I live. I will sing praise to my God while I have any being.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Let your meditation be sweet to him. I will rejoice in Yahweh.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Let sinners be consumed out of the earth. Let the wicked be no more. Bless Yahweh, my soul. Praise Yah!
