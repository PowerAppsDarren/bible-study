# Psalms 52 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Why boastest thou thyself in mischief, O mighty man? The lovingkindness of God endureth continually.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Thy tongue deviseth very wickedness, Like a sharp razor, working deceitfully.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thou lovest evil more than good, And lying rather than to speak righteousness. [Selah

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thou lovest all devouring words, O thou deceitful tongue.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** God will likewise destroy thee for ever; He will take thee up, and pluck thee out of thy tent, And root thee out of the land of the living. [Selah

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The righteous also shall see it, and fear, And shall laugh at him, saying,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Lo, this is the man that made not God his strength, But trusted in the abundance of his riches, And strengthened himself in his wickedness.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But as for me, I am like a green olive-tree in the house of God: I trust in the lovingkindness of God for ever and ever.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will give thee thanks for ever, because thou hast done it; And I will hope in thy name, for it is good, in the presence of thy saints.
