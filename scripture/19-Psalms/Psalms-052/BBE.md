# Psalms 52 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Why do you take pride in wrongdoing, lifting yourself up against the upright man all the day?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Purposing destruction, using deceit; your tongue is like a sharp blade.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You have more love for evil than for good, for deceit than for works of righteousness. (Selah.)

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Destruction is in all your words, O false tongue.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But God will put an end to you for ever; driving you out from your tent, uprooting you from the land of the living. (Selah.)

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The upright will see it with fear, and will say, laughing at you:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** See, this is the man who did not make God his strength, but had faith in his goods and his property, and made himself strong in his wealth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But I am like a branching olive-tree in the house of God; I have put my faith in his mercy for ever and ever.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will give you praise without end for what you have done; I will give honour to your name before your saints, for it is good.
