# Psalms 141 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Jehovah, I have called upon thee; make haste unto me: Give ear unto my voice, when I call unto thee.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let my prayer be set forth as incense before thee; The lifting up of my hands as the evening sacrifice.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Set a watch, O Jehovah, before my mouth; Keep the door of my lips.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Incline not my heart to any evil thing, To practise deeds of wickedness With men that work iniquity: And let me not eat of their dainties.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let the righteous smite me, it shall bea kindness; And let him reprove me, it shall be asoil upon the head; Let not my head refuse it: For even in their wickedness shall my prayer continue.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Their judges are thrown down by the sides of the rock; And they shall hear my words; for they are sweet.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** As when one ploweth and cleaveth the earth, Our bones are scattered at the mouth of Sheol.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For mine eyes are unto thee, O Jehovah the Lord: In thee do I take refuge; leave not my soul destitute.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Keep me from the snare which they have laid for me, And from the gins of the workers of iniquity.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Let the wicked fall into their own nets, Whilst that I withal escape.
