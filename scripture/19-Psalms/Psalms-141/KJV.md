# Psalms 141 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Lord, I cry unto thee: make haste unto me; give ear unto my voice, when I cry unto thee.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let my prayer be set forth before thee as incense; and the lifting up of my hands as the evening sacrifice.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Set a watch, O Lord, before my mouth; keep the door of my lips.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Incline not my heart to any evil thing, to practise wicked works with men that work iniquity: and let me not eat of their dainties.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let the righteous smite me; it shall be a kindness: and let him reprove me; it shall be an excellent oil, which shall not break my head: for yet my prayer also shall be in their calamities.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** When their judges are overthrown in stony places, they shall hear my words; for they are sweet.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Our bones are scattered at the grave’s mouth, as when one cutteth and cleaveth wood upon the earth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But mine eyes are unto thee, O God the Lord: in thee is my trust; leave not my soul destitute.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Keep me from the snares which they have laid for me, and the gins of the workers of iniquity.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Let the wicked fall into their own nets, whilst that I withal escape.
