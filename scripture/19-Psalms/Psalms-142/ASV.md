# Psalms 142 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I cry with my voice unto Jehovah; With my voice unto Jehovah do I make supplication.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I pour out my complaint before him; I show before him my trouble.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When my spirit was overwhelmed within me, Thou knewest my path. In the way wherein I walk Have they hidden a snare for me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Look onmy right hand, and see; For there is no man that knoweth me: Refuge hath failed me; No man careth for my soul.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I cried unto thee, O Jehovah; I said, Thou art my refuge, My portion in the land of the living.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Attend unto my cry; For I am brought very low: Deliver me from my persecutors; For they are stronger than I.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Bring my soul out of prison, That I may give thanks unto thy name: The righteous shall compass me about; For thou wilt deal bountifully with me.
