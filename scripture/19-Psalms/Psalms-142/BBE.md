# Psalms 142 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The sound of my cry went up to the Lord; with my voice I made my prayer for grace to the Lord.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I put all my sorrows before him; and made clear to him all my trouble.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When my spirit is overcome, your eyes are on my goings; nets have been secretly placed in the way in which I go.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Looking to my right side, I saw no man who was my friend: I had no safe place; no one had any care for my soul.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I have made my cry to you, O Lord; I have said, You are my safe place, and my heritage in the land of the living.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Give ear to my cry, for I am made very low: take me out of the hands of my haters, for they are stronger than I.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Take my soul out of prison, so that I may give praise to your name: the upright will give praise because of me; for you have given me a full reward.
