# Psalms 142 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I cry with my voice to Yahweh. With my voice, I ask Yahweh for mercy.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I pour out my complaint before him. I tell him my troubles.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When my spirit was overwhelmed within me, you knew my route. On the path in which I walk, they have hidden a snare for me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Look on my right, and see; for there is no one who is concerned for me. Refuge has fled from me. No one cares for my soul.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I cried to you, Yahweh. I said, “You are my refuge, my portion in the land of the living.”

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Listen to my cry, for I am in desperate need. deliver me from my persecutors, For they are stronger than me.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Bring my soul out of prison, that I may give thanks to your name. The righteous will surround me, for you will be good to me.
