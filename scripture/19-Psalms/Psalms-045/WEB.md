# Psalms 45 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My heart overflows with a noble theme. I recite my verses for the king. My tongue is like the pen of a skillful writer.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You are the most excellent of the sons of men. Grace has anointed your lips, therefore God has blessed you forever.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Strap your sword on your thigh, mighty one: your splendor and your majesty.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** In your majesty ride on victoriously on behalf of truth, humility, and righteousness. Let your right hand display awesome deeds.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Your arrows are sharp. The nations fall under you, with arrows in the heart of the king’s enemies.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Your throne, God, is forever and ever. A scepter of equity is the scepter of your kingdom.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You have loved righteousness, and hated wickedness. Therefore God, your God, has anointed you with the oil of gladness above your fellows.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** All your garments smell like myrrh, aloes, and cassia. Out of ivory palaces stringed instruments have made you glad.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Kings’ daughters are among your honorable women. At your right hand the queen stands in gold of Ophir.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Listen, daughter, consider, and turn your ear. Forget your own people, and also your father’s house.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** So the king will desire your beauty, honor him, for he is your lord.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The daughter of Tyre comes with a gift. The rich among the people entreat your favor.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The princess inside is all glorious. Her clothing is interwoven with gold.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** She shall be led to the king in embroidered work. The virgins, her companions who follow her, shall be brought to you.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** With gladness and rejoicing they shall be led. They shall enter into the king’s palace.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Your sons will take the place of your fathers. You shall make them princes in all the earth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I will make your name to be remembered in all generations. Therefore the peoples shall give you thanks forever and ever.
