# Psalms 45 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My heart overfloweth with a goodly matter; I speak the things which I have made touching the king: My tongue is the pen of a ready writer.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Thou art fairer than the children of men; Grace is poured into thy lips: Therefore God hath blessed thee for ever.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Gird thy sword upon thy thigh, O mighty one, Thy glory and thy majesty.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And in thy majesty ride on prosperously, Because of truth and meekness and righteousness: And thy right hand shall teach thee terrible things.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thine arrows are sharp; The peoples fall under thee; They arein the heart of the king’s enemies.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Thy throne, O God, is for ever and ever: A sceptre of equity is the sceptre of thy kingdom.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Thou hast loved righteousness, and hated wickedness: Therefore God, thy God, hath anointed thee With the oil of gladness above thy fellows.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** All thy garments smell of myrrh, and aloes, and cassia; Out of ivory palaces stringed instruments have made thee glad.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Kings’ daughters are among thy honorable women: At thy right hand doth stand the queen in gold of Ophir.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Hearken, O daughter, and consider, and incline thine ear; Forget also thine own people, and thy father’s house:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** So will the king desire thy beauty; For he is thy lord; and reverence thou him.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And the daughter of Tyre shall be there with a gift; The rich among the people shall entreat thy favor.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The king’s daughter within the palace is all glorious: Her clothing is inwrought with gold.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** She shall be led unto the king in broidered work: The virgins her companions that follow her Shall be brought unto thee.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** With gladness and rejoicing shall they be led: They shall enter into the king’s palace.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Instead of thy fathers shall be thy children, Whom thou shalt make princes in all the earth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I will make thy name to be remembered in all generations: Therefore shall the peoples give thee thanks for ever and ever.
