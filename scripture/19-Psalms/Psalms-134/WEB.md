# Psalms 134 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Look! Praise Yahweh, all you servants of Yahweh, who stand by night in Yahweh’s house!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Lift up your hands in the sanctuary. Praise Yahweh!

<a id="v3"></a>**3** May Yahweh bless you from Zion; even he who made heaven and earth.
