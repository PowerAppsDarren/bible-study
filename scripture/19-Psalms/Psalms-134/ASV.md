# Psalms 134 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Behold, bless ye Jehovah, all ye servants of Jehovah, That by night stand in the house of Jehovah.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Lift up your hands to the sanctuary, And bless ye Jehovah.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Jehovah bless thee out of Zion; Even he that made heaven and earth.
