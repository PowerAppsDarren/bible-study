# Psalms 134 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Behold, bless ye the Lord, all ye servants of the Lord, which by night stand in the house of the Lord.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Lift up your hands in the sanctuary, and bless the Lord.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The Lord that made heaven and earth bless thee out of Zion.
