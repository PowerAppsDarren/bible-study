# Psalms 95 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O come, let us sing unto the Lord: let us make a joyful noise to the rock of our salvation.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let us come before his presence with thanksgiving, and make a joyful noise unto him with psalms.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For the Lord is a great God, and a great King above all gods.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** In his hand are the deep places of the earth: the strength of the hills is his also.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The sea is his, and he made it: and his hands formed the dry land.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** O come, let us worship and bow down: let us kneel before the Lord our maker.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For he is our God; and we are the people of his pasture, and the sheep of his hand. To day if ye will hear his voice,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Harden not your heart, as in the provocation, and as in the day of temptation in the wilderness:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** When your fathers tempted me, proved me, and saw my work.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Forty years long was I grieved with this generation, and said, It is a people that do err in their heart, and they have not known my ways:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Unto whom I sware in my wrath that they should not enter into my rest.
