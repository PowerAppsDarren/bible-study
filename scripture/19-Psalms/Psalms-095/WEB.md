# Psalms 95 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Oh come, let’s sing to Yahweh. Let’s shout aloud to the rock of our salvation!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let’s come before his presence with thanksgiving. Let’s extol him with songs!

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For Yahweh is a great God, a great King above all gods.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** In his hand are the deep places of the earth. The heights of the mountains are also his.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The sea is his, and he made it. His hands formed the dry land.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Oh come, let’s worship and bow down. Let’s kneel before Yahweh, our Maker,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** for he is our God. We are the people of his pasture, and the sheep in his care. Today, oh that you would hear his voice!

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Don’t harden your heart, as at Meribah, as in the day of Massah in the wilderness,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** when your fathers tempted me, tested me, and saw my work.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Forty long years I was grieved with that generation, and said, “It is a people that errs in their heart. They have not known my ways.”

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Therefore I swore in my wrath, “They won’t enter into my rest.”
