# Psalms 95 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O come, let us make songs to the Lord; sending up glad voices to the Rock of our salvation.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let us come before his face with praises; and make melody with holy songs.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For the Lord is a great God, and a great King over all gods.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The deep places of the earth are in his hand; and the tops of the mountains are his.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The sea is his, and he made it; and the dry land was formed by his hands.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** O come, let us give worship, falling down on our knees before the Lord our Maker.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For he is our God; and we are the people to whom he gives food, and the sheep of his flock. Today, if you would only give ear to his voice!

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let not your hearts be hard, as at Meribah, as in the day of Massah in the waste land;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** When your fathers put me to the test and saw my power and my work.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For forty years I was angry with this generation, and said, They are a people whose hearts are turned away from me, for they have no knowledge of my ways;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And I made an oath in my wrath, that they might not come into my place of rest.
