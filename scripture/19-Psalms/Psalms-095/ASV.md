# Psalms 95 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Oh come, let us sing unto Jehovah; Let us make a joyful noise to the rock of our salvation.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let us come before his presence with thanksgiving; Let us make a joyful noise unto him with psalms.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For Jehovah is a great God, And a great King above all gods.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** In his hand are the deep places of the earth; The heights of the mountains are his also.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The sea is his, and he made it; And his hands formed the dry land.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Oh come, let us worship and bow down; Let us kneel before Jehovah our Maker:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For he is our God, And we are the people of his pasture, and the sheep of his hand. To-day, oh that ye would hear his voice!

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Harden not your heart, as at Meribah, As in the day of Massah in the wilderness;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** When your fathers tempted me, Proved me, and saw my work.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Forty years long was I grieved with that generation, And said, It is a people that do err in their heart, And they have not known my ways:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Wherefore I sware in my wrath, That they should not enter into my rest.
