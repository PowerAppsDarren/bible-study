# Psalms 116 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I love Jehovah, because he heareth My voice and my supplications.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Because he hath inclined his ear unto me, Therefore will I callupon himas long as I live.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The cords of death compassed me, And the pains of Sheol gat hold upon me: I found trouble and sorrow.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Then called I upon the name of Jehovah: O Jehovah, I beseech thee, deliver my soul.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Gracious is Jehovah, and righteous; Yea, our God is merciful.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Jehovah preserveth the simple: I was brought low, and he saved me.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Return unto thy rest, O my soul; For Jehovah hath dealt bountifully with thee.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For thou hast delivered my soul from death, Mine eyes from tears, Andmy feet from falling.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will walk before Jehovah  In the land of the living.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I believe, for I will speak: I was greatly afflicted:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I said in my haste, All men are liars.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** What shall I render unto Jehovah For all his benefits toward me?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I will take the cup of salvation, And call upon the name of Jehovah.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I will pay my vows unto Jehovah, Yea, in the presence of all his people.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Precious in the sight of Jehovah  Is the death of his saints.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** O Jehovah, truly I am thy servant: I am thy servant, the son of thy handmaid; Thou hast loosed my bonds.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I will offer to thee the sacrifice of thanksgiving, And will call upon the name of Jehovah.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** I will pay my vows unto Jehovah, Yea, in the presence of all his people,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** In the courts of Jehovah’s house, In the midst of thee, O Jerusalem. Praise ye Jehovah.
