# Psalms 116 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I love Yahweh, because he listens to my voice, and my cries for mercy.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Because he has turned his ear to me, therefore I will call on him as long as I live.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The cords of death surrounded me, the pains of Sheol got a hold of me. I found trouble and sorrow.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Then I called on Yahweh’s name: “Yahweh, I beg you, deliver my soul.”

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Yahweh is Gracious and righteous. Yes, our God is merciful.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Yahweh preserves the simple. I was brought low, and he saved me.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Return to your rest, my soul, for Yahweh has dealt bountifully with you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For you have delivered my soul from death, my eyes from tears, and my feet from falling.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will walk before Yahweh in the land of the living.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I believed, therefore I said, “I was greatly afflicted.”

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I said in my haste, “All men are liars.”

<a id="v12"></a>**12** What will I give to Yahweh for all his benefits toward me?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I will take the cup of salvation, and call on Yahweh’s name.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I will pay my vows to Yahweh, yes, in the presence of all his people.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Precious in the sight of Yahweh is the death of his saints.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Yahweh, truly I am your servant. I am your servant, the son of your handmaid. You have freed me from my chains.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I will offer to you the sacrifice of thanksgiving, and will call on Yahweh’s name.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** I will pay my vows to Yahweh, yes, in the presence of all his people,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** in the courts of Yahweh’s house, in your midst, Jerusalem. Praise Yah!
