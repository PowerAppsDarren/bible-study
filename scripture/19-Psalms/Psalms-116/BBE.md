# Psalms 116 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I have given my love to the Lord, because he has given ear to the voice of my cry and my prayer.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He has let my request come before him, and I will make my prayer to him all my days.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The nets of death were round me, and the pains of the underworld had me in their grip; I was full of trouble and sorrow.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Then I made my prayer to the Lord, saying, O Lord, take my soul out of trouble.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The Lord is full of grace and righteousness; truly, he is a God of mercy.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The Lord keeps the simple; I was made low, and he was my saviour.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Come back to your rest, O my soul; for the Lord has given you your reward.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** You have taken my soul from the power of death, keeping my eyes from weeping, and my feet from falling.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will go before the Lord in the land of the living.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I still had faith, though I said, I am in great trouble;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Though I said in my fear, All men are false.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** What may I give to the Lord for all the good things which he has done for me?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I will take the cup of salvation, and give praise to the name of the Lord.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I will make the offering of my oath to the Lord, even before all his people.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Dear in the eyes of the Lord is the death of his saints.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** O Lord, truly I am your servant; I am your servant, the son of her who is your servant; by you have my cords been broken.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I will give an offering of praise to you, and make my prayer in the name of the Lord.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** I will make the offerings of my oath, even before all his people;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** In the Lord's house, even in Jerusalem. Praise be to the Lord.
