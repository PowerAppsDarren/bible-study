# Psalms 116 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I love the Lord, because he hath heard my voice and my supplications.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Because he hath inclined his ear unto me, therefore will I call upon him as long as I live.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The sorrows of death compassed me, and the pains of hell gat hold upon me: I found trouble and sorrow.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Then called I upon the name of the Lord; O Lord, I beseech thee, deliver my soul.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Gracious is the Lord, and righteous; yea, our God is merciful.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The Lord preserveth the simple: I was brought low, and he helped me.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Return unto thy rest, O my soul; for the Lord hath dealt bountifully with thee.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For thou hast delivered my soul from death, mine eyes from tears, and my feet from falling.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will walk before the Lord in the land of the living.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I believed, therefore have I spoken: I was greatly afflicted:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I said in my haste, All men are liars.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** What shall I render unto the Lord for all his benefits toward me?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I will take the cup of salvation, and call upon the name of the Lord.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I will pay my vows unto the Lord now in the presence of all his people.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Precious in the sight of the Lord is the death of his saints.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** O Lord, truly I am thy servant; I am thy servant, and the son of thine handmaid: thou hast loosed my bonds.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I will offer to thee the sacrifice of thanksgiving, and will call upon the name of the Lord.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** I will pay my vows unto the Lord now in the presence of all his people,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** In the courts of the Lord’s house, in the midst of thee, O Jerusalem. Praise ye the Lord.
