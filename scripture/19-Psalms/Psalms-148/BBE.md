# Psalms 148 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give praise to the Lord. Let the Lord be praised from the heavens: give him praise in the skies.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Give praise to him, all you his angels: give praise to him, all his armies.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Give praise to him, you sun and moon: give praise to him, all you stars of light.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Give praise to him, you highest heavens, and you waters which are over the heavens.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let them give praise to the name of the Lord: for he gave the order, and they were made.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He has put them in their places for ever; he has given them their limits which may not be broken.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Give praise to the Lord from the earth, you great sea-beasts, and deep places:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Fire and rain of ice, snow and mists; storm-wind, doing his word:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Mountains and all hills; fruit-trees and all trees of the mountains:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Beasts and all cattle; insects and winged birds:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Kings of the earth, and all peoples; rulers and all judges of the earth:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Young men and virgins; old men and children:

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Let them give glory to the name of the Lord: for his name only is to be praised: his kingdom is over the earth and the heaven.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He has put on high the horn of his people, for the praise of all his saints; even the children of Israel, a people which is near to him. Let the Lord be praised.
