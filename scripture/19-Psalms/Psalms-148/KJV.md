# Psalms 148 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye the Lord. Praise ye the Lord from the heavens: praise him in the heights.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Praise ye him, all his angels: praise ye him, all his hosts.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Praise ye him, sun and moon: praise him, all ye stars of light.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Praise him, ye heavens of heavens, and ye waters that be above the heavens.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let them praise the name of the Lord: for he commanded, and they were created.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He hath also stablished them for ever and ever: he hath made a decree which shall not pass.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Praise the Lord from the earth, ye dragons, and all deeps:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Fire, and hail; snow, and vapour; stormy wind fulfilling his word:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Mountains, and all hills; fruitful trees, and all cedars:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Beasts, and all cattle; creeping things, and flying fowl:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Kings of the earth, and all people; princes, and all judges of the earth:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Both young men, and maidens; old men, and children:

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Let them praise the name of the Lord: for his name alone is excellent; his glory is above the earth and heaven.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He also exalteth the horn of his people, the praise of all his saints; even of the children of Israel, a people near unto him. Praise ye the Lord.
