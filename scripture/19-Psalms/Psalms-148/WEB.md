# Psalms 148 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise Yah! Praise Yahweh from the heavens! Praise him in the heights!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Praise him, all his angels! Praise him, all his army!

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Praise him, sun and moon! Praise him, all you shining stars!

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Praise him, you heavens of heavens, You waters that are above the heavens.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let them praise Yahweh’s name, For he commanded, and they were created.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He has also established them forever and ever. He has made a decree which will not pass away.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Praise Yahweh from the earth, you great sea creatures, and all depths!

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Lightning and hail, snow and clouds; stormy wind, fulfilling his word;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** mountains and all hills; fruit trees and all cedars;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** wild animals and all livestock; small creatures and flying birds;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** kings of the earth and all peoples; princes and all judges of the earth;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** both young men and maidens; old men and children:

<a id="v13"></a>**13** let them praise Yahweh’s name, for his name alone is exalted. His glory is above the earth and the heavens.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He has lifted up the horn of his people, the praise of all his saints; even of the children of Israel, a people near to him. Praise Yah!
