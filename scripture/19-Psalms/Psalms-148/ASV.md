# Psalms 148 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Praise ye Jehovah. Praise ye Jehovah from the heavens: Praise him in the heights.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Praise ye him, all his angels: Praise ye him, all his host.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Praise ye him, sun and moon: Praise him, all ye stars of light.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Praise him, ye heavens of heavens, And ye waters that are above the heavens.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let them praise the name of Jehovah; For he commanded, and they were created.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He hath also established them for ever and ever: He hath made a decree which shall not pass away.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Praise Jehovah from the earth, Ye sea-monsters, and all deeps;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Fire and hail, snow and vapor; Stormy wind, fulfilling his word;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Mountains and all hills; Fruitful trees and all cedars;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Beasts and all cattle; Creeping things and flying birds;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Kings of the earth and all peoples; Princes and all judges of the earth;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Both young men and virgins; Old men and children:

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Let them praise the name of Jehovah; For his name alone is exalted; His glory is above the earth and the heavens.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And he hath lifted up the horn of his people, The praise of all his saints; Even of the children of Israel, a people near unto him. Praise ye Jehovah.
