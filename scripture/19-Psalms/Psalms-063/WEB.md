# Psalms 63 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** God, you are my God. I will earnestly seek you. My soul thirsts for you. My flesh longs for you, in a dry and weary land, where there is no water.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** So I have seen you in the sanctuary, watching your power and your glory.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Because your loving kindness is better than life, my lips shall praise you.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** So I will bless you while I live. I will lift up my hands in your name.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My soul shall be satisfied as with the richest food. My mouth shall praise you with joyful lips,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** when I remember you on my bed, and think about you in the night watches.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For you have been my help. I will rejoice in the shadow of your wings.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** My soul stays close to you. Your right hand holds me up.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But those who seek my soul, to destroy it, shall go into the lower parts of the earth.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** They shall be given over to the power of the sword. They shall be jackal food.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But the king shall rejoice in God. Everyone who swears by him will praise him, for the mouth of those who speak lies shall be silenced.
