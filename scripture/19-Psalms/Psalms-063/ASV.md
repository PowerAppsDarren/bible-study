# Psalms 63 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O God, thou art my God; earnestly will I seek thee: My soul thirsteth for thee, my flesh longeth for thee, In a dry and weary land, where no water is.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** So have I looked upon thee in the sanctuary, To see thy power and thy glory.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Because thy lovingkindness is better than life, My lips shall praise thee.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** So will I bless thee while I live: I will lift up my hands in thy name.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My soul shall be satisfied as with marrow and fatness; And my mouth shall praise thee with joyful lips;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** When I remember thee upon my bed, And meditate on thee in the night-watches.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For thou hast been my help, And in the shadow of thy wings will I rejoice.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** My soul followeth hard after thee: Thy right hand upholdeth me.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But those that seek my soul, to destroy it, Shall go into the lower parts of the earth.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** They shall be given over to the power of the sword: They shall be a portion for foxes.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But the king shall rejoice in God: Every one that sweareth by him shall glory; For the mouth of them that speak lies shall be stopped.
