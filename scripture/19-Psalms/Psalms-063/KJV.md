# Psalms 63 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O God, thou art my God; early will I seek thee: my soul thirsteth for thee, my flesh longeth for thee in a dry and thirsty land, where no water is;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** To see thy power and thy glory, so as I have seen thee in the sanctuary.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Because thy lovingkindness is better than life, my lips shall praise thee.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thus will I bless thee while I live: I will lift up my hands in thy name.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My soul shall be satisfied as with marrow and fatness; and my mouth shall praise thee with joyful lips:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** When I remember thee upon my bed, and meditate on thee in the night watches.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Because thou hast been my help, therefore in the shadow of thy wings will I rejoice.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** My soul followeth hard after thee: thy right hand upholdeth me.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But those that seek my soul, to destroy it, shall go into the lower parts of the earth.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** They shall fall by the sword: they shall be a portion for foxes.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But the king shall rejoice in God; every one that sweareth by him shall glory: but the mouth of them that speak lies shall be stopped.
