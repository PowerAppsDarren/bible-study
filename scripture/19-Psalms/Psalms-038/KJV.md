# Psalms 38 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Lord, rebuke me not in thy wrath: neither chasten me in thy hot displeasure.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For thine arrows stick fast in me, and thy hand presseth me sore.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** There is no soundness in my flesh because of thine anger; neither is there any rest in my bones because of my sin.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For mine iniquities are gone over mine head: as an heavy burden they are too heavy for me.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My wounds stink and are corrupt because of my foolishness.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I am troubled; I am bowed down greatly; I go mourning all the day long.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For my loins are filled with a loathsome disease: and there is no soundness in my flesh.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I am feeble and sore broken: I have roared by reason of the disquietness of my heart.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Lord, all my desire is before thee; and my groaning is not hid from thee.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** My heart panteth, my strength faileth me: as for the light of mine eyes, it also is gone from me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My lovers and my friends stand aloof from my sore; and my kinsmen stand afar off.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They also that seek after my life lay snares for me: and they that seek my hurt speak mischievous things, and imagine deceits all the day long.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But I, as a deaf man, heard not; and I was as a dumb man that openeth not his mouth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Thus I was as a man that heareth not, and in whose mouth are no reproofs.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For in thee, O Lord, do I hope: thou wilt hear, O Lord my God.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For I said, Hear me, lest otherwise they should rejoice over me: when my foot slippeth, they magnify themselves against me.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For I am ready to halt, and my sorrow is continually before me.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For I will declare mine iniquity; I will be sorry for my sin.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** But mine enemies are lively, and they are strong: and they that hate me wrongfully are multiplied.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** They also that render evil for good are mine adversaries; because I follow the thing that good is.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Forsake me not, O Lord: O my God, be not far from me.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Make haste to help me, O Lord my salvation.
