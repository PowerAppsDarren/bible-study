# Psalms 38 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Jehovah, rebuke me not in thy wrath; Neither chasten me in thy hot displeasure.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For thine arrows stick fast in me, And thy hand presseth me sore.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** There is no soundness in my flesh because of thine indignation; Neither is there any health in my bones because of my sin.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For mine iniquities are gone over my head: As a heavy burden they are too heavy for me.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My wounds are loathsome and corrupt, Because of my foolishness.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I am pained and bowed down greatly; I go mourning all the day long.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For my loins are filled with burning; And there is no soundness in my flesh.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I am faint and sore bruised: I have groaned by reason of the disquietness of my heart.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Lord, all my desire is before thee; And my groaning is not hid from thee.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** My heart throbbeth, my strength faileth me: As for the light of mine eyes, it also is gone from me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My lovers and my friends stand aloof from my plague; And my kinsmen stand afar off.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They also that seek after my life lay snares for me; And they that seek my hurt speak mischievous things, And meditate deceits all the day long.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But I, as a deaf man, hear not; And I am as a dumb man that openeth not his mouth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Yea, I am as a man that heareth not, And in whose mouth are no reproofs.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For in thee, O Jehovah, do I hope: Thou wilt answer, O Lord my God.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For I said, Lest they rejoice over me: When my foot slippeth, they magnify themselves against me.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For I am ready to fall, And my sorrow is continually before me.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For I will declare mine iniquity; I will be sorry for my sin.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** But mine enemies are lively, and are strong; And they that hate me wrongfully are multiplied.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** They also that render evil for good Are adversaries unto me, because I follow the thing that is good.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Forsake me not, O Jehovah: O my God, be not far from me.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Make haste to help me, O Lord, my salvation.
