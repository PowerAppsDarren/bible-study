# Psalms 38 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh, don’t rebuke me in your wrath, neither chasten me in your hot displeasure.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For your arrows have pierced me, your hand presses hard on me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** There is no soundness in my flesh because of your indignation, neither is there any health in my bones because of my sin.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For my iniquities have gone over my head. As a heavy burden, they are too heavy for me.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My wounds are loathsome and corrupt, because of my foolishness.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I am pained and bowed down greatly. I go mourning all day long.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For my waist is filled with burning. There is no soundness in my flesh.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I am faint and severely bruised. I have groaned by reason of the anguish of my heart.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Lord, all my desire is before you. My groaning is not hidden from you.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** My heart throbs. My strength fails me. As for the light of my eyes, it has also left me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My lovers and my friends stand aloof from my plague. My kinsmen stand far away.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They also who seek after my life lay snares. Those who seek my hurt speak mischievous things, and meditate deceits all day long.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But I, as a deaf man, don’t hear. I am as a mute man who doesn’t open his mouth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Yes, I am as a man who doesn’t hear, in whose mouth are no reproofs.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For in you, Yahweh, do I hope. You will answer, Lord my God.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For I said, “Don’t let them gloat over me, or exalt themselves over me when my foot slips.”

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For I am ready to fall. My pain is continually before me.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For I will declare my iniquity. I will be sorry for my sin.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** But my enemies are vigorous and many. Those who hate me without reason are numerous.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** They who also render evil for good are adversaries to me, because I follow what is good.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Don’t forsake me, Yahweh. My God, don’t be far from me.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Hurry to help me, Lord, my salvation.
