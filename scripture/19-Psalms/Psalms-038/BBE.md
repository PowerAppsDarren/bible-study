# Psalms 38 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Lord, be not bitter with me in your wrath; let not your hand be on me in the heat of your passion.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For your arrows have gone into my flesh, and I am crushed under the weight of your hand.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** My flesh is wasted because of your wrath; and there is no peace in my bones because of my sin.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For my crimes have gone over my head; they are like a great weight which is more than my strength.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My wounds are poisoned and evil-smelling, because of my foolish behaviour.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I am troubled, I am made low; I go weeping all the day.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For my body is full of burning; all my flesh is unhealthy.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I am feeble and crushed down; I gave a cry like a lion because of the grief in my heart.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Lord, all my desire is before you; my sorrow is not kept secret from you.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** My heart goes out in pain, my strength is wasting away; as for the light of my eyes, it is gone from me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My lovers and my friends keep away from my disease; my relations keep far away.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Those who have a desire to take my life put nets for me; those who are designing my destruction say evil things against me, all the day their minds are full of deceit.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But I kept my ears shut like a man without hearing; like a man without a voice, never opening his mouth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** So I was like a man whose ears are shut, and in whose mouth there are no sharp words.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** In you, O Lord, is my hope: you will give me an answer, O Lord, my God.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** I said, Let them not be glad over me; when my foot is moved, let them not be lifted up with pride against me.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** My feet are near to falling, and my sorrow is ever before me.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** I will make clear my wrongdoing, with sorrow in my heart for my sin.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** But they are strong who have hate for me without cause: those who are against me falsely are increased in numbers.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** They give me back evil for good; they are my haters because I go after the thing which is right.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Do not give me up, O Lord; O my God, be near to me.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Come quickly to give me help, O Lord, my salvation.
