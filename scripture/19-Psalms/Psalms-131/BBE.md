# Psalms 131 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Lord, there is no pride in my heart and my eyes are not lifted up; and I have not taken part in great undertakings, or in things over-hard for me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** See, I have made my soul calm and quiet, like a child on its mother's breast; my soul is like a child on its mother's breast.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** O Israel, have hope in the Lord, from this time and for ever.
