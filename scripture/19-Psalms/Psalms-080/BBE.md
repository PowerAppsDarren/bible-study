# Psalms 80 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give ear, O Keeper of Israel, guiding Joseph like a flock; you who have your seat on the winged ones, let your glory be seen.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Before Ephraim and Benjamin and Manasseh, let your strength be awake from sleep, and come as our salvation.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Take us back again, O God; let us see the shining of your face, and let us be safe.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** O Lord God of armies, how long will your wrath be burning against the rest of your people?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You have given them the bread of weeping for food; for their drink you have given them sorrow in great measure.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You make us a cause of war among our neighbours; our haters are laughing at us among themselves.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Take us back again, O God of armies; let us see the shining of your face, and let us be safe.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** You took a vine out of Egypt: driving out the nations, and planting it in their land.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** You made ready a place for it, so that it might take deep root, and it sent out its branches over all the land.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The mountains were covered with its shade, and the great trees with its branches.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** It sent out its arms to the Sea, and its branches to the River.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Why are its walls broken down by your hands, so that all who go by may take its fruit?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** It is uprooted by the pigs from the woods, the beasts of the field get their food from it.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Come back, O God of armies: from heaven let your eyes be turned to this vine, and give your mind to it,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Even to the tree which was planted by your right hand, and to the branch which you made strong for yourself.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** It is burned with fire; it is cut down: they are made waste by the wrath of your face.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Let your hand be on the man of your right hand, on the son of man whom you made strong for yourself.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** So will we not be turned back from you; keep us in life, and we will give praise to your name.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Take us back, O Lord God of armies; let us see the shining of your face, and let us be safe.
