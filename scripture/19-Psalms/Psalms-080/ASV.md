# Psalms 80 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give ear, O Shepherd of Israel, Thou that leadest Joseph like a flock; Thou that sittest above the cherubim, shine forth.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Before Ephraim and Benjamin and Manasseh, stir up thy might, And come to save us.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Turn us again, O God; And cause thy face to shine, and we shall be saved.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** O Jehovah God of hosts, How long wilt thou be angry against the prayer of thy people?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thou hast fed them with the bread of tears, And given them tears to drink in large measure.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Thou makest us a strife unto our neighbors; And our enemies laugh among themselves.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Turn us again, O God of hosts; And cause thy face to shine, and we shall be saved.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Thou broughtest a vine out of Egypt: Thou didst drive out the nations, and plantedst it.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Thou preparedst room before it, And it took deep root, and filled the land.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The mountains were covered with the shadow of it, And the boughs thereof were like cedars of God.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** It sent out its branches unto the sea, And its shoots unto the River.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Why hast thou broken down its walls, So that all they that pass by the way do pluck it?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The boar out of the wood doth ravage it, And the wild beasts of the field feed on it.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Turn again, we beseech thee, O God of hosts: Look down from heaven, and behold, and visit this vine,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And the stock which thy right hand planted, And the branch that thou madest strong for thyself.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** It is burned with fire, it is cut down: They perish at the rebuke of thy countenance.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Let thy hand be upon the man of thy right hand, Upon the son of man whom thou madest strong for thyself.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** So shall we not go back from thee: Quicken thou us, and we will call upon thy name.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Turn us again, O Jehovah God of hosts; Cause thy face to shine, and we shall be saved.
