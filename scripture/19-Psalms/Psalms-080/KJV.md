# Psalms 80 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give ear, O Shepherd of Israel, thou that leadest Joseph like a flock; thou that dwellest between the cherubims, shine forth.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Before Ephraim and Benjamin and Manasseh stir up thy strength, and come and save us.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Turn us again, O God, and cause thy face to shine; and we shall be saved.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** O Lord God of hosts, how long wilt thou be angry against the prayer of thy people?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thou feedest them with the bread of tears; and givest them tears to drink in great measure.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Thou makest us a strife unto our neighbours: and our enemies laugh among themselves.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Turn us again, O God of hosts, and cause thy face to shine; and we shall be saved.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Thou hast brought a vine out of Egypt: thou hast cast out the heathen, and planted it.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Thou preparedst room before it, and didst cause it to take deep root, and it filled the land.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The hills were covered with the shadow of it, and the boughs thereof were like the goodly cedars.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** She sent out her boughs unto the sea, and her branches unto the river.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Why hast thou then broken down her hedges, so that all they which pass by the way do pluck her?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The boar out of the wood doth waste it, and the wild beast of the field doth devour it.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Return, we beseech thee, O God of hosts: look down from heaven, and behold, and visit this vine;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And the vineyard which thy right hand hath planted, and the branch that thou madest strong for thyself.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** It is burned with fire, it is cut down: they perish at the rebuke of thy countenance.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Let thy hand be upon the man of thy right hand, upon the son of man whom thou madest strong for thyself.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** So will not we go back from thee: quicken us, and we will call upon thy name.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Turn us again, O Lord God of hosts, cause thy face to shine; and we shall be saved.
