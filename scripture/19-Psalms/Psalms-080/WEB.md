# Psalms 80 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear us, Shepherd of Israel, you who lead Joseph like a flock, you who sit above the cherubim, shine out.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Before Ephraim and Benjamin and Manasseh, stir up your might! Come to save us!

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Turn us again, God. Cause your face to shine, and we will be saved.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Yahweh God of Armies, How long will you be angry against the prayer of your people?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You have fed them with the bread of tears, and given them tears to drink in large measure.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You make us a source of contention to our neighbors. Our enemies laugh among themselves.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Turn us again, God of Armies. Cause your face to shine, and we will be saved.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** You brought a vine out of Egypt. You drove out the nations, and planted it.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** You cleared the ground for it. It took deep root, and filled the land.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The mountains were covered with its shadow. Its boughs were like God’s cedars.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** It sent out its branches to the sea, Its shoots to the River.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Why have you broken down its walls, so that all those who pass by the way pluck it?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The boar out of the wood ravages it. The wild animals of the field feed on it.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Turn again, we beg you, God of Armies. Look down from heaven, and see, and visit this vine,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** the stock which your right hand planted, the branch that you made strong for yourself.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** It’s burned with fire. It’s cut down. They perish at your rebuke.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Let your hand be on the man of your right hand, on the son of man whom you made strong for yourself.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** So we will not turn away from you. Revive us, and we will call on your name.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Turn us again, Yahweh God of Armies. Cause your face to shine, and we will be saved.
