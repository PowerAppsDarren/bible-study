# Psalms 93 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh reigns! He is clothed with majesty! Yahweh is armed with strength. The world also is established. It can’t be moved.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Your throne is established from long ago. You are from everlasting.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The floods have lifted up, Yahweh, the floods have lifted up their voice. The floods lift up their waves.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Above the voices of many waters, the mighty breakers of the sea, Yahweh on high is mighty.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Your statutes stand firm. Holiness adorns your house, Yahweh, forever more.
