# Psalms 93 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The Lord is King; he is clothed with glory; the Lord is clothed with strength; power is the cord of his robe; the world is fixed, so that it may not be moved.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The seat of your power has been from the past; you are eternal.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The rivers send up, O Lord, the rivers send up their voices; they send them up with a loud cry.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The Lord in heaven is stronger than the noise of great waters, yes, he is stronger than the great waves of the sea.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Your witness is most certain; it is right for your house to be holy, O Lord, for ever.
