# Psalms 93 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Jehovah reigneth; he is clothed with majesty; Jehovah is clothed with strength; he hath girded himself therewith: The world also is established, that it cannot be moved.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Thy throne is established of old: Thou art from everlasting.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The floods have lifted up, O Jehovah, The floods have lifted up their voice; The floods lift up their waves.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Above the voices of many waters, The mighty breakers of the sea, Jehovah on high is mighty.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thy testimonies are very sure: Holiness becometh thy house, O Jehovah, for evermore.
