# Psalms 93 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The Lord reigneth, he is clothed with majesty; the Lord is clothed with strength, wherewith he hath girded himself: the world also is stablished, that it cannot be moved.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Thy throne is established of old: thou art from everlasting.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The floods have lifted up, O Lord, the floods have lifted up their voice; the floods lift up their waves.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The Lord on high is mightier than the noise of many waters, yea, than the mighty waves of the sea.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thy testimonies are very sure: holiness becometh thine house, O Lord, for ever.
