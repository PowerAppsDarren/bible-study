# Psalms 17 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Let my cause come to your ears, O Lord, give attention to my cry; give ear to my prayer which goes not out from false lips.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Be my judge; for your eyes see what is right.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You have put my heart to the test, searching me in the night; you have put me to the test and seen no evil purpose in me; I will keep my mouth from sin.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** As for the works of men, by the word of your lips I have kept myself from the ways of the violent.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I have kept my feet in your ways, my steps have not been turned away.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** My cry has gone up to you, for you will give me an answer, O God: let your ear be turned to me, and give attention to my words.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Make clear the wonder of your mercy, O saviour of those who put their faith in your right hand, from those who come out against them.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Keep me as the light of your eyes, covering me with the shade of your wings,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** From the evil-doers who are violent to me, and from those who are round me, desiring my death.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** They are shut up in their fat: with their mouths they say words of pride.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They have made a circle round our steps: their eyes are fixed on us, forcing us down to the earth;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Like a lion desiring its food, and like a young lion waiting in secret places.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Up! Lord, come out against him, make him low, with your sword be my saviour from the evil-doer.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** With your hand, O Lord, from men, even men of the world, whose heritage is in this life, and whom you make full with your secret wealth: they are full of children; after their death their offspring take the rest of their goods.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** As for me, I will see your face in righteousness: when I am awake it will be joy enough for me to see your form.
