# Psalms 17 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear the right, O Jehovah, attend unto my cry; Give ear unto my prayer, that goeth not out of feigned lips.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let my sentence come forth from thy presence; Let thine eyes look upon equity.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thou hast proved my heart; thou hast visited me in the night; Thou hast tried me, and findest nothing; I am purposed that my mouth shall not transgress.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** As for the works of men, by the word of thy lips I have kept me from the ways of the violent.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My steps have held fast to thy paths, My feet have not slipped.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I have called upon thee, for thou wilt answer me, O God: Incline thine ear unto me, and hear my speech.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Show thy marvellous lovingkindness, O thou that savest by thy right hand them that take refuge in thee From those that rise upagainst them.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Keep me as the apple of the eye; Hide me under the shadow of thy wings,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** From the wicked that oppress me, My deadly enemies, that compass me about.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** They are inclosed in their own fat: With their mouth they speak proudly.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They have now compassed us in our steps; They set their eyes to castusdown to the earth.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** He is like a lion that is greedy of his prey, And as it were a young lion lurking in secret places.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Arise, O Jehovah, Confront him, cast him down: Deliver my soul from the wicked by thy sword;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** From men by thy hand, O Jehovah, From men of the world, whose portion is inthislife, And whose belly thou fillest with thy treasure: They are satisfied with children, And leave the rest of their substance to their babes.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** As for me, I shall behold thy face in righteousness; I shall be satisfied, when I awake, with beholding thy form.
