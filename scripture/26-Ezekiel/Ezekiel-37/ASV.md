# Ezekiel 37 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The hand of Jehovah was upon me, and he brought me out in the Spirit of Jehovah, and set me down in the midst of the valley; and it was full of bones.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And he caused me to pass by them round about: and, behold, there were very many in the open valley; and, lo, they were very dry.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And he said unto me, Son of man, can these bones live? And I answered, O Lord Jehovah, thou knowest.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Again he said unto me, Prophesy over these bones, and say unto them, O ye dry bones, hear the word of Jehovah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thus saith the Lord Jehovah unto these bones: Behold, I will cause breath to enter into you, and ye shall live.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And I will lay sinews upon you, and will bring up flesh upon you, and cover you with skin, and put breath in you, and ye shall live; and ye shall know that I am Jehovah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** So I prophesied as I was commanded: and as I prophesied, there was a noise, and, behold, an earthquake; and the bones came together, bone to its bone.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And I beheld, and, lo, there were sinews upon them, and flesh came up, and skin covered them above; but there was no breath in them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Then said he unto me, Prophesy unto the wind, prophesy, son of man, and say to the wind, Thus saith the Lord Jehovah: Come from the four winds, O breath, and breathe upon these slain, that they may live.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** So I prophesied as he commanded me, and the breath came into them, and they lived, and stood up upon their feet, an exceeding great army.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Then he said unto me, Son of man, these bones are the whole house of Israel: behold, they say, Our bones are dried up, and our hope is lost; we are clean cut off.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Therefore prophesy, and say unto them, Thus saith the Lord Jehovah: Behold, I will open your graves, and cause you to come up out of your graves, O my people; and I will bring you into the land of Israel.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And ye shall know that I am Jehovah, when I have opened your graves, and caused you to come up out of your graves, O my people.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And I will put my Spirit in you, and ye shall live, and I will place you in your own land: and ye shall know that I, Jehovah, have spoken it and performed it, saith Jehovah.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The word of Jehovah came again unto me, saying,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And thou, son of man, take thee one stick, and write upon it, For Judah, and for the children of Israel his companions: then take another stick, and write upon it, For Joseph, the stick of Ephraim, and for all the house of Israel his companions:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** and join them for thee one to another into one stick, that they may become one in thy hand.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And when the children of thy people shall speak unto thee, saying, Wilt thou not show us what thou meanest by these?

<a id="v19"></a>**19** say unto them, Thus saith the Lord Jehovah: Behold, I will take the stick of Joseph, which is in the hand of Ephraim, and the tribes of Israel his companions; and I will put them with it, even with the stick of Judah, and make them one stick, and they shall be one in my hand.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And the sticks whereon thou writest shall be in thy hand before their eyes.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And say unto them, Thus saith the Lord Jehovah: Behold, I will take the children of Israel from among the nations, whither they are gone, and will gather them on every side, and bring them into their own land:

<a id="v22"></a>**22** and I will make them one nation in the land, upon the mountains of Israel; and one king shall be king to them all; and they shall be no more two nations, neither shall they be divided into two kingdoms any more at all;

<a id="v23"></a>**23** neither shall they defile themselves any more with their idols, nor with their detestable things, nor with any of their transgressions; but I will save them out of all their dwelling-places, wherein they have sinned, and will cleanse them: so shall they be my people, and I will be their God.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And my servant David shall be king over them; and they all shall have one shepherd: they shall also walk in mine ordinances, and observe my statutes, and do them.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And they shall dwell in the land that I have given unto Jacob my servant, wherein your fathers dwelt; and they shall dwell therein, they, and their children, and their children’s children, for ever: and David my servant shall be their prince for ever.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Moreover I will make a covenant of peace with them; it shall be an everlasting covenant with them; and I will place them, and multiply them, and will set my sanctuary in the midst of them for evermore.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** My tabernacle also shall be with them; and I will be their God, and they shall be my people.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** And the nations shall know that I am Jehovah that sanctifieth Israel, when my sanctuary shall be in the midst of them for evermore.
