# Ezekiel 15 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh’s word came to me, saying,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Son of man, what is the vine tree more than any tree, the vine-branch which is among the trees of the forest?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Will wood be taken of it to make anything? Will men take a pin of it to hang any vessel on it?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Behold, it is cast into the fire for fuel; the fire has devoured both its ends, and its midst is burned: is it profitable for any work?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Behold, when it was whole, it was meet for no work: how much less, when the fire has devoured it, and it is burned, shall it yet be meet for any work!

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Therefore thus says the Lord Yahweh: As the vine tree among the trees of the forest, which I have given to the fire for fuel, so will I give the inhabitants of Jerusalem.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I will set my face against them; they shall go out from the fire, but the fire shall devour them; and you shall know that I am Yahweh, when I set my face against them.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will make the land desolate, because they have committed a trespass, says the Lord Yahweh.
