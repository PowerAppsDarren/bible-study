# Ezekiel 15 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And the word of the Lord came unto me, saying,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Son of man, What is the vine tree more than any tree, or than a branch which is among the trees of the forest?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Shall wood be taken thereof to do any work? or will men take a pin of it to hang any vessel thereon?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Behold, it is cast into the fire for fuel; the fire devoureth both the ends of it, and the midst of it is burned. Is it meet for any work?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Behold, when it was whole, it was meet for no work: how much less shall it be meet yet for any work, when the fire hath devoured it, and it is burned?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Therefore thus saith the Lord God; As the vine tree among the trees of the forest, which I have given to the fire for fuel, so will I give the inhabitants of Jerusalem.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And I will set my face against them; they shall go out from one fire, and another fire shall devour them; and ye shall know that I am the Lord, when I set my face against them.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And I will make the land desolate, because they have committed a trespass, saith the Lord God.
