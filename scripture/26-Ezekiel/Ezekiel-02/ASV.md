# Ezekiel 2 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And he said unto me, Son of man, stand upon thy feet, and I will speak with thee.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And the Spirit entered into me when he spake unto me, and set me upon my feet; and I heard him that spake unto me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And he said unto me, Son of man, I send thee to the children of Israel, to nations that are rebellious, which have rebelled against me: they and their fathers have transgressed against me even unto this very day.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And the children are impudent and stiffhearted: I do send thee unto them; and thou shalt say unto them, Thus saith the Lord Jehovah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And they, whether they will hear, or whether they will forbear (for they are a rebellious house), yet shall know that there hath been a prophet among them.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And thou, son of man, be not afraid of them, neither be afraid of their words, though briers and thorns are with thee, and thou dost dwell among scorpions: be not afraid of their words, nor be dismayed at their looks, though they are a rebellious house.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And thou shalt speak my words unto them, whether they will hear, or whether they will forbear; for they are most rebellious.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But thou, son of man, hear what I say unto thee; be not thou rebellious like that rebellious house: open thy mouth, and eat that which I give thee.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And when I looked, behold, a hand was put forth unto me; and, lo, a roll of a book was therein;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** and he spread it before me: and it was written within and without; and there were written therein lamentations, and mourning, and woe.
