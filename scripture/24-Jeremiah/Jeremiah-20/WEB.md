# Jeremiah 20 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Now Pashhur, the son of Immer the priest, who was chief officer in Yahweh’s house, heard Jeremiah prophesying these things.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Then Pashhur struck Jeremiah the prophet, and put him in the stocks that were in the upper gate of Benjamin, which was in Yahweh’s house.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** On the next day, Pashhur released Jeremiah out of the stocks. Then Jeremiah said to him, Yahweh has not called your name Pashhur, but Magormissabib.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For thus says Yahweh, Behold, I will make you a terror to yourself, and to all your friends; and they shall fall by the sword of their enemies, and your eyes shall see it; and I will give all Judah into the hand of the king of Babylon, and he shall carry them captive to Babylon, and shall kill them with the sword.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Moreover I will give all the riches of this city, and all its gains, and all the precious things of it, yes, all the treasures of the kings of Judah will I give into the hand of their enemies; and they shall make them captives, and take them, and carry them to Babylon.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You, Pashhur, and all who dwell in your house shall go into captivity; and you shall come to Babylon, and there you shall die, and there you shall be buried, you, and all your friends, to whom you have prophesied falsely.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Yahweh, you have persuaded me, and I was persuaded; you are stronger than I, and have prevailed: I am become a laughing-stock all the day, every one mocks me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For as often as I speak, I cry out; I cry, Violence and destruction! because Yahweh’s word is made a reproach to me, and a derision, all the day.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** If I say, I will not make mention of him, nor speak any more in his name, then there is in my heart as it were a burning fire shut up in my bones, and I am weary with forbearing, and I can’t.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For I have heard the defaming of many, terror on every side. Denounce, and we will denounce him, say all my familiar friends, those who watch for my fall; perhaps he will be persuaded, and we shall prevail against him, and we shall take our revenge on him.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But Yahweh is with me as an awesome mighty one: therefore my persecutors shall stumble, and they shall not prevail; they shall be utterly disappointed, because they have not dealt wisely, even with an everlasting dishonor which shall never be forgotten.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But, Yahweh of Armies, who tests the righteous, who sees the heart and the mind, let me see your vengeance on them; for to you have I revealed my cause.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Sing to Yahweh, praise Yahweh; for he has delivered the soul of the needy from the hand of evildoers.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Cursed is the day in which I was born: don’t let the day in which my mother bore me be blessed.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Cursed is the man who brought news to my father, saying, A boy is born to you; making him very glad.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Let that man be as the cities which Yahweh overthrew, and didn’t repent: and let him hear a cry in the morning, and shouting at noontime;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** because he didn’t kill me from the womb; and so my mother would have been my grave, and her womb always great.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Why did I come out of the womb to see labor and sorrow, that my days should be consumed with shame?
