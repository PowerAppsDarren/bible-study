# 2 John 1 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The elder, to the chosen lady and her children, whom I love in truth; and not I only, but also all those who know the truth;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** for the truth’s sake, which remains in us, and it will be with us forever:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Grace, mercy, and peace will be with us, from God the Father, and from the Lord Jesus Christ, the Son of the Father, in truth and love.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I rejoice greatly that I have found some of your children walking in truth, even as we have been commanded by the Father.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Now I beg you, dear lady, not as though I wrote to you a new commandment, but that which we had from the beginning, that we love one another.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** This is love, that we should walk according to his commandments. This is the commandment, even as you heard from the beginning, that you should walk in it.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For many deceivers have gone out into the world, those who don’t confess that Jesus Christ came in the flesh. This is the deceiver and the Antichrist.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Watch yourselves, that we don’t lose the things which we have accomplished, but that we receive a full reward.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Whoever transgresses and doesn’t remain in the teaching of Christ, doesn’t have God. He who remains in the teaching, the same has both the Father and the Son.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** If anyone comes to you, and doesn’t bring this teaching, don’t receive him into your house, and don’t welcome him,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** for he who welcomes him participates in his evil works.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Having many things to write to you, I don’t want to do so with paper and ink, but I hope to come to you, and to speak face to face, that our joy may be made full.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The children of your chosen sister greet you. Amen.
