# 1 Samuel 8 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And it came to pass, when Samuel was old, that he made his sons judges over Israel.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Now the name of his first-born was Joel; and the name of his second, Abijah: they were judges in Beer-sheba.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And his sons walked not in his ways, but turned aside after lucre, and took bribes, and perverted justice.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Then all the elders of Israel gathered themselves together, and came to Samuel unto Ramah;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** and they said unto him, Behold, thou art old, and thy sons walk not in thy ways: now make us a king to judge us like all the nations.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** But the thing displeased Samuel, when they said, Give us a king to judge us. And Samuel prayed unto Jehovah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And Jehovah said unto Samuel, Hearken unto the voice of the people in all that they say unto thee; for they have not rejected thee, but they have rejected me, that I should not be king over them.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** According to all the works which they have done since the day that I brought them up out of Egypt even unto this day, in that they have forsaken me, and served other gods, so do they also unto thee.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Now therefore hearken unto their voice: howbeit thou shalt protest solemnly unto them, and shalt show them the manner of the king that shall reign over them.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And Samuel told all the words of Jehovah unto the people that asked of him a king.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And he said, This will be the manner of the king that shall reign over you: he will take your sons, and appoint them unto him, for his chariots, and to be his horsemen; and they shall run before his chariots;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** and he will appoint them unto him for captains of thousands, and captains of fifties; and he will set some to plow his ground, and to reap his harvest, and to make his instruments of war, and the instruments of his chariots.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And he will take your daughters to be perfumers, and to be cooks, and to be bakers.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And he will take your fields, and your vineyards, and your oliveyards, even the best of them, and give them to his servants.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And he will take the tenth of your seed, and of your vineyards, and give to his officers, and to his servants.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And he will take your men-servants, and your maid-servants, and your goodliest young men, and your asses, and put them to his work.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He will take the tenth of your flocks: and ye shall be his servants.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And ye shall cry out in that day because of your king whom ye shall have chosen you; and Jehovah will not answer you in that day.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** But the people refused to hearken unto the voice of Samuel; and they said, Nay: but we will have a king over us,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** that we also may be like all the nations, and that our king may judge us, and go out before us, and fight our battles.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And Samuel heard all the words of the people, and he rehearsed them in the ears of Jehovah.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And Jehovah said to Samuel, Hearken unto their voice, and make them a king. And Samuel said unto the men of Israel, Go ye every man unto his city.
