# 1 Samuel 3 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And the child Samuel ministered unto Jehovah before Eli. And the word of Jehovah was precious in those days; there was no frequent vision.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And it came to pass at that time, when Eli was laid down in his place (now his eyes had begun to wax dim, so that he could not see),

<a id="v3"></a>**3** and the lamp of God was not yet gone out, and Samuel was laid down to sleep, in the temple of Jehovah, where the ark of God was;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** that Jehovah called Samuel: and he said, Here am I.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And he ran unto Eli, and said, Here am I; for thou calledst me. And he said, I called not; lie down again. And he went and lay down.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And Jehovah called yet again, Samuel. And Samuel arose and went to Eli, and said, Here am I; for thou calledst me. And he answered, I called not, my son; lie down again.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Now Samuel did not yet know Jehovah, neither was the word of Jehovah yet revealed unto him.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And Jehovah called Samuel again the third time. And he arose and went to Eli, and said, Here am I; for thou calledst me. And Eli perceived that Jehovah had called the child.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Therefore Eli said unto Samuel, Go, lie down: and it shall be, if he call thee, that thou shalt say, Speak, Jehovah; for thy servant heareth. So Samuel went and lay down in his place.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And Jehovah came, and stood, and called as at other times, Samuel, Samuel. Then Samuel said, Speak; for thy servant heareth.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And Jehovah said to Samuel, Behold, I will do a thing in Israel, at which both the ears of every one that heareth it shall tingle.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** In that day I will perform against Eli all that I have spoken concerning his house, from the beginning even unto the end.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For I have told him that I will judge his house for ever, for the iniquity which he knew, because his sons did bring a curse upon themselves, and he restrained them not.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And therefore I have sworn unto the house of Eli, that the iniquity of Eli’s house shall not be expiated with sacrifice nor offering for ever.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And Samuel lay until the morning, and opened the doors of the house of Jehovah. And Samuel feared to show Eli the vision.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Then Eli called Samuel, and said, Samuel, my son. And he said, Here am I.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And he said, What is the thing thatJehovah hath spoken unto thee? I pray thee, hide it not from me: God do so to thee, and more also, if thou hide anything from me of all the things that he spake unto thee.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And Samuel told him every whit, and hid nothing from him. And he said, It is Jehovah: let him do what seemeth him good.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And Samuel grew, and Jehovah was with him, and did let none of his words fall to the ground.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And all Israel from Dan even to Beer-sheba knew that Samuel was established to be a prophet of Jehovah.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And Jehovah appeared again in Shiloh; for Jehovah revealed himself to Samuel in Shiloh by the word of Jehovah.
