# Song of Songs 1 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The song of Songs, which is Solomon's.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let him give me the kisses of his mouth: for his love is better than wine.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Sweet is the smell of your perfumes; your name is as perfume running out; so the young girls give you their love.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Take me to you, and we will go after you: the king has taken me into his house. We will be glad and full of joy in you, we will give more thought to your love than to wine: rightly are they your lovers.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I am dark, but fair of form, O daughters of Jerusalem, as the tents of Kedar, as the curtains of Solomon.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let not your eyes be turned on me, because I am dark, because I was looked on by the sun; my mother's children were angry with me; they made me the keeper of the vine-gardens; but my vine-garden I have not kept.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Say, O love of my soul, where you give food to your flock, and where you make them take their rest in the heat of the day; why have I to be as one wandering by the flocks of your friends?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** If you have not knowledge, O most beautiful among women, go on your way in the footsteps of the flock, and give your young goats food by the tents of the keepers.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I have made a comparison of you, O my love, to a horse in Pharaoh's carriages.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Your face is a delight with rings of hair, your neck with chains of jewels.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** We will make you chains of gold with ornaments of silver.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** While the king is seated at his table, my spices send out their perfume.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** As a bag of myrrh is my well-loved one to me, when he is at rest all night between my breasts.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** My love is to me as a branch of the cypress-tree in the vine-gardens of En-gedi.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** See, you are fair, my love, you are fair; you have the eyes of a dove.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** See, you are fair, my loved one, and a pleasure; our bed is green.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Cedar-trees are the pillars of our house; and our boards are made of fir-trees.
