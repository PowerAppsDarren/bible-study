# Song of Songs 1 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The Song of songs, which is Solomon’s.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let him kiss me with the kisses of his mouth; For thy love is better than wine.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thine oils have a goodly fragrance; Thy name is as oil poured forth; Therefore do the virgins love thee.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Draw me; we will run after thee: The king hath brought me into his chambers; We will be glad and rejoice in thee; We will make mention of thy love more than of wine: Rightly do they love thee.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I am black, but comely, Oh ye daughters of Jerusalem, As the tents of Kedar, As the curtains of Solomon.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Look not upon me, because I am swarthy, Because the sun hath scorched me. My mother’s sons were incensed against me; They made me keeper of the vineyards; But mine own vineyard have I not kept.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Tell me, O thou whom my soul loveth, Where thou feedest thy flock, Where thou makest it to rest at noon: For why should I be as one that is veiled Beside the flocks of thy companions?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** If thou know not, O thou fairest among women, Go thy way forth by the footsteps of the flock, And feed thy kids beside the shepherds’ tents.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I have compared thee, O my love, To a steed in Pharaoh’s chariots.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Thy cheeks are comely with plaits of hair, Thy neck with strings of jewels.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** We will make thee plaits of gold With studs of silver.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** While the king sat at his table, My spikenard sent forth its fragrance.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** My beloved is unto me as a bundle of myrrh, That lieth betwixt my breasts.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** My beloved is unto me as a cluster of henna-flowers In the vineyards of En-gedi.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Behold, thou art fair, my love; Behold thou art fair; Thine eyes are as doves.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Behold, thou art fair, my beloved, yea, pleasant: Also our couch is green.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The beams of our house are cedars, And our rafters are firs.
