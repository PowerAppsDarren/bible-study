# Song of Songs 1 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The song of songs, which is Solomon’s.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let him kiss me with the kisses of his mouth: for thy love is better than wine.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Because of the savour of thy good ointments thy name is as ointment poured forth, therefore do the virgins love thee.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Draw me, we will run after thee: the king hath brought me into his chambers: we will be glad and rejoice in thee, we will remember thy love more than wine: the upright love thee.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I am black, but comely, O ye daughters of Jerusalem, as the tents of Kedar, as the curtains of Solomon.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Look not upon me, because I am black, because the sun hath looked upon me: my mother’s children were angry with me; they made me the keeper of the vineyards; but mine own vineyard have I not kept.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Tell me, O thou whom my soul loveth, where thou feedest, where thou makest thy flock to rest at noon: for why should I be as one that turneth aside by the flocks of thy companions?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** If thou know not, O thou fairest among women, go thy way forth by the footsteps of the flock, and feed thy kids beside the shepherds’ tents.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I have compared thee, O my love, to a company of horses in Pharaoh’s chariots.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Thy cheeks are comely with rows of jewels, thy neck with chains of gold.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** We will make thee borders of gold with studs of silver.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** While the king sitteth at his table, my spikenard sendeth forth the smell thereof.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** A bundle of myrrh is my wellbeloved unto me; he shall lie all night betwixt my breasts.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** My beloved is unto me as a cluster of camphire in the vineyards of En–gedi.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Behold, thou art fair, my love; behold, thou art fair; thou hast doves’ eyes.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Behold, thou art fair, my beloved, yea, pleasant: also our bed is green.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The beams of our house are cedar, and our rafters of fir.
