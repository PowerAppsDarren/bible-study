# Song of Songs 6 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Where is your loved one gone, O most fair among women? Where is your loved one turned away, that we may go looking for him with you?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** My loved one is gone down into his garden, to the beds of spices, to take food in the gardens, and to get lilies.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I am for my loved one, and my loved one is for me; he takes food among the lilies.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You are beautiful, O my love, as Tirzah, as fair as Jerusalem; you are to be feared like an army with flags.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let your eyes be turned away from me; see, they have overcome me; your hair is as a flock of goats which take their rest on the side of Gilead.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Your teeth are like a flock of sheep which come up from the washing; every one has two lambs, and there is not one without young.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Like pomegranate fruit are the sides of your head under your veil.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** There are sixty queens, and eighty servant-wives, and young girls without number.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** My dove, my very beautiful one, is but one; she is the only one of her mother, she is the dearest one of her who gave her birth. The daughters saw her, and gave her a blessing; yes, the queens and the servant-wives, and they gave her praises.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Who is she, looking down as the morning light, fair as the moon, clear as the sun, who is to be feared like an army with flags?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I went down into the garden of nuts to see the green plants of the valley, and to see if the vine was in bud, and the pomegranate-trees were in flower.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Before I was conscious of it, ...

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Come back, come back, O Shulammite; come back, come back, so that our eyes may see you. What will you see in the Shulammite? A sword-dance.
