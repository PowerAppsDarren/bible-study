# Song of Songs 8 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Oh that you were my brother, who took milk from my mother's breasts! When I came to you in the street, I would give you kisses; yes, I would not be looked down on.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I would take you by the hand into my mother's house, and she would be my teacher. I would give you drink of spiced wine, drink of the pomegranate.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** His left hand would be under my head, and his right hand about me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I say to you, O daughters of Jerusalem, do not let love be moved till it is ready.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Who is this, who comes up from the waste places, resting on her loved one? It was I who made you awake under the apple-tree, where your mother gave you birth; there she was in pain at your birth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Put me as a sign on your heart, as a sign on your arm; love is strong as death, and wrath bitter as the underworld: its coals are coals of fire; violent are its flames.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Much water may not put out love, or the deep waters overcome it: if a man would give all the substance of his house for love, it would be judged a price not great enough.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** We have a young sister, and she has no breasts; what are we to do for our sister in the day when she is given to a man?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** If she is a wall, we will make on her a strong base of silver; and if she is a door, we will let her be shut up with cedar-wood.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I am a wall, and my breasts are like towers; then was I in his eyes as one to whom good chance had come.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Solomon had a vine-garden at Baal-hamon; he let out the vine-garden to keepers; every one had to give a thousand bits of silver for its fruit.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** My vine-garden, which is mine, is before me: you, O Solomon, will have the thousand, and those who keep the fruit of them two hundred.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** You who have your resting-place in the gardens, the friends give ear to your voice; make me give ear to it.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Come quickly, my loved one, and be like a roe on the mountains of spice.
