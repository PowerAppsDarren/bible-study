# Song of Songs 7 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** How beautiful are your feet in their shoes, O king's daughter! The curves of your legs are like jewels, the work of the hands of a good workman:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Your stomach is a store of grain with lilies round it, and in the middle a round cup full of wine.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Your two breasts are like two young roes of the same birth.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Your neck is as a tower of ivory; your eyes like the waters in Heshbon, by the doorway of Bath-rabbim; your nose is as the tower on Lebanon looking over Damascus:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Your head is like Carmel, and the hair of your head is like purple, in whose net the king is prisoner.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** How beautiful and how sweet you are, O love, for delight.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You are tall like a palm-tree, and your breasts are like the fruit of the vine.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I said, Let me go up the palm-tree, and let me take its branches in my hands: your breasts will be as the fruit of the vine, and the smell of your breath like apples;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And the roof of your mouth like good wine flowing down smoothly for my loved one, moving gently over my lips and my teeth.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I am for my loved one, and his desire is for me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Come, my loved one, let us go out into the field; let us take rest among the cypress-trees.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Let us go out early to the vine-gardens; let us see if the vine is in bud, if it has put out its young fruit, and the pomegranate is in flower. There I will give you my love.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The mandrakes give out a sweet smell, and at our doors are all sorts of good fruits, new and old, which I have kept for my loved one.
