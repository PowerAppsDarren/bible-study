# Philippians 1 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Paul and Timothy, servants of Christ Jesus, to all the saints in Christ Jesus that are at Philippi, with the bishops and deacons:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Grace to you and peace from God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I thank my God upon all my remembrance of you,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** always in every supplication of mine on behalf of you all making my supplication with joy,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** for your fellowship in furtherance of the gospel from the first day until now;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** being confident of this very thing, that he who began a good work in you will perfect it until the day of Jesus Christ:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** even as it is right for me to be thus minded on behalf of you all, because I have you in my heart, inasmuch as, both in my bonds and in the defence and confirmation of the gospel, ye all are partakers with me of grace.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For God is my witness, how I long after you all in the tender mercies of Christ Jesus.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And this I pray, that your love may abound yet more and more in knowledge and all discernment;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** so that ye may approve the things that are excellent; that ye may be sincere and void of offence unto the day of Christ;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** being filled with the fruits of righteousness, which are through Jesus Christ, unto the glory and praise of God.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Now I would have you know, brethren, that the thingswhich happened unto me have fallen out rather unto the progress of the gospel;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** so that my bonds became manifest in Christ throughout the whole prætorian guard, and to all the rest;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** and that most of the brethren in the Lord, being confident through my bonds, are more abundantly bold to speak the word of God without fear.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Some indeed preach Christ even of envy and strife; and some also of good will:

<a id="v16"></a>**16** the one do it of love, knowing that I am set for the defence of the gospel;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** but the other proclaim Christ of faction, not sincerely, thinking to raise up affliction for me in my bonds.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** What then? only that in every way, whether in pretence or in truth, Christ is proclaimed; and therein I rejoice, yea, and will rejoice.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** For I know that this shall turn out to my salvation, through your supplication and the supply of the Spirit of Jesus Christ,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** according to my earnest expectation and hope, that in nothing shall I be put to shame, but that with all boldness, as always, so now also Christ shall be magnified in my body, whether by life, or by death.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For to me to live is Christ, and to die is gain.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But if to live in the flesh,—if this shall bring fruit from my work, then what I shall choose I know not.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** But I am in a strait betwixt the two, having the desire to depart and be with Christ; for it is very far better:

<a id="v24"></a>**24** yet to abide in the flesh is more needful for your sake.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And having this confidence, I know that I shall abide, yea, and abide with you all, for your progress and joy in the faith;

<a id="v26"></a>**26** that your glorying may abound in Christ Jesus in me through my presence with you again.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Only let your manner of life be worthy of the gospel of Christ: that, whether I come and see you or be absent, I may hear of your state, that ye stand fast in one spirit, with one soul striving for the faith of the gospel;

<a id="v28"></a>**28** and in nothing affrighted by the adversaries: which is for them an evident token of perdition, but of your salvation, and that from God;

<a id="v29"></a>**29** because to you it hath been granted in the behalf of Christ, not only to believe on him, but also to suffer in his behalf:

<a id="v30"></a>**30** having the same conflict which ye saw in me, and now hear to be in me.
