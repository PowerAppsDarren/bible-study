# Philippians 1 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Paul and Timotheus, the servants of Jesus Christ, to all the saints in Christ Jesus which are at Philippi, with the bishops and deacons:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Grace be unto you, and peace, from God our Father, and from the Lord Jesus Christ.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I thank my God upon every remembrance of you,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Always in every prayer of mine for you all making request with joy,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For your fellowship in the gospel from the first day until now;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Being confident of this very thing, that he which hath begun a good work in you will perform it until the day of Jesus Christ:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Even as it is meet for me to think this of you all, because I have you in my heart; inasmuch as both in my bonds, and in the defence and confirmation of the gospel, ye all are partakers of my grace.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For God is my record, how greatly I long after you all in the bowels of Jesus Christ.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And this I pray, that your love may abound yet more and more in knowledge and in all judgment;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** That ye may approve things that are excellent; that ye may be sincere and without offence till the day of Christ;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Being filled with the fruits of righteousness, which are by Jesus Christ, unto the glory and praise of God.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But I would ye should understand, brethren, that the things which happened unto me have fallen out rather unto the furtherance of the gospel;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** So that my bonds in Christ are manifest in all the palace, and in all other places;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And many of the brethren in the Lord, waxing confident by my bonds, are much more bold to speak the word without fear.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Some indeed preach Christ even of envy and strife; and some also of good will:

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The one preach Christ of contention, not sincerely, supposing to add affliction to my bonds:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** But the other of love, knowing that I am set for the defence of the gospel.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** What then? notwithstanding, every way, whether in pretence, or in truth, Christ is preached; and I therein do rejoice, yea, and will rejoice.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** For I know that this shall turn to my salvation through your prayer, and the supply of the Spirit of Jesus Christ,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** According to my earnest expectation and my hope, that in nothing I shall be ashamed, but that with all boldness, as always, so now also Christ shall be magnified in my body, whether it be by life, or by death.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For to me to live is Christ, and to die is gain.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But if I live in the flesh, this is the fruit of my labour: yet what I shall choose I wot not.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** For I am in a strait betwixt two, having a desire to depart, and to be with Christ; which is far better:

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Nevertheless to abide in the flesh is more needful for you.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And having this confidence, I know that I shall abide and continue with you all for your furtherance and joy of faith;

<a id="v26"></a>**26** That your rejoicing may be more abundant in Jesus Christ for me by my coming to you again.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Only let your conversation be as it becometh the gospel of Christ: that whether I come and see you, or else be absent, I may hear of your affairs, that ye stand fast in one spirit, with one mind striving together for the faith of the gospel;

<a id="v28"></a>**28** And in nothing terrified by your adversaries: which is to them an evident token of perdition, but to you of salvation, and that of God.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** For unto you it is given in the behalf of Christ, not only to believe on him, but also to suffer for his sake;

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Having the same conflict which ye saw in me, and now hear to be in me.
