# Lamentations 4 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** How the gold has become dim! The most pure gold has changed!  The stones of the sanctuary are poured out at the head of every street.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The precious sons of Zion, comparable to fine gold, How are they esteemed as earthen pitchers, the work of the hands of the potter!

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Even the jackals draw out the breast, they nurse their young ones: The daughter of my people has become cruel, like the ostriches in the wilderness.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The tongue of the nursing child clings to the roof of his mouth for thirst: The young children ask bread, and no man breaks it to them.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Those who fed delicately are desolate in the streets: Those who were brought up in scarlet embrace dunghills.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For the iniquity of the daughter of my people is greater than the sin of Sodom, That was overthrown as in a moment, and no hands were laid on her.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Her nobles were purer than snow, they were whiter than milk; They were more ruddy in body than rubies, their polishing was as of sapphire.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Their appearance is blacker than a coal; they are not known in the streets: Their skin clings to their bones; it is withered, it has become like a stick.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Those who are killed with the sword are better than those who are killed with hunger; For these pine away, stricken through, for want of the fruits of the field.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The hands of the pitiful women have boiled their own children; They were their food in the destruction of the daughter of my people.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Yahweh has accomplished his wrath, he has poured out his fierce anger; He has kindled a fire in Zion, which has devoured its foundations.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The kings of the earth didn’t believe, neither all the inhabitants of the world, That the adversary and the enemy would enter into the gates of Jerusalem.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** It is because of the sins of her prophets, and the iniquities of her priests, That have shed the blood of the just in the midst of her.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** They wander as blind men in the streets, they are polluted with blood, So that men can’t touch their garments.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Depart! they cried to them, Unclean! depart, depart, don’t touch! When they fled away and wandered, men said among the nations, They shall not live here any more.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Yahweh’s anger has scattered them; he will no more regard them: They didn’t respect the persons of the priests, they didn’t favor the elders.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Our eyes still fail, looking in vain for our help: In our watching we have watched for a nation that could not save.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** They hunt our steps, so that we can’t go in our streets: Our end is near, our days are fulfilled; for our end has come.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Our pursuers were swifter than the eagles of the sky: They chased us on the mountains, they laid wait for us in the wilderness.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The breath of our nostrils, the anointed of Yahweh, was taken in their pits; Of whom we said, Under his shadow we shall live among the nations.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Rejoice and be glad, daughter of Edom, that dwell in the land of Uz: The cup shall pass through to you also; you shall be drunken, and shall make yourself naked.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The punishment of your iniquity is accomplished, daughter of Zion; he will no more carry you away into captivity: He will visit your iniquity, daughter of Edom; he will uncover your sins.
