# Lamentations 3 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I am the man that has seen affliction by the rod of his wrath.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He has led me and caused me to walk in darkness, and not in light.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Surely against me he turns his hand again and again all the day.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** My flesh and my skin has he made old; he has broken my bones.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He has built against me, and surrounded me with gall and travail.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He has made me to dwell in dark places, as those that have been long dead.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He has walled me about, that I can’t go out; he has made my chain heavy.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yes, when I cry, and call for help, he shuts out my prayer.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He has walled up my ways with cut stone; he has made my paths crooked.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He is to me as a bear lying in wait, as a lion in secret places.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He has turned aside my ways, and pulled me in pieces; he has made me desolate.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** He has bent his bow, and set me as a mark for the arrow.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He has caused the shafts of his quiver to enter into my kidneys.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I am become a derision to all my people, and their song all the day.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He has filled me with bitterness, he has sated me with wormwood.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He has also broken my teeth with gravel stones; he has covered me with ashes.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** You have removed my soul far off from peace; I forgot prosperity.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** I said, My strength is perished, and my expectation from Yahweh.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Remember my affliction and my misery, the wormwood and the gall.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My soul still remembers them, and is bowed down within me.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** This I recall to my mind; therefore have I hope.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** It is because of Yahweh’s loving kindnesses that we are not consumed, because his compassion doesn’t fail.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** They are new every morning; great is your faithfulness.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Yahweh is my portion, says my soul; therefore will I hope in him.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Yahweh is good to those who wait for him, to the soul that seeks him.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** It is good that a man should hope and quietly wait for the salvation of Yahweh.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** It is good for a man that he bear the yoke in his youth.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Let him sit alone and keep silence, because he has laid it on him.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Let him put his mouth in the dust, if so be there may be hope.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Let him give his cheek to him who strikes him; let him be filled full with reproach.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For the Lord will not cast off forever.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** For though he cause grief, yet he will have compassion according to the multitude of his loving kindnesses.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** For he does not afflict willingly, nor grieve the children of men.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** To crush under foot all the prisoners of the earth,

<a id="v35"></a>**35** To turn aside the right of a man before the face of the Most High,

<a id="v36"></a>**36** To subvert a man in his cause, the Lord doesn’t approve.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Who is he who says, and it comes to pass, when the Lord doesn’t command it?

<a id="v38"></a>**38** Doesn’t evil and good come out of the mouth of the Most High?

<a id="v39"></a>**39** Why does a living man complain, a man for the punishment of his sins?

<a id="v40"></a>**40** Let us search and try our ways, and turn again to Yahweh.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** Let us lift up our heart with our hands to God in the heavens.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** We have transgressed and have rebelled; you have not pardoned.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** You have covered with anger and pursued us; you have killed, you have not pitied.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** You have covered yourself with a cloud, so that no prayer can pass through.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** You have made us an off-scouring and refuse in the midst of the peoples.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** All our enemies have opened their mouth wide against us.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** Fear and the pit have come on us, devastation and destruction.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** My eye runs down with streams of water, for the destruction of the daughter of my people.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** My eye pours down, and doesn’t cease, without any intermission,

<a id="v50"></a>**50** Until Yahweh look down, and see from heaven.

<a id="v51"></a>**51** My eye affects my soul, because of all the daughters of my city.

<a id="v52"></a>**52** They have chased me relentlessly like a bird, those who are my enemies without cause.

<a id="v53"></a>**53** They have cut off my life in the dungeon, and have cast a stone on me.

<a id="v54"></a>**54** Waters flowed over my head; I said, I am cut off.

<a id="v55"></a>**55** I called on your name, Yahweh, out of the lowest dungeon.

<a id="v56"></a>**56** You heard my voice; don’t hide your ear at my breathing, at my cry.

<a id="v57"></a>**57** You drew near in the day that I called on you; you said, Don’t be afraid.

<a id="v58"></a>**58** Lord, you have pleaded the causes of my soul; you have redeemed my life.

<a id="v59"></a>**59** Yahweh, you have seen my wrong. Judge my cause.

<a id="v60"></a>**60** You have seen all their vengeance and all their devices against me.

<a id="v61"></a>**61** You have heard their reproach, Yahweh, and all their devices against me,

<a id="v62"></a>**62** The lips of those that rose up against me, and their device against me all the day.

<a id="v63"></a>**63** You see their sitting down, and their rising up; I am their song.

<a id="v64"></a>**64** You will render to them a recompense, Yahweh, according to the work of their hands.

<a id="v65"></a>**65** You will give them hardness of heart, your curse to them.

<a id="v66"></a>**66** You will pursue them in anger, and destroy them from under the heavens of Yahweh.
