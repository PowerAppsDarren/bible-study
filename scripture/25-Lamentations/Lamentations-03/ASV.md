# Lamentations 3 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I am the man that hath seen affliction by the rod of his wrath.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He hath led me and caused me to walk in darkness, and not in light.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Surely against me he turneth his hand again and again all the day.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** My flesh and my skin hath he made old; he hath broken my bones.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He hath builded against me, and compassed me with gall and travail.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He hath made me to dwell in dark places, as those that have been long dead.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He hath walled me about, that I cannot go forth; he hath made my chain heavy.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yea, when I cry, and call for help, he shutteth out my prayer.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He hath walled up my ways with hewn stone; he hath made my paths crooked.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He is unto me as a bear lying in wait, as a lion in secret places.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He hath turned aside my ways, and pulled me in pieces; he hath made me desolate.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** He hath bent his bow, and set me as a mark for the arrow.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He hath caused the shafts of his quiver to enter into my reins.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I am become a derision to all my people, and their song all the day.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He hath filled me with bitterness, he hath sated me with wormwood.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He hath also broken my teeth with gravel stones; he hath covered me with ashes.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And thou hast removed my soul far off from peace; I forgat prosperity.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And I said, My strength is perished, and mine expectation from Jehovah.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Remember mine affliction and my misery, the wormwood and the gall.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My soul hath them still in remembrance, and is bowed down within me.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** This I recall to my mind; therefore have I hope.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** It is of Jehovah’s lovingkindnesses that we are not consumed, because his compassions fail not.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** They are new every morning; great is thy faithfulness.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Jehovah is my portion, saith my soul; therefore will I hope in him.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Jehovah is good unto them that wait for him, to the soul that seeketh him.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** It is good that a man should hope and quietly wait for the salvation of Jehovah.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** It is good for a man that he bear the yoke in his youth.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Let him sit alone and keep silence, because he hath laid it upon him.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Let him put his mouth in the dust, if so be there may be hope.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Let him give his cheek to him that smiteth him; let him be filled full with reproach.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For the Lord will not cast off for ever.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** For though he cause grief, yet will he have compassion according to the multitude of his lovingkindnesses.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** For he doth not afflict willingly, nor grieve the children of men.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** To crush under foot all the prisoners of the earth,

<a id="v35"></a>**35** To turn aside the right of a man before the face of the Most High,

<a id="v36"></a>**36** To subvert a man in his cause, the Lord approveth not.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Who is he that saith, and it cometh to pass, when the Lord commandeth it not?

<a id="v38"></a>**38** Out of the mouth of the Most High cometh there not evil and good?

<a id="v39"></a>**39** Wherefore doth a living man complain, a man for the punishment of his sins?

<a id="v40"></a>**40** Let us search and try our ways, and turn again to Jehovah.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** Let us lift up our heart with our hands unto God in the heavens.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** We have transgressed and have rebelled; thou hast not pardoned.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Thou hast covered with anger and pursued us; thou hast slain, thou hast not pitied.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** Thou hast covered thyself with a cloud, so that no prayer can pass through.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** Thou hast made us an offscouring and refuse in the midst of the peoples.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** All our enemies have opened their mouth wide against us.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** Fear and the pit are come upon us, devastation and destruction.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** Mine eye runneth down with streams of water, for the destruction of the daughter of my people.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** Mine eye poureth down, and ceaseth not, without any intermission,

<a id="v50"></a>**50** Till Jehovah look down, and behold from heaven.

<a id="v51"></a>**51** Mine eye affecteth my soul, because of all the daughters of my city.

<a id="v52"></a>**52** They have chased me sore like a bird, they that are mine enemies without cause.

<a id="v53"></a>**53** They have cut off my life in the dungeon, and have cast a stone upon me.

<a id="v54"></a>**54** Waters flowed over my head; I said, I am cut off.

<a id="v55"></a>**55** I called upon thy name, O Jehovah, out of the lowest dungeon.

<a id="v56"></a>**56** Thou heardest my voice; hide not thine ear at my breathing, at my cry.

<a id="v57"></a>**57** Thou drewest near in the day that I called upon thee; thou saidst, Fear not.

<a id="v58"></a>**58** O Lord, thou hast pleaded the causes of my soul; thou hast redeemed my life.

<a id="v59"></a>**59** O Jehovah, thou hast seen my wrong; judge thou my cause.

<a id="v60"></a>**60** Thou hast seen all their vengeance and all their devices against me.

<a id="v61"></a>**61** Thou hast heard their reproach, O Jehovah, and all their devices against me,

<a id="v62"></a>**62** The lips of those that rose up against me, and their device against me all the day.

<a id="v63"></a>**63** Behold thou their sitting down, and their rising up; I am their song.

<a id="v64"></a>**64** Thou wilt render unto them a recompense, O Jehovah, according to the work of their hands.

<a id="v65"></a>**65** Thou wilt give them hardness of heart, thy curse unto them.

<a id="v66"></a>**66** Thou wilt pursue them in anger, and destroy them from under the heavens of Jehovah.
