# Lamentations 3 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I am the man who has seen trouble by the rod of his wrath.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** By him I have been made to go in the dark where there is no light.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Truly against me his hand has been turned again and again all the day.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** My flesh and my skin have been used up by him and my bones broken.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He has put up a wall against me, shutting me in with bitter sorrow.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He has kept me in dark places, like those who have been long dead.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He has put a wall round me, so that I am not able to go out; he has made great the weight of my chain.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Even when I send up a cry for help, he keeps my prayer shut out.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He has put up a wall of cut stones about my ways, he has made my roads twisted.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He is like a bear waiting for me, like a lion in secret places.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** By him my ways have been turned on one side and I have been pulled in bits; he has made me waste.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** With his bow bent, he has made me the mark for his arrows.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He has let loose his arrows into the inmost parts of my body.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I have become the sport of all the peoples; I am their song all the day.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He has made my life nothing but pain, he has given me the bitter root in full measure.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** By him my teeth have been broken with crushed stones, and I am bent low in the dust.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** My soul is sent far away from peace, I have no more memory of good.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And I said, My strength is cut off, and my hope from the Lord.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Keep in mind my trouble and my wandering, the bitter root and the poison.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My soul still keeps the memory of them; and is bent down in me.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** This I keep in mind, and because of this I have hope.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** It is through the Lord's love that we have not come to destruction, because his mercies have no limit.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** They are new every morning; great is your good faith.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** I said to myself, The Lord is my heritage; and because of this I will have hope in him.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** The Lord is good to those who are waiting for him, to the soul which is looking for him.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** It is good to go on hoping and quietly waiting for the salvation of the Lord.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** It is good for a man to undergo the yoke when he is young.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Let him be seated by himself, saying nothing, because he has put it on him.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Let him put his mouth in the dust, if by chance there may be hope.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Let his face be turned to him who gives him blows; let him be full of shame.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For the Lord does not give a man up for ever.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** For though he sends grief, still he will have pity in the full measure of his love.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** For he has no pleasure in troubling and causing grief to the children of men.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** In a man's crushing under his feet all the prisoners of the earth,

<a id="v35"></a>**35** In his turning away the right of a man before the face of the Most High.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** In his doing wrong to a man in his cause, the Lord has no pleasure.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Who is able to say a thing, and give effect to it, if it has not been ordered by the Lord?

<a id="v38"></a>**38** Do not evil and good come from the mouth of the Most High?

<a id="v39"></a>**39** What protest may a living man make, even a man about the punishment of his sin?

<a id="v40"></a>**40** Let us make search and put our ways to the test, turning again to the Lord;

<a id="v41"></a>**41** Lifting up our hearts with our hands to God in the heavens.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** We have done wrong and gone against your law; we have not had your forgiveness.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Covering yourself with wrath you have gone after us, cutting us off without pity;

<a id="v44"></a>**44** Covering yourself with a cloud, so that prayer may not get through.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** You have made us like waste and that for which there is no use, among the peoples.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** The mouths of all our haters are open wide against us.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** Fear and deep waters have come on us, wasting and destruction.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** Rivers of water are running down from my eyes, for the destruction of the daughter of my people.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** My eyes are streaming without stopping, they have no rest,

<a id="v50"></a>**50** Till the Lord's eye is turned on me, till he sees my trouble from heaven.

<a id="v51"></a>**51** The Lord is unkind to my soul, more than all the daughters of my town.

<a id="v52"></a>**52** They who are against me without cause have gone hard after me as if I was a bird;

<a id="v53"></a>**53** They have put an end to my life in the prison, stoning me with stones.

<a id="v54"></a>**54** Waters were flowing over my head; I said, I am cut off.

<a id="v55"></a>**55** I was making prayer to your name, O Lord, out of the lowest prison.

<a id="v56"></a>**56** My voice came to you; let not your ear be shut to my breathing, to my cry.

<a id="v57"></a>**57** You came near in the day when I made my prayer to you: you said, Have no fear.

<a id="v58"></a>**58** O Lord, you have taken up the cause of my soul, you have made my life safe.

<a id="v59"></a>**59** O Lord, you have seen my wrong; be judge in my cause.

<a id="v60"></a>**60** You have seen all the evil rewards they have sent on me, and all their designs against me.

<a id="v61"></a>**61** Their bitter words have come to your ears, O Lord, and all their designs against me;

<a id="v62"></a>**62** The lips of those who came up against me, and their thoughts against me all the day.

<a id="v63"></a>**63** Take note of them when they are seated, and when they get up; I am their song.

<a id="v64"></a>**64** You will give them their reward, O Lord, answering to the work of their hands.

<a id="v65"></a>**65** You will let their hearts be covered over with your curse on them.

<a id="v66"></a>**66** You will go after them in wrath, and put an end to them from under the heavens of the Lord.
