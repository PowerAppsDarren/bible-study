# Lamentations 5 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Remember, O Jehovah, what is come upon us: Behold, and see our reproach.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Our inheritance is turned unto strangers, Our houses unto aliens.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** We are orphans and fatherless; Our mothers are as widows.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** We have drunken our water for money; Our wood is sold unto us.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Our pursuers are upon our necks: We are weary, and have no rest.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** We have given the hand to the Egyptians, And to the Assyrians, to be satisfied with bread.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Our fathers sinned, and are not; And we have borne their iniquities.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Servants rule over us: There is none to deliver us out of their hand.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** We get our bread at the peril of our lives, Because of the sword of the wilderness.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Our skin is black like an oven, Because of the burning heat of famine.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They ravished the women in Zion, The virgins in the cities of Judah.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Princes were hanged up by their hand: The faces of elders were not honored.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The young men bare the mill; And the children stumbled under the wood.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The elders have ceased from the gate, The young men from their music.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The joy of our heart is ceased; Our dance is turned into mourning.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The crown is fallen from our head: Woe unto us! for we have sinned.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For this our heart is faint; For these things our eyes are dim;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For the mountain of Zion, which is desolate: The foxes walk upon it.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Thou, O Jehovah, abidest for ever; Thy throne is from generation to generation.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Wherefore dost thou forget us for ever, Andforsake us so long time?

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Turn thou us unto thee, O Jehovah, and we shall be turned; Renew our days as of old.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But thou hast utterly rejected us; Thou art very wroth against us.
