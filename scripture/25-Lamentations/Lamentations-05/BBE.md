# Lamentations 5 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Keep in mind, O Lord, what has come to us: take note and see our shame.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Our heritage is given up to men of strange lands, our houses to those who are not our countrymen.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** We are children without fathers, our mothers are like widows.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** We give money for a drink of water, we get our wood for a price.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Our attackers are on our necks: overcome with weariness, we have no rest.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** We have given our hands to the Egyptians and to the Assyrians so that we might have enough bread.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Our fathers were sinners and are dead; and the weight of their evil-doing is on us.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Servants are ruling over us, and there is no one to make us free from their hands.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** We put our lives in danger to get our bread, because of the sword of the waste land.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Our skin is heated like an oven because of our burning heat from need of food.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They took by force the women in Zion, the virgins in the towns of Judah.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Their hands put princes to death by hanging: the faces of old men were not honoured.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The young men were crushing the grain, and the boys were falling under the wood.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The old men are no longer seated in the doorway, and the music of the young men has come to an end.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The joy of our hearts is ended; our dancing is changed into sorrow.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The crown has been taken from our head: sorrow is ours, for we are sinners.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Because of this our hearts are feeble; for these things our eyes are dark;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Because of the mountain of Zion which is a waste; jackals go over it.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** You, O Lord, are seated as King for ever; the seat of your power is eternal.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Why have we gone from your memory for ever? why have you been turned away from us for so long?

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Make us come back to you, O Lord, and let us be turned; make our days new again as in the past.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But you have quite given us up; you are full of wrath against us.
