# Lamentations 5 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Remember, O Lord, what is come upon us: consider, and behold our reproach.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Our inheritance is turned to strangers, our houses to aliens.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** We are orphans and fatherless, our mothers are as widows.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** We have drunken our water for money; our wood is sold unto us.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Our necks are under persecution: we labour, and have no rest.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** We have given the hand to the Egyptians, and to the Assyrians, to be satisfied with bread.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Our fathers have sinned, and are not; and we have borne their iniquities.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Servants have ruled over us: there is none that doth deliver us out of their hand.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** We gat our bread with the peril of our lives because of the sword of the wilderness.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Our skin was black like an oven because of the terrible famine.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They ravished the women in Zion, and the maids in the cities of Judah.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Princes are hanged up by their hand: the faces of elders were not honoured.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** They took the young men to grind, and the children fell under the wood.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The elders have ceased from the gate, the young men from their musick.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The joy of our heart is ceased; our dance is turned into mourning.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The crown is fallen from our head: woe unto us, that we have sinned!

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For this our heart is faint; for these things our eyes are dim.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Because of the mountain of Zion, which is desolate, the foxes walk upon it.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Thou, O Lord, remainest for ever; thy throne from generation to generation.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Wherefore dost thou forget us for ever, and forsake us so long time?

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Turn thou us unto thee, O Lord, and we shall be turned; renew our days as of old.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But thou hast utterly rejected us; thou art very wroth against us.
