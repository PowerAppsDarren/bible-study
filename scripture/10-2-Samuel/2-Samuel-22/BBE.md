# 2 Samuel 22 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And David made a song to the Lord in these words, on the day when the Lord made him free from the hands of all his haters, and from the hand of Saul:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And he said, The Lord is my Rock, my walled town, and my saviour, even mine;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** My God, my Rock, in him will I put my faith; my breastplate, and the horn of my salvation, my high tower, and my safe place; my saviour, who keeps me safe from the violent man.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will send up my cry to the Lord, who is to be praised; so will I be made safe from those who are against me.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For the waves of death came round me, and the seas of evil put me in fear;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The cords of hell were round me: the nets of death came on me.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** In my trouble my voice went up to the Lord, and my cry to my God: my voice came to his hearing in his holy Temple, and my prayer came to his ears.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Then the earth was moved with a violent shock; the bases of heaven were moved and shaking, because he was angry.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** There went up a smoke from his nose, and a fire of destruction from his mouth: coals were lighted by it.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The heavens were bent, so that he might come down; and it was dark under his feet.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And he went through the air, seated on a storm-cloud: going quickly on the wings of the wind.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And he made the dark his tent round him, a mass of waters, thick clouds of the skies.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Before his shining light his dark clouds went past, raining ice and coals of fire.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The Lord made thunder in the heavens, and the voice of the Highest was sounding out.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And he sent out his arrows, driving them in all directions; by his flames of fire they were troubled.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Then the deep beds of the sea were seen, and the bases of the world were uncovered, because of the Lord's wrath, because of the breath of his mouth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He sent from on high, he took me, pulling me out of great waters.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He made me free from my strong hater, from those who were against me, because they were stronger than I.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** They came on me in the day of my trouble: but the Lord was my support.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He took me out into a wide place; he was my saviour because he had delight in me.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The Lord gives me the reward of my righteousness, because my hands are clean before him.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For I have kept the ways of the Lord; I have not been turned away in sin from my God.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** For all his decisions were before me, and I did not put away his laws from me.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And I was upright before him, and I kept myself from sin.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Because of this the Lord has given me the reward of my righteousness, because my hands are clean in his eyes.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** On him who has mercy you will have mercy; to the upright you will be upright;

<a id="v27"></a>**27** He who is holy will see that you are holy; but to the man whose way is not straight you will be a hard judge.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** For you are the saviour of those who are in trouble; but your eyes are on men of pride, to make them low.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** For you are my light, O Lord; and the Lord will make the dark bright for me.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** By your help I have made a way through the wall which was shutting me in: by the help of my God I have gone over a wall.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** As for God, his way is all good: the word of the Lord is tested; he is a safe cover for all those who put their faith in him.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** For who is God but the Lord? and who is a Rock but our God?

<a id="v33"></a>**33** God puts a strong band about me, guiding me in a straight way.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** He makes my feet like roes' feet, and puts me on high places.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** He makes my hands expert in war, so that a bow of brass is bent by my arms.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** You have given me the breastplate of your salvation, and your mercy has made me great.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** You have made my steps wide under me, so that my feet make no slip.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** I go after my haters and overtake them; not turning back till they are all overcome.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** I have sent destruction on them and given them wounds, so that they are not able to get up: they are stretched under my feet.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** For I have been armed by you with strength for the fight: you have made low under me those who came out against me.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** By you their backs are turned in flight, so that my haters are cut off.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** They were crying out, but there was no one to come to their help: even to the Lord, but he gave them no answer.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Then they were crushed as small as the dust of the earth, stamped down under my feet like the waste of the streets.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** You have made me free from the fightings of my people; you have made me the head of the nations: a people of whom I had no knowledge will be my servants.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** Men of other countries will, with false hearts, put themselves under my authority: from the time when my name comes to their ears, they will be ruled by me.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** They will be wasted away, they will come out of their secret places shaking with fear.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** The Lord is living; praise be to my Rock, and let the God of my salvation be honoured:

<a id="v48"></a>**48** It is God who sends punishment on my haters, and puts peoples under my rule.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** He makes me free from my haters: I am lifted up over those who come up against me: you have made me free from the violent man.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** Because of this I will give you praise, O Lord, among the nations, and will make a song of praise to your name.

<a id="v51"></a>**51** Great salvation does he give to his king; he has mercy on the king of his selection, David, and on his seed for ever.
