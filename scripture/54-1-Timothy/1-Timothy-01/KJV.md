# 1 Timothy 1 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Paul, an apostle of Jesus Christ by the commandment of God our Saviour, and Lord Jesus Christ, which is our hope;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Unto Timothy, my own son in the faith: Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** As I besought thee to abide still at Ephesus, when I went into Macedonia, that thou mightest charge some that they teach no other doctrine,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Neither give heed to fables and endless genealogies, which minister questions, rather than godly edifying which is in faith: so do.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Now the end of the commandment is charity out of a pure heart, and of a good conscience, and of faith unfeigned:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** From which some having swerved have turned aside unto vain jangling;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Desiring to be teachers of the law; understanding neither what they say, nor whereof they affirm.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But we know that the law is good, if a man use it lawfully;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Knowing this, that the law is not made for a righteous man, but for the lawless and disobedient, for the ungodly and for sinners, for unholy and profane, for murderers of fathers and murderers of mothers, for manslayers,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For whoremongers, for them that defile themselves with mankind, for menstealers, for liars, for perjured persons, and if there be any other thing that is contrary to sound doctrine;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** According to the glorious gospel of the blessed God, which was committed to my trust.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And I thank Christ Jesus our Lord, who hath enabled me, for that he counted me faithful, putting me into the ministry;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Who was before a blasphemer, and a persecutor, and injurious: but I obtained mercy, because I did it ignorantly in unbelief.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And the grace of our Lord was exceeding abundant with faith and love which is in Christ Jesus.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** This is a faithful saying, and worthy of all acceptation, that Christ Jesus came into the world to save sinners; of whom I am chief.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Howbeit for this cause I obtained mercy, that in me first Jesus Christ might shew forth all longsuffering, for a pattern to them which should hereafter believe on him to life everlasting.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Now unto the King eternal, immortal, invisible, the only wise God, be honour and glory for ever and ever. Amen.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** This charge I commit unto thee, son Timothy, according to the prophecies which went before on thee, that thou by them mightest war a good warfare;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Holding faith, and a good conscience; which some having put away concerning faith have made shipwreck:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Of whom is Hymeneus and Alexander; whom I have delivered unto Satan, that they may learn not to blaspheme.
