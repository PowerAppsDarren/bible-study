# 1 Timothy 1 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Paul, an apostle of Christ Jesus according to the commandment of God our Savior, and Christ Jesus our hope;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** to Timothy, my true child in faith: Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father and Christ Jesus our Lord.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** As I urged you when I was going into Macedonia, stay at Ephesus that you might command certain men not to teach a different doctrine,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** neither to pay attention to myths and endless genealogies, which cause disputes, rather than God’s stewardship, which is in faith—

<a id="v5"></a>**5** but the goal of this command is love, out of a pure heart and a good conscience and sincere faith;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** from which things some, having missed the mark, have turned aside to vain talking;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** desiring to be teachers of the law, though they understand neither what they say, nor about what they strongly affirm.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But we know that the law is good, if a man uses it lawfully,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** as knowing this, that law is not made for a righteous man, but for the lawless and insubordinate, for the ungodly and sinners, for the unholy and profane, for murderers of fathers and murderers of mothers, for man slayers,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** for the sexually immoral, for homosexuals, for slave-traders, for liars, for perjurers, and for any other thing contrary to the sound doctrine;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** according to the Good News of the glory of the blessed God, which was committed to my trust.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And I thank him who enabled me, Christ Jesus our Lord, because he counted me faithful, appointing me to service;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** although I was before a blasphemer, a persecutor, and insolent. However, I obtained mercy, because I did it ignorantly in unbelief.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The grace of our Lord abounded exceedingly with faith and love which is in Christ Jesus.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The saying is faithful and worthy of all acceptance, that Christ Jesus came into the world to save sinners; of whom I am chief.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** However, for this cause I obtained mercy, that in me first, Jesus Christ might display all his patience, for an example of those who were going to believe in him for eternal life.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Now to the King eternal, immortal, invisible, to God who alone is wise, be honor and glory forever and ever. Amen.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** This instruction I commit to you, my child Timothy, according to the prophecies which led the way to you, that by them you may wage the good warfare;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** holding faith and a good conscience; which some having thrust away made a shipwreck concerning the faith;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** of whom is Hymenaeus and Alexander; whom I delivered to Satan, that they might be taught not to blaspheme.
