# 1 Timothy 2 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I exhort therefore, first of all, that petitions, prayers, intercessions, and givings of thanks, be made for all men:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** for kings and all who are in high places; that we may lead a tranquil and quiet life in all godliness and reverence.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For this is good and acceptable in the sight of God our Savior;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** who desires all people to be saved and come to full knowledge of the truth.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For there is one God, and one mediator between God and men, the man Christ Jesus,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** who gave himself as a ransom for all; the testimony in its own times;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** to which I was appointed a preacher and an apostle (I am telling the truth in Christ, not lying), a teacher of the Gentiles in faith and truth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I desire therefore that the men in every place pray, lifting up holy hands without anger and doubting.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** In the same way, that women also adorn themselves in decent clothing, with modesty and propriety; not just with braided hair, gold, pearls, or expensive clothing;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** but (which becomes women professing godliness) with good works.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let a woman learn in quietness with all subjection.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But I don’t permit a woman to teach, nor to exercise authority over a man, but to be in quietness.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For Adam was first formed, then Eve.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Adam wasn’t deceived, but the woman, being deceived, has fallen into disobedience;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** but she will be saved through her childbearing, if they continue in faith, love, and sanctification with sobriety.
