# 1 Timothy 2 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My desire is, first of all, that you will make requests and prayers and give praise for all men;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For kings and all those in authority; so that we may have a calm and quiet life in all fear of God and serious behaviour.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** This is good and pleasing in the eyes of God our Saviour;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Whose desire is that all men may have salvation and come to the knowledge of what is true.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For there is one God and one peacemaker between God and men, the man Christ Jesus,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Who gave himself as an offering for all; witness of which was to be given at the right time;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And of this I became a preacher and an Apostle (what I say is true, not false,) and a teacher of the Gentiles in the true faith.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** It is my desire, then, that in every place men may give themselves to prayer, lifting up holy hands, without wrath or argument.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And that women may be dressed in simple clothing, with a quiet and serious air; not with twisted hair and gold or jewels or robes of great price;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But clothed with good works, as is right for women who are living in the fear of God.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let a woman quietly take the place of a learner and be under authority.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** In my opinion it is right for a woman not to be a teacher, or to have rule over a man, but to be quiet.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For Adam was first formed, then Eve;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And Adam was not taken by deceit, but the woman, being tricked, became a wrongdoer.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But if they go on in faith and love and holy self-control, she will be kept safe at the time of childbirth.
