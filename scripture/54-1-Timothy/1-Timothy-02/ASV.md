# 1 Timothy 2 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I exhort therefore, first of all, that supplications, prayers, intercessions, thanksgivings, be made for all men;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** for kings and all that are in high place; that we may lead a tranquil and quiet life in all godliness and gravity.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** This is good and acceptable in the sight of God our Saviour;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** who would have all men to be saved, and come to the knowledge of the truth.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For there is one God, one mediator also between God and men, himself man, Christ Jesus,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** who gave himself a ransom for all; the testimony to be borne in its own times;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** whereunto I was appointed a preacher and an apostle (I speak the truth, I lie not), a teacher of the Gentiles in faith and truth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I desire therefore that the men pray in every place, lifting up holy hands, without wrath and disputing.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** In like manner, that women adorn themselves in modest apparel, with shamefastness and sobriety; not with braided hair, and gold or pearls or costly raiment;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** but (which becometh women professing godliness) through good works.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Let a woman learn in quietness with all subjection.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But I permit not a woman to teach, nor to have dominion over a man, but to be in quietness.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For Adam was first formed, then Eve;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** and Adam was not beguiled, but the woman being beguiled hath fallen into transgression:

<a id="v15"></a>**15** but she shall be saved through her child-bearing, if they continue in faith and love and sanctification with sobriety.
