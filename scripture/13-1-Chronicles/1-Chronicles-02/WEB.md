# 1 Chronicles 2 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** These are the sons of Israel: Reuben, Simeon, Levi, and Judah, Issachar, and Zebulun,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Dan, Joseph, and Benjamin, Naphtali, Gad, and Asher.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The sons of Judah: Er, and Onan, and Shelah; which three were born to him of Shua’s daughter the Canaanitess. Er, Judah’s firstborn, was wicked in the sight of Yahweh; and he killed him.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Tamar his daughter-in-law bore him Perez and Zerah. All the sons of Judah were five.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The sons of Perez: Hezron, and Hamul.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The sons of Zerah: Zimri, and Ethan, and Heman, and Calcol, and Dara; five of them in all.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The sons of Carmi: Achar, the troubler of Israel, who committed a trespass in the devoted thing.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The sons of Ethan: Azariah.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The sons also of Hezron, who were born to him: Jerahmeel, and Ram, and Chelubai.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Ram became the father of Amminadab, and Amminadab became the father of Nahshon, prince of the children of Judah;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** and Nahshon became the father of Salma, and Salma became the father of Boaz,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** and Boaz became the father of Obed, and Obed became the father of Jesse;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** and Jesse became the father of his firstborn Eliab, and Abinadab the second, and Shimea the third,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Nethanel the fourth, Raddai the fifth,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Ozem the sixth, David the seventh;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** and their sisters were Zeruiah and Abigail. The sons of Zeruiah: Abishai, and Joab, and Asahel, three.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Abigail bore Amasa; and the father of Amasa was Jether the Ishmaelite.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Caleb the son of Hezron became the father of children of Azubah his wife, and of Jerioth; and these were her sons: Jesher, and Shobab, and Ardon.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Azubah died, and Caleb took to him Ephrath, who bore him Hur.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Hur became the father of Uri, and Uri became the father of Bezalel.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Afterward Hezron went in to the daughter of Machir the father of Gilead, whom he took as wife when he was sixty years old; and she bore him Segub.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Segub became the father of Jair, who had twenty-three cities in the land of Gilead.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Geshur and Aram took the towns of Jair from them, with Kenath, and its villages, even sixty cities. All these were the sons of Machir the father of Gilead.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** After that Hezron was dead in Caleb Ephrathah, then Abijah Hezron’s wife bore him Ashhur the father of Tekoa.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** The sons of Jerahmeel the firstborn of Hezron were Ram the firstborn, and Bunah, and Oren, and Ozem, Ahijah.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Jerahmeel had another wife, whose name was Atarah; she was the mother of Onam.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** The sons of Ram the firstborn of Jerahmeel were Maaz, and Jamin, and Eker.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The sons of Onam were Shammai, and Jada. The sons of Shammai: Nadab, and Abishur.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** The name of the wife of Abishur was Abihail; and she bore him Ahban, and Molid.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** The sons of Nadab: Seled, and Appaim; but Seled died without children.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** The sons of Appaim: Ishi. The sons of Ishi: Sheshan. The sons of Sheshan: Ahlai.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The sons of Jada the brother of Shammai: Jether, and Jonathan; and Jether died without children.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** The sons of Jonathan: Peleth, and Zaza. These were the sons of Jerahmeel.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Now Sheshan had no sons, but daughters. Sheshan had a servant, an Egyptian, whose name was Jarha.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Sheshan gave his daughter to Jarha his servant as wife; and she bore him Attai.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Attai became the father of Nathan, and Nathan became the father of Zabad,

<a id="v37"></a>**37** and Zabad became the father of Ephlal, and Ephlal became the father of Obed,

<a id="v38"></a>**38** and Obed became the father of Jehu, and Jehu became the father of Azariah,

<a id="v39"></a>**39** and Azariah became the father of Helez, and Helez became the father of Eleasah,

<a id="v40"></a>**40** and Eleasah became the father of Sismai, and Sismai became the father of Shallum,

<a id="v41"></a>**41** and Shallum became the father of Jekamiah, and Jekamiah became the father of Elishama.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** The sons of Caleb the brother of Jerahmeel were Mesha his firstborn, who was the father of Ziph; and the sons of Mareshah the father of Hebron.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** The sons of Hebron: Korah, and Tappuah, and Rekem, and Shema.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** Shema became the father of Raham, the father of Jorkeam; and Rekem became the father of Shammai.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** The son of Shammai was Maon; and Maon was the father of Beth Zur.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** Ephah, Caleb’s concubine, bore Haran, and Moza, and Gazez; and Haran became the father of Gazez.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** The sons of Jahdai: Regem, and Jothan, and Geshan, and Pelet, and Ephah, and Shaaph.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** Maacah, Caleb’s concubine, bore Sheber and Tirhanah.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** She bore also Shaaph the father of Madmannah, Sheva the father of Machbena, and the father of Gibea; and the daughter of Caleb was Achsah.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** These were the sons of Caleb, the son of Hur, the firstborn of Ephrathah: Shobal the father of Kiriath Jearim,

<a id="v51"></a>**51** Salma the father of Bethlehem, Hareph the father of Beth Gader.

<a id="v52"></a>**52** Shobal the father of Kiriath Jearim had sons: Haroeh, half of the Menuhoth.

<a id="v53"></a>**53** The families of Kiriath Jearim: The Ithrites, and the Puthites, and the Shumathites, and the Mishraites; of them came the Zorathites and the Eshtaolites.

<a id="v54"></a>**54** The sons of Salma: Bethlehem, and the Netophathites, Atroth Beth Joab, and half of the Manahathites, the Zorites.

<a id="v55"></a>**55** The families of scribes who lived at Jabez: the Tirathites, the Shimeathites, the Sucathites. These are the Kenites who came of Hammath, the father of the house of Rechab.
