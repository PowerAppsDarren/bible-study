# 1 Chronicles 2 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** These are the sons of Israel: Reuben, Simeon, Levi and Judah, Issachar and Zebulun;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Dan, Joseph and Benjamin, Naphtali, Gad and Asher.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The sons of Judah: Er and Onan and Shelah; these three were his sons by Bathshua, the Canaanite woman. And Er, Judah's oldest son, did evil in the eyes of the Lord; and he put him to death.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And Tamar, his daughter-in-law, had Perez and Zerah by him. All the sons of Judah were five.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The sons of Perez: Hezron and Hamul.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And the sons of Zerah: Zimri and Ethan and Heman and Calcol and Dara; five of them.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And the sons of Carmi: Achan, the troubler of Israel, who did wrong about the cursed thing.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And the son of Ethan: Azariah.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And the sons of Hezron, the offspring of his body: Jerahmeel and Ram and Chelubai.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And Ram was the father of Amminadab; and Amminadab was the father of Nahshon, chief of the children of Judah;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And Nahshon was the father of Salma, and Salma was the father of Boaz,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And Boaz was the father of Obed, and Obed was the father of Jesse,

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And Jesse was the father of Eliab, his oldest son, and Abinadab, the second, and Shimea, the third,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Nethanel, the fourth, Raddai, the fifth,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Ozem, the sixth, David, the seventh;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And their sisters were Zeruiah and Abigail. And Zeruiah had three sons: Abishai and Joab and Asahel.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And Abigail was the mother of Amasa; and the father of Amasa was Jether the Ishmaelite.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And Caleb, the son of Hezron, had children by Azubah his wife, the daughter of Jerioth; and these were her sons: Jesher and Shobab and Ardon.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And after the death of Azubah, Caleb took as his wife Ephrath, who was the mother of Hur.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And Hur was the father of Uri; and Uri was the father of Bezalel.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And after that, Hezron had connection with the daughter of Machir, the father of Gilead, whom he took as his wife when he was sixty years old; and she had Segub by him.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And Segub was the father of Jair, who had twenty-three towns in the land of Gilead.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And Geshur and Aram took the tent-towns of Jair from them, with Kenath and the small places round it, even sixty towns. All these were the sons of Machir, the father of Gilead.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And after the death of Hezron, Caleb had connection with Ephrath, his father Hezron's wife, and she gave birth to his son Asshur, the father of Tekoa.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And the sons of Jerahmeel, the oldest son of Hezron, were Ram, the oldest, and Bunah and Oren and Ozem and Ahijah.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And Jerahmeel had another wife, whose name was Atarah: she was the mother of Onam.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** And the sons of Ram, the oldest son of Jerahmeel, were Maaz and Jamin and Eker.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** And the sons of Onam were Shammai and Jada; and the sons of Shammai: Nadab and Abishur.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And the name of Abishur's wife was Abihail; and she had Ahban and Molid by him.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** And the sons of Nadab: Seled and Appaim; but Seled came to his end without sons.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** And the sons of Appaim: Ishi. And the sons of Ishi: Sheshan. And the sons of Sheshan: Ahlai.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And the sons of Jada, the brother of Shammai: Jether and Jonathan; and Jether came to his end without sons.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** And the sons of Jonathan: Peleth and Zaza. These were the sons of Jerahmeel.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Now Sheshan had no sons, but only daughters. And Sheshan had an Egyptian servant, whose name was Jarha.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** And Sheshan gave his daughter to Jarha, his servant, as a wife; and she had Attai by him.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** And Attai was the father of Nathan, and Nathan was the father of Zabad,

<a id="v37"></a>**37** And Zabad was the father of Ephlal, and Ephlal was the father of Obed,

<a id="v38"></a>**38** And Obed was the father of Jehu, and Jehu was the father of Azariah,

<a id="v39"></a>**39** And Azariah was the father of Helez, and Helez was the father of Eleasah,

<a id="v40"></a>**40** And Eleasah was the father of Sismai, and Sismai was the father of Shallum,

<a id="v41"></a>**41** And Shallum was the father of Jekamiah, and Jekamiah was the father of Elishama.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** And the sons of Caleb, the brother of Jerahmeel, were Mareshah, his oldest son, who was the father of Ziph and Hebron.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** And the sons of Hebron: Korah and Tappuah and Rekem and Shema.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** And Shema was the father of Raham, the father of Jorkeam, and Rekem was the father of Shammai.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** And the son of Shammai was Maon; and Maon was the father of Beth-zur.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** And Ephah, Caleb's servant-wife, had Haran and Moza and Gazez; and Haran was the father of Gazez.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** And the sons of Jahdai: Regem and Jotham and Geshan and Pelet and Ephah and Shaaph.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** Maacah, Caleb's servant-wife, was the mother of Sheber and Tirhanah,

<a id="v49"></a>**49** And Shaaph, the father of Madmannah, Sheva, the father of Machbena and the father of Gibea; and Caleb's daughter was Achsah. These were the sons of Caleb.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** The sons of Hur, the oldest son of Ephrathah; Shobal, the father of Kiriath-jearim,

<a id="v51"></a>**51** Salma, the father of Beth-lehem, Hareph, the father of Beth-gader.

<a id="v52"></a>**52** And Shobal, the father of Kiriath-jearim, had sons: Haroeh, half of the Manahathites.

<a id="v53"></a>**53** And the families of Kiriath-jearim: the Ithrites and the Puthites and the Shumathites and the Mishraites; from them came the Zorathites and the Eshtaolites.

<a id="v54"></a>**54** The sons of Salma: Beth-lehem and the Netophathites, Atroth-beth-Joab and half of the Manahathites, the Zorites.

<a id="v55"></a>**55** And the families of scribes who were living at Jabez: the Tirathites, the Shimeathites, the Sucathites. These are the Kenites, the offspring of Hammath, the father of the family of Rechab.
