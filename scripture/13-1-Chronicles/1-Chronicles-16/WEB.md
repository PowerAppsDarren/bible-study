# 1 Chronicles 16 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** They brought in the ark of God, and set it in the midst of the tent that David had pitched for it: and they offered burnt offerings and peace offerings before God.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** When David had made an end of offering the burnt offering and the peace offerings, he blessed the people in Yahweh’s name.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He dealt to everyone of Israel, both man and woman, to every one a loaf of bread, and a portion of meat, and a cake of raisins.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He appointed certain of the Levites to minister before Yahweh’s ark, and to celebrate and to thank and praise Yahweh, the God of Israel:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Asaph the chief, and second to him Zechariah, Jeiel, and Shemiramoth, and Jehiel, and Mattithiah, and Eliab, and Benaiah, and Obed-Edom, and Jeiel, with stringed instruments and with harps; and Asaph with cymbals, sounding aloud;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** and Benaiah and Jahaziel the priests with trumpets continually, before the ark of the covenant of God.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Then on that day David first ordained to give thanks to Yahweh, by the hand of Asaph and his brothers.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Oh give thanks to Yahweh. Call on his name. Make his doings known among the peoples.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Sing to him. Sing praises to him. Tell of all his marvelous works.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Glory in his holy name. Let the heart of those who seek Yahweh rejoice.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Seek Yahweh and his strength. Seek his face forever more.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Remember his marvelous works that he has done, his wonders, and the judgments of his mouth,

<a id="v13"></a>**13** you seed of Israel his servant, you children of Jacob, his chosen ones.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He is Yahweh our God. His judgments are in all the earth.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Remember his covenant forever, the word which he commanded to a thousand generations,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** the covenant which he made with Abraham, his oath to Isaac.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He confirmed the same to Jacob for a statute, and to Israel for an everlasting covenant,

<a id="v18"></a>**18** saying, “I will give you the land of Canaan, The lot of your inheritance,”

<a id="v19"></a>**19** when you were but a few men in number, yes, very few, and foreigners were in it.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** They went about from nation to nation, from one kingdom to another people.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He allowed no man to do them wrong. Yes, he reproved kings for their sakes,

<a id="v22"></a>**22** “Don’t touch my anointed ones! Do my prophets no harm!”

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Sing to Yahweh, all the earth! Display his salvation from day to day.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Declare his glory among the nations, and his marvelous works among all the peoples.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** For great is Yahweh, and greatly to be praised. He also is to be feared above all gods.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** For all the gods of the peoples are idols, but Yahweh made the heavens.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Honor and majesty are before him. Strength and gladness are in his place.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Ascribe to Yahweh, you relatives of the peoples, ascribe to Yahweh glory and strength!

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Ascribe to Yahweh the glory due to his name. Bring an offering, and come before him. Worship Yahweh in holy array.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Tremble before him, all the earth. The world also is established that it can’t be moved.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice! Let them say among the nations, “Yahweh reigns!”

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Let the sea roar, and its fullness! Let the field exult, and all that is therein!

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Then the trees of the forest will sing for joy before Yahweh, for he comes to judge the earth.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Oh give thanks to Yahweh, for he is good, for his loving kindness endures forever.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Say, “Save us, God of our salvation! Gather us together and deliver us from the nations, to give thanks to your holy name, to triumph in your praise.”

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Blessed be Yahweh, the God of Israel, from everlasting even to everlasting.    All the people said, “Amen,” and praised Yahweh.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** So he left there, before the ark of the covenant of Yahweh, Asaph and his brothers, to minister before the ark continually, as every day’s work required;

<a id="v38"></a>**38** and Obed-Edom with their brothers, sixty-eight; Obed-Edom also the son of Jeduthun and Hosah to be doorkeepers;

<a id="v39"></a>**39** and Zadok the priest, and his brothers the priests, before Yahweh’s tabernacle in the high place that was at Gibeon,

<a id="v40"></a>**40** to offer burnt offerings to Yahweh on the altar of burnt offering continually morning and evening, even according to all that is written in Yahweh’s law, which he commanded to Israel;

<a id="v41"></a>**41** and with them Heman and Jeduthun, and the rest who were chosen, who were mentioned by name, to give thanks to Yahweh, because his loving kindness endures forever;

<a id="v42"></a>**42** and with them Heman and Jeduthun with trumpets and cymbals for those that should sound aloud, and with instruments for the songs of God; and the sons of Jeduthun to be at the gate.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** All the people departed every man to his house: and David returned to bless his house.
