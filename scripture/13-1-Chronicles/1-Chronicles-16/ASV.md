# 1 Chronicles 16 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And they brought in the ark of God, and set it in the midst of the tent that David had pitched for it: and they offered burnt-offerings and peace-offerings before God.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And when David had made an end of offering the burnt-offering and the peace-offerings, he blessed the people in the name of Jehovah.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And he dealt to every one of Israel, both man and woman, to every one a loaf of bread, and a portion of flesh, and a cake of raisins.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And he appointed certain of the Levites to minister before the ark of Jehovah, and to celebrate and to thank and praise Jehovah, the God of Israel:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Asaph the chief, and second to him Zechariah, Jeiel, and Shemiramoth, and Jehiel, and Mattithiah, and Eliab, and Benaiah, and Obed-edom, and Jeiel, with psalteries and with harps; and Asaph with cymbals, sounding aloud;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** and Benaiah and Jahaziel the priests with trumpets continually, before the ark of the covenant of God.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Then on that day did David first ordain to give thanks unto Jehovah, by the hand of Asaph and his brethren.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** O give thanks unto Jehovah, call upon his name; Make known his doings among the peoples.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Sing unto him, sing praises unto him; Talk ye of all his marvellous works.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Glory ye in his holy name; Let the heart of them rejoice that seek Jehovah.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Seek ye Jehovah and his strength; Seek his face evermore.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Remember his marvellous works that he hath done, His wonders, and the judgments of his mouth,

<a id="v13"></a>**13** O ye seed of Israel his servant, Ye children of Jacob, his chosen ones.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He is Jehovah our God; His judgments are in all the earth.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Remember his covenant for ever, The word which he commanded to a thousand generations,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The covenantwhich he made with Abraham, And his oath unto Isaac,

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And confirmed the same unto Jacob for a statute, To Israel for an everlasting covenant,

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Saying, Unto thee will I give the land of Canaan, The lot of your inheritance;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** When ye were but a few men in number, Yea, very few, and sojourners in it;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And they went about from nation to nation, And from one kingdom to another people.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He suffered no man to do them wrong; Yea, he reproved kings for their sakes,

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Saying, Touch not mine anointed ones, And do my prophets no harm.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Sing unto Jehovah, all the earth; Show forth his salvation from day to day.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Declare his glory among the nations, His marvellous works among all the peoples.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** For great is Jehovah, and greatly to be praised: He also is to be feared above all gods.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** For all the gods of the peoples are idols: But Jehovah made the heavens.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Honor and majesty are before him: Strength and gladness are in his place.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Ascribe unto Jehovah, ye kindreds of the peoples, Ascribe unto Jehovah glory and strength;

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Ascribe unto Jehovah the glory due unto his name: Bring an offering, and come before him; Worship Jehovah in holy array.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Tremble before him, all the earth: The world also is established that it cannot be moved.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice; And let them say among the nations, Jehovah reigneth.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof; Let the field exult, and all that is therein;

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Then shall the trees of the wood sing for joy before Jehovah; For he cometh to judge the earth.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** O give thanks unto Jehovah; for he is good; For his lovingkindness endureth for ever.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** And say ye, Save us, O God of our salvation, And gather us together and deliver us from the nations, To give thanks unto thy holy name, And to triumph in thy praise.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Blessed be Jehovah, the God of Israel, From everlasting even to everlasting.  And all the people said, Amen, and praised Jehovah.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** So he left there, before the ark of the covenant of Jehovah, Asaph and his brethren, to minister before the ark continually, as every day’s work required;

<a id="v38"></a>**38** and Obed-edom with their brethren, threescore and eight; Obed-edom also the son of Jeduthun and Hosah to be doorkeepers;

<a id="v39"></a>**39** and Zadok the priest, and his brethren the priests, before the tabernacle of Jehovah in the high place that was at Gibeon,

<a id="v40"></a>**40** to offer burnt-offerings unto Jehovah upon the altar of burnt-offering continually morning and evening, even according to all that is written in the law of Jehovah, which he commanded unto Israel;

<a id="v41"></a>**41** and with them Heman and Jeduthun, and the rest that were chosen, who were mentioned by name, to give thanks to Jehovah, because his lovingkindness endureth for ever;

<a id="v42"></a>**42** and with them Heman and Jeduthun with trumpets and cymbals for those that should sound aloud, and with instruments for the songs of God; and the sons of Jeduthun to be at the gate.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** And all the people departed every man to his house: and David returned to bless his house.
