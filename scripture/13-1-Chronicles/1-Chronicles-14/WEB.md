# 1 Chronicles 14 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hiram king of Tyre sent messengers to David, and cedar trees, and masons, and carpenters, to build him a house.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** David perceived that Yahweh had established him king over Israel; for his kingdom was exalted on high, for his people Israel’s sake.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** David took more wives at Jerusalem; and David became the father of more sons and daughters.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** These are the names of the children whom he had in Jerusalem: Shammua, and Shobab, Nathan, and Solomon,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** and Ibhar, and Elishua, and Elpelet,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** and Nogah, and Nepheg, and Japhia,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** and Elishama, and Beeliada, and Eliphelet.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** When the Philistines heard that David was anointed king over all Israel, all the Philistines went up to seek David: and David heard of it, and went out against them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Now the Philistines had come and made a raid in the valley of Rephaim.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** David inquired of God, saying, “Shall I go up against the Philistines? Will you deliver them into my hand?”   Yahweh said to him, “Go up; for I will deliver them into your hand.”

<a id="v11"></a>**11** So they came up to Baal Perazim, and David struck them there; and David said, God has broken my enemies by my hand, like the breach of waters. Therefore they called the name of that place Baal Perazim.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They left their gods there; and David gave commandment, and they were burned with fire.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The Philistines yet again made a raid in the valley.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** David inquired again of God; and God said to him, “You shall not go up after them. Turn away from them, and come on them over against the mulberry trees.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** It shall be, when you hear the sound of marching in the tops of the mulberry trees, that then you shall go out to battle; for God has gone out before you to strike the army of the Philistines.”

<a id="v16"></a>**16** David did as God commanded him: and they struck the army of the Philistines from Gibeon even to Gezer.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The fame of David went out into all lands; and Yahweh brought the fear of him on all nations.
