# 1 Chronicles 14 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Hiram king of Tyre sent messengers to David, and cedar-trees, and masons, and carpenters, to build him a house.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And David perceived that Jehovah had established him king over Israel; for his kingdom was exalted on high, for his people Israel’s sake.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And David took more wives at Jerusalem; and David begat more sons and daughters.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And these are the names of the children whom he had in Jerusalem: Shammua, and Shobab, Nathan, and Solomon,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** and Ibhar, and Elishua, and Elpelet,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** and Nogah, and Nepheg, and Japhia,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** and Elishama, and Beeliada, and Eliphelet.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And when the Philistines heard that David was anointed king over all Israel, all the Philistines went up to seek David: and David heard of it, and went out against them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Now the Philistines had come and made a raid in the valley of Rephaim.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And David inquired of God, saying, Shall I go up against the Philistines? and wilt thou deliver them into my hand? And Jehovah said unto him, Go up; for I will deliver them into thy hand.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** So they came up to Baal-perazim, and David smote them there; and David said, God hath broken mine enemies by my hand, like the breach of waters. Therefore they called the name of that place Baal-perazim.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And they left their gods there; and David gave commandment, and they were burned with fire.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And the Philistines yet again made a raid in the valley.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And David inquired again of God; and God said unto him, Thou shalt not go up after them: turn away from them, and come upon them over against the mulberry-trees.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And it shall be, when thou hearest the sound of marching in the tops of the mulberry-trees, that then thou shalt go out to battle; for God is gone out before thee to smite the host of the Philistines.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And David did as God commanded him: and they smote the host of the Philistines from Gibeon even to Gezer.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And the fame of David went out into all lands; and Jehovah brought the fear of him upon all nations.
