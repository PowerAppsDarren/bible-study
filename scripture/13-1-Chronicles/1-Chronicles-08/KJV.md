# 1 Chronicles 8 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Now Benjamin begat Bela his firstborn, Ashbel the second, and Aharah the third,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Nohah the fourth, and Rapha the fifth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And the sons of Bela were, Addar, and Gera, and Abihud,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And Abishua, and Naaman, and Ahoah,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And Gera, and Shephuphan, and Huram.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And these are the sons of Ehud: these are the heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of Geba, and they removed them to Manahath:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And Naaman, and Ahiah, and Gera, he removed them, and begat Uzza, and Ahihud.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And Shaharaim begat children in the country of Moab, after he had sent them away; Hushim and Baara were his wives.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And he begat of Hodesh his wife, Jobab, and Zibia, and Mesha, and Malcham,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And Jeuz, and Shachia, and Mirma. These were his sons, heads of the fathers.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And of Hushim he begat Abitub, and Elpaal.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The sons of Elpaal; Eber, and Misham, and Shamed, who built Ono, and Lod, with the towns thereof:

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Beriah also, and Shema, who were heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of Aijalon, who drove away the inhabitants of Gath:

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And Ahio, Shashak, and Jeremoth,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And Zebadiah, and Arad, and Ader,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And Michael, and Ispah, and Joha, the sons of Beriah;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And Zebadiah, and Meshullam, and Hezeki, and Heber,

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Ishmerai also, and Jezliah, and Jobab, the sons of Elpaal;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And Jakim, and Zichri, and Zabdi,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And Elienai, and Zilthai, and Eliel,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And Adaiah, and Beraiah, and Shimrath, the sons of Shimhi;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And Ishpan, and Heber, and Eliel,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And Abdon, and Zichri, and Hanan,

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And Hananiah, and Elam, and Antothijah,

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And Iphedeiah, and Penuel, the sons of Shashak;

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And Shamsherai, and Shehariah, and Athaliah,

<a id="v27"></a>**27** And Jaresiah, and Eliah, and Zichri, the sons of Jeroham.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** These were heads of the fathers, by their generations, chief men. These dwelt in Jerusalem.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And at Gibeon dwelt the father of Gibeon; whose wife’s name was Maachah:

<a id="v30"></a>**30** And his firstborn son Abdon, and Zur, and Kish, and Baal, and Nadab,

<a id="v31"></a>**31** And Gedor, and Ahio, and Zacher.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And Mikloth begat Shimeah. And these also dwelt with their brethren in Jerusalem, over against them.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** And Ner begat Kish, and Kish begat Saul, and Saul begat Jonathan, and Malchi–shua, and Abinadab, and Esh–baal.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** And the son of Jonathan was Merib–baal; and Merib–baal begat Micah.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** And the sons of Micah were, Pithon, and Melech, and Tarea, and Ahaz.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** And Ahaz begat Jehoadah; and Jehoadah begat Alemeth, and Azmaveth, and Zimri; and Zimri begat Moza,

<a id="v37"></a>**37** And Moza begat Binea: Rapha was his son, Eleasah his son, Azel his son:

<a id="v38"></a>**38** And Azel had six sons, whose names are these, Azrikam, Bocheru, and Ishmael, and Sheariah, and Obadiah, and Hanan. All these were the sons of Azel.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** And the sons of Eshek his brother were, Ulam his firstborn, Jehush the second, and Eliphelet the third.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** And the sons of Ulam were mighty men of valour, archers, and had many sons, and sons’ sons, an hundred and fifty. All these are of the sons of Benjamin.
