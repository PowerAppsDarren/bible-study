# 1 Chronicles 8 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Benjamin begat Bela his first-born, Ashbel the second, and Aharah the third,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Nohah the fourth, and Rapha the fifth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And Bela had sons: Addar, and Gera, and Abihud,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** and Abishua, and Naaman, and Ahoah,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** and Gera, and Shephuphan, and Huram.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And these are the sons of Ehud: these are the heads of fathers’ houses of the inhabitants of Geba, and they carried them captive to Manahath:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** and Naaman, and Ahijah, and Gera, he carried them captive; and he begat Uzza and Ahihud.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And Shaharaim begat children in the field of Moab, after he had sent them away; Hushim and Baara were his wives.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And he begat of Hodesh his wife, Jobab, and Zibia, and Mesha, and Malcam,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** and Jeuz, and Shachia, and Mirmah. These were his sons, heads of fathers’ houses.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And of Hushim he begat Abitub and Elpaal.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And the sons of Elpaal: Eber, and Misham, and Shemed, who built Ono and Lod, with the towns thereof;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** and Beriah, and Shema, who were heads of fathers’ houses of the inhabitants of Aijalon, who put to flight the inhabitants of Gath;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** and Ahio, Shashak, and Jeremoth,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** and Zebadiah, and Arad, and Eder,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** and Michael, and Ishpah, and Joha, the sons of Beriah,

<a id="v17"></a>**17** and Zebadiah, and Meshullam, and Hizki, and Heber,

<a id="v18"></a>**18** and Ishmerai, and Izliah, and Jobab, the sons of Elpaal,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** and Jakim, and Zichri, and Zabdi,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** and Elienai, and Zillethai, and Eliel,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** and Adaiah, and Beraiah, and Shimrath, the sons of Shimei,

<a id="v22"></a>**22** and Ishpan, and Eber, and Eliel,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** and Abdon, and Zichri, and Hanan,

<a id="v24"></a>**24** and Hananiah, and Elam, and Anthothijah,

<a id="v25"></a>**25** and Iphdeiah, and Penuel, the sons of Shashak,

<a id="v26"></a>**26** and Shamsherai, and Shehariah, and Athaliah,

<a id="v27"></a>**27** and Jaareshiah, and Elijah, and Zichri, the sons of Jeroham.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** These were heads of fathers’ houses throughout their generations, chief men: these dwelt in Jerusalem.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And in Gibeon there dwelt the father of Gibeon, Jeiel, whose wife’s name was Maacah;

<a id="v30"></a>**30** and his first-born son Abdon, and Zur, and Kish, and Baal, and Nadab,

<a id="v31"></a>**31** and Gedor, and Ahio, and Zecher.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And Mikloth begat Shimeah. And they also dwelt with their brethren in Jerusalem, over against their brethren.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** And Ner begat Kish; and Kish begat Saul; and Saul begat Jonathan, and Malchi-shua, and Abinadab, and Eshbaal.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** And the son of Jonathan was Merib-baal; and Merib-baal begat Micah.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** And the sons of Micah: Pithon, and Melech, and Tarea, and Ahaz.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** And Ahaz begat Jehoaddah; and Jehoaddah begat Alemeth, and Azmaveth, and Zimri; and Zimri begat Moza.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** And Moza begat Binea; Raphah was his son, Eleasah his son, Azel his son.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** And Azel had six sons, whose names are these: Azrikam, Bocheru, and Ishmael, and Sheariah, and Obadiah, and Hanan. All these were the sons of Azel.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** And the sons of Eshek his brother: Ulam his first-born, Jeush the second, and Eliphelet the third.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** And the sons of Ulam were mighty men of valor, archers, and had many sons, and sons’ sons, a hundred and fifty. All these were of the sons of Benjamin.
