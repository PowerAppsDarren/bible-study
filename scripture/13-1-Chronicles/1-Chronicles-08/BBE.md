# 1 Chronicles 8 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Benjamin was the father of Bela his oldest son, Ashbel the second, and Aharah the third,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Nohah the fourth, and Rapha the fifth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And Bela had sons, Addar and Gera, the father of Ehud,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And Abishua and Naaman and Ahoah

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And Gera and Shephuphan and Huram.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And these are the sons of Ehud, heads of families of those living in Geba: Iglaam and Alemeth

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And Naaman and Ahijah and Gera; and Iglaam was the father of Uzza and Ahihud.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And Shaharaim became the father of children in the country of the Moabites after driving out Hushim and Beerah his wives;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And by Hodesh his wife he became the father of Jobab and Zibia and Mesha and Malcam.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And Jeuz and Shachia and Mirmah. These were his sons, heads of families.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And Hushim became the father of Abitub and Elpaal.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And the sons of Elpaal: Eber and Misham and Shemed (he was the builder of Ono and Lod and their daughter-towns);

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And Beriah and Shema, who were heads of the families of those who were living in Aijalon, who put to flight the people living in Gath;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And their brothers Shashak and Jeremoth.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And Zebadiah and Arad and Eder

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And Michael and Ishpah and Joha, the sons of Beriah;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And Zebadiah and Meshullam and Hizki and Heber

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And Ishmerai and Izliah and Jobab, the sons of Elpaal;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And Jakim and Zichri and Zabdi

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And Elienai and Zillethai and Eliel

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And Adaiah and Beraiah and Shimrath, the sons of Shimei;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And Ishpan and Eber and Eliel

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And Abdon and Zichri and Hanan

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And Hananiah and Elam and Anathothijah

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And Iphdeiah and Penuel, the sons of Shashak;

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And Shamsherai and Shehariah and Athaliah

<a id="v27"></a>**27** And Jaareshiah and Elijah and Zichri, the sons of Jeremoth.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** These were heads of families in their generations; chief men: these were living in Jerusalem.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And in Gibeon was living the father of Gibeon, Jeiel, whose wife's name was Maacah;

<a id="v30"></a>**30** And his oldest son Abdon, and Zur and Kish and Baal and Ner and Nadab

<a id="v31"></a>**31** And Gedor and Ahio and Zechariah and Mikloth.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And Mikloth was the father of Shimeah. And they were living with their brothers in Jerusalem opposite their brothers.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** And Ner was the father of Abner, and Kish was the father of Saul, and Saul was the father of Jonathan and Malchi-shua and Abinadab and Eshbaal.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** And the son of Jonathan was Merib-baal; and Merib-baal was the father of Micah.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** And the sons of Micah: Pithon and Melech and Tarea and Ahaz.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** And Ahaz was the father of Jehoaddah; and Jehoaddah was the father of Alemeth and Azmaveth and Zimri; and Zimri was the father of Moza;

<a id="v37"></a>**37** And Moza was the father of Binea: Raphah was his son, Eleasah his son, Azel his son;

<a id="v38"></a>**38** And Azel had five sons, whose names are: Azrikam, his oldest, and Ishmael and Sheariah and Obadiah and Hanan. All these were the sons of Azel.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** And the sons of Eshek his brother: Ulam his oldest son, Jeush the second, and Eliphelet the third.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** And the sons of Ulam were men of war, bowmen, and had a great number of sons and sons' sons, a hundred and fifty. All these were the sons of Benjamin.
