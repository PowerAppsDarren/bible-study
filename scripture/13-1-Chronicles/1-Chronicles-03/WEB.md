# 1 Chronicles 3 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Now these were the sons of David, who were born to him in Hebron: the firstborn, Amnon, of Ahinoam the Jezreelitess; the second, Daniel, of Abigail the Carmelitess;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** the third, Absalom the son of Maacah the daughter of Talmai king of Geshur; the fourth, Adonijah the son of Haggith;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** the fifth, Shephatiah of Abital; the sixth, Ithream by Eglah his wife:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** six were born to him in Hebron; and there he reigned seven years and six months. In Jerusalem he reigned thirty-three years;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** and these were born to him in Jerusalem: Shimea, and Shobab, and Nathan, and Solomon, four, of Bathshua the daughter of Ammiel;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** and Ibhar, and Elishama, and Eliphelet,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** and Nogah, and Nepheg, and Japhia,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** and Elishama, and Eliada, and Eliphelet, nine.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** All these were the sons of David, besides the sons of the concubines; and Tamar was their sister.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Solomon’s son was Rehoboam, Abijah his son, Asa his son, Jehoshaphat his son,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Joram his son, Ahaziah his son, Joash his son,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Amaziah his son, Azariah his son, Jotham his son,

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Ahaz his son, Hezekiah his son, Manasseh his son,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Amon his son, Josiah his son.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The sons of Josiah: the firstborn Johanan, the second Jehoiakim, the third Zedekiah, the fourth Shallum.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The sons of Jehoiakim: Jeconiah his son, Zedekiah his son.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The sons of Jeconiah, the captive: Shealtiel his son,

<a id="v18"></a>**18** and Malchiram, and Pedaiah, and Shenazzar, Jekamiah, Hoshama, and Nedabiah.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The sons of Pedaiah: Zerubbabel, and Shimei. The sons of Zerubbabel: Meshullam, and Hananiah; and Shelomith was their sister;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** and Hashubah, and Ohel, and Berechiah, and Hasadiah, Jushab Hesed, five.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The sons of Hananiah: Pelatiah, and Jeshaiah; the sons of Rephaiah, the sons of Arnan, the sons of Obadiah, the sons of Shecaniah.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The sons of Shecaniah: Shemaiah. The sons of Shemaiah: Hattush, and Igal, and Bariah, and Neariah, and Shaphat, six.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** The sons of Neariah: Elioenai, and Hizkiah, and Azrikam, three.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** The sons of Elioenai: Hodaviah, and Eliashib, and Pelaiah, and Akkub, and Johanan, and Delaiah, and Anani, seven.
