# 1 Chronicles 3 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Now these were the sons of David, which were born unto him in Hebron; the firstborn Amnon, of Ahinoam the Jezreelitess; the second Daniel, of Abigail the Carmelitess:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The third, Absalom the son of Maachah the daughter of Talmai king of Geshur: the fourth, Adonijah the son of Haggith:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The fifth, Shephatiah of Abital: the sixth, Ithream by Eglah his wife.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** These six were born unto him in Hebron; and there he reigned seven years and six months: and in Jerusalem he reigned thirty and three years.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And these were born unto him in Jerusalem; Shimea, and Shobab, and Nathan, and Solomon, four, of Bath–shua the daughter of Ammiel:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Ibhar also, and Elishama, and Eliphelet,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And Nogah, and Nepheg, and Japhia,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And Elishama, and Eliada, and Eliphelet, nine.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** These were all the sons of David, beside the sons of the concubines, and Tamar their sister.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And Solomon’s son was Rehoboam, Abia his son, Asa his son, Jehoshaphat his son,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Joram his son, Ahaziah his son, Joash his son,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Amaziah his son, Azariah his son, Jotham his son,

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Ahaz his son, Hezekiah his son, Manasseh his son,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Amon his son, Josiah his son.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And the sons of Josiah were, the firstborn Johanan, the second Jehoiakim, the third Zedekiah, the fourth Shallum.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And the sons of Jehoiakim: Jeconiah his son, Zedekiah his son.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And the sons of Jeconiah; Assir, Salathiel his son,

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Malchiram also, and Pedaiah, and Shenazar, Jecamiah, Hoshama, and Nedabiah.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And the sons of Pedaiah were, Zerubbabel, and Shimei: and the sons of Zerubbabel; Meshullam, and Hananiah, and Shelomith their sister:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And Hashubah, and Ohel, and Berechiah, and Hasadiah, Jushab–hesed, five.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And the sons of Hananiah; Pelatiah, and Jesaiah: the sons of Rephaiah, the sons of Arnan, the sons of Obadiah, the sons of Shechaniah.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And the sons of Shechaniah; Shemaiah: and the sons of Shemaiah; Hattush, and Igeal, and Bariah, and Neariah, and Shaphat, six.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And the sons of Neariah; Elioenai, and Hezekiah, and Azrikam, three.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And the sons of Elioenai were, Hodaiah, and Eliashib, and Pelaiah, and Akkub, and Johanan, and Dalaiah, and Anani, seven.
