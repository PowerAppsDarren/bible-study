# 1 Chronicles 3 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Now these were David's sons, whose birth took place in Hebron: the oldest Amnon, by Ahinoam of Jezreel; the second Daniel, by Abigail the Carmelite woman;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The third Absalom, the son of Maacah, the daughter of Talmai, king of Geshur; the fourth Adonijah, the son of Haggith;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The fifth Shephatiah, by Abital; the sixth Ithream, by Eglah his wife.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He had six sons in Hebron; he was ruling there for seven years and six months, and in Jerusalem for thirty-three years.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And in Jerusalem he had four sons, Shimea and Shobab and Nathan and Solomon, by Bath-shua, the daughter of Ammiel;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And Ibhar and Elishama and Eliphelet

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And Nogah and Nepheg and Japhia

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And Elishama and Eliada and Eliphelet, nine.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** All these were the sons of David, in addition to the sons of his servant-wives; and Tamar was their sister.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And Solomon's son was Rehoboam, Abijah was his son, Asa his son, Jehoshaphat his son,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Joram his son, Ahaziah his son, Joash his son,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Amaziah his son, Azariah his son, Jotham his son,

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Ahaz his son, Hezekiah his son, Manasseh his son,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Amon his son, Josiah his son.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And the sons of Josiah: the oldest Johanan, the second Jehoiakim, the third Zedekiah, the fourth Shallum.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And the sons of Jehoiakim: Jeconiah his son, Zedekiah his son.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And the sons of Jeconiah, who was taken prisoner: Shealtiel his son,

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And Malchiram and Pedaiah and Shenazzar, Jekamiah, Hoshama and Nedabiah.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And the sons of Pedaiah: Zerubbabel and Shimei; and the sons of Zerubbabel: Meshullam and Hananiah; and Shelomith was their sister;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And Hashubah and Ohel and Berechiah and Hasadiah, Jushab-hesed, five.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And the sons of Hananiah: Pelatiah and Jeshaiah; the sons of Rephaiah, the sons of Arnan, the sons of Obadiah, the sons of Shecaniah.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And the sons of Shecaniah: Shemaiah; and the sons of Shemaiah: Hattush and Igal and Bariah and Neariah and Shaphat, six.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And the sons of Neariah: Elioenai and Hizkiah and Azrikam, three.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And the sons of Elioenai: Hodaviah and Eliashib and Pelaiah and Akkub and Johanan and Delaiah and Anani, seven.
