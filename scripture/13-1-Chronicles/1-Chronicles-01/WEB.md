# 1 Chronicles 1 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Adam, Seth, Enosh,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Kenan, Mahalalel, Jared,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Enoch, Methuselah, Lamech,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The sons of Japheth: Gomer, and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The sons of Gomer: Ashkenaz, and Diphath, and Togarmah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The sons of Javan: Elishah, and Tarshish, Kittim, and Rodanim.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The sons of Ham: Cush, and Mizraim, Put, and Canaan.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The sons of Cush: Seba, and Havilah, and Sabta, and Raama, and Sabteca. The sons of Raamah: Sheba, and Dedan.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Cush became the father of Nimrod; he began to be a mighty one in the earth.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Mizraim became the father of Ludim, and Anamim, and Lehabim, and Naphtuhim,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** and Pathrusim, and Casluhim (where the Philistines came from), and Caphtorim.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Canaan became the father of Sidon his firstborn, and Heth,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** and the Jebusite, and the Amorite, and the Girgashite,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** and the Hivite, and the Arkite, and the Sinite,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** and the Arvadite, and the Zemarite, and the Hamathite.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The sons of Shem: Elam, and Asshur, and Arpachshad, and Lud, and Aram, and Uz, and Hul, and Gether, and Meshech.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Arpachshad became the father of Shelah, and Shelah became the father of Eber.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** To Eber were born two sons: the name of the one was Peleg; for in his days the earth was divided; and his brother’s name was Joktan.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Joktan became the father of Almodad, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** and Hadoram, and Uzal, and Diklah,

<a id="v22"></a>**22** and Ebal, and Abimael, and Sheba,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** and Ophir, and Havilah, and Jobab. All these were the sons of Joktan.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Shem, Arpachshad, Shelah,

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Eber, Peleg, Reu,

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Serug, Nahor, Terah,

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Abram (the same is Abraham).

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The sons of Abraham: Isaac, and Ishmael.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** These are their generations: the firstborn of Ishmael, Nebaioth; then Kedar, and Adbeel, and Mibsam,

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Mishma, and Dumah, Massa, Hadad, and Tema,

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah. These are the sons of Ishmael.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The sons of Keturah, Abraham’s concubine: she bore Zimran, and Jokshan, and Medan, and Midian, and Ishbak, and Shuah. The sons of Jokshan: Sheba, and Dedan.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** The sons of Midian: Ephah, and Epher, and Hanoch, and Abida, and Eldaah. All these were the sons of Keturah.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Abraham became the father of Isaac. The sons of Isaac: Esau, and Israel.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** The sons of Esau: Eliphaz, Reuel, and Jeush, and Jalam, and Korah.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** The sons of Eliphaz: Teman, and Omar, Zephi, and Gatam, Kenaz, and Timna, and Amalek.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** The sons of Reuel: Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** The sons of Seir: Lotan, and Shobal, and Zibeon, and Anah, and Dishon, and Ezer, and Dishan.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** The sons of Lotan: Hori, and Homam; and Timna was Lotan’s sister.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** The sons of Shobal: Alian, and Manahath, and Ebal, Shephi, and Onam. The sons of Zibeon: Aiah, and Anah.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** The sons of Anah: Dishon. The sons of Dishon: Hamran, and Eshban, and Ithran, and Cheran.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** The sons of Ezer: Bilhan, and Zaavan, Jaakan. The sons of Dishan: Uz, and Aran.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Now these are the kings who reigned in the land of Edom, before there reigned any king over the children of Israel: Bela the son of Beor; and the name of his city was Dinhabah.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** Bela died, and Jobab the son of Zerah of Bozrah reigned in his place.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** Jobab died, and Husham of the land of the Temanites reigned in his place.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** Husham died, and Hadad the son of Bedad, who struck Midian in the field of Moab, reigned in his place; and the name of his city was Avith.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** Hadad died, and Samlah of Masrekah reigned in his place.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** Samlah died, and Shaul of Rehoboth by the River reigned in his place.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** Shaul died, and Baal Hanan the son of Achbor reigned in his place.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** Baal Hanan died, and Hadad reigned in his place; and the name of his city was Pai: and his wife’s name was Mehetabel, the daughter of Matred, the daughter of Mezahab.

<a id="v51"></a>**51** Hadad died. The chiefs of Edom were: chief Timna, chief Aliah, chief Jetheth,

<a id="v52"></a>**52** chief Oholibamah, chief Elah, chief Pinon,

<a id="v53"></a>**53** chief Kenaz, chief Teman, chief Mibzar,

<a id="v54"></a>**54** chief Magdiel, chief Iram. These are the chiefs of Edom.
