# 1 Chronicles 1 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Adam, Seth, Enosh;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Kenan, Mahalalel, Jared,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Enoch, Methuselah, Lamech;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The sons of Japheth: Gomer and Magog and Madai and Javan and Tubal and Meshech and Tiras.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And the sons of Gomer: Ashkenaz and Diphath and Togarmah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And the sons of Javan: Elishah and Tarshish, Kittim and Rodanim.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The sons of Ham: Cush and Egypt, Put and Canaan.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And the sons of Cush: Seba and Havilah and Sabta and Raama and Sabteca. And the sons of Raamah: Sheba and Dedan.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And Cush was the father of Nimrod: he was the first to be a great man in the earth.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And Egypt was the father of the Ludim and the Anamim and the Lehabim and the Naphtuhim

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And the Pathrusim and the Casluhim and the Caphtorim (from whom came the Philistines).

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And Canaan was the father of Zidon, his oldest son, and Heth,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And the Jebusite and the Amorite and the Girgashite,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And the Hivite and the Arkite and the Sinite,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And the Arvadite and the Zemarite and the Hamathite.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The sons of Shem: Elam and Asshur and Arpachshad and Lud and Aram and Uz and Hul and Gether and Meshech.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And Arpachshad was the father of Shelah, and Shelah was the father of Eber.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And Eber had two sons: the name of the one was Peleg, because in his days a division was made of the earth; and his brother's name was Joktan.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And Joktan was the father of Almodad and Sheleph and Hazarmaveth and Jerah

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And Hadoram and Uzal and Diklah

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And Ebal and Abimael and Sheba

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And Ophir and Havilah and Jobab. All these were the sons of Joktan.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Shem, Arpachshad, Shelah,

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Eber, Peleg, Reu,

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Serug, Nahor, Terah,

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Abram (that is Abraham).

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The sons of Abraham: Isaac and Ishmael.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** These are their generations: the oldest son of Ishmael, Nebaioth; then Kedar and Adbeel and Mibsam,

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Mishma and Dumah, Massa, Hadad and Tema,

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah. These are the sons of Ishmael.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And the sons of Keturah, Abraham's servant-wife: she was the mother of Zimran and Jokshan and Medan and Midian and Ishbak and Shuah. And the sons of Jokshan: Sheba and Dedan.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** And the sons of Midian: Ephah and Epher and Hanoch and Abida and Eldaah. All these were the sons of Keturah.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** And Abraham was the father of Isaac. The sons of Isaac: Esau and Israel.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** The sons of Esau: Eliphaz, Reuel and Jeush and Jalam and Korah.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** The sons of Eliphaz: Teman and Omar, Zephi and Gatam, Kenaz and Timna and Amalek.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** The sons of Reuel: Nahath, Zerah, Shammah and Mizzah.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** And the sons of Seir: Lotan and Shobal and Zibeon and Anah and Dishon and Ezer and Dishan.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** And the sons of Lotan: Hori and Homam; and Timna was Lotan's sister.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** The sons of Shobal: Alian and Manahath and Ebal, Shephi and Onam. And the sons of Zibeon: Aiah and Anah.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** The sons of Anah: Dishon. And the sons of Dishon: Hamran and Eshban and Ithran and Cheran.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** The sons of Ezer: Bilhan and Zaavan, Jaakan. The sons of Dishan: Uz and Aran.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Now these are the kings who were ruling in the land of Edom, before there was any king over Israel: Bela, the son of Beor; his town was named Dinhabah.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** At his death, Jobab, the son of Zerah of Bozrah, became king in his place.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** At the death of Jobab, Husham, from the land of the Temanites, became king in his place.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** And at the death of Husham, Hadad, the son of Bedad, who overcame Midian in the field of Moab, became king; his town was named Avith.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** And at the death of Hadad, Samlah of Masrekah became king in his place.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** And at the death of Samlah, Shaul of Rehoboth by the river became king in his place,

<a id="v49"></a>**49** And at the death of Shaul, Baal-hanan, the son of Achbor, became king in his place.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** And at the death of Baal-hanan, Hadad became king in his place; his town was named Pai, and his wife's name was Mehetabel, the daughter of Matred, the daughter of Me-zahab.

<a id="v51"></a>**51** And Hadad came to his end. Now the chiefs of Edom were: the chief of Timna, the chief of Aliah, the chief of Jetheth,

<a id="v52"></a>**52** The chief of Oholibamah, the chief of Elah, the chief of Pinon,

<a id="v53"></a>**53** The chief of Kenaz, the chief of Teman, the chief of Mibzar,

<a id="v54"></a>**54** The chief of Magdiel, the chief of Iram. These are the chiefs of Edom.
