# 1 Chronicles 1 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Adam, Sheth, Enosh,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Kenan, Mahalaleel, Jered,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Henoch, Methuselah, Lamech,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The sons of Japheth; Gomer, and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And the sons of Gomer; Ashchenaz, and Riphath, and Togarmah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And the sons of Javan; Elishah, and Tarshish, Kittim, and Dodanim.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The sons of Ham; Cush, and Mizraim, Put, and Canaan.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And the sons of Cush; Seba, and Havilah, and Sabta, and Raamah, and Sabtecha. And the sons of Raamah; Sheba, and Dedan.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And Cush begat Nimrod: he began to be mighty upon the earth.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And Mizraim begat Ludim, and Anamim, and Lehabim, and Naphtuhim,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And Pathrusim, and Casluhim, (of whom came the Philistines,) and Caphthorim.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And Canaan begat Zidon his firstborn, and Heth,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The Jebusite also, and the Amorite, and the Girgashite,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And the Hivite, and the Arkite, and the Sinite,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And the Arvadite, and the Zemarite, and the Hamathite.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The sons of Shem; Elam, and Asshur, and Arphaxad, and Lud, and Aram, and Uz, and Hul, and Gether, and Meshech.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And Arphaxad begat Shelah, and Shelah begat Eber.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And unto Eber were born two sons: the name of the one was Peleg; because in his days the earth was divided: and his brother’s name was Joktan.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And Joktan begat Almodad, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Hadoram also, and Uzal, and Diklah,

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And Ebal, and Abimael, and Sheba,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And Ophir, and Havilah, and Jobab. All these were the sons of Joktan.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Shem, Arphaxad, Shelah,

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Eber, Peleg, Reu,

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Serug, Nahor, Terah,

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Abram; the same is Abraham.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The sons of Abraham; Isaac, and Ishmael.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** These are their generations: The firstborn of Ishmael, Nebaioth; then Kedar, and Adbeel, and Mibsam,

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Mishma, and Dumah, Massa, Hadad, and Tema,

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah. These are the sons of Ishmael.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Now the sons of Keturah, Abraham’s concubine: she bare Zimran, and Jokshan, and Medan, and Midian, and Ishbak, and Shuah. And the sons of Jokshan; Sheba, and Dedan.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** And the sons of Midian; Ephah, and Epher, and Henoch, and Abida, and Eldaah. All these are the sons of Keturah.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** And Abraham begat Isaac. The sons of Isaac; Esau and Israel.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** The sons of Esau; Eliphaz, Reuel, and Jeush, and Jaalam, and Korah.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** The sons of Eliphaz; Teman, and Omar, Zephi, and Gatam, Kenaz, and Timna, and Amalek.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** The sons of Reuel; Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** And the sons of Seir; Lotan, and Shobal, and Zibeon, and Anah, and Dishon, and Ezer, and Dishan.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** And the sons of Lotan; Hori, and Homam: and Timna was Lotan’s sister.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** The sons of Shobal; Alian, and Manahath, and Ebal, Shephi, and Onam. And the sons of Zibeon; Aiah, and Anah.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** The sons of Anah; Dishon. And the sons of Dishon; Amram, and Eshban, and Ithran, and Cheran.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** The sons of Ezer; Bilhan, and Zavan, and Jakan. The sons of Dishan; Uz, and Aran.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Now these are the kings that reigned in the land of Edom before any king reigned over the children of Israel; Bela the son of Beor: and the name of his city was Dinhabah.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** And when Bela was dead, Jobab the son of Zerah of Bozrah reigned in his stead.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** And when Jobab was dead, Husham of the land of the Temanites reigned in his stead.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** And when Husham was dead, Hadad the son of Bedad, which smote Midian in the field of Moab, reigned in his stead: and the name of his city was Avith.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** And when Hadad was dead, Samlah of Masrekah reigned in his stead.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** And when Samlah was dead, Shaul of Rehoboth by the river reigned in his stead.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** And when Shaul was dead, Baal–hanan the son of Achbor reigned in his stead.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** And when Baal–hanan was dead, Hadad reigned in his stead: and the name of his city was Pai; and his wife’s name was Mehetabel, the daughter of Matred, the daughter of Mezahab.

<a id="v51"></a>**51** Hadad died also. And the dukes of Edom were; duke Timnah, duke Aliah, duke Jetheth,

<a id="v52"></a>**52** Duke Aholibamah, duke Elah, duke Pinon,

<a id="v53"></a>**53** Duke Kenaz, duke Teman, duke Mibzar,

<a id="v54"></a>**54** Duke Magdiel, duke Iram. These are the dukes of Edom.
