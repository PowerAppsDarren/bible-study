# Romans 15 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Now we that are strong ought to bear the infirmities of the weak, and not to please ourselves.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let each one of us please his neighbor for that which is good, unto edifying.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For Christ also pleased not himself; but, as it is written, The reproaches of them that reproached thee fell upon me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For whatsoever things were written aforetime were written for our learning, that through patience and through comfort of the scriptures we might have hope.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Now the God of patience and of comfort grant you to be of the same mind one with another according to Christ Jesus:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** that with one accord ye may with one mouth glorify the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Wherefore receive ye one another, even as Christ also received you, to the glory of God.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For I say that Christ hath been made a minister of the circumcision for the truth of God, that he might confirm the promises given unto the fathers,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** and that the Gentiles might glorify God for his mercy; as it is written,  Therefore will I give praise unto thee among the Gentiles, And sing unto thy name.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And again he saith,  Rejoice, ye Gentiles, with his people.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And again,  Praise the Lord, all ye Gentiles; And let all the peoples praise him.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And again, Isaiah saith,  There shall be the root of Jesse, And he that ariseth to rule over the Gentiles; On him shall the Gentiles hope.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Now the God of hope fill you with all joy and peace in believing, that ye may abound in hope, in the power of the Holy Spirit.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And I myself also am persuaded of you, my brethren, that ye yourselves are full of goodness, filled with all knowledge, able also to admonish one another.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But I write the more boldly unto you in some measure, as putting you again in remembrance, because of the grace that was given me of God,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** that I should be a minister of Christ Jesus unto the Gentiles, ministering the gospel of God, that the offering up of the Gentiles might be made acceptable, being sanctified by the Holy Spirit.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I have therefore my glorying in Christ Jesus in things pertaining to God.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For I will not dare to speak of any things save those which Christ wrought through me, for the obedience of the Gentiles, by word and deed,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** in the power of signs and wonders, in the power of the Holy Spirit; so that from Jerusalem, and round about even unto Illyricum, I have fully preached the gospel of Christ;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** yea, making it my aim so to preach the gospel, not where Christ was already named, that I might not build upon another man’s foundation;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** but, as it is written,  They shall see, to whom no tidings of him came, And they who have not heard shall understand.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Wherefore also I was hindered these many times from coming to you:

<a id="v23"></a>**23** but now, having no more any place in these regions, and having these many years a longing to come unto you,

<a id="v24"></a>**24** whensoever I go unto Spain (for I hope to see you in my journey, and to be brought on my way thitherward by you, if first in some measure I shall have been satisfied with your company)—

<a id="v25"></a>**25** but now, I say, I go unto Jerusalem, ministering unto the saints.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** For it hath been the good pleasure of Macedonia and Achaia to make a certain contribution for the poor among the saints that are at Jerusalem.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Yea, it hath been their good pleasure; and their debtors they are. For if the Gentiles have been made partakers of their spiritual things, they owe it to them also to minister unto them in carnal things.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** When therefore I have accomplished this, and have sealed to them this fruit, I will go on by you unto Spain.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And I know that, when I come unto you, I shall come in the fulness of the blessing of Christ.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Now I beseech you, brethren, by our Lord Jesus Christ, and by the love of the Spirit, that ye strive together with me in your prayers to God for me;

<a id="v31"></a>**31** that I may be delivered from them that are disobedient in Judæa, and that my ministration which I have for Jerusalem may be acceptable to the saints;

<a id="v32"></a>**32** that I may come unto you in joy through the will of God, and together with you find rest.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Now the God of peace be with you all. Amen.
