# Romans 16 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I commend to you Phoebe, our sister, who is a servant of the assembly that is at Cenchreae,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** that you receive her in the Lord, in a way worthy of the saints, and that you assist her in whatever matter she may need from you, for she herself also has been a helper of many, and of my own self.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Greet Prisca and Aquila, my fellow workers in Christ Jesus,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** who for my life, laid down their own necks; to whom not only I give thanks, but also all the assemblies of the Gentiles.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Greet the assembly that is in their house. Greet Epaenetus, my beloved, who is the first fruits of Achaia to Christ.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Greet Mary, who labored much for us.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Greet Andronicus and Junia, my relatives and my fellow prisoners, who are notable among the apostles, who also were in Christ before me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Greet Amplias, my beloved in the Lord.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Greet Urbanus, our fellow worker in Christ, and Stachys, my beloved.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Greet Apelles, the approved in Christ. Greet those who are of the household of Aristobulus.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Greet Herodion, my kinsman. Greet them of the household of Narcissus, who are in the Lord.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Greet Tryphaena and Tryphosa, who labor in the Lord. Greet Persis, the beloved, who labored much in the Lord.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Greet Rufus, the chosen in the Lord, and his mother and mine.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Greet Asyncritus, Phlegon, Hermes, Patrobas, Hermas, and the brothers  who are with them.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Greet Philologus and Julia, Nereus and his sister, and Olympas, and all the saints who are with them.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Greet one another with a holy kiss. The assemblies of Christ greet you.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Now I beg you, brothers, look out for those who are causing the divisions and occasions of stumbling, contrary to the doctrine which you learned, and turn away from them.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For those who are such don’t serve our Lord, Jesus Christ, but their own belly; and by their smooth and flattering speech, they deceive the hearts of the innocent.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** For your obedience has become known to all. I rejoice therefore over you. But I desire to have you wise in that which is good, but innocent in that which is evil.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And the God of peace will quickly crush Satan under your feet. The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Timothy, my fellow worker, greets you, as do Lucius, Jason, and Sosipater, my relatives.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** I, Tertius, who write the letter, greet you in the Lord.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Gaius, my host and host of the whole assembly, greets you. Erastus, the treasurer of the city, greets you, as does Quartus, the brother.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all! Amen.
