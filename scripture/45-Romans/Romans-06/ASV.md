# Romans 6 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** What shall we say then? Shall we continue in sin, that grace may abound?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** God forbid. We who died to sin, how shall we any longer live therein?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Or are ye ignorant that all we who were baptized into Christ Jesus were baptized into his death?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** We were buried therefore with him through baptism into death: that like as Christ was raised from the dead through the glory of the Father, so we also might walk in newness of life.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For if we have become united with him in the likeness of his death, we shall be also in the likeness of his resurrection;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** knowing this, that our old man was crucified with him, that the body of sin might be done away, that so we should no longer be in bondage to sin;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** for he that hath died is justified from sin.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But if we died with Christ, we believe that we shall also live with him;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** knowing that Christ being raised from the dead dieth no more; death no more hath dominion over him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For the death that he died, he died unto sin once: but the life that he liveth, he liveth unto God.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Even so reckon ye also yourselves to be dead unto sin, but alive unto God in Christ Jesus.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Let not sin therefore reign in your mortal body, that ye should obey the lusts thereof:

<a id="v13"></a>**13** neither present your members unto sin as instruments of unrighteousness; but present yourselves unto God, as alive from the dead, and your members as instruments of righteousness unto God.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For sin shall not have dominion over you: for ye are not under law, but under grace.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** What then? shall we sin, because we are not under law, but under grace? God forbid.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Know ye not, that to whom ye present yourselves as servants unto obedience, his servants ye are whom ye obey; whether of sin unto death, or of obedience unto righteousness?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** But thanks be to God, that, whereas ye were servants of sin, ye became obedient from the heart to that form of teaching whereunto ye were delivered;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** and being made free from sin, ye became servants of righteousness.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** I speak after the manner of men because of the infirmity of your flesh: for as ye presented your members as servants to uncleanness and to iniquity unto iniquity, even so now present your members as servants to righteousness unto sanctification.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** For when ye were servants of sin, ye were free in regard of righteousness.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** What fruit then had ye at that time in the things whereof ye are now ashamed? for the end of those things is death.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But now being made free from sin and become servants to God, ye have your fruit unto sanctification, and the end eternal life.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** For the wages of sin is death; but the free gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus our Lord.
