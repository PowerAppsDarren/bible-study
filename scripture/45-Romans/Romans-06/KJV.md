# Romans 6 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** What shall we say then? Shall we continue in sin, that grace may abound?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** God forbid. How shall we, that are dead to sin, live any longer therein?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Know ye not, that so many of us as were baptized into Jesus Christ were baptized into his death?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Therefore we are buried with him by baptism into death: that like as Christ was raised up from the dead by the glory of the Father, even so we also should walk in newness of life.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For if we have been planted together in the likeness of his death, we shall be also in the likeness of his resurrection:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Knowing this, that our old man is crucified with him, that the body of sin might be destroyed, that henceforth we should not serve sin.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For he that is dead is freed from sin.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Now if we be dead with Christ, we believe that we shall also live with him:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Knowing that Christ being raised from the dead dieth no more; death hath no more dominion over him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For in that he died, he died unto sin once: but in that he liveth, he liveth unto God.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Likewise reckon ye also yourselves to be dead indeed unto sin, but alive unto God through Jesus Christ our Lord.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Let not sin therefore reign in your mortal body, that ye should obey it in the lusts thereof.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Neither yield ye your members as instruments of unrighteousness unto sin: but yield yourselves unto God, as those that are alive from the dead, and your members as instruments of righteousness unto God.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For sin shall not have dominion over you: for ye are not under the law, but under grace.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** What then? shall we sin, because we are not under the law, but under grace? God forbid.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Know ye not, that to whom ye yield yourselves servants to obey, his servants ye are to whom ye obey; whether of sin unto death, or of obedience unto righteousness?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** But God be thanked, that ye were the servants of sin, but ye have obeyed from the heart that form of doctrine which was delivered you.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Being then made free from sin, ye became the servants of righteousness.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** I speak after the manner of men because of the infirmity of your flesh: for as ye have yielded your members servants to uncleanness and to iniquity unto iniquity; even so now yield your members servants to righteousness unto holiness.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** For when ye were the servants of sin, ye were free from righteousness.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** What fruit had ye then in those things whereof ye are now ashamed? for the end of those things is death.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But now being made free from sin, and become servants to God, ye have your fruit unto holiness, and the end everlasting life.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** For the wages of sin is death; but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord.
