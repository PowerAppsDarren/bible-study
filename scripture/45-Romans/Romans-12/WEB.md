# Romans 12 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Therefore I urge you, brothers, by the mercies of God, to present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable to God, which is your spiritual service.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Don’t be conformed to this world, but be transformed by the renewing of your mind, so that you may prove what is the good, well-pleasing, and perfect will of God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For I say, through the grace that was given me, to every man who is among you, not to think of himself more highly than he ought to think; but to think reasonably, as God has apportioned to each person a measure of faith.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For even as we have many members in one body, and all the members don’t have the same function,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** so we, who are many, are one body in Christ, and individually members one of another.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Having gifts differing according to the grace that was given to us, if prophecy, let us prophesy according to the proportion of our faith;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** or service, let us give ourselves to service; or he who teaches, to his teaching;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** or he who exhorts, to his exhorting: he who gives, let him do it with liberality; he who rules, with diligence; he who shows mercy, with cheerfulness.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let love be without hypocrisy. Abhor that which is evil. Cling to that which is good.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** In love of the brothers be tenderly affectionate one to another; in honor preferring one another;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** not lagging in diligence; fervent in spirit; serving the Lord;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** rejoicing in hope; enduring in troubles; continuing steadfastly in prayer;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** contributing to the needs of the saints; given to hospitality.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Bless those who persecute you; bless, and don’t curse.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Rejoice with those who rejoice. Weep with those who weep.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Be of the same mind one toward another. Don’t set your mind on high things, but associate with the humble. Don’t be wise in your own conceits.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Repay no one evil for evil. Respect what is honorable in the sight of all men.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** If it is possible, as much as it is up to you, be at peace with all men.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Don’t seek revenge yourselves, beloved, but give place to God’s wrath. For it is written, “Vengeance belongs to me; I will repay, says the Lord.”

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Therefore  “If your enemy is hungry, feed him. If he is thirsty, give him a drink; for in doing so, you will heap coals of fire on his head.”

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Don’t be overcome by evil, but overcome evil with good.
