# Romans 12 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I beseech you therefore, brethren, by the mercies of God, that ye present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto God, which is your reasonable service.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And be not conformed to this world: but be ye transformed by the renewing of your mind, that ye may prove what is that good, and acceptable, and perfect, will of God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For I say, through the grace given unto me, to every man that is among you, not to think of himself more highly than he ought to think; but to think soberly, according as God hath dealt to every man the measure of faith.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For as we have many members in one body, and all members have not the same office:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** So we, being many, are one body in Christ, and every one members one of another.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Having then gifts differing according to the grace that is given to us, whether prophecy, let us prophesy according to the proportion of faith;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Or ministry, let us wait on our ministering: or he that teacheth, on teaching;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Or he that exhorteth, on exhortation: he that giveth, let him do it with simplicity; he that ruleth, with diligence; he that sheweth mercy, with cheerfulness.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let love be without dissimulation. Abhor that which is evil; cleave to that which is good.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Be kindly affectioned one to another with brotherly love; in honour preferring one another;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Not slothful in business; fervent in spirit; serving the Lord;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Rejoicing in hope; patient in tribulation; continuing instant in prayer;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Distributing to the necessity of saints; given to hospitality.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Bless them which persecute you: bless, and curse not.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Rejoice with them that do rejoice, and weep with them that weep.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Be of the same mind one toward another. Mind not high things, but condescend to men of low estate. Be not wise in your own conceits.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Recompense to no man evil for evil. Provide things honest in the sight of all men.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** If it be possible, as much as lieth in you, live peaceably with all men.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Dearly beloved, avenge not yourselves, but rather give place unto wrath: for it is written, Vengeance is mine; I will repay, saith the Lord.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Therefore if thine enemy hunger, feed him; if he thirst, give him drink: for in so doing thou shalt heap coals of fire on his head.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Be not overcome of evil, but overcome evil with good.
