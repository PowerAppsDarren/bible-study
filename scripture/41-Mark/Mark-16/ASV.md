# Mark 16 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And when the sabbath was past, Mary Magdalene, and Mary the mother of James, and Salome, bought spices, that they might come and anoint him.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And very early on the first day of the week, they come to the tomb when the sun was risen.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And they were saying among themselves, Who shall roll us away the stone from the door of the tomb?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** and looking up, they see that the stone is rolled back: for it was exceeding great.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And entering into the tomb, they saw a young man sitting on the right side, arrayed in a white robe; and they were amazed.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And he saith unto them, Be not amazed: ye seek Jesus, the Nazarene, who hath been crucified: he is risen; he is not here: behold, the place where they laid him!

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But go, tell his disciples and Peter, He goeth before you into Galilee: there shall ye see him, as he said unto you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And they went out, and fled from the tomb; for trembling and astonishment had come upon them: and they said nothing to any one; for they were afraid.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Now when he was risen early on the first day of the week, he appeared first to Mary Magdalene, from whom he had cast out seven demons.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** She went and told them that had been with him, as they mourned and wept.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And they, when they heard that he was alive, and had been seen of her, disbelieved.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And after these things he was manifested in another form unto two of them, as they walked, on their way into the country.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And they went away and told it unto the rest: neither believed they them.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And afterward he was manifested unto the eleven themselves as they sat at meat; and he upbraided them with their unbelief and hardness of heart, because they believed not them that had seen him after he was risen.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And he said unto them, Go ye into all the world, and preach the gospel to the whole creation.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He that believeth and is baptized shall be saved; but he that disbelieveth shall be condemned.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And these signs shall accompany them that believe: in my name shall they cast out demons; they shall speak with new tongues;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** they shall take up serpents, and if they drink any deadly thing, it shall in no wise hurt them; they shall lay hands on the sick, and they shall recover.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** So then the Lord Jesus, after he had spoken unto them, was received up into heaven, and sat down at the right hand of God.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And they went forth, and preached everywhere, the Lord working with them, and confirming the word by the signs that followed. Amen.
