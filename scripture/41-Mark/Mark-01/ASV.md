# Mark 1 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The beginning of the gospel of Jesus Christ, the Son of God.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Even as it is written in Isaiah the prophet,  Behold, I send my messenger before thy face, Who shall prepare thy way;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The voice of one crying in the wilderness, Make ye ready the way of the Lord, Make his paths straight;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** John came, who baptized in the wilderness and preached the baptism of repentance unto remission of sins.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And there went out unto him all the country of Judæa, and all they of Jerusalem; and they were baptized of him in the river Jordan, confessing their sins.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And John was clothed with camel’s hair, and had a leathern girdle about his loins, and did eat locusts and wild honey.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And he preached, saying, There cometh after me he that is mightier than I, the latchet of whose shoes I am not worthy to stoop down and unloose.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I baptized you in water; but he shall baptize you in the Holy Spirit.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And it came to pass in those days, that Jesus came from Nazareth of Galilee, and was baptized of John in the Jordan.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And straightway coming up out of the water, he saw the heavens rent asunder, and the Spirit as a dove descending upon him:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** and a voice came out of the heavens, Thou art my beloved Son, in thee I am well pleased.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And straightway the Spirit driveth him forth into the wilderness.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And he was in the wilderness forty days tempted of Satan; and he was with the wild beasts; and the angels ministered unto him.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Now after John was delivered up, Jesus came into Galilee, preaching the gospel of God,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** and saying, The time is fulfilled, and the kingdom of God is at hand: repent ye, and believe in the gospel.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And passing along by the sea of Galilee, he saw Simon and Andrew the brother of Simon casting a net in the sea; for they were fishers.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And Jesus said unto them, Come ye after me, and I will make you to become fishers of men.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And straightway they left the nets, and followed him.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And going on a little further, he saw James the son of Zebedee, and John his brother, who also were in the boat mending the nets.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And straightway he called them: and they left their father Zebedee in the boat with the hired servants, and went after him.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And they go into Capernaum; and straightway on the sabbath day he entered into the synagogue and taught.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And they were astonished at his teaching: for he taught them as having authority, and not as the scribes.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And straightway there was in their synagogue a man with an unclean spirit; and he cried out,

<a id="v24"></a>**24** saying, What have we to do with thee, Jesus thou Nazarene? art thou come to destroy us? I know thee who thou art, the Holy One of God.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And Jesus rebuked him, saying, Hold thy peace, and come out of him.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And the unclean spirit, tearing him and crying with a loud voice, came out of him.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** And they were all amazed, insomuch that they questioned among themselves, saying, What is this? a new teaching! with authority he commandeth even the unclean spirits, and they obey him.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** And the report of him went out straightway everywhere into all the region of Galilee round about.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And straightway, when they were come out of the synagogue, they came into the house of Simon and Andrew, with James and John.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Now Simon’s wife’s mother lay sick of a fever; and straightway they tell him of her:

<a id="v31"></a>**31** and he came and took her by the hand, and raised her up; and the fever left her, and she ministered unto them.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And at even, when the sun did set, they brought unto him all that were sick, and them that were possessed with demons.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** And all the city was gathered together at the door.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** And he healed many that were sick with divers diseases, and cast out many demons; and he suffered not the demons to speak, because they knew him.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** And in the morning, a great while before day, he rose up and went out, and departed into a desert place, and there prayed.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** And Simon and they that were with him followed after him;

<a id="v37"></a>**37** and they found him, and say unto him, All are seeking thee.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** And he saith unto them, Let us go elsewhere into the next towns, that I may preach there also; for to this end came I forth.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** And he went into their synagogues throughout all Galilee, preaching and casting out demons.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** And there cometh to him a leper, beseeching him, and kneeling down to him, and saying unto him, If thou wilt, thou canst make me clean.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** And being moved with compassion, he stretched forth his hand, and touched him, and saith unto him, I will; be thou made clean.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** And straightway the leprosy departed from him, and he was made clean.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** And he strictly charged him, and straightway sent him out,

<a id="v44"></a>**44** and saith unto him, See thou say nothing to any man: but go show thyself to the priest, and offer for thy cleansing the things which Moses commanded, for a testimony unto them.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** But he went out, and began to publish it much, and to spread abroad the matter, insomuch that Jesus could no more openly enter into a city, but was without in desert places: and they came to him from every quarter.
