# Jonah 2 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Jonah prayed to Yahweh, his God, out of the fish’s belly.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He said,  “I called because of my affliction to Yahweh. He answered me. Out of the belly of Sheol I cried. You heard my voice.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For you threw me into the depths, in the heart of the seas. The flood was all around me. All your waves and your billows passed over me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I said, ‘I have been banished from your sight; yet I will look again toward your holy temple.’

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The waters surrounded me, even to the soul. The deep was around me. The weeds were wrapped around my head.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I went down to the bottoms of the mountains. The earth barred me in forever: yet have you brought up my life from the pit, Yahweh my God.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** “When my soul fainted within me, I remembered Yahweh. My prayer came in to you, into your holy temple.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Those who regard lying vanities forsake their own mercy.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But I will sacrifice to you with the voice of thanksgiving. I will pay that which I have vowed. Salvation belongs to Yahweh.”

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Yahweh spoke to the fish, and it vomited out Jonah on the dry land.
