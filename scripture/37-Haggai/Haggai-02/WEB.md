# Haggai 2 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In the seventh month, in the twenty-first day of the month, Yahweh’s word came by Haggai the prophet, saying,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** “Speak now to Zerubbabel, the son of Shealtiel, governor of Judah, and to Joshua, the son of Jehozadak, the high priest, and to the remnant of the people, saying,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** ‘Who is left among you who saw this house in its former glory? How do you see it now? Isn’t it in your eyes as nothing?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Yet now be strong, Zerubbabel,’ says Yahweh. ‘Be strong, Joshua, son of Jehozadak, the high priest. Be strong, all you people of the land,’ says Yahweh, ‘and work, for I am with you,’ says Yahweh of Armies.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** This is the word that I covenanted with you when you came out of Egypt, and my Spirit lived among you. ‘Don’t be afraid.’

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For this is what Yahweh of Armies says: ‘Yet once, it is a little while, and I will shake the heavens, the earth, the sea, and the dry land;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** and I will shake all nations. The precious things of all nations will come, and I will fill this house with glory, says Yahweh of Armies.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The silver is mine, and the gold is mine,’ says Yahweh of Armies.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** ‘The latter glory of this house will be greater than the former,’ says Yahweh of Armies; ‘and in this place will I give peace,’ says Yahweh of Armies.”

<a id="v10"></a>**10** In the twenty-fourth day of the ninth month, in the second year of Darius, Yahweh’s word came by Haggai the prophet, saying,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** “Thus says Yahweh of Armies: Ask now the priests concerning the law, saying,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** ‘If someone carries holy meat in the fold of his garment, and with his fold touches bread, stew, wine, oil, or any food, will it become holy?’”   The priests answered, “No.”

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Then Haggai said, “If one who is unclean by reason of a dead body touch any of these, will it be unclean?”   The priests answered, “It will be unclean.”

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Then Haggai answered, “‘So is this people, and so is this nation before me,’ says Yahweh; ‘and so is every work of their hands. That which they offer there is unclean.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Now, please consider from this day and backward, before a stone was laid on a stone in Yahweh’s temple.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Through all that time, when one came to a heap of twenty measures, there were only ten. When one came to the wine vat to draw out fifty, there were only twenty.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I struck you with blight, mildew, and hail in all the work of your hands; yet you didn’t turn to me,’ says Yahweh.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** ‘Consider, please, from this day and backward, from the twenty-fourth day of the ninth month, since the day that the foundation of Yahweh’s temple was laid, consider it.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Is the seed yet in the barn? Yes, the vine, the fig tree, the pomegranate, and the olive tree haven’t produced. From this day will I bless you.’”

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Yahweh’s word came the second time to Haggai in the twenty-fourth day of the month, saying,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** “Speak to Zerubbabel, governor of Judah, saying, ‘I will shake the heavens and the earth.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** I will overthrow the throne of kingdoms. I will destroy the strength of the kingdoms of the nations. I will overthrow the chariots, and those who ride in them. The horses and their riders will come down, everyone by the sword of his brother.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** In that day, says Yahweh of Armies, will I take you, Zerubbabel, my servant, the son of Shealtiel,’ says Yahweh, ‘and will make you as a signet, for I have chosen you,’ says Yahweh of Armies.”
