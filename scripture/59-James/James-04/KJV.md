# James 4 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** From whence come wars and fightings among you? come they not hence, even of your lusts that war in your members?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Ye lust, and have not: ye kill, and desire to have, and cannot obtain: ye fight and war, yet ye have not, because ye ask not.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Ye ask, and receive not, because ye ask amiss, that ye may consume it upon your lusts.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Ye adulterers and adulteresses, know ye not that the friendship of the world is enmity with God? whosoever therefore will be a friend of the world is the enemy of God.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Do ye think that the scripture saith in vain, The spirit that dwelleth in us lusteth to envy?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** But he giveth more grace. Wherefore he saith, God resisteth the proud, but giveth grace unto the humble.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Submit yourselves therefore to God. Resist the devil, and he will flee from you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Draw nigh to God, and he will draw nigh to you. Cleanse your hands, ye sinners; and purify your hearts, ye double minded.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Be afflicted, and mourn, and weep: let your laughter be turned to mourning, and your joy to heaviness.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Humble yourselves in the sight of the Lord, and he shall lift you up.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Speak not evil one of another, brethren. He that speaketh evil of his brother, and judgeth his brother, speaketh evil of the law, and judgeth the law: but if thou judge the law, thou art not a doer of the law, but a judge.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** There is one lawgiver, who is able to save and to destroy: who art thou that judgest another?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Go to now, ye that say, To day or to morrow we will go into such a city, and continue there a year, and buy and sell, and get gain:

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Whereas ye know not what shall be on the morrow. For what is your life? It is even a vapour, that appeareth for a little time, and then vanisheth away.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For that ye ought to say, If the Lord will, we shall live, and do this, or that.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** But now ye rejoice in your boastings: all such rejoicing is evil.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Therefore to him that knoweth to do good, and doeth it not, to him it is sin.
