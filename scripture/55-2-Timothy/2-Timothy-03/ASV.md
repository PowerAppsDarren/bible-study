# 2 Timothy 3 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** But know this, that in the last days grievous times shall come.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For men shall be lovers of self, lovers of money, boastful, haughty, railers, disobedient to parents, unthankful, unholy,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** without natural affection, implacable, slanderers, without self-control, fierce, no lovers of good,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** traitors, headstrong, puffed up, lovers of pleasure rather than lovers of God;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** holding a form of godliness, but having denied the power thereof: from these also turn away.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For of these are they that creep into houses, and take captive silly women laden with sins, led away by divers lusts,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** ever learning, and never able to come to the knowledge of the truth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And even as Jannes and Jambres withstood Moses, so do these also withstand the truth; men corrupted in mind, reprobate concerning the faith.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But they shall proceed no further: for their folly shall be evident unto all men, as theirs also came to be.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But thou didst follow my teaching, conduct, purpose, faith, longsuffering, love, patience,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** persecutions, sufferings; what things befell me at Antioch, at Iconium, at Lystra; what persecutions I endured: and out of them all the Lord delivered me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yea, and all that would live godly in Christ Jesus shall suffer persecution.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But evil men and impostors shall wax worse and worse, deceiving and being deceived.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But abide thou in the things which thou hast learned and hast been assured of, knowing of whom thou hast learned them;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** and that from a babe thou hast known the sacred writings which are able to make thee wise unto salvation through faith which is in Christ Jesus.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Every scripture inspired of God is also profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, for instruction which is in righteousness:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** that the man of God may be complete, furnished completely unto every good work.
