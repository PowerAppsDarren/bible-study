# 2 Timothy 3 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** This know also, that in the last days perilous times shall come.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For men shall be lovers of their own selves, covetous, boasters, proud, blasphemers, disobedient to parents, unthankful, unholy,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Without natural affection, trucebreakers, false accusers, incontinent, fierce, despisers of those that are good,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Traitors, heady, highminded, lovers of pleasures more than lovers of God;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Having a form of godliness, but denying the power thereof: from such turn away.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For of this sort are they which creep into houses, and lead captive silly women laden with sins, led away with divers lusts,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Ever learning, and never able to come to the knowledge of the truth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Now as Jannes and Jambres withstood Moses, so do these also resist the truth: men of corrupt minds, reprobate concerning the faith.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But they shall proceed no further: for their folly shall be manifest unto all men, as theirs also was.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But thou hast fully known my doctrine, manner of life, purpose, faith, longsuffering, charity, patience,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Persecutions, afflictions, which came unto me at Antioch, at Iconium, at Lystra; what persecutions I endured: but out of them all the Lord delivered me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yea, and all that will live godly in Christ Jesus shall suffer persecution.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But evil men and seducers shall wax worse and worse, deceiving, and being deceived.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But continue thou in the things which thou hast learned and hast been assured of, knowing of whom thou hast learned them;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And that from a child thou hast known the holy scriptures, which are able to make thee wise unto salvation through faith which is in Christ Jesus.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** All scripture is given by inspiration of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** That the man of God may be perfect, throughly furnished unto all good works.
