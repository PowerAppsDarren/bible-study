# 2 Timothy 3 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** But know this, that in the last days, grievous times will come.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For men will be lovers of self, lovers of money, boastful, arrogant, blasphemers, disobedient to parents, unthankful, unholy,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** without natural affection, unforgiving, slanderers, without self-control, fierce, not lovers of good,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** traitors, headstrong, conceited, lovers of pleasure rather than lovers of God;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** holding a form of godliness, but having denied its power. Turn away from these, also.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For some of these are people who creep into houses, and take captive gullible women loaded down with sins, led away by various lusts,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** always learning, and never able to come to the knowledge of the truth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Even as Jannes and Jambres opposed Moses, so do these also oppose the truth; men corrupted in mind, who concerning the faith, are rejected.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But they will proceed no further. For their folly will be evident to all men, as theirs also came to be.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But you did follow my teaching, conduct, purpose, faith, patience, love, steadfastness,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** persecutions, and sufferings: those things that happened to me at Antioch, Iconium, and Lystra. I endured those persecutions. The Lord delivered me out of them all.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yes, and all who desire to live godly in Christ Jesus will suffer persecution.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But evil men and impostors will grow worse and worse, deceiving and being deceived.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But you remain in the things which you have learned and have been assured of, knowing from whom you have learned them.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** From infancy, you have known the holy Scriptures which are able to make you wise for salvation through faith, which is in Christ Jesus.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Every Scripture is God-breathed and profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, and for instruction in righteousness,

<a id="v17"></a>**17** that the man of God may be complete, thoroughly equipped for every good work.
