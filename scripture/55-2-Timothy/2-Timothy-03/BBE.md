# 2 Timothy 3 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** But be certain of this, that in the last days times of trouble will come.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For men will be lovers of self, lovers of money, uplifted in pride, given to bitter words, going against the authority of their fathers, never giving praise, having no religion,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Without natural love, bitter haters, saying evil of others, violent and uncontrolled, hating all good,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** False to their friends, acting without thought, lifted up in mind, loving pleasure more than God;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Having a form of religion, but turning their backs on the power of it: go not with these.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For these are they who go secretly into houses, making prisoners of foolish women, weighted down with sin, turned from the way by their evil desires,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Ever learning, and never coming to the knowledge of what is true.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And as James and Jambres went against Moses, so do these go against what is true: men of evil minds, who, tested by faith, are seen to be false.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But they will go no farther: for their foolish behaviour will be clear to all men, as theirs was in the end.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But you took as your example my teaching, behaviour, purpose, and faith; my long waiting, my love, my quiet undergoing of trouble;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My punishments and pain; the things which came to me at Antioch, at Iconium, at Lystra; the cruel attacks made on me: and the Lord made me free from them all.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yes, and all whose purpose is to be living in the knowledge of God in Christ Jesus, will be cruelly attacked.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Evil and false men will become worse and worse, using deceit and themselves overcome by deceit.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But see that you keep to the teaching you have been given and the things of which you are certain, conscious of who has been your teacher;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And that from the time when you were a child, you have had knowledge of the holy Writings, which are able to make you wise to salvation, through faith in Christ Jesus.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Every holy Writing which comes from God is of profit for teaching, for training, for guiding, for education in righteousness:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** So that the man of God may be complete, trained and made ready for every good work.
