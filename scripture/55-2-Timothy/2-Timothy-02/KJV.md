# 2 Timothy 2 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Thou therefore, my son, be strong in the grace that is in Christ Jesus.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And the things that thou hast heard of me among many witnesses, the same commit thou to faithful men, who shall be able to teach others also.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thou therefore endure hardness, as a good soldier of Jesus Christ.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** No man that warreth entangleth himself with the affairs of this life; that he may please him who hath chosen him to be a soldier.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And if a man also strive for masteries, yet is he not crowned, except he strive lawfully.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The husbandman that laboureth must be first partaker of the fruits.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Consider what I say; and the Lord give thee understanding in all things.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Remember that Jesus Christ of the seed of David was raised from the dead according to my gospel:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Wherein I suffer trouble, as an evil doer, even unto bonds; but the word of God is not bound.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Therefore I endure all things for the elect’s sakes, that they may also obtain the salvation which is in Christ Jesus with eternal glory.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** It is a faithful saying: For if we be dead with him, we shall also live with him:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** If we suffer, we shall also reign with him: if we deny him, he also will deny us:

<a id="v13"></a>**13** If we believe not, yet he abideth faithful: he cannot deny himself.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Of these things put them in remembrance, charging them before the Lord that they strive not about words to no profit, but to the subverting of the hearers.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** But shun profane and vain babblings: for they will increase unto more ungodliness.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And their word will eat as doth a canker: of whom is Hymeneus and Philetus;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Who concerning the truth have erred, saying that the resurrection is past already; and overthrow the faith of some.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Nevertheless the foundation of God standeth sure, having this seal, The Lord knoweth them that are his. And, Let every one that nameth the name of Christ depart from iniquity.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** But in a great house there are not only vessels of gold and of silver, but also of wood and of earth; and some to honour, and some to dishonour.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** If a man therefore purge himself from these, he shall be a vessel unto honour, sanctified, and meet for the master’s use, and prepared unto every good work.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Flee also youthful lusts: but follow righteousness, faith, charity, peace, with them that call on the Lord out of a pure heart.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** But foolish and unlearned questions avoid, knowing that they do gender strifes.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And the servant of the Lord must not strive; but be gentle unto all men, apt to teach, patient,

<a id="v25"></a>**25** In meekness instructing those that oppose themselves; if God peradventure will give them repentance to the acknowledging of the truth;

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And that they may recover themselves out of the snare of the devil, who are taken captive by him at his will.
