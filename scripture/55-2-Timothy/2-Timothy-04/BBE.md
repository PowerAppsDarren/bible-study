# 2 Timothy 4 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I give you orders, before God and Christ Jesus, who will be the judge of the living and the dead, and by his revelation and his kingdom;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Be preaching the word at all times, in every place; make protests, say sharp words, give comfort, with long waiting and teaching;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For the time will come when they will not take the true teaching; but, moved by their desires, they will get for themselves a great number of teachers for the pleasure of hearing them;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And shutting their ears to what is true, will be turned away to belief in foolish stories.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But be self-controlled in all things, do without comfort, go on preaching the good news, completing the work which has been given you to do.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For I am even now being offered, and my end is near.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I have made a good fight, I have come to the end of my journey, I have kept the faith:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** From now on, the crown of righteousness is made ready for me, which the Lord, the upright judge, Will give to me at that day: and not only to me, but to all those who have had love for his revelation.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Do your best to come to me before long:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For Demas has gone away from me, for love of this present life, and has gone to Thessalonica: Crescens has gone to Galatia, Titus to Dalmatia.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Only Luke is with me. Get Mark and take him with you; for he is of use to me in the work.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Tychicus I sent to Ephesus.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The coat which I did not take from Troas and which is with Carpus, get when you come, and the books, specially the papers.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Alexander the copper-worker did me much wrong: the Lord will give him the reward of his works:

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But be on the watch for him, for he was violent in his attacks on our teaching.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** At my first meeting with my judges, no one took my part, but all went away from me. May it not be put to their account.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** But the Lord was by my side and gave me strength; so that through me the news might be given out in full measure, and all the Gentiles might give ear: and I was taken out of the mouth of the lion.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The Lord will keep me safe from every evil work and will give me salvation in his kingdom in heaven: to whom be glory for ever and ever. So be it.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Give my love to Prisca and Aquila and those of the house of Onesiphorus.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Erastus was stopping at Corinth; but Trophimus, when I last saw him was at Miletus, ill.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Do your best to come before the winter. Eubulus sends you his love, and Pudens and Linus and Claudia, and all the brothers.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The Lord be with your spirit. Grace be with you.
