# Proverbs 5 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My son, give attention to my wisdom; let your ear be turned to my teaching:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** So that you may be ruled by a wise purpose, and your lips may keep knowledge.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For honey is dropping from the lips of the strange woman, and her mouth is smoother than oil;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But her end is bitter as wormwood, and sharp as a two-edged sword;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Her feet go down to death, and her steps to the underworld;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** She never keeps her mind on the road of life; her ways are uncertain, she has no knowledge.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Give ear to me then, my sons, and do not put away my words from you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Go far away from her, do not come near the door of her house;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For fear that you may give your honour to others, and your wealth to strange men:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And strange men may be full of your wealth, and the fruit of your work go to the house of others;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And you will be full of grief at the end of your life, when your flesh and your body are wasted;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And you will say, How was teaching hated by me, and my heart put no value on training;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I did not give attention to the voice of my teachers, my ear was not turned to those who were guiding me!

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I was in almost all evil in the company of the people.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Let water from your store and not that of others be your drink, and running water from your fountain.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Let not your springs be flowing in the streets, or your streams of water in the open places.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Let them be for yourself only, not for other men with you.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Let blessing be on your fountain; have joy in the wife of your early years.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** As a loving hind and a gentle doe, let her breasts ever give you rapture; let your passion at all times be moved by her love.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Why let yourself, my son, go out of the way with a strange woman, and take another woman in your arms?

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For a man's ways are before the eyes of the Lord, and he puts all his goings in the scales.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The evil-doer will be taken in the net of his crimes, and prisoned in the cords of his sin.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** He will come to his end for need of teaching; he is so foolish that he will go wandering from the right way.
