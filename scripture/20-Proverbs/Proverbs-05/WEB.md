# Proverbs 5 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My son, pay attention to my wisdom. Turn your ear to my understanding:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** that you may maintain discretion, that your lips may preserve knowledge.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For the lips of an adulteress drip honey. Her mouth is smoother than oil,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But in the end she is as bitter as wormwood, and as sharp as a two-edged sword.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Her feet go down to death. Her steps lead straight to Sheol.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** She gives no thought to the way of life. Her ways are crooked, and she doesn’t know it.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Now therefore, my sons, listen to me. Don’t depart from the words of my mouth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Remove your way far from her. Don’t come near the door of her house,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** lest you give your honor to others, and your years to the cruel one;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** lest strangers feast on your wealth, and your labors enrich another man’s house.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** You will groan at your latter end, when your flesh and your body are consumed,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** and say, “How I have hated instruction, and my heart despised reproof;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** neither have I obeyed the voice of my teachers, nor turned my ear to those who instructed me!

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I have come to the brink of utter ruin, in the midst of the gathered assembly.”

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Drink water out of your own cistern, running water out of your own well.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Should your springs overflow in the streets, streams of water in the public squares?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Let them be for yourself alone, not for strangers with you.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Let your spring be blessed. Rejoice in the wife of your youth.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** A loving doe and a graceful deer— let her breasts satisfy you at all times. Be captivated always with her love.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** For why should you, my son, be captivated with an adulteress? Why embrace the bosom of another?

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For the ways of man are before the eyes of Yahweh. He examines all his paths.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The evil deeds of the wicked ensnare him. The cords of his sin hold him firmly.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** He will die for lack of instruction. In the greatness of his folly, he will go astray.
