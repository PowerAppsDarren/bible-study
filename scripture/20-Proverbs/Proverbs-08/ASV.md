# Proverbs 8 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Doth not wisdom cry, And understanding put forth her voice?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** On the top of high places by the way, Where the paths meet, she standeth;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Beside the gates, at the entry of the city, At the coming in at the doors, she crieth aloud:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Unto you, O men, I call; And my voice is to the sons of men.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** O ye simple, understand prudence; And, ye fools, be of an understanding heart.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Hear, for I will speak excellent things; And the opening of my lips shall be right things.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For my mouth shall utter truth; And wickedness is an abomination to my lips.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** All the words of my mouth are in righteousness; There is nothing crooked or perverse in them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** They are all plain to him that understandeth, And right to them that find knowledge.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Receive my instruction, and not silver; And knowledge rather than choice gold.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For wisdom is better than rubies; And all the things that may be desired are not to be compared unto it.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I wisdom have made prudence my dwelling, And find out knowledgeanddiscretion.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The fear of Jehovah is to hate evil: Pride, and arrogancy, and the evil way, And the perverse mouth, do I hate.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Counsel is mine, and sound knowledge: I am understanding; I have might.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** By me kings reign, And princes decree justice.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** By me princes rule, And nobles, evenall the judges of the earth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I love them that love me; And those that seek me diligently shall find me.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Riches and honor are with me; Yea, durable wealth and righteousness.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** My fruit is better than gold, yea, than fine gold; And my revenue than choice silver.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** I walk in the way of righteousness, In the midst of the paths of justice;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** That I may cause those that love me to inherit substance, And that I may fill their treasuries.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Jehovah possessed me in the beginning of his way, Before his works of old.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** I was set up from everlasting, from the beginning, Before the earth was.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** When there were no depths, I was brought forth, When there were no fountains abounding with water.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Before the mountains were settled, Before the hills was I brought forth;

<a id="v26"></a>**26** While as yet he had not made the earth, nor the fields, Nor the beginning of the dust of the world.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** When he established the heavens, I was there: When he set a circle upon the face of the deep,

<a id="v28"></a>**28** When he made firm the skies above, When the fountains of the deep became strong,

<a id="v29"></a>**29** When he gave to the sea its bound, That the waters should not transgress his commandment, When he marked out the foundations of the earth;

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Then I was by him, asa master workman; And I was dailyhisdelight, Rejoicing always before him,

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Rejoicing in his habitable earth; And my delight was with the sons of men.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Now therefore, mysons, hearken unto me; For blessed are they that keep my ways.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Hear instruction, and be wise, And refuse it not.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Blessed is the man that heareth me, Watching daily at my gates, Waiting at the posts of my doors.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** For whoso findeth me findeth life, And shall obtain favor of Jehovah.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** But he that sinneth against me wrongeth his own soul: All they that hate me love death.
