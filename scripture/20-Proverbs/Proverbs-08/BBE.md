# Proverbs 8 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Is not wisdom crying out, and the voice of knowledge sounding?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** At the top of the highways, at the meeting of the roads, she takes her place;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Where the roads go into the town her cry goes out, at the doorways her voice is loud:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I am crying out to you, O men; my voice comes to the sons of men.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Become expert in reason, O you simple ones; you foolish ones, take training to heart.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Give ear, for my words are true, and my lips are open to give out what is upright.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For good faith goes out of my mouth, and false lips are disgusting to me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** All the words of my mouth are righteousness; there is nothing false or twisted in them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** They are all true to him whose mind is awake, and straightforward to those who get knowledge.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Take my teaching, and not silver; get knowledge in place of the best gold.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For wisdom is better than jewels, and all things which may be desired are nothing in comparison with her.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I, wisdom, have made wise behaviour my near relation; I am seen to be the special friend of wise purposes.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The fear of the Lord is seen in hating evil: pride, a high opinion of oneself, the evil way, and the false tongue, are unpleasing to me.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Wise design and good sense are mine; reason and strength are mine.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Through me kings have their power, and rulers give right decisions.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Through me chiefs have authority, and the noble ones are judging in righteousness.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Those who have given me their love are loved by me, and those who make search for me with care will get me.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Wealth and honour are in my hands, even wealth without equal and righteousness.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** My fruit is better than gold, even than the best gold; and my increase is more to be desired than silver.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** I go in the road of righteousness, in the way of right judging:

<a id="v21"></a>**21** So that I may give my lovers wealth for their heritage, making their store-houses full.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The Lord made me as the start of his way, the first of his works in the past.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** From eternal days I was given my place, from the birth of time, before the earth was.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** When there was no deep I was given birth, when there were no fountains flowing with water.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Before the mountains were put in their places, before the hills was my birth:

<a id="v26"></a>**26** When he had not made the earth or the fields or the dust of the world.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** When he made ready the heavens I was there: when he put an arch over the face of the deep:

<a id="v28"></a>**28** When he made strong the skies overhead: when the fountains of the deep were fixed:

<a id="v29"></a>**29** When he put a limit to the sea, so that the waters might not go against his word: when he put in position the bases of the earth:

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Then I was by his side, as a master workman: and I was his delight from day to day, playing before him at all times;

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Playing in his earth; and my delight was with the sons of men.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Give ear to me then, my sons: for happy are those who keep my ways.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Take my teaching and be wise; do not let it go.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Happy is the man who gives ear to me, watching at my doors day by day, keeping his place by the pillars of my house.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** For whoever gets me gets life, and grace from the Lord will come to him.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** But he who does evil to me, does wrong to his soul: all my haters are in love with death.
