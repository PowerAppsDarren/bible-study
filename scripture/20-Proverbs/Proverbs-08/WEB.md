# Proverbs 8 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Doesn’t wisdom cry out? Doesn’t understanding raise her voice?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** On the top of high places by the way, where the paths meet, she stands.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Beside the gates, at the entry of the city, at the entry doors, she cries aloud:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** “To you men, I call! I send my voice to the sons of mankind.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You simple, understand prudence. You fools, be of an understanding heart.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Hear, for I will speak excellent things. The opening of my lips is for right things.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For my mouth speaks truth. Wickedness is an abomination to my lips.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** All the words of my mouth are in righteousness. There is nothing crooked or perverse in them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** They are all plain to him who understands, right to those who find knowledge.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Receive my instruction rather than silver; knowledge rather than choice gold.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For wisdom is better than rubies. All the things that may be desired can’t be compared to it.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** “I, wisdom, have made prudence my dwelling. Find out knowledge and discretion.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The fear of Yahweh is to hate evil. I hate pride, arrogance, the evil way, and the perverse mouth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Counsel and sound knowledge are mine. I have understanding and power.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** By me kings reign, and princes decree justice.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** By me princes rule; nobles, and all the righteous rulers of the earth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I love those who love me. Those who seek me diligently will find me.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** With me are riches, honor, enduring wealth, and prosperity.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** My fruit is better than gold, yes, than fine gold; my yield than choice silver.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** I walk in the way of righteousness, in the midst of the paths of justice;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** That I may give wealth to those who love me. I fill their treasuries.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** “Yahweh possessed me in the beginning of his work, before his deeds of old.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** I was set up from everlasting, from the beginning, before the earth existed.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** When there were no depths, I was born, when there were no springs abounding with water.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Before the mountains were settled in place, before the hills, I was born;

<a id="v26"></a>**26** while as yet he had not made the earth, nor the fields, nor the beginning of the dust of the world.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** When he established the heavens, I was there; when he set a circle on the surface of the deep,

<a id="v28"></a>**28** when he established the clouds above, when the springs of the deep became strong,

<a id="v29"></a>**29** when he gave to the sea its boundary, that the waters should not violate his commandment, when he marked out the foundations of the earth;

<a id="v30"></a>**30** then I was the craftsman by his side. I was a delight day by day, always rejoicing before him,

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Rejoicing in his whole world. My delight was with the sons of men.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** “Now therefore, my sons, listen to me, for blessed are those who keep my ways.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Hear instruction, and be wise. Don’t refuse it.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Blessed is the man who hears me, watching daily at my gates, waiting at my door posts.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** For whoever finds me, finds life, and will obtain favor from Yahweh.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** But he who sins against me wrongs his own soul. All those who hate me love death.”
