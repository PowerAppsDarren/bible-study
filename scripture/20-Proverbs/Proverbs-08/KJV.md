# Proverbs 8 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Doth not wisdom cry? and understanding put forth her voice?

<a id="v2"></a>**2** She standeth in the top of high places, by the way in the places of the paths.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** She crieth at the gates, at the entry of the city, at the coming in at the doors.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Unto you, O men, I call; and my voice is to the sons of man.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** O ye simple, understand wisdom: and, ye fools, be ye of an understanding heart.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Hear; for I will speak of excellent things; and the opening of my lips shall be right things.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For my mouth shall speak truth; and wickedness is an abomination to my lips.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** All the words of my mouth are in righteousness; there is nothing froward or perverse in them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** They are all plain to him that understandeth, and right to them that find knowledge.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Receive my instruction, and not silver; and knowledge rather than choice gold.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For wisdom is better than rubies; and all the things that may be desired are not to be compared to it.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I wisdom dwell with prudence, and find out knowledge of witty inventions.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The fear of the Lord is to hate evil: pride, and arrogancy, and the evil way, and the froward mouth, do I hate.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Counsel is mine, and sound wisdom: I am understanding; I have strength.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** By me kings reign, and princes decree justice.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** By me princes rule, and nobles, even all the judges of the earth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I love them that love me; and those that seek me early shall find me.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Riches and honour are with me; yea, durable riches and righteousness.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** My fruit is better than gold, yea, than fine gold; and my revenue than choice silver.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** I lead in the way of righteousness, in the midst of the paths of judgment:

<a id="v21"></a>**21** That I may cause those that love me to inherit substance; and I will fill their treasures.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The Lord possessed me in the beginning of his way, before his works of old.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** I was set up from everlasting, from the beginning, or ever the earth was.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** When there were no depths, I was brought forth; when there were no fountains abounding with water.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Before the mountains were settled, before the hills was I brought forth:

<a id="v26"></a>**26** While as yet he had not made the earth, nor the fields, nor the highest part of the dust of the world.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** When he prepared the heavens, I was there: when he set a compass upon the face of the depth:

<a id="v28"></a>**28** When he established the clouds above: when he strengthened the fountains of the deep:

<a id="v29"></a>**29** When he gave to the sea his decree, that the waters should not pass his commandment: when he appointed the foundations of the earth:

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Then I was by him, as one brought up with him: and I was daily his delight, rejoicing always before him;

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Rejoicing in the habitable part of his earth; and my delights were with the sons of men.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Now therefore hearken unto me, O ye children: for blessed are they that keep my ways.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Hear instruction, and be wise, and refuse it not.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Blessed is the man that heareth me, watching daily at my gates, waiting at the posts of my doors.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** For whoso findeth me findeth life, and shall obtain favour of the Lord.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** But he that sinneth against me wrongeth his own soul: all they that hate me love death.
