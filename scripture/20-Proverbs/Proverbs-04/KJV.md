# Proverbs 4 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear, ye children, the instruction of a father, and attend to know understanding.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For I give you good doctrine, forsake ye not my law.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For I was my father’s son, tender and only beloved in the sight of my mother.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He taught me also, and said unto me, Let thine heart retain my words: keep my commandments, and live.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Get wisdom, get understanding: forget it not; neither decline from the words of my mouth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Forsake her not, and she shall preserve thee: love her, and she shall keep thee.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Wisdom is the principal thing; therefore get wisdom: and with all thy getting get understanding.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Exalt her, and she shall promote thee: she shall bring thee to honour, when thou dost embrace her.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** She shall give to thine head an ornament of grace: a crown of glory shall she deliver to thee.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Hear, O my son, and receive my sayings; and the years of thy life shall be many.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I have taught thee in the way of wisdom; I have led thee in right paths.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** When thou goest, thy steps shall not be straitened; and when thou runnest, thou shalt not stumble.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Take fast hold of instruction; let her not go: keep her; for she is thy life.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Enter not into the path of the wicked, and go not in the way of evil men.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Avoid it, pass not by it, turn from it, and pass away.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For they sleep not, except they have done mischief; and their sleep is taken away, unless they cause some to fall.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For they eat the bread of wickedness, and drink the wine of violence.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But the path of the just is as the shining light, that shineth more and more unto the perfect day.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The way of the wicked is as darkness: they know not at what they stumble.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My son, attend to my words; incline thine ear unto my sayings.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Let them not depart from thine eyes; keep them in the midst of thine heart.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For they are life unto those that find them, and health to all their flesh.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Keep thy heart with all diligence; for out of it are the issues of life.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Put away from thee a froward mouth, and perverse lips put far from thee.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Let thine eyes look right on, and let thine eyelids look straight before thee.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Ponder the path of thy feet, and let all thy ways be established.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Turn not to the right hand nor to the left: remove thy foot from evil.
