# Proverbs 4 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Listen, sons, to a father’s instruction. Pay attention and know understanding;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** for I give you sound learning. Don’t forsake my law.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For I was a son to my father, tender and an only child in the sight of my mother.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He taught me, and said to me: “Let your heart retain my words. Keep my commandments, and live.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Get wisdom. Get understanding. Don’t forget, neither swerve from the words of my mouth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Don’t forsake her, and she will preserve you. Love her, and she will keep you.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Wisdom is supreme. Get wisdom. Yes, though it costs all your possessions, get understanding.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Esteem her, and she will exalt you. She will bring you to honor, when you embrace her.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** She will give to your head a garland of grace. She will deliver a crown of splendor to you.”

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Listen, my son, and receive my sayings. The years of your life will be many.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I have taught you in the way of wisdom. I have led you in straight paths.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** When you go, your steps will not be hampered. When you run, you will not stumble.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Take firm hold of instruction. Don’t let her go. Keep her, for she is your life.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Don’t enter into the path of the wicked. Don’t walk in the way of evil men.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Avoid it, and don’t pass by it. Turn from it, and pass on.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For they don’t sleep, unless they do evil. Their sleep is taken away, unless they make someone fall.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For they eat the bread of wickedness, and drink the wine of violence.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But the path of the righteous is like the dawning light, that shines more and more until the perfect day.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The way of the wicked is like darkness. They don’t know what they stumble over.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My son, attend to my words. Turn your ear to my sayings.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Let them not depart from your eyes. Keep them in the midst of your heart.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For they are life to those who find them, and health to their whole body.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Keep your heart with all diligence, for out of it is the wellspring of life.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Put away from yourself a perverse mouth. Put corrupt lips far from you.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Let your eyes look straight ahead. Fix your gaze directly before you.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Make the path of your feet level. Let all of your ways be established.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Don’t turn to the right hand nor to the left. Remove your foot from evil.
