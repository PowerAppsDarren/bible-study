# Proverbs 4 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Give ear, my sons, to the teaching of a father; give attention so that you may have knowledge:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For I give you good teaching; do not give up the knowledge you are getting from me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For I was a son to my father, a gentle and an only one to my mother.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And he gave me teaching, saying to me, Keep my words in your heart; keep my rules so that you may have life:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Get wisdom, get true knowledge; keep it in memory, do not be turned away from the words of my mouth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Do not give her up, and she will keep you; give her your love, and she will make you safe.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The first sign of wisdom is to get wisdom; go, give all you have to get true knowledge.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Put her in a high place, and you will be lifted up by her; she will give you honour, when you give her your love.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** She will put a crown of grace on your head, giving you a head-dress of glory.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Give ear, O my son, and let your heart be open to my sayings; and long life will be yours.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I have given you teaching in the way of wisdom, guiding your steps in the straight way.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** When you go, your way will not be narrow, and in running you will not have a fall.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Take learning in your hands, do not let her go: keep her, for she is your life.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Do not go in the road of sinners, or be walking in the way of evil men.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Keep far from it, do not go near; be turned from it, and go on your way.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For they take no rest till they have done evil; their sleep is taken away if they have not been the cause of someone's fall.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The bread of evil-doing is their food, the wine of violent acts their drink.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But the way of the upright is like the light of early morning, getting brighter and brighter till the full day.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The way of sinners is dark; they see not the cause of their fall.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My son, give attention to my words; let your ear be turned to my sayings.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Let them not go from your eyes; keep them deep in your heart.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For they are life to him who gets them, and strength to all his flesh.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And keep watch over your heart with all care; so you will have life.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Put away from you an evil tongue, and let false lips be far from you.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Keep your eyes on what is in front of you, looking straight before you.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Keep a watch on your behaviour; let all your ways be rightly ordered.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Let there be no turning to the right or to the left, keep your feet from evil.
