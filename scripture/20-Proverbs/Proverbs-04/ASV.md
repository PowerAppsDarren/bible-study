# Proverbs 4 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear, mysons, the instruction of a father, And attend to know understanding:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For I give you good doctrine; Forsake ye not my law.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For I was a son unto my father, Tender and only beloved in the sight of my mother.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And he taught me, and said unto me: Let thy heart retain my words; Keep my commandments, and live;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Get wisdom, get understanding; Forget not, neither decline from the words of my mouth;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Forsake her not, and she will preserve thee; Love her, and she will keep thee.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Wisdomisthe principal thing; therefore get wisdom; Yea, with all thy getting get understanding.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Exalt her, and she will promote thee; She will bring thee to honor, when thou dost embrace her.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** She will give to thy head a chaplet of grace; A crown of beauty will she deliver to thee.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Hear, O my son, and receive my sayings; And the years of thy life shall be many.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I have taught thee in the way of wisdom; I have led thee in paths of uprightness.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** When thou goest, thy steps shall not be straitened; And if thou runnest, thou shalt not stumble.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Take fast hold of instruction; let her not go: Keep her; for she is thy life.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Enter not into the path of the wicked, And walk not in the way of evil men.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Avoid it, pass not by it; Turn from it, and pass on.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For they sleep not, except they do evil; And their sleep is taken away, unless they cause some to fall.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For they eat the bread of wickedness, And drink the wine of violence.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But the path of the righteous is as the dawning light, That shineth more and more unto the perfect day.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The way of the wicked is as darkness: They know not at what they stumble.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My son, attend to my words; Incline thine ear unto my sayings.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Let them not depart from thine eyes; Keep them in the midst of thy heart.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For they are life unto those that find them, And health to all their flesh.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Keep thy heart with all diligence; For out of it are the issues of life.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Put away from thee a wayward mouth, And perverse lips put far from thee.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Let thine eyes look right on, And let thine eyelids look straight before thee.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Make level the path of thy feet, And let all thy ways be established.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Turn not to the right hand nor to the left: Remove thy foot from evil.
