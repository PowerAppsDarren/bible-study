# Proverbs 11 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Scales of deceit are hated by the Lord, but a true weight is his delight.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** When pride comes, there comes shame, but wisdom is with the quiet in spirit.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The righteousness of the upright will be their guide, but the twisted ways of the false will be their destruction.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Wealth is of no profit in the day of wrath, but righteousness keeps a man safe from death.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The righteousness of the good man will make his way straight, but the sin of the evil-doer will be the cause of his fall.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The righteousness of the upright will be their salvation, but the false will themselves be taken in their evil designs.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** At the death of an upright man his hope does not come to an end, but the hope of the evil-doer comes to destruction.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The upright man is taken out of trouble, and in his place comes the sinner.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** With his mouth the evil man sends destruction on his neighbour; but through knowledge the upright are taken out of trouble.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** When things go well for the upright man, all the town is glad; at the death of sinners, there are cries of joy.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** By the blessing of the upright man the town is made great, but it is overturned by the mouth of the evil-doer.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** He who has a poor opinion of his neighbour has no sense, but a wise man keeps quiet.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He who goes about talking of others makes secrets public, but the true-hearted man keeps things covered.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** When there is no helping suggestion the people will have a fall, but with a number of wise guides they will be safe.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He who makes himself responsible for a strange man will undergo much loss; but the hater of such undertakings will be safe.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** A woman who is full of grace is honoured, but a woman hating righteousness is a seat of shame: those hating work will undergo loss, but the strong keep their wealth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The man who has mercy will be rewarded, but the cruel man is the cause of trouble to himself.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The sinner gets the payment of deceit; but his reward is certain who puts in the seed of righteousness.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** So righteousness gives life; but he who goes after evil gets death for himself.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The uncontrolled are hated by the Lord, but those whose ways are without error are his delight

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Certainly the evil-doer will not go free from punishment, but the seed of the upright man will be safe.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Like a ring of gold in the nose of a pig, is a beautiful woman who has no sense.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** The desire of the upright man is only for good, but wrath is waiting for the evil-doer.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** A man may give freely, and still his wealth will be increased; and another may keep back more than is right, but only comes to be in need.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** He who gives blessing will be made fat, but the curser will himself be cursed.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** He who keeps back grain will be cursed by the people; but a blessing will be on the head of him who lets them have it for a price.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** He who, with all his heart, goes after what is good is searching for grace; but he who is looking for trouble will get it.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** He who puts his faith in wealth will come to nothing; but the upright man will be full of growth like the green leaf.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** The troubler of his house will have the wind for his heritage, and the foolish will be servant to the wise-hearted.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** The fruit of righteousness is a tree of life, but violent behaviour takes away souls.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** If the upright man is rewarded on earth, how much more the evil-doer and the sinner!
