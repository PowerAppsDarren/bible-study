# Proverbs 15 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** A soft answer turneth away wrath; But a grievous word stirreth up anger.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The tongue of the wise uttereth knowledge aright; But the mouth of fools poureth out folly.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The eyes of Jehovah are in every place, Keeping watch upon the evil and the good.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** A gentle tongue is a tree of life; But perverseness therein is a breaking of the spirit.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** A fool despiseth his father’s correction; But he that regardeth reproof getteth prudence.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** In the house of the righteous is much treasure; But in the revenues of the wicked is trouble.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The lips of the wise disperse knowledge; But the heart of the foolishdoethnot so.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The sacrifice of the wicked is an abomination to Jehovah; But the prayer of the upright is his delight.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The way of the wicked is an abomination to Jehovah; But he loveth him that followeth after righteousness.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** There is grievous correction for him that forsaketh the way; And he that hateth reproof shall die.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Sheol and Abaddon are before Jehovah; How much more then the hearts of the children of men!

<a id="v12"></a>**12** A scoffer loveth not to be reproved; He will not go unto the wise.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** A glad heart maketh a cheerful countenance; But by sorrow of heart the spirit is broken.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The heart of him that hath understanding seeketh knowledge; But the mouth of fools feedeth on folly.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** All the days of the afflicted are evil; But he that is of a cheerful heart hath a continual feast.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Better is little, with the fear of Jehovah, Than great treasure and trouble therewith.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Better is a dinner of herbs, where love is, Than a stalled ox and hatred therewith.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** A wrathful man stirreth up contention; But he that is slow to anger appeaseth strife.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The way of the sluggard is as a hedge of thorns; But the path of the upright is made a highway.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** A wise son maketh a glad father; But a foolish man despiseth his mother.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Folly is joy to him that is void of wisdom; But a man of understanding maketh straight his going.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Where there is no counsel, purposes are disappointed; But in the multitude of counsellors they are established.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** A man hath joy in the answer of his mouth; And a word in due season, how good is it!

<a id="v24"></a>**24** To the wise the way of lifegoethupward, That he may depart from Sheol beneath.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Jehovah will root up the house of the proud; But he will establish the border of the widow.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Evil devices are an abomination to Jehovah; But pleasant wordsarepure.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** He that is greedy of gain troubleth his own house; But he that hateth bribes shall live.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The heart of the righteous studieth to answer; But the mouth of the wicked poureth out evil things.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Jehovah is far from the wicked; But he heareth the prayer of the righteous.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** The light of the eyes rejoiceth the heart; Andgood tidings make the bones fat.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** The ear that hearkeneth to the reproof of life Shall abide among the wise.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** He that refuseth correction despiseth his own soul; But he that hearkeneth to reproof getteth understanding.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** The fear of Jehovah is the instruction of wisdom; And before honorgoethhumility.
