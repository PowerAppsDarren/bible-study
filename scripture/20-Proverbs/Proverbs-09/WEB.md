# Proverbs 9 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Wisdom has built her house. She has carved out her seven pillars.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** She has prepared her meat. She has mixed her wine. She has also set her table.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** She has sent out her maidens. She cries from the highest places of the city:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** “Whoever is simple, let him turn in here!” As for him who is void of understanding, she says to him,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** “Come, eat some of my bread, Drink some of the wine which I have mixed!

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Leave your simple ways, and live. Walk in the way of understanding.”

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He who corrects a mocker invites insult. He who reproves a wicked man invites abuse.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Don’t reprove a scoffer, lest he hate you. Reprove a wise man, and he will love you.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Instruct a wise man, and he will be still wiser. Teach a righteous man, and he will increase in learning.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The fear of Yahweh is the beginning of wisdom. The knowledge of the Holy One is understanding.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For by me your days will be multiplied. The years of your life will be increased.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** If you are wise, you are wise for yourself. If you mock, you alone will bear it.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The foolish woman is loud, Undisciplined, and knows nothing.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** She sits at the door of her house, on a seat in the high places of the city,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** To call to those who pass by, who go straight on their ways,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** “Whoever is simple, let him turn in here.” as for him who is void of understanding, she says to him,

<a id="v17"></a>**17** “Stolen water is sweet. Food eaten in secret is pleasant.”

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But he doesn’t know that the departed spirits are there, that her guests are in the depths of Sheol.
