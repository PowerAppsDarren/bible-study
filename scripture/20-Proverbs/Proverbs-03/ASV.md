# Proverbs 3 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My son, forget not my law; But let thy heart keep my commandments:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For length of days, and years of life, And peace, will they add to thee.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Let not kindness and truth forsake thee: Bind them about thy neck; Write them upon the tablet of thy heart:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** So shalt thou find favor and good understanding In the sight of God and man.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Trust in Jehovah with all thy heart, And lean not upon thine own understanding:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** In all thy ways acknowledge him, And he will direct thy paths.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Be not wise in thine own eyes; Fear Jehovah, and depart from evil:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** It will be health to thy navel, And marrow to thy bones.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Honor Jehovah with thy substance, And with the first-fruits of all thine increase:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** So shall thy barns be filled with plenty, And thy vats shall overflow with new wine.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My son, despise not the chastening of Jehovah; Neither be weary of his reproof:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For whom Jehovah loveth he reproveth, Even as a father the son in whom he delighteth.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Happy is the man that findeth wisdom, And the man that getteth understanding.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For the gaining of it is better than the gaining of silver, And the profit thereof than fine gold.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** She is more precious than rubies: And none of the things thou canst desire are to be compared unto her.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Length of days is in her right hand; In her left hand are riches and honor.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Her ways are ways of pleasantness, And all her paths are peace.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** She is a tree of life to them that lay hold upon her: And happy is every one that retaineth her.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Jehovah by wisdom founded the earth; By understanding he established the heavens.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** By his knowledge the depths were broken up, And the skies drop down the dew.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** My son, let them not depart from thine eyes; Keep sound wisdom and discretion:

<a id="v22"></a>**22** So shall they be life unto thy soul, And grace to thy neck.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Then shalt thou walk in thy way securely, And thy foot shall not stumble.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** When thou liest down, thou shalt not be afraid: Yea, thou shalt lie down, and thy sleep shall be sweet.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Be not afraid of sudden fear, Neither of the desolation of the wicked, when it cometh:

<a id="v26"></a>**26** For Jehovah will be thy confidence, And will keep thy foot from being taken.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Withhold not good from them to whom it is due, When it is in the power of thy hand to do it.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Say not unto thy neighbor, Go, and come again, And to-morrow I will give; When thou hast it by thee.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Devise not evil against thy neighbor, Seeing he dwelleth securely by thee.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Strive not with a man without cause, If he have done thee no harm.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Envy thou not the man of violence, And choose none of his ways.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** For the perverse is an abomination to Jehovah; But his friendship is with the upright.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** The curse of Jehovah is in the house of the wicked; But he blesseth the habitation of the righteous.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Surely he scoffeth at the scoffers; But he giveth grace unto the lowly.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** The wise shall inherit glory; But shame shall be the promotion of fools.
