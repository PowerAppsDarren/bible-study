# Proverbs 3 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My son, don’t forget my teaching; but let your heart keep my commandments:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** for length of days, and years of life, and peace, will they add to you.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Don’t let kindness and truth forsake you. Bind them around your neck. Write them on the tablet of your heart.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** So you will find favor, and good understanding in the sight of God and man.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Trust in Yahweh with all your heart, and don’t lean on your own understanding.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** In all your ways acknowledge him, and he will make your paths straight.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Don’t be wise in your own eyes. Fear Yahweh, and depart from evil.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** It will be health to your body, and nourishment to your bones.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Honor Yahweh with your substance, with the first fruits of all your increase:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** so your barns will be filled with plenty, and your vats will overflow with new wine.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My son, don’t despise Yahweh’s discipline, neither be weary of his reproof:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** for whom Yahweh loves, he reproves; even as a father reproves the son in whom he delights.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Happy is the man who finds wisdom, the man who gets understanding.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For her good profit is better than getting silver, and her return is better than fine gold.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** She is more precious than rubies. None of the things you can desire are to be compared to her.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Length of days is in her right hand. In her left hand are riches and honor.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Her ways are ways of pleasantness. All her paths are peace.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** She is a tree of life to those who lay hold of her. Happy is everyone who retains her.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** By wisdom Yahweh founded the earth. By understanding, he established the heavens.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** By his knowledge, the depths were broken up, and the skies drop down the dew.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** My son, let them not depart from your eyes. Keep sound wisdom and discretion:

<a id="v22"></a>**22** so they will be life to your soul, and grace for your neck.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Then you shall walk in your way securely. Your foot won’t stumble.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** When you lie down, you will not be afraid. Yes, you will lie down, and your sleep will be sweet.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Don’t be afraid of sudden fear, neither of the desolation of the wicked, when it comes:

<a id="v26"></a>**26** for Yahweh will be your confidence, and will keep your foot from being taken.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Don’t withhold good from those to whom it is due, when it is in the power of your hand to do it.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Don’t say to your neighbor, “Go, and come again; tomorrow I will give it to you,” when you have it by you.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Don’t devise evil against your neighbor, since he dwells securely by you.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Don’t strive with a man without cause, if he has done you no harm.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Don’t envy the man of violence. Choose none of his ways.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** For the perverse is an abomination to Yahweh, but his friendship is with the upright.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Yahweh’s curse is in the house of the wicked, but he blesses the habitation of the righteous.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Surely he mocks the mockers, but he gives grace to the humble.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** The wise will inherit glory, but shame will be the promotion of fools.
