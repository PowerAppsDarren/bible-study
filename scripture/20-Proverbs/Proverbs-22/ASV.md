# Proverbs 22 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Agoodname is rather to be chosen than great riches, And loving favor rather than silver and gold.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The rich and the poor meet together: Jehovah is the maker of them all.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** A prudent man seeth the evil, and hideth himself; But the simple pass on, and suffer for it.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The reward of humility  and  the fear of Jehovah  Isriches, and honor, and life.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thornsandsnares are in the way of the perverse: He that keepeth his soul shall be far from them.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Train up a child in the way he should go, And even when he is old he will not depart from it.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The rich ruleth over the poor; And the borrower is servant to the lender.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He that soweth iniquity shall reap calamity; And the rod of his wrath shall fail.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He that hath a bountiful eye shall be blessed; For he giveth of his bread to the poor.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Cast out the scoffer, and contention will go out; Yea, strife and ignominy will cease.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He that loveth pureness of heart, Forthe grace of his lips the king will be his friend.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The eyes of Jehovah preservehim that hathknowledge; But he overthroweth the words of the treacherous man.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The sluggard saith, There is a lion without; I shall be slain in the streets.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The mouth of strange women is a deep pit; He that is abhorred of Jehovah shall fall therein.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Foolishness is bound up in the heart of a child; Butthe rod of correction shall drive it far from him.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He that oppresseth the poor to increase his gain, Andhe that giveth to the rich, shall come only to want.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Incline thine ear, and hear the words of the wise, And apply thy heart unto my knowledge.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For it is a pleasant thing if thou keep them within thee, If they be established together upon thy lips.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** That thy trust may be in Jehovah, I have madethemknown to thee this day, even to thee.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Have not I written unto thee excellent things Of counsels and knowledge,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** To make thee know the certainty of the words of truth, That thou mayest carry back words of truth to them that send thee?

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Rob not the poor, because he is poor; Neither oppress the afflicted in the gate:

<a id="v23"></a>**23** For Jehovah will plead their cause, And despoil of life those that despoil them.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Make no friendship with a man that is given to anger; And with a wrathful man thou shalt not go:

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Lest thou learn his ways, And get a snare to thy soul.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Be thou not one of them that strike hands, Orof them that are sureties for debts.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** If thou hast not wherewith to pay, Why should he take away thy bed from under thee?

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Remove not the ancient landmark, Which thy fathers have set.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Seest thou a man diligent in his business? he shall stand before kings; He shall not stand before mean men.
