# Proverbs 6 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My son, if you have become collateral for your neighbor, if you have struck your hands in pledge for a stranger;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You are trapped by the words of your mouth. You are ensnared with the words of your mouth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Do this now, my son, and deliver yourself, since you have come into the hand of your neighbor. Go, humble yourself. Press your plea with your neighbor.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Give no sleep to your eyes, nor slumber to your eyelids.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Free yourself, like a gazelle from the hand of the hunter, like a bird from the snare of the fowler.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Go to the ant, you sluggard. Consider her ways, and be wise;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** which having no chief, overseer, or ruler,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** provides her bread in the summer, and gathers her food in the harvest.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** How long will you sleep, sluggard? When will you arise out of your sleep?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** A little sleep, a little slumber, a little folding of the hands to sleep:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** so your poverty will come as a robber, and your scarcity as an armed man.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** A worthless person, a man of iniquity, is he who walks with a perverse mouth;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** who winks with his eyes, who signals with his feet, who motions with his fingers;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** in whose heart is perverseness, who devises evil continually, who always sows discord.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Therefore his calamity will come suddenly. He will be broken suddenly, and that without remedy.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** There are six things which Yahweh hates; yes, seven which are an abomination to him:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** haughty eyes, a lying tongue, hands that shed innocent blood;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** a heart that devises wicked schemes, feet that are swift in running to mischief,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** a false witness who utters lies, and he who sows discord among brothers.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My son, keep your father’s commandment, and don’t forsake your mother’s teaching.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Bind them continually on your heart. Tie them around your neck.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** When you walk, it will lead you. When you sleep, it will watch over you. When you awake, it will talk with you.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** For the commandment is a lamp, and the law is light. Reproofs of instruction are the way of life,

<a id="v24"></a>**24** to keep you from the immoral woman, from the flattery of the wayward wife’s tongue.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Don’t lust after her beauty in your heart, neither let her captivate you with her eyelids.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** For a prostitute reduces you to a piece of bread. The adulteress hunts for your precious life.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Can a man scoop fire into his lap, and his clothes not be burned?

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Or can one walk on hot coals, and his feet not be scorched?

<a id="v29"></a>**29** So is he who goes in to his neighbor’s wife. Whoever touches her will not be unpunished.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Men don’t despise a thief, if he steals to satisfy himself when he is hungry:

<a id="v31"></a>**31** but if he is found, he shall restore seven times. He shall give all the wealth of his house.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** He who commits adultery with a woman is void of understanding. He who does it destroys his own soul.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** He will get wounds and dishonor. His reproach will not be wiped away.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** For jealousy arouses the fury of the husband. He won’t spare in the day of vengeance.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** He won’t regard any ransom, neither will he rest content, though you give many gifts.
