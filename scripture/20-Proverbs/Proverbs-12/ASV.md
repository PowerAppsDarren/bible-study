# Proverbs 12 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Whoso loveth correction loveth knowledge; But he that hateth reproof is brutish.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** A good man shall obtain favor of Jehovah; But a man of wicked devices will he condemn.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** A man shall not be established by wickedness; But the root of the righteous shall not be moved.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** A worthy woman is the crown of her husband; But she that maketh ashamed is as rottenness in his bones.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The thoughts of the righteous are just; Butthe counsels of the wicked are deceit.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The words of the wicked are of lying in wait for blood; But the mouth of the upright shall deliver them.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The wicked are overthrown, and are not; But the house of the righteous shall stand.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** A man shall be commended according to his wisdom; But he that is of a perverse heart shall be despised.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Better is he that is lightly esteemed, and hath a servant, Than he that honoreth himself, and lacketh bread.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** A righteous man regardeth the life of his beast; But the tender mercies of the wicked are cruel.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He that tilleth his land shall have plenty of bread; But he that followeth after vain persons is void of understanding.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The wicked desireth the net of evil men; But the root of the righteous yieldeth fruit.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** In the transgression of the lips is a snare to the evil man; But the righteous shall come out of trouble.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** A man shall be satisfied with good by the fruit of his mouth; And the doings of a man’s hands shall be rendered unto him.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The way of a fool is right in his own eyes; But he that is wise hearkeneth unto counsel.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** A fool’s vexation is presently known; But a prudent man concealeth shame.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He that uttereth truth showeth forth righteousness; But a false witness, deceit.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** There is that speaketh rashly like the piercings of a sword; But the tongue of the wise is health.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The lip of truth shall be established for ever; But a lying tongue is but for a moment.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Deceit is in the heart of them that devise evil; But to the counsellors of peace is joy.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** There shall no mischief happen to the righteous; But the wicked shall be filled with evil.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Lying lips are an abomination to Jehovah; But they that deal truly are his delight.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** A prudent man concealeth knowledge; But the heart of fools proclaimeth foolishness.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** The hand of the diligent shall bear rule; But the slothful shall be put under taskwork.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Heaviness in the heart of a man maketh it stoop; But a good word maketh it glad.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** The righteous is a guide to his neighbor; But the way of the wicked causeth them to err.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** The slothful man roasteth not that which he took in hunting; But the precious substance of menis tothe diligent.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** In the way of righteousness is life; And in the pathway thereof there is no death.
