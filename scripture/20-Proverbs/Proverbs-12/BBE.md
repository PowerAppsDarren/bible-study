# Proverbs 12 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** A lover of training is a lover of knowledge; but a hater of teaching is like a beast.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** A good man has grace in the eyes of the Lord; but the man of evil designs gets punishment from him.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** No man will make himself safe through evil-doing; but the root of upright men will never be moved.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** A woman of virtue is a crown to her husband; but she whose behaviour is a cause of shame is like a wasting disease in his bones.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The purposes of upright men are right, but the designs of evil-doers are deceit.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The words of sinners are destruction for the upright; but the mouth of upright men is their salvation.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Evil-doers are overturned and never seen again, but the house of upright men will keep its place.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** A man will be praised in the measure of his wisdom, but a wrong-minded man will be looked down on.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He who is of low position and has a servant, is better than one who has a high opinion of himself and is in need of bread.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** An upright man has thought for the life of his beast, but the hearts of evil-doers are cruel.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He who does work on his land will not be short of bread; but he who goes after foolish men is without sense.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The resting-place of the sinner will come to destruction, but the root of upright men is for ever.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** In the sin of the lips is a net which takes the sinner, but the upright man will come out of trouble.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** From the fruit of his mouth will a man have good food in full measure, and the work of a man's hands will be rewarded.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The way of the foolish man seems right to him? but the wise man gives ear to suggestions.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** A foolish man lets his trouble be openly seen, but a sharp man keeps shame secret.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The breathing out of true words gives knowledge of righteousness; but a false witness gives out deceit.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** There are some whose uncontrolled talk is like the wounds of a sword, but the tongue of the wise makes one well again.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** True lips are certain for ever, but a false tongue is only for a minute.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Deceit is in the heart of those whose designs are evil, but for those purposing peace there is joy.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** No trouble will come to upright men, but sinners will be full of evil.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** False lips are hated by the Lord, but those whose acts are true are his delight.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** A sharp man keeps back his knowledge; but the heart of foolish men makes clear their foolish thoughts.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** The hand of the ready worker will have authority, but he who is slow in his work will be put to forced work.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Care in the heart of a man makes it weighted down, but a good word makes it glad.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** The upright man is a guide to his neighbour, but the way of evil-doers is a cause of error to them.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** He who is slow in his work does not go in search of food; but the ready worker gets much wealth.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** In the road of righteousness is life, but the way of the evil-doer goes to death.
