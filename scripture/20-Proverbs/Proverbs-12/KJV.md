# Proverbs 12 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Whoso loveth instruction loveth knowledge: but he that hateth reproof is brutish.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** A good man obtaineth favour of the Lord: but a man of wicked devices will he condemn.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** A man shall not be established by wickedness: but the root of the righteous shall not be moved.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** A virtuous woman is a crown to her husband: but she that maketh ashamed is as rottenness in his bones.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The thoughts of the righteous are right: but the counsels of the wicked are deceit.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The words of the wicked are to lie in wait for blood: but the mouth of the upright shall deliver them.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The wicked are overthrown, and are not: but the house of the righteous shall stand.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** A man shall be commended according to his wisdom: but he that is of a perverse heart shall be despised.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He that is despised, and hath a servant, is better than he that honoureth himself, and lacketh bread.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** A righteous man regardeth the life of his beast: but the tender mercies of the wicked are cruel.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He that tilleth his land shall be satisfied with bread: but he that followeth vain persons is void of understanding.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** The wicked desireth the net of evil men: but the root of the righteous yieldeth fruit.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The wicked is snared by the transgression of his lips: but the just shall come out of trouble.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** A man shall be satisfied with good by the fruit of his mouth: and the recompence of a man’s hands shall be rendered unto him.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The way of a fool is right in his own eyes: but he that hearkeneth unto counsel is wise.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** A fool’s wrath is presently known: but a prudent man covereth shame.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He that speaketh truth sheweth forth righteousness: but a false witness deceit.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** There is that speaketh like the piercings of a sword: but the tongue of the wise is health.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The lip of truth shall be established for ever: but a lying tongue is but for a moment.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Deceit is in the heart of them that imagine evil: but to the counsellors of peace is joy.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** There shall no evil happen to the just: but the wicked shall be filled with mischief.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Lying lips are abomination to the Lord: but they that deal truly are his delight.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** A prudent man concealeth knowledge: but the heart of fools proclaimeth foolishness.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** The hand of the diligent shall bear rule: but the slothful shall be under tribute.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Heaviness in the heart of man maketh it stoop: but a good word maketh it glad.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** The righteous is more excellent than his neighbour: but the way of the wicked seduceth them.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** The slothful man roasteth not that which he took in hunting: but the substance of a diligent man is precious.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** In the way of righteousness is life; and in the pathway thereof there is no death.
