# Proverbs 28 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The wicked flee when no man pursueth; But the righteous are bold as a lion.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For the transgression of a land many are the princes thereof; But by men of understandingandknowledge the state thereof shall be prolonged.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** A needy man that oppresseth the poor Islikea sweeping rain which leaveth no food.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They that forsake the law praise the wicked; But such as keep the law contend with them.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Evil men understand not justice; But they that seek Jehovah understand all things.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Better is the poor that walketh in his integrity, Than he that is perverse inhisways, though he be rich.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Whoso keepeth the law is a wise son; But he that is a companion of gluttons shameth his father.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He that augmenteth his substance by interest and increase, Gathereth it for him that hath pity on the poor.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He that turneth away his ear from hearing the law, Even his prayer is an abomination.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Whoso causeth the upright to go astray in an evil way, He shall fall himself into his own pit; But the perfect shall inherit good.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The rich man is wise in his own conceit; But the poor that hath understanding searcheth him out.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** When the righteous triumph, there is great glory; But when the wicked rise, men hide themselves.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He that covereth his transgressions shall not prosper; But whoso confesseth and forsaketh them shall obtain mercy.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Happy is the man that feareth alway; But he that hardeneth his heart shall fall into mischief.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Asa roaring lion, and a ranging bear, So isa wicked ruler over a poor people.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The prince that lacketh understanding is also a great oppressor; Buthe that hateth covetousness shall prolong his days.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** A man that is laden with the blood of any person  Shall flee unto the pit; let no man stay him.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Whoso walketh uprightly shall be delivered; But he that is perverse in his ways shall fall at once.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He that tilleth his land shall have plenty of bread; But he that followeth after vain persons shall have poverty enough.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** A faithful man shall abound with blessings; But he that maketh haste to be rich shall not be unpunished.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** To have respect of persons is not good; Neither that a man should transgress for a piece of bread.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** He that hath an evil eye hasteth after riches, And knoweth not that want shall come upon him.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** He that rebuketh a man shall afterward find more favor Than he that flattereth with the tongue.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Whoso robbeth his father or his mother, and saith, It is no transgression, The same is the companion of a destroyer.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** He that is of a greedy spirit stirreth up strife; But he that putteth his trust in Jehovah shall be made fat.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** He that trusteth in his own heart is a fool; But whoso walketh wisely, he shall be delivered.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** He that giveth unto the poor shall not lack; But he that hideth his eyes shall have many a curse.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** When the wicked rise, men hide themselves; But when they perish, the righteous increase.
