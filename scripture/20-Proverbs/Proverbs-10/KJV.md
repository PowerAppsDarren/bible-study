# Proverbs 10 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The proverbs of Solomon. A wise son maketh a glad father: but a foolish son is the heaviness of his mother.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Treasures of wickedness profit nothing: but righteousness delivereth from death.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The Lord will not suffer the soul of the righteous to famish: but he casteth away the substance of the wicked.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He becometh poor that dealeth with a slack hand: but the hand of the diligent maketh rich.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He that gathereth in summer is a wise son: but he that sleepeth in harvest is a son that causeth shame.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Blessings are upon the head of the just: but violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The memory of the just is blessed: but the name of the wicked shall rot.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The wise in heart will receive commandments: but a prating fool shall fall.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He that walketh uprightly walketh surely: but he that perverteth his ways shall be known.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He that winketh with the eye causeth sorrow: but a prating fool shall fall.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The mouth of a righteous man is a well of life: but violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Hatred stirreth up strifes: but love covereth all sins.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** In the lips of him that hath understanding wisdom is found: but a rod is for the back of him that is void of understanding.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Wise men lay up knowledge: but the mouth of the foolish is near destruction.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The rich man’s wealth is his strong city: the destruction of the poor is their poverty.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The labour of the righteous tendeth to life: the fruit of the wicked to sin.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He is in the way of life that keepeth instruction: but he that refuseth reproof erreth.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He that hideth hatred with lying lips, and he that uttereth a slander, is a fool.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** In the multitude of words there wanteth not sin: but he that refraineth his lips is wise.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The tongue of the just is as choice silver: the heart of the wicked is little worth.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The lips of the righteous feed many: but fools die for want of wisdom.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The blessing of the Lord, it maketh rich, and he addeth no sorrow with it.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** It is as sport to a fool to do mischief: but a man of understanding hath wisdom.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** The fear of the wicked, it shall come upon him: but the desire of the righteous shall be granted.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** As the whirlwind passeth, so is the wicked no more: but the righteous is an everlasting foundation.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** As vinegar to the teeth, and as smoke to the eyes, so is the sluggard to them that send him.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** The fear of the Lord prolongeth days: but the years of the wicked shall be shortened.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The hope of the righteous shall be gladness: but the expectation of the wicked shall perish.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** The way of the Lord is strength to the upright: but destruction shall be to the workers of iniquity.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** The righteous shall never be removed: but the wicked shall not inhabit the earth.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** The mouth of the just bringeth forth wisdom: but the froward tongue shall be cut out.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The lips of the righteous know what is acceptable: but the mouth of the wicked speaketh frowardness.
