# Proverbs 10 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The proverbs of Solomon.  A wise son makes a glad father; but a foolish son brings grief to his mother.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Treasures of wickedness profit nothing, but righteousness delivers from death.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yahweh will not allow the soul of the righteous to go hungry, but he thrusts away the desire of the wicked.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He becomes poor who works with a lazy hand, but the hand of the diligent brings wealth.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He who gathers in summer is a wise son, but he who sleeps during the harvest is a son who causes shame.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Blessings are on the head of the righteous, but violence covers the mouth of the wicked.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The memory of the righteous is blessed, but the name of the wicked will rot.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The wise in heart accept commandments, but a chattering fool will fall.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He who walks blamelessly walks surely, but he who perverts his ways will be found out.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** One winking with the eye causes sorrow, but a chattering fool will fall.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The mouth of the righteous is a spring of life, but violence covers the mouth of the wicked.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Hatred stirs up strife, but love covers all wrongs.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Wisdom is found on the lips of him who has discernment, but a rod is for the back of him who is void of understanding.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Wise men lay up knowledge, but the mouth of the foolish is near ruin.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The rich man’s wealth is his strong city. The destruction of the poor is their poverty.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The labor of the righteous leads to life. The increase of the wicked leads to sin.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He is in the way of life who heeds correction, but he who forsakes reproof leads others astray.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He who hides hatred has lying lips. He who utters a slander is a fool.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** In the multitude of words there is no lack of disobedience, but he who restrains his lips does wisely.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The tongue of the righteous is like choice silver. The heart of the wicked is of little worth.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The lips of the righteous feed many, but the foolish die for lack of understanding.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Yahweh’s blessing brings wealth, and he adds no trouble to it.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** It is a fool’s pleasure to do wickedness, but wisdom is a man of understanding’s pleasure.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** What the wicked fear, will overtake them, but the desire of the righteous will be granted.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** When the whirlwind passes, the wicked is no more; but the righteous stand firm forever.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** As vinegar to the teeth, and as smoke to the eyes, so is the sluggard to those who send him.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** The fear of Yahweh prolongs days, but the years of the wicked shall be shortened.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The prospect of the righteous is joy, but the hope of the wicked will perish.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** The way of Yahweh is a stronghold to the upright, but it is a destruction to the workers of iniquity.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** The righteous will never be removed, but the wicked will not dwell in the land.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** The mouth of the righteous produces wisdom, but the perverse tongue will be cut off.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The lips of the righteous know what is acceptable, but the mouth of the wicked is perverse.
