# Proverbs 10 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The proverbs of Solomon.   A wise son maketh a glad father; But a foolish son is the heaviness of his mother.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Treasures of wickedness profit nothing; But righteousness delivereth from death.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Jehovah will not suffer the soul of the righteous to famish; But he thrusteth away the desire of the wicked.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He becometh poor that worketh with a slack hand; But the hand of the diligent maketh rich.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He that gathereth in summer is a wise son; Buthe that sleepeth in harvest is a son that causeth shame.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Blessings are upon the head of the righteous; But violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The memory of the righteous is blessed; But the name of the wicked shall rot.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The wise in heart will receive commandments; But a prating fool shall fall.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He that walketh uprightly walketh surely; But he that perverteth his ways shall be known.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He that winketh with the eye causeth sorrow; But a prating fool shall fall.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The mouth of the righteous is a fountain of life; But violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Hatred stirreth up strifes; But love covereth all transgressions.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** In the lips of him that hath discernment wisdom is found; But a rod is for the back of him that is void of understanding.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Wise men lay up knowledge; But the mouth of the foolish is a present destruction.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The rich man’s wealth is his strong city: The destruction of the poor is their poverty.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** The labor of the righteoustendethto life; The increase of the wicked, to sin.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He is in the way of life that heedeth correction; But he that forsaketh reproof erreth.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He that hideth hatred is of lying lips; And he that uttereth a slander is a fool.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** In the multitude of words there wanteth not transgression; But he that refraineth his lips doeth wisely.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The tongue of the righteous isaschoice silver: The heart of the wicked is little worth.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The lips of the righteous feed many; But the foolish die for lack of understanding.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The blessing of Jehovah, it maketh rich; And he addeth no sorrow therewith.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** It is as sport to a fool to do wickedness; Andso iswisdom to a man of understanding.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** The fear of the wicked, it shall come upon him; And the desire of the righteous shall be granted.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** When the whirlwind passeth, the wicked is no more; But the righteous is an everlasting foundation.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** As vinegar to the teeth, and as smoke to the eyes, So is the sluggard to them that send him.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** The fear of Jehovah prolongeth days; But the years of the wicked shall be shortened.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** The hope of the righteousshall begladness; But the expectation of the wicked shall perish.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** The way of Jehovah is a stronghold to the upright; But it is a destruction to the workers of iniquity.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** The righteous shall never be removed; But the wicked shall not dwell in the land.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** The mouth of the righteous bringeth forth wisdom; But the perverse tongue shall be cut off.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The lips of the righteous know what is acceptable; But the mouth of the wickedspeaketh perverseness.
