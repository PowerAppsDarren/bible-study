# Proverbs 29 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** He that being often reproved hardeneth his neck Shall suddenly be destroyed, and that without remedy.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** When the righteous are increased, the people rejoice; But when a wicked man beareth rule, the people sigh.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Whoso loveth wisdom rejoiceth his father; But he that keepeth company with harlots wasteth his substance.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The king by justice establisheth the land; But he that exacteth gifts overthroweth it.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** A man that flattereth his neighbor Spreadeth a net for his steps.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** In the transgression of an evil man there is a snare; But the righteous doth sing and rejoice.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The righteous taketh knowledge of the cause of the poor; The wicked hath not understanding to know it.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Scoffers set a city in a flame; But wise men turn away wrath.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** If a wise man hath a controversy with a foolish man, Whether he be angry or laugh, there will be no rest.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The bloodthirsty hate him that is perfect; And as for the upright, they seek his life.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** A fool uttereth all his anger; But a wise man keepeth it back and stilleth it.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** If a ruler hearkeneth to falsehood, All his servants are wicked.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The poor man and the oppressor meet together; Jehovah lighteneth the eyes of them both.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The king that faithfully judgeth the poor, His throne shall be established for ever.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The rod and reproof give wisdom; But a child left to himself causeth shame to his mother.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** When the wicked are increased, transgression increaseth; But the righteous shall look upon their fall.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Correct thy son, and he will give thee rest; Yea, he will give delight unto thy soul.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Where there is no vision, the people cast off restraint; But he that keepeth the law, happy is he.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** A servant will not be corrected by words; For though he understand, he will not give heed.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Seest thou a man that is hasty in his words? There is more hope of a fool than of him.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He that delicately bringeth up his servant from a child Shall have him become a son at the last.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** An angry man stirreth up strife, And a wrathful man aboundeth in transgression.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** A man’s pride shall bring him low; But he that is of a lowly spirit shall obtain honor.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Whoso is partner with a thief hateth his own soul; He heareth the adjuration and uttereth nothing.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** The fear of man bringeth a snare; But whoso putteth his trust in Jehovah shall be safe.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Many seek the ruler’s favor; But a man’s judgmentcomethfrom Jehovah.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** An unjust man is an abomination to the righteous; And he that is upright in the way is an abomination to the wicked.
