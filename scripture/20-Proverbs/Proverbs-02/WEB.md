# Proverbs 2 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My son, if you will receive my words, and store up my commandments within you;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** So as to turn your ear to wisdom, and apply your heart to understanding;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yes, if you call out for discernment, and lift up your voice for understanding;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** If you seek her as silver, and search for her as for hidden treasures:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** then you will understand the fear of Yahweh, and find the knowledge of God.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For Yahweh gives wisdom. Out of his mouth comes knowledge and understanding.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He lays up sound wisdom for the upright. He is a shield to those who walk in integrity;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** that he may guard the paths of justice, and preserve the way of his saints.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Then you will understand righteousness and justice, equity and every good path.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For wisdom will enter into your heart. Knowledge will be pleasant to your soul.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Discretion will watch over you. Understanding will keep you,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** to deliver you from the way of evil, from the men who speak perverse things;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** who forsake the paths of uprightness, to walk in the ways of darkness;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** who rejoice to do evil, and delight in the perverseness of evil;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** who are crooked in their ways, and wayward in their paths:

<a id="v16"></a>**16** To deliver you from the strange woman, even from the foreigner who flatters with her words;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** who forsakes the friend of her youth, and forgets the covenant of her God:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** for her house leads down to death, her paths to the departed spirits.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** None who go to her return again, neither do they attain to the paths of life:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** that you may walk in the way of good men, and keep the paths of the righteous.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For the upright will dwell in the land. The perfect will remain in it.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But the wicked will be cut off from the land. The treacherous will be rooted out of it.
