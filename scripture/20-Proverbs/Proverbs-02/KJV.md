# Proverbs 2 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My son, if thou wilt receive my words, and hide my commandments with thee;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** So that thou incline thine ear unto wisdom, and apply thine heart to understanding;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yea, if thou criest after knowledge, and liftest up thy voice for understanding;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** If thou seekest her as silver, and searchest for her as for hid treasures;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Then shalt thou understand the fear of the Lord, and find the knowledge of God.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For the Lord giveth wisdom: out of his mouth cometh knowledge and understanding.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He layeth up sound wisdom for the righteous: he is a buckler to them that walk uprightly.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He keepeth the paths of judgment, and preserveth the way of his saints.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Then shalt thou understand righteousness, and judgment, and equity; yea, every good path.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** When wisdom entereth into thine heart, and knowledge is pleasant unto thy soul;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Discretion shall preserve thee, understanding shall keep thee:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** To deliver thee from the way of the evil man, from the man that speaketh froward things;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Who leave the paths of uprightness, to walk in the ways of darkness;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Who rejoice to do evil, and delight in the frowardness of the wicked;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Whose ways are crooked, and they froward in their paths:

<a id="v16"></a>**16** To deliver thee from the strange woman, even from the stranger which flattereth with her words;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Which forsaketh the guide of her youth, and forgetteth the covenant of her God.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For her house inclineth unto death, and her paths unto the dead.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** None that go unto her return again, neither take they hold of the paths of life.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** That thou mayest walk in the way of good men, and keep the paths of the righteous.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For the upright shall dwell in the land, and the perfect shall remain in it.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But the wicked shall be cut off from the earth, and the transgressors shall be rooted out of it.
