# Proverbs 2 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My son, if thou wilt receive my words, And lay up my commandments with thee;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** So as to incline thine ear unto wisdom, And apply thy heart to understanding;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yea, if thou cry after discernment, And lift up thy voice for understanding;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** If thou seek her as silver, And search for her as for hid treasures:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Then shalt thou understand the fear of Jehovah, And find the knowledge of God.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For Jehovah giveth wisdom; Out of his mouth cometh knowledge and understanding:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He layeth up sound wisdom for the upright; He isa shield to them that walk in integrity;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** That he may guard the paths of justice, And preserve the way of his saints.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Then shalt thou understand righteousness and justice, And equity, yea, every good path.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For wisdom shall enter into thy heart, And knowledge shall be pleasant unto thy soul;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Discretion shall watch over thee; Understanding shall keep thee:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** To deliver thee from the way of evil, From the men that speak perverse things;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Who forsake the paths of uprightness, To walk in the ways of darkness;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Who rejoice to do evil, And delight in the perverseness of evil;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Who are crooked in their ways, And wayward in their paths:

<a id="v16"></a>**16** To deliver thee from the strange woman, Even from the foreigner that flattereth with her words;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** That forsaketh the friend of her youth, And forgetteth the covenant of her God:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For her house inclineth unto death, And her paths unto the dead;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** None that go unto her return again, Neither do they attain unto the paths of life:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** That thou mayest walk in the way of good men, And keep the paths of the righteous.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For the upright shall dwell in the land, And the perfect shall remain in it.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But the wicked shall be cut off from the land, And the treacherous shall be rooted out of it.
