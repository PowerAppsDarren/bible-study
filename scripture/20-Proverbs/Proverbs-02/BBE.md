# Proverbs 2 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My son, if you will take my words to your heart, storing up my laws in your mind;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** So that your ear gives attention to wisdom, and your heart is turned to knowledge;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Truly, if you are crying out for good sense, and your request is for knowledge;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** If you are looking for her as for silver, and searching for her as for stored-up wealth;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Then the fear of the Lord will be clear to you, and knowledge of God will be yours.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For the Lord gives wisdom; out of his mouth come knowledge and reason:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He has salvation stored up for the upright, he is a breastplate to those in whom there is no evil;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He keeps watch on the ways which are right, and takes care of those who have the fear of him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Then you will have knowledge of righteousness and right acting, and upright behaviour, even of every good way.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For wisdom will come into your heart, and knowledge will be pleasing to your soul;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Wise purposes will be watching over you, and knowledge will keep you;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Giving you salvation from the evil man, from those whose words are false;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Who give up the way of righteousness, to go by dark roads;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Who take pleasure in wrongdoing, and have joy in the evil designs of the sinner;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Whose ways are not straight, and whose footsteps are turned to evil:

<a id="v16"></a>**16** To take you out of the power of the strange woman, who says smooth words with her tongue;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Who is false to the husband of her early years, and does not keep the agreement of her God in mind:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For her house is on the way down to death; her footsteps go down to the shades:

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Those who go to her do not come back again; their feet do not keep in the ways of life:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** So that you may go in the way of good men, and keep in the footsteps of the upright.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For the upright will be living in the land, and the good will have it for their heritage.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But sinners will be cut off from the land, and those whose acts are false will be uprooted.
