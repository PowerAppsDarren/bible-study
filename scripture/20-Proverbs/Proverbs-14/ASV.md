# Proverbs 14 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Every wise woman buildeth her house; But the foolish plucketh it down with her own hands.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He that walketh in his uprightness feareth Jehovah; But he that is perverse in his ways despiseth him.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** In the mouth of the foolish is a rod forhispride; But the lips of the wise shall preserve them.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Where no oxen are, the crib is clean; But much increase is by the strength of the ox.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** A faithful witness will not lie; But a false witness uttereth lies.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** A scoffer seeketh wisdom, and findeth it not; But knowledge is easy unto him that hath understanding.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Go into the presence of a foolish man, And thou shalt not perceive in him the lips of knowledge.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The wisdom of the prudent is to understand his way; But the folly of fools is deceit.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** A trespass-offering mocketh fools; But among the upright there is good will.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The heart knoweth its own bitterness; And a stranger doth not intermeddle with its joy.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The house of the wicked shall be overthrown; But the tent of the upright shall flourish.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** There is a way which seemeth right unto a man; But the end thereof are the ways of death.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Even in laughter the heart is sorrowful; And the end of mirth is heaviness.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The backslider in heart shall be filled with his own ways; And a good manshall be satisfiedfrom himself.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The simple believeth every word; But the prudent man looketh well to his going.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** A wise man feareth, and departeth from evil; But the fool beareth himself insolently, and is confident.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He that is soon angry will deal foolishly; And a man of wicked devices is hated.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The simple inherit folly; But the prudent are crowned with knowledge.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The evil bow down before the good; And the wicked, at the gates of the righteous.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The poor is hated even of his own neighbor; But the rich hath many friends.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He that despiseth his neighbor sinneth; But he that hath pity on the poor, happy is he.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Do they not err that devise evil? But mercy and truth shall be to them that devise good.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** In all labor there is profit; But the talk of the lipstendethonly to penury.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** The crown of the wise is their riches; But the folly of fools is only folly.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** A true witness delivereth souls; But he that uttereth lies causeth deceit.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** In the fear of Jehovah is strong confidence; And his children shall have a place of refuge.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** The fear of Jehovah is a fountain of life, That one may depart from the snares of death.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** In the multitude of people is the king’s glory; But in the want of people is the destruction of the prince.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** He that is slow to anger is of great understanding; But he that is hasty of spirit exalteth folly.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** A tranquil heart is the life of the flesh; But envy is the rottenness of the bones.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** He that oppresseth the poor reproacheth his Maker; But he that hath mercy on the needy honoreth him.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** The wicked is thrust down in his evil-doing; But the righteous hath a refuge in his death.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Wisdom resteth in the heart of him that hath understanding; Butthat which isin the inward part of fools is made known.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Righteousness exalteth a nation; But sin is a reproach to any people.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** The king’s favor is toward a servant that dealeth wisely; But his wrath will beagainsthim that causeth shame.
