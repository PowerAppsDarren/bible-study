# Proverbs 13 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** A wise son heareth his father’s instruction: but a scorner heareth not rebuke.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** A man shall eat good by the fruit of his mouth: but the soul of the transgressors shall eat violence.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He that keepeth his mouth keepeth his life: but he that openeth wide his lips shall have destruction.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The soul of the sluggard desireth, and hath nothing: but the soul of the diligent shall be made fat.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** A righteous man hateth lying: but a wicked man is loathsome, and cometh to shame.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Righteousness keepeth him that is upright in the way: but wickedness overthroweth the sinner.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** There is that maketh himself rich, yet hath nothing: there is that maketh himself poor, yet hath great riches.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The ransom of a man’s life are his riches: but the poor heareth not rebuke.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The light of the righteous rejoiceth: but the lamp of the wicked shall be put out.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Only by pride cometh contention: but with the well advised is wisdom.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Wealth gotten by vanity shall be diminished: but he that gathereth by labour shall increase.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Hope deferred maketh the heart sick: but when the desire cometh, it is a tree of life.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Whoso despiseth the word shall be destroyed: but he that feareth the commandment shall be rewarded.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The law of the wise is a fountain of life, to depart from the snares of death.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Good understanding giveth favour: but the way of transgressors is hard.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Every prudent man dealeth with knowledge: but a fool layeth open his folly.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** A wicked messenger falleth into mischief: but a faithful ambassador is health.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Poverty and shame shall be to him that refuseth instruction: but he that regardeth reproof shall be honoured.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The desire accomplished is sweet to the soul: but it is abomination to fools to depart from evil.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He that walketh with wise men shall be wise: but a companion of fools shall be destroyed.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Evil pursueth sinners: but to the righteous good shall be repayed.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** A good man leaveth an inheritance to his children’s children: and the wealth of the sinner is laid up for the just.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Much food is in the tillage of the poor: but there is that is destroyed for want of judgment.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** He that spareth his rod hateth his son: but he that loveth him chasteneth him betimes.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** The righteous eateth to the satisfying of his soul: but the belly of the wicked shall want.
