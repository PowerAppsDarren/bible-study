# Proverbs 16 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The plans of the heart belong to man, but the answer of the tongue is from Yahweh.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** All the ways of a man are clean in his own eyes; but Yahweh weighs the motives.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Commit your deeds to Yahweh, and your plans shall succeed.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Yahweh has made everything for its own end— yes, even the wicked for the day of evil.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Everyone who is proud in heart is an abomination to Yahweh: they shall certainly not be unpunished.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** By mercy and truth iniquity is atoned for. By the fear of Yahweh men depart from evil.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** When a man’s ways please Yahweh, he makes even his enemies to be at peace with him.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Better is a little with righteousness, than great revenues with injustice.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** A man’s heart plans his course, but Yahweh directs his steps.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Inspired judgments are on the lips of the king. He shall not betray his mouth.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Honest balances and scales are Yahweh’s; all the weights in the bag are his work.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** It is an abomination for kings to do wrong, for the throne is established by righteousness.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Righteous lips are the delight of kings. They value one who speaks the truth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The king’s wrath is a messenger of death, but a wise man will pacify it.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** In the light of the king’s face is life. His favor is like a cloud of the spring rain.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** How much better it is to get wisdom than gold! Yes, to get understanding is to be chosen rather than silver.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The highway of the upright is to depart from evil. He who keeps his way preserves his soul.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Pride goes before destruction, and a haughty spirit before a fall.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** It is better to be of a lowly spirit with the poor, than to divide the plunder with the proud.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He who heeds the Word finds prosperity. Whoever trusts in Yahweh is blessed.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The wise in heart shall be called prudent. Pleasantness of the lips promotes instruction.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Understanding is a fountain of life to one who has it, but the punishment of fools is their folly.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** The heart of the wise instructs his mouth, and adds learning to his lips.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Pleasant words are a honeycomb, sweet to the soul, and health to the bones.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** There is a way which seems right to a man, but in the end it leads to death.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** The appetite of the laboring man labors for him; for his mouth urges him on.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** A worthless man devises mischief. His speech is like a scorching fire.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** A perverse man stirs up strife. A whisperer separates close friends.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** A man of violence entices his neighbor, and leads him in a way that is not good.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** One who winks his eyes to plot perversities, one who compresses his lips, is bent on evil.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Gray hair is a crown of glory. It is attained by a life of righteousness.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** One who is slow to anger is better than the mighty; one who rules his spirit, than he who takes a city.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** The lot is cast into the lap, but its every decision is from Yahweh.
