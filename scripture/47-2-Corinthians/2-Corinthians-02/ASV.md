# 2 Corinthians 2 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** But I determined this for myself, that I would not come again to you with sorrow.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For if I make you sorry, who then is he that maketh me glad but he that is made sorry by me?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And I wrote this very thing, lest, when I came, I should have sorrow from them of whom I ought to rejoice; having confidence in you all, that my joy is the joy of you all.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For out of much affliction and anguish of heart I wrote unto you with many tears; not that ye should be made sorry, but that ye might know the love which I have more abundantly unto you.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But if any hath caused sorrow, he hath caused sorrow, not to me, but in part (that I press not too heavily) to you all.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Sufficient to such a one is this punishment which was inflicted by the many;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** so that contrariwise ye should rather forgive him and comfort him, lest by any means such a one should be swallowed up with his overmuch sorrow.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Wherefore I beseech you to confirm your love toward him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For to this end also did I write, that I might know the proof of you, whether ye are obedient in all things.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But to whom ye forgive anything, I forgive also: for what I also have forgiven, if I have forgiven anything, for your sakes have I forgiven it in the presence of Christ;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** that no advantage may be gained over us by Satan: for we are not ignorant of his devices.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Now when I came to Troas for the gospel of Christ, and when a door was opened unto me in the Lord,

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I had no relief for my spirit, because I found not Titus my brother: but taking my leave of them, I went forth into Macedonia.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But thanks be unto God, who always leadeth us in triumph in Christ, and maketh manifest through us the savor of his knowledge in every place.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For we are a sweet savor of Christ unto God, in them that are saved, and in them that perish;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** to the one a savor from death unto death; to the other a savor from life unto life. And who is sufficient for these things?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For we are not as the many, corrupting the word of God: but as of sincerity, but as of God, in the sight of God, speak we in Christ.
