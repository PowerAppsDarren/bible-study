# 2 Corinthians 2 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** But I determined this with myself, that I would not come again to you in heaviness.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For if I make you sorry, who is he then that maketh me glad, but the same which is made sorry by me?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And I wrote this same unto you, lest, when I came, I should have sorrow from them of whom I ought to rejoice; having confidence in you all, that my joy is the joy of you all.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For out of much affliction and anguish of heart I wrote unto you with many tears; not that ye should be grieved, but that ye might know the love which I have more abundantly unto you.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But if any have caused grief, he hath not grieved me, but in part: that I may not overcharge you all.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Sufficient to such a man is this punishment, which was inflicted of many.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** So that contrariwise ye ought rather to forgive him, and comfort him, lest perhaps such a one should be swallowed up with overmuch sorrow.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Wherefore I beseech you that ye would confirm your love toward him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For to this end also did I write, that I might know the proof of you, whether ye be obedient in all things.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** To whom ye forgive any thing, I forgive also: for if I forgave any thing, to whom I forgave it, for your sakes forgave I it in the person of Christ;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Lest Satan should get an advantage of us: for we are not ignorant of his devices.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Furthermore, when I came to Troas to preach Christ’s gospel, and a door was opened unto me of the Lord,

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I had no rest in my spirit, because I found not Titus my brother: but taking my leave of them, I went from thence into Macedonia.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Now thanks be unto God, which always causeth us to triumph in Christ, and maketh manifest the savour of his knowledge by us in every place.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For we are unto God a sweet savour of Christ, in them that are saved, and in them that perish:

<a id="v16"></a>**16** To the one we are the savour of death unto death; and to the other the savour of life unto life. And who is sufficient for these things?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For we are not as many, which corrupt the word of God: but as of sincerity, but as of God, in the sight of God speak we in Christ.
