# 2 Corinthians 6 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Working together, we entreat also that you not receive the grace of God in vain,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** for he says,  “At an acceptable time I listened to you, in a day of salvation I helped you.”    Behold, now is the acceptable time. Behold, now is the day of salvation.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** We give no occasion of stumbling in anything, that our service may not be blamed,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** but in everything commending ourselves, as servants of God, in great endurance, in afflictions, in hardships, in distresses,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** in beatings, in imprisonments, in riots, in labors, in watchings, in fastings;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** in pureness, in knowledge, in patience, in kindness, in the Holy Spirit, in sincere love,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** in the word of truth, in the power of God; by the armor of righteousness on the right hand and on the left,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** by glory and dishonor, by evil report and good report; as deceivers, and yet true;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** as unknown, and yet well known; as dying, and behold, we live; as punished, and not killed;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** as sorrowful, yet always rejoicing; as poor, yet making many rich; as having nothing, and yet possessing all things.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Our mouth is open to you, Corinthians. Our heart is enlarged.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** You are not restricted by us, but you are restricted by your own affections.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Now in return, I speak as to my children, you also be open wide.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Don’t be unequally yoked with unbelievers, for what fellowship have righteousness and iniquity? Or what fellowship has light with darkness?

<a id="v15"></a>**15** What agreement has Christ with Belial? Or what portion has a believer with an unbeliever?

<a id="v16"></a>**16** What agreement has a temple of God with idols? For you are a temple of the living God. Even as God said, “I will dwell in them, and walk in them; and I will be their God, and they will be my people.”

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Therefore  “‘Come out from among them, and be separate,’ says the Lord. ‘Touch no unclean thing. I will receive you.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** I will be to you a Father. You will be to me sons and daughters,’    says the Lord Almighty.”
