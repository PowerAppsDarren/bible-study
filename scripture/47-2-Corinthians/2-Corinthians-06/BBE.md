# 2 Corinthians 6 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** We then, working together with God, make our request to you not to take the grace of God to no purpose.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** (For he says, I have given ear to you at a good time, and I have been your helper in a day of salvation: see, now is the good time; now is the day of salvation):

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Giving no cause for trouble in anything, so that no one may be able to say anything against our work;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But in everything making it clear that we are the servants of God, in quiet strength, in troubles, in need, in sorrow,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** In blows, in prisons, in attacks, in hard work, in watchings, in going without food;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** In a clean heart, in knowledge, in long waiting, in being kind, in the Holy Spirit, in true love,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** In the true word, in the power of God; with the arms of righteousness on the right hand and on the left,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** By glory and by shame, by an evil name and a good name; as untrue, and still true;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Unnoted, but still kept fully in mind; as near to death, but still living; as undergoing punishment, but not put to death;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** As full of sorrow, but ever glad; as poor, but giving wealth to others; as having nothing, but still having all things.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Our mouth is open to you, O Corinthians, our heart is wide.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** It is not our feelings to you which are narrow, but yours to us.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Now to give me back payment of the same sort (I am talking as to my children), let your hearts be wide open to me.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Do not keep company with those who have not faith: for what is there in common between righteousness and evil, or between light and dark?

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And what agreement is there between Christ and the Evil One? or what part has one who has faith with one who has not?

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And what agreement has the house of God with images? for we are a house of the living God; even as God has said, I will be living among them, and walking with them; and I will be their God, and they will be my people.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For which cause, Come out from among them, and be separate, says the Lord, and let no unclean thing come near you; and I will take you for myself,

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And will be a Father to you; and you will be my sons and daughters, says the Lord, the Ruler of all.
