# Colossians 3 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** If then you were raised together with Christ, seek the things that are above, where Christ is, seated on the right hand of God.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Set your mind on the things that are above, not on the things that are on the earth.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For you died, and your life is hidden with Christ in God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** When Christ, our life, is revealed, then you will also be revealed with him in glory.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Put to death therefore your members which are on the earth: sexual immorality, uncleanness, depraved passion, evil desire, and covetousness, which is idolatry;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** for which things’ sake the wrath of God comes on the children of disobedience.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You also once walked in those, when you lived in them;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** but now you also put them all away: anger, wrath, malice, slander, and shameful speaking out of your mouth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Don’t lie to one another, seeing that you have put off the old man with his doings,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** and have put on the new man, who is being renewed in knowledge after the image of his Creator,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** where there can’t be Greek and Jew, circumcision and uncircumcision, barbarian, Scythian, bondservant, freeman; but Christ is all, and in all.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Put on therefore, as God’s chosen ones, holy and beloved, a heart of compassion, kindness, lowliness, humility, and perseverance;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** bearing with one another, and forgiving each other, if any man has a complaint against any; even as Christ forgave you, so you also do.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Above all these things, walk in love, which is the bond of perfection.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And let the peace of God rule in your hearts, to which also you were called in one body; and be thankful.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Let the word of Christ dwell in you richly; in all wisdom teaching and admonishing one another with psalms, hymns, and spiritual songs, singing with grace in your heart to the Lord.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Whatever you do, in word or in deed, do all in the name of the Lord Jesus, giving thanks to God the Father, through him.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Wives, be in subjection to your husbands, as is fitting in the Lord.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Husbands, love your wives, and don’t be bitter against them.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Children, obey your parents in all things, for this pleases the Lord.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Fathers, don’t provoke your children, so that they won’t be discouraged.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Servants, obey in all things those who are your masters according to the flesh, not just when they are looking, as men pleasers, but in singleness of heart, fearing God.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And whatever you do, work heartily, as for the Lord, and not for men,

<a id="v24"></a>**24** knowing that from the Lord you will receive the reward of the inheritance; for you serve the Lord Christ.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** But he who does wrong will receive again for the wrong that he has done, and there is no partiality.
