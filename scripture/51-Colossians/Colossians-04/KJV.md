# Colossians 4 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Masters, give unto your servants that which is just and equal; knowing that ye also have a Master in heaven.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Continue in prayer, and watch in the same with thanksgiving;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Withal praying also for us, that God would open unto us a door of utterance, to speak the mystery of Christ, for which I am also in bonds:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** That I may make it manifest, as I ought to speak.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Walk in wisdom toward them that are without, redeeming the time.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let your speech be alway with grace, seasoned with salt, that ye may know how ye ought to answer every man.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** All my state shall Tychicus declare unto you, who is a beloved brother, and a faithful minister and fellowservant in the Lord:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Whom I have sent unto you for the same purpose, that he might know your estate, and comfort your hearts;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** With Onesimus, a faithful and beloved brother, who is one of you. They shall make known unto you all things which are done here.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Aristarchus my fellowprisoner saluteth you, and Marcus, sister’s son to Barnabas, (touching whom ye received commandments: if he come unto you, receive him;)

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And Jesus, which is called Justus, who are of the circumcision. These only are my fellowworkers unto the kingdom of God, which have been a comfort unto me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Epaphras, who is one of you, a servant of Christ, saluteth you, always labouring fervently for you in prayers, that ye may stand perfect and complete in all the will of God.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For I bear him record, that he hath a great zeal for you, and them that are in Laodicea, and them in Hierapolis.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Luke, the beloved physician, and Demas, greet you.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Salute the brethren which are in Laodicea, and Nymphas, and the church which is in his house.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And when this epistle is read among you, cause that it be read also in the church of the Laodiceans; and that ye likewise read the epistle from Laodicea.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And say to Archippus, Take heed to the ministry which thou hast received in the Lord, that thou fulfil it.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The salutation by the hand of me Paul. Remember my bonds. Grace be with you. Amen.  Written from Rome to the Colossians by Tychicus and Onesimus.
