# Colossians 4 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Masters, give your servants what is right and equal, conscious that you have a Master in heaven.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Give yourselves to prayer at all times, keeping watch with praise;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And making prayer for us, that God may give us an open door for the preaching of the word, the secret of Christ, for which I am now in chains;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** So that I may make it clear, as it is right for me to do.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Be wise in your behaviour to those who are outside, making good use of the time.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let your talk be with grace, mixed with salt, so that you may be able to give an answer to everyone.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Tychicus will give you news of all my business: he is a dear brother and true servant and helper in the word;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And I have sent him to you for this very purpose, so that you may have news of how we are, and so that he may give your hearts comfort;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And with him I have sent Onesimus, the true and well-loved brother, who is one of you. They will give you word of everything which is taking place here.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Aristarchus, my brother-prisoner, sends his love to you, and Mark, a relation of Barnabas (about whom you have been given orders: if he comes to you, be kind to him),

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And Jesus, whose other name is Justus; these are of the circumcision: they are my only brother-workers for the kingdom of God, who have been a comfort to me.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Epaphras, who is one of you, a servant of Christ Jesus, sends you his love, ever taking thought for you in his prayers, that you may be complete and fully certain of all the purpose of God.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For I give witness of him that he has undergone much trouble for you and for those in Laodicea and in Hierapolis.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Luke, our well-loved medical friend, and Demas, send you their love.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Give my love to the brothers in Laodicea and to Nymphas and the church in their house.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And when this letter has been made public among you, let the same be done in the church of Laodicea; and see that you have the letter from Laodicea.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Say to Archippus, See that you do the work which the Lord has given you to do.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** I, Paul, give you this word of love in my handwriting. Keep in memory that I am a prisoner. Grace be with you.
