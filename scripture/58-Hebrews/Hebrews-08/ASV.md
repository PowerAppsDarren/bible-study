# Hebrews 8 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Now in the things which we are saying the chief pointis this: We have such a high priest, who sat down on the right hand of the throne of the Majesty in the heavens,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** a minister of the sanctuary, and of the true tabernacle, which the Lord pitched, not man.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For every high priest is appointed to offer both gifts and sacrifices: wherefore it is necessary that thishigh priestalso have somewhat to offer.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Now if he were on earth, he would not be a priest at all, seeing there are those who offer the gifts according to the law;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** who servethat which isa copy and shadow of the heavenly things, even as Moses is warnedof Godwhen he is about to make the tabernacle: for, See, saith he, that thou make all things according to the pattern that was showed thee in the mount.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** But now hath he obtained a ministry the more excellent, by so much as he is also the mediator of a better covenant, which hath been enacted upon better promises.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For if that first covenant had been faultless, then would no place have been sought for a second.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For finding fault with them, he saith,  Behold, the days come, saith the Lord, That I will make a new covenant with the house of Israel and with the house of Judah;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Not according to the covenant that I made with their fathers  In the day that I took them by the hand to lead them forth out of the land of Egypt; For they continued not in my covenant, And I regarded them not, saith the Lord.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For this is the covenant that I will make with the house of Israel  After those days, saith the Lord; I will put my laws into their mind, And on their heart also will I write them: And I will be to them a God, And they shall be to me a people:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And they shall not teach every man his fellow-citizen, And every man his brother, saying, Know the Lord: For all shall know me, From the least to the greatest of them.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For I will be merciful to their iniquities, And their sins will I remember no more.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** In that he saith, A new covenant, he hath made the first old. But that which is becoming old and waxeth aged is nigh unto vanishing away.
