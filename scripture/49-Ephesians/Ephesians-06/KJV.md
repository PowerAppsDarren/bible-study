# Ephesians 6 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Children, obey your parents in the Lord: for this is right.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Honour thy father and mother; (which is the first commandment with promise;)

<a id="v3"></a>**3** That it may be well with thee, and thou mayest live long on the earth.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And, ye fathers, provoke not your children to wrath: but bring them up in the nurture and admonition of the Lord.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Servants, be obedient to them that are your masters according to the flesh, with fear and trembling, in singleness of your heart, as unto Christ;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Not with eyeservice, as menpleasers; but as the servants of Christ, doing the will of God from the heart;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** With good will doing service, as to the Lord, and not to men:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Knowing that whatsoever good thing any man doeth, the same shall he receive of the Lord, whether he be bond or free.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And, ye masters, do the same things unto them, forbearing threatening: knowing that your Master also is in heaven; neither is there respect of persons with him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Finally, my brethren, be strong in the Lord, and in the power of his might.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Put on the whole armour of God, that ye may be able to stand against the wiles of the devil.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For we wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this world, against spiritual wickedness in high places.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Wherefore take unto you the whole armour of God, that ye may be able to withstand in the evil day, and having done all, to stand.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Stand therefore, having your loins girt about with truth, and having on the breastplate of righteousness;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And your feet shod with the preparation of the gospel of peace;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Above all, taking the shield of faith, wherewith ye shall be able to quench all the fiery darts of the wicked.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And take the helmet of salvation, and the sword of the Spirit, which is the word of God:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Praying always with all prayer and supplication in the Spirit, and watching thereunto with all perseverance and supplication for all saints;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And for me, that utterance may be given unto me, that I may open my mouth boldly, to make known the mystery of the gospel,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** For which I am an ambassador in bonds: that therein I may speak boldly, as I ought to speak.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** But that ye also may know my affairs, and how I do, Tychicus, a beloved brother and faithful minister in the Lord, shall make known to you all things:

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Whom I have sent unto you for the same purpose, that ye might know our affairs, and that he might comfort your hearts.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Peace be to the brethren, and love with faith, from God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Grace be with all them that love our Lord Jesus Christ in sincerity. Amen.  Written from Rome unto the Ephesians by Tychicus.
