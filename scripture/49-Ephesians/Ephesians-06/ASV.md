# Ephesians 6 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Children, obey your parents in the Lord: for this is right.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Honor thy father and mother (which is the first commandment with promise),

<a id="v3"></a>**3** that it may be well with thee, and thou mayest live long on the earth.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And, ye fathers, provoke not your children to wrath: but nurture them in the chastening and admonition of the Lord.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Servants, be obedient unto them that according to the flesh are your masters, with fear and trembling, in singleness of your heart, as unto Christ;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** not in the way of eyeservice, as men-pleasers; but as servants of Christ, doing the will of God from the heart;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** with good will doing service, as unto the Lord, and not unto men:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** knowing that whatsoever good thing each one doeth, the same shall he receive again from the Lord, whether he be bond or free.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And, ye masters, do the same things unto them, and forbear threatening: knowing that he who is both their Master and yours is in heaven, and there is no respect of persons with him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Finally, be strong in the Lord, and in the strength of his might.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Put on the whole armor of God, that ye may be able to stand against the wiles of the devil.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For our wrestling is not against flesh and blood, but against the principalities, against the powers, against the world-rulers of this darkness, against the spiritual hosts of wickedness in the heavenly places.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Wherefore take up the whole armor of God, that ye may be able to withstand in the evil day, and, having done all, to stand.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Stand therefore, having girded your loins with truth, and having put on the breastplate of righteousness,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** and having shod your feet with the preparation of the gospel of peace;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** withal taking up the shield of faith, wherewith ye shall be able to quench all the fiery darts of the evil one.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And take the helmet of salvation, and the sword of the Spirit, which is the word of God:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** with all prayer and supplication praying at all seasons in the Spirit, and watching thereunto in all perseverance and supplication for all the saints,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** and on my behalf, that utterance may be given unto me in opening my mouth, to make known with boldness the mystery of the gospel,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** for which I am an ambassador in chains; that in it I may speak boldly, as I ought to speak.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** But that ye also may know my affairs, how I do, Tychicus, the beloved brother and faithful minister in the Lord, shall make known to you all things:

<a id="v22"></a>**22** whom I have sent unto you for this very purpose, that ye may know our state, and that he may comfort your hearts.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Peace be to the brethren, and love with faith, from God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Grace be with all them that love our Lord Jesus Christ witha loveincorruptible.
