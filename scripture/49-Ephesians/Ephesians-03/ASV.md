# Ephesians 3 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** For this cause I Paul, the prisoner of Christ Jesus in behalf of you Gentiles,—

<a id="v2"></a>**2** if so be that ye have heard of the dispensation of that grace of God which was given me to you-ward;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** how that by revelation was made known unto me the mystery, as I wrote before in few words,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** whereby, when ye read, ye can perceive my understanding in the mystery of Christ;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** which in other generations was not made known unto the sons of men, as it hath now been revealed unto his holy apostles and prophets in the Spirit;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** to wit, that the Gentiles are fellow-heirs, and fellow-members of the body, and fellow-partakers of the promise in Christ Jesus through the gospel,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** whereof I was made a minister, according to the gift of that grace of God which was given me according to the working of his power.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Unto me, who am less than the least of all saints, was this grace given, to preach unto the Gentiles the unsearchable riches of Christ;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** and to make all men see what is the dispensation of the mystery which for ages hath been hid in God who created all things;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** to the intent that now unto the principalities and the powers in the heavenly places might be made known through the church the manifold wisdom of God,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** according to the eternal purpose which he purposed in Christ Jesus our Lord:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** in whom we have boldness and access in confidence through our faith in him.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Wherefore I ask that ye may not faint at my tribulations for you, which are your glory.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For this cause I bow my knees unto the Father,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** from whom every family in heaven and on earth is named,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** that he would grant you, according to the riches of his glory, that ye may be strengthened with power through his Spirit in the inward man;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** that Christ may dwell in your hearts through faith; to the end that ye, being rooted and grounded in love,

<a id="v18"></a>**18** may be strong to apprehend with all the saints what is the breadth and length and height and depth,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** and to know the love of Christ which passeth knowledge, that ye may be filled unto all the fulness of God.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Now unto him that is able to do exceeding abundantly above all that we ask or think, according to the power that worketh in us,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** unto him be the glory in the church and in Christ Jesus unto all generations for ever and ever. Amen.
