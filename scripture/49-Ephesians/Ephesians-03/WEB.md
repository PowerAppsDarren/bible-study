# Ephesians 3 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** For this cause I, Paul, am the prisoner of Christ Jesus on behalf of you Gentiles,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** if it is so that you have heard of the administration of that grace of God which was given me toward you;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** how that by revelation the mystery was made known to me, as I wrote before in few words,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** by which, when you read, you can perceive my understanding in the mystery of Christ;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** which in other generations was not made known to the children of men, as it has now been revealed to his holy apostles and prophets in the Spirit;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** that the Gentiles are fellow heirs, and fellow members of the body, and fellow partakers of his promise in Christ Jesus through the Good News,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** of which I was made a servant, according to the gift of that grace of God which was given me according to the working of his power.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** To me, the very least of all saints, was this grace given, to preach to the Gentiles the unsearchable riches of Christ,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** and to make all men see what is the administration of the mystery which for ages has been hidden in God, who created all things through Jesus Christ;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** to the intent that now through the assembly the manifold wisdom of God might be made known to the principalities and the powers in the heavenly places,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** according to the eternal purpose which he purposed in Christ Jesus our Lord;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** in whom we have boldness and access in confidence through our faith in him.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Therefore I ask that you may not lose heart at my troubles for you, which are your glory.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For this cause, I bow my knees to the Father of our Lord Jesus Christ,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** from whom every family in heaven and on earth is named,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** that he would grant you, according to the riches of his glory, that you may be strengthened with power through his Spirit in the inward man;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** that Christ may dwell in your hearts through faith; to the end that you, being rooted and grounded in love,

<a id="v18"></a>**18** may be strengthened to comprehend with all the saints what is the breadth and length and height and depth,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** and to know Christ’s love which surpasses knowledge, that you may be filled with all the fullness of God.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Now to him who is able to do exceedingly abundantly above all that we ask or think, according to the power that works in us,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** to him be the glory in the assembly and in Christ Jesus to all generations forever and ever. Amen.
