# Ephesians 3 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** For this cause I Paul, the prisoner of Christ Jesus for you Gentiles,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** If that ordering of the grace of God has come to your knowledge, which was given to me for you,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** How by revelation the secret was made clear to me, as I said before in a short letter,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** By the reading of which you will be clear about my knowledge of the secret of Christ;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Which in other generations was not given to the sons of men, but the revelation of it has now been made to his holy Apostles and prophets in the Spirit;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Which is that the Gentiles have a part in the heritage, and in the same body, and in the same hope in Christ through the good news,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Of which I was made a preacher, through that grace of God which was given to me in the measure of the working of his power.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** To me, who am less than the least of all the saints, was this grace given, so that I might make clear to the Gentiles the good news of the unending wealth of Christ:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And make all men see what is the ordering of the secret which from the first has been kept in God who made all things;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** So that now to the rulers and the authorities in the heavens might be made clear through the church the wide-shining wisdom of God,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Which is seen in his eternal purpose in Christ Jesus our Lord:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** By whom we come near to God without fear through faith in him.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For this reason it is my prayer that you may not become feeble because of my troubles for you, which are your glory.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For this cause I go down on my knees before the Father,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** From whom every family in heaven and on earth is named,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** That in the wealth of his glory he would make you strong with power through his Spirit in your hearts;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** So that Christ may have his place in your hearts through faith; and that you, being rooted and based in love,

<a id="v18"></a>**18** May have strength to see with all the saints how wide and long and high and deep it is,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And to have knowledge of the love of Christ which is outside all knowledge, so that you may be made complete as God himself is complete.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Now to him who is able to do in full measure more than all our desires or thoughts, through the power which is working in us,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** To him be the glory in the church and in Christ Jesus to all generations for ever and ever. So be it.
