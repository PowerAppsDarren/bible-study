# Ephesians 1 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Paul, an apostle of Jesus Christ by the will of God, to the saints which are at Ephesus, and to the faithful in Christ Jesus:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Grace be to you, and peace, from God our Father, and from the Lord Jesus Christ.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who hath blessed us with all spiritual blessings in heavenly places in Christ:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** According as he hath chosen us in him before the foundation of the world, that we should be holy and without blame before him in love:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Having predestinated us unto the adoption of children by Jesus Christ to himself, according to the good pleasure of his will,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** To the praise of the glory of his grace, wherein he hath made us accepted in the beloved.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** In whom we have redemption through his blood, the forgiveness of sins, according to the riches of his grace;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Wherein he hath abounded toward us in all wisdom and prudence;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Having made known unto us the mystery of his will, according to his good pleasure which he hath purposed in himself:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** That in the dispensation of the fulness of times he might gather together in one all things in Christ, both which are in heaven, and which are on earth; even in him:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** In whom also we have obtained an inheritance, being predestinated according to the purpose of him who worketh all things after the counsel of his own will:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** That we should be to the praise of his glory, who first trusted in Christ.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** In whom ye also trusted, after that ye heard the word of truth, the gospel of your salvation: in whom also after that ye believed, ye were sealed with that holy Spirit of promise,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Which is the earnest of our inheritance until the redemption of the purchased possession, unto the praise of his glory.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Wherefore I also, after I heard of your faith in the Lord Jesus, and love unto all the saints,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Cease not to give thanks for you, making mention of you in my prayers;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** That the God of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of glory, may give unto you the spirit of wisdom and revelation in the knowledge of him:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The eyes of your understanding being enlightened; that ye may know what is the hope of his calling, and what the riches of the glory of his inheritance in the saints,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And what is the exceeding greatness of his power to us-ward who believe, according to the working of his mighty power,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Which he wrought in Christ, when he raised him from the dead, and set him at his own right hand in the heavenly places,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Far above all principality, and power, and might, and dominion, and every name that is named, not only in this world, but also in that which is to come:

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And hath put all things under his feet, and gave him to be the head over all things to the church,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Which is his body, the fulness of him that filleth all in all.
