# Ephesians 1 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Paul, an apostle of Christ Jesus through the will of God, to the saints that are at Ephesus, and the faithful in Christ Jesus:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Grace to you and peace from God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Blessedbethe God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who hath blessed us with every spiritual blessing in the heavenly places in Christ:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** even as he chose us in him before the foundation of the world, that we should be holy and without blemish before him in love:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** having foreordained us unto adoption as sons through Jesus Christ unto himself, according to the good pleasure of his will,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** to the praise of the glory of his grace, which he freely bestowed on us in the Beloved:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** in whom we have our redemption through his blood, the forgiveness of our trespasses, according to the riches of his grace,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** which he made to abound toward us in all wisdom and prudence,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** making known unto us the mystery of his will, according to his good pleasure which he purposed in him

<a id="v10"></a>**10** unto a dispensation of the fulness of the times, to sum up all things in Christ, the things in the heavens, and the things upon the earth; in him, I say,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** in whom also we were made a heritage, having been foreordained according to the purpose of him who worketh all things after the counsel of his will;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** to the end that we should be unto the praise of his glory, we who had before hoped in Christ:

<a id="v13"></a>**13** in whom ye also, having heard the word of the truth, the gospel of your salvation,—in whom, having also believed, ye were sealed with the Holy Spirit of promise,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** which is an earnest of our inheritance, unto the redemption of God’s own possession, unto the praise of his glory.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For this cause I also, having heard of the faith in the Lord Jesus which is among you, and the love which ye show toward all the saints,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** cease not to give thanks for you, making mention of you in my prayers;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** that the God of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of glory, may give unto you a spirit of wisdom and revelation in the knowledge of him;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** having the eyes of your heart enlightened, that ye may know what is the hope of his calling, what the riches of the glory of his inheritance in the saints,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** and what the exceeding greatness of his power to us-ward who believe, according to that working of the strength of his might

<a id="v20"></a>**20** which he wrought in Christ, when he raised him from the dead, and made him to sit at his right hand in the heavenly places,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** far above all rule, and authority, and power, and dominion, and every name that is named, not only in this world, but also in that which is to come:

<a id="v22"></a>**22** and he put all things in subjection under his feet, and gave him to be head over all things to the church,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** which is his body, the fulness of him that filleth all in all.
