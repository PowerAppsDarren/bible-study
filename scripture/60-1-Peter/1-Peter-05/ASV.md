# 1 Peter 5 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The elders therefore among you I exhort, who am a fellow-elder, and a witness of the sufferings of Christ, who am also a partaker of the glory that shall be revealed:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Tend the flock of God which is among you, exercising the oversight, not of constraint, but willingly, according to the will of God; nor yet for filthy lucre, but of a ready mind;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** neither as lording it over the charge allotted to you, but making yourselves ensamples to the flock.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And when the chief Shepherd shall be manifested, ye shall receive the crown of glory that fadeth not away.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Likewise, ye younger, be subject unto the elder. Yea, all of you gird yourselves with humility, to serve one another: for God resisteth the proud, but giveth grace to the humble.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Humble yourselves therefore under the mighty hand of God, that he may exalt you in due time;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** casting all your anxiety upon him, because he careth for you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Be sober, be watchful: your adversary the devil, as a roaring lion, walketh about, seeking whom he may devour:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** whom withstand stedfast in your faith, knowing that the same sufferings are accomplished in your brethren who are in the world.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And the God of all grace, who called you unto his eternal glory in Christ, after that ye have suffered a little while, shall himself perfect, establish, strengthen you.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** To him be the dominion for ever and ever. Amen.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** By Silvanus, our faithful brother, as I accounthim, I have written unto you briefly, exhorting, and testifying that this is the true grace of God: stand ye fast therein.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** She that is in Babylon, elect together with you, saluteth you; and so doth Mark my son.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Salute one another with a kiss of love. Peace be unto you all that are in Christ.
