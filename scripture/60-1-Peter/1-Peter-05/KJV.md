# 1 Peter 5 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The elders which are among you I exhort, who am also an elder, and a witness of the sufferings of Christ, and also a partaker of the glory that shall be revealed:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Feed the flock of God which is among you, taking the oversight thereof, not by constraint, but willingly; not for filthy lucre, but of a ready mind;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Neither as being lords over God’s heritage, but being ensamples to the flock.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And when the chief Shepherd shall appear, ye shall receive a crown of glory that fadeth not away.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Likewise, ye younger, submit yourselves unto the elder. Yea, all of you be subject one to another, and be clothed with humility: for God resisteth the proud, and giveth grace to the humble.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Humble yourselves therefore under the mighty hand of God, that he may exalt you in due time:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Casting all your care upon him; for he careth for you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Be sober, be vigilant; because your adversary the devil, as a roaring lion, walketh about, seeking whom he may devour:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Whom resist stedfast in the faith, knowing that the same afflictions are accomplished in your brethren that are in the world.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But the God of all grace, who hath called us unto his eternal glory by Christ Jesus, after that ye have suffered a while, make you perfect, stablish, strengthen, settle you.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** To him be glory and dominion for ever and ever. Amen.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** By Silvanus, a faithful brother unto you, as I suppose, I have written briefly, exhorting, and testifying that this is the true grace of God wherein ye stand.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The church that is at Babylon, elected together with you, saluteth you; and so doth Marcus my son.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Greet ye one another with a kiss of charity. Peace be with you all that are in Christ Jesus. Amen.
