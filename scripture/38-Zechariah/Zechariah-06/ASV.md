# Zechariah 6 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And again I lifted up mine eyes, and saw, and, behold, there came four chariots out from between two mountains; and the mountains were mountains of brass.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** In the first chariot were red horses; and in the second chariot black horses;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** and in the third chariot white horses; and in the fourth chariot grizzled strong horses.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Then I answered and said unto the angel that talked with me, What are these, my lord?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And the angel answered and said unto me, These are the four winds of heaven, which go forth from standing before the Lord of all the earth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The chariot wherein are the black horses goeth forth toward the north country; and the white went forth after them; and the grizzled went forth toward the south country.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And the strong went forth, and sought to go that they might walk to and fro through the earth: and he said, Get you hence, walk to and fro through the earth. So they walked to and fro through the earth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Then cried he to me, and spake unto me, saying, Behold, they that go toward the north country have quieted my spirit in the north country.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And the word of Jehovah came unto me, saying,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Take of them of the captivity, even of Heldai, of Tobijah, and of Jedaiah; and come thou the same day, and go into the house of Josiah the son of Zephaniah, whither they are come from Babylon;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** yea, take of them silver and gold, and make crowns, and set them upon the head of Joshua the son of Jehozadak, the high priest;

<a id="v12"></a>**12** and speak unto him, saying, Thus speaketh Jehovah of hosts, saying, Behold, the man whose name is the Branch: and he shall grow up out of his place; and he shall build the temple of Jehovah;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** even he shall build the temple of Jehovah; and he shall bear the glory, and shall sit and rule upon his throne; and he shall be a priest upon his throne; and the counsel of peace shall be between them both.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And the crowns shall be to Helem, and to Tobijah, and to Jedaiah, and to Hen the son of Zephaniah, for a memorial in the temple of Jehovah.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And they that are far off shall come and build in the temple of Jehovah; and ye shall know that Jehovah of hosts hath sent me unto you. And this shall come to pass, if ye will diligently obey the voice of Jehovah your God.
