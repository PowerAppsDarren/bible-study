# Zechariah 5 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then again lifting up my eyes I saw a roll in flight through the air.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And he said to me, What do you see? And I said, A roll going through the air; it is twenty cubits long and ten cubits wide.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Then he said to me, This is the curse which goes out over the face of all the land: for long enough has every thief gone without punishment, and long enough has every taker of false oaths gone without punishment.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And I will send it out, says the Lord of armies, and it will go into the house of the thief and into the house of him who takes a false oath by my name: and it will be in his house, causing its complete destruction, with its woodwork and its stones.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And the angel who was talking to me went out and said to me, Let your eyes be lifted up now, and see the ephah which is going out.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And I said, What is it? And he said, This is an ephah which is going out. And he said further, This is their evil-doing in all the land.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And I saw a round cover of lead lifted up; and a woman was seated in the middle of the ephah.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And he said, This is Sin; and pushing her down into the ephah, he put the weight of lead on the mouth of it.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And lifting up my eyes I saw two women coming out, and the wind was in their wings; and they had wings like the wings of a stork: and they took the ephah, lifting it up between earth and heaven.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And I said to the angel who was talking to me, Where are they taking the ephah?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And he said to me, To make a house for her in the land of Shinar: and they will make a place ready, and put her there in the place which is hers.
