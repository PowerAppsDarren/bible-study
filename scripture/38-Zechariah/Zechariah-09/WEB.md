# Zechariah 9 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** An oracle.  Yahweh’s word is against the land of Hadrach, and will rest upon Damascus; for the eye of man and of all the tribes of Israel is toward Yahweh;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** and Hamath, also, which borders on it; Tyre and Sidon, because they are very wise.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Tyre built herself a stronghold, and heaped up silver like the dust, and fine gold like the mire of the streets.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Behold, the Lord will dispossess her, and he will strike her power in the sea; and she will be devoured with fire.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Ashkelon will see it, and fear; Gaza also, and will writhe in agony; as will Ekron, for her expectation will be disappointed; and the king will perish from Gaza, and Ashkelon will not be inhabited.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Foreigners will dwell in Ashdod, and I will cut off the pride of the Philistines.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I will take away his blood out of his mouth, and his abominations from between his teeth; and he also will be a remnant for our God; and he will be as a chieftain in Judah, and Ekron as a Jebusite.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will encamp around my house against the army, that no one pass through or return; and no oppressor will pass through them any more: for now I have seen with my eyes.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Rejoice greatly, daughter of Zion! Shout, daughter of Jerusalem! Behold, your King comes to you! He is righteous, and having salvation; lowly, and riding on a donkey, even on a colt, the foal of a donkey.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I will cut off the chariot from Ephraim, and the horse from Jerusalem; and the battle bow will be cut off; and he will speak peace to the nations: and his dominion will be from sea to sea, and from the River to the ends of the earth.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** As for you also, because of the blood of your covenant, I have set free your prisoners from the pit in which is no water.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Turn to the stronghold, you prisoners of hope! Even today I declare that I will restore double to you.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For indeed I bend Judah as a bow for me. I have filled the bow with Ephraim; and I will stir up your sons, Zion, against your sons, Greece, and will make you like the sword of a mighty man.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Yahweh will be seen over them; and his arrow will go flash like lightning; and the Lord Yahweh will blow the trumpet, and will go with whirlwinds of the south.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Yahweh of Armies will defend them; and they will destroy and overcome with sling stones; and they will drink, and roar as through wine; and they will be filled like bowls, like the corners of the altar.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Yahweh their God will save them in that day as the flock of his people; for they are like the jewels of a crown, lifted on high over his land.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For how great is his goodness, and how great is his beauty! Grain will make the young men flourish, and new wine the virgins.
