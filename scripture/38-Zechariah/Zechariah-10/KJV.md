# Zechariah 10 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Ask ye of the Lord rain in the time of the latter rain; so the Lord shall make bright clouds, and give them showers of rain, to every one grass in the field.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** For the idols have spoken vanity, and the diviners have seen a lie, and have told false dreams; they comfort in vain: therefore they went their way as a flock, they were troubled, because there was no shepherd.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Mine anger was kindled against the shepherds, and I punished the goats: for the Lord of hosts hath visited his flock the house of Judah, and hath made them as his goodly horse in the battle.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Out of him came forth the corner, out of him the nail, out of him the battle bow, out of him every oppressor together.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And they shall be as mighty men, which tread down their enemies in the mire of the streets in the battle: and they shall fight, because the Lord is with them, and the riders on horses shall be confounded.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And I will strengthen the house of Judah, and I will save the house of Joseph, and I will bring them again to place them; for I have mercy upon them: and they shall be as though I had not cast them off: for I am the Lord their God, and will hear them.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And they of Ephraim shall be like a mighty man, and their heart shall rejoice as through wine: yea, their children shall see it, and be glad; their heart shall rejoice in the Lord.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will hiss for them, and gather them; for I have redeemed them: and they shall increase as they have increased.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And I will sow them among the people: and they shall remember me in far countries; and they shall live with their children, and turn again.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I will bring them again also out of the land of Egypt, and gather them out of Assyria; and I will bring them into the land of Gilead and Lebanon; and place shall not be found for them.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And he shall pass through the sea with affliction, and shall smite the waves in the sea, and all the deeps of the river shall dry up: and the pride of Assyria shall be brought down, and the sceptre of Egypt shall depart away.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And I will strengthen them in the Lord; and they shall walk up and down in his name, saith the Lord.
