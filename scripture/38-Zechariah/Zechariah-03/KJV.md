# Zechariah 3 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And he shewed me Joshua the high priest standing before the angel of the Lord, and Satan standing at his right hand to resist him.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And the Lord said unto Satan, The Lord rebuke thee, O Satan; even the Lord that hath chosen Jerusalem rebuke thee: is not this a brand plucked out of the fire?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Now Joshua was clothed with filthy garments, and stood before the angel.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And he answered and spake unto those that stood before him, saying, Take away the filthy garments from him. And unto him he said, Behold, I have caused thine iniquity to pass from thee, and I will clothe thee with change of raiment.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And I said, Let them set a fair mitre upon his head. So they set a fair mitre upon his head, and clothed him with garments. And the angel of the Lord stood by.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And the angel of the Lord protested unto Joshua, saying,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Thus saith the Lord of hosts; If thou wilt walk in my ways, and if thou wilt keep my charge, then thou shalt also judge my house, and shalt also keep my courts, and I will give thee places to walk among these that stand by.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Hear now, O Joshua the high priest, thou, and thy fellows that sit before thee: for they are men wondered at: for, behold, I will bring forth my servant the BRANCH.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For behold the stone that I have laid before Joshua; upon one stone shall be seven eyes: behold, I will engrave the graving thereof, saith the Lord of hosts, and I will remove the iniquity of that land in one day.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** In that day, saith the Lord of hosts, shall ye call every man his neighbour under the vine and under the fig tree.
