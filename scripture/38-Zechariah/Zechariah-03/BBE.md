# Zechariah 3 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And he let me see Joshua, the high priest, in his place before the angel of the Lord, and the Satan at his right hand ready to take up a cause against him.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And the Lord said to the Satan, May the Lord's word be sharp against you, O Satan, the word of the Lord who has taken Jerusalem for himself: is this not a burning branch pulled out of the fire?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Now Joshua was clothed in unclean robes, and he was in his place before the angel.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And he made answer and said to those who were there before him, Take the unclean robes off him, and let him be clothed in clean robes;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And let them put a clean head-dress on his head. So they put a clean head-dress on his head, clothing him with clean robes: and to him he said, See, I have taken your sin away from you.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And the angel of the Lord made a statement to Joshua, and said,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** These are the words of the Lord of armies: If you will go in my ways and keep what I have put in your care, then you will be judge over my Temple and have the care of my house, and I will give you the right to come in among those who are there.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Give ear now, O Joshua, the high priest, you and your friends who are seated before you; for these are men who are a sign: for see, I will let my servant the Branch be seen.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For see, the stone which I have put before Joshua; on one stone are seven eyes: see, the design cut on it will be my work, says the Lord of armies, and I will take away the sin of that land in one day.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** In that day, says the Lord of armies, you will be one another's guests under the vine and under the fig-tree.
