# Zechariah 2 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I lifted up my eyes, and saw, and behold, a man with a measuring line in his hand.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Then I asked, “Where are you going?”   He said to me, “To measure Jerusalem, to see what is its breadth and what is its length.”

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Behold, the angel who talked with me went out, and another angel went out to meet him,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** and said to him, “Run, speak to this young man, saying, ‘Jerusalem will be inhabited as villages without walls, because of the multitude of men and livestock in it.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For I,’ says Yahweh, ‘will be to her a wall of fire around it, and I will be the glory in the midst of her.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Come! Come! Flee from the land of the north,’ says Yahweh; ‘for I have spread you abroad as the four winds of the sky,’ says Yahweh.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** ‘Come, Zion! Escape, you who dwell with the daughter of Babylon.’

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For thus says Yahweh of Armies: ‘For honor he has sent me to the nations which plundered you; for he who touches you touches the apple of his eye.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For, behold, I will shake my hand over them, and they will be a plunder to those who served them; and you will know that Yahweh of Armies has sent me.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Sing and rejoice, daughter of Zion; for, behold, I come, and I will dwell in your midst,’ says Yahweh.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Many nations shall join themselves to Yahweh in that day, and shall be my people; and I will dwell in your midst, and you shall know that Yahweh of Armies has sent me to you.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yahweh will inherit Judah as his portion in the holy land, and will again choose Jerusalem.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Be silent, all flesh, before Yahweh; for he has roused himself from his holy habitation!”
