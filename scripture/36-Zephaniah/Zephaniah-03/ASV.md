# Zephaniah 3 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Woe to her that is rebellious and polluted! to the oppressing city!

<a id="v2"></a>**2** She obeyed not the voice; she received not correction; she trusted not in Jehovah; she drew not near to her God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Her princes in the midst of her are roaring lions; her judges are evening wolves; they leave nothing till the morrow.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Her prophets are light and treacherous persons; her priests have profaned the sanctuary, they have done violence to the law.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Jehovah in the midst of her is righteous; he will not do iniquity; every morning doth he bring his justice to light, he faileth not; but the unjust knoweth no shame.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I have cut off nations; their battlements are desolate; I have made their streets waste, so that none passeth by; their cities are destroyed, so that there is no man, so that there is no inhabitant.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I said, Only fear thou me; receive correction; so her dwelling shall not be cut off, according to all that I have appointed concerning her: but they rose early and corrupted all their doings.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Therefore wait ye for me, saith Jehovah, until the day that I rise up to the prey; for my determination is to gather the nations, that I may assemble the kingdoms, to pour upon them mine indignation, even all my fierce anger; for all the earth shall be devoured with the fire of my jealousy.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For then will I turn to the peoples of a pure language, that they may all call upon the name of Jehovah, to serve him with one consent.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** From beyond the rivers of Ethiopia my suppliants, even the daughter of my dispersed, shall bring mine offering.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** In that day shalt thou not be put to shame for all thy doings, wherein thou hast transgressed against me; for then I will take away out of the midst of thee thy proudly exulting ones, and thou shalt no more be haughty in my holy mountain.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But I will leave in the midst of thee an afflicted and poor people, and they shall take refuge in the name of Jehovah.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The remnant of Israel shall not do iniquity, nor speak lies; neither shall a deceitful tongue be found in their mouth; for they shall feed and lie down, and none shall make them afraid.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Sing, O daughter of Zion; shout, O Israel; be glad and rejoice with all the heart, O daughter of Jerusalem.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Jehovah hath taken away thy judgments, he hath cast out thine enemy: the King of Israel, even Jehovah, is in the midst of thee; thou shalt not fear evil any more.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** In that day it shall be said to Jerusalem, Fear thou not; O Zion, let not thy hands be slack.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Jehovah thy God is in the midst of thee, a mighty one who will save; he will rejoice over thee with joy; he will rest in his love; he will joy over thee with singing.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** I will gather them that sorrow for the solemn assembly, who were of thee; to whom the burden upon her was a reproach.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Behold, at that time I will deal with all them that afflict thee; and I will save that which is lame, and gather that which was driven away; and I will make them a praise and a name, whose shame hath been in all the earth.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** At that time will I bring you in, and at that time will I gather you; for I will make you a name and a praise among all the peoples of the earth, when I bring back your captivity before your eyes, saith Jehovah.
