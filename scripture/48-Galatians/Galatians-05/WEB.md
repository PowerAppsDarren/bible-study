# Galatians 5 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Stand firm therefore in the liberty by which Christ has made us free, and don’t be entangled again with a yoke of bondage.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Behold, I, Paul, tell you that if you receive circumcision, Christ will profit you nothing.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yes, I testify again to every man who receives circumcision, that he is a debtor to do the whole law.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You are alienated from Christ, you who desire to be justified by the law. You have fallen away from grace.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For we, through the Spirit, by faith wait for the hope of righteousness.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For in Christ Jesus neither circumcision amounts to anything, nor uncircumcision, but faith working through love.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You were running well! Who interfered with you that you should not obey the truth?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** This persuasion is not from him who calls you.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** A little yeast grows through the whole lump.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I have confidence toward you in the Lord that you will think no other way. But he who troubles you will bear his judgment, whoever he is.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But I, brothers, if I still preach circumcision, why am I still persecuted? Then the stumbling block of the cross has been removed.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I wish that those who disturb you would cut themselves off.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For you, brothers, were called for freedom. Only don’t use your freedom for gain to the flesh, but through love be servants to one another.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For the whole law is fulfilled in one word, in this: “You shall love your neighbor as yourself.”

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But if you bite and devour one another, be careful that you don’t consume one another.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** But I say, walk by the Spirit, and you won’t fulfill the lust of the flesh.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For the flesh lusts against the Spirit, and the Spirit against the flesh; and these are contrary to one another, that you may not do the things that you desire.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But if you are led by the Spirit, you are not under the law.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Now the works of the flesh are obvious, which are: adultery, sexual immorality, uncleanness, lustfulness,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** idolatry, sorcery, hatred, strife, jealousies, outbursts of anger, rivalries, divisions, heresies,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** envyings, murders, drunkenness, orgies, and things like these; of which I forewarn you, even as I also forewarned you, that those who practice such things will not inherit the Kingdom of God.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faith,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** gentleness, and self-control. Against such things there is no law.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Those who belong to Christ have crucified the flesh with its passions and lusts.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** If we live by the Spirit, let’s also walk by the Spirit.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Let’s not become conceited, provoking one another, and envying one another.
