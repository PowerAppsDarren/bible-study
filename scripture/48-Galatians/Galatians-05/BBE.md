# Galatians 5 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Christ has truly made us free: then keep your free condition and let no man put a yoke on you again.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** See, I Paul say to you, that if you undergo circumcision, Christ will be of no use to you.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yes, I give witness again to every man who undergoes circumcision, that he will have to keep all the law.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You are cut off from Christ, you who would have righteousness by the law; you are turned away from grace.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For we through the Spirit by faith are waiting for the hope of righteousness.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Because in Christ Jesus, having circumcision or not having circumcision are equally of no profit; but only faith working through love.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** You were going on well; who was the cause of your not giving ear to what is true?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** This ready belief did not come from him who had made you his.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** A little leaven makes a change in all the mass.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I am certain about you in the Lord, that you will be of no other mind; but he who is troubling you will have his punishment, whoever he is.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But I, brothers, if I am still preaching circumcision, why am I still attacked? then has the shame of the cross been taken away.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** My desire is that they who give you trouble might even be cut off themselves.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Because you, brothers, were marked out to be free; only do not make use of your free condition to give the flesh its chance, but through love be servants one to another.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For all the law is made complete in one word, even in this, Have love for your neighbour as for yourself.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But if you are given to fighting with one another, take care that you are not the cause of destruction one to another.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** But I say, Go on in the Spirit, and you will not come under the rule of the evil desires of the flesh.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For the flesh has desires against the Spirit, and the Spirit against the flesh; because these are opposite the one to the other; so that you may not do the things which you have a mind to do.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But if you are guided by the Spirit, you are not under the law.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Now the works of the flesh are clear, which are these: evil desire, unclean things, wrong use of the senses,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Worship of images, use of strange powers, hates, fighting, desire for what another has, angry feelings, attempts to get the better of others, divisions, false teachings,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Envy, uncontrolled drinking and feasting, and such things: of which I give you word clearly, even as I did in the past, that they who do such things will have no part in the kingdom of God.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, a quiet mind, kind acts, well-doing, faith,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Gentle behaviour, control over desires: against such there is no law.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And those who are Christ's have put to death on the cross the flesh with its passions and its evil desires.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** If we are living by the Spirit, by the Spirit let us be guided.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Let us not be full of self-glory, making one another angry, having envy of one another.
