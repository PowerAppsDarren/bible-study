# Galatians 5 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Stand fast therefore in the liberty wherewith Christ hath made us free, and be not entangled again with the yoke of bondage.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Behold, I Paul say unto you, that if ye be circumcised, Christ shall profit you nothing.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For I testify again to every man that is circumcised, that he is a debtor to do the whole law.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Christ is become of no effect unto you, whosoever of you are justified by the law; ye are fallen from grace.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For we through the Spirit wait for the hope of righteousness by faith.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For in Jesus Christ neither circumcision availeth any thing, nor uncircumcision; but faith which worketh by love.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Ye did run well; who did hinder you that ye should not obey the truth?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** This persuasion cometh not of him that calleth you.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** A little leaven leaveneth the whole lump.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I have confidence in you through the Lord, that ye will be none otherwise minded: but he that troubleth you shall bear his judgment, whosoever he be.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And I, brethren, if I yet preach circumcision, why do I yet suffer persecution? then is the offence of the cross ceased.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I would they were even cut off which trouble you.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For, brethren, ye have been called unto liberty; only use not liberty for an occasion to the flesh, but by love serve one another.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For all the law is fulfilled in one word, even in this; Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But if ye bite and devour one another, take heed that ye be not consumed one of another.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** This I say then, Walk in the Spirit, and ye shall not fulfil the lust of the flesh.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For the flesh lusteth against the Spirit, and the Spirit against the flesh: and these are contrary the one to the other: so that ye cannot do the things that ye would.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But if ye be led of the Spirit, ye are not under the law.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Now the works of the flesh are manifest, which are these; Adultery, fornication, uncleanness, lasciviousness,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Idolatry, witchcraft, hatred, variance, emulations, wrath, strife, seditions, heresies,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Envyings, murders, drunkenness, revellings, and such like: of the which I tell you before, as I have also told you in time past, that they which do such things shall not inherit the kingdom of God.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, longsuffering, gentleness, goodness, faith,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Meekness, temperance: against such there is no law.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And they that are Christ’s have crucified the flesh with the affections and lusts.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** If we live in the Spirit, let us also walk in the Spirit.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Let us not be desirous of vain glory, provoking one another, envying one another.
