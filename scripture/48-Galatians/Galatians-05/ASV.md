# Galatians 5 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** For freedom did Christ set us free: stand fast therefore, and be not entangled again in a yoke of bondage.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Behold, I Paul say unto you, that, if ye receive circumcision, Christ will profit you nothing.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yea, I testify again to every man that receiveth circumcision, that he is a debtor to do the whole law.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Ye are severed from Christ, ye who would be justified by the law; ye are fallen away from grace.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For we through the Spirit by faith wait for the hope of righteousness.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For in Christ Jesus neither circumcision availeth anything, nor uncircumcision; but faith working through love.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Ye were running well; who hindered you that ye should not obey the truth?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** This persuasion came not of him that calleth you.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** A little leaven leaveneth the whole lump.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** I have confidence to you-ward in the Lord, that ye will be none otherwise minded: but he that troubleth you shall bear his judgment, whosoever he be.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But I, brethren, if I still preach circumcision, why am I still persecuted? then hath the stumbling-block of the cross been done away.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I would that they that unsettle you would even go beyond circumcision.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For ye, brethren, were called for freedom; only use not your freedom for an occasion to the flesh, but through love be servants one to another.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For the whole law is fulfilled in one word, even in this: Thou shalt love thy neighbor as thyself.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But if ye bite and devour one another, take heed that ye be not consumed one of another.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** But I say, Walk by the Spirit, and ye shall not fulfil the lust of the flesh.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For the flesh lusteth against the Spirit, and the Spirit against the flesh; for these are contrary the one to the other; that ye may not do the things that ye would.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But if ye are led by the Spirit, ye are not under the law.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Now the works of the flesh are manifest, which are these: fornication, uncleanness, lasciviousness,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** idolatry, sorcery, enmities, strife, jealousies, wraths, factions, divisions, parties,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** envyings, drunkenness, revellings, and such like; of which I forewarn you, even as I did forewarn you, that they who practise such things shall not inherit the kingdom of God.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, longsuffering, kindness, goodness, faithfulness,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** meekness, self-control; against such there is no law.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And they that are of Christ Jesus have crucified the flesh with the passions and the lusts thereof.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** If we live by the Spirit, by the Spirit let us also walk.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Let us not become vainglorious, provoking one another, envying one another.
