# Galatians 1 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Paul, an apostle (not from men, neither through man, but through Jesus Christ, and God the Father, who raised him from the dead),

<a id="v2"></a>**2** and all the brethren that are with me, unto the churches of Galatia:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Grace to you and peace from God the Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** who gave himself for our sins, that he might deliver us out of this present evil world, according to the will of our God and Father:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** to whom be the glory for ever and ever. Amen.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I marvel that ye are so quickly removing from him that called you in the grace of Christ unto a different gospel;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** which is not another gospel: only there are some that trouble you, and would pervert the gospel of Christ.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But though we, or an angel from heaven, should preach unto you any gospel other than that which we preached unto you, let him be anathema.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** As we have said before, so say I now again, If any man preacheth unto you any gospel other than that which ye received, let him be anathema.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For am I now seeking the favor of men, or of God? or am I striving to please men? if I were still pleasing men, I should not be a servant of Christ.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For I make known to you, brethren, as touching the gospel which was preached by me, that it is not after man.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For neither did I receive it from man, nor was I taught it, but it came to me through revelation of Jesus Christ.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For ye have heard of my manner of life in time past in the Jews’ religion, how that beyond measure I persecuted the church of God, and made havoc of it:

<a id="v14"></a>**14** and I advanced in the Jews’ religion beyond many of mine own age among my countrymen, being more exceedingly zealous for the traditions of my fathers.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But when it was the good pleasure of God, who separated me, even from my mother’s womb, and called me through his grace,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** to reveal his Son in me, that I might preach him among the Gentiles; straightway I conferred not with flesh and blood:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** neither went I up to Jerusalem to them that were apostles before me: but I went away into Arabia; and again I returned unto Damascus.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Then after three years I went up to Jerusalem to visit Cephas, and tarried with him fifteen days.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** But other of the apostles saw I none, save James the Lord’s brother.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Now touching the things which I write unto you, behold, before God, I lie not.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Then I came into the regions of Syria and Cilicia.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And I was still unknown by face unto the churches of Judæa which were in Christ:

<a id="v23"></a>**23** but they only heard say, He that once persecuted us now preacheth the faith of which he once made havoc;

<a id="v24"></a>**24** and they glorified God in me.
