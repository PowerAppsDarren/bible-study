# Galatians 1 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Paul, an Apostle (not from men, and not through man, but through Jesus Christ, and God the Father, who made him come back from the dead),

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And all the brothers who are with me, to the churches of Galatia:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Grace to you and peace from God the Father and our Lord Jesus Christ,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Who gave himself for our sins, so that he might make us free from this present evil world, after the purpose of our God and Father:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** To whom be the glory for ever and ever. So be it.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I am surprised that you are being so quickly turned away from him whose word came to you in the grace of Christ, to good news of a different sort;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Which is not another sort: only there are some who give you trouble, desiring to make changes in the good news of Christ.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But even if we, or an angel from heaven, were to be a preacher to you of good news other than that which we have given you, let there be a curse on him.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** As we have said before, so say I now again, If any man is a preacher to you of any good news other than that which has been given to you, let there be a curse on him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Am I now using arguments to men, or God? or is it my desire to give men pleasure? if I was still pleasing men, I would not be a servant of Christ.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Because I say to you, my brothers, that the good news of which I was the preacher is not man's.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For I did not get it from man, and I was not given teaching in it, but it came to me through revelation of Jesus Christ.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For news has come to you of my way of life in the past in the Jews' religion, how I was cruel without measure to the church of God, and did great damage to it:

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And I went farther in the Jews' religion than a number of my generation among my countrymen, having a more burning interest in the beliefs handed down from my fathers.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But when it was the good pleasure of God, by whom I was marked out even from my mother's body, through his grace,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** To give the revelation of his Son in me, so that I might give the news of him to the Gentiles; then I did not take the opinion of flesh and blood,

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And I went not up to Jerusalem to those who were Apostles before me; but I went away into Arabia, and again I came back to Damascus.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Then after three years I went up to Jerusalem to see Cephas, and was there with him fifteen days.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** But of the other Apostles I saw only James, the Lord's brother.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Now God is witness that the things which I am writing to you are true.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Then I came to the parts of Syria and Cilicia.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And the churches of Judaea which were in Christ still had no knowledge of my face or person:

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Only it came to their ears that he who at one time was cruel to us is now preaching the faith which before had been attacked by him;

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And they gave glory to God in me.
