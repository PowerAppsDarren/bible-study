# Galatians 1 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Paul, an apostle (not from men, neither through man, but through Jesus Christ, and God the Father, who raised him from the dead),

<a id="v2"></a>**2** and all the brothers who are with me, to the assemblies of Galatia:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Grace to you and peace from God the Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** who gave himself for our sins, that he might deliver us out of this present evil age, according to the will of our God and Father—

<a id="v5"></a>**5** to whom be the glory forever and ever. Amen.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I marvel that you are so quickly deserting him who called you in the grace of Christ to a different “good news”;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** and there isn’t another “good news.” Only there are some who trouble you, and want to pervert the Good News of Christ.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But even though we, or an angel from heaven, should preach to you any “good news” other than that which we preached to you, let him be cursed.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** As we have said before, so I now say again: if any man preaches to you any “good news” other than that which you received, let him be cursed.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For am I now seeking the favor of men, or of God? Or am I striving to please men? For if I were still pleasing men, I wouldn’t be a servant of Christ.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But I make known to you, brothers, concerning the Good News which was preached by me, that it is not according to man.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For neither did I receive it from man, nor was I taught it, but it came to me through revelation of Jesus Christ.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For you have heard of my way of living in time past in the Jews’ religion, how that beyond measure I persecuted the assembly of God, and ravaged it.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I advanced in the Jews’ religion beyond many of my own age among my countrymen, being more exceedingly zealous for the traditions of my fathers.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But when it was the good pleasure of God, who separated me from my mother’s womb, and called me through his grace,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** to reveal his Son in me, that I might preach him among the Gentiles, I didn’t immediately confer with flesh and blood,

<a id="v17"></a>**17** nor did I go up to Jerusalem to those who were apostles before me, but I went away into Arabia. Then I returned to Damascus.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Then after three years I went up to Jerusalem to visit Peter, and stayed with him fifteen days.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** But of the other apostles I saw no one, except James, the Lord’s brother.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Now about the things which I write to you, behold, before God, I’m not lying.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Then I came to the regions of Syria and Cilicia.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** I was still unknown by face to the assemblies of Judea which were in Christ,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** but they only heard: “He who once persecuted us now preaches the faith that he once tried to destroy.”

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And they glorified God in me.
