# Galatians 1 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Paul, an apostle, (not of men, neither by man, but by Jesus Christ, and God the Father, who raised him from the dead;)

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And all the brethren which are with me, unto the churches of Galatia:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Grace be to you and peace from God the Father, and from our Lord Jesus Christ,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Who gave himself for our sins, that he might deliver us from this present evil world, according to the will of God and our Father:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** To whom be glory for ever and ever. Amen.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I marvel that ye are so soon removed from him that called you into the grace of Christ unto another gospel:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Which is not another; but there be some that trouble you, and would pervert the gospel of Christ.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But though we, or an angel from heaven, preach any other gospel unto you than that which we have preached unto you, let him be accursed.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** As we said before, so say I now again, If any man preach any other gospel unto you than that ye have received, let him be accursed.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For do I now persuade men, or God? or do I seek to please men? for if I yet pleased men, I should not be the servant of Christ.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** But I certify you, brethren, that the gospel which was preached of me is not after man.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For I neither received it of man, neither was I taught it, but by the revelation of Jesus Christ.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For ye have heard of my conversation in time past in the Jews’ religion, how that beyond measure I persecuted the church of God, and wasted it:

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And profited in the Jews’ religion above many my equals in mine own nation, being more exceedingly zealous of the traditions of my fathers.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But when it pleased God, who separated me from my mother’s womb, and called me by his grace,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** To reveal his Son in me, that I might preach him among the heathen; immediately I conferred not with flesh and blood:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Neither went I up to Jerusalem to them which were apostles before me; but I went into Arabia, and returned again unto Damascus.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Then after three years I went up to Jerusalem to see Peter, and abode with him fifteen days.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** But other of the apostles saw I none, save James the Lord’s brother.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Now the things which I write unto you, behold, before God, I lie not.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Afterwards I came into the regions of Syria and Cilicia;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And was unknown by face unto the churches of Judea which were in Christ:

<a id="v23"></a>**23** But they had heard only, That he which persecuted us in times past now preacheth the faith which once he destroyed.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And they glorified God in me.
