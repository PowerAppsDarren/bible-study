# Galatians 6 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Brothers, even if a man is caught in some fault, you who are spiritual must restore such a one in a spirit of gentleness; looking to yourself so that you also aren’t tempted.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Bear one another’s burdens, and so fulfill the law of Christ.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For if a man thinks himself to be something when he is nothing, he deceives himself.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But let each man test his own work, and then he will take pride in himself and not in his neighbor.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For each man will bear his own burden.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** But let him who is taught in the word share all good things with him who teaches.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Don’t be deceived. God is not mocked, for whatever a man sows, that he will also reap.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For he who sows to his own flesh will from the flesh reap corruption. But he who sows to the Spirit will from the Spirit reap eternal life.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let us not be weary in doing good, for we will reap in due season, if we don’t give up.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** So then, as we have opportunity, let’s do what is good toward all men, and especially toward those who are of the household of the faith.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** See with what large letters I write to you with my own hand.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** As many as desire to look good in the flesh, they compel you to be circumcised; only that they may not be persecuted for the cross of Christ.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For even they who receive circumcision don’t keep the law themselves, but they desire to have you circumcised, that they may boast in your flesh.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But far be it from me to boast, except in the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ, through which the world has been crucified to me, and I to the world.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For in Christ Jesus neither is circumcision anything, nor uncircumcision, but a new creation.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** As many as walk by this rule, peace and mercy be on them, and on God’s Israel.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** From now on, let no one cause me any trouble, for I bear the marks of the Lord Jesus branded on my body.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit, brothers. Amen.
