# Galatians 6 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Brethren, if a man be overtaken in a fault, ye which are spiritual, restore such an one in the spirit of meekness; considering thyself, lest thou also be tempted.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Bear ye one another’s burdens, and so fulfil the law of Christ.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For if a man think himself to be something, when he is nothing, he deceiveth himself.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But let every man prove his own work, and then shall he have rejoicing in himself alone, and not in another.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For every man shall bear his own burden.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Let him that is taught in the word communicate unto him that teacheth in all good things.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Be not deceived; God is not mocked: for whatsoever a man soweth, that shall he also reap.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For he that soweth to his flesh shall of the flesh reap corruption; but he that soweth to the Spirit shall of the Spirit reap life everlasting.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And let us not be weary in well doing: for in due season we shall reap, if we faint not.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** As we have therefore opportunity, let us do good unto all men, especially unto them who are of the household of faith.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Ye see how large a letter I have written unto you with mine own hand.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** As many as desire to make a fair shew in the flesh, they constrain you to be circumcised; only lest they should suffer persecution for the cross of Christ.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For neither they themselves who are circumcised keep the law; but desire to have you circumcised, that they may glory in your flesh.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But God forbid that I should glory, save in the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ, by whom the world is crucified unto me, and I unto the world.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For in Christ Jesus neither circumcision availeth any thing, nor uncircumcision, but a new creature.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And as many as walk according to this rule, peace be on them, and mercy, and upon the Israel of God.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** From henceforth let no man trouble me: for I bear in my body the marks of the Lord Jesus.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Brethren, the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit. Amen.  Unto the Galatians written from Rome.
