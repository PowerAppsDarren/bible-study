# Galatians 6 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Brethren, even if a man be overtaken in any trespass, ye who are spiritual, restore such a one in a spirit of gentleness; looking to thyself, lest thou also be tempted.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Bear ye one another’s burdens, and so fulfil the law of Christ.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For if a man thinketh himself to be something when he is nothing, he deceiveth himself.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But let each man prove his own work, and then shall he have his glorying in regard of himself alone, and not of his neighbor.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For each man shall bear his own burden.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** But let him that is taught in the word communicate unto him that teacheth in all good things.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Be not deceived; God is not mocked: for whatsoever a man soweth, that shall he also reap.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For he that soweth unto his own flesh shall of the flesh reap corruption; but he that soweth unto the Spirit shall of the Spirit reap eternal life.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And let us not be weary in well-doing: for in due season we shall reap, if we faint not.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** So then, as we have opportunity, let us work that which is good toward all men, and especially toward them that are of the household of the faith.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** See with how large letters I write unto you with mine own hand.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** As many as desire to make a fair show in the flesh, they compel you to be circumcised; only that they may not be persecuted for the cross of Christ.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** For not even they who receive circumcision do themselves keep the law; but they desire to have you circumcised, that they may glory in your flesh.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But far be it from me to glory, save in the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ, through which the world hath been crucified unto me, and I unto the world.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For neither is circumcision anything, nor uncircumcision, but a new creature.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And as many as shall walk by this rule, peace be upon them, and mercy, and upon the Israel of God.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Henceforth let no man trouble me; for I bear branded on my body the marks of Jesus.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit, brethren. Amen.
