# Isaiah 3 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** For the Lord, the Lord of armies, is about to take away from Jerusalem and from Judah all their support; their store of bread and of water;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The strong man and the man of war; the judge and the prophet; the man who has knowledge of secret arts, and the man who is wise because of his years;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The captain of fifty, and the man of high position, and the wise guide, and the wonder-worker, and he who makes use of secret powers.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And I will make children their chiefs, and foolish ones will have rule over them.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And the people will be crushed, every one by his neighbour; the young will be full of pride against the old, and those of low position will be lifted up against the noble.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** When one man puts his hand on another in his father's house, and says, You have clothing, be our ruler and be responsible for us in our sad condition:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Then he will say with an oath, I will not be a helper, for in my house there is no bread or clothing: I will not let you make me a ruler of the people.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For Jerusalem has become feeble, and destruction has come on Judah, because their words and their acts are against the Lord, moving the eyes of his glory to wrath.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Their respect for a man's position is a witness against them; and their sin is open to the view of all; like that of Sodom, it is not covered. A curse on their soul! for the measure of their sin is full.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Happy is the upright man! for he will have joy of the fruit of his ways.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Unhappy is the sinner! for the reward of his evil doings will come on him.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** As for my people, their ruler is acting like a child, and those who have authority over them are women. O my people, your guides are the cause of your wandering, turning your footsteps out of the right way.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The Lord is ready to take up his cause against his people, and is about to come forward as their judge.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The Lord comes to be the judge of their responsible men and of their rulers: it is you who have made waste the vine-garden, and in your houses is the property of the poor which you have taken by force.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** By what right are you crushing my people, and putting a bitter yoke on the necks of the poor? This is the word of the Lord, the Lord of armies.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Again, the Lord has said, Because the daughters of Zion are full of pride, and go with outstretched necks and wandering eyes, with their foot-chains sounding when they go:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The Lord will send disease on the heads of the daughters of Zion, and the Lord will let their secret parts be seen.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** In that day the Lord will take away the glory of their foot-rings, and their sun-jewels, and their moon-ornaments,

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The ear-rings, and the chains, and the delicate clothing,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The head-bands, and the arm-chains, and the worked bands, and the perfume-boxes, and the jewels with secret powers,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The rings, and the nose-jewels,

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The feast-day dresses, and the robes, and the wide skirts, and the handbags,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** The looking-glasses, and the fair linen, and the high head-dresses, and the veils.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And in the place of sweet spices will be an evil smell, and for a fair band a thick cord; for a well-dressed head there will be the cutting-off of the hair, and for a beautiful robe there will be the clothing of sorrow; the mark of the prisoner in place of the ornaments of the free.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Your men will be put to the sword, and your men of war will come to destruction in the fight.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And in the public places of her towns will be sorrow and weeping; and she will be seated on the earth, waste and uncovered.
