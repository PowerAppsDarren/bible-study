# Isaiah 26 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In that day shall this song be sung in the land of Judah: We have a strong city; salvation will he appoint for walls and bulwarks.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Open ye the gates, that the righteous nation which keepeth faith may enter in.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thou wilt keep him in perfect peace, whose mind is stayed on thee; because he trusteth in thee.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Trust ye in Jehovah for ever; for in Jehovah, even Jehovah, is an everlasting rock.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For he hath brought down them that dwell on high, the lofty city: he layeth it low, he layeth it low even to the ground; he bringeth it even to the dust.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The foot shall tread it down; even the feet of the poor, and the steps of the needy.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The way of the just is uprightness: thou that art upright dost direct the path of the just.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yea, in the way of thy judgments, O Jehovah, have we waited for thee; to thy name, even to thy memorial name, is the desire of our soul.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** With my soul have I desired thee in the night; yea, with my spirit within me will I seek thee earnestly: for when thy judgments are in the earth, the inhabitants of the world learn righteousness.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Let favor be showed to the wicked, yet will he not learn righteousness; in the land of uprightness will he deal wrongfully, and will not behold the majesty of Jehovah.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Jehovah, thy hand is lifted up, yet they see not: but they shall seethyzeal for the people, and be put to shame; yea, fire shall devour thine adversaries.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Jehovah, thou wilt ordain peace for us; for thou hast also wrought all our works for us.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** O Jehovah our God, other lords besides thee have had dominion over us; but by thee only will we make mention of thy name.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** They are dead, they shall not live; they are deceased, they shall not rise: therefore hast thou visited and destroyed them, and made all remembrance of them to perish.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Thou hast increased the nation, O Jehovah, thou hast increased the nation; thou art glorified; thou hast enlarged all the borders of the land.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Jehovah, in trouble have they visited thee; they poured out a prayer when thy chastening was upon them.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Like as a woman with child, that draweth near the time of her delivery, is in pain and crieth out in her pangs; so we have been before thee, O Jehovah.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** We have been with child, we have been in pain, we have as it were brought forth wind; we have not wrought any deliverance in the earth; neither have the inhabitants of the world fallen.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Thy dead shall live; my dead bodies shall arise. Awake and sing, ye that dwell in the dust; for thy dew is as the dew of herbs, and the earth shall cast forth the dead.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Come, my people, enter thou into thy chambers, and shut thy doors about thee: hide thyself for a little moment, until the indignation be overpast.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For, behold, Jehovah cometh forth out of his place to punish the inhabitants of the earth for their iniquity: the earth also shall disclose her blood, and shall no more cover her slain.
