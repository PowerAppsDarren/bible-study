# Isaiah 26 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In that day shall this song be sung in the land of Judah; We have a strong city; salvation will God appoint for walls and bulwarks.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Open ye the gates, that the righteous nation which keepeth the truth may enter in.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thou wilt keep him in perfect peace, whose mind is stayed on thee: because he trusteth in thee.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Trust ye in the Lord for ever: for in the Lord Jehovah is everlasting strength:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For he bringeth down them that dwell on high; the lofty city, he layeth it low; he layeth it low, even to the ground; he bringeth it even to the dust.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The foot shall tread it down, even the feet of the poor, and the steps of the needy.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The way of the just is uprightness: thou, most upright, dost weigh the path of the just.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yea, in the way of thy judgments, O Lord, have we waited for thee; the desire of our soul is to thy name, and to the remembrance of thee.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** With my soul have I desired thee in the night; yea, with my spirit within me will I seek thee early: for when thy judgments are in the earth, the inhabitants of the world will learn righteousness.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Let favour be shewed to the wicked, yet will he not learn righteousness: in the land of uprightness will he deal unjustly, and will not behold the majesty of the Lord.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Lord, when thy hand is lifted up, they will not see: but they shall see, and be ashamed for their envy at the people; yea, the fire of thine enemies shall devour them.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Lord, thou wilt ordain peace for us: for thou also hast wrought all our works in us.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** O Lord our God, other lords beside thee have had dominion over us: but by thee only will we make mention of thy name.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** They are dead, they shall not live; they are deceased, they shall not rise: therefore hast thou visited and destroyed them, and made all their memory to perish.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Thou hast increased the nation, O Lord, thou hast increased the nation: thou art glorified: thou hadst removed it far unto all the ends of the earth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Lord, in trouble have they visited thee, they poured out a prayer when thy chastening was upon them.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Like as a woman with child, that draweth near the time of her delivery, is in pain, and crieth out in her pangs; so have we been in thy sight, O Lord.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** We have been with child, we have been in pain, we have as it were brought forth wind; we have not wrought any deliverance in the earth; neither have the inhabitants of the world fallen.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Thy dead men shall live, together with my dead body shall they arise. Awake and sing, ye that dwell in dust: for thy dew is as the dew of herbs, and the earth shall cast out the dead.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Come, my people, enter thou into thy chambers, and shut thy doors about thee: hide thyself as it were for a little moment, until the indignation be overpast.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For, behold, the Lord cometh out of his place to punish the inhabitants of the earth for their iniquity: the earth also shall disclose her blood, and shall no more cover her slain.
