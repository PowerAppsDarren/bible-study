# Isaiah 26 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In that day will this song be made in the land of Judah: We have a strong town; he will make salvation our walls and towers.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Let the doors be open, so that the upright nation which keeps faith may come in.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The man whose heart is unmoved you will keep in peace, because his hope is in you.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Let your hope be in the Lord for ever: for the Lord Jah is an unchanging Rock.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For he has made low those who are lifted up, all the people of the town of pride: he makes it low, crushing it down to the earth; he makes it low in the dust.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** It will be crushed under the feet of the poor and the steps of those who are in need.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The way of the good man is straight; the road of the upright is made smooth by you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** We have been waiting for you, O Lord; the desire of our soul is for the memory of your name.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** In the night the desire of my soul has been for you; early will my spirit be searching for you; for when your punishments come on the earth, the people of the world will get the knowledge of righteousness.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Even if you are kind to the evil-doer, he will not go after righteousness; even in the land of the upright he will still go on in his wrongdoing, and will not see the glory of the Lord.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Lord, your hand is lifted up, but they do not see: let them see ... yes, your haters will be burned up in the fire.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Lord, you will give us peace: for all our works are the outcome of your purpose.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** O Lord, our God, other lords than you have had rule over us; but in you only is our salvation, and no other name will we take on our lips.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The dead will not come back to life: their spirits will not come back to earth; for this cause you have sent destruction on them, so that the memory of them is dead.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** You have made the nation great, O Lord, you have made it great; glory is yours: you have made wide the limits of the land.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Lord, in trouble our eyes have been turned to you, we sent up a prayer when your punishment was on us.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** As a woman with child, whose time is near, is troubled, crying out in her pain; so have we been before you, O Lord.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** We have been with child, we have been in pain, we have given birth to wind; no salvation has come to the earth through us, and no children have come into the world.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Your dead will come back; their dead bodies will come to life again. Those in the dust, awaking from their sleep, will send out a song; for your dew is a dew of light, and the earth will give birth to the shades.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Come, my people, into your secret places, and let your doors be shut: keep yourself safe for a short time, till his wrath is over.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For the Lord is coming out of his place to send punishment on the people of the earth for their evil-doing: the earth will let the blood drained out on her be seen, and will keep her dead covered no longer.
