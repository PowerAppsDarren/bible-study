# Isaiah 62 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** For Zion’s sake will I not hold my peace, and for Jerusalem’s sake I will not rest, until her righteousness go out as brightness, and her salvation as a lamp that burns.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The nations shall see your righteousness, and all kings your glory, and you shall be called by a new name, which the mouth of Yahweh shall name.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You shall also be a crown of beauty in the hand of Yahweh, and a royal diadem in the hand of your God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You shall no more be termed Forsaken; neither shall your land any more be termed Desolate: but you shall be called Hephzibah, and your land Beulah; for Yahweh delights in you, and your land shall be married.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For as a young man marries a virgin, so your sons shall marry you; and as the bridegroom rejoices over the bride, so your God will rejoice over you.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I have set watchmen on your walls, Jerusalem; they shall never hold their peace day nor night: you who call on Yahweh, take no rest,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** and give him no rest, until he establishes, and until he makes Jerusalem a praise in the earth.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Yahweh has sworn by his right hand, and by the arm of his strength, “Surely I will no more give your grain to be food for your enemies; and foreigners shall not drink your new wine, for which you have labored:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** but those who have garnered it shall eat it, and praise Yahweh; and those who have gathered it shall drink it in the courts of my sanctuary.”

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Go through, go through the gates! Prepare the way of the people! Cast up, cast up the highway! Gather out the stones! Lift up a banner for the peoples.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Behold, Yahweh has proclaimed to the end of the earth,  “Say to the daughter of Zion, ‘Behold, your salvation comes. Behold, his reward is with him, and his recompense before him.’”

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They shall call them The holy people, The redeemed of Yahweh: and you shall be called Sought out, A city not forsaken.
