# Isaiah 12 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And in that day thou shalt say, O Lord, I will praise thee: though thou wast angry with me, thine anger is turned away, and thou comfortedst me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Behold, God is my salvation; I will trust, and not be afraid: for the Lord Jehovah is my strength and my song; he also is become my salvation.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Therefore with joy shall ye draw water out of the wells of salvation.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And in that day shall ye say, Praise the Lord, call upon his name, declare his doings among the people, make mention that his name is exalted.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Sing unto the Lord; for he hath done excellent things: this is known in all the earth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Cry out and shout, thou inhabitant of Zion: for great is the Holy One of Israel in the midst of thee.
