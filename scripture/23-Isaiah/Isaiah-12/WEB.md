# Isaiah 12 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** In that day you will say, “I will give thanks to you, Yahweh; for though you were angry with me, your anger has turned away and you comfort me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Behold, God is my salvation. I will trust, and will not be afraid; for Yah, Yahweh, is my strength and song; and he has become my salvation.”

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Therefore with joy you will draw water out of the wells of salvation.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** In that day you will say, “Give thanks to Yahweh! Call on his name. Declare his doings among the peoples. Proclaim that his name is exalted!

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Sing to Yahweh, for he has done excellent things! Let this be known in all the earth!

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Cry aloud and shout, you inhabitant of Zion; for the Holy One of Israel is great in your midst!”
