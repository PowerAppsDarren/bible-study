# Isaiah 15 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The word about Moab. For in a night Ar of Moab has become waste, and is seen no longer; for in a night Kir of Moab has become waste, and is seen no longer.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The daughter of Dibon has gone up to the high places, weeping: Moab is sounding her cry of sorrow over Nebo, and over Medeba: everywhere the hair of the head and of the face is cut off.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** In their streets they are covering themselves with haircloth: on the tops of their houses, and in their public places, there is crying and bitter weeping.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Heshbon is crying out, and Elealeh; their voice is sounding even to Jahaz: for this cause the heart of Moab is shaking; his soul is shaking with fear.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** My heart is crying out for Moab; her people go in flight to Zoar, and to Eglath-shelishiyah: for they go up with weeping by the slope of Luhith; on the way to Horonaim they send up a cry of destruction.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The waters of Nimrim will become dry: for the grass is burned up, the young grass is coming to an end, every green thing is dead.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For this cause they will take away their wealth, and the stores they have got together, over the stream of the water-plants.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For the cry has gone round the limits of Moab; as far as to Eglaim and Beer-elim.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For the waters of Dimon are full of blood: and I'm sending even more on Moab, a lion on those of Moab who go in flight, and on the rest of the land.
