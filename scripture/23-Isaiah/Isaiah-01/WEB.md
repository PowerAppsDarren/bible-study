# Isaiah 1 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The vision of Isaiah the son of Amoz, which he saw concerning Judah and Jerusalem, in the days of Uzziah, Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah, kings of Judah.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Hear, heavens, and listen, earth; for Yahweh has spoken: “I have nourished and brought up children, and they have rebelled against me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The ox knows his owner, and the donkey his master’s crib; but Israel doesn’t know, my people don’t consider.”

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Ah sinful nation, a people loaded with iniquity, a seed of evildoers, children who deal corruptly! They have forsaken Yahweh. They have despised the Holy One of Israel. They are estranged and backward.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Why should you be beaten more, that you revolt more and more? The whole head is sick, and the whole heart faint.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** From the sole of the foot even to the head there is no soundness in it: wounds, welts, and open sores. They haven’t been closed, neither bandaged, neither soothed with oil.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Your country is desolate. Your cities are burned with fire. Strangers devour your land in your presence, and it is desolate, as overthrown by strangers.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The daughter of Zion is left like a shelter in a vineyard, like a hut in a field of melons, like a besieged city.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Unless Yahweh of Armies had left to us a very small remnant, we would have been as Sodom; we would have been like Gomorrah.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Hear Yahweh’s word, you rulers of Sodom! Listen to the law of our God, you people of Gomorrah!

<a id="v11"></a>**11** “What are the multitude of your sacrifices to me?”, says Yahweh. “I have had enough of the burnt offerings of rams, and the fat of fed animals. I don’t delight in the blood of bulls, or of lambs, or of male goats.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** When you come to appear before me, who has required this at your hand, to trample my courts?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Bring no more vain offerings. Incense is an abomination to me; new moons, Sabbaths, and convocations: I can’t bear with evil assemblies.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** My soul hates your New Moons and your appointed feasts. They are a burden to me. I am weary of bearing them.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** When you spread out your hands, I will hide my eyes from you. Yes, when you make many prayers, I will not hear. Your hands are full of blood.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Wash yourselves, make yourself clean. Put away the evil of your doings from before my eyes. Cease to do evil.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Learn to do well. Seek justice. Relieve the oppressed. Judge the fatherless. Plead for the widow.”

<a id="v18"></a>**18** “Come now, and let us reason together,” says Yahweh: “Though your sins be as scarlet, they shall be as white as snow. Though they be red like crimson, they shall be as wool.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** If you are willing and obedient, you shall eat the good of the land;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** but if you refuse and rebel, you shall be devoured with the sword; for the mouth of Yahweh has spoken it.”

<a id="v21"></a>**21** How the faithful city has become a prostitute! She was full of justice; righteousness lodged in her, but now murderers.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Your silver has become dross, your wine mixed with water.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Your princes are rebellious, and companions of thieves. Everyone loves bribes, and follows after rewards. They don’t judge the fatherless, neither does the cause of the widow come to them.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Therefore the Lord, Yahweh of Armies, the Mighty One of Israel, says: “Ah, I will get relief from my adversaries, and avenge myself on my enemies;

<a id="v25"></a>**25** and I will turn my hand on you, thoroughly purge away your dross, and will take away all your tin.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** I will restore your judges as at the first, and your counselors as at the beginning. Afterward you shall be called ‘The city of righteousness, a faithful town.’

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Zion shall be redeemed with justice, and her converts with righteousness.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** But the destruction of transgressors and sinners shall be together, and those who forsake Yahweh shall be consumed.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** For they shall be ashamed of the oaks which you have desired, and you shall be confounded for the gardens that you have chosen.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** For you shall be as an oak whose leaf fades, and as a garden that has no water.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** The strong will be like tinder, and his work like a spark. They will both burn together, and no one will quench them.”
