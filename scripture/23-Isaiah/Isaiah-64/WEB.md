# Isaiah 64 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Oh that you would tear the heavens, that you would come down, that the mountains might quake at your presence,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** as when fire kindles the brushwood, and the fire causes the waters to boil; to make your name known to your adversaries, that the nations may tremble at your presence!

<a id="v3"></a>**3** When you did awesome things which we didn’t look for, you came down, the mountains quaked at your presence.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For from of old men have not heard, nor perceived by the ear, neither has the eye seen a God besides you, who works for him who waits for him.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You meet him who rejoices and works righteousness, those who remember you in your ways. Behold, you were angry, and we sinned. We have been in sin for a long time; and shall we be saved?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For we have all become as one who is unclean, and all our righteousness is as a polluted garment: and we all fade as a leaf; and our iniquities, like the wind, take us away.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** There is no one who calls on your name, who stirs up himself to take hold of you; for you have hidden your face from us, and have consumed us by means of our iniquities.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But now, Yahweh, you are our Father; we are the clay, and you our potter; and we all are the work of your hand.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Don’t be furious, Yahweh, neither remember iniquity forever: see, look, we beg you, we are all your people.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Your holy cities are become a wilderness, Zion has become a wilderness, Jerusalem a desolation.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Our holy and our beautiful house, where our fathers praised you, is burned with fire; and all our pleasant places are laid waste.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Will you refrain yourself for these things, Yahweh? Will you hold your peace, and afflict us very severely?
