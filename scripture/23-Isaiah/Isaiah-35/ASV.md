# Isaiah 35 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The wilderness and the dry land shall be glad; and the desert shall rejoice, and blossom as the rose.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** It shall blossom abundantly, and rejoice even with joy and singing; the glory of Lebanon shall be given unto it, the excellency of Carmel and Sharon: they shall see the glory of Jehovah, the excellency of our God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Strengthen ye the weak hands, and confirm the feeble knees.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Say to them that are of a fearful heart, Be strong, fear not: behold, your God will come with vengeance, with the recompense of God; he will come and save you.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Then the eyes of the blind shall be opened, and the ears of the deaf shall be unstopped.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Then shall the lame man leap as a hart, and the tongue of the dumb shall sing; for in the wilderness shall waters break out, and streams in the desert.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And the glowing sand shall become a pool, and the thirsty ground springs of water: in the habitation of jackals, where they lay, shall be grass with reeds and rushes.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And a highway shall be there, and a way, and it shall be called The way of holiness; the unclean shall not pass over it; but it shall be for the redeemed: the wayfaring men, yea fools, shall not err therein.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** No lion shall be there, nor shall any ravenous beast go up thereon; they shall not be found there; but the redeemed shall walk there:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** and the ransomed of Jehovah shall return, and come with singing unto Zion; and everlasting joy shall be upon their heads: they shall obtain gladness and joy, and sorrow and sighing shall flee away.
