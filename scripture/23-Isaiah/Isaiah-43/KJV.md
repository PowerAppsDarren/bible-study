# Isaiah 43 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** But now thus saith the Lord that created thee, O Jacob, and he that formed thee, O Israel, Fear not: for I have redeemed thee, I have called thee by thy name; thou art mine.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** When thou passest through the waters, I will be with thee; and through the rivers, they shall not overflow thee: when thou walkest through the fire, thou shalt not be burned; neither shall the flame kindle upon thee.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For I am the Lord thy God, the Holy One of Israel, thy Saviour: I gave Egypt for thy ransom, Ethiopia and Seba for thee.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Since thou wast precious in my sight, thou hast been honourable, and I have loved thee: therefore will I give men for thee, and people for thy life.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Fear not: for I am with thee: I will bring thy seed from the east, and gather thee from the west;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I will say to the north, Give up; and to the south, Keep not back: bring my sons from far, and my daughters from the ends of the earth;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Even every one that is called by my name: for I have created him for my glory, I have formed him; yea, I have made him.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Bring forth the blind people that have eyes, and the deaf that have ears.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Let all the nations be gathered together, and let the people be assembled: who among them can declare this, and shew us former things? let them bring forth their witnesses, that they may be justified: or let them hear, and say, It is truth.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Ye are my witnesses, saith the Lord, and my servant whom I have chosen: that ye may know and believe me, and understand that I am he: before me there was no God formed, neither shall there be after me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I, even I, am the Lord; and beside me there is no saviour.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I have declared, and have saved, and I have shewed, when there was no strange god among you: therefore ye are my witnesses, saith the Lord, that I am God.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Yea, before the day was I am he; and there is none that can deliver out of my hand: I will work, and who shall let it?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Thus saith the Lord, your redeemer, the Holy One of Israel; For your sake I have sent to Babylon, and have brought down all their nobles, and the Chaldeans, whose cry is in the ships.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I am the Lord, your Holy One, the creator of Israel, your King.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Thus saith the Lord, which maketh a way in the sea, and a path in the mighty waters;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Which bringeth forth the chariot and horse, the army and the power; they shall lie down together, they shall not rise: they are extinct, they are quenched as tow.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Remember ye not the former things, neither consider the things of old.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Behold, I will do a new thing; now it shall spring forth; shall ye not know it? I will even make a way in the wilderness, and rivers in the desert.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** The beast of the field shall honour me, the dragons and the owls: because I give waters in the wilderness, and rivers in the desert, to give drink to my people, my chosen.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** This people have I formed for myself; they shall shew forth my praise.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** But thou hast not called upon me, O Jacob; but thou hast been weary of me, O Israel.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Thou hast not brought me the small cattle of thy burnt offerings; neither hast thou honoured me with thy sacrifices. I have not caused thee to serve with an offering, nor wearied thee with incense.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Thou hast bought me no sweet cane with money, neither hast thou filled me with the fat of thy sacrifices: but thou hast made me to serve with thy sins, thou hast wearied me with thine iniquities.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** I, even I, am he that blotteth out thy transgressions for mine own sake, and will not remember thy sins.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Put me in remembrance: let us plead together: declare thou, that thou mayest be justified.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Thy first father hath sinned, and thy teachers have transgressed against me.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Therefore I have profaned the princes of the sanctuary, and have given Jacob to the curse, and Israel to reproaches.
