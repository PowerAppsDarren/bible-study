# 1 John 4 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Beloved, believe not every spirit, but prove the spirits, whether they are of God; because many false prophets are gone out into the world.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Hereby know ye the Spirit of God: every spirit that confesseth that Jesus Christ is come in the flesh is of God:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** and every spirit that confesseth not Jesus is not of God: and this is the spirit of the antichrist, whereof ye have heard that it cometh; and now it is in the world already.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Ye are of God, my little children, and have overcome them: because greater is he that is in you than he that is in the world.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They are of the world: therefore speak they as of the world, and the world heareth them.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** We are of God: he that knoweth God heareth us; he who is not of God heareth us not. By this we know the spirit of truth, and the spirit of error.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Beloved, let us love one another: for love is of God; and every one that loveth is begotten of God, and knoweth God.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He that loveth not knoweth not God; for God is love.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Herein was the love of God manifested in us, that God hath sent his only begotten Son into the world that we might live through him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Herein is love, not that we loved God, but that he loved us, and sent his Son to be the propitiation for our sins.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Beloved, if God so loved us, we also ought to love one another.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** No man hath beheld God at any time: if we love one another, God abideth in us, and his love is perfected in us:

<a id="v13"></a>**13** hereby we know that we abide in him and he in us, because he hath given us of his Spirit.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And we have beheld and bear witness that the Father hath sent the Son to be the Saviour of the world.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Whosoever shall confess that Jesus is the Son of God, God abideth in him, and he in God.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And we know and have believed the love which God hath in us. God is love; and he that abideth in love abideth in God, and God abideth in him.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Herein is love made perfect with us, that we may have boldness in the day of judgment; because as he is, even so are we in this world.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** There is no fear in love: but perfect love casteth out fear, because fear hath punishment; and he that feareth is not made perfect in love.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** We love, because he first loved us.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** If a man say, I love God, and hateth his brother, he is a liar: for he that loveth not his brother whom he hath seen, cannot love God whom he hath not seen.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And this commandment have we from him, that he who loveth God love his brother also.
