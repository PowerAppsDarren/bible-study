# 1 John 3 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Behold what manner of love the Father hath bestowed upon us, that we should be called children of God; and such we are. For this cause the world knoweth us not, because it knew him not.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Beloved, now are we children of God, and it is not yet made manifest what we shall be. We know that, if he shall be manifested, we shall be like him; for we shall see him even as he is.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And every one that hath this hope set on him purifieth himself, even as he is pure.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Every one that doeth sin doeth also lawlessness; and sin is lawlessness.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And ye know that he was manifested to take away sins; and in him is no sin.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Whosoever abideth in him sinneth not: whosoever sinneth hath not seen him, neither knoweth him.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** My little children, let no man lead you astray: he that doeth righteousness is righteous, even as he is righteous:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** he that doeth sin is of the devil; for the devil sinneth from the beginning. To this end was the Son of God manifested, that he might destroy the works of the devil.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Whosoever is begotten of God doeth no sin, because his seed abideth in him: and he cannot sin, because he is begotten of God.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** In this the children of God are manifest, and the children of the devil: whosoever doeth not righteousness is not of God, neither he that loveth not his brother.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For this is the message which ye heard from the beginning, that we should love one another:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** not as Cain was of the evil one, and slew his brother. And wherefore slew he him? Because his works were evil, and his brother’s righteous.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Marvel not, brethren, if the world hateth you.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** We know that we have passed out of death into life, because we love the brethren. He that loveth not abideth in death.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Whosoever hateth his brother is a murderer: and ye know that no murderer hath eternal life abiding in him.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Hereby know we love, because he laid down his life for us: and we ought to lay down our lives for the brethren.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** But whoso hath the world’s goods, and beholdeth his brother in need, and shutteth up his compassion from him, how doth the love of God abide in him?

<a id="v18"></a>**18** My little children, let us not love in word, neither with the tongue; but in deed and truth.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Hereby shall we know that we are of the truth, and shall assure our heart before him:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** because if our heart condemn us, God is greater than our heart, and knoweth all things.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Beloved, if our heart condemn us not, we have boldness toward God;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** and whatsoever we ask we receive of him, because we keep his commandments and do the things that are pleasing in his sight.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And this is his commandment, that we should believe in the name of his Son Jesus Christ, and love one another, even as he gave us commandment.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And he that keepeth his commandments abideth in him, and he in him. And hereby we know that he abideth in us, by the Spirit which he gave us.
