# 1 John 3 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Behold, what manner of love the Father hath bestowed upon us, that we should be called the sons of God: therefore the world knoweth us not, because it knew him not.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Beloved, now are we the sons of God, and it doth not yet appear what we shall be: but we know that, when he shall appear, we shall be like him; for we shall see him as he is.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And every man that hath this hope in him purifieth himself, even as he is pure.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Whosoever committeth sin transgresseth also the law: for sin is the transgression of the law.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And ye know that he was manifested to take away our sins; and in him is no sin.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Whosoever abideth in him sinneth not: whosoever sinneth hath not seen him, neither known him.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Little children, let no man deceive you: he that doeth righteousness is righteous, even as he is righteous.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He that committeth sin is of the devil; for the devil sinneth from the beginning. For this purpose the Son of God was manifested, that he might destroy the works of the devil.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Whosoever is born of God doth not commit sin; for his seed remaineth in him: and he cannot sin, because he is born of God.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** In this the children of God are manifest, and the children of the devil: whosoever doeth not righteousness is not of God, neither he that loveth not his brother.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For this is the message that ye heard from the beginning, that we should love one another.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Not as Cain, who was of that wicked one, and slew his brother. And wherefore slew he him? Because his own works were evil, and his brother’s righteous.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Marvel not, my brethren, if the world hate you.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** We know that we have passed from death unto life, because we love the brethren. He that loveth not his brother abideth in death.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Whosoever hateth his brother is a murderer: and ye know that no murderer hath eternal life abiding in him.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Hereby perceive we the love of God, because he laid down his life for us: and we ought to lay down our lives for the brethren.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** But whoso hath this world’s good, and seeth his brother have need, and shutteth up his bowels of compassion from him, how dwelleth the love of God in him?

<a id="v18"></a>**18** My little children, let us not love in word, neither in tongue; but in deed and in truth.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And hereby we know that we are of the truth, and shall assure our hearts before him.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** For if our heart condemn us, God is greater than our heart, and knoweth all things.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Beloved, if our heart condemn us not, then have we confidence toward God.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And whatsoever we ask, we receive of him, because we keep his commandments, and do those things that are pleasing in his sight.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And this is his commandment, That we should believe on the name of his Son Jesus Christ, and love one another, as he gave us commandment.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And he that keepeth his commandments dwelleth in him, and he in him. And hereby we know that he abideth in us, by the Spirit which he hath given us.
