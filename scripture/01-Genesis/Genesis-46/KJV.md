# Genesis 46 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Israel took his journey with all that he had, and came to Beer–sheba, and offered sacrifices unto the God of his father Isaac.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And God spake unto Israel in the visions of the night, and said, Jacob, Jacob. And he said, Here am I.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And he said, I am God, the God of thy father: fear not to go down into Egypt; for I will there make of thee a great nation:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will go down with thee into Egypt; and I will also surely bring thee up again: and Joseph shall put his hand upon thine eyes.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And Jacob rose up from Beer–sheba: and the sons of Israel carried Jacob their father, and their little ones, and their wives, in the wagons which Pharaoh had sent to carry him.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And they took their cattle, and their goods, which they had gotten in the land of Canaan, and came into Egypt, Jacob, and all his seed with him:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** His sons, and his sons’ sons with him, his daughters, and his sons’ daughters, and all his seed brought he with him into Egypt.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And these are the names of the children of Israel, which came into Egypt, Jacob and his sons: Reuben, Jacob’s firstborn.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And the sons of Reuben; Hanoch, and Phallu, and Hezron, and Carmi.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And the sons of Simeon; Jemuel, and Jamin, and Ohad, and Jachin, and Zohar, and Shaul the son of a Canaanitish woman.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And the sons of Levi; Gershon, Kohath, and Merari.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And the sons of Judah; Er, and Onan, and Shelah, and Pharez, and Zerah: but Er and Onan died in the land of Canaan. And the sons of Pharez were Hezron and Hamul.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And the sons of Issachar; Tola, and Phuvah, and Job, and Shimron.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And the sons of Zebulun; Sered, and Elon, and Jahleel.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** These be the sons of Leah, which she bare unto Jacob in Padan–aram, with his daughter Dinah: all the souls of his sons and his daughters were thirty and three.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And the sons of Gad; Ziphion, and Haggi, Shuni, and Ezbon, Eri, and Arodi, and Areli.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And the sons of Asher; Jimnah, and Ishuah, and Isui, and Beriah, and Serah their sister: and the sons of Beriah; Heber, and Malchiel.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** These are the sons of Zilpah, whom Laban gave to Leah his daughter, and these she bare unto Jacob, even sixteen souls.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The sons of Rachel Jacob’s wife; Joseph, and Benjamin.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And unto Joseph in the land of Egypt were born Manasseh and Ephraim, which Asenath the daughter of Poti–pherah priest of On bare unto him.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And the sons of Benjamin were Belah, and Becher, and Ashbel, Gera, and Naaman, Ehi, and Rosh, Muppim, and Huppim, and Ard.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** These are the sons of Rachel, which were born to Jacob: all the souls were fourteen.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And the sons of Dan; Hushim.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And the sons of Naphtali; Jahzeel, and Guni, and Jezer, and Shillem.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** These are the sons of Bilhah, which Laban gave unto Rachel his daughter, and she bare these unto Jacob: all the souls were seven.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** All the souls that came with Jacob into Egypt, which came out of his loins, besides Jacob’s sons’ wives, all the souls were threescore and six;

<a id="v27"></a>**27** And the sons of Joseph, which were born him in Egypt, were two souls: all the souls of the house of Jacob, which came into Egypt, were threescore and ten.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** And he sent Judah before him unto Joseph, to direct his face unto Goshen; and they came into the land of Goshen.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And Joseph made ready his chariot, and went up to meet Israel his father, to Goshen, and presented himself unto him; and he fell on his neck, and wept on his neck a good while.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** And Israel said unto Joseph, Now let me die, since I have seen thy face, because thou art yet alive.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** And Joseph said unto his brethren, and unto his father’s house, I will go up, and shew Pharaoh, and say unto him, My brethren, and my father’s house, which were in the land of Canaan, are come unto me;

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And the men are shepherds, for their trade hath been to feed cattle; and they have brought their flocks, and their herds, and all that they have.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** And it shall come to pass, when Pharaoh shall call you, and shall say, What is your occupation?

<a id="v34"></a>**34** That ye shall say, Thy servants’ trade hath been about cattle from our youth even until now, both we, and also our fathers: that ye may dwell in the land of Goshen; for every shepherd is an abomination unto the Egyptians.
