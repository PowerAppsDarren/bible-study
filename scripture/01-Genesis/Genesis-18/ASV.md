# Genesis 18 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Jehovah appeared unto him by the oaks of Mamre, as he sat in the tent door in the heat of the day;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** and he lifted up his eyes and looked, and, lo, three men stood over against him: and when he saw them, he ran to meet them from the tent door, and bowed himself to the earth,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** and said, My lord, if now I have found favor in thy sight, pass not away, I pray thee, from thy servant:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** let now a little water be fetched, and wash your feet, and rest yourselves under the tree:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** and I will fetch a morsel of bread, and strengthen ye your heart; after that ye shall pass on: forasmuch as ye are come to your servant. And they said, So do, as thou hast said.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And Abraham hastened into the tent unto Sarah, and said, Make ready quickly three measures of fine meal, knead it, and make cakes.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And Abraham ran unto the herd, and fetched a calf tender and good, and gave it unto the servant; and he hasted to dress it.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And he took butter, and milk, and the calf which he had dressed, and set it before them; and he stood by them under the tree, and they did eat.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And they said unto him, Where is Sarah thy wife? And he said, Behold, in the tent.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And he said, I will certainly return unto thee when the season cometh round; and, lo, Sarah thy wife shall have a son. And Sarah heard in the tent door, which was behind him.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Now Abraham and Sarah were old, and well stricken in age; it had ceased to be with Sarah after the manner of women.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And Sarah laughed within herself, saying, After I am waxed old shall I have pleasure, my lord being old also?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And Jehovah said unto Abraham, Wherefore did Sarah laugh, saying, Shall I of a surety bear a child, who am old?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Is anything too hard for Jehovah? At the set time I will return unto thee, when the season cometh round, and Sarah shall have a son.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Then Sarah denied, saying, I laughed not; for she was afraid. And he said, Nay; but thou didst laugh.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And the men rose up from thence, and looked toward Sodom: and Abraham went with them to bring them on the way.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And Jehovah said, Shall I hide from Abraham that which I do;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** seeing that Abraham shall surely become a great and mighty nation, and all the nations of the earth shall be blessed in him?

<a id="v19"></a>**19** For I have known him, to the end that he may command his children and his household after him, that they may keep the way of Jehovah, to do righteousness and justice; to the end that Jehovah may bring upon Abraham that which he hath spoken of him.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And Jehovah said, Because the cry of Sodom and Gomorrah is great, and because their sin is very grievous;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** I will go down now, and see whether they have done altogether according to the cry of it, which is come unto me; and if not, I will know.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And the men turned from thence, and went toward Sodom: but Abraham stood yet before Jehovah.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And Abraham drew near, and said, Wilt thou consume the righteous with the wicked?

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Peradventure there are fifty righteous within the city: wilt thou consume and not spare the place for the fifty righteous that are therein?

<a id="v25"></a>**25** That be far from thee to do after this manner, to slay the righteous with the wicked, that so the righteous should be as the wicked; that be far from thee: shall not the Judge of all the earth do right?

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And Jehovah said, If I find in Sodom fifty righteous within the city, then I will spare all the place for their sake.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** And Abraham answered and said, Behold now, I have taken upon me to speak unto the Lord, who am but dust and ashes:

<a id="v28"></a>**28** peradventure there shall lack five of the fifty righteous: wilt thou destroy all the city for lack of five? And he said, I will not destroy it, if I find there forty and five.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And he spake unto him yet again, and said, Peradventure there shall be forty found there. And he said, I will not do it for the forty’s sake.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** And he said, Oh let not the Lord be angry, and I will speak: peradventure there shall thirty be found there. And he said, I will not do it, if I find thirty there.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** And he said, Behold now, I have taken upon me to speak unto the Lord: peradventure there shall be twenty found there. And he said, I will not destroy it for the twenty’s sake.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And he said, Oh let not the Lord be angry, and I will speak yet but this once: peradventure ten shall be found there. And he said, I will not destroy it for the ten’s sake.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** And Jehovah went his way, as soon as he had left off communing with Abraham: and Abraham returned unto his place.
