# Genesis 15 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** After these things, the word of the Lord came to Abram in a vision, saying, Have no fear, Abram: I will keep you safe, and great will be your reward.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And Abram said, What will you give me? for I have no child and this Eliezer of Damascus will have all my wealth after me.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And Abram said, You have given me no child, and a servant in my house will get the heritage.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Then said the Lord, This man will not get the heritage, but a son of your body will have your property after you.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And he took him out into the open air, and said to him, Let your eyes be lifted to heaven, and see if the stars may be numbered; even so will your seed be.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And he had faith in the Lord, and it was put to his account as righteousness.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And he said to him, I am the Lord, who took you from Ur of the Chaldees, to give you this land for your heritage.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And he said, O Lord God, how may I be certain that it will be mine?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And he said, Take a young cow of three years old, and a she-goat of three years old, and a sheep of three years old, and a dove and a young pigeon.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** All these he took, cutting them in two and putting one half opposite the other, but not cutting the birds in two.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And evil birds came down on the bodies, but Abram sent them away.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Now when the sun was going down, a deep sleep came on Abram, and a dark cloud of fear.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And he said to Abram, Truly, your seed will be living in a land which is not theirs, as servants to a people who will be cruel to them for four hundred years;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But I will be the judge of that nation whose servants they are, and they will come out from among them with great wealth.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** As for you, you will go to your fathers in peace; at the end of a long life you will be put in your last resting-place.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And in the fourth generation they will come back here; for at present the sin of the Amorite is not full.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Then when the sun went down and it was dark, he saw a smoking fire and a flaming light which went between the parts of the bodies.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** In that day the Lord made an agreement with Abram, and said, To your seed have I given this land from the river of Egypt to the great river, the river Euphrates:

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The Kenite, the Kenizzite, and the Kadmonite,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And the Hittite, and the Perizzite, and the Rephaim,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And the Amorite, and the Canaanite, and the Girgashite, and the Jebusite.
