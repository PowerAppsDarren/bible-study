# Genesis 15 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** After these things the word of the Lord came unto Abram in a vision, saying, Fear not, Abram: I am thy shield, and thy exceeding great reward.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And Abram said, Lord God, what wilt thou give me, seeing I go childless, and the steward of my house is this Eliezer of Damascus?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And Abram said, Behold, to me thou hast given no seed: and, lo, one born in my house is mine heir.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And, behold, the word of the Lord came unto him, saying, This shall not be thine heir; but he that shall come forth out of thine own bowels shall be thine heir.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And he brought him forth abroad, and said, Look now toward heaven, and tell the stars, if thou be able to number them: and he said unto him, So shall thy seed be.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And he believed in the Lord; and he counted it to him for righteousness.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And he said unto him, I am the Lord that brought thee out of Ur of the Chaldees, to give thee this land to inherit it.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And he said, Lord God, whereby shall I know that I shall inherit it?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And he said unto him, Take me an heifer of three years old, and a she goat of three years old, and a ram of three years old, and a turtledove, and a young pigeon.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And he took unto him all these, and divided them in the midst, and laid each piece one against another: but the birds divided he not.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And when the fowls came down upon the carcases, Abram drove them away.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And when the sun was going down, a deep sleep fell upon Abram; and, lo, an horror of great darkness fell upon him.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And he said unto Abram, Know of a surety that thy seed shall be a stranger in a land that is not theirs, and shall serve them; and they shall afflict them four hundred years;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And also that nation, whom they shall serve, will I judge: and afterward shall they come out with great substance.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And thou shalt go to thy fathers in peace; thou shalt be buried in a good old age.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** But in the fourth generation they shall come hither again: for the iniquity of the Amorites is not yet full.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And it came to pass, that, when the sun went down, and it was dark, behold a smoking furnace, and a burning lamp that passed between those pieces.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** In the same day the Lord made a covenant with Abram, saying, Unto thy seed have I given this land, from the river of Egypt unto the great river, the river Euphrates:

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The Kenites, and the Kenizzites, and the Kadmonites,

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And the Hittites, and the Perizzites, and the Rephaims,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And the Amorites, and the Canaanites, and the Girgashites, and the Jebusites.
