# Genesis 6 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And after a time, when men were increasing on the earth, and had daughters,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The sons of God saw that the daughters of men were fair; and they took wives for themselves from those who were pleasing to them.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And the Lord said, My spirit will not be in man for ever, for he is only flesh; so the days of his life will be a hundred and twenty years.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** There were men of great strength and size on the earth in those days; and after that, when the sons of God had connection with the daughters of men, they gave birth to children: these were the great men of old days, the men of great name.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And the Lord saw that the sin of man was great on the earth, and that all the thoughts of his heart were evil.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And the Lord had sorrow because he had made man on the earth, and grief was in his heart.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And the Lord said, I will take away man, whom I have made, from the face of the earth, even man and beast and that which goes on the earth and every bird of the air; for I have sorrow for having made them.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But Noah had grace in the eyes of God.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** These are the generations of Noah. Noah was an upright man and without sin in his generation: he went in the ways of God.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And Noah had three sons, Shem, Ham, and Japheth.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And the earth was evil in God's eyes and full of violent ways.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And God, looking on the earth, saw that it was evil: for the way of all flesh had become evil on the earth.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And God said to Noah, The end of all flesh has come; the earth is full of their violent doings, and now I will put an end to them with the earth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Make for yourself an ark of gopher wood with rooms in it, and make it safe from the water inside and out.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And this is the way you are to make it: it is to be three hundred cubits long, fifty cubits wide, and thirty cubits high.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** You are to put a window in the ark, a cubit from the roof, and a door in the side of it, and you are to make it with a lower and second and third floors.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For truly, I will send a great flow of waters over the earth, for the destruction from under the heaven of all flesh in which is the breath of life; everything on the earth will come to an end.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But with you I will make an agreement; and you will come into the ark, you and your sons and your wife and your sons' wives with you.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And you will take with you into the ark two of every sort of living thing, and keep them safe with you; they will be male and female.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Two of every sort of bird and cattle and of every sort of living thing which goes on the earth will you take with you to keep them from destruction.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And make a store of every sort of food for yourself and them.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And all these things Noah did; as God said, so he did.
