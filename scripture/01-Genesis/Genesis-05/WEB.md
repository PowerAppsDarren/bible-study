# Genesis 5 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** This is the book of the generations of Adam. In the day that God created man, he made him in God’s likeness.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** He created them male and female, and blessed them. On the day they were created, he named them “Adam”.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Adam lived one hundred thirty years, and became the father of a son in his own likeness, after his image, and named him Seth.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The days of Adam after he became the father of Seth were eight hundred years, and he became the father of other sons and daughters.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** All the days that Adam lived were nine hundred thirty years, then he died.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Seth lived one hundred five years, then became the father of Enosh.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Seth lived after he became the father of Enosh eight hundred seven years, and became the father of other sons and daughters.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** All the days of Seth were nine hundred twelve years, then he died.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Enosh lived ninety years, and became the father of Kenan.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Enosh lived after he became the father of Kenan, eight hundred fifteen years, and became the father of other sons and daughters.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** All the days of Enosh were nine hundred five years, then he died.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Kenan lived seventy years, then became the father of Mahalalel.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Kenan lived after he became the father of Mahalalel eight hundred forty years, and became the father of other sons and daughters

<a id="v14"></a>**14** and all the days of Kenan were nine hundred ten years, then he died.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Mahalalel lived sixty-five years, then became the father of Jared.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Mahalalel lived after he became the father of Jared eight hundred thirty years, and became the father of other sons and daughters.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** All the days of Mahalalel were eight hundred ninety-five years, then he died.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Jared lived one hundred sixty-two years, then became the father of Enoch.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Jared lived after he became the father of Enoch eight hundred years, and became the father of other sons and daughters.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** All the days of Jared were nine hundred sixty-two years, then he died.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Enoch lived sixty-five years, then became the father of Methuselah.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** After Methuselah’s birth, Enoch walked with God for three hundred years, and became the father of more sons and daughters.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** All the days of Enoch were three hundred sixty-five years.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Enoch walked with God, and he was not found, for God took him.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Methuselah lived one hundred eighty-seven years, then became the father of Lamech.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Methuselah lived after he became the father of Lamech seven hundred eighty-two years, and became the father of other sons and daughters.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** All the days of Methuselah were nine hundred sixty-nine years, then he died.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Lamech lived one hundred eighty-two years, then became the father of a son.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** He named him Noah, saying, “This one will comfort us in our work and in the toil of our hands, caused by the ground which Yahweh has cursed.”

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Lamech lived after he became the father of Noah five hundred ninety-five years, and became the father of other sons and daughters.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** All the days of Lamech were seven hundred seventy-seven years, then he died.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Noah was five hundred years old, then Noah became the father of Shem, Ham, and Japheth.
