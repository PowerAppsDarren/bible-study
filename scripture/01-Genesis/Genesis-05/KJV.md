# Genesis 5 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** This is the book of the generations of Adam. In the day that God created man, in the likeness of God made he him;

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Male and female created he them; and blessed them, and called their name Adam, in the day when they were created.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And Adam lived an hundred and thirty years, and begat a son in his own likeness, after his image; and called his name Seth:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And the days of Adam after he had begotten Seth were eight hundred years: and he begat sons and daughters:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And all the days that Adam lived were nine hundred and thirty years: and he died.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And Seth lived an hundred and five years, and begat Enos:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And Seth lived after he begat Enos eight hundred and seven years, and begat sons and daughters:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And all the days of Seth were nine hundred and twelve years: and he died.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And Enos lived ninety years, and begat Cainan:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And Enos lived after he begat Cainan eight hundred and fifteen years, and begat sons and daughters:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And all the days of Enos were nine hundred and five years: and he died.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And Cainan lived seventy years, and begat Mahalaleel:

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And Cainan lived after he begat Mahalaleel eight hundred and forty years, and begat sons and daughters:

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And all the days of Cainan were nine hundred and ten years: and he died.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And Mahalaleel lived sixty and five years, and begat Jared:

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And Mahalaleel lived after he begat Jared eight hundred and thirty years, and begat sons and daughters:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And all the days of Mahalaleel were eight hundred ninety and five years: and he died.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And Jared lived an hundred sixty and two years, and he begat Enoch:

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And Jared lived after he begat Enoch eight hundred years, and begat sons and daughters:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And all the days of Jared were nine hundred sixty and two years: and he died.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And Enoch lived sixty and five years, and begat Methuselah:

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And Enoch walked with God after he begat Methuselah three hundred years, and begat sons and daughters:

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And all the days of Enoch were three hundred sixty and five years:

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And Enoch walked with God: and he was not; for God took him.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And Methuselah lived an hundred eighty and seven years, and begat Lamech:

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And Methuselah lived after he begat Lamech seven hundred eighty and two years, and begat sons and daughters:

<a id="v27"></a>**27** And all the days of Methuselah were nine hundred sixty and nine years: and he died.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** And Lamech lived an hundred eighty and two years, and begat a son:

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And he called his name Noah, saying, This same shall comfort us concerning our work and toil of our hands, because of the ground which the Lord hath cursed.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** And Lamech lived after he begat Noah five hundred ninety and five years, and begat sons and daughters:

<a id="v31"></a>**31** And all the days of Lamech were seven hundred seventy and seven years: and he died.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And Noah was five hundred years old: and Noah begat Shem, Ham, and Japheth.
