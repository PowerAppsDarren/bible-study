# Genesis 9 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And God gave his blessing to Noah and his sons, and said, Be fertile, and have increase, and make the earth full.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And the fear of you will be strong in every beast of the earth and every bird of the air; everything which goes on the land, and all the fishes of the sea, are given into your hands.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Every living and moving thing will be food for you; I give them all to you as before I gave you all green things.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But flesh with the life-blood in it you may not take for food.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And for your blood, which is your life, will I take payment; from every beast I will take it, and from every man will I take payment for the blood of his brother-man.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Whoever takes a man's life, by man will his life be taken; because God made man in his image.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And now, be fertile and have increase; have offspring on the earth and become great in number.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And God said to Noah and to his sons,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Truly, I will make my agreement with you and with your seed after you,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And with every living thing with you, all birds and cattle and every beast of the earth which comes out of the ark with you.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And I will make my agreement with you; never again will all flesh be cut off by the waters; never again will the waters come over all the earth for its destruction.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And God said, This is the sign of the agreement which I make between me and you and every living thing with you, for all future generations:

<a id="v13"></a>**13** I will put my bow in the cloud and it will be for a sign of the agreement between me and the earth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And whenever I make a cloud come over the earth, the bow will be seen in the cloud,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And I will keep in mind the agreement between me and you and every living thing; and never again will there be a great flow of waters causing destruction to all flesh.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And the bow will be in the cloud, and looking on it, I will keep in mind the eternal agreement between God and every living thing on the earth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And God said to Noah, This is the sign of the agreement which I have made between me and all flesh on the earth.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And the sons of Noah who went out of the ark were Shem, Ham, and Japheth; and Ham is the father of Canaan.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** These three were the sons of Noah and from them all the earth was peopled.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** In those days Noah became a farmer, and he made a vine-garden.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And he took of the wine of it and was overcome by drink; and he was uncovered in his tent.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And Ham, the father of Canaan, saw his father unclothed, and gave news of it to his two brothers outside.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And Shem and Japheth took a robe, and putting it on their backs went in with their faces turned away, and put it over their father so that they might not see him unclothed.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And, awaking from his wine, Noah saw what his youngest son had done to him, and he said,

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Cursed be Canaan; let him be a servant of servants to his brothers.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And he said, Praise to the Lord, the God of Shem; let Canaan be his servant.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** May God make Japheth great, and let his living-place be in the tents of Shem, and let Canaan be his servant.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** And Noah went on living three hundred and fifty years after the great flow of waters;

<a id="v29"></a>**29** all the years of his life were nine hundred and fifty: and he came to his end.
