# Genesis 10 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Now these are the generations of the sons of Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth: and unto them were sons born after the flood.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The sons of Japheth; Gomer, and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And the sons of Gomer; Ashkenaz, and Riphath, and Togarmah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And the sons of Javan; Elishah, and Tarshish, Kittim, and Dodanim.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** By these were the isles of the Gentiles divided in their lands; every one after his tongue, after their families, in their nations.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And the sons of Ham; Cush, and Mizraim, and Phut, and Canaan.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And the sons of Cush; Seba, and Havilah, and Sabtah, and Raamah, and Sabtecha: and the sons of Raamah; Sheba, and Dedan.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And Cush begat Nimrod: he began to be a mighty one in the earth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He was a mighty hunter before the Lord: wherefore it is said, Even as Nimrod the mighty hunter before the Lord.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And the beginning of his kingdom was Babel, and Erech, and Accad, and Calneh, in the land of Shinar.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Out of that land went forth Asshur, and builded Nineveh, and the city Rehoboth, and Calah,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And Resen between Nineveh and Calah: the same is a great city.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And Mizraim begat Ludim, and Anamim, and Lehabim, and Naphtuhim,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And Pathrusim, and Casluhim, (out of whom came Philistim,) and Caphtorim.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And Canaan begat Sidon his firstborn, and Heth,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And the Jebusite, and the Amorite, and the Girgasite,

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And the Hivite, and the Arkite, and the Sinite,

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And the Arvadite, and the Zemarite, and the Hamathite: and afterward were the families of the Canaanites spread abroad.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And the border of the Canaanites was from Sidon, as thou comest to Gerar, unto Gaza; as thou goest, unto Sodom, and Gomorrah, and Admah, and Zeboim, even unto Lasha.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** These are the sons of Ham, after their families, after their tongues, in their countries, and in their nations.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Unto Shem also, the father of all the children of Eber, the brother of Japheth the elder, even to him were children born.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The children of Shem; Elam, and Asshur, and Arphaxad, and Lud, and Aram.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And the children of Aram; Uz, and Hul, and Gether, and Mash.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And Arphaxad begat Salah; and Salah begat Eber.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And unto Eber were born two sons: the name of one was Peleg; for in his days was the earth divided; and his brother’s name was Joktan.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And Joktan begat Almodad, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah,

<a id="v27"></a>**27** And Hadoram, and Uzal, and Diklah,

<a id="v28"></a>**28** And Obal, and Abimael, and Sheba,

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And Ophir, and Havilah, and Jobab: all these were the sons of Joktan.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** And their dwelling was from Mesha, as thou goest unto Sephar a mount of the east.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** These are the sons of Shem, after their families, after their tongues, in their lands, after their nations.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** These are the families of the sons of Noah, after their generations, in their nations: and by these were the nations divided in the earth after the flood.
