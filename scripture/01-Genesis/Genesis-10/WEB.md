# Genesis 10 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Now this is the history of the generations of the sons of Noah and of Shem, Ham, and Japheth. Sons were born to them after the flood.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The sons of Japheth were: Gomer, Magog, Madai, Javan, Tubal, Meshech, and Tiras.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The sons of Gomer were: Ashkenaz, Riphath, and Togarmah.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The sons of Javan were: Elishah, Tarshish, Kittim, and Dodanim.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Of these were the islands of the nations divided in their lands, everyone after his language, after their families, in their nations.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The sons of Ham were: Cush, Mizraim, Put, and Canaan.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The sons of Cush were: Seba, Havilah, Sabtah, Raamah, and Sabteca. The sons of Raamah were: Sheba and Dedan.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Cush became the father of Nimrod. He began to be a mighty one in the earth.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He was a mighty hunter before Yahweh. Therefore it is said, “like Nimrod, a mighty hunter before Yahweh”.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The beginning of his kingdom was Babel, Erech, Accad, and Calneh, in the land of Shinar.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Out of that land he went into Assyria, and built Nineveh, Rehoboth Ir, Calah,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** and Resen between Nineveh and the great city Calah.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Mizraim became the father of Ludim, Anamim, Lehabim, Naphtuhim,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Pathrusim, Casluhim (which the Philistines descended from), and Caphtorim.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Canaan became the father of Sidon (his firstborn), Heth,

<a id="v16"></a>**16** the Jebusites, the Amorites, the Girgashites,

<a id="v17"></a>**17** the Hivites, the Arkites, the Sinites,

<a id="v18"></a>**18** the Arvadites, the Zemarites, and the Hamathites. Afterward the families of the Canaanites were spread abroad.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The border of the Canaanites was from Sidon—as you go toward Gerar—to Gaza—as you go toward Sodom, Gomorrah, Admah, and Zeboiim—to Lasha.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** These are the sons of Ham, after their families, according to their languages, in their lands and their nations.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Children were also born to Shem, the father of all the children of Eber, the elder brother of Japheth.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The sons of Shem were: Elam, Asshur, Arpachshad, Lud, and Aram.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** The sons of Aram were: Uz, Hul, Gether, and Mash.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Arpachshad became the father of Shelah. Shelah became the father of Eber.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** To Eber were born two sons. The name of the one was Peleg, for in his days the earth was divided. His brother’s name was Joktan.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Joktan became the father of Almodad, Sheleph, Hazarmaveth, Jerah,

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Hadoram, Uzal, Diklah,

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Obal, Abimael, Sheba,

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Ophir, Havilah, and Jobab. All these were the sons of Joktan.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Their dwelling extended from Mesha, as you go toward Sephar, the mountain of the east.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** These are the sons of Shem, by their families, according to their languages, lands, and nations.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** These are the families of the sons of Noah, by their generations, according to their nations. The nations divided from these in the earth after the flood.
