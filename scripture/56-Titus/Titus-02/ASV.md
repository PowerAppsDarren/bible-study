# Titus 2 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** But speak thou the things which befit the sound doctrine:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** that aged men be temperate, grave, sober-minded, sound in faith, in love, in patience:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** that aged women likewise be reverent in demeanor, not slanderers nor enslaved to much wine, teachers of that which is good;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** that they may train the young women to love their husbands, to love their children,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** to be sober-minded, chaste, workers at home, kind, being in subjection to their own husbands, that the word of God be not blasphemed:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** the younger men likewise exhort to be sober-minded:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** in all things showing thyself an ensample of good works; in thy doctrine showing uncorruptness, gravity,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** sound speech, that cannot be condemned; that he that is of the contrary part may be ashamed, having no evil thing to say of us.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Exhort servants to be in subjection to their own masters, and to be well-pleasing to them in all things; not gainsaying;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** not purloining, but showing all good fidelity; that they may adorn the doctrine of God our Saviour in all things.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For the grace of God hath appeared, bringing salvation to all men,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** instructing us, to the intent that, denying ungodliness and worldly lusts, we should live soberly and righteously and godly in this present world;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** looking for the blessed hope and appearing of the glory of the great God and our Saviour Jesus Christ;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** who gave himself for us, that he might redeem us from all iniquity, and purify unto himself a people for his own possession, zealous of good works.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** These things speak and exhort and reprove with all authority. Let no man despise thee.
