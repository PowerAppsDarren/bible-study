# Titus 2 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** But speak thou the things which become sound doctrine:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** That the aged men be sober, grave, temperate, sound in faith, in charity, in patience.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** The aged women likewise, that they be in behaviour as becometh holiness, not false accusers, not given to much wine, teachers of good things;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** That they may teach the young women to be sober, to love their husbands, to love their children,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** To be discreet, chaste, keepers at home, good, obedient to their own husbands, that the word of God be not blasphemed.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Young men likewise exhort to be sober minded.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** In all things shewing thyself a pattern of good works: in doctrine shewing uncorruptness, gravity, sincerity,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Sound speech, that cannot be condemned; that he that is of the contrary part may be ashamed, having no evil thing to say of you.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Exhort servants to be obedient unto their own masters, and to please them well in all things; not answering again;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Not purloining, but shewing all good fidelity; that they may adorn the doctrine of God our Saviour in all things.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For the grace of God that bringeth salvation hath appeared to all men,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Teaching us that, denying ungodliness and worldly lusts, we should live soberly, righteously, and godly, in this present world;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Looking for that blessed hope, and the glorious appearing of the great God and our Saviour Jesus Christ;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Who gave himself for us, that he might redeem us from all iniquity, and purify unto himself a peculiar people, zealous of good works.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** These things speak, and exhort, and rebuke with all authority. Let no man despise thee.
