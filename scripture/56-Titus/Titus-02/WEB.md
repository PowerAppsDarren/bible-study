# Titus 2 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** But say the things which fit sound doctrine,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** that older men should be temperate, sensible, sober minded, sound in faith, in love, and in patience:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** and that older women likewise be reverent in behavior, not slanderers nor enslaved to much wine, teachers of that which is good;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** that they may train the young women to love their husbands, to love their children,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** to be sober minded, chaste, workers at home, kind, being in subjection to their own husbands, that God’s word may not be blasphemed.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Likewise, exhort the younger men to be sober minded;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** in all things showing yourself an example of good works; in your teaching showing integrity, seriousness, incorruptibility,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** and soundness of speech that can’t be condemned; that he who opposes you may be ashamed, having no evil thing to say about us.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Exhort servants to be in subjection to their own masters, and to be well-pleasing in all things; not contradicting;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** not stealing, but showing all good fidelity; that they may adorn the doctrine of God, our Savior, in all things.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For the grace of God has appeared, bringing salvation to all men,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** instructing us to the intent that, denying ungodliness and worldly lusts, we would live soberly, righteously, and godly in this present world;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** looking for the blessed hope and appearing of the glory of our great God and Savior, Jesus Christ;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** who gave himself for us, that he might redeem us from all iniquity, and purify for himself a people for his own possession, zealous for good works.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Say these things and exhort and reprove with all authority. Let no man despise you.
