# Malachi 4 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** “For, behold, the day comes, it burns as a furnace; and all the proud, and all who work wickedness, will be stubble; and the day that comes will burn them up,” says Yahweh of Armies, “that it shall leave them neither root nor branch.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** But to you who fear my name shall the sun of righteousness arise with healing in its wings. You will go out, and leap like calves of the stall.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You shall tread down the wicked; for they will be ashes under the soles of your feet in the day that I make,” says Yahweh of Armies.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** “Remember the law of Moses my servant, which I commanded to him in Horeb for all Israel, even statutes and ordinances.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Behold, I will send you Elijah the prophet before the great and terrible day of Yahweh comes.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He will turn the hearts of the fathers to the children, and the hearts of the children to their fathers, lest I come and strike the earth with a curse.”
