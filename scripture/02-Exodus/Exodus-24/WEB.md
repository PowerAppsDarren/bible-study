# Exodus 24 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** He said to Moses, “Come up to Yahweh, you, and Aaron, Nadab, and Abihu, and seventy of the elders of Israel; and worship from a distance.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Moses alone shall come near to Yahweh, but they shall not come near, neither shall the people go up with him.”

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Moses came and told the people all Yahweh’s words, and all the ordinances; and all the people answered with one voice, and said, “All the words which Yahweh has spoken will we do.”

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Moses wrote all Yahweh’s words, and rose up early in the morning, and built an altar under the mountain, and twelve pillars for the twelve tribes of Israel.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He sent young men of the children of Israel, who offered burnt offerings and sacrificed peace offerings of cattle to Yahweh.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Moses took half of the blood and put it in basins, and half of the blood he sprinkled on the altar.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He took the book of the covenant and read it in the hearing of the people, and they said, “All that Yahweh has spoken will we do, and be obedient.”

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Moses took the blood, and sprinkled it on the people, and said, “Look, this is the blood of the covenant, which Yahweh has made with you concerning all these words.”

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Then Moses, Aaron, Nadab, Abihu, and seventy of the elders of Israel went up.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** They saw the God of Israel. Under his feet was like a paved work of sapphire  stone, like the skies for clearness.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He didn’t lay his hand on the nobles of the children of Israel. They saw God, and ate and drank.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yahweh said to Moses, “Come up to me on the mountain, and stay here, and I will give you the stone tablets with the law and the commands that I have written, that you may teach them.”

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Moses rose up with Joshua, his servant, and Moses went up onto God’s Mountain.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He said to the elders, “Wait here for us, until we come again to you. Behold, Aaron and Hur are with you. Whoever is involved in a dispute can go to them.”

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Moses went up on the mountain, and the cloud covered the mountain.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Yahweh’s glory settled on Mount Sinai, and the cloud covered it six days. The seventh day he called to Moses out of the midst of the cloud.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The appearance of Yahweh’s glory was like devouring fire on the top of the mountain in the eyes of the children of Israel.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Moses entered into the midst of the cloud, and went up on the mountain; and Moses was on the mountain forty days and forty nights.
