# Exodus 27 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And thou shalt make the altar of acacia wood, five cubits long, and five cubits broad; the altar shall be foursquare: and the height thereof shall be three cubits.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And thou shalt make the horns of it upon the four corners thereof; the horns thereof shall be of one piece with it: and thou shalt overlay it with brass.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And thou shalt make its pots to take away its ashes, and its shovels, and its basins, and its flesh-hooks, and its firepans: all the vessels thereof thou shalt make of brass.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And thou shalt make for it a grating of network of brass: and upon the net shalt thou make four brazen rings in the four corners thereof.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And thou shalt put it under the ledge round the altar beneath, that the net may reach halfway up the altar.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And thou shalt make staves for the altar, staves of acacia wood, and overlay them with brass.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And the staves thereof shall be put into the rings, and the staves shall be upon the two sides of the altar, in bearing it.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Hollow with planks shalt thou make it: as it hath been showed thee in the mount, so shall they make it.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And thou shalt make the court of the tabernacle: for the south side southward there shall be hangings for the court of fine twined linen a hundred cubits long for one side:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** and the pillars thereof shall be twenty, and their sockets twenty, of brass; the hooks of the pillars and their fillets shall be of silver.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And likewise for the north side in length there shall be hangings a hundred cubits long, and the pillars thereof twenty, and their sockets twenty, of brass; the hooks of the pillars, and their fillets, of silver.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And for the breadth of the court on the west side shall be hangings of fifty cubits; their pillars ten, and their sockets ten.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And the breadth of the court on the east side eastward shall be fifty cubits.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The hangings for the one side of the gate shall be fifteen cubits; their pillars three, and their sockets three.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And for the other side shall be hangings of fifteen cubits; their pillars three, and their sockets three.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And for the gate of the court shall be a screen of twenty cubits, of blue, and purple, and scarlet, and fine twined linen, the work of the embroiderer; their pillars four, and their sockets four.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** All the pillars of the court round about shall be filleted with silver; their hooks of silver, and their sockets of brass.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The length of the court shall be a hundred cubits, and the breadth fifty every where, and the height five cubits, of fine twined linen, and their sockets of brass.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** All the instruments of the tabernacle in all the service thereof, and all the pins thereof, and all the pins of the court, shall be of brass.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And thou shalt command the children of Israel, that they bring unto thee pure olive oil beaten for the light, to cause a lamp to burn continually.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** In the tent of meeting, without the veil which is before the testimony, Aaron and his sons shall keep it in order from evening to morning before Jehovah: it shall be a statute for ever throughout their generations on the behalf of the children of Israel.
