# Exodus 27 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And make an altar of hard wood, a square altar, five cubits long, five cubits wide and three cubits high.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Put horns at the four angles of it, made of the same, plating it all with brass.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And make all its vessels, the baskets for taking away the dust of the fire, the spades and basins and meat-hooks and fire-trays, of brass.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And make a network of brass, with four brass rings at its four angles.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And put the network under the shelf round the altar so that the net comes half-way up the altar.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And make rods for the altar, of hard wood, plated with brass.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And put the rods through the rings at the two opposite sides of the altar, for lifting it.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The altar is to be hollow, boarded in with wood; make it from the design which you saw on the mountain.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And let there be an open space round the House, with hangings for its south side of the best linen, a hundred cubits long.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Their twenty pillars and their twenty bases are to be of brass; the hooks of the pillars and their bands are to be of silver.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And on the north side in the same way, hangings a hundred cubits long, with twenty pillars of brass on bases of brass; their hooks and their bands are to be of silver.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And for the open space on the west side, the hangings are to be fifty cubits wide, with ten pillars and ten bases;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And on the east side the space is to be fifty cubits wide.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** On the one side of the doorway will be hangings fifteen cubits long, with three pillars and three bases;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And on the other side, hangings fifteen cubits long, with three pillars and three bases.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And across the doorway, a veil of twenty cubits of the best linen, made of needlework of blue and purple and red, with four pillars and four bases.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** All the pillars round the open space are to have silver bands, with hooks of silver and bases of brass.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The open space is to be a hundred cubits long, fifty cubits wide, with sides five cubits high, curtained with the best linen, with bases of brass.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** All the instruments for the work of the House, and all its nails, and the nails of the open space are to be of brass.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Give orders to the children of Israel to give you clear olive oil for the lights, so that a light may be burning there at all times.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Let Aaron and his sons put this in order, evening and morning, before the Lord, inside the Tent of meeting, outside the veil which is before the ark; this is to be an order for ever, from generation to generation, to be kept by the children of Israel.
