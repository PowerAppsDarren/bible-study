# Exodus 27 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** “You shall make the altar of acacia wood, five cubits long, and five cubits broad; the altar shall be square: and its height shall be three cubits.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** You shall make its horns on its four corners; its horns shall be of one piece with it; and you shall overlay it with brass.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You shall make its pots to take away its ashes, its shovels, its basins, its flesh hooks, and its fire pans: all its vessels you shall make of brass.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You shall make a grating for it of network of brass: and on the net you shall make four bronze rings in its four corners.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** You shall put it under the ledge around the altar beneath, that the net may reach halfway up the altar.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** You shall make poles for the altar, poles of acacia wood, and overlay them with brass.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Its poles shall be put into the rings, and the poles shall be on the two sides of the altar, when carrying it.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** You shall make it with hollow planks. They shall make it as it has been shown you on the mountain.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** “You shall make the court of the tabernacle: for the south side southward there shall be hangings for the court of fine twined linen one hundred cubits long for one side:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** and its pillars shall be twenty, and their sockets twenty, of brass; the hooks of the pillars and their fillets shall be of silver.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Likewise for the north side in length there shall be hangings one hundred cubits long, and its pillars twenty, and their sockets twenty, of brass; the hooks of the pillars, and their fillets, of silver.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** For the breadth of the court on the west side shall be hangings of fifty cubits; their pillars ten, and their sockets ten.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The breadth of the court on the east side eastward shall be fifty cubits.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The hangings for the one side of the gate shall be fifteen cubits; their pillars three, and their sockets three.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For the other side shall be hangings of fifteen cubits; their pillars three, and their sockets three.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For the gate of the court shall be a screen of twenty cubits, of blue, and purple, and scarlet, and fine twined linen, the work of the embroiderer; their pillars four, and their sockets four.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** All the pillars of the court around shall be filleted with silver; their hooks of silver, and their sockets of brass.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The length of the court shall be one hundred cubits, and the breadth fifty every where, and the height five cubits, of fine twined linen, and their sockets of brass.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** All the instruments of the tabernacle in all its service, and all its pins, and all the pins of the court, shall be of brass.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** “You shall command the children of Israel, that they bring to you pure olive oil beaten for the light, to cause a lamp to burn continually.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** In the Tent of Meeting, outside the veil which is before the testimony, Aaron and his sons shall keep it in order from evening to morning before Yahweh: it shall be a statute forever throughout their generations on the behalf of the children of Israel.
