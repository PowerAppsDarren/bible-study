# Exodus 25 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And the Lord spake unto Moses, saying,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Speak unto the children of Israel, that they bring me an offering: of every man that giveth it willingly with his heart ye shall take my offering.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And this is the offering which ye shall take of them; gold, and silver, and brass,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And blue, and purple, and scarlet, and fine linen, and goats’ hair,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And rams’ skins dyed red, and badgers’ skins, and shittim wood,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Oil for the light, spices for anointing oil, and for sweet incense,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Onyx stones, and stones to be set in the ephod, and in the breastplate.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And let them make me a sanctuary; that I may dwell among them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** According to all that I shew thee, after the pattern of the tabernacle, and the pattern of all the instruments thereof, even so shall ye make it.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And they shall make an ark of shittim wood: two cubits and a half shall be the length thereof, and a cubit and a half the breadth thereof, and a cubit and a half the height thereof.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And thou shalt overlay it with pure gold, within and without shalt thou overlay it, and shalt make upon it a crown of gold round about.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And thou shalt cast four rings of gold for it, and put them in the four corners thereof; and two rings shall be in the one side of it, and two rings in the other side of it.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And thou shalt make staves of shittim wood, and overlay them with gold.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And thou shalt put the staves into the rings by the sides of the ark, that the ark may be borne with them.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The staves shall be in the rings of the ark: they shall not be taken from it.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And thou shalt put into the ark the testimony which I shall give thee.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And thou shalt make a mercy seat of pure gold: two cubits and a half shall be the length thereof, and a cubit and a half the breadth thereof.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And thou shalt make two cherubims of gold, of beaten work shalt thou make them, in the two ends of the mercy seat.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And make one cherub on the one end, and the other cherub on the other end: even of the mercy seat shall ye make the cherubims on the two ends thereof.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And the cherubims shall stretch forth their wings on high, covering the mercy seat with their wings, and their faces shall look one to another; toward the mercy seat shall the faces of the cherubims be.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And thou shalt put the mercy seat above upon the ark; and in the ark thou shalt put the testimony that I shall give thee.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And there I will meet with thee, and I will commune with thee from above the mercy seat, from between the two cherubims which are upon the ark of the testimony, of all things which I will give thee in commandment unto the children of Israel.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Thou shalt also make a table of shittim wood: two cubits shall be the length thereof, and a cubit the breadth thereof, and a cubit and a half the height thereof.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And thou shalt overlay it with pure gold, and make thereto a crown of gold round about.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And thou shalt make unto it a border of an hand breadth round about, and thou shalt make a golden crown to the border thereof round about.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And thou shalt make for it four rings of gold, and put the rings in the four corners that are on the four feet thereof.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Over against the border shall the rings be for places of the staves to bear the table.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** And thou shalt make the staves of shittim wood, and overlay them with gold, that the table may be borne with them.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And thou shalt make the dishes thereof, and spoons thereof, and covers thereof, and bowls thereof, to cover withal: of pure gold shalt thou make them.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** And thou shalt set upon the table shewbread before me alway.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** And thou shalt make a candlestick of pure gold: of beaten work shall the candlestick be made: his shaft, and his branches, his bowls, his knops, and his flowers, shall be of the same.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And six branches shall come out of the sides of it; three branches of the candlestick out of the one side, and three branches of the candlestick out of the other side:

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Three bowls made like unto almonds, with a knop and a flower in one branch; and three bowls made like almonds in the other branch, with a knop and a flower: so in the six branches that come out of the candlestick.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** And in the candlestick shall be four bowls made like unto almonds, with their knops and their flowers.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** And there shall be a knop under two branches of the same, and a knop under two branches of the same, and a knop under two branches of the same, according to the six branches that proceed out of the candlestick.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Their knops and their branches shall be of the same: all it shall be one beaten work of pure gold.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** And thou shalt make the seven lamps thereof: and they shall light the lamps thereof, that they may give light over against it.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** And the tongs thereof, and the snuffdishes thereof, shall be of pure gold.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** Of a talent of pure gold shall he make it, with all these vessels.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** And look that thou make them after their pattern, which was shewed thee in the mount.
