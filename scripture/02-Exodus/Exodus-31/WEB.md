# Exodus 31 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** “Behold, I have called by name Bezalel the son of Uri, the son of Hur, of the tribe of Judah:

<a id="v3"></a>**3** and I have filled him with the Spirit of God, in wisdom, and in understanding, and in knowledge, and in all kinds of workmanship,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** to devise skillful works, to work in gold, and in silver, and in brass,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** and in cutting of stones for setting, and in carving of wood, to work in all kinds of workmanship.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Behold, I myself have appointed with him Oholiab, the son of Ahisamach, of the tribe of Dan; and in the heart of all who are wise-hearted I have put wisdom, that they may make all that I have commanded you:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** the Tent of Meeting, the ark of the testimony, the mercy seat that is on it, all the furniture of the Tent,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** the table and its vessels, the pure lamp stand with all its vessels, the altar of incense,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** the altar of burnt offering with all its vessels, the basin and its base,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** the finely worked garments—the holy garments for Aaron the priest—the garments of his sons to minister in the priest’s office,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** the anointing oil, and the incense of sweet spices for the holy place: according to all that I have commanded you they shall do.”

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,

<a id="v13"></a>**13** “Speak also to the children of Israel, saying, ‘Most certainly you shall keep my Sabbaths: for it is a sign between me and you throughout your generations; that you may know that I am Yahweh who sanctifies you.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** You shall keep the Sabbath therefore; for it is holy to you. Everyone who profanes it shall surely be put to death; for whoever does any work therein, that soul shall be cut off from among his people.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Six days shall work be done, but on the seventh day is a Sabbath of solemn rest, holy to Yahweh. Whoever does any work on the Sabbath day shall surely be put to death.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Therefore the children of Israel shall keep the Sabbath, to observe the Sabbath throughout their generations, for a perpetual covenant.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** It is a sign between me and the children of Israel forever; for in six days Yahweh made heaven and earth, and on the seventh day he rested, and was refreshed.’”

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He gave to Moses, when he finished speaking with him on Mount Sinai, the two tablets of the testimony, stone tablets, written with God’s finger.
