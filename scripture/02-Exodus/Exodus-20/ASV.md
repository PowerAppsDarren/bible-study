# Exodus 20 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And God spake all these words, saying,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I am Jehovah thy God, who brought thee out of the land of Egypt, out of the house of bondage.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Thou shalt have no other gods before me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thou shalt not make unto thee a graven image, nor any likeness of any thing that is in heaven above, or that is in the earth beneath, or that is in the water under the earth:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** thou shalt not bow down thyself unto them, nor serve them; for I Jehovah thy God am a jealous God, visiting the iniquity of the fathers upon the children, upon the third and upon the fourth generation of them that hate me,

<a id="v6"></a>**6** and showing lovingkindness unto thousands of them that love me and keep my commandments.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Thou shalt not take the name of Jehovah thy God in vain; for Jehovah will not hold him guiltless that taketh his name in vain.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Remember the sabbath day, to keep it holy.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Six days shalt thou labor, and do all thy work;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** but the seventh day is a sabbath unto Jehovah thy God: in it thou shalt not do any work, thou, nor thy son, nor thy daughter, thy man-servant, nor thy maid-servant, nor thy cattle, nor thy stranger that is within thy gates:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** for in six days Jehovah made heaven and earth, the sea, and all that in them is, and rested the seventh day: wherefore Jehovah blessed the sabbath day, and hallowed it.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Honor thy father and thy mother, that thy days may be long in the land which Jehovah thy God giveth thee.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Thou shalt not kill.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Thou shalt not commit adultery.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Thou shalt not steal.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Thou shalt not bear false witness against thy neighbor.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Thou shalt not covet thy neighbor’s house, thou shalt not covet thy neighbor’s wife, nor his man-servant, nor his maid-servant, nor his ox, nor his ass, nor anything that is thy neighbor’s.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And all the people perceived the thunderings, and the lightnings, and the voice of the trumpet, and the mountain smoking: and when the people saw it, they trembled, and stood afar off.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And they said unto Moses, Speak thou with us, and we will hear; but let not God speak with us, lest we die.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And Moses said unto the people, Fear not: for God is come to prove you, and that his fear may be before you, that ye sin not.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And the people stood afar off, and Moses drew near unto the thick darkness where God was.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And Jehovah said unto Moses, Thus thou shalt say unto the children of Israel, Ye yourselves have seen that I have talked with you from heaven.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Ye shall not make other gods with me; gods of silver, or gods of gold, ye shall not make unto you.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** An altar of earth thou shalt make unto me, and shalt sacrifice thereon thy burnt-offerings, and thy peace-offerings, thy sheep, and thine oxen: in every place where I record my name I will come unto thee and I will bless thee.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And if thou make me an altar of stone, thou shalt not build it of hewn stones; for if thou lift up thy tool upon it, thou hast polluted it.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Neither shalt thou go up by steps unto mine altar, that thy nakedness be not uncovered thereon.
