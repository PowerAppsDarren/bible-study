# Exodus 6 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Yahweh said to Moses, “Now you shall see what I will do to Pharaoh, for by a strong hand he shall let them go, and by a strong hand he shall drive them out of his land.”

<a id="v2"></a>**2** God spoke to Moses, and said to him, “I am Yahweh;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** and I appeared to Abraham, to Isaac, and to Jacob, as God Almighty; but by my name Yahweh I was not known to them.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I have also established my covenant with them, to give them the land of Canaan, the land of their travels, in which they lived as aliens.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Moreover I have heard the groaning of the children of Israel, whom the Egyptians keep in bondage, and I have remembered my covenant.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Therefore tell the children of Israel, ‘I am Yahweh, and I will bring you out from under the burdens of the Egyptians, and I will rid you out of their bondage, and I will redeem you with an outstretched arm, and with great judgments:

<a id="v7"></a>**7** and I will take you to me for a people, and I will be to you a God; and you shall know that I am Yahweh your God, who brings you out from under the burdens of the Egyptians.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** I will bring you into the land which I swore to give to Abraham, to Isaac, and to Jacob; and I will give it to you for a heritage: I am Yahweh.’”

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Moses spoke so to the children of Israel, but they didn’t listen to Moses for anguish of spirit, and for cruel bondage.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** “Go in, speak to Pharaoh king of Egypt, that he let the children of Israel go out of his land.”

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Moses spoke before Yahweh, saying, “Behold, the children of Israel haven’t listened to me. How then shall Pharaoh listen to me, who am of uncircumcised lips?”

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Yahweh spoke to Moses and to Aaron, and gave them a command to the children of Israel, and to Pharaoh king of Egypt, to bring the children of Israel out of the land of Egypt.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** These are the heads of their fathers’ houses. The sons of Reuben the firstborn of Israel: Hanoch, and Pallu, Hezron, and Carmi; these are the families of Reuben.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** The sons of Simeon: Jemuel, and Jamin, and Ohad, and Jachin, and Zohar, and Shaul the son of a Canaanite woman; these are the families of Simeon.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** These are the names of the sons of Levi according to their generations: Gershon, and Kohath, and Merari; and the years of the life of Levi were one hundred thirty-seven years.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The sons of Gershon: Libni and Shimei, according to their families.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** The sons of Kohath: Amram, and Izhar, and Hebron, and Uzziel; and the years of the life of Kohath were one hundred thirty-three years.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The sons of Merari: Mahli and Mushi. These are the families of the Levites according to their generations.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Amram took Jochebed his father’s sister to himself as wife; and she bore him Aaron and Moses: and the years of the life of Amram were a hundred and thirty-seven years.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The sons of Izhar: Korah, and Nepheg, and Zichri.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The sons of Uzziel: Mishael, and Elzaphan, and Sithri.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Aaron took Elisheba, the daughter of Amminadab, the sister of Nahshon, as his wife; and she bore him Nadab and Abihu, Eleazar and Ithamar.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** The sons of Korah: Assir, and Elkanah, and Abiasaph; these are the families of the Korahites.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Eleazar Aaron’s son took one of the daughters of Putiel as his wife; and she bore him Phinehas. These are the heads of the fathers’ houses of the Levites according to their families.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** These are that Aaron and Moses, to whom Yahweh said, “Bring out the children of Israel from the land of Egypt according to their armies.”

<a id="v27"></a>**27** These are those who spoke to Pharaoh king of Egypt, to bring out the children of Israel from Egypt. These are that Moses and Aaron.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** On the day when Yahweh spoke to Moses in the land of Egypt,

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying, “I am Yahweh. Speak to Pharaoh king of Egypt all that I speak to you.”

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Moses said before Yahweh, “Behold, I am of uncircumcised lips, and how shall Pharaoh listen to me?”
