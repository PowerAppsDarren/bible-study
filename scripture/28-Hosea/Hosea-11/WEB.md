# Hosea 11 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** “When Israel was a child, then I loved him,  and called my son out of Egypt.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** They called to them, so they went from them. They sacrificed to the Baals, and burned incense to engraved images.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Yet I taught Ephraim to walk. I took them by his arms; but they didn’t know that I healed them.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I drew them with cords of a man, with ties of love; and I was to them like those who lift up the yoke on their necks; and I bent down to him and I fed him.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** “They won’t return into the land of Egypt; but the Assyrian will be their king, because they refused to repent.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The sword will fall on their cities, and will destroy the bars of their gates, and will put an end to their plans.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** My people are determined to turn from me. Though they call to the Most High, he certainly won’t exalt them.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** “How can I give you up, Ephraim? How can I hand you over, Israel? How can I make you like Admah? How can I make you like Zeboiim? My heart is turned within me, my compassion is aroused.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I will not execute the fierceness of my anger. I will not return to destroy Ephraim: for I am God, and not man; the Holy One in your midst; and I will not come in wrath.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** They will walk after Yahweh, who will roar like a lion; for he will roar, and the children will come trembling from the west.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** They will come trembling like a bird out of Egypt, and like a dove out of the land of Assyria; and I will settle them in their houses,” says Yahweh.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Ephraim surrounds me with falsehood, and the house of Israel with deceit. Judah still strays from God, and is unfaithful to the Holy One.
