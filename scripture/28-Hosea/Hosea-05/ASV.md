# Hosea 5 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear this, O ye priests, and hearken, O house of Israel, and give ear, O house of the king; for unto you pertaineth the judgment; for ye have been a snare at Mizpah, and a net spread upon Tabor.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And the revolters are gone deep in making slaughter; but I am a rebuker of them all.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I know Ephraim, and Israel is not hid from me; for now, O Ephraim, thou hast played the harlot, Israel is defiled.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their doings will not suffer them to turn unto their God; for the spirit of whoredom is within them, and they know not Jehovah.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And the pride of Israel doth testify to his face: therefore Israel and Ephraim shall stumble in their iniquity; Judah also shall stumble with them.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They shall go with their flocks and with their herds to seek Jehovah; but they shall not find him: he hath withdrawn himself from them.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They have dealt treacherously against Jehovah; for they have borne strange children: now shall the new moon devour them with their fields.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Blow ye the cornet in Gibeah, and the trumpet in Ramah: sound an alarm at Beth-aven; behind thee, O Benjamin.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Ephraim shall become a desolation in the day of rebuke: among the tribes of Israel have I made known that which shall surely be.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The princes of Judah are like them that remove the landmark: I will pour out my wrath upon them like water.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Ephraim is oppressed, he is crushed in judgment; because he was content to walk after man’s command.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Therefore am I unto Ephraim as a moth, and to the house of Judah as rottenness.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** When Ephraim saw his sickness, and Judah saw his wound, then went Ephraim to Assyria, and sent to king Jareb: but he is not able to heal you, neither will he cure you of your wound.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For I will be unto Ephraim as a lion, and as a young lion to the house of Judah: I, even I, will tear and go away; I will carry off, and there shall be none to deliver.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I will go and return to my place, till they acknowledge their offence, and seek my face: in their affliction they will seek me earnestly.
