# Hosea 5 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** “Listen to this, you priests!  Listen, house of Israel, and give ear, house of the king! For the judgment is against you; for you have been a snare at Mizpah, and a net spread on Tabor.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The rebels are deep in slaughter; but I discipline all of them.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I know Ephraim, and Israel is not hidden from me; for now, Ephraim, you have played the prostitute. Israel is defiled.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Their deeds won’t allow them to turn to their God; for the spirit of prostitution is within them, and they don’t know Yahweh.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The pride of Israel testifies to his face. Therefore Israel and Ephraim will stumble in their iniquity. Judah also will stumble with them.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** They will go with their flocks and with their herds to seek Yahweh; but they won’t find him. He has withdrawn himself from them.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They are unfaithful to Yahweh; for they have borne illegitimate children. Now the new moon will devour them with their fields.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** “Blow the cornet in Gibeah, and the trumpet in Ramah! Sound a battle cry at Beth Aven, behind you, Benjamin!

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Ephraim will become a desolation in the day of rebuke. Among the tribes of Israel, I have made known that which will surely be.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The princes of Judah are like those who remove a landmark. I will pour out my wrath on them like water.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Ephraim is oppressed, he is crushed in judgment; Because he is intent in his pursuit of idols.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Therefore I am to Ephraim like a moth, and to the house of Judah like rottenness.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** “When Ephraim saw his sickness, and Judah his wound, Then Ephraim went to Assyria, and sent to king Jareb: but he is not able to heal you, neither will he cure you of your wound.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For I will be to Ephraim like a lion, and like a young lion to the house of Judah. I myself will tear in pieces and go away. I will carry off, and there will be no one to deliver.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I will go and return to my place, until they acknowledge their offense, and seek my face. In their affliction they will seek me earnestly.”
