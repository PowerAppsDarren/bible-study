# Hosea 6 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Come, and let us return unto Jehovah; for he hath torn, and he will heal us; he hath smitten, and he will bind us up.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** After two days will he revive us: on the third day he will raise us up, and we shall live before him.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And let us know, let us follow on to know Jehovah: his going forth is sure as the morning; and he will come unto us as the rain, as the latter rain that watereth the earth.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** O Ephraim, what shall I do unto thee? O Judah, what shall I do unto thee? for your goodness is as a morning cloud, and as the dew that goeth early away.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Therefore have I hewed them by the prophets; I have slain them by the words of my mouth: and thy judgments are as the light that goeth forth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For I desire goodness, and not sacrifice; and the knowledge of God more than burnt-offerings.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But they like Adam have transgressed the covenant: there have they dealt treacherously against me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Gilead is a city of them that work iniquity; it is stained with blood.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And as troops of robbers wait for a man, so the company of priests murder in the way toward Shechem; yea, they have committed lewdness.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** In the house of Israel I have seen a horrible thing: there whoredom is found in Ephraim, Israel is defiled.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Also, O Judah, there is a harvest appointed for thee, when I bring back the captivity of my people.
