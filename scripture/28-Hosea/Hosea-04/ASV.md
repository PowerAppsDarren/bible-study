# Hosea 4 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Hear the word of Jehovah, ye children of Israel; for Jehovah hath a controversy with the inhabitants of the land, because there is no truth, nor goodness, nor knowledge of God in the land.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** There is nought but swearing and breaking faith, and killing, and stealing, and committing adultery; they break out, and blood toucheth blood.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Therefore shall the land mourn, and every one that dwelleth therein shall languish, with the beasts of the field and the birds of the heavens; yea, the fishes of the sea also shall be taken away.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Yet let no man strive, neither let any man reprove; for thy people are as they that strive with the priest.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And thou shalt stumble in the day, and the prophet also shall stumble with thee in the night; and I will destroy thy mother.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** My people are destroyed for lack of knowledge: because thou hast rejected knowledge, I will also reject thee, that thou shalt be no priest to me: seeing thou hast forgotten the law of thy God, I also will forget thy children.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** As they were multiplied, so they sinned against me: I will change their glory into shame.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They feed on the sin of my people, and set their heart on their iniquity.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And it shall be, like people, like priest; and I will punish them for their ways, and will requite them their doings.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And they shall eat, and not have enough; they shall play the harlot, and shall not increase; because they have left off taking heed to Jehovah.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Whoredom and wine and new wine take away the understanding.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** My people ask counsel at their stock, and their staff declareth unto them; for the spirit of whoredom hath caused them to err, and they have played the harlot, departing from under their God.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** They sacrifice upon the tops of the mountains, and burn incense upon the hills, under oaks and poplars and terebinths, because the shadow thereof is good: therefore your daughters play the harlot, and your brides commit adultery.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I will not punish your daughters when they play the harlot, nor your brides when they commit adultery; for the men themselves go apart with harlots, and they sacrifice with the prostitutes; and the people that doth not understand shall be overthrown.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Though thou, Israel, play the harlot, yet let not Judah offend; and come not ye unto Gilgal, neither go ye up to Beth-aven, nor swear, As Jehovah liveth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For Israel hath behaved himself stubbornly, like a stubborn heifer: now will Jehovah feed them as a lamb in a large place.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Ephraim is joined to idols; let him alone.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Their drink is become sour; they play the harlot continually; her rulers dearly love shame.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The wind hath wrapped her up in its wings; and they shall be put to shame because of their sacrifices.
