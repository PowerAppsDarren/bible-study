# Hosea 8 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Put the horn to your mouth. He comes like an eagle against the house of the Lord; because they have gone against my agreement, they have not kept my law.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** They will send up to me a cry for help: We, Israel, have knowledge of you, O God of Israel.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Israel has given up what is good; his haters will go after him.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They have put up kings, but not by me; they have made princes, but I had no knowledge of it; they have made images of silver and gold, so that they may be cut off.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I will have nothing to do with your young ox, O Samaria; my wrath is burning against them; how long will it be before the children of Israel make themselves clean?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The workman made it, it is no god; the ox of Samaria will be broken into bits.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** For they have been planting the wind, and their fruit will be the storm; his grain has no stem, it will give no meal, and if it does, a strange nation will take it.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Israel has come to destruction; now they are among the nations like a cup in which there is no pleasure.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For they have gone up to Assyria like an ass going by himself; Ephraim has given money to get lovers.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But though they give money to the nations for help, still I will send them in all directions; and in a short time they will be without a king and rulers.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Because Ephraim has been increasing altars for sin, altars have become a cause of sin to him.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Though I put my law in writing for him in ten thousand rules, they are to him as a strange thing.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He gives the offerings of his lovers, and takes the flesh for food; but the Lord has no pleasure in them; now he will keep in mind their evil-doing and give them the punishment of their sins; they will go back to Egypt.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For Israel has no memory of his Maker, and has put up the houses of kings; and Judah has made great the number of his walled towns. But I will send a fire on his towns and put an end to his great houses.
