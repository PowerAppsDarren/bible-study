# Hosea 7 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** When I would heal Israel,  then the iniquity of Ephraim is uncovered, also the wickedness of Samaria; for they commit falsehood, and the thief enters in, and the gang of robbers ravages outside.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** They don’t consider in their hearts that I remember all their wickedness. Now their own deeds have engulfed them. They are before my face.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They make the king glad with their wickedness, and the princes with their lies.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They are all adulterers. They are burning like an oven that the baker stops stirring, from the kneading of the dough, until it is leavened.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** On the day of our king, the princes made themselves sick with the heat of wine. He joined his hand with mockers.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** For they have prepared their heart like an oven, while they lie in wait. Their baker sleeps all the night. In the morning it burns as a flaming fire.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They are all hot as an oven, and devour their judges. All their kings have fallen. There is no one among them who calls to me.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Ephraim, he mixes himself among the nations. Ephraim is a pancake not turned over.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Strangers have devoured his strength, and he doesn’t realize it. Indeed, gray hairs are here and there on him, and he doesn’t realize it.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The pride of Israel testifies to his face; yet they haven’t returned to Yahweh their God, nor sought him, for all this.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** “Ephraim is like an easily deceived dove, without understanding. They call to Egypt. They go to Assyria.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** When they go, I will spread my net on them. I will bring them down like the birds of the sky. I will chastise them, as their congregation has heard.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Woe to them! For they have wandered from me. Destruction to them! For they have trespassed against me. Though I would redeem them, yet they have spoken lies against me.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** They haven’t cried to me with their heart, but they howl on their beds. They assemble themselves for grain and new wine. They turn away from me.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Though I have taught and strengthened their arms, yet they plot evil against me.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** They return, but not to the Most High. They are like a faulty bow. Their princes will fall by the sword for the rage of their tongue. This will be their derision in the land of Egypt.
