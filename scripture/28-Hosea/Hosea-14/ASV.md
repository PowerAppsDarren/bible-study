# Hosea 14 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** O Israel, return unto Jehovah thy God; for thou hast fallen by thine iniquity.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Take with you words, and return unto Jehovah: say unto him, Take away all iniquity, and accept that which is good: so will we render as bullocks the offering of our lips.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Assyria shall not save us; we will not ride upon horses; neither will we say any more to the work of our hands, Ye are our gods; for in thee the fatherless findeth mercy.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will heal their backsliding, I will love them freely; for mine anger is turned away from him.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I will be as the dew unto Israel; he shall blossom as the lily, and cast forth his roots as Lebanon.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** His branches shall spread, and his beauty shall be as the olive-tree, and his smell as Lebanon.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** They that dwell under his shadow shall return; they shall revive as the grain, and blossom as the vine: the scent thereof shall be as the wine of Lebanon.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Ephraim shall say, What have I to do any more with idols? I have answered, and will regard him: I am like a green fir-tree; from me is thy fruit found.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Who is wise, that he may understand these things? prudent, that he may know them? for the ways of Jehovah are right, and the just shall walk in them; but transgressors shall fall therein.
