# Hosea 14 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Israel, return to Yahweh your God; for you have fallen because of your sin.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Take words with you, and return to Yahweh. Tell him, “Forgive all our sins, and accept that which is good: so we offer our lips like bulls.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Assyria can’t save us. We won’t ride on horses; neither will we say any more to the work of our hands, ‘Our gods!’ for in you the fatherless finds mercy.”

<a id="v4"></a>**4** “I will heal their waywardness. I will love them freely; for my anger is turned away from him.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I will be like the dew to Israel. He will blossom like the lily, and send down his roots like Lebanon.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** His branches will spread, and his beauty will be like the olive tree, and his fragrance like Lebanon.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Men will dwell in his shade. They will revive like the grain, and blossom like the vine. Their fragrance will be like the wine of Lebanon.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Ephraim, what have I to do any more with idols? I answer, and will take care of him. I am like a green fir tree; from me your fruit is found.”

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Who is wise, that he may understand these things? Who is prudent, that he may know them? For the ways of Yahweh are right, and the righteous walk in them; But the rebellious stumble in them.
