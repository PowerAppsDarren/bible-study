# Hosea 10 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Israel is a luxuriant vine, that putteth forth his fruit: according to the abundance of his fruit he hath multiplied his altars; according to the goodness of their land they have made goodly pillars.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Their heart is divided; now shall they be found guilty: he will smite their altars, he will destroy their pillars.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Surely now shall they say, We have no king; for we fear not Jehovah; and the king, what can he do for us?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** They speakvainwords, swearing falsely in making covenants: therefore judgment springeth up as hemlock in the furrows of the field.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The inhabitants of Samaria shall be in terror for the calves of Beth-aven; for the people thereof shall mourn over it, and the priests thereof that rejoiced over it, for the glory thereof, because it is departed from it.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** It also shall be carried unto Assyria for a present to king Jareb: Ephraim shall receive shame, and Israel shall be ashamed of his own counsel.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** As for Samaria, her king is cut off, as foam upon the water.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The high places also of Aven, the sin of Israel, shall be destroyed: the thorn and the thistle shall come up on their altars; and they shall say to the mountains, Cover us; and to the hills, Fall on us.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** O Israel, thou hast sinned from the days of Gibeah: there they stood; the battle against the children of iniquity doth not overtake them in Gibeah.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** When it is my desire, I will chastise them; and the peoples shall be gathered against them, when they are bound to their two transgressions.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And Ephraim is a heifer that is taught, that loveth to tread out the grain; but I have passed over upon her fair neck: I will set a rider on Ephraim; Judah shall plow, Jacob shall break his clods.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Sow to yourselves in righteousness, reap according to kindness; break up your fallow ground; for it is time to seek Jehovah, till he come and rain righteousness upon you.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Ye have plowed wickedness, ye have reaped iniquity; ye have eaten the fruit of lies; for thou didst trust in thy way, in the multitude of thy mighty men.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Therefore shall a tumult arise among thy people, and all thy fortresses shall be destroyed, as Shalman destroyed Beth-arbel in the day of battle: the mother was dashed in pieces with her children.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** So shall Beth-el do unto you because of your great wickedness: at daybreak shall the king of Israel be utterly cut off.
