# 1 Kings 4 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** So king Solomon was king over all Israel.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And these were the princes which he had; Azariah the son of Zadok the priest,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Elihoreph and Ahiah, the sons of Shisha, scribes; Jehoshaphat the son of Ahilud, the recorder.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And Benaiah the son of Jehoiada was over the host: and Zadok and Abiathar were the priests:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And Azariah the son of Nathan was over the officers: and Zabud the son of Nathan was principal officer, and the king’s friend:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And Ahishar was over the household: and Adoniram the son of Abda was over the tribute.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And Solomon had twelve officers over all Israel, which provided victuals for the king and his household: each man his month in a year made provision.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And these are their names: The son of Hur, in mount Ephraim:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The son of Dekar, in Makaz, and in Shaalbim, and Beth–shemesh, and Elon–beth–hanan:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The son of Hesed, in Aruboth; to him pertained Sochoh, and all the land of Hepher:

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The son of Abinadab, in all the region of Dor; which had Taphath the daughter of Solomon to wife:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Baana the son of Ahilud; to him pertained Taanach and Megiddo, and all Beth–shean, which is by Zartanah beneath Jezreel, from Beth–shean to Abel–meholah, even unto the place that is beyond Jokneam:

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The son of Geber, in Ramoth–gilead; to him pertained the towns of Jair the son of Manasseh, which are in Gilead; to him also pertained the region of Argob, which is in Bashan, threescore great cities with walls and brasen bars:

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Ahinadab the son of Iddo had Mahanaim:

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Ahimaaz was in Naphtali; he also took Basmath the daughter of Solomon to wife:

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Baanah the son of Hushai was in Asher and in Aloth:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Jehoshaphat the son of Paruah, in Issachar:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Shimei the son of Elah, in Benjamin:

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Geber the son of Uri was in the country of Gilead, in the country of Sihon king of the Amorites, and of Og king of Bashan; and he was the only officer which was in the land.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Judah and Israel were many, as the sand which is by the sea in multitude, eating and drinking, and making merry.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And Solomon reigned over all kingdoms from the river unto the land of the Philistines, and unto the border of Egypt: they brought presents, and served Solomon all the days of his life.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And Solomon’s provision for one day was thirty measures of fine flour, and threescore measures of meal,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Ten fat oxen, and twenty oxen out of the pastures, and an hundred sheep, beside harts, and roebucks, and fallowdeer, and fatted fowl.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** For he had dominion over all the region on this side the river, from Tiphsah even to Azzah, over all the kings on this side the river: and he had peace on all sides round about him.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And Judah and Israel dwelt safely, every man under his vine and under his fig tree, from Dan even to Beer–sheba, all the days of Solomon.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And Solomon had forty thousand stalls of horses for his chariots, and twelve thousand horsemen.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** And those officers provided victual for king Solomon, and for all that came unto king Solomon’s table, every man in his month: they lacked nothing.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Barley also and straw for the horses and dromedaries brought they unto the place where the officers were, every man according to his charge.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And God gave Solomon wisdom and understanding exceeding much, and largeness of heart, even as the sand that is on the sea shore.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** And Solomon’s wisdom excelled the wisdom of all the children of the east country, and all the wisdom of Egypt.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For he was wiser than all men; than Ethan the Ezrahite, and Heman, and Chalcol, and Darda, the sons of Mahol: and his fame was in all nations round about.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And he spake three thousand proverbs: and his songs were a thousand and five.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** And he spake of trees, from the cedar tree that is in Lebanon even unto the hyssop that springeth out of the wall: he spake also of beasts, and of fowl, and of creeping things, and of fishes.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** And there came of all people to hear the wisdom of Solomon, from all kings of the earth, which had heard of his wisdom.
