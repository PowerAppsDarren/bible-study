# 1 Kings 4 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And king Solomon was king over all Israel.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And these were the princes whom he had: Azariah the son of Zadok, the priest;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Elihoreph and Ahijah, the sons of Shisha, scribes; Jehoshaphat the son of Ahilud, the recorder;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** and Benaiah the son of Jehoiada was over the host; and Zadok and Abiathar were priests;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** and Azariah the son of Nathan was over the officers; and Zabud the son of Nathan was chief minister, and the king’s friend;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** and Ahishar was over the household; and Adoniram the son of Abda was over the men subject to taskwork.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And Solomon had twelve officers over all Israel, who provided victuals for the king and his household: each man had to make provision for a month in the year.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And these are their names: Ben-hur, in the hill-country of Ephraim;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Ben-deker, in Makaz, and in Shaalbim, and Beth-shemesh, and Elon-beth-hanan;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Ben-hesed, in Arubboth (to him pertained Socoh, and all the land of Hepher);

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Ben-abinadab, in all the height of Dor (he had Taphath the daughter of Solomon to wife);

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Baana the son of Ahilud, in Taanach and Megiddo, and all Beth-shean which is beside Zarethan, beneath Jezreel, from Beth-shean to Abel-meholah, as far as beyond Jokmeam;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Ben-geber, in Ramoth-gilead (to him pertained the towns of Jair the son of Manasseh, which are in Gilead; even to him pertained the region of Argob, which is in Bashan, threescore great cities with walls and brazen bars);

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Ahinadab the son of Iddo, in Mahanaim;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Ahimaaz, in Naphtali (he also took Basemath the daughter of Solomon to wife);

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Baana the son of Hushai, in Asher and Bealoth;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Jehoshaphat the son of Paruah, in Issachar;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Shimei the son of Ela, in Benjamin;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Geber the son of Uri, in the land of Gilead, the country of Sihon king of the Amorites and of Og king of Bashan; and he was the only officer that was in the land.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Judah and Israel were many as the sand which is by the sea in multitude, eating and drinking and making merry.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And Solomon ruled over all the kingdoms from the River unto the land of the Philistines, and unto the border of Egypt: they brought tribute, and served Solomon all the days of his life.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And Solomon’s provision for one day was thirty measures of fine flour, and threescore measures of meal,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** ten fat oxen, and twenty oxen out of the pastures, and a hundred sheep, besides harts, and gazelles, and roebucks, and fatted fowl.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** For he had dominion over all the region on this side the River, from Tiphsah even to Gaza, over all the kings on this side the River: and he had peace on all sides round about him.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And Judah and Israel dwelt safely, every man under his vine and under his fig-tree, from Dan even to Beer-sheba, all the days of Solomon.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And Solomon had forty thousand stalls of horses for his chariots, and twelve thousand horsemen.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** And those officers provided victuals for king Solomon, and for all that came unto king Solomon’s table, every man in his month; they let nothing be lacking.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Barley also and straw for the horses and swift steeds brought they unto the place where the officers were, every man according to his charge.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And God gave Solomon wisdom and understanding exceeding much, and largeness of heart, even as the sand that is on the sea-shore.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** And Solomon’s wisdom excelled the wisdom of all the children of the east, and all the wisdom of Egypt.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For he was wiser than all men; than Ethan the Ezrahite, and Heman, and Calcol, and Darda, the sons of Mahol: and his fame was in all the nations round about.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And he spake three thousand proverbs; and his songs were a thousand and five.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** And he spake of trees, from the cedar that is in Lebanon even unto the hyssop that springeth out of the wall; he spake also of beasts, and of birds, and of creeping things, and of fishes.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** And there came of all peoples to hear the wisdom of Solomon, from all kings of the earth, who had heard of his wisdom.
