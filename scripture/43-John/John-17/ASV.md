# John 17 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** These things spake Jesus; and lifting up his eyes to heaven, he said, Father, the hour is come; glorify thy Son, that the Son may glorify thee:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** even as thou gavest him authority over all flesh, that to all whom thou hast given him, he should give eternal life.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And this is life eternal, that they should know thee the only true God, and him whom thou didst send, even Jesus Christ.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I glorified thee on the earth, having accomplished the work which thou hast given me to do.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And now, Father, glorify thou me with thine own self with the glory which I had with thee before the world was.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** I manifested thy name unto the men whom thou gavest me out of the world: thine they were, and thou gavest them to me; and they have kept thy word.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Now they know that all things whatsoever thou hast given me are from thee:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** for the words which thou gavest me I have given unto them; and they received them, and knew of a truth that I came forth from thee, and they believed that thou didst send me.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I pray for them: I pray not for the world, but for those whom thou hast given me; for they are thine:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** and all things that are mine are thine, and thine are mine: and I am glorified in them.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And I am no more in the world, and these are in the world, and I come to thee. Holy Father, keep them in thy name which thou hast given me, that they may be one, even as we are.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** While I was with them, I kept them in thy name which thou hast given me: and I guarded them, and not one of them perished, but the son of perdition; that the scripture might be fulfilled.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But now I come to thee; and these things I speak in the world, that they may have my joy made full in themselves.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I have given them thy word; and the world hated them, because they are not of the world, even as I am not of the world.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I pray not that thou shouldest take them from the world, but that thou shouldest keep them from the evil one.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** They are not of the world, even as I am not of the world.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Sanctify them in the truth: thy word is truth.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** As thou didst send me into the world, even so sent I them into the world.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And for their sakes I sanctify myself, that they themselves also may be sanctified in truth.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Neither for these only do I pray, but for them also that believe on me through their word;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** that they may all be one; even as thou, Father, art in me, and I in thee, that they also may be in us: that the world may believe that thou didst send me.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And the glory which thou hast given me I have given unto them; that they may be one, even as we are one;

<a id="v23"></a>**23** I in them, and thou in me, that they may be perfected into one; that the world may know that thou didst send me, and lovedst them, even as thou lovedst me.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Father, I desire that they also whom thou hast given me be with me where I am, that they may behold my glory, which thou hast given me: for thou lovedst me before the foundation of the world.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** O righteous Father, the world knew thee not, but I knew thee; and these knew that thou didst send me;

<a id="v26"></a>**26** and I made known unto them thy name, and will make it known; that the love wherewith thou lovedst me may be in them, and I in them.
