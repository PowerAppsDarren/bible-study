# John 16 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** These things have I spoken unto you, that ye should not be caused to stumble.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** They shall put you out of the synagogues: yea, the hour cometh, that whosoever killeth you shall think that he offereth service unto God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And these things will they do, because they have not known the Father, nor me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But these things have I spoken unto you, that when their hour is come, ye may remember them, how that I told you. And these things I said not unto you from the beginning, because I was with you.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But now I go unto him that sent me; and none of you asketh me, Whither goest thou?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** But because I have spoken these things unto you, sorrow hath filled your heart.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Nevertheless I tell you the truth: It is expedient for you that I go away; for if I go not away, the Comforter will not come unto you; but if I go, I will send him unto you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And he, when he is come, will convict the world in respect of sin, and of righteousness, and of judgment:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** of sin, because they believe not on me;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** of righteousness, because I go to the Father, and ye behold me no more;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** of judgment, because the prince of this world hath been judged.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I have yet many things to say unto you, but ye cannot bear them now.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Howbeit when he, the Spirit of truth, is come, he shall guide you into all the truth: for he shall not speak from himself; but what things soever he shall hear, these shall he speak: and he shall declare unto you the things that are to come.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He shall glorify me: for he shall take of mine, and shall declare it unto you.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** All things whatsoever the Father hath are mine: therefore said I, that he taketh of mine, and shall declare it unto you.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** A little while, and ye behold me no more; and again a little while, and ye shall see me.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Some of his disciples therefore said one to another, What is this that he saith unto us, A little while, and ye behold me not; and again a little while, and ye shall see me: and, Because I go to the Father?

<a id="v18"></a>**18** They said therefore, What is this that he saith, A little while? We know not what he saith.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Jesus perceived that they were desirous to ask him, and he said unto them, Do ye inquire among yourselves concerning this, that I said, A little while, and ye behold me not, and again a little while, and ye shall see me?

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Verily, verily, I say unto you, that ye shall weep and lament, but the world shall rejoice: ye shall be sorrowful, but your sorrow shall be turned into joy.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** A woman when she is in travail hath sorrow, because her hour is come: but when she is delivered of the child, she remembereth no more the anguish, for the joy that a man is born into the world.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And ye therefore now have sorrow: but I will see you again, and your heart shall rejoice, and your joy no one taketh away from you.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And in that day ye shall ask me no question. Verily, verily, I say unto you, If ye shall ask anything of the Father, he will give it you in my name.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Hitherto have ye asked nothing in my name: ask, and ye shall receive, that your joy may be made full.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** These things have I spoken unto you in dark sayings: the hour cometh, when I shall no more speak unto you in dark sayings, but shall tell you plainly of the Father.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** In that day ye shall ask in my name: and I say not unto you, that I will pray the Father for you;

<a id="v27"></a>**27** for the Father himself loveth you, because ye have loved me, and have believed that I came forth from the Father.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** I came out from the Father, and am come into the world: again, I leave the world, and go unto the Father.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** His disciples say, Lo, now speakest thou plainly, and speakest no dark saying.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Now know we that thou knowest all things, and needest not that any man should ask thee: by this we believe that thou camest forth from God.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Jesus answered them, Do ye now believe?

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Behold, the hour cometh, yea, is come, that ye shall be scattered, every man to his own, and shall leave me alone: and yet I am not alone, because the Father is with me.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** These things have I spoken unto you, that in me ye may have peace. In the world ye have tribulation: but be of good cheer; I have overcome the world.
