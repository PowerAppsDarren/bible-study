# John 16 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** “These things have I spoken to you, so that you wouldn’t be caused to stumble.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** They will put you out of the synagogues. Yes, the time comes that whoever kills you will think that he offers service to God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They will do these thingsbecause they have not known the Father, nor me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But I have told you these things, so that when the time comes, you may remember that I told you about them. I didn’t tell you these things from the beginning, because I was with you.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But now I am going to him who sent me, and none of you asks me, ‘Where are you going?’

<a id="v6"></a>**6** But because I have told you these things, sorrow has filled your heart.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Nevertheless I tell you the truth: It is to your advantage that I go away, for if I don’t go away, the Counselor won’t come to you. But if I go, I will send him to you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** When he has come, he will convict the world about sin, about righteousness, and about judgment;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** about sin, because they don’t believe in me;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** about righteousness, because I am going to my Father, and you won’t see me any more;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** about judgment, because the prince of this world has been judged.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** “I have yet many things to tell you, but you can’t bear them now.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** However when he, the Spirit of truth, has come, he will guide you into all truth, for he will not speak from himself; but whatever he hears, he will speak. He will declare to you things that are coming.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He will glorify me, for he will take from what is mine, and will declare it to you.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** All things whatever the Father has are mine; therefore I said that he takesof mine, and will declare it to you.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** A little while, and you will not see me. Again a little while, and you will see me.”

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Some of his disciples therefore said to one another, “What is this that he says to us, ‘A little while, and you won’t see me, and again a little while, and you will see me;’ and, ‘Because I go to the Father’?”

<a id="v18"></a>**18** They said therefore, “What is this that he says, ‘A little while’? We don’t know what he is saying.”

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Therefore Jesus perceived that they wanted to ask him, and he said to them, “Do you inquire among yourselves concerning this, that I said, ‘A little while, and you won’t see me, and again a little while, and you will see me?’

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Most certainly I tell you, that you will weep and lament, but the world will rejoice. You will be sorrowful, but your sorrow will be turned into joy.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** A woman, when she gives birth, has sorrow, because her time has come. But when she has delivered the child, she doesn’t remember the anguish any more, for the joy that a human being is born into the world.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Therefore you now have sorrow, but I will see you again, and your heart will rejoice, and no one will take your joy away from you.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** “In that day you will ask me no questions. Most certainly I tell you, whatever you may ask of the Father in my name, he will give it to you.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Until now, you have asked nothing in my name. Ask, and you will receive, that your joy may be made full.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** I have spoken these things to you in figures of speech. But the time is coming when I will no more speak to you in figures of speech, but will tell you plainly about the Father.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** In that day you will ask in my name; and I don’t say to you, that I will pray to the Father for you,

<a id="v27"></a>**27** for the Father himself loves you, because you have loved me, and have believed that I came from God.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** I came from the Father, and have come into the world. Again, I leave the world, and go to the Father.”

<a id="v29"></a>**29** His disciples said to him, “Behold, now you speak plainly, and speak no figures of speech.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Now we know that you know all things, and don’t need for anyone to question you. By this we believe that you came from God.”

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Jesus answered them, “Do you now believe?

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Behold, the time is coming, yes, and has now come, that you will be scattered, everyone to his own place, and you will leave me alone. Yet I am not alone, because the Father is with me.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** I have told you these things, that in me you may have peace. In the world you have oppression; but cheer up! I have overcome the world.”
