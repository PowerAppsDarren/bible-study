# John 16 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I have said these things to you so that you may not be in doubt.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** They will put you out of the Synagogues: yes, the time is coming when whoever puts you to death will have the belief that he is doing God's pleasure.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They will do these things to you because they have not had knowledge of the Father or of me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I have said these things to you so that when the time comes, what I have said may come to your mind. I did not say them to you at the first, because then I was still with you.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But now I am going to him who sent me; and not one of you says to me, Where are you going?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** But your hearts are full of sorrow because I have said these things.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But what I am saying is true: my going is for your good: for if I do not go away, the Helper will not come to you; but if I go, I will send him to you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And he, when he comes, will make the world conscious of sin, and of righteousness, and of being judged:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Of sin, because they have not faith in me;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Of righteousness, because I go to the Father and you will see me no more;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Of being judged, because the ruler of this world has been judged.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I have still much to say to you, but you are not strong enough for it now.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** However, when he, the Spirit of true knowledge, has come, he will be your guide into all true knowledge: for his words will not come from himself, but whatever has come to his hearing, that he will say: and he will make clear to you the things to come.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He will give me glory, because he will take of what is mine, and make it clear to you.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Everything which the Father has is mine: that is why I say, He will take of what is mine and will make it clear to you.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** After a little time you will see me no longer; and then again, after a little time, you will see me.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** So some of the disciples said one to another, What is this he is saying, After a little time, you will see me no longer; and then again, after a little time, you will see me? and, I am going to the Father?

<a id="v18"></a>**18** So they said again and again, What is this he is saying, A little time? His words are not clear to us.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Jesus saw that they had a desire to put the question to him, so he said to them, Is this what you are questioning one with another, why I said, After a little time, you will see me no longer; and then again, after a little time, you will see me?

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Truly I say to you, You will be weeping and sorrowing, but the world will be glad: you will be sad, but your sorrow will be turned into joy.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** When a woman is about to give birth she has sorrow, because her hour is come; but when she has given birth to the child, the pain is put out of her mind by the joy that a man has come into the world.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** So you have sorrow now: but I will see you again, and your hearts will be glad, and no one will take away your joy.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And on that day you will put no questions to me. Truly I say to you, Whatever request you make to the Father, he will give it to you in my name.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Up to now you have made no request in my name: do so, and it will be answered, so that your hearts may be full of joy.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** All this I have said to you in veiled language: but the time is coming when I will no longer say things in veiled language but will give you knowledge of the Father clearly.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** In that day you will make requests in my name: and I do not say that I will make prayer to the Father for you,

<a id="v27"></a>**27** For the Father himself gives his love to you, because you have given your love to me and have had faith that I came from God.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** I came out from the Father and have come into the world: again, I go away from the world and go to the Father.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** His disciples said, Now you are talking clearly and not in veiled language.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Now we are certain that you have knowledge of all things and have no need for anyone to put questions to you: through this we have faith that you came from God.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Jesus made answer, Have you faith now?

<a id="v32"></a>**32** See, a time is coming, yes, it is now here, when you will go away in all directions, every man to his house, and I will be by myself: but I am not by myself, because the Father is with me.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** I have said all these things to you so that in me you may have peace. In the world you have trouble: but take heart! I have overcome the world.
