# John 15 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** “I am the true vine, and my Father is the farmer.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Every branch in me that doesn’t bear fruit, he takes away. Every branch that bears fruit, he prunes, that it may bear more fruit.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** You are already pruned clean because of the word which I have spoken to you.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Remain in me, and I in you. As the branch can’t bear fruit by itself, unless it remains in the vine, so neither can you, unless you remain in me.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I am the vine. You are the branches. He who remains in me, and I in him, the same bears much fruit, for apart from me you can do nothing.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** If a man doesn’t remain in me, he is thrown out as a branch, and is withered; and they gather them, throw them into the fire, and they are burned.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** If you remain in me, and my words remain in you, you will ask whatever you desire, and it will be done for you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** “In this is my Father glorified, that you bear much fruit; and so you will be my disciples.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Even as the Father has loved me, I also have loved you. Remain in my love.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** If you keep my commandments, you will remain in my love; even as I have kept my Father’s commandments, and remain in his love.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I have spoken these things to you, that my joy may remain in you, and that your joy may be made full.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** “This is my commandment, that you love one another, even as I have loved you.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Greater love has no one than this, that someone lay down his life for his friends.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** You are my friends, if you do whatever I command you.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** No longer do I call you servants, for the servant doesn’t know what his lord does. But I have called you friends, for everything that I heard from my Father, I have made known to you.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** You didn’t choose me, but I chose you, and appointed you, that you should go and bear fruit, and that your fruit should remain; that whatever you will ask of the Father in my name, he may give it to you.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** “I command these things to you, that you may love one another.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** If the world hates you, you know that it has hated me before it hated you.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** If you were of the world, the world would love its own. But because you are not of the world, since I chose you out of the world, therefore the world hates you.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Remember the word that I said to you: ‘A servant is not greater than his lord.’If they persecuted me, they will also persecute you. If they kept my word, they will keep yours also.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** But all these things will they do to you for my name’s sake, because they don’t know him who sent me.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** If I had not come and spoken to them, they would not have had sin; but now they have no excuse for their sin.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** He who hates me, hates my Father also.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** If I hadn’t done among them the works which no one else did, they wouldn’t have had sin. But now have they seen and also hated both me and my Father.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** But this happened so that the word may be fulfilled which was written in their law, ‘They hated me without a cause.’

<a id="v26"></a>**26** “When the Counselorhas come, whom I will send to you from the Father, the Spirit of truth, who proceeds from the Father, he will testify about me.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** You will also testify, because you have been with me from the beginning.
