# John 15 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** I am the true vine, and my Father is the husbandman.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Every branch in me that beareth not fruit, he taketh it away: and every branch that beareth fruit, he cleanseth it, that it may bear more fruit.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Already ye are clean because of the word which I have spoken unto you.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Abide in me, and I in you. As the branch cannot bear fruit of itself, except it abide in the vine; so neither can ye, except ye abide in me.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I am the vine, ye are the branches: He that abideth in me, and I in him, the same beareth much fruit: for apart from me ye can do nothing.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** If a man abide not in me, he is cast forth as a branch, and is withered; and they gather them, and cast them into the fire, and they are burned.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** If ye abide in me, and my words abide in you, ask whatsoever ye will, and it shall be done unto you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Herein is my Father glorified, that ye bear much fruit; and so shall ye be my disciples.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Even as the Father hath loved me, I also have loved you: abide ye in my love.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** If ye keep my commandments, ye shall abide in my love; even as I have kept my Father’s commandments, and abide in his love.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** These things have I spoken unto you, that my joy may be in you, and that your joy may be made full.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** This is my commandment, that ye love one another, even as I have loved you.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Greater love hath no man than this, that a man lay down his life for his friends.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Ye are my friends, if ye do the things which I command you.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** No longer do I call you servants; for the servant knoweth not what his lord doeth: but I have called you friends; for all things that I heard from my Father I have made known unto you.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Ye did not choose me, but I chose you, and appointed you, that ye should go and bear fruit, and that your fruit should abide: that whatsoever ye shall ask of the Father in my name, he may give it you.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** These things I command you, that ye may love one another.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** If the world hateth you, ye know that it hath hated me before it hated you.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** If ye were of the world, the world would love its own: but because ye are not of the world, but I chose you out of the world, therefore the world hateth you.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Remember the word that I said unto you, A servant is not greater than his lord. If they persecuted me, they will also persecute you; if they kept my word, they will keep yours also.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** But all these things will they do unto you for my name’s sake, because they know not him that sent me.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** If I had not come and spoken unto them, they had not had sin: but now they have no excuse for their sin.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** He that hateth me hateth my Father also.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** If I had not done among them the works which none other did, they had not had sin: but now have they both seen and hated both me and my Father.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** But this cometh to pass, that the word may be fulfilled that is written in their law, They hated me without a cause.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** But when the Comforter is come, whom I will send unto you from the Father, even the Spirit of truth, which proceedeth from the Father, he shall bear witness of me:

<a id="v27"></a>**27** and ye also bear witness, because ye have been with me from the beginning.
