# John 8 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** but Jesus went unto the mount of Olives.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And early in the morning he came again into the temple, and all the people came unto him; and he sat down, and taught them.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And the scribes and the Pharisees bring a woman taken in adultery; and having set her in the midst,

<a id="v4"></a>**4** they say unto him, Teacher, this woman hath been taken in adultery, in the very act.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Now in the law Moses commanded us to stone such: what then sayest thou of her?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And this they said, trying him, that they might have whereof to accuse him. But Jesus stooped down, and with his finger wrote on the ground.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But when they continued asking him, he lifted up himself, and said unto them, He that is without sin among you, let him first cast a stone at her.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And again he stooped down, and with his finger wrote on the ground.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And they, when they heard it, went out one by one, beginning from the eldest, even unto the last: and Jesus was left alone, and the woman, where she was, in the midst.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And Jesus lifted up himself, and said unto her, Woman, where are they? did no man condemn thee?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And she said, No man, Lord. And Jesus said, Neither do I condemn thee: go thy way; from henceforth sin no more.]

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Again therefore Jesus spake unto them, saying, I am the light of the world: he that followeth me shall not walk in the darkness, but shall have the light of life.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The Pharisees therefore said unto him, Thou bearest witness of thyself; thy witness is not true.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Jesus answered and said unto them, Even if I bear witness of myself, my witness is true; for I know whence I came, and whither I go; but ye know not whence I come, or whither I go.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Ye judge after the flesh; I judge no man.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Yea and if I judge, my judgment is true; for I am not alone, but I and the Father that sent me.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Yea and in your law it is written, that the witness of two men is true.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** I am he that beareth witness of myself, and the Father that sent me beareth witness of me.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** They said therefore unto him, Where is thy Father? Jesus answered, Ye know neither me, nor my Father: if ye knew me, ye would know my Father also.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** These words spake he in the treasury, as he taught in the temple: and no man took him; because his hour was not yet come.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He said therefore again unto them, I go away, and ye shall seek me, and shall die in your sin: whither I go, ye cannot come.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** The Jews therefore said, Will he kill himself, that he saith, Whither I go, ye cannot come?

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And he said unto them, Ye are from beneath; I am from above: ye are of this world; I am not of this world.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** I said therefore unto you, that ye shall die in your sins: for except ye believe that I am he, ye shall die in your sins.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** They said therefore unto him, Who art thou? Jesus said unto them, Even that which I have also spoken unto you from the beginning.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** I have many things to speak and to judge concerning you: howbeit he that sent me is true; and the things which I heard from him, these speak I unto the world.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** They perceived not that he spake to them of the Father.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Jesus therefore said, When ye have lifted up the Son of man, then shall ye know that I am he, and that I do nothing of myself, but as the Father taught me, I speak these things.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And he that sent me is with me; he hath not left me alone; for I do always the things that are pleasing to him.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** As he spake these things, many believed on him.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Jesus therefore said to those Jews that had believed him, If ye abide in my word, then are ye truly my disciples;

<a id="v32"></a>**32** and ye shall know the truth, and the truth shall make you free.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** They answered unto him, We are Abraham’s seed, and have never yet been in bondage to any man: how sayest thou, Ye shall be made free?

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Jesus answered them, Verily, verily, I say unto you, Every one that committeth sin is the bondservant of sin.

<a id="v35"></a>**35** And the bondservant abideth not in the house for ever: the son abideth for ever.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** If therefore the Son shall make you free, ye shall be free indeed.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** I know that ye are Abraham’s seed; yet ye seek to kill me, because my word hath not free course in you.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** I speak the things which I have seen with my Father: and ye also do the things which ye heard from your father.

<a id="v39"></a>**39** They answered and said unto him, Our father is Abraham. Jesus saith unto them, If ye were Abraham’s children, ye would do the works of Abraham.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** But now ye seek to kill me, a man that hath told you the truth, which I heard from God: this did not Abraham.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** Ye do the works of your father. They said unto him, We were not born of fornication; we have one Father, even God.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** Jesus said unto them, If God were your Father, ye would love me: for I came forth and am come from God; for neither have I come of myself, but he sent me.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Why do ye not understand my speech? Even because ye cannot hear my word.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** Ye are of your father the devil, and the lusts of your father it is your will to do. He was a murderer from the beginning, and standeth not in the truth, because there is no truth in him. When he speaketh a lie, he speaketh of his own: for he is a liar, and the father thereof.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** But because I say the truth, ye believe me not.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** Which of you convicteth me of sin? If I say truth, why do ye not believe me?

<a id="v47"></a>**47** He that is of God heareth the words of God: for this cause ye hear them not, because ye are not of God.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** The Jews answered and said unto him, Say we not well that thou art a Samaritan, and hast a demon?

<a id="v49"></a>**49** Jesus answered, I have not a demon; but I honor my Father, and ye dishonor me.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** But I seek not mine own glory: there is one that seeketh and judgeth.

<a id="v51"></a>**51** Verily, verily, I say unto you, If a man keep my word, he shall never see death.

<a id="v52"></a>**52** The Jews said unto him, Now we know that thou hast a demon. Abraham died, and the prophets; and thou sayest, If a man keep my word, he shall never taste of death.

<a id="v53"></a>**53** Art thou greater than our father Abraham, who died? and the prophets died: whom makest thou thyself?

<a id="v54"></a>**54** Jesus answered, If I glorify myself, my glory is nothing: it is my Father that glorifieth me; of whom ye say, that he is your God;

<a id="v55"></a>**55** and ye have not known him: but I know him; and if I should say, I know him not, I shall be like unto you, a liar: but I know him, and keep his word.

<a id="v56"></a>**56** Your father Abraham rejoiced to see my day; and he saw it, and was glad.

<a id="v57"></a>**57** The Jews therefore said unto him, Thou art not yet fifty years old, and hast thou seen Abraham?

<a id="v58"></a>**58** Jesus said unto them, Verily, verily, I say unto you, Before Abraham was born, I am.

<a id="v59"></a>**59** They took up stones therefore to cast at him: but Jesus hid himself, and went out of the temple.
