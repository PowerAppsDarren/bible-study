# John 12 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Jesus therefore six days before the passover came to Bethany, where Lazarus was, whom Jesus raised from the dead.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** So they made him a supper there: and Martha served; but Lazarus was one of them that sat at meat with him.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Mary therefore took a pound of ointment of pure nard, very precious, and anointed the feet of Jesus, and wiped his feet with her hair: and the house was filled with the odor of the ointment.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But Judas Iscariot, one of his disciples, that should betray him, saith,

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Why was not this ointment sold for three hundred shillings, and given to the poor?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Now this he said, not because he cared for the poor; but because he was a thief, and having the bag took away what was put therein.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Jesus therefore said, Suffer her to keep it against the day of my burying.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For the poor ye have always with you; but me ye have not always.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The common people therefore of the Jews learned that he was there: and they came, not for Jesus’ sake only, but that they might see Lazarus also, whom he had raised from the dead.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But the chief priests took counsel that they might put Lazarus also to death;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** because that by reason of him many of the Jews went away, and believed on Jesus.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** On the morrow a great multitude that had come to the feast, when they heard that Jesus was coming to Jerusalem,

<a id="v13"></a>**13** took the branches of the palm trees, and went forth to meet him, and cried out, Hosanna: Blessed is he that cometh in the name of the Lord, even the King of Israel.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And Jesus, having found a young ass, sat thereon; as it is written,

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Fear not, daughter of Zion: behold, thy King cometh, sitting on an ass’s colt.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** These things understood not his disciples at the first: but when Jesus was glorified, then remembered they that these things were written of him, and that they had done these things unto him.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The multitude therefore that was with him when he called Lazarus out of the tomb, and raised him from the dead, bare witness.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For this cause also the multitude went and met him, for that they heard that he had done this sign.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The Pharisees therefore said among themselves, Behold how ye prevail nothing; lo, the world is gone after him.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Now there were certain Greeks among those that went up to worship at the feast:

<a id="v21"></a>**21** these therefore came to Philip, who was of Bethsaida of Galilee, and asked him, saying, Sir, we would see Jesus.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Philip cometh and telleth Andrew: Andrew cometh, and Philip, and they tell Jesus.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** And Jesus answereth them, saying, The hour is come, that the Son of man should be glorified.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Verily, verily, I say unto you, Except a grain of wheat fall into the earth and die, it abideth by itself alone; but if it die, it beareth much fruit.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** He that loveth his life loseth it; and he that hateth his life in this world shall keep it unto life eternal.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** If any man serve me, let him follow me; and where I am, there shall also my servant be: if any man serve me, him will the Father honor.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Now is my soul troubled; and what shall I say? Father, save me from this hour. But for this cause came I unto this hour.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Father, glorify thy name. There came therefore a voice out of heaven, saying, I have both glorified it, and will glorify it again.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** The multitude therefore, that stood by, and heard it, said that it had thundered: others said, An angel hath spoken to him.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** Jesus answered and said, This voice hath not come for my sake, but for your sakes.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Now is the judgment of this world: now shall the prince of this world be cast out.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And I, if I be lifted up from the earth, will draw all men unto myself.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** But this he said, signifying by what manner of death he should die.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** The multitude therefore answered him, We have heard out of the law that the Christ abideth for ever: and how sayest thou, The Son of man must be lifted up? who is this Son of man?

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Jesus therefore said unto them, Yet a little while is the light among you. Walk while ye have the light, that darkness overtake you not: and he that walketh in the darkness knoweth not whither he goeth.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** While ye have the light, believe on the light, that ye may become sons of light. These things spake Jesus, and he departed and hid himself from them.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** But though he had done so many signs before them, yet they believed not on him:

<a id="v38"></a>**38** that the word of Isaiah the prophet might be fulfilled, which he spake,  Lord, who hath believed our report? And to whom hath the arm of the Lord been revealed?

<a id="v39"></a>**39** For this cause they could not believe, for that Isaiah said again,

<a id="v40"></a>**40** He hath blinded their eyes, and he hardened their heart; Lest they should see with their eyes, and perceive with their heart, And should turn, And I should heal them.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** These things said Isaiah, because he saw his glory; and he spake of him.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** Nevertheless even of the rulers many believed on him; but because of the Pharisees they did not confess it, lest they should be put out of the synagogue:

<a id="v43"></a>**43** for they loved the glory that is of men more than the glory that is of God.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** And Jesus cried and said, He that believeth on me, believeth not on me, but on him that sent me.

<a id="v45"></a>**45** And he that beholdeth me beholdeth him that sent me.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** I am come a light into the world, that whosoever believeth on me may not abide in the darkness.

<a id="v47"></a>**47** And if any man hear my sayings, and keep them not, I judge him not: for I came not to judge the world, but to save the world.

<a id="v48"></a>**48** He that rejecteth me, and receiveth not my sayings, hath one that judgeth him: the word that I spake, the same shall judge him in the last day.

<a id="v49"></a>**49** For I spake not from myself; but the Father that sent me, he hath given me a commandment, what I should say, and what I should speak.

<a id="v50"></a>**50** And I know that his commandment is life eternal; the things therefore which I speak, even as the Father hath said unto me, so I speak.
