# John 14 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Let not your heart be troubled: believe in God, believe also in me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** In my Father’s house are many mansions; if it were not so, I would have told you; for I go to prepare a place for you.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And if I go and prepare a place for you, I come again, and will receive you unto myself; that where I am, there ye may be also.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And whither I go, ye know the way.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Thomas saith unto him, Lord, we know not whither thou goest; how know we the way?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, and the truth, and the life: no one cometh unto the Father, but by me.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** If ye had known me, ye would have known my Father also: from henceforth ye know him, and have seen him.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Philip saith unto him, Lord, show us the Father, and it sufficeth us.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Jesus saith unto him, Have I been so long time with you, and dost thou not know me, Philip? he that hath seen me hath seen the Father; how sayest thou, Show us the Father?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Believest thou not that I am in the Father, and the Father in me? the words that I say unto you I speak not from myself: but the Father abiding in me doeth his works.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Believe me that I am in the Father, and the Father in me: or else believe me for the very works’ sake.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Verily, verily, I say unto you, He that believeth on me, the works that I do shall he do also; and greater works than these shall he do; because I go unto the Father.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And whatsoever ye shall ask in my name, that will I do, that the Father may be glorified in the Son.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** If ye shall ask anything in my name, that will I do.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** If ye love me, ye will keep my commandments.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And I will pray the Father, and he shall give you another Comforter, that he may be with you for ever,

<a id="v17"></a>**17** even the Spirit of truth: whom the world cannot receive; for it beholdeth him not, neither knoweth him: ye know him; for he abideth with you, and shall be in you.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** I will not leave you desolate: I come unto you.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Yet a little while, and the world beholdeth me no more; but ye behold me: because I live, ye shall live also.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** In that day ye shall know that I am in my Father, and ye in me, and I in you.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He that hath my commandments, and keepeth them, he it is that loveth me: and he that loveth me shall be loved of my Father, and I will love him, and will manifest myself unto him.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Judas (not Iscariot) saith unto him, Lord, what is come to pass that thou wilt manifest thyself unto us, and not unto the world?

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Jesus answered and said unto him, If a man love me, he will keep my word: and my Father will love him, and we will come unto him, and make our abode with him.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** He that loveth me not keepeth not my words: and the word which ye hear is not mine, but the Father’s who sent me.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** These things have I spoken unto you, while yet abiding with you.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** But the Comforter, even the Holy Spirit, whom the Father will send in my name, he shall teach you all things, and bring to your remembrance all that I said unto you.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Peace I leave with you; my peace I give unto you: not as the world giveth, give I unto you. Let not your heart be troubled, neither let it be fearful.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Ye heard how I said to you, I go away, and I come unto you. If ye loved me, ye would have rejoiced, because I go unto the Father: for the Father is greater than I.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And now I have told you before it come to pass, that, when it is come to pass, ye may believe.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** I will no more speak much with you, for the prince of the world cometh: and he hath nothing in me;

<a id="v31"></a>**31** but that the world may know that I love the Father, and as the Father gave me commandment, even so I do. Arise, let us go hence.
