# Job 36 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Elihu went on to say,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Give me a little more time, and I will make it clear to you; for I have still something to say for God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** I will get my knowledge from far, and I will give righteousness to my Maker.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For truly my words are not false; one who has all knowledge is talking with you.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Truly, God gives up the hard-hearted, and will not give life to the sinner.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** His eyes are ever on the upright, and he gives to the crushed their right;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Lifting them up to the seat of kings, and making them safe for ever.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And if they have been prisoned in chains, and taken in cords of trouble,

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Then he makes clear to them what they have done, even their evil works in which they have taken pride.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Their ear is open to his teaching, and he gives them orders so that their hearts may be turned from evil.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** If they give ear to his voice, and do his word, then he gives them long life, and years full of pleasure.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But if not, they come to their end, and give up their breath without knowledge.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Those who have no fear of God keep wrath stored up in their hearts; they give no cry for help when they are made prisoners.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** They come to their end while they are still young, their life is short like that of those who are used for sex purposes in the worship of their gods.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He makes the wrong done to the poor the way of their salvation, opening their ears by their trouble.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Take care not to be turned to sin, for you have taken evil for your part in place of sorrow.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Truly God is lifted up in strength; who is a ruler like him?

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Who ever gave orders to him, or said to him, You have done wrong?

<a id="v24"></a>**24** See that you give praise to his work, about which men make songs.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** All people are looking on it; man sees it from far.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Truly, God is great, greater than all our knowledge; the number of his years may not be searched out.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** For he takes up the drops from the sea; he sends them through his mist as rain,

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Flowing down from the sky, and dropping on the peoples.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And who has knowledge of how the clouds are stretched out, or of the thunders of his tent?

<a id="v30"></a>**30** See, he is stretching out his mist, covering the tops of the mountains with it.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For by these he gives food to the peoples, and bread in full measure.

<a id="v32"></a>**32** He takes the light in his hands, sending it against the mark.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** The thunder makes clear his passion, and the storm gives news of his wrath.
