# Job 18 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then answered Bildad the Shuhite, and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How long will ye hunt for words? Consider, and afterwards we will speak.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Wherefore are we counted as beasts, Andare become unclean in your sight?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Thou that tearest thyself in thine anger, Shall the earth be forsaken for thee? Or shall the rock be removed out of its place?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Yea, the light of the wicked shall be put out, And the spark of his fire shall not shine.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The light shall be dark in his tent, And his lamp above him shall be put out.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The steps of his strength shall be straitened, And his own counsel shall cast him down.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For he is cast into a net by his own feet, And he walketh upon the toils.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** A gin shall take him by the heel, Anda snare shall lay hold on him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** A noose is hid for him in the ground, And a trap for him in the way.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Terrors shall make him afraid on every side, And shall chase him at his heels.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** His strength shall be hunger-bitten, And calamity shall be ready at his side.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The members of his body shall be devoured, Yea, the first-born of death shall devour his members.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He shall be rooted out of his tent wherein he trusteth; And he shall be brought to the king of terrors.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** There shall dwell in his tent that which is none of his: Brimstone shall be scattered upon his habitation.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** His roots shall be dried up beneath, And above shall his branch be cut off.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** His remembrance shall perish from the earth, And he shall have no name in the street.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He shall be driven from light into darkness, And chased out of the world.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He shall have neither son nor son’s son among his people, Nor any remaining where he sojourned.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** They that come after shall be astonished at his day, As they that went before were affrighted.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Surely such are the dwellings of the unrighteous, And this is the place of him that knoweth not God.
