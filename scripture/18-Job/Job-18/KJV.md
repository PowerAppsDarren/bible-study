# Job 18 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then answered Bildad the Shuhite, and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How long will it be ere ye make an end of words? mark, and afterwards we will speak.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Wherefore are we counted as beasts, and reputed vile in your sight?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He teareth himself in his anger: shall the earth be forsaken for thee? and shall the rock be removed out of his place?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Yea, the light of the wicked shall be put out, and the spark of his fire shall not shine.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The light shall be dark in his tabernacle, and his candle shall be put out with him.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The steps of his strength shall be straitened, and his own counsel shall cast him down.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For he is cast into a net by his own feet, and he walketh upon a snare.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The gin shall take him by the heel, and the robber shall prevail against him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** The snare is laid for him in the ground, and a trap for him in the way.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Terrors shall make him afraid on every side, and shall drive him to his feet.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** His strength shall be hungerbitten, and destruction shall be ready at his side.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** It shall devour the strength of his skin: even the firstborn of death shall devour his strength.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** His confidence shall be rooted out of his tabernacle, and it shall bring him to the king of terrors.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** It shall dwell in his tabernacle, because it is none of his: brimstone shall be scattered upon his habitation.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** His roots shall be dried up beneath, and above shall his branch be cut off.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** His remembrance shall perish from the earth, and he shall have no name in the street.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He shall be driven from light into darkness, and chased out of the world.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He shall neither have son nor nephew among his people, nor any remaining in his dwellings.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** They that come after him shall be astonied at his day, as they that went before were affrighted.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Surely such are the dwellings of the wicked, and this is the place of him that knoweth not God.
