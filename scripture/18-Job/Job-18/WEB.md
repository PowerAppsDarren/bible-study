# Job 18 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Bildad the Shuhite answered,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** “How long will you hunt for words? Consider, and afterwards we will speak.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Why are we counted as animals, which have become unclean in your sight?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You who tear yourself in your anger, shall the earth be forsaken for you? Or shall the rock be removed out of its place?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** “Yes, the light of the wicked shall be put out, The spark of his fire shall not shine.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The light shall be dark in his tent. His lamp above him shall be put out.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** The steps of his strength shall be shortened. His own counsel shall cast him down.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For he is cast into a net by his own feet, and he wanders into its mesh.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** A snare will take him by the heel. A trap will catch him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** A noose is hidden for him in the ground, a trap for him on the path.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Terrors shall make him afraid on every side, and shall chase him at his heels.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** His strength shall be famished. Calamity shall be ready at his side.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** The members of his body shall be devoured. The firstborn of death shall devour his members.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He shall be rooted out of his tent where he trusts. He shall be brought to the king of terrors.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** There shall dwell in his tent that which is none of his. Sulfur shall be scattered on his habitation.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** His roots shall be dried up beneath. Above shall his branch be cut off.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** His memory shall perish from the earth. He shall have no name in the street.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He shall be driven from light into darkness, and chased out of the world.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He shall have neither son nor grandson among his people, nor any remaining where he lived.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Those who come after shall be astonished at his day, as those who went before were frightened.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Surely such are the dwellings of the unrighteous. This is the place of him who doesn’t know God.”
