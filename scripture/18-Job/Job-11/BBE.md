# Job 11 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Zophar the Naamathite made answer and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Are all these words to go unanswered? and is a man seen to be right because he is full of talk?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Are your words of pride to make men keep quiet? and are you to make sport, with no one to put you to shame?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You may say, My way is clean, and I am free from sin in your eyes.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But if only God would take up the word, opening his lips in argument with you;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And would make clear to you the secrets of wisdom, and the wonders of his purpose!

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Are you able to take God's measure, to make discovery of the limits of the Ruler of all?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** They are higher than heaven; what is there for you to do? deeper than the underworld, and outside your knowledge;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Longer in measure than the earth, and wider than the sea.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** If he goes on his way, shutting a man up and putting him to death, who may make him go back from his purpose?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For in his eyes men are as nothing; he sees evil and takes note of it.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And so a hollow-minded man will get wisdom, when a young ass of the field gets teaching.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But if you put your heart right, stretching out your hands to him;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** If you put far away the evil of your hands, and let no wrongdoing have a place in your tent;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Then truly your face will be lifted up, with no mark of sin, and you will be fixed in your place without fear:

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For your sorrow will go from your memory, like waters flowing away:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And your life will be brighter than day; though it is dark, it will become like the morning.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And you will be safe because there is hope; after looking round, you will take your rest in quiet;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Sleeping with no fear of danger; and men will be desiring to have grace in your eyes;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** But the eyes of the evil-doers will be wasting away; their way of flight is gone, and their only hope is the taking of their last breath.
