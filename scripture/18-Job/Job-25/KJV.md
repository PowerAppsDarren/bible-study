# Job 25 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then answered Bildad the Shuhite, and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Dominion and fear are with him, he maketh peace in his high places.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Is there any number of his armies? and upon whom doth not his light arise?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** How then can man be justified with God? or how can he be clean that is born of a woman?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Behold even to the moon, and it shineth not; yea, the stars are not pure in his sight.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** How much less man, that is a worm? and the son of man, which is a worm?
