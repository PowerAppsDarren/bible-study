# Job 25 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Bildad the Shuhite answered,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** “Dominion and fear are with him. He makes peace in his high places.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Can his armies be counted? On whom does his light not arise?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** How then can man be just with God? Or how can he who is born of a woman be clean?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Behold, even the moon has no brightness, and the stars are not pure in his sight;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** How much less man, who is a worm, the son of man, who is a worm!”
