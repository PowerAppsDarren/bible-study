# Job 25 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Bildad the Shuhite made answer and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Rule and power are his; he makes peace in his high places.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Is it possible for his armies to be numbered? and on whom is not his light shining?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** How then is it possible for man to be upright before God? or how may he be clean who is a son of woman?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** See, even the moon is not bright, and the stars are not clean in his eyes:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** How much less man who is an insect, and the son of man who is a worm!
