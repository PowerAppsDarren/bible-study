# Job 32 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** So these three men ceased to answer Job, because he was righteous in his own eyes.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Then was kindled the wrath of Elihu the son of Barachel the Buzite, of the family of Ram: against Job was his wrath kindled, because he justified himself rather than God.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Also against his three friends was his wrath kindled, because they had found no answer, and yet had condemned Job.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Now Elihu had waited to speak unto Job, because they were elder than he.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And when Elihu saw that there was no answer in the mouth of these three men, his wrath was kindled.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And Elihu the son of Barachel the Buzite answered and said,  I am young, and ye are very old; Wherefore I held back, and durst not show you mine opinion.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I said, Days should speak, And multitude of years should teach wisdom.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But there is a spirit in man, And the breath of the Almighty giveth them understanding.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** It is not the great that are wise, Nor the aged that understand justice.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Therefore I said, Hearken to me; I also will show mine opinion.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Behold, I waited for your words, I listened for your reasonings, Whilst ye searched out what to say.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yea, I attended unto you, And, behold, there was none that convinced Job, Or that answered his words, among you.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Beware lest ye say, We have found wisdom; God may vanquish him, not man:

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For he hath not directed his words against me; Neither will I answer him with your speeches.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** They are amazed, they answer no more: They have not a word to say.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And shall I wait, because they speak not, Because they stand still, and answer no more?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I also will answer my part, I also will show mine opinion.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For I am full of words; The spirit within me constraineth me.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Behold, my breast is as wine which hath no vent; Like new wine-skins it is ready to burst.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** I will speak, that I may be refreshed; I will open my lips and answer.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Let me not, I pray you, respect any man’s person; Neither will I give flattering titles unto any man.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For I know not to give flattering titles; Elsewould my Maker soon take me away.
