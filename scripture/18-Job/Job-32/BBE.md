# Job 32 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** So these three men gave no more answers to Job, because he seemed to himself to be right.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And Elihu, the son of Barachel the Buzite, of the family of Ram, was angry, burning with wrath against Job, because he seemed to himself more right than God;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And he was angry with his three friends, because they had been unable to give him an answer, and had not made Job's sin clear.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Now Elihu had kept quiet while Job was talking, because they were older than he;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And when Elihu saw that there was no answer in the mouth of the three men, he was very angry.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And Elihu, the son of Barachel the Buzite, made answer and said, I am young, and you are very old, so I was in fear, and kept myself from putting my knowledge before you.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** I said to myself, It is right for the old to say what is in their minds, and for those who are far on in years to give out wisdom.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** But truly it is the spirit in man, even the breath of the Ruler of all, which gives them knowledge.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** It is not the old who are wise, and those who are full of years have not the knowledge of what is right.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** So I say, Give ear to me, and I will put forward my knowledge.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** I was waiting for your words, I was giving ear to your wise sayings; while you were searching out what to say,

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I was taking note; and truly not one of you was able to make clear Job's error, or to give an answer to his words.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Take care that you do not say, Wisdom is here; God may overcome him, but not man.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I will not put forward words like these, or make use of your sayings in answer to him.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Fear has overcome them, they have no more answers to give; they have come to an end of words.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And am I to go on waiting while they have nothing to say? while they keep quiet and give no more answers?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** I will give my answer; I will put forward my knowledge:

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For I am full of words, I am unable to keep in my breath any longer:

<a id="v19"></a>**19** My stomach is like wine which is unable to get out; like skins full of new wine, it is almost burst.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Let me say what is in my mind, so that I may get comfort; let me give answer with open mouth.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Let me not give respect to any man, or give names of honour to any living.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For I am not able to give names of honour to any man; and if I did, my Maker would quickly take me away.
