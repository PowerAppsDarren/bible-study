# Job 17 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** “My spirit is consumed. My days are extinct, And the grave is ready for me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Surely there are mockers with me. My eye dwells on their provocation.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** “Now give a pledge, be collateral for me with yourself. Who is there who will strike hands with me?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For you have hidden their heart from understanding, Therefore you shall not exalt them.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He who denounces his friends for plunder, Even the eyes of his children shall fail.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** “But he has made me a byword of the people. They spit in my face.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** My eye also is dim by reason of sorrow. All my members are as a shadow.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Upright men shall be astonished at this. The innocent shall stir up himself against the godless.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Yet shall the righteous hold on his way. He who has clean hands shall grow stronger and stronger.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But as for you all, come on now again; I shall not find a wise man among you.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My days are past, my plans are broken off, as are the thoughts of my heart.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They change the night into day, saying ‘The light is near’ in the presence of darkness.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** If I look for Sheol as my house, if I have spread my couch in the darkness,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** If I have said to corruption, ‘You are my father;’ to the worm, ‘My mother,’ and ‘my sister;’

<a id="v15"></a>**15** where then is my hope? as for my hope, who shall see it?

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Shall it go down with me to the gates of Sheol, or descend together into the dust?”
