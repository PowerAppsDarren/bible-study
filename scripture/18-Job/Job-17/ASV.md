# Job 17 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My spirit is consumed, my days are extinct, The grave isreadyfor me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Surely there are mockers with me, And mine eye dwelleth upon their provocation.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Give now a pledge, be surety for me with thyself; Who is there that will strike hands with me?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For thou hast hid their heart from understanding: Therefore shalt thou not exalt them.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He that denounceth his friends for a prey, Even the eyes of his children shall fail.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** But he hath made me a byword of the people; And they spit in my face.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Mine eye also is dim by reason of sorrow, And all my members are as a shadow.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Upright men shall be astonished at this, And the innocent shall stir up himself against the godless.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Yet shall the righteous hold on his way, And he that hath clean hands shall wax stronger and stronger.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But as for you all, come on now again; And I shall not find a wise man among you.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My days are past, my purposes are broken off, Even the thoughts of my heart.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They change the night into day: The light, say they, is near unto the darkness.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** If I look for Sheol as my house; If I have spread my couch in the darkness;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** If I have said to corruption, Thou art my father; To the worm, Thou artmy mother, and my sister;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Where then is my hope? And as for my hope, who shall see it?

<a id="v16"></a>**16** It shall go down to the bars of Sheol, When once there is rest in the dust.
