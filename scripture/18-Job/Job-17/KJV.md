# Job 17 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My breath is corrupt, my days are extinct, the graves are ready for me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Are there not mockers with me? and doth not mine eye continue in their provocation?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Lay down now, put me in a surety with thee; who is he that will strike hands with me?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** For thou hast hid their heart from understanding: therefore shalt thou not exalt them.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** He that speaketh flattery to his friends, even the eyes of his children shall fail.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He hath made me also a byword of the people; and aforetime I was as a tabret.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Mine eye also is dim by reason of sorrow, and all my members are as a shadow.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Upright men shall be astonied at this, and the innocent shall stir up himself against the hypocrite.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** The righteous also shall hold on his way, and he that hath clean hands shall be stronger and stronger.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But as for you all, do ye return, and come now: for I cannot find one wise man among you.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My days are past, my purposes are broken off, even the thoughts of my heart.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They change the night into day: the light is short because of darkness.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** If I wait, the grave is mine house: I have made my bed in the darkness.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I have said to corruption, Thou art my father: to the worm, Thou art my mother, and my sister.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And where is now my hope? as for my hope, who shall see it?

<a id="v16"></a>**16** They shall go down to the bars of the pit, when our rest together is in the dust.
