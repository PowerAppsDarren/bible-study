# Job 17 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** My spirit is broken, my days are ended, the last resting-place is ready for me.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Truly, those who make sport of me are round about me, and my eyes become dark because of their bitter laughing.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Be pleased, now, to be responsible for me to yourself; for there is no other who will put his hand in mine.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** You have kept their hearts from wisdom: for this cause you will not give them honour.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** As for him who is false to his friend for a reward, light will be cut off from the eyes of his children.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He has made me a word of shame to the peoples; I have become a mark for their sport.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** My eyes have become dark because of my pain, and all my body is wasted to a shade.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The upright are surprised at this, and he who has done no wrong is troubled because of the evil-doers.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Still the upright keeps on his way, and he who has clean hands gets new strength.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But come back, now, all of you, come; and I will not see a wise man among you.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My days are past, my purposes are broken off, even the desires of my heart.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** They are changing night into day; they say, The light is near the dark.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** If I am waiting for the underworld as my house, if I have made my bed in the dark;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** If I say to the earth, You are my father; and to the worm, My mother and my sister;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Where then is my hope? and who will see my desire?

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Will they go down with me into the underworld? Will we go down together into the dust?
