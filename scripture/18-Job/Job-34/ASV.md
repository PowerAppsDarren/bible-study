# Job 34 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Moreover Elihu answered and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Hear my words, ye wise men; And give ear unto me, ye that have knowledge.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For the ear trieth words, As the palate tasteth food.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Let us choose for us that which is right: Let us know among ourselves what is good.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** For Job hath said, I am righteous, And God hath taken away my right:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Notwithstanding my right I am accounted a liar; My wound is incurable, though I amwithout transgression.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** What man is like Job, Who drinketh up scoffing like water,

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Who goeth in company with the workers of iniquity, And walketh with wicked men?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** For he hath said, It profiteth a man nothing That he should delight himself with God.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Therefore hearken unto me, ye men of understanding: Far be it from God, that he should do wickedness, And from the Almighty, that he should commit iniquity.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** For the work of a man will he render unto him, And cause every man to find according to his ways.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Yea, of a surety, God will not do wickedly, Neither will the Almighty pervert justice.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Who gave him a charge over the earth? Or who hath disposed the whole world?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** If he set his heart upon himself, Ifhe gather unto himself his spirit and his breath;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** All flesh shall perish together, And man shall turn again unto dust.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** If nowthou hastunderstanding, hear this: Hearken to the voice of my words.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Shall even one that hateth justice govern? And wilt thou condemn him that is righteous and mighty?—

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Himthat saith to a king, Thou art vile, Orto nobles, Ye arewicked;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** That respecteth not the persons of princes, Nor regardeth the rich more than the poor; For they all are the work of his hands.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** In a moment they die, even at midnight; The people are shaken and pass away, And the mighty are taken away without hand.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** For his eyes are upon the ways of a man, And he seeth all his goings.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** There is no darkness, nor thick gloom, Where the workers of iniquity may hide themselves.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** For he needeth not further to consider a man, That he should go before God in judgment.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** He breaketh in pieces mighty men in ways past finding out, And setteth others in their stead.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Therefore he taketh knowledge of their works; And he overturneth them in the night, so that they are destroyed.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** He striketh them as wicked men In the open sight of others;

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Because they turned aside from following him, And would not have regard in any of his ways:

<a id="v28"></a>**28** So that they caused the cry of the poor to come unto him, And he heard the cry of the afflicted.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** When he giveth quietness, who then can condemn? And when he hideth his face, who then can behold him? Alike whether it be done unto a nation, or unto a man:

<a id="v30"></a>**30** That the godless man reign not, That there be none to ensnare the people.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** For hath any said unto God, I have bornechastisement, I will not offend any more:

<a id="v32"></a>**32** That which I see not teach thou me: If I have done iniquity, I will do it no more?

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Shall his recompense be as thou wilt, that thou refusest it? For thou must choose, and not I: Therefore speak what thou knowest.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Men of understanding will say unto me, Yea, every wise man that heareth me:

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Job speaketh without knowledge, And his words are without wisdom.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Would that Job were tried unto the end, Because of his answering like wicked men.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** For he addeth rebellion unto his sin; He clappeth his hands among us, And multiplieth his words against God.
