# Job 38 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And the Lord made answer to Job out of the storm-wind, and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Who is this who makes the purpose of God dark by words without knowledge?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Get your strength together like a man of war; I will put questions to you, and you will give me the answers.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Where were you when I put the earth on its base? Say, if you have knowledge.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** By whom were its measures fixed? Say, if you have wisdom; or by whom was the line stretched out over it?

<a id="v6"></a>**6** On what were its pillars based, or who put down its angle-stone,

<a id="v7"></a>**7** When the morning stars made songs together, and all the sons of the gods gave cries of joy?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Or where were you when the sea came to birth, pushing out from its secret place;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** When I made the cloud its robe, and put thick clouds as bands round it,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Ordering a fixed limit for it, with locks and doors;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And said, So far you may come, and no farther; and here the pride of your waves will be stopped?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Have you, from your earliest days, given orders to the morning, or made the dawn conscious of its place;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** So that it might take a grip of the skirts of the earth, shaking all the evil-doers out of it?

<a id="v14"></a>**14** It is changed like wet earth under a stamp, and is coloured like a robe;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And from the evil-doers their light is kept back, and the arm of pride is broken.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Have you come into the springs of the sea, walking in the secret places of the deep?

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Have the doors of death been open to you, or have the door-keepers of the dark ever seen you?

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Have you taken note of the wide limits of the earth? Say, if you have knowledge of it all.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Which is the way to the resting-place of the light, and where is the store-house of the dark;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** So that you might take it to its limit, guiding it to its house?

<a id="v21"></a>**21** No doubt you have knowledge of it, for then you had come to birth, and the number of your days is great.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Have you come into the secret place of snow, or have you seen the store-houses of the ice-drops,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Which I have kept for the time of trouble, for the day of war and fighting?

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Which is the way to the place where the wind is measured out, and the east wind sent out over the earth?

<a id="v25"></a>**25** By whom has the way been cut for the flowing of the rain, and the flaming of the thunder;

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Causing rain to come on a land where no man is living, on the waste land which has no people;

<a id="v27"></a>**27** To give water to the land where there is waste and destruction, and to make the dry land green with young grass?

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Has the rain a father? or who gave birth to the drops of night mist?

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Out of whose body came the ice? and who gave birth to the cold mist of heaven?

<a id="v30"></a>**30** The waters are joined together, hard as a stone, and the face of the deep is covered.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** Are the bands of the Pleiades fixed by you, or are the cords of Orion made loose?

<a id="v32"></a>**32** Do you make Mazzaroth come out in its right time, or are the Bear and its children guided by you?

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Have you knowledge of the laws of the heavens? did you give them rule over the earth?

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Is your voice sent up to the cloud, so that you may be covered by the weight of waters?

<a id="v35"></a>**35** Do you send out the thunder-flames, so that they may go, and say to you, Here we are?

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Who has put wisdom in the high clouds, or given knowledge to the lights of the north?

<a id="v37"></a>**37** By whose wisdom are the clouds numbered, or the water-skins of the heavens turned to the earth,

<a id="v38"></a>**38** When the earth becomes hard as metal, and is joined together in masses?

<a id="v39"></a>**39** Do you go after food for the she-lion, or get meat so that the young lions may have enough,

<a id="v40"></a>**40** When they are stretched out in their holes, and are waiting in the brushwood?

<a id="v41"></a>**41** Who gives in the evening the meat he is searching for, when his young ones are crying to God; when the young lions with loud noise go wandering after their food?
