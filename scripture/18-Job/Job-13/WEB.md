# Job 13 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** “Behold, my eye has seen all this. My ear has heard and understood it.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** What you know, I know also. I am not inferior to you.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** “Surely I would speak to the Almighty. I desire to reason with God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But you are forgers of lies. You are all physicians of no value.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Oh that you would be completely silent! Then you would be wise.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Hear now my reasoning. Listen to the pleadings of my lips.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Will you speak unrighteously for God, and talk deceitfully for him?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Will you show partiality to him? Will you contend for God?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Is it good that he should search you out? Or as one deceives a man, will you deceive him?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He will surely reprove you if you secretly show partiality.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Shall not his majesty make you afraid, And his dread fall on you?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Your memorable sayings are proverbs of ashes, Your defenses are defenses of clay.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** “Be silent, leave me alone, that I may speak. Let come on me what will.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Why should I take my flesh in my teeth, and put my life in my hand?

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Behold, he will kill me. I have no hope. Nevertheless, I will maintain my ways before him.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** This also shall be my salvation, that a godless man shall not come before him.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Hear diligently my speech. Let my declaration be in your ears.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** See now, I have set my cause in order. I know that I am righteous.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Who is he who will contend with me? For then would I hold my peace and give up the spirit.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** “Only don’t do two things to me; then I will not hide myself from your face:

<a id="v21"></a>**21** withdraw your hand far from me; and don’t let your terror make me afraid.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Then call, and I will answer; or let me speak, and you answer me.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** How many are my iniquities and sins? Make me know my disobedience and my sin.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Why hide you your face, and hold me for your enemy?

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Will you harass a driven leaf? Will you pursue the dry stubble?

<a id="v26"></a>**26** For you write bitter things against me, and make me inherit the iniquities of my youth:

<a id="v27"></a>**27** You also put my feet in the stocks, and mark all my paths. You set a bound to the soles of my feet,

<a id="v28"></a>**28** though I am decaying like a rotten thing, like a garment that is moth-eaten.
