# Job 13 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Truly, my eye has seen all this, word of it has come to my ear, and I have knowledge of it.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** The same things are in my mind as in yours; I am equal to you.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But I would have talk with the Ruler of all, and my desire is to have an argument with God.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But you put a false face on things; all your attempts to put things right are of no value.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** If only you would keep quiet, it would be a sign of wisdom!

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Give ear to the argument of my mouth, and take note of the words of my lips.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Will you say in God's name what is not right, and put false words into his mouth?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Will you have respect for God's person in this cause, and put yourselves forward as his supporters?

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Will it be good for you to be searched out by him, or have you the thought that he may be guided into error like a man?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He will certainly put you right, if you have respect for persons in secret.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Will not his glory put you in fear, so that your hearts will be overcome before him?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Your wise sayings are only dust, and your strong places are only earth.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Keep quiet, and let me say what is in my mind, whatever may come to me.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I will take my flesh in my teeth, and put my life in my hand.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Truly, he will put an end to me; I have no hope; but I will not give way in argument before him;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And that will be my salvation, for an evil-doer would not come before him,

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Give ear with care to my words, and keep what I say in your minds.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** See now, I have put my cause in order, and I am certain that I will be seen to be right.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Is any one able to take up the argument against me? If so, I would keep quiet and give up my breath.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Only two things do not do to me, then I will come before your face:

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Take your hand far away from me; and let me not be overcome by fear of you.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Then at the sound of your voice I will give answer; or let me put forward my cause for you to give me an answer.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** What is the number of my evil-doings and my sins? give me knowledge of them.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Why is your face veiled from me, as if I was numbered among your haters?

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Will you be hard on a leaf in flight before the wind? will you make a dry stem go more quickly on its way?

<a id="v26"></a>**26** For you put bitter things on record against me, and send punishment on me for the sins of my early years;

<a id="v27"></a>**27** And you put chains on my feet, watching all my ways, and making a limit for my steps;

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Though a man comes to nothing like a bit of dead wood, or like a robe which has become food for the worm.
