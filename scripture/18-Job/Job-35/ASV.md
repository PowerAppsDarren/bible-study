# Job 35 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Moreover Elihu answered and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Thinkest thou this to be thy right, Or sayest thou, My righteousness is more than God’s,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** That thou sayest, What advantage will it be unto thee? And, What profit shall I have, more than if I had sinned?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will answer thee, And thy companions with thee.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Look unto the heavens, and see; And behold the skies, which are higher than thou.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** If thou hast sinned, what effectest thou against him? And if thy transgressions be multiplied, what doest thou unto him?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** If thou be righteous, what givest thou him? Or what receiveth he of thy hand?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Thy wickednessmay hurta man as thou art; And thy righteousness may profit a son of man.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** By reason of the multitude of oppressions they cry out; They cry for help by reason of the arm of the mighty.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But none saith, Where is God my Maker, Who giveth songs in the night,

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Who teacheth us more than the beasts of the earth, And maketh us wiser than the birds of the heavens?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** There they cry, but none giveth answer, Because of the pride of evil men.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Surely God will not hear an emptycry, Neither will the Almighty regard it.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** How much less when thou sayest thou beholdest him not, The cause is before him, and thou waitest for him!

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But now, because he hath not visited in his anger, Neither doth he greatly regard arrogance;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Therefore doth Job open his mouth in vanity; He multiplieth words without knowledge.
