# Job 35 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Elihu made answer and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Does it seem to you to be right, and righteousness before God, to say,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** What profit is it to me, and how am I better off than if I had done wrong?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I will make answer to you and to your friends:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Let your eyes be turned to the heavens, and lifted up to see the skies; they are higher than you.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** If you have done wrong, is he any the worse for it? and if your sins are great in number, what is it to him?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** If you are upright, what do you give to him? or what does he take from your hand?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Your evil-doing may have an effect on a man like yourself, or your righteousness on a son of man.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Because the hand of the cruel is hard on them, men are making sounds of grief; they are crying out for help because of the arm of the strong.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But no one has said, Where is God my Maker, who gives songs in the night;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Who gives us more knowledge than the beasts of the earth, and makes us wiser than the birds of the heaven?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** There they are crying out because of the pride of the evil-doers, but he gives them no answer.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But God will not give ear to what is false, or the Ruler of all take note of it;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** How much less when you say that you do not see him; that the cause is before him, and you are waiting for him.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And now ... ;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And Job's mouth is open wide to give out what is of no profit, increasing words without knowledge.
