# Job 4 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Eliphaz the Temanite made answer and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** If one says a word, will it be a weariness to you? but who is able to keep from saying what is in his mind?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Truly, you have been a helper to others, and you have made feeble hands strong;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He who was near to falling has been lifted up by your words, and you have given strength to bent knees.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But now it has come on you and it is a weariness to you; you are touched by it and your mind is troubled.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Is not your fear of God your support, and your upright way of life your hope?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Have you ever seen destruction come to an upright man? or when were the god-fearing ever cut off?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** What I have seen is that those by whom trouble has been ploughed, and evil planted, get the same for themselves.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** By the breath of God destruction takes them, and by the wind of his wrath they are cut off.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Though the noise of the lion and the sounding of his voice, may be loud, the teeth of the young lions are broken.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The old lion comes to his end for need of food, and the young of the she-lion go wandering in all directions.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** A word was given to me secretly, and the low sound of it came to my ears.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** In troubled thoughts from visions of the night, when deep sleep comes on men,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Fear came on me and shaking, and my bones were full of trouble;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And a breath was moving over my face; the hair of my flesh became stiff:

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Something was present before me, but I was not able to see it clearly; there was a form before my eyes: a quiet voice came to my ears, saying:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** May a man be upright before God? or a man be clean before his Maker?

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Truly, he puts no faith in his servants, and he sees error in his angels;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** How much more those living in houses of earth, whose bases are in the dust! They are crushed more quickly than an insect;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Between morning and evening they are completely broken; they come to an end for ever, and no one takes note.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** If their tent-cord is pulled up, do they not come to an end, and without wisdom?
