# Job 19 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Job answered and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How long will ye vex my soul, and break me in pieces with words?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** These ten times have ye reproached me: ye are not ashamed that ye make yourselves strange to me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And be it indeed that I have erred, mine error remaineth with myself.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** If indeed ye will magnify yourselves against me, and plead against me my reproach:

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Know now that God hath overthrown me, and hath compassed me with his net.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Behold, I cry out of wrong, but I am not heard: I cry aloud, but there is no judgment.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He hath fenced up my way that I cannot pass, and he hath set darkness in my paths.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He hath stripped me of my glory, and taken the crown from my head.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He hath destroyed me on every side, and I am gone: and mine hope hath he removed like a tree.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He hath also kindled his wrath against me, and he counteth me unto him as one of his enemies.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** His troops come together, and raise up their way against me, and encamp round about my tabernacle.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He hath put my brethren far from me, and mine acquaintance are verily estranged from me.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** My kinsfolk have failed, and my familiar friends have forgotten me.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** They that dwell in mine house, and my maids, count me for a stranger: I am an alien in their sight.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** I called my servant, and he gave me no answer; I intreated him with my mouth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** My breath is strange to my wife, though I intreated for the children’s sake of mine own body.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Yea, young children despised me; I arose, and they spake against me.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** All my inward friends abhorred me: and they whom I loved are turned against me.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My bone cleaveth to my skin and to my flesh, and I am escaped with the skin of my teeth.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Have pity upon me, have pity upon me, O ye my friends; for the hand of God hath touched me.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Why do ye persecute me as God, and are not satisfied with my flesh?

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Oh that my words were now written! oh that they were printed in a book!

<a id="v24"></a>**24** That they were graven with an iron pen and lead in the rock for ever!

<a id="v25"></a>**25** For I know that my redeemer liveth, and that he shall stand at the latter day upon the earth:

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And though after my skin worms destroy this body, yet in my flesh shall I see God:

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Whom I shall see for myself, and mine eyes shall behold, and not another; though my reins be consumed within me.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** But ye should say, Why persecute we him, seeing the root of the matter is found in me?

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Be ye afraid of the sword: for wrath bringeth the punishments of the sword, that ye may know there is a judgment.
