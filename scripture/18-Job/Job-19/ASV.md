# Job 19 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Job answered and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How long will ye vex my soul, And break me in pieces with words?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** These ten times have ye reproached me: Ye are not ashamed that ye deal hardly with me.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And be it indeed that I have erred, Mine error remaineth with myself.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** If indeed ye will magnify yourselves against me, And plead against me my reproach;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Know now that God hath subverted me in my cause, And hath compassed me with his net.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Behold, I cry out of wrong, but I am not heard: I cry for help, but there is no justice.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He hath walled up my way that I cannot pass, And hath set darkness in my paths.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He hath stripped me of my glory, And taken the crown from my head.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He hath broken me down on every side, and I am gone; And my hope hath he plucked up like a tree.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He hath also kindled his wrath against me, And he counteth me unto him as one of his adversaries.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** His troops come on together, And cast up their way against me, And encamp round about my tent.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** He hath put my brethren far from me, And mine acquaintance are wholly estranged from me.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** My kinsfolk have failed, And my familiar friends have forgotten me.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** They that dwell in my house, and my maids, count me for a stranger: I am an alien in their sight.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** I call unto my servant, and he giveth me no answer, ThoughI entreat him with my mouth.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** My breath is strange to my wife, And my supplication to the children of mine own mother.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Even young children despise me; If I arise, they speak against me.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** All my familiar friends abhor me, And they whom I loved are turned against me.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My bone cleaveth to my skin and to my flesh, And I am escaped with the skin of my teeth.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Have pity upon me, have pity upon me, O ye my friends; For the hand of God hath touched me.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Why do ye persecute me as God, And are not satisfied with my flesh?

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Oh that my words were now written! Oh that they were inscribed in a book!

<a id="v24"></a>**24** That with an iron pen and lead They were graven in the rock for ever!

<a id="v25"></a>**25** But as for me I know that my Redeemer liveth, And at last he will stand up upon the earth:

<a id="v26"></a>**26** And after my skin, eventhisbody, is destroyed, Then without my flesh shall I see God;

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Whom I, even I, shall see, on my side, And mine eyes shall behold, and not as a stranger. My heart is consumed within me.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** If ye say, How we will persecute him! And that the root of the matter is found in me;

<a id="v29"></a>**29** Be ye afraid of the sword: For wrathbringeththe punishments of the sword, That ye may know there is a judgment.
