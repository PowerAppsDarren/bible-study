# Job 8 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then answered Bildad the Shuhite, and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How long wilt thou speak these things? and how long shall the words of thy mouth be like a strong wind?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Doth God pervert judgment? or doth the Almighty pervert justice?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** If thy children have sinned against him, and he have cast them away for their transgression;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** If thou wouldest seek unto God betimes, and make thy supplication to the Almighty;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** If thou wert pure and upright; surely now he would awake for thee, and make the habitation of thy righteousness prosperous.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Though thy beginning was small, yet thy latter end should greatly increase.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** For enquire, I pray thee, of the former age, and prepare thyself to the search of their fathers:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** (For we are but of yesterday, and know nothing, because our days upon earth are a shadow:)

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Shall not they teach thee, and tell thee, and utter words out of their heart?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Can the rush grow up without mire? can the flag grow without water?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Whilst it is yet in his greenness, and not cut down, it withereth before any other herb.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** So are the paths of all that forget God; and the hypocrite’s hope shall perish:

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Whose hope shall be cut off, and whose trust shall be a spider’s web.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He shall lean upon his house, but it shall not stand: he shall hold it fast, but it shall not endure.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He is green before the sun, and his branch shooteth forth in his garden.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** His roots are wrapped about the heap, and seeth the place of stones.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** If he destroy him from his place, then it shall deny him, saying, I have not seen thee.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Behold, this is the joy of his way, and out of the earth shall others grow.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Behold, God will not cast away a perfect man, neither will he help the evil doers:

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Till he fill thy mouth with laughing, and thy lips with rejoicing.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** They that hate thee shall be clothed with shame; and the dwelling place of the wicked shall come to nought.
