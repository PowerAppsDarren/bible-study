# Job 8 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Bildad the Shuhite made answer and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How long will you say these things, and how long will the words of your mouth be like a strong wind?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Does God give wrong decisions? or is the Ruler of all not upright in his judging?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** If your children have done evil against him, then their punishment is from his hand.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** If you will make search for God with care, and put your request before the Ruler of all;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** If you are clean and upright; then he will certainly be moved to take up your cause, and will make clear your righteousness by building up your house again.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And though your start was small, your end will be very great.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Put the question now to the past generations, and give attention to what has been searched out by their fathers:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** (For we are but of yesterday, and have no knowledge, because our days on earth are gone like a shade:)

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Will they not give you teaching, and say words of wisdom to you?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Will the river-plant come up in its pride without wet earth? will the grass get tall without water?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** When it is still green, without being cut down, it becomes dry and dead before any other plant.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** So is the end of all who do not keep God in mind; and the hope of the evil-doer comes to nothing:

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Whose support is cut off, and whose hope is no stronger than a spider's thread.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He is looking to his family for support, but it is not there; he puts his hope in it, but it comes to nothing.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He is full of strength before the sun, and his branches go out over his garden.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** His roots are twisted round the stones, forcing their way in between them.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** If he is taken away from his place, then it will say, I have not seen you.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Such is the joy of his way, and out of the dust another comes up to take his place.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Truly, God will not give up him who is without sin, and will not take evil-doers by the hand.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** The time will come when your mouth will be full of laughing, and cries of joy will come from your lips.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Your haters will be clothed with shame, and the tent of the sinner will not be seen again.
