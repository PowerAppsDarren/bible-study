# Job 8 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Bildad the Shuhite answered,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** “How long will you speak these things? Shall the words of your mouth be a mighty wind?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Does God pervert justice? Or does the Almighty pervert righteousness?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** If your children have sinned against him, He has delivered them into the hand of their disobedience.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** If you want to seek God diligently, make your supplication to the Almighty.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** If you were pure and upright, surely now he would awaken for you, and make the habitation of your righteousness prosperous.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Though your beginning was small, yet your latter end would greatly increase.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** “Please inquire of past generations. Find out about the learning of their fathers.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** (For we are but of yesterday, and know nothing, because our days on earth are a shadow.)

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Shall they not teach you, tell you, and utter words out of their heart?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** “Can the papyrus grow up without mire? Can the rushes grow without water?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** While it is yet in its greenness, not cut down, it withers before any other reed.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** So are the paths of all who forget God. The hope of the godless man shall perish,

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Whose confidence shall break apart, Whose trust is a spider’s web.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** He shall lean on his house, but it shall not stand. He shall cling to it, but it shall not endure.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** He is green before the sun. His shoots go out along his garden.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** His roots are wrapped around the rock pile. He sees the place of stones.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** If he is destroyed from his place, then it shall deny him, saying, ‘I have not seen you.’

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Behold, this is the joy of his way: out of the earth, others shall spring.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** “Behold, God will not cast away a blameless man, neither will he uphold the evildoers.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He will still fill your mouth with laughter, your lips with shouting.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Those who hate you shall be clothed with shame. The tent of the wicked shall be no more.”
