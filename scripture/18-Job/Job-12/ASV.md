# Job 12 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Job answered and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** No doubt but ye are the people, And wisdom shall die with you.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But I have understanding as well as you; I am not inferior to you: Yea, who knoweth not such things as these?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I am as one that is a laughing-stock to his neighbor, I who called upon God, and he answered: The just, the perfect man is a laughing-stock.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** In the thought of him that is at ease there is contempt for misfortune; It is ready for them whose foot slippeth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The tents of robbers prosper, And they that provoke God are secure; Into whose hand God bringeth abundantly.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But ask now the beasts, and they shall teach thee; And the birds of the heavens, and they shall tell thee:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Or speak to the earth, and it shall teach thee; And the fishes of the sea shall declare unto thee.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Who knoweth not in all these, That the hand of Jehovah hath wrought this,

<a id="v10"></a>**10** In whose hand is the soul of every living thing, And the breath of all mankind?

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Doth not the ear try words, Even as the palate tasteth its food?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** With aged men is wisdom, And in length of days understanding.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** WithGodis wisdom and might; He hath counsel and understanding.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Behold, he breaketh down, and it cannot be built again; He shutteth up a man, and there can be no opening.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Behold, he withholdeth the waters, and they dry up; Again, he sendeth them out, and they overturn the earth.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** With him is strength and wisdom; The deceived and the deceiver are his.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He leadeth counsellors away stripped, And judges maketh he fools.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He looseth the bond of kings, And he bindeth their loins with a girdle.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He leadeth priests away stripped, And overthroweth the mighty.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He removeth the speech of the trusty, And taketh away the understanding of the elders.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He poureth contempt upon princes, And looseth the belt of the strong.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** He uncovereth deep things out of darkness, And bringeth out to light the shadow of death.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** He increaseth the nations, and he destroyeth them: He enlargeth the nations, and he leadeth them captive.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** He taketh away understanding from the chiefs of the people of the earth, And causeth them to wander in a wilderness where there is no way.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** They grope in the dark without light; And he maketh them to stagger like a drunken man.
