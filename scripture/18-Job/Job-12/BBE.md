# Job 12 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Job made answer and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** No doubt you have knowledge, and wisdom will come to an end with you.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** But I have a mind as well as you; I am equal to you: yes, who has not knowledge of such things as these?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** It seems that I am to be as one who is a cause of laughing to his neighbour, one who makes his prayer to God and is answered! the upright man who has done no wrong is to be made sport of!

<a id="v5"></a>**5** In the thought of him who is in comfort there is no respect for one who is in trouble; such is the fate of those whose feet are slipping.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** There is wealth in the tents of those who make destruction, and those by whom God is moved to wrath are safe; even those whose god is their strength.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But put now a question to the beasts, and get teaching from them; or to the birds of the heaven, and they will make it clear to you;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Or to the things which go flat on the earth, and they will give you wisdom; and the fishes of the sea will give you news of it.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Who does not see by all these that the hand of the Lord has done this?

<a id="v10"></a>**10** In whose hand is the soul of every living thing, and the breath of all flesh of man.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Are not words tested by the ear, even as food is tasted by the mouth?

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Old men have wisdom, and a long life gives knowledge.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** With him there is wisdom and strength; power and knowledge are his.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Truly, there is no building up of what is pulled down by him; when a man is shut up by him, no one may let him loose.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Truly, he keeps back the waters and they are dry; he sends them out and the earth is overturned.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** With him are strength and wise designs; he who is guided into error, together with his guide, are in his hands;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** He takes away the wisdom of the wise guides, and makes judges foolish;

<a id="v18"></a>**18** He undoes the chains of kings, and puts his band on them;

<a id="v19"></a>**19** He makes priests prisoners, overturning those in safe positions;

<a id="v20"></a>**20** He makes the words of responsible persons without effect, and takes away the good sense of the old;

<a id="v21"></a>**21** He puts shame on chiefs, and takes away the power of the strong;

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Uncovering deep things out of the dark, and making the deep shade bright;

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Increasing nations, and sending destruction on them; making wide the lands of peoples, and then giving them up.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** He takes away the wisdom of the rulers of the earth, and sends them wandering in a waste where there is no way.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** They go feeling about in the dark without light, wandering without help like those overcome with wine.
