# Job 26 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Job made answer and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How have you given help to him who has no power! how have you been the salvation of the arm which has no strength!

<a id="v3"></a>**3** How have you given teaching to him who has no wisdom, and fully made clear true knowledge!

<a id="v4"></a>**4** To whom have your words been said? and whose spirit came out from you?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** The shades in the underworld are shaking; the waters and those living in them.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The underworld is uncovered before him, and Destruction has no veil.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** By his hand the north is stretched out in space, and the earth is hanging on nothing.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** By him the waters are shut up in his thick clouds, and the cloud does not give way under them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** By him the face of his high seat is veiled, and his cloud stretched out over it.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** By him a circle is marked out on the face of the waters, to the limits of the light and the dark.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The pillars of heaven are shaking, and are overcome by his sharp words.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** By his power the sea was made quiet; and by his wisdom Rahab was wounded.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** By his wind the heavens become bright: by his hand the quickly moving snake was cut through.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** See, these are only the outskirts of his ways; and how small is that which comes to our ears about him! But the thunder of his acts of power is outside all knowledge.
