# Job 26 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** But Job answered and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How hast thou helped him that is without power? how savest thou the arm that hath no strength?

<a id="v3"></a>**3** How hast thou counselled him that hath no wisdom? and how hast thou plentifully declared the thing as it is?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** To whom hast thou uttered words? and whose spirit came from thee?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Dead things are formed from under the waters, and the inhabitants thereof.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Hell is naked before him, and destruction hath no covering.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He stretcheth out the north over the empty place, and hangeth the earth upon nothing.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He bindeth up the waters in his thick clouds; and the cloud is not rent under them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He holdeth back the face of his throne, and spreadeth his cloud upon it.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He hath compassed the waters with bounds, until the day and night come to an end.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The pillars of heaven tremble and are astonished at his reproof.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** He divideth the sea with his power, and by his understanding he smiteth through the proud.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** By his spirit he hath garnished the heavens; his hand hath formed the crooked serpent.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Lo, these are parts of his ways: but how little a portion is heard of him? but the thunder of his power who can understand?
