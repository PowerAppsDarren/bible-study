# Job 26 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Job answered and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** How hast thou helped him that is without power! How hast thou saved the arm that hath no strength!

<a id="v3"></a>**3** How hast thou counselled him that hath no wisdom, And plentifully declared sound knowledge!

<a id="v4"></a>**4** To whom hast thou uttered words? And whose spirit came forth from thee?

<a id="v5"></a>**5** They that are deceased tremble Beneath the waters and the inhabitants thereof.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Sheol is naked beforeGod, And Abaddon hath no covering.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** He stretcheth out the north over empty space, And hangeth the earth upon nothing.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** He bindeth up the waters in his thick clouds; And the cloud is not rent under them.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He incloseth the face of his throne, And spreadeth his cloud upon it.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He hath described a boundary upon the face of the waters, Unto the confines of light and darkness.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** The pillars of heaven tremble  And are astonished at his rebuke.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** He stirreth up the sea with his power, And by his understanding he smiteth through Rahab.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** By his Spirit the heavens are garnished; His hand hath pierced the swift serpent.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Lo, these are but the outskirts of his ways: And how small a whisper do we hear of him! But the thunder of his power who can understand?
