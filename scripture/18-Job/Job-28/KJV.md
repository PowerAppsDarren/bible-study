# Job 28 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Surely there is a vein for the silver, and a place for gold where they fine it.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Iron is taken out of the earth, and brass is molten out of the stone.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** He setteth an end to darkness, and searcheth out all perfection: the stones of darkness, and the shadow of death.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** The flood breaketh out from the inhabitant; even the waters forgotten of the foot: they are dried up, they are gone away from men.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** As for the earth, out of it cometh bread: and under it is turned up as it were fire.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The stones of it are the place of sapphires: and it hath dust of gold.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** There is a path which no fowl knoweth, and which the vulture’s eye hath not seen:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The lion’s whelps have not trodden it, nor the fierce lion passed by it.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He putteth forth his hand upon the rock; he overturneth the mountains by the roots.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He cutteth out rivers among the rocks; and his eye seeth every precious thing.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He bindeth the floods from overflowing; and the thing that is hid bringeth he forth to light.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But where shall wisdom be found? and where is the place of understanding?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Man knoweth not the price thereof; neither is it found in the land of the living.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The depth saith, It is not in me: and the sea saith, It is not with me.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** It cannot be gotten for gold, neither shall silver be weighed for the price thereof.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** It cannot be valued with the gold of Ophir, with the precious onyx, or the sapphire.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** The gold and the crystal cannot equal it: and the exchange of it shall not be for jewels of fine gold.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** No mention shall be made of coral, or of pearls: for the price of wisdom is above rubies.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The topaz of Ethiopia shall not equal it, neither shall it be valued with pure gold.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Whence then cometh wisdom? and where is the place of understanding?

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Seeing it is hid from the eyes of all living, and kept close from the fowls of the air.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Destruction and death say, We have heard the fame thereof with our ears.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** God understandeth the way thereof, and he knoweth the place thereof.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** For he looketh to the ends of the earth, and seeth under the whole heaven;

<a id="v25"></a>**25** To make the weight for the winds; and he weigheth the waters by measure.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** When he made a decree for the rain, and a way for the lightning of the thunder:

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Then did he see it, and declare it; he prepared it, yea, and searched it out.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** And unto man he said, Behold, the fear of the Lord, that is wisdom; and to depart from evil is understanding.
