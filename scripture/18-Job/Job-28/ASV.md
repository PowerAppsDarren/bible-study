# Job 28 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Surely there is a mine for silver, And a place for gold which they refine.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Iron is taken out of the earth, And copper is molten out of the stone.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Man setteth an end to darkness, And searcheth out, to the furthest bound, The stones of obscurity and of thick darkness.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** He breaketh open a shaft away from where men sojourn; They are forgotten of the foot; They hang afar from men, they swing to and fro.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** As for the earth, out of it cometh bread; And underneath it is turned up as it were by fire.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** The stones thereof are the place of sapphires, And it hath dust of gold.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** That path no bird of prey knoweth, Neither hath the falcon’s eye seen it:

<a id="v8"></a>**8** The proud beasts have not trodden it, Nor hath the fierce lion passed thereby.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He putteth forth his hand upon the flinty rock; He overturneth the mountains by the roots.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** He cutteth out channels among the rocks; And his eye seeth every precious thing.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** He bindeth the streams that they trickle not; And the thing that is hid bringeth he forth to light.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But where shall wisdom be found? And where is the place of understanding?

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Man knoweth not the price thereof; Neither is it found in the land of the living.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** The deep saith, It is not in me; And the sea saith, It is not with me.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** It cannot be gotten for gold, Neither shall silver be weighed for the price thereof.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** It cannot be valued with the gold of Ophir, With the precious onyx, or the sapphire.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Gold and glass cannot equal it, Neither shall it be exchanged for jewels of fine gold.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** No mention shall be made of coral or of crystal: Yea, the price of wisdom is above rubies.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** The topaz of Ethiopia shall not equal it, Neither shall it be valued with pure gold.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** Whence then cometh wisdom? And where is the place of understanding?

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Seeing it is hid from the eyes of all living, And kept close from the birds of the heavens.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Destruction and Death say, We have heard a rumor thereof with our ears.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** God understandeth the way thereof, And he knoweth the place thereof.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** For he looketh to the ends of the earth, And seeth under the whole heaven;

<a id="v25"></a>**25** To make a weight for the wind: Yea, he meteth out the waters by measure.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** When he made a decree for the rain, And a way for the lightning of the thunder;

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Then did he see it, and declare it; He established it, yea, and searched it out.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** And unto man he said, Behold, the fear of the Lord, that is wisdom; And to depart from evil is understanding.
