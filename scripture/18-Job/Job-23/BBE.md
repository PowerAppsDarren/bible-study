# Job 23 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Job made answer and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Even today my outcry is bitter; his hand is hard on my sorrow.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** If only I had knowledge of where he might be seen, so that I might come even to his seat!

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I would put my cause in order before him, and my mouth would be full of arguments.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I would see what his answers would be, and have knowledge of what he would say to me.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Would he make use of his great power to overcome me? No, but he would give attention to me.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** There an upright man might put his cause before him; and I would be free for ever from my judge.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** See, I go forward, but he is not there; and back, but I do not see him;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I am looking for him on the left hand, but there is no sign of him; and turning to the right, I am not able to see him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** For he has knowledge of the way I take; after I have been tested I will come out like gold.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My feet have gone in his steps; I have kept in his way, without turning to one side or to the other.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I have never gone against the orders of his lips; the words of his mouth have been stored up in my heart.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But his purpose is fixed and there is no changing it; and he gives effect to the desire of his soul.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For what has been ordered for me by him will be gone through to the end: and his mind is full of such designs.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** For this cause I am in fear before him, my thoughts of him overcome me.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For God has made my heart feeble, and my mind is troubled before the Ruler of all.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** For I am overcome by the dark, and by the black night which is covering my face.
