# Job 23 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Job answered and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Even to-day is my complaint rebellious: My stroke is heavier than my groaning.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Oh that I knew where I might find him! That I might come even to his seat!

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I would set my cause in order before him, And fill my mouth with arguments.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I would know the words which he would answer me, And understand what he would say unto me.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Would he contend with me in the greatness of his power? Nay; but he would give heed unto me.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** There the upright might reason with him; So should I be delivered for ever from my judge.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Behold, I go forward, but he is notthere; And backward, but I cannot perceive him;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** On the left hand, when he doth work, but I cannot behold him; He hideth himself on the right hand, that I cannot see him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But he knoweth the way that I take; When he hath tried me, I shall come forth as gold.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My foot hath held fast to his steps; His way have I kept, and turned not aside.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I have not gone back from the commandment of his lips; I have treasured up the words of his mouth more than my necessary food.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But he is in onemind, and who can turn him? And what his soul desireth, even that he doeth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For he performeth that which is appointed for me: And many such things are with him.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Therefore am I terrified at his presence; When I consider, I am afraid of him.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For God hath made my heart faint, And the Almighty hath terrified me;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Because I was not cut off before the darkness, Neither did he cover the thick darkness from my face.
