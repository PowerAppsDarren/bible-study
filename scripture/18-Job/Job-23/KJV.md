# Job 23 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Job answered and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Even to day is my complaint bitter: my stroke is heavier than my groaning.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Oh that I knew where I might find him! that I might come even to his seat!

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I would order my cause before him, and fill my mouth with arguments.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I would know the words which he would answer me, and understand what he would say unto me.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Will he plead against me with his great power? No; but he would put strength in me.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** There the righteous might dispute with him; so should I be delivered for ever from my judge.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Behold, I go forward, but he is not there; and backward, but I cannot perceive him:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** On the left hand, where he doth work, but I cannot behold him: he hideth himself on the right hand, that I cannot see him:

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But he knoweth the way that I take: when he hath tried me, I shall come forth as gold.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My foot hath held his steps, his way have I kept, and not declined.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Neither have I gone back from the commandment of his lips; I have esteemed the words of his mouth more than my necessary food.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But he is in one mind, and who can turn him? and what his soul desireth, even that he doeth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For he performeth the thing that is appointed for me: and many such things are with him.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Therefore am I troubled at his presence: when I consider, I am afraid of him.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For God maketh my heart soft, and the Almighty troubleth me:

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Because I was not cut off before the darkness, neither hath he covered the darkness from my face.
