# Job 23 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Job answered,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** “Even today my complaint is rebellious. His hand is heavy in spite of my groaning.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Oh that I knew where I might find him! That I might come even to his seat!

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I would set my cause in order before him, and fill my mouth with arguments.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I would know the words which he would answer me, and understand what he would tell me.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Would he contend with me in the greatness of his power? No, but he would listen to me.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** There the upright might reason with him, so I should be delivered forever from my judge.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** “If I go east, he is not there; if west, I can’t find him;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He works to the north, but I can’t see him. He turns south, but I can’t catch a glimpse of him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** But he knows the way that I take. When he has tried me, I shall come out like gold.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** My foot has held fast to his steps. I have kept his way, and not turned aside.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I haven’t gone back from the commandment of his lips. I have treasured up the words of his mouth more than my necessary food.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** But he stands alone, and who can oppose him? What his soul desires, even that he does.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For he performs that which is appointed for me. Many such things are with him.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Therefore I am terrified at his presence. When I consider, I am afraid of him.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** For God has made my heart faint. The Almighty has terrified me.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Because I was not cut off before the darkness, neither did he cover the thick darkness from my face.
