# Job 16 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And Job made answer and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Such things have frequently come to my ears: you are comforters who only give trouble.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** May words which are like the wind be stopped? or what is troubling you to make answer to them?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** It would not be hard for me to say such things if your souls were in my soul's place; joining words together against you, and shaking my head at you:

<a id="v5"></a>**5** I might give you strength with my mouth, and not keep back the comfort of my lips.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** If I say what is in my mind, my pain becomes no less: and if I keep quiet, how much of it goes from me?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But now he has overcome me with weariness and fear, and I am in the grip of all my trouble.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** It has come up as a witness against me, and the wasting of my flesh makes answer to my face.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** I am broken by his wrath, and his hate has gone after me; he has made his teeth sharp against me: my haters are looking on me with cruel eyes;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Their mouths are open wide against me; the blows of his bitter words are falling on my face; all of them come together in a mass against me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** God gives me over to the power of sinners, sending me violently into the hands of evil-doers.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I was in comfort, but I have been broken up by his hands; he has taken me by the neck, shaking me to bits; he has put me up as a mark for his arrows.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** His bowmen come round about me; their arrows go through my body without mercy; my life is drained out on the earth.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** I am broken with wound after wound; he comes rushing on me like a man of war.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I have made haircloth the clothing of my skin, and my horn is rolled in the dust.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** My face is red with weeping, and my eyes are becoming dark;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Though my hands have done no violent acts, and my prayer is clean.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** O earth, let not my blood be covered, and let my cry have no resting-place!

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Even now my witness is in heaven, and the supporter of my cause is on high.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My friends make sport of me; to God my eyes are weeping,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** So that he may give decision for a man in his cause with God, and between a son of man and his neighbour.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For in a short time I will take the journey from which I will not come back.
