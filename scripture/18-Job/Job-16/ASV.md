# Job 16 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then Job answered and said,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** I have heard many such things: Miserable comforters are ye all.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Shall vain words have an end? Or what provoketh thee that thou answerest?

<a id="v4"></a>**4** I also could speak as ye do; If your soul were in my soul’s stead, I could join words together against you, And shake my head at you.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** But I would strengthen you with my mouth, And the solace of my lips would assuage your grief.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Though I speak, my grief is not assuaged; And though I forbear, what am I eased?

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But now he hath made me weary: Thou hast made desolate all my company.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And thou hast laid fast hold on me, which is a witness against me: And my leanness riseth up against me, It testifieth to my face.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** He hath torn me in his wrath, and persecuted me; He hath gnashed upon me with his teeth: Mine adversary sharpeneth his eyes upon me.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** They have gaped upon me with their mouth; They have smitten me upon the cheek reproachfully: They gather themselves together against me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** God delivereth me to the ungodly, And casteth me into the hands of the wicked.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** I was at ease, and he brake me asunder; Yea, he hath taken me by the neck, and dashed me to pieces: He hath also set me up for his mark.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** His archers compass me round about; He cleaveth my reins asunder, and doth not spare; He poureth out my gall upon the ground.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** He breaketh me with breach upon breach; He runneth upon me like a giant.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** I have sewed sackcloth upon my skin, And have laid my horn in the dust.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** My face is red with weeping, And on my eyelids is the shadow of death;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Although there is no violence in my hands, And my prayer is pure.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** O earth, cover not thou my blood, And let my cry have noresting-place.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Even now, behold, my witness is in heaven, And he that voucheth for me is on high.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** My friends scoff at me: But mine eye poureth out tears unto God,

<a id="v21"></a>**21** That he would maintain the right of a man with God, And of a son of man with his neighbor!

<a id="v22"></a>**22** For when a few years are come, I shall go the way whence I shall not return.
