# Matthew 28 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Now late on the sabbath day, as it began to dawn toward the first day of the week, came Mary Magdalene and the other Mary to see the sepulchre.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And behold, there was a great earthquake; for an angel of the Lord descended from heaven, and came and rolled away the stone, and sat upon it.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** His appearance was as lightning, and his raiment white as snow:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** and for fear of him the watchers did quake, and became as dead men.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And the angel answered and said unto the women, Fear not ye; for I know that ye seek Jesus, who hath been crucified.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** He is not here; for he is risen, even as he said. Come, see the place where the Lord lay.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And go quickly, and tell his disciples, He is risen from the dead; and lo, he goeth before you into Galilee; there shall ye see him: lo, I have told you.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And they departed quickly from the tomb with fear and great joy, and ran to bring his disciples word.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And behold, Jesus met them, saying, All hail. And they came and took hold of his feet, and worshipped him.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Then saith Jesus unto them, Fear not: go tell my brethren that they depart into Galilee, and there shall they see me.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Now while they were going, behold, some of the guard came into the city, and told unto the chief priests all the things that were come to pass.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And when they were assembled with the elders, and had taken counsel, they gave much money unto the soldiers,

<a id="v13"></a>**13** saying, Say ye, His disciples came by night, and stole him away while we slept.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And if this come to the governor’s ears, we will persuade him, and rid you of care.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** So they took the money, and did as they were taught: and this saying was spread abroad among the Jews, and continueth until this day.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** But the eleven disciples went into Galilee, unto the mountain where Jesus had appointed them.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And when they saw him, they worshipped him; but some doubted.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** And Jesus came to them and spake unto them, saying, All authority hath been given unto me in heaven and on earth.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Go ye therefore, and make disciples of all the nations, baptizing them into the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit:

<a id="v20"></a>**20** teaching them to observe all things whatsoever I commanded you: and lo, I am with you always, even unto the end of the world.
