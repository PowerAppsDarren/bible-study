# Matthew 5 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And seeing the multitudes, he went up into the mountain: and when he had sat down, his disciples came unto him:

<a id="v2"></a>**2** and he opened his mouth and taught them, saying,

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Blessed are the poor in spirit: for theirs is the kingdom of heaven.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Blessed are they that mourn: for they shall be comforted.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Blessed are the meek: for they shall inherit the earth.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Blessed are they that hunger and thirst after righteousness: for they shall be filled.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Blessed are the merciful: for they shall obtain mercy.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Blessed are the pure in heart: for they shall see God.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Blessed are the peacemakers: for they shall be called sons of God.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Blessed are they that have been persecuted for righteousness’ sake: for theirs is the kingdom of heaven.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Blessed are ye when men shall reproach you, and persecute you, and say all manner of evil against you falsely, for my sake.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** Rejoice, and be exceeding glad: for great is your reward in heaven: for so persecuted they the prophets that were before you.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Ye are the salt of the earth: but if the salt have lost its savor, wherewith shall it be salted? it is thenceforth good for nothing, but to be cast out and trodden under foot of men.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Ye are the light of the world. A city set on a hill cannot be hid.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Neither do men light a lamp, and put it under the bushel, but on the stand; and it shineth unto all that are in the house.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Even so let your light shine before men; that they may see your good works, and glorify your Father who is in heaven.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** Think not that I came to destroy the law or the prophets: I came not to destroy, but to fulfil.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** For verily I say unto you, Till heaven and earth pass away, one jot or one tittle shall in no wise pass away from the law, till all things be accomplished.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Whosoever therefore shall break one of these least commandments, and shall teach men so, shall be called least in the kingdom of heaven: but whosoever shall do and teach them, he shall be called great in the kingdom of heaven.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** For I say unto you, that except your righteousness shall exceed the righteousness of the scribes and Pharisees, ye shall in no wise enter into the kingdom of heaven.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** Ye have heard that it was said to them of old time, Thou shalt not kill; and whosoever shall kill shall be in danger of the judgment:

<a id="v22"></a>**22** but I say unto you, that every one who is angry with his brother shall be in danger of the judgment; and whosoever shall say to his brother, Raca, shall be in danger of the council; and whosoever shall say, Thou fool, shall be in danger of the hell of fire.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** If therefore thou art offering thy gift at the altar, and there rememberest that thy brother hath aught against thee,

<a id="v24"></a>**24** leave there thy gift before the altar, and go thy way, first be reconciled to thy brother, and then come and offer thy gift.

<a id="v25"></a>**25** Agree with thine adversary quickly, while thou art with him in the way; lest haply the adversary deliver thee to the judge, and the judge deliver thee to the officer, and thou be cast into prison.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** Verily I say unto thee, Thou shalt by no means come out thence, till thou have paid the last farthing.

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Ye have heard that it was said, Thou shalt not commit adultery:

<a id="v28"></a>**28** but I say unto you, that every one that looketh on a woman to lust after her hath committed adultery with her already in his heart.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** And if thy right eye causeth thee to stumble, pluck it out, and cast it from thee: for it is profitable for thee that one of thy members should perish, and not thy whole body be cast into hell.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** And if thy right hand causeth thee to stumble, cut it off, and cast it from thee: for it is profitable for thee that one of thy members should perish, and not thy whole body go into hell.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** It was said also, Whosoever shall put away his wife, let him give her a writing of divorcement:

<a id="v32"></a>**32** but I say unto you, that every one that putteth away his wife, saving for the cause of fornication, maketh her an adulteress: and whosoever shall marry her when she is put away committeth adultery.

<a id="v33"></a>**33** Again, ye have heard that it was said to them of old time, Thou shalt not forswear thyself, but shalt perform unto the Lord thine oaths:

<a id="v34"></a>**34** but I say unto you, Swear not at all; neither by the heaven, for it is the throne of God;

<a id="v35"></a>**35** nor by the earth, for it is the footstool of his feet; nor by Jerusalem, for it is the city of the great King.

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Neither shalt thou swear by thy head, for thou canst not make one hair white or black.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** But let your speech be, Yea, yea; Nay, nay: and whatsoever is more than these is of the evil one.

<a id="v38"></a>**38** Ye have heard that it was said, An eye for an eye, and a tooth for a tooth:

<a id="v39"></a>**39** but I say unto you, Resist not him that is evil: but whosoever smiteth thee on thy right cheek, turn to him the other also.

<a id="v40"></a>**40** And if any man would go to law with thee, and take away thy coat, let him have thy cloak also.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** And whosoever shall compel thee to go one mile, go with him two.

<a id="v42"></a>**42** Give to him that asketh thee, and from him that would borrow of thee turn not thou away.

<a id="v43"></a>**43** Ye have heard that it was said, Thou shalt love thy neighbor, and hate thine enemy:

<a id="v44"></a>**44** but I say unto you, Love your enemies, and pray for them that persecute you;

<a id="v45"></a>**45** that ye may be sons of your Father who is in heaven: for he maketh his sun to rise on the evil and the good, and sendeth rain on the just and the unjust.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** For if ye love them that love you, what reward have ye? do not even the publicans the same?

<a id="v47"></a>**47** And if ye salute your brethren only, what do ye more than others? do not even the Gentiles the same?

<a id="v48"></a>**48** Ye therefore shall be perfect, as your heavenly Father is perfect.
