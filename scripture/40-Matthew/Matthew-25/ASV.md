# Matthew 25 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then shall the kingdom of heaven be likened unto ten virgins, who took their lamps, and went forth to meet the bridegroom.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And five of them were foolish, and five were wise.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For the foolish, when they took their lamps, took no oil with them:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** but the wise took oil in their vessels with their lamps.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Now while the bridegroom tarried, they all slumbered and slept.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** But at midnight there is a cry, Behold, the bridegroom! Come ye forth to meet him.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Then all those virgins arose, and trimmed their lamps.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And the foolish said unto the wise, Give us of your oil; for our lamps are going out.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But the wise answered, saying, Peradventure there will not be enough for us and you: go ye rather to them that sell, and buy for yourselves.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And while they went away to buy, the bridegroom came; and they that were ready went in with him to the marriage feast: and the door was shut.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Afterward came also the other virgins, saying, Lord, Lord, open to us.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But he answered and said, Verily I say unto you, I know you not.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Watch therefore, for ye know not the day nor the hour.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Forit isaswhena man, going into another country, called his own servants, and delivered unto them his goods.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And unto one he gave five talents, to another two, to another one; to each according to his several ability; and he went on his journey.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Straightway he that received the five talents went and traded with them, and made other five talents.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** In like manner he also that received the two gained other two.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But he that received the one went away and digged in the earth, and hid his lord’s money.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Now after a long time the lord of those servants cometh, and maketh a reckoning with them.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And he that received the five talents came and brought other five talents, saying, Lord, thou deliveredst unto me five talents: lo, I have gained other five talents.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** His lord said unto him, Well done, good and faithful servant: thou hast been faithful over a few things, I will set thee over many things; enter thou into the joy of thy lord.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** And he also that received the two talents came and said, Lord, thou deliveredst unto me two talents: lo, I have gained other two talents.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** His lord said unto him, Well done, good and faithful servant: thou hast been faithful over a few things, I will set thee over many things; enter thou into the joy of thy lord.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And he also that had received the one talent came and said, Lord, I knew thee that thou art a hard man, reaping where thou didst not sow, and gathering where thou didst not scatter;

<a id="v25"></a>**25** and I was afraid, and went away and hid thy talent in the earth: lo, thou hast thine own.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** But his lord answered and said unto him, Thou wicked and slothful servant, thou knewest that I reap where I sowed not, and gather where I did not scatter;

<a id="v27"></a>**27** thou oughtest therefore to have put my money to the bankers, and at my coming I should have received back mine own with interest.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Take ye away therefore the talent from him, and give it unto him that hath the ten talents.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** For unto every one that hath shall be given, and he shall have abundance: but from him that hath not, even that which he hath shall be taken away.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** And cast ye out the unprofitable servant into the outer darkness: there shall be the weeping and the gnashing of teeth.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** But when the Son of man shall come in his glory, and all the angels with him, then shall he sit on the throne of his glory:

<a id="v32"></a>**32** and before him shall be gathered all the nations: and he shall separate them one from another, as the shepherd separateth the sheep from the goats;

<a id="v33"></a>**33** and he shall set the sheep on his right hand, but the goats on the left.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Then shall the King say unto them on his right hand, Come, ye blessed of my Father, inherit the kingdom prepared for you from the foundation of the world:

<a id="v35"></a>**35** for I was hungry, and ye gave me to eat; I was thirsty, and ye gave me drink; I was a stranger, and ye took me in;

<a id="v36"></a>**36** naked, and ye clothed me; I was sick, and ye visited me; I was in prison, and ye came unto me.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Then shall the righteous answer him, saying, Lord, when saw we thee hungry, and fed thee? or athirst, and gave thee drink?

<a id="v38"></a>**38** And when saw we thee a stranger, and took thee in? or naked, and clothed thee?

<a id="v39"></a>**39** And when saw we thee sick, or in prison, and came unto thee?

<a id="v40"></a>**40** And the King shall answer and say unto them, Verily I say unto you, Inasmuch as ye did it unto one of these my brethren, even these least, ye did it unto me.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** Then shall he say also unto them on the left hand, Depart from me, ye cursed, into the eternal fire which is prepared for the devil and his angels:

<a id="v42"></a>**42** for I was hungry, and ye did not give me to eat; I was thirsty, and ye gave me no drink;

<a id="v43"></a>**43** I was a stranger, and ye took me not in; naked, and ye clothed me not; sick, and in prison, and ye visited me not.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** Then shall they also answer, saying, Lord, when saw we thee hungry, or athirst, or a stranger, or naked, or sick, or in prison, and did not minister unto thee?

<a id="v45"></a>**45** Then shall he answer them, saying, Verily I say unto you, Inasmuch as ye did it not unto one of these least, ye did it not unto me.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** And these shall go away into eternal punishment: but the righteous into eternal life.
