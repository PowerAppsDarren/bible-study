# Matthew 25 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** Then shall the kingdom of heaven be likened unto ten virgins, which took their lamps, and went forth to meet the bridegroom.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** And five of them were wise, and five were foolish.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** They that were foolish took their lamps, and took no oil with them:

<a id="v4"></a>**4** But the wise took oil in their vessels with their lamps.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** While the bridegroom tarried, they all slumbered and slept.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And at midnight there was a cry made, Behold, the bridegroom cometh; go ye out to meet him.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Then all those virgins arose, and trimmed their lamps.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And the foolish said unto the wise, Give us of your oil; for our lamps are gone out.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** But the wise answered, saying, Not so; lest there be not enough for us and you: but go ye rather to them that sell, and buy for yourselves.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And while they went to buy, the bridegroom came; and they that were ready went in with him to the marriage: and the door was shut.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Afterward came also the other virgins, saying, Lord, Lord, open to us.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** But he answered and said, Verily I say unto you, I know you not.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Watch therefore, for ye know neither the day nor the hour wherein the Son of man cometh.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** For the kingdom of heaven is as a man travelling into a far country, who called his own servants, and delivered unto them his goods.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And unto one he gave five talents, to another two, and to another one; to every man according to his several ability; and straightway took his journey.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Then he that had received the five talents went and traded with the same, and made them other five talents.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And likewise he that had received two, he also gained other two.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** But he that had received one went and digged in the earth, and hid his lord’s money.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** After a long time the lord of those servants cometh, and reckoneth with them.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** And so he that had received five talents came and brought other five talents, saying, Lord, thou deliveredst unto me five talents: behold, I have gained beside them five talents more.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** His lord said unto him, Well done, thou good and faithful servant: thou hast been faithful over a few things, I will make thee ruler over many things: enter thou into the joy of thy lord.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** He also that had received two talents came and said, Lord, thou deliveredst unto me two talents: behold, I have gained two other talents beside them.

<a id="v23"></a>**23** His lord said unto him, Well done, good and faithful servant; thou hast been faithful over a few things, I will make thee ruler over many things: enter thou into the joy of thy lord.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Then he which had received the one talent came and said, Lord, I knew thee that thou art an hard man, reaping where thou hast not sown, and gathering where thou hast not strawed:

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And I was afraid, and went and hid thy talent in the earth: lo, there thou hast that is thine.

<a id="v26"></a>**26** His lord answered and said unto him, Thou wicked and slothful servant, thou knewest that I reap where I sowed not, and gather where I have not strawed:

<a id="v27"></a>**27** Thou oughtest therefore to have put my money to the exchangers, and then at my coming I should have received mine own with usury.

<a id="v28"></a>**28** Take therefore the talent from him, and give it unto him which hath ten talents.

<a id="v29"></a>**29** For unto every one that hath shall be given, and he shall have abundance: but from him that hath not shall be taken away even that which he hath.

<a id="v30"></a>**30** And cast ye the unprofitable servant into outer darkness: there shall be weeping and gnashing of teeth.

<a id="v31"></a>**31** When the Son of man shall come in his glory, and all the holy angels with him, then shall he sit upon the throne of his glory:

<a id="v32"></a>**32** And before him shall be gathered all nations: and he shall separate them one from another, as a shepherd divideth his sheep from the goats:

<a id="v33"></a>**33** And he shall set the sheep on his right hand, but the goats on the left.

<a id="v34"></a>**34** Then shall the King say unto them on his right hand, Come, ye blessed of my Father, inherit the kingdom prepared for you from the foundation of the world:

<a id="v35"></a>**35** For I was an hungred, and ye gave me meat: I was thirsty, and ye gave me drink: I was a stranger, and ye took me in:

<a id="v36"></a>**36** Naked, and ye clothed me: I was sick, and ye visited me: I was in prison, and ye came unto me.

<a id="v37"></a>**37** Then shall the righteous answer him, saying, Lord, when saw we thee an hungred, and fed thee? or thirsty, and gave thee drink?

<a id="v38"></a>**38** When saw we thee a stranger, and took thee in? or naked, and clothed thee?

<a id="v39"></a>**39** Or when saw we thee sick, or in prison, and came unto thee?

<a id="v40"></a>**40** And the King shall answer and say unto them, Verily I say unto you, Inasmuch as ye have done it unto one of the least of these my brethren, ye have done it unto me.

<a id="v41"></a>**41** Then shall he say also unto them on the left hand, Depart from me, ye cursed, into everlasting fire, prepared for the devil and his angels:

<a id="v42"></a>**42** For I was an hungred, and ye gave me no meat: I was thirsty, and ye gave me no drink:

<a id="v43"></a>**43** I was a stranger, and ye took me not in: naked, and ye clothed me not: sick, and in prison, and ye visited me not.

<a id="v44"></a>**44** Then shall they also answer him, saying, Lord, when saw we thee an hungred, or athirst, or a stranger, or naked, or sick, or in prison, and did not minister unto thee?

<a id="v45"></a>**45** Then shall he answer them, saying, Verily I say unto you, Inasmuch as ye did it not to one of the least of these, ye did it not to me.

<a id="v46"></a>**46** And these shall go away into everlasting punishment: but the righteous into life eternal.
