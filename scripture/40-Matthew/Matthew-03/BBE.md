# Matthew 3 — Basic English Bible (BBE)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** And in those days John the Baptist came preaching in the waste land of Judaea,

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Saying, Let your hearts be turned from sin; for the kingdom of heaven is near.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** For this is he of whom Isaiah the prophet said, The voice of one crying in the waste land, Make ready the way of the Lord, make his roads straight.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Now John was clothed in camel's hair, with a leather band about him; and his food was locusts and honey.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Then Jerusalem and all Judaea went out to him, and all the people from near Jordan;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And they were given baptism by him in the river Jordan, saying openly that they had done wrong.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** But when he saw a number of the Pharisees and Sadducees coming to his baptism, he said to them, Offspring of snakes, at whose word are you going in flight from the wrath to come?

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Let your change of heart be seen in your works:

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And say not to yourselves, We have Abraham for our father; because I say to you that God is able from these stones to make children for Abraham.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And even now the axe is put to the root of the trees; every tree then which does not give good fruit is cut down, and put into the fire.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Truly, I give baptism with water to those of you whose hearts are changed; but he who comes after me is greater than I, whose shoes I am not good enough to take up: he will give you baptism with the Holy Spirit and with fire:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** In whose hand is the instrument with which he will make clean his grain; he will put the good grain in his store, but the waste will be burned up in the fire which will never be put out.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Then Jesus came from Galilee to John at the Jordan, to be given baptism by him.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** But John would have kept him back, saying, It is I who have need of baptism from you, and do you come to me?

<a id="v15"></a>**15** But Jesus made answer, saying to him, Let it be so now: because so it is right for us to make righteousness complete. Then he gave him baptism.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And Jesus, having been given baptism, straight away went up from the water; and, the heavens opening, he saw the Spirit of God coming down on him as a dove;

<a id="v17"></a>**17** And a voice came out of heaven, saying, This is my dearly loved Son, with whom I am well pleased.
