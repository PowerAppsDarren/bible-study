# Matthew 1 — World English Bible (WEB)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The book of the genealogy of Jesus Christ, the son of David, the son of Abraham.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Abraham became the father of Isaac. Isaac became the father of Jacob. Jacob became the father of Judah and his brothers.

<a id="v3"></a>**3** Judah became the father of Perez and Zerah by Tamar. Perez became the father of Hezron. Hezron became the father of Ram.

<a id="v4"></a>**4** Ram became the father of Amminadab. Amminadab became the father of Nahshon. Nahshon became the father of Salmon.

<a id="v5"></a>**5** Salmon became the father of Boaz by Rahab. Boaz became the father of Obed by Ruth. Obed became the father of Jesse.

<a id="v6"></a>**6** Jesse became the father of King David. David became the father of Solomon by her who had been the wife of Uriah.

<a id="v7"></a>**7** Solomon became the father of Rehoboam. Rehoboam became the father of Abijah. Abijah became the father of Asa.

<a id="v8"></a>**8** Asa became the father of Jehoshaphat. Jehoshaphat became the father of Joram. Joram became the father of Uzziah.

<a id="v9"></a>**9** Uzziah became the father of Jotham. Jotham became the father of Ahaz. Ahaz became the father of Hezekiah.

<a id="v10"></a>**10** Hezekiah became the father of Manasseh. Manasseh became the father of Amon. Amon became the father of Josiah.

<a id="v11"></a>**11** Josiah became the father of Jechoniah and his brothers, at the time of the exile to Babylon.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** After the exile to Babylon, Jechoniah became the father of Shealtiel. Shealtiel became the father of Zerubbabel.

<a id="v13"></a>**13** Zerubbabel became the father of Abiud. Abiud became the father of Eliakim. Eliakim became the father of Azor.

<a id="v14"></a>**14** Azor became the father of Zadok. Zadok became the father of Achim. Achim became the father of Eliud.

<a id="v15"></a>**15** Eliud became the father of Eleazar. Eleazar became the father of Matthan. Matthan became the father of Jacob.

<a id="v16"></a>**16** Jacob became the father of Joseph, the husband of Mary, from whom was born Jesus, who is called Christ.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** So all the generations from Abraham to David are fourteen generations; from David to the exile to Babylon fourteen generations; and from the carrying away to Babylon to the Christ, fourteen generations.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Now the birth of Jesus Christ was like this; for after his mother, Mary, was engaged to Joseph, before they came together, she was found pregnant by the Holy Spirit.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Joseph, her husband, being a righteous man, and not willing to make her a public example, intended to put her away secretly.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** But when he thought about these things, behold, an angel of the Lord appeared to him in a dream, saying, “Joseph, son of David, don’t be afraid to take to yourself Mary, your wife, for that which is conceived in her is of the Holy Spirit.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** She shall give birth to a son. You shall call his name Jesus, for it is he who shall save his people from their sins.”

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Now all this has happened, that it might be fulfilled which was spoken by the Lord through the prophet, saying,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** “Behold, the virgin shall be with child, and shall give birth to a son. They shall call his name Immanuel”; which is, being interpreted, “God with us.”

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Joseph arose from his sleep, and did as the angel of the Lord commanded him, and took his wife to himself;

<a id="v25"></a>**25** and didn’t know her sexually until she had given birth to her firstborn son. He named him Jesus.
