# Matthew 1 — American Standard Version (ASV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The book of the generation of Jesus Christ, the son of David, the son of Abraham.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Abraham begat Isaac; and Isaac begat Jacob; and Jacob begat Judah and his brethren;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** and Judah begat Perez and Zerah of Tamar; and Perez begat Hezron; and Hezron begat Ram;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** and Ram begat Amminadab; and Amminadab begat Nahshon; and Nahshon begat Salmon;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** and Salmon begat Boaz of Rahab; and Boaz begat Obed of Ruth; and Obed begat Jesse;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** and Jesse begat David the king. And David begat Solomon of her that had been the wife of Uriah;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** and Solomon begat Rehoboam; and Rehoboam begat Abijah; and Abijah begat Asa;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** and Asa begat Jehoshaphat; and Jehoshaphat begat Joram; and Joram begat Uzziah;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** and Uzziah begat Jotham; and Jotham begat Ahaz; and Ahaz begat Hezekiah;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** and Hezekiah begat Manasseh; and Manasseh begat Amon; and Amon begat Josiah;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** and Josiah begat Jechoniah and his brethren, at the time of the carrying away to Babylon.

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And after the carrying away to Babylon, Jechoniah begat Shealtiel; and Shealtiel begat Zerubbabel;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** and Zerubbabel begat Abiud; and Abiud begat Eliakim; and Eliakim begat Azor;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** and Azor begat Sadoc; and Sadoc begat Achim; and Achim begat Eliud;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** and Eliud begat Eleazar; and Eleazar begat Matthan; and Matthan begat Jacob;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** and Jacob begat Joseph the husband of Mary, of whom was born Jesus, who is called Christ.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** So all the generations from Abraham unto David are fourteen generations; and from David unto the carrying away to Babylon fourteen generations; and from the carrying away to Babylon unto the Christ fourteen generations.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Now the birth of Jesus Christ was on this wise: When his mother Mary had been betrothed to Joseph, before they came together she was found with child of the Holy Spirit.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** And Joseph her husband, being a righteous man, and not willing to make her a public example, was minded to put her away privily.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** But when he thought on these things, behold, an angel of the Lord appeared unto him in a dream, saying, Joseph, thou son of David, fear not to take unto thee Mary thy wife: for that which is conceived in her is of the Holy Spirit.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And she shall bring forth a son; and thou shalt call his name JESUS; for it is he that shall save his people from their sins.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Now all this is come to pass, that it might be fulfilled which was spoken by the Lord through the prophet, saying,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Behold, the virgin shall be with child, and shall bring forth a son, And they shall call his name Immanuel;  which is, being interpreted, God with us.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** And Joseph arose from his sleep, and did as the angel of the Lord commanded him, and took unto him his wife;

<a id="v25"></a>**25** and knew her not till she had brought forth a son: and he called his name JESUS.
