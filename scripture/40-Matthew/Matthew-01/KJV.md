# Matthew 1 — King James Version (KJV)

*Public domain. Source: getbible.net.*

---

<a id="v1"></a>**1** The book of the generation of Jesus Christ, the son of David, the son of Abraham.

<a id="v2"></a>**2** Abraham begat Isaac; and Isaac begat Jacob; and Jacob begat Judas and his brethren;

<a id="v3"></a>**3** And Judas begat Phares and Zara of Thamar; and Phares begat Esrom; and Esrom begat Aram;

<a id="v4"></a>**4** And Aram begat Aminadab; and Aminadab begat Naasson; and Naasson begat Salmon;

<a id="v5"></a>**5** And Salmon begat Booz of Rachab; and Booz begat Obed of Ruth; and Obed begat Jesse;

<a id="v6"></a>**6** And Jesse begat David the king; and David the king begat Solomon of her that had been the wife of Urias;

<a id="v7"></a>**7** And Solomon begat Roboam; and Roboam begat Abia; and Abia begat Asa;

<a id="v8"></a>**8** And Asa begat Josaphat; and Josaphat begat Joram; and Joram begat Ozias;

<a id="v9"></a>**9** And Ozias begat Joatham; and Joatham begat Achaz; and Achaz begat Ezekias;

<a id="v10"></a>**10** And Ezekias begat Manasses; and Manasses begat Amon; and Amon begat Josias;

<a id="v11"></a>**11** And Josias begat Jechonias and his brethren, about the time they were carried away to Babylon:

<a id="v12"></a>**12** And after they were brought to Babylon, Jechonias begat Salathiel; and Salathiel begat Zorobabel;

<a id="v13"></a>**13** And Zorobabel begat Abiud; and Abiud begat Eliakim; and Eliakim begat Azor;

<a id="v14"></a>**14** And Azor begat Sadoc; and Sadoc begat Achim; and Achim begat Eliud;

<a id="v15"></a>**15** And Eliud begat Eleazar; and Eleazar begat Matthan; and Matthan begat Jacob;

<a id="v16"></a>**16** And Jacob begat Joseph the husband of Mary, of whom was born Jesus, who is called Christ.

<a id="v17"></a>**17** So all the generations from Abraham to David are fourteen generations; and from David until the carrying away into Babylon are fourteen generations; and from the carrying away into Babylon unto Christ are fourteen generations.

<a id="v18"></a>**18** Now the birth of Jesus Christ was on this wise: When as his mother Mary was espoused to Joseph, before they came together, she was found with child of the Holy Ghost.

<a id="v19"></a>**19** Then Joseph her husband, being a just man, and not willing to make her a publick example, was minded to put her away privily.

<a id="v20"></a>**20** But while he thought on these things, behold, the angel of the Lord appeared unto him in a dream, saying, Joseph, thou son of David, fear not to take unto thee Mary thy wife: for that which is conceived in her is of the Holy Ghost.

<a id="v21"></a>**21** And she shall bring forth a son, and thou shalt call his name JESUS: for he shall save his people from their sins.

<a id="v22"></a>**22** Now all this was done, that it might be fulfilled which was spoken of the Lord by the prophet, saying,

<a id="v23"></a>**23** Behold, a virgin shall be with child, and shall bring forth a son, and they shall call his name Emmanuel, which being interpreted is, God with us.

<a id="v24"></a>**24** Then Joseph being raised from sleep did as the angel of the Lord had bidden him, and took unto him his wife:

<a id="v25"></a>**25** And knew her not till she had brought forth her firstborn son: and he called his name JESUS.
