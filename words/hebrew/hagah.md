# hâgâh — הָגָה  ·  H1897

**Language:** Hebrew  ·  **Strong's:** H1897  ·  **Pronunciation:** daw-gaw'

## Definition

to murmur (in pleasure or anger); by implication, to ponder

## Derivation

a primitive root (compare H1901 (הָגִיג));

## KJV usage

imagine, meditate, mourn, mutter, roar, [idiom] sore, speak, study, talk, utter.
